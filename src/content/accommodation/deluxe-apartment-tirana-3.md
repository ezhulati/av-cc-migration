This air-conditioned apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a haven of comfort with its well-appointed amenities. From the moment guests step inside, they are greeted with a space that boasts a cozy bedroom, a pristine bathroom stocked with fresh bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with streaming services for entertainment. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to guests' culinary needs, while the balcony offers captivating city views, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

What sets Deluxe Apartment Tirana 3 apart are the thoughtful touches that enhance the guest experience. The provision of wine or champagne and fruits adds a luxurious feel, while in-room treats like chocolates or cookies ensure a delightful stay.

For those looking to immerse themselves in Tirana's culture and shopping scene, the apartment is conveniently close to Saint Paul Cathedral, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana. Moreover, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 16 km away, guests enjoy hassle-free travel connections.

Deluxe Apartment Tirana 3 stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering not just a place to sleep, but a memorable part of the Tirana experience.
