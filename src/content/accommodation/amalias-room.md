The accommodation boasts air-conditioned spaces complete with a terrace, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests. Inside, the homestay presents a spacious layout, equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. Attention to detail is evident in the provision of towels and bed linen, enhancing the overall guest experience. Additionally, an outdoor dining area is available, perfect for those who wish to enjoy their meals amidst the natural beauty of Himare.

For moments of relaxation, the property features a well-maintained garden, inviting guests to unwind in a serene setting.

Located conveniently close to Prinos Beach, less than 1 km away, Amalias Room offers an ideal base for beach lovers. Despite its secluded feel, the homestay remains accessible, with Ioannina Airport situated 143 km away, making it a feasible getaway for both local and international travelers.

Amalias Room stands as a testament to the charm and hospitality Himare has to offer, promising an unforgettable stay for those seeking both adventure and tranquility by the sea.
