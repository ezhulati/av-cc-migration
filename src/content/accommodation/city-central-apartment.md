This air-conditioned apartment doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a home away from home. With two bedrooms, a balcony for those serene morning coffees, and a living space equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services, guests are guaranteed both relaxation and entertainment. The kitchen is a dream for those who love to cook, boasting modern appliances including an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, and the entire apartment is a smoke-free zone.

For those little necessities or quick snacks, the apartment complex houses its own minimarket. Additionally, guests looking to explore Tirana's vibrant streets and attractions can take advantage of the car rental service available on-site.

City Central Apartment is ideally situated for those eager to explore the city's rich history and culture, with the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania all within easy reach. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, with the apartment offering a convenient paid airport shuttle service.

In summary, City Central Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation option for visitors to Tirana, offering both the luxuries of modern living and the charm of Albanian hospitality.
