The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with fresh bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for those evenings in. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to all culinary needs, whether it's a quick breakfast before exploring the city or a leisurely dinner with sea views. The added luxury of a terrace invites guests to unwind and revel in the picturesque surroundings.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Arteg Apartments - Superior is conveniently located 41 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 45 km from the adventure that awaits at the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 36 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

Arteg Apartments - Superior stands out as an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and scenic beauty in Durrës.
