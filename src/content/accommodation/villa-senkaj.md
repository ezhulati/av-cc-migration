Each apartment at Villa Senkaj is designed to offer a home-away-from-home experience, featuring a terrace that opens up to breathtaking mountain views. The living spaces are thoughtfully arranged with a cozy seating area and a flat-screen TV, complemented by a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven, a fridge, and even a coffee machine for those morning refreshments. Depending on the selection, guests might find themselves enjoying additional luxuries such as a patio or mesmerizing sea views. For convenience, all units are fitted with heating facilities and share a bathroom equipped with a bidet.

The property boasts a tranquil garden, inviting guests to unwind and soak in the peaceful ambiance. For those looking to explore the local beaches, Ri Beach and Liro Beach are conveniently located within a 1.5 km and 1.7 km radius, respectively. For international travelers, Villa Senkaj is situated 155 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, with the option of a paid airport shuttle service to ensure a smooth transition to and from the property.

Villa Senkaj stands out as an exemplary choice for families and travelers alike, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it a memorable stay for anyone visiting the area.
