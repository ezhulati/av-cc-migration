The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, featuring a cozy bedroom, a living room equipped with satellite TV for entertainment, and a fully stocked kitchen boasting an oven and fridge for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a hairdryer underscores the attention to detail in ensuring guest comfort. Importantly, the property maintains a non-smoking policy, catering to the health and well-being of all guests.

For those with an adventurous spirit, the location serves as a perfect base to explore the vibrant activities Durrës has to offer, with cycling being a popular choice among visitors. The property facilitates this exploration through its bicycle rental service, adding a layer of convenience for guests inclined to discover the area on two wheels.

Notably, Golem Beach is situated a mere 2.5 km away, offering another scenic spot for guests to unwind. For those looking to venture further, Skanderbeg Square is located 41 km from the apartment, presenting an opportunity for a day trip to explore the historical and cultural richness of the area. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 36 km away, making the apartment readily accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

Apartment 50m Away From The Beach emerges as a distinguished option for those in pursuit of a tranquil beachside getaway, backed by the convenience of modern amenities and the allure of local attractions.
