Guests at Eri Apartment are treated to a seamless stay, thanks to the inclusion of complimentary WiFi and private parking. The apartment itself boasts a modern design with two bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, and a cozy seating area, making it an ideal retreat after a day of exploration. The addition of a flat-screen TV and a bathroom equipped with a shower ensures that comfort is never compromised. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, and the apartment's soundproofing guarantees a peaceful night's sleep.

Situated less than a kilometer from the National Gallery of Arts Tirana and a stone's throw away from the Clock Tower Tirana, Eri Apartment is perfectly positioned for guests to immerse themselves in the local culture. The proximity to Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania further enhances its appeal to those looking to experience the best of Tirana. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, Eri Apartment stands out as an accessible and inviting option for both short and extended stays in this dynamic city.
