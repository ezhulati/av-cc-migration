Each air-conditioned studio at Heksamil Hotel is a haven of comfort, featuring soundproofing for a tranquil retreat, a TV for entertainment, and a refrigerator for convenience. The highlight, however, is the balcony that opens up to breathtaking sea views, promising guests a picturesque start and end to their day. The en-suite bathrooms are well-appointed with a shower and toilet, ensuring privacy and comfort.

For those who prefer the tranquility of a private pool, Heksamil Hotel does not disappoint. It offers an exclusive aquatic escape, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Additionally, the convenience of complimentary parking adds to the hassle-free experience of staying here.

Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to local dining options, with a restaurant just around the corner ready to satisfy any cravings. Moreover, the vibrant city center of Sarande is merely 12 km away, offering a plethora of dining, shopping, and entertainment options.

Heksamil Hotel emerges as a distinguished choice for travelers who seek a blend of comfort, convenience, and the allure of the Albanian Riviera.
