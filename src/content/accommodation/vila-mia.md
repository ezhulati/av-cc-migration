This esteemed apartment complex caters to the needs of its guests with meticulously designed air-conditioned units. Each room is equipped with essential amenities such as a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a shower, ensuring a comfortable and secure stay. The attention to detail extends to the provision of bed linen and towels in each unit, highlighting the establishment's dedication to guest comfort.

Adding to the appeal of Vila Mia is the availability of complimentary WiFi throughout the property and the convenience of free private parking for those arriving by car. The presence of a bar on-site offers a relaxing space for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

For travelers flying into the region, the nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, located 94 km away, making Vila Mia an accessible option for both domestic and international visitors. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and unwavering commitment to guest satisfaction, Vila Mia stands as a distinguished choice for those looking to experience the best of Sarandë.
