Guests at Saranda Apartaments Meta are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The property boasts a 24-hour front desk, ensuring assistance is always on hand, and an elevator for easy access to all floors. Complimentary WiFi is available throughout, keeping guests connected during their stay.

The apartments themselves are a haven of comfort, featuring balconies that offer breathtaking sea views. Each unit is thoughtfully equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their own meals. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a bidet, air conditioning, and ample wardrobe space adds to the convenience, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, Maestral Beach is just over a kilometer away, offering another spot for relaxation and sunbathing. The historic Butrint National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a mere 16 km from the property, presenting an excellent opportunity for a day trip. Additionally, Ioannina Airport is the nearest international gateway, located 96 km from the apartments, making Saranda Apartaments Meta an accessible choice for international travelers.

In summary, Saranda Apartaments Meta stands out as a top-tier accommodation option for those visiting Sarandë, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and access to the best the city has to offer.
