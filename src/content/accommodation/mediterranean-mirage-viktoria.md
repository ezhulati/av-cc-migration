This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious retreat after a day of exploration. With two bedrooms, a bathroom, and a balcony offering captivating city views, guests have ample space to unwind. The inclusion of modern amenities such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of travel. The outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy their meals while soaking in the local atmosphere, making every dinner an experience to remember.

For those concerned about travel logistics, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 97 km away, adds an extra layer of convenience, making Mediterranean Mirage Viktoria an ideal base for exploring the wonders of Sarandë and beyond.
