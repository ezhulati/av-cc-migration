The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that invites guests to relax while gazing at the sea. Modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen with an oven and microwave, and a bathroom with a bidet, ensure a stay that combines home comfort with the ease of hotel living. The provision of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of convenience.

For those who appreciate the importance of connectivity, free WiFi is available throughout the property, ensuring that guests remain connected with the world. Additionally, the apartment benefits from an elevator and round-the-clock security, providing peace of mind for a worry-free stay.

Convenience is further enhanced by the presence of a minimarket on the premises, catering to guests' everyday needs. For those looking to explore beyond the local beaches, Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are within accessible distances, offering unique experiences and breathtaking views.

Located 36 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Cinco 523 also offers a paid airport shuttle service, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible. This apartment stands out as a top selection for travelers who value comfort, location, and the little luxuries that make a holiday memorable.
