The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring a cozy bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom complete with a walk-in shower and complimentary slippers. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of free WiFi and the convenience of free private parking, making stays here hassle-free.

For those keen to explore, the apartment is within a reasonable distance from cultural sites, with Port Ohrid and the Church of St. John at Kaneo located 34 km and 35 km away, respectively. Additionally, the proximity to Ohrid Airport, just 41 km away, adds to the appeal for international travelers.

Tushemisht Lake View Studio Apartment is a smoke-free property, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for guests. With its picturesque setting and well-appointed amenities, this apartment is an ideal choice for travelers seeking a peaceful escape by the lake.
