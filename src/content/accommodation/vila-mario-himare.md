Guests are welcomed into a spacious air-conditioned villa, thoughtfully designed with two separate bedrooms, dual living areas, a fully equipped kitchen, and three well-appointed bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for all. Entertainment needs are catered to with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal setting for families or groups looking to unwind in style.

Situated just 1.5 km away from the tranquil Potam Beach and with Ioannina Airport located 142 km away, Vila Mario Himare serves as a perfect base for exploring the enchanting surroundings or simply relishing a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle.
