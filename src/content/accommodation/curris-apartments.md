The apartment, a strict non-smoking establishment, is a mere 2.8 km away from the picturesque Golem Beach, making it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. The accommodation itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a 2-bedroom layout complete with a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a minibar. The addition of free WiFi ensures that guests remain connected and entertained throughout their stay.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Curri's Apartments is strategically located with Skanderbeg Square 41 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich history and culture of the area. Additionally, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 45 km from the property, presents an opportunity for an unforgettable adventure with its breathtaking views. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently 35 km away, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

Curri's Apartments stands out not just for its prime location and comprehensive amenities but also for offering a slice of tranquility amidst the vibrant city life of Durrës.
