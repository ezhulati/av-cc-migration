This elegantly appointed apartment boasts two generously sized bedrooms, a modern bathroom, and an array of amenities designed to cater to the discerning traveler. Guests will appreciate the inclusion of plush bed linen, soft towels, and a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at their fingertips. The dining area provides a cozy setting for meals, while the fully equipped kitchen invites guests to whip up their culinary creations. Perhaps the jewel in the crown is the balcony, offering captivating views of the cityscape below.

For those looking to explore the area, Miku Apartment-DELUXE Apartment at City Center is conveniently located near several of Tirana's cultural landmarks, including the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the bustling Toptani Shopping Centre. Additionally, the provision of a car rental service directly from the apartment facilitates seamless adventures around the city and beyond.

Situated 43 km from the Rock of Kavaje and a mere 1.6 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the property ensures that guests are well-placed to uncover the stories and sights that define Tirana. For those arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, making Miku Apartment-DELUXE Apartment at City Center an accessible and attractive option for both short and extended stays in Albania's vibrant capital.
