The hotel's accommodations are thoughtfully designed to ensure maximum comfort and convenience. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with modern amenities including a desk, kettle, fridge, minibar, and safety deposit box. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV and a private balcony, some of which offer captivating city views. Moreover, the attention to detail is evident in the provision of quality bed linen and towels in every room, ensuring a restful night's sleep.

For those eager to explore the surroundings, Valmar Hotel serves as an ideal base. The area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering a variety of activities such as hiking, fishing, and snorkeling. The hotel's proximity to notable attractions like Ksamil Beach, Sunset Beach, and Coco Beach adds to its appeal, making it easy for guests to experience the best of Ksamil's coastal charm.

Located 93 km from Corfu International Airport, the hotel also offers a convenient paid airport shuttle service, making travel arrangements hassle-free for international guests. Whether it's the allure of the beaches, the thrill of outdoor activities, or simply the desire for a peaceful retreat, Valmar Hotel caters to all, promising a memorable stay in the heart of Ksamil.
