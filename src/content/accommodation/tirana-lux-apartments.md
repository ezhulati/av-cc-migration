The apartments stand out for their thoughtful amenities designed to cater to families and groups alike. Each unit is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with an array of satellite channels for entertainment. The inclusion of a fully furnished kitchen, complete with an oven, toaster, fridge, coffee machine, and kettle, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. The private bathrooms, adorned with slippers and equipped with a shower and hair dryer, add a touch of luxury to the daily routine. Moreover, the provision of fresh bed linen and towels ensures a comfortable and carefree stay.

Adding to the charm of Tirana LUX Apartments is its terrace, offering guests a serene spot to unwind and soak in the city's ambiance. For those keen to explore, the property is conveniently located near historical and cultural landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, House of Leaves, and Rinia Park, making it an ideal base for cultural enthusiasts.

For travelers arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 15 km away, with the apartments facilitating travel arrangements through their paid airport shuttle service. Whether it's the strategic location, the array of in-room amenities, or the attentive services, Tirana LUX Apartments promises a memorable stay in the vibrant city of Tirana.
