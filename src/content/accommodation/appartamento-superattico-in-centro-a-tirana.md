The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with streaming services for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen and dining area cater to those who enjoy preparing their own meals, while the terrace offers breathtaking mountain views, adding a natural backdrop to the urban living experience. Notably, the property ensures a restful night's sleep thanks to soundproofing, while the inclusion of air conditioning, a bidet, and a dressing room in the unit underscores the attention to guest comfort. For a touch of luxury, wine or champagne is available as an additional in-room amenity.

Convenience is further highlighted by the provision of complimentary private parking and WiFi, ensuring that both digital connectivity and transportation needs are seamlessly met. Situated just 3.8 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, adventure seekers have the opportunity to easily venture beyond the cityscape.

For those inclined towards shopping and cultural exploration, the proximity to Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana offers a diverse itinerary. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 15 km away, Appartamento SuperAttico in Centro a Tirana presents an accessible and inviting option for both international visitors and local explorers alike.
