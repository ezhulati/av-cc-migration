The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes two bedrooms, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a balcony that offers captivating city views. The kitchen is fully equipped with a dishwasher and an oven, catering to guests' culinary needs, while the two bathrooms feature walk-in showers for a touch of luxury. Notably, the apartment is designed with soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle, and includes allergy-free and non-smoking environments, prioritizing guest health and comfort.

For those looking to unwind with a drink, the on-site bar provides the perfect setting. While AquaZen Apartments offers a tranquil escape, it also serves as an ideal base for exploring the wider area. Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are within manageable distances, offering unique experiences and breathtaking views. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 32 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

In summary, AquaZen Apartments stands out for its thoughtful amenities, comfortable accommodations, and strategic location, making it an excellent choice for visitors to Durrës looking for a blend of relaxation and adventure.
