Each spacious apartment at Gruda is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. Guests will find a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, a fully equipped kitchen for all their culinary needs, and a pristine bathroom. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV in each apartment, making it easy to unwind after a day of exploration. Importantly, the entire accommodation maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the city, Podgorica Airport is conveniently located just 58 km away, making Gruda Apartments an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Shkodër.
