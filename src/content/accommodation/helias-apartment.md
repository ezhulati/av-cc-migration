Positioned strategically, guests find themselves a mere 1.8 km from the vibrant Golem Beach, while also enjoying proximity to significant cultural landmarks. The historic Skanderbeg Square and the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha are both within a 48 km radius, offering a glimpse into the rich tapestry of the region's history. For those intrigued by natural wonders, the Rock of Kavaje is just 6.3 km away, presenting an ideal excursion for nature enthusiasts.

The apartment itself boasts a harmonious blend of functionality and style, featuring a bedroom designed for restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for culinary adventures, and a bathroom that caters to the essentials of daily refreshment. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring leisure moments are well spent.

For dining enthusiasts, the area surrounding Helia's Apartment does not disappoint, with a variety of dining options that promise to satisfy diverse palates. Further afield, the Durres Amphitheatre and the House of Leaves are accessible at 17 km and 47 km respectively, inviting guests to explore the historical and cultural depth of the area.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located just 42 km away, making Helia's Apartment an ideal base for both short-term getaways and longer explorations of Golem and its surroundings.
