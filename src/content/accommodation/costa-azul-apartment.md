This generously sized apartment is designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms and a living space that is both functional and stylish. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, ensuring guests remain connected and entertained throughout their stay. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen, comes complete with an oven, microwave, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, empowering guests with the choice of home-cooked meals.

One of the standout features of Costa Azul Apartment is its balcony, offering breathtaking mountain views. Furnished for comfort, it invites guests to unwind in the open air, adding an element of relaxation to the stay. The addition of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to privacy and exclusivity.

For those looking to explore beyond the local beaches, Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car lie within a manageable distance, promising adventure and cultural enrichment. Conveniently, the property is situated 39 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, with a paid shuttle service available, ensuring seamless arrivals and departures.

In summary, Costa Azul Apartment stands out for its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and thoughtful touches designed to enhance the guest experience, making it a compelling option for travelers to Golem.
