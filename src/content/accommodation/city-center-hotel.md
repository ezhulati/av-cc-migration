Guests are welcomed into a realm of sophistication from the moment they step inside, where a shared lounge and a well-appointed bar set the stage for a relaxing stay. The hotel prides itself on its commitment to service, offering a 24-hour front desk and attentive room service to cater to guests' needs around the clock.

Accommodations at City Center Hotel are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each air-conditioned room is a sanctuary of comfort, featuring a private bathroom, a functional desk, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The promise of a continental breakfast awaits every morning, providing a perfect start to the day.

For those eager to explore Tirana's rich cultural tapestry, City Center Hotel serves as an ideal base. The former residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet, and the National Arts Gallery are all within easy reach, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local heritage. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 18 km away, ensures seamless travel arrangements.

In summary, City Center Hotel stands out as a beacon of hospitality, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural proximity that makes it a top pick for visitors to Tirana.
