Following a comprehensive renovation in 2010, SEA & SUN Seaview Apartments now offers modern, air-conditioned accommodations equipped with all the comforts of home. Guests can enjoy the luxury of free WiFi throughout their stay, ensuring they remain connected and entertained. For those looking to explore the area or require assistance with travel arrangements, the apartment complex provides both airport transfer services and car rental options, adding a layer of convenience to the travel experience.

Each apartment is designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring a private balcony that offers stunning views of the sea. The living spaces are thoughtfully arranged with a cozy seating area and a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen comes fully equipped with an oven and microwave, allowing guests to prepare their meals with ease. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which include a bidet and slippers, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing stay. Additionally, the provision of bed linen and towels in every unit underscores the apartment's commitment to guest satisfaction.

For those needing to pick up essentials or snacks, a convenient minimarket is located on the premises. While the allure of the beach may be the primary draw for many, SEA & SUN Seaview Apartments also serves as an excellent base for exploring further afield. The historic Skanderbeg Square is 41 km away, and the adventure of the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car awaits at a distance of 45 km. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 35 km from the property, making SEA & SUN Seaview Apartments an accessible and attractive option for both domestic and international travelers.
