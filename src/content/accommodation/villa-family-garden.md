The establishment prides itself on its array of family-friendly accommodations, each equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, satellite flat-screen TVs, refrigerators, and kettles, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. The thoughtful addition of a private entrance to each apartment underscores the property's commitment to guest convenience and privacy.

Designed with families in mind, Villa Family Garden doesn't just stop at comfortable rooms. The property boasts a vibrant children's playground, making it an ideal spot for guests traveling with little ones. The charm of outdoor dining on private balconies overlooking lush gardens adds a special touch to the experience, allowing guests to savor their meals amidst nature.

For those looking to unwind, the apartment complex offers a snack bar and bar, alongside barbecue facilities perfect for a leisurely evening. The option to relax in the garden or enjoy a picnic in the designated area provides a versatile outdoor experience.

Strategically located, Villa Family Garden is a short walk from the enchanting Bora Bora Beach and the popular Ksamil Beach, placing guests in the perfect spot to explore the area's natural beauty. For those venturing further, Corfu International Airport is within reach, with the property facilitating travel arrangements through its paid airport shuttle service.

In essence, Villa Family Garden stands as a testament to thoughtful hospitality, where every detail is curated to enhance the guest experience, making it a top recommendation for those visiting Ksamil.
