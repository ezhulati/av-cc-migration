The accommodations at Bral Apartments 4 are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air-conditioned units that come equipped with a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV complete with streaming services, a fully outfitted kitchen, and a dining area. The private bathrooms add a touch of luxury with complimentary slippers, a bidet, and a hairdryer. For those who revel in the beauty of the sea, each unit offers a patio with an outdoor dining area, allowing guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the breathtaking sea vistas.

Convenience is key at Bral Apartments 4, with an on-site coffee shop and a minimarket catering to guests' needs. The surrounding area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering ample opportunities for fishing, walking tours, diving, and cycling. The property facilitates these adventures by providing car hire services, a bicycle rental service, and even access to a private beach area, ensuring an unforgettable experience for all who visit.

Situated 41 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and a mere 7.8 km from the Rock of Kavaje, Bral Apartments 4 serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural wonders of the region. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 31 km away, with the property offering a paid airport shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

In summary, Bral Apartments 4 stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and adventure of Durrës, providing a perfect mix of luxury, convenience, and access to a plethora of outdoor activities.
