The hotel prides itself on providing breathtaking mountain views, complemented by an array of amenities including a barbecue area for guests to enjoy. The commitment to service is evident with a 24-hour front desk ensuring all needs are met around the clock, and the convenience of free WiFi accessible throughout the premises.

Accommodations at Hotel Roas Ksamil are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is air-conditioned and features a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment, and a safety deposit box for peace of mind. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with a shower, complimentary toiletries, and a hairdryer, ensuring a refreshing start and end to each day. For added convenience, rooms are equipped with a kettle, and select accommodations offer a kitchen complete with a fridge, an oven, and a microwave, catering to those who prefer the comforts of home-cooked meals. The attention to detail is further highlighted with the provision of quality bed linen and towels in every room.

For international travelers, the Corfu International Airport is located 94 kilometers away, making Hotel Roas Ksamil an accessible retreat for both local and global visitors seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and exploration in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
