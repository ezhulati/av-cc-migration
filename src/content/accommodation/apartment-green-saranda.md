The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living and modern amenities. With two bedrooms, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and an equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher and an oven, guests have everything they need at their fingertips. The highlight, however, is the terrace offering breathtaking sea views, perfect for enjoying meals in the outdoor dining area while soaking in the city's vibrant atmosphere. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further enhances the quality of stay for all guests.

For those who might need last-minute essentials or snacks, a convenient minimarket on the premises caters to these needs without the hassle of a trip to the city center. Additionally, the allure of exploration is catered to with Butrint National Park located just 15 km away, promising adventures in one of Albania's most cherished natural and historical sites. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 95 km from the apartment, making Apartment Green Saranda an accessible and appealing option for both domestic and international visitors.

With on-site parking, an outdoor swimming pool, and elevator access, Apartment Green Saranda not only promises a comfortable stay but also delivers on convenience and luxury, making it a standout accommodation choice in Sarandë.
