The apartment itself is a testament to contemporary living, boasting a balcony that offers serene mountain views. Designed with a keen eye for detail, it features a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen that comes fully equipped with a dishwasher and an oven, catering to all culinary needs. The inclusion of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with provided towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Notably, the apartment is non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful environment for all guests.

Adding to the convenience, Moreas Apartment hosts a coffee shop and a minimarket on-site, catering to guests' everyday needs without having to venture far. Families visiting with children will appreciate the outdoor play equipment, making it a child-friendly option. For those looking to explore Tirana and its surroundings, the apartment offers both bicycle and car rental services, facilitating easy exploration of the city's must-visit sites such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the Toptani Shopping Centre. 

Located just 13 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Moreas Apartment ensures a hassle-free journey to and from the accommodation, making it an ideal choice for both short-term and extended stays in Tirana.
