Offering seamless connectivity with free WiFi throughout, this property stands out for its generous space and serene garden views.

Guests can enjoy the luxury of a private balcony, two well-appointed bedrooms, and a cozy living room, all complemented by modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV.

The kitchen, equipped with an oven and microwave, caters to those who prefer home-cooked meals, while including a bathroom with a bidet adds to the convenience.

Emphasizing a smoke-free environment, Mino Apartment ensures a clean and healthy stay.

Located just 49 km from the Port of Bar and 58 km from Podgorica Airport, it offers easy access to explore the region or make smooth travel arrangements.
