This homestay distinguishes itself with its spacious layout, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a bathroom equipped with essential amenities, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and the convenience of free WiFi throughout the property ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxuries of a hotel.

One of the highlights of this apartment is its balcony, offering serene garden views that provide a perfect backdrop for a morning coffee or a relaxing evening after a day of exploration. The property prides itself on being non-smoking, catering to guests' health and comfort.

For those interested in exploring the cultural aspects of Tirana, the Bektashi World Centre is within walking distance, while Tanners' Bridge, a historical ottoman era bridge, is just a short drive away. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 18 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

Modern Apartment in Tirana stands out not only for its strategic location and comprehensive amenities but also for offering a tranquil retreat amidst the hustle and bustle of the city. It is an ideal choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture while enjoying the comforts of a thoughtfully furnished living space.
