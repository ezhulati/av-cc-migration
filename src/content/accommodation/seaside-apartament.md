The accommodation boasts a well-appointed air-conditioned space featuring two cozy bedrooms, a living room designed for relaxation, and a balcony that invites guests to enjoy the serene surroundings. Entertainment and comfort are guaranteed with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV and a dining area perfect for enjoying meals. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped with an oven among other essentials, catering to all culinary needs.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures that guests have easy access to basic groceries and necessities without having to leave the comfort of the apartment.

Explorers looking to venture beyond the beach will find Vjetër Beach just 2.8 km away, offering another scenic spot for relaxation and sunbathing. The historical site of Kuzum Baba is also within reach, located 3.9 km from the apartment, providing a glimpse into the local culture and history.

For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 153 km away, making Seaside Apartament an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors seeking a tranquil beach getaway in Vlorë.
