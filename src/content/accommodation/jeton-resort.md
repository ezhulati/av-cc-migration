Guests are treated to an array of rooms, each featuring its own balcony for a private slice of paradise. The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, offering private bathrooms equipped with showers and hairdryers. For those in pursuit of connectivity, complimentary WiFi is available throughout the property, ensuring guests stay connected with the world, should they choose to. Select rooms elevate the experience further by offering breathtaking river views, adding a touch of natural beauty to the stay. Comfort is paramount at JETON RESORT, with every room outfitted with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a restful and enjoyable stay.

Located 37 km from the revered Saint Jovan Bigorski Monastery and 77 km from Ohrid Airport, JETON RESORT presents itself as a perfect base for exploring the rich cultural landscape of the region or simply unwinding in a peaceful setting.
