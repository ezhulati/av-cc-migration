The villa itself is a testament to comfort and privacy, featuring a spacious terrace that invites guests to unwind in the tranquility of its surroundings. Inside, the villa boasts a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-away-from-home experience.

Situated 44 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and equally close to the Grand Park of Tirana and the poignant Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, Relax Villa serves as an ideal base for explorers keen to delve into Albania's rich history and natural beauty. Moreover, its proximity to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offers an easy escape to stunning mountain views.

For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 56 km away, ensuring that arrivals and departures are as stress-free as the stay at Relax Villa.
