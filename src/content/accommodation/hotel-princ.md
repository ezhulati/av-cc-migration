Each guest room at Hotel PRINC is a haven of relaxation, featuring its own balcony for private outdoor enjoyment. The comfort of air conditioning and the convenience of a private bathroom are standard in all accommodations, ensuring a stay marked by comfort and privacy.

For travelers flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 82 km from the hotel, making Hotel PRINC an accessible choice for both domestic and international guests seeking a peaceful retreat by the Albanian coast.
