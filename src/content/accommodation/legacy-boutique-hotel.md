Morning starts are made delightful with options for either a buffet or continental breakfast, catering to diverse palates. For those inclined towards leisure activities, Legacy Boutique Hotel does not disappoint, offering table tennis and promoting cycling in its scenic surroundings. The hotel's commitment to convenience extends to its ironing service and business facilities, including fax and photocopying, ensuring both leisure and business travelers find their needs met.

Located a stone's throw from the historic Monastery Saint Naum and within accessible distance of 72 km from Kastoria National Airport, Legacy Boutique Hotel stands as a beacon of hospitality, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Korçë.
