The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, boasting a tastefully furnished bedroom complemented by modern amenities including a flat-screen TV with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at guests' fingertips. The fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and toaster, invites guests to prepare their own gourmet meals, while the inclusion of a washing machine adds an extra layer of convenience to the stay. The bathroom, complete with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the property's attention to detail in ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free experience.

Outdoor enthusiasts will appreciate the apartment's garden and outdoor dining area, offering a perfect setting for a leisurely breakfast or a tranquil evening under the stars. The availability of free WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests remain connected, whether for leisure or work.

Beyond the confines of Vila 29A, the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks such as Reja - The Cloud, Rinia Park, and Saint Paul Cathedral enriches the stay with cultural immersion. For those keen on exploring further, the Grand Park of Tirana, Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, and the Pyramid of Tirana are also within easy reach, inviting guests to delve into the city's vibrant culture and history.

Conveniently located 16 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Vila 29A stands as an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and an authentic Tirana experience.
