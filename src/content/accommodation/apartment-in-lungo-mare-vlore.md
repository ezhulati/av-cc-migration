Perched just 2.5 km from the picturesque Vjetër Beach and a brief drive from the historic Kuzum Baba at 3.3 km, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the coastal charm of Vlorë. The apartment itself is a sanctuary of modern living, boasting a balcony that frames breathtaking sea views. Inside, a well-appointed living room, a sleek flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, and a contemporary bathroom with a walk-in shower and bidet await. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home, while a dedicated dressing room provides added convenience for beach-bound guests.

Situated 3.6 km from the iconic Independence Square and with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 153 km away, the property also offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring seamless arrivals and departures. Apartment in Lungo Mare Vlore is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the captivating beauty of Albania's coast, designed for discerning travelers who seek the perfect balance of leisure and adventure.
