This generously sized apartment is designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, ensuring guests are never far from their favorite shows. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and a fridge, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. Additionally, the convenience of a washing machine simplifies longer stays. The bathroom, equipped with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the apartment's commitment to comfort.

Outdoor dining becomes a delightful option thanks to the apartment's dedicated area, perfect for enjoying meals with a view. For those looking to explore, Kuzum Baba is within a 3.1 km reach, and Independence Square is just a bit further at 3.3 km, making Ari's Apartment an excellent base for cultural excursions. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, connecting them to this charming Vlorë retreat.

Ari's Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering both the tranquility of beachfront living and the convenience of modern amenities, making it an ideal spot for travelers looking to experience the best of Vlorë.
