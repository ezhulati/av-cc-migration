The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, equipped with complimentary WiFi, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine for convenience. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully stocked with an oven and toaster, ready for guests to whip up a meal at any time. While towels and linens are available upon request, the emphasis on guest comfort is evident. Adding to the allure, the apartment offers captivating city views, allowing guests to soak in the urban landscape from the comfort of their private retreat.

For those keen to explore, the apartment is conveniently located near several of Tirana's attractions, including the historic Tanners' Bridge, the bustling Toptani Shopping Centre, and the esteemed National Gallery of Arts Tirana. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 16 km away, Central Studio Apartment 1, Tirana, stands out as an accessible and inviting option for both short-term and extended stays in the city. This property not only promises a stay that is as enriching as it is comfortable but also positions itself as a gateway to discovering the charms and dynamism of Tirana.
