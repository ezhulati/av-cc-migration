The highlight of this accommodation is undoubtedly its spacious apartment, boasting a balcony that offers unobstructed sea views. Designed to cater to both relaxation and functionality, the apartment features two cozy bedrooms, a comfortable living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, perfect for preparing home-cooked meals. Additionally, the convenience of an ironing service adds to the seamless experience provided.

For those looking to explore the local area or needing assistance with travel arrangements, the property offers airport transfer services and a car rental service, making it easy to plan day trips or explore the surrounding attractions. Notably, Golem Beach is just a short drive away, offering another picturesque spot for sunbathing and swimming, while the historic Skanderbeg Square is within reach for a cultural day out.

Adding to the convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures that guests have easy access to essentials without having to venture far from the comfort of their apartment.

Situated a comfortable distance from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Apartment in Front of the Beach presents an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and scenic beauty during their stay in Durrës.
