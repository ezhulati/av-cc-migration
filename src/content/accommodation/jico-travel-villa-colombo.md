The property boasts a lush garden that provides a tranquil retreat amidst the city's hustle and bustle, complemented by captivating views of both the garden and the cityscape. For those arriving by air, the convenience of airport transfers is offered, ensuring a seamless journey from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 154 km away. Additionally, the availability of a bicycle rental service on-site encourages guests to explore the surrounding area at their own pace.

Accommodations at Jico Travel - Villa Colombo are designed with guest comfort in mind. Each air-conditioned unit is well-appointed with a fully equipped kitchen, including a fridge and stovetop, alongside a dining area for a home-away-from-home experience. The presence of a kettle, private bathroom, and complimentary WiFi in every room enhances the stay, while select units also feature a terrace or boast sea views. To ensure a restful night's sleep, bed linen and towels are thoughtfully provided in each unit.

For those keen on discovering the local sights, the apartment complex facilitates sightseeing tours, making it effortless to immerse in the local culture and attractions. Additionally, a car rental service is available for guests preferring to venture further afield, offering the freedom to explore Vlorë and its surroundings at their leisure.

Situated a mere 4.9 km from Independence Square, Jico Travel - Villa Colombo serves as a perfect base for travelers seeking to experience the best of Vlorë, whether it's soaking up the sun on nearby beaches or delving into the city's rich history.
