The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living and modern conveniences, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and two bathrooms, ensuring ample space for guests traveling in groups or with family. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of quality bed linen and towels, enhancing the comfort of each stay. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, positioned in a cozy dining area that invites guests to unwind. The heart of the apartment, a fully equipped kitchen, offers the freedom to craft meals at any time of day, while the balcony presents an opportunity to soak in the serene mountain views, adding a touch of tranquility to the urban dwelling. It's important to note, the accommodation upholds a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the confines of the apartment, the property facilitates exploration with its airport transfer services and the availability of a car rental service, making travel arrangements hassle-free. The nearest gateway, Podgorica Airport, is located 59 km away, making City Centre Apartment 1 an ideal choice for travelers seeking both accessibility and a retreat in the vibrant city of Shkodër.
