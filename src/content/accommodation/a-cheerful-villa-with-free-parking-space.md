The villa boasts a generously sized apartment that is thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. Guests can enjoy the comfort of a well-appointed bedroom, unwind in front of a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, and make use of a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a toaster for those home-cooked meals. The convenience of a washing machine and the inclusion of a bathroom with a shower add to the homely feel. To ensure a comfortable stay, towels and bed linen are readily provided, and the added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy.

Situated just 1.8 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, A Cheerful Villa is perfectly positioned for guests eager to explore Tirana's rich cultural heritage. Attractions such as Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana are easily accessible, making it an ideal base for those looking to immerse themselves in the local scene. For travelers arriving by air, the convenience of being only 15 km away from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes this villa a compelling choice for both short and extended stays in Albania's vibrant capital.
