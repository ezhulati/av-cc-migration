Each air-conditioned unit within this apartment complex is thoughtfully equipped with a fully functional kitchen, empowering visitors with the option to craft their own culinary experiences. The inclusion of private bathrooms and complimentary WiFi in all units adds to the comfort, with select rooms further enhancing the stay through the addition of balconies.

Situated a mere 16 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and 96 km from Ioannina Airport, Monna Tours offers an ideal base for explorers keen to uncover the natural and historical treasures of the region. This property not only promises a stay marked by ease and accessibility but also invites guests into a world where every detail is tailored to ensure a memorable visit to Sarandë.
