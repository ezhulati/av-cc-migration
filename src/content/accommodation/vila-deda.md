Guests are treated to well-appointed apartments that boast not only balconies with captivating garden views but also terraces that open up to the serene inner courtyard. Each apartment is equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable stay regardless of the weather. The convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi adds to the seamless experience at Vila Deda.

The living spaces are thoughtfully designed with a fully equipped kitchen, including a fridge and stovetop, a dining area, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which feature walk-in showers and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

Morning delights at Vila Deda include a Full English/Irish breakfast with a variety of options such as pancakes, fruits, and juice, perfectly complemented by the on-site coffee shop. This culinary experience promises a delightful start to the day.

For those looking to explore beyond the comforts of Vila Deda, the property is conveniently located just 43 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it an accessible choice for both local and international travelers. Vila Deda stands as a testament to comfort, convenience, and tranquility, promising an unforgettable stay in Lezhë.
