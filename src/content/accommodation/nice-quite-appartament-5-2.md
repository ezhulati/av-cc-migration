Boasting a tranquil setting, this air-conditioned apartment offers a haven of comfort with its well-appointed separate bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a relaxing stay. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the accommodation proudly maintains a non-smoking policy throughout.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy the convenience of complimentary WiFi available across the premises. For those looking to explore, Skanderbeg Square is a mere 3 km away, offering a glimpse into the local lifestyle and culture. Additional nearby sights include Tanners' Bridge and the Clock Tower of Tirana, each presenting unique insights into the city's rich history.

Situated just 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Nice, Quiet Apartment 5-2 represents an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and access to Tirana's vibrant attractions.
