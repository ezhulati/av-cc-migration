Each apartment at Sheme's is a haven of relaxation, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, complimentary private parking to ease the worries of car travelers, and free WiFi to keep guests connected with the world or to plan their next adventure in Sarandë. The attention to detail is evident in the well-appointed rooms, which come equipped with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a private bathroom, ensuring a comfortable and private retreat after a day of exploration.

For those intrigued by history and nature, the Butrint National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is merely 16 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity to immerse in the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of Albania. Moreover, the convenience of Ioannina Airport being just 96 km away makes Sheme's Apartments an accessible getaway for international travelers.

Sheme's Apartments stands out as a testament to thoughtful hospitality and an ideal base for guests eager to discover the enchanting city of Sarandë and its surrounding attractions.
