Guests are welcomed into a world of comfort with air-conditioned rooms that boast not only a view of the bustling city but also come equipped with essential amenities such as a desk and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with connectivity. Each room extends an invitation to the outdoors with its own private balcony, providing a perfect nook for morning coffees or evening reflections.

The resort is enveloped in a lush garden, offering a tranquil retreat with spaces for relaxation and contemplation, complemented by the convenience of free private parking and the leisure of a sun-drenched terrace. The multilingual staff, proficient in Greek, English, and Albanian, stand ready at the reception to offer personalized guidance, ensuring every guest's needs and inquiries are met with the utmost attention and care.

For those venturing from afar, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 92 km away, makes Noa Resorts an accessible haven for international travelers seeking the perfect blend of beachside bliss and cultural exploration in Sarandë.
