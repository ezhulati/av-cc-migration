Notably, the property maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all.

Located just 2.6 km from the lively Golem Beach and 41 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, Tiara Beach Apartment serves as a perfect base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of the area. The private entrance to the apartment enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy.

Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 36 km from Tiara Beach Apartment, facilitating easy access for international travelers. This apartment not only promises a serene beachside escape but also serves as a gateway to the diverse attractions of the region, making it a top choice for discerning guests.
