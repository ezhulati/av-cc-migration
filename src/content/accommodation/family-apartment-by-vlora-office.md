The apartment boasts a generous layout, featuring a bedroom, a bathroom, and is fully stocked with bed linen and towels for guest comfort. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook. The highlight, however, is the balcony offering breathtaking sea views, perfect for dining al fresco while admiring the mountainous backdrop. The added benefit of a private entrance elevates the sense of exclusivity and privacy for guests.

Located just 2.9 km from Independence Square, the apartment offers a serene retreat with easy access to the city's main attractions. For international travelers, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making Family Apartment by Vlora Office an ideal choice for both short and long stays in Vlorë.
