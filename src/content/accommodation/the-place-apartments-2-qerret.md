Guests are welcomed into a world of modern amenities within this air-conditioned apartment, which boasts two cozy bedrooms, a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a dining area perfect for intimate meals. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, invites guests to prepare their own gourmet meals, while the living room provides a comfortable space to unwind after a day of adventure. For added convenience, towels and linens are available upon request.

The Place Apartments 2 doesn't just stop at providing a comfortable stay; it ensures a hassle-free experience with complimentary private parking and WiFi, catering to the needs of today's digital-savvy traveler. While the property maintains a non-smoking policy to ensure the comfort and well-being of all guests, it also offers proximity to notable attractions such as the Durres Amphitheatre and the House of Leaves, located 19 km and 49 km away, respectively. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 44 km from the property, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, The Place Apartments 2, Qerret, stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and culture of Golem, offering both the tranquility of a beachfront location and the convenience of modern amenities.
