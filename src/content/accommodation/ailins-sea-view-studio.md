The studio is thoughtfully designed with modern travelers in mind, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, a fully equipped kitchenette for intimate meals, and a sleek bathroom. The commitment to a non-smoking environment ensures a fresh and inviting atmosphere throughout the stay.

Beyond the comfort of the studio, guests are invited to unwind in the lush garden, a peaceful oasis that adds an extra layer of relaxation to the experience. For those looking to explore beyond the city's beaches, Butrint National Park offers a rich tapestry of history and natural beauty just 13 km away, while the ancient ruins of Fanoti beckon at a distance of 47 km, promising adventures steeped in antiquity.

With the convenience of free private parking and the luxury of complimentary WiFi, Ailin's Sea View Studio stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of serenity, comfort, and accessibility. The nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is located 97 km away, making the studio a convenient gateway for both domestic and international visitors.
