The hotel prides itself on offering seamless service around the clock, with a 24-hour front desk ensuring all needs are met promptly, day or night. Complimentary WiFi throughout the property allows guests to stay connected, whether for leisure or business purposes. Select accommodations further elevate the experience, featuring balconies that present captivating city views, adding a touch of urban scenery to the tranquil ambiance.

For those eager to explore, Hotel Citrus serves as an ideal base. A short stroll leads to the vibrant Ksamil Beach, while Coco Beach and Bora Bora Beach are also within easy reach, offering diverse beach experiences. For international travelers, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 94 km away, underscores the hotel's appeal as a gateway to the region's natural beauty and cultural attractions.

In summary, Hotel Citrus stands out as a top-tier choice for visitors to Ksamil, combining strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and attentive service to create memorable stays.
