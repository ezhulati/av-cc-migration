The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort. It boasts a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring modern appliances such as a dishwasher and an oven. The inclusion of air conditioning adds to the comfort, making it a cozy retreat after a day under the sun. The highlight, however, is the terrace that offers breathtaking sea views, allowing guests to soak in the beauty of the Ionian Sea from the comfort of their accommodation. For those who appreciate the allure of nature, the apartment also provides captivating mountain views, adding to the overall tranquility of the stay.

Practical amenities have not been overlooked. Guests benefit from complimentary private parking and free WiFi, ensuring both convenience and connectivity throughout their stay. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further enhances the appeal for those seeking a clean and healthy living space.

For explorers eager to delve into the local culture and natural beauty, Violet Sea View Apartment serves as an ideal base. It is conveniently located just a short drive from the enchanting Butrint National Park and within proximity to Maestral Beach. Moreover, for guests arriving from afar, the property offers a paid airport shuttle service from Ioannina Airport, located 98 km away, adding a layer of ease to the travel experience.

In summary, Violet Sea View Apartment stands as a beacon of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it a highly recommended choice for those planning a visit to Sarandë.
