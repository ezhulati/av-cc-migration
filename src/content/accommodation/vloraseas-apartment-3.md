Boasting a spacious layout, the apartment is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that is as relaxing as it is connected. The one-bedroom configuration, complete with a full bathroom, bed linen, and towels, caters to both solo adventurers and couples looking for a serene getaway. The fully equipped kitchen and dining area invite guests to prepare and enjoy their meals with a view, thanks to the apartment's balcony that offers breathtaking sea vistas and an additional outdoor dining area that presents a picturesque backdrop of the city.

For those keen on exploring the local culture and landmarks, VloraSeas Apartment 3 serves as an ideal base. The historic Kuzum Baba and the vibrant Independence Square are just a short drive away, offering a glimpse into the rich heritage and lively spirit of Vlorë.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 152 km from the apartment, making it a convenient option for international travelers. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a tranquil retreat amidst the bustling city life, VloraSeas Apartment 3 stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for visitors to Vlorë.
