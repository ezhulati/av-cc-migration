The apartment itself is a haven of spaciousness and modern amenities. Guests are treated to a beautifully appointed living space that includes a bedroom with sweeping sea views, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen boasting a dishwasher and an oven. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and air conditioning ensures a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. The bathroom, featuring a walk-in shower, along with a bidet and a dressing room, underscores the apartment's commitment to luxury and convenience. Notably, the property is an allergy-free and non-smoking environment, catering to the well-being of all guests.

For those who relish the outdoors, Eli's Seafront Boutique Apartment does not disappoint. An on-site coffee shop provides the perfect start to a day of exploration, while the availability of barbecue facilities invites guests to enjoy leisurely evenings under the stars. The adventurous at heart will appreciate the bike and car hire services, opening up a world of activities from canoeing to hiking. The sun terrace, offering a tranquil spot to unwind, adds to the apartment's allure.

Situated a brief journey from La Petite Beach and the historic Butrint National Park, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural treasures of the region. With Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, guests find both seclusion and accessibility.

Eli's Seafront Boutique Apartment stands out as a sanctuary for those desiring a memorable stay in Sarandë, offering a unique blend of natural beauty, comfort, and adventure.
