The apartment within Pine Trees Veranda is thoughtfully appointed with two bedrooms, ensuring ample space for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen is fully equipped, boasting an oven, fridge, washing machine, stovetop, and kitchenware, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, an outdoor dining area enhances the experience, allowing guests to enjoy their meals amidst the refreshing sea breeze.

For those eager to explore the local beaches, Golem Beach is conveniently located less than 1 km away, offering a picturesque setting for sunbathing and leisurely strolls. Qerret Beach, known for its serene ambiance, is just an 18-minute walk from the property. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 41 km away, making Pine Trees Veranda an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors seeking a peaceful getaway by the sea.
