This apartment doesn't just boast an ideal location; it's a haven of comfort and style. Guests will find themselves in a beautifully appointed space featuring one bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complemented by free WiFi that extends throughout the property, ensuring that guests stay connected and entertained throughout their visit.

For those traveling to Vlorë from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making the apartment a convenient choice for international and domestic travelers alike. Whether it's the allure of Vlorë's beaches or the rich history of its squares, this apartment serves as the perfect base for explorations and unforgettable memories.
