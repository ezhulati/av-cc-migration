The establishment prides itself on offering air-conditioned units, a rarity within a mere 600 meters from the enchanting La Petite Beach. Guests are welcomed around the clock with a 24-hour front desk, ensuring assistance is always on hand, whether it's for a late-night check-in or early morning tour organization. The convenience of an elevator adds to the seamless experience, catering to all needs and preferences.

For those who appreciate the finer details, select units boast a cable flat-screen TV, alongside a fully equipped kitchen featuring a fridge, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with a shower and hair dryer, adds an extra layer of comfort to the stay.

A highlight for many is the on-site bar, offering a perfect setting to unwind after a day of exploration. Situated 800 meters from the serene Maestral Beach and a short drive from the historic Butrint National Park, Joanna Suites positions itself as an ideal base for both relaxation and adventure. With Ioannina Airport located 96 km away, the journey to this exquisite retreat is a straightforward affair, promising an unforgettable stay in one of Sarandë's most sought-after accommodations.
