This one-bedroom apartment doesn't just boast a prime location; it offers the comfort of modern living with air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and a flat-screen TV. The inclusion of a washing machine and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge ensures that guests have all the conveniences of home at their fingertips. Moreover, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of hotel-like luxury to this non-smoking accommodation.

For those eager to delve into the local scene, the apartment is proximate to several of Tirana's attractions, including the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the quaint Tanners' Bridge, and the bustling Toptani Shopping Centre. Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, making 23° Bs Pazari City Center Comfort an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital. Private parking is available, ensuring that those arriving by car are well-accommodated.
