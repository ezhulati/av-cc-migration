Guests are welcomed into a generously sized apartment that boasts two well-appointed bedrooms and a bathroom, complete with all the necessary linens and towels. The living space is designed for comfort, featuring a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a dining area for communal meals, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony, offering breathtaking sea views that promise to enchant guests from dawn till dusk.

Adding to the appeal is the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking, ensuring that every aspect of the stay is hassle-free. The apartment also benefits from a private entrance, offering an extra layer of privacy for those who value seclusion and tranquility.

For those intrigued by the allure of ancient civilizations, the mysterious Ancient Fanoti lies within a reachable distance, beckoning history enthusiasts to uncover its secrets. Moreover, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 96 km away, provides ease of access for international travelers, making Meltin Apartments a compelling choice for both leisure and exploration in the heart of Sarandë.
