The accommodations at Doni Apartments are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air-conditioned units that come with their own private entrance, ensuring a blend of privacy and comfort. Each apartment boasts a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain views, setting the stage for serene mornings or tranquil evenings. Inside, guests are treated to a suite of amenities including a cable flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a well-equipped kitchen, making it a home away from home. The private bathrooms are fitted with walk-in showers and hair dryers, and the provision of bed linen and towels adds a touch of convenience to the stay.

For those who appreciate the start of their day with a delightful meal, Doni Apartments does not disappoint. The continental breakfast selection includes local specialties, fresh fruits, and juices, ensuring a flavorful beginning to the day. Additionally, an on-site bar caters to guests looking to unwind with a drink in hand.

The location of Doni Apartments is not just about beach proximity; it is also a gateway to exploring the area's attractions. Lori Beach is a mere 600 meters away, offering another spot for beach enthusiasts. For those intrigued by history and nature, Butrint National Park is just 2.9 km away, promising a day of adventure and discovery. Moreover, for international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 92 km away, making Doni Apartments a convenient base for both local and international visitors.

In essence, Doni Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering not just a place to stay, but a place to experience the best of Ksamil in comfort and style.
