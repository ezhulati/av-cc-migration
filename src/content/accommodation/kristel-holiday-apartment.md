Boasting a tranquil setting, the apartment is a mere 500 meters from the picturesque Vjetër Beach, offering guests a unique blend of convenience and scenic beauty. The inclusion of complimentary private parking and WiFi enhances the stay, ensuring a seamless and connected experience throughout.

The accommodation itself is a testament to comfort and elegance. Guests are welcomed into a space bathed in natural light, leading out onto a terrace that offers serene garden views-a perfect backdrop for morning coffees or evening relaxation. The air-conditioned apartment features a spacious bedroom and a fully equipped kitchen, catering to all the needs of modern travelers. Entertainment is readily available with the provision of a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal setting for both leisure and relaxation.

For those venturing from afar, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 151 km away, underscores the apartment's appeal to international visitors, offering a straightforward journey to this tranquil haven.

Kristel Holiday Apartment stands out as a distinguished option for those in pursuit of a peaceful getaway, combined with the ease of exploring Vlorë's enchanting surroundings.
