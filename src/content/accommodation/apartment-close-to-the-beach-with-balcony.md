The apartment itself is a haven of tranquility, featuring air-conditioning for those warm summer days, a separate bedroom for privacy, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures that entertainment is never far away. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the property maintains a strict non-smoking policy throughout.

Strategically located, the apartment is not only close to the beach but also offers easy access to notable attractions. It is situated conveniently near the Rock of Kavaje, only 6.1 km away, and the historical Durres Amphitheatre, which is 6.9 km from the property. For those interested in exploring further, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the House of Leaves are within a manageable distance, offering a glimpse into the country's rich history.

For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest, located 33 km away, making the Apartment Close to the Beach with Balcony an ideal choice for both convenience and a peaceful getaway. With free WiFi available throughout the property, guests can stay connected while enjoying the tranquility and beauty of their surroundings.
