The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring two separate bedrooms and two bathrooms, making it an ideal retreat for families or groups. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, invites guests to prepare and enjoy meals together, with the convenience of a microwave for quick snacks or meals. The living room, anchored by a flat-screen TV, provides a cozy gathering space for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

For those with a taste for the finer things, the apartment offers the added touch of wine or champagne, ensuring that evenings can be celebrated with a touch of luxury. Beyond the confines of this inviting apartment, Maestral Beach awaits just 2 km away, offering a picturesque spot for sunbathing or swimming. Further afield, the historical allure of Butrint National Park, located 17 km from the apartment, offers a fascinating day trip for those keen to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of the region's past.

Situated 97 km from Ioannina Airport, Valentina Apartment 1 represents not just a place to stay, but a gateway to the myriad of experiences that Sarandë has to offer, making it a compelling choice for discerning travelers looking for a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury.
