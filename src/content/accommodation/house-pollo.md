The accommodations at House Pollo are thoughtfully designed with air conditioning to ensure a comfortable respite from the warm Mediterranean climate. The option of rooms featuring either a terrace or balcony presents guests with delightful views of a well-tended garden or a peaceful street, setting the stage for a tranquil getaway.

Each room is equipped with a private bathroom, catering to the needs of families and solo travelers alike. The presence of a shared lounge and garden access invites guests to unwind in a communal setting, fostering a sense of camaraderie among visitors.

A short stroll away, Lori Beach awaits, offering another picturesque spot for relaxation and sunbathing, while the historical allure of Butrint National Park is just 3 km away, promising a blend of natural beauty and cultural exploration. For those arriving from afar, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km from the guest house, making House Pollo an accessible haven for international and local travelers seeking a peaceful escape by the Albanian Riviera.
