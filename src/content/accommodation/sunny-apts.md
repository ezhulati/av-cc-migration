Each apartment at Sunny Apts is designed to be a home away from home, featuring a balcony that offers sweeping sea views, a cozy seating area for unwinding, and a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, fridge, and essential kitchenware, invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like. The inclusion of a kettle adds a touch of comfort, making it easy to enjoy a warm cup of tea or coffee at any time. Private bathrooms equipped with either a bath or shower, along with complimentary slippers, ensure privacy and convenience. For added ease, each unit is supplied with fresh bed linen and towels.

For those who need to pick up essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises caters to guests' needs without the need to venture far. Sunny Apts is not just about the comfort of its rooms; its location is equally impressive. Maestral Beach is a mere 700 meters away, offering another spot for sunbathing and swimming. For nature and history enthusiasts, the Butrint National Park, located 14 km from the property, presents an opportunity to explore Albania's rich heritage and natural beauty. Furthermore, the property is conveniently situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, making it accessible for international travelers.

Sunny Apts stands out as an ideal choice for families and travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the opportunity to explore the enchanting surroundings of Sarandë.
