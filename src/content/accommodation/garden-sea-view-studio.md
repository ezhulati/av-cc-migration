This air-conditioned haven boasts a thoughtfully designed space that includes a bedroom equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests are treated to a seamless blend of functionality and comfort, with a flat-screen TV, washing machine, and a kitchen outfitted with a fridge and stovetop at their disposal. The highlight, however, is the private balcony that offers breathtaking sea views, complemented by outdoor furniture for guests to unwind and soak in the serene surroundings.

Privacy and peace are paramount at Garden Sea View Studio, underscored by soundproofing and a private entrance that guarantees an undisturbed escape. The addition of complimentary WiFi allows guests to stay connected while embracing the tranquility of their surroundings.

Situated within easy reach of the enchanting Heaven Beach and the historical Butrint National Park, this studio serves as a perfect base for explorers keen to delve into the natural and cultural riches of the area. With Ioannina Airport located 98 km away, Garden Sea View Studio stands as a beacon of comfort and convenience for both domestic and international travelers, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Sarandë.
