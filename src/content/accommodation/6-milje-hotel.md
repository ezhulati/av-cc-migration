The hotel prides itself on providing modern conveniences in each room, including free WiFi to keep guests connected and a bar where they can unwind with a drink in hand. Select accommodations feature balconies that present breathtaking sea views, allowing guests to soak in the serene beauty of the surroundings from the comfort of their rooms.

Every guest room is thoughtfully equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a comfortable and productive stay. The private bathrooms come with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, adding a touch of luxury to the daily routine. For added convenience, rooms are also furnished with fresh bed linen and towels.

Morning risers are greeted with a choice of a buffet or continental breakfast, setting the perfect tone for the day ahead. The hotel's location makes it an ideal base for exploring Ksamil and engaging in a variety of activities such as hiking, fishing, and snorkeling, catering to the adventurous spirit of every traveler.

The multilingual staff at the reception, fluent in Greek, English, Spanish, and French, are always available to assist guests, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable stay. Nearby attractions include the vibrant Bora Bora Beach and the scenic Ksamil Beach 7 and 9, making it easy for guests to experience the best of the area.

Situated a mere 93 km from Corfu International Airport, 6 Milje Hotel serves as a perfect retreat for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and adventure in the heart of Ksamil.
