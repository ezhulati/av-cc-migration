Accessibility is a breeze with the inclusion of an elevator and the thoughtful provision of private check-in and check-out services, catering to the needs of every guest. The interior of Appartamento Nr. 27 is meticulously designed to cater to both aesthetic and functional needs. It houses a single bedroom, a bathroom equipped with essentials such as bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV and a dining space. The fully equipped kitchen allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, while the terrace offers breathtaking mountain views, perfect for unwinding.

One of the apartment's unique features is its private entrance, which welcomes guests into a space where they can relax with a glass of wine or champagne, adding a touch of luxury to their stay. Moreover, the apartment is designed to be allergy-free and soundproof, ensuring a comfortable and uninterrupted experience.

For those traveling by air, Podgorica Airport is conveniently located 58 km away, making Appartamento Nr. 27. Centrale e luminoso. an accessible and attractive option for both short and long-term stays.
