The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a balcony that offers breathtaking mountain views. It features a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. The inclusion of a modern bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of convenience to the stay.

Connectivity is seamless, with free WiFi enveloping the property, ensuring guests stay connected with the world. Notably, Skanderbeg Square, a hub of cultural and social activity, is a mere 600 metres away, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local culture.

For those keen on exploring the cultural landmarks, the proximity to Clock Tower Tirana, Et'hem Bey Mosque, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana offers a cultural feast just a short walk away. Moreover, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 15 km from CozyFlat, ensures smooth travel arrangements for international guests.

In essence, CozyFlat stands out as an ideal lodging for those eager to dive into the heart of Tirana, offering both the serenity of mountain views and the convenience of city living.
