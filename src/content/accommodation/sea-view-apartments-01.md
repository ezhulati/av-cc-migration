This apartment is a haven of tranquility, ensuring a smoke-free environment complemented by the luxury of free private parking. Its proximity to Coco Bongo Beach, just 1.5 km away, adds to its appeal for beach lovers and those seeking leisurely strolls along the shore.

Designed with the modern traveler in mind, the apartment features a suite of amenities including complimentary WiFi, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine for convenience. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a fridge, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, making it an ideal choice for both short and extended stays.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the apartment's closeness to significant landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, located 7.3 km and 7.5 km away, respectively. These sites offer a glimpse into the rich history and vibrant culture of Vlorë.

For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 156 km from the property, making Sea View Apartments 01 a compelling choice for international visitors seeking a seamless travel experience to Vlorë.
