The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful living spaces, boasting a bedroom equipped with a flat-screen TV that offers a variety of streaming services for entertainment at any time. The presence of a fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and a toaster, alongside a washing machine, caters to the needs of guests preferring the comforts of home-cooked meals and the convenience of in-house laundry. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a shower, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the apartment's appeal. Notably, the accommodation guarantees privacy with its exclusive entrance.

For those eager to explore Tirana's cultural landscape, Kristi Apartments 02 is ideally positioned near the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, and the historic Tanners' Bridge. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, merely 14 km away, coupled with available services such as airport transfers and car rental options, facilitates effortless travel planning and exploration of the surrounding area.

Kristi Apartments 02 represents a compelling choice for visitors to Tirana, offering a harmonious blend of modern amenities, privacy, and a strategic location to enrich the travel experience.
