Accommodations at Handmade Stone Villa are designed with comfort and relaxation in mind. Each room extends into a private balcony, presenting an idyllic outdoor dining area with serene garden views, perfect for unwinding. The interiors are thoughtfully furnished, including a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and kitchenware, catering to all culinary needs. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with modern fixtures and showers, ensuring a refreshing experience. Additionally, ample storage is provided with the inclusion of wardrobes in all units.

The villa's terrace is a highlight, offering breathtaking mountain vistas that promise a tranquil retreat. Guests are invited to rejuvenate in the lush garden, a peaceful haven for relaxation.

Strategically located, Handmade Stone Villa is just a short stroll from the vibrant Ksamil Beach 9 and the tranquil Lori Beach, making it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. For those looking to explore beyond, Corfu International Airport is situated 92 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements.

Handmade Stone Villa stands out as a perfect blend of traditional elegance and contemporary comfort, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Ksamil.
