The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, a flat-screen TV with cable channels for entertainment, and a dining area for intimate meals. The kitchen comes fully equipped, including an oven, catering to guests' every culinary need. The inclusion of a cozy living room, complete with plush furnishings, ensures a home-away-from-home experience. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided.

One of the highlights of Apartment Barden is its outdoor dining area, allowing guests to enjoy their meals al fresco against the backdrop of the stunning surroundings. Despite being a non-smoking property, it ensures all guests enjoy a clean and fresh environment.

Situated just a short drive from notable landmarks such as Independence Square and Kuzum Baba, visitors have the opportunity to explore the rich history and culture of the area. For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 151 km away, making Apartment Barden an accessible retreat for both local and international guests.

In summary, Apartment Barden stands out as an idyllic destination for those in search of a peaceful beachfront getaway, combined with the comforts of modern living and the allure of Vlorë's natural and historical treasures.
