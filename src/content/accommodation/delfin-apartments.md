Each apartment within this complex is thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV, ensuring entertainment is always within reach. Privacy and security are paramount, with a private bathroom and safety deposit box standard across all units. For guests who desire a bit more, select apartments offer the luxury of a balcony and the serene beauty of sea views. Connectivity is never an issue, as complimentary WiFi keeps guests linked to the world beyond.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of bed linen and towels in each unit, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. For those looking to explore, La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach are conveniently located within 1.3 km and 1.5 km respectively, promising delightful beach days just a stone's throw away. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the premises.

Delfin Apartments stands out as a stellar accommodation choice, offering a harmonious blend of essential amenities and strategic location for an unforgettable Sarandë experience.
