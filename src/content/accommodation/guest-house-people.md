Accommodations at Guest House People are thoughtfully designed to cater to a variety of needs and preferences. Select rooms boast the luxury of a private entrance, alongside practical amenities such as a desk and wardrobe, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers. The allure of a balcony with a garden view, coupled with the comfort of air conditioning and entertainment options like a cable flat-screen TV, adds to the appeal. For those requiring additional space, certain units also offer the convenience of a dressing room.

Adding to the charm of this guest house is an on-site coffee shop and bar, providing a cozy spot for guests to unwind with a drink or a light snack. Families traveling with little ones will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a baby safety gate, ensuring a worry-free stay for parents.

Situated 70 km from Kastoria National Airport, Guest House People offers a blend of accessibility and tranquility, making it an attractive option for travelers exploring the enchanting city of Korçë and its surroundings.
