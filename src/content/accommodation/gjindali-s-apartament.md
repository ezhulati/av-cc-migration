A mere 1.7 km from the tranquil Baro Beach and within easy reach of the historic Kuzum Baba at 14 km, the apartment is perfectly positioned for guests looking to explore the natural and cultural highlights of Vlorë. Independence Square, a significant landmark, is also accessible, located 15 km from the accommodation.

The living space is thoughtfully designed with guests' comfort in mind, comprising a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for those home-cooked meals, and a bathroom that caters to all your needs. Entertainment is guaranteed with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Moreover, the apartment offers delightful garden views, adding a touch of tranquility to your stay.

For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 164 km away, making Gjindali's Apartment a convenient base for both short and extended stays in the enchanting city of Vlorë.
