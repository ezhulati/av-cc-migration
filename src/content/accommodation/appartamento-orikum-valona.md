The apartment itself is a haven of space and comfort, featuring a generous terrace that invites guests to unwind in the open air. Inside, the layout includes two cozy bedrooms, a convivial living room, and a kitchen equipped with modern conveniences such as a microwave and stovetop, catering to guests' culinary needs. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that leisure time is well spent.

For those interested in exploring the local culture and landmarks, Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are both located 18 km from the apartment, making for easy day trips. Additionally, for guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 167 km from the property, making Appartamento Orikum Valona an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers. This apartment stands out as a compelling choice for those in search of a peaceful escape combined with the convenience of home comforts and the proximity to beautiful beaches and local attractions.
