Each apartment at Siloria is a testament to thoughtful design, featuring balconies that present breathtaking mountain vistas. Guests can expect modern comforts within each unit, including satellite flat-screen TVs, kitchens equipped with all the essentials, and private bathrooms complete with bidets and hair dryers. The attention to detail extends to the provision of bed linen and towels in all units, underscoring the complex's commitment to guest comfort.

For those looking to explore the area at their own pace, Siloria Apartments offers a car rental service, adding a layer of convenience to the adventure-seeking traveler's stay. The property's location is ideal for those eager to explore, with the serene Beach at Government Villas and the historic Kuzum Baba within easy reach. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 154 km away, making Siloria Apartments a compelling choice for both local and international visitors seeking a tranquil retreat by the sea in Vlorë.
