Each room at Hotel SEADEL is thoughtfully designed to maximize comfort and convenience. Guests are treated to air-conditioned spaces that boast not only a desk and a private balcony with captivating city views but also a private bathroom equipped with modern amenities. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, fresh bed linen, and towels, along with a handy fridge, underscores the hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction.

Strategically positioned, Hotel SEADEL is just a short stroll away from the vibrant Bora Bora Beach and the tranquil Ksamil Beach 9, both approximately 700 meters away, offering guests easy access to some of the area's most beautiful coastlines. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is situated 93 km away, making Hotel SEADEL an ideal choice for travelers seeking both convenience and a touch of luxury.
