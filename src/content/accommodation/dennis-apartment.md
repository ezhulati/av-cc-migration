Strategically located, Dennis Apartment is a mere 43 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, making it an ideal base for those looking to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the area. Adventure seekers will appreciate the proximity to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 47 km away, offering breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience. For those intrigued by the natural beauty of the region, the Rock of Kavaje is just 1.7 km away, with Mali I Robit Beach offering another picturesque beach option at 2.7 km distance.

The apartment itself is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring three cozy bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchenette for those home-cooked meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have access to their favorite shows or movies during their stay.

For history enthusiasts, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is accessible at 44 km, offering a glimpse into the country's past, while the Durres Amphitheatre, 13 km away, stands as a testament to the architectural prowess of ancient civilizations. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, located 38 km from the property, facilitating easy arrivals and departures.

Dennis Apartment not only promises a stay filled with comfort and convenience but also serves as a gateway to exploring the enchanting surroundings of Golem and beyond.
