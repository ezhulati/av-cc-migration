The apartment itself presents a well-appointed living space that includes a bedroom, a living room complete with a flat-screen TV, and a terrace that offers captivating city views. The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven and a fridge, catering to guests' culinary needs, while the bathroom provides a relaxing bath experience. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available in the apartment.

For those needing to pick up essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises adds to the convenience factor. Positioned just 400 meters from Saranda City Beach, the apartment offers easy access to one of the area's most beautiful beaches. Further enhancing its appeal, La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach are also within close proximity, less than 1 km away, making it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts.

Travelers looking to explore beyond the local area will find Ioannina Airport 95 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements for international guests. Luka Apartment in Sarande represents an excellent choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and access to Sarandë's beautiful coastal attractions.
