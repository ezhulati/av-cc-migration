Connectivity is a breeze at Hotel De Luna, thanks to the complimentary WiFi available throughout the property. For those who value convenience, the hotel's 24-hour front desk and room service ensure needs are met at any hour. Moreover, the allergy-free environment promises a comfortable stay for all guests.

Accommodations at Hotel De Luna are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of modernity. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with bed linen and towels, adds to the convenience. Guests will appreciate the added luxury of a balcony, offering city views that bring the vibrancy of Ksamil to your doorstep. Select rooms elevate the experience further with a kitchen, complete with an oven and stovetop, not to mention a fridge for added convenience.

Mornings at Hotel De Luna are greeted with a continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. For those looking to explore the area, bike and car hire services are readily available, making it easy to venture into the popular hiking and fishing spots nearby.

The hotel's proximity to notable attractions, including Lori Beach just 500 meters away and the historic Butrint National Park within 2.9 km, positions it as an ideal base for discovering the natural and cultural treasures of the region. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is accessible, located 93 km from the hotel, ensuring a smooth journey to and from this exceptional destination.

In summary, Hotel De Luna offers a harmonious blend of location, amenities, and service, making it a compelling choice for those visiting Ksamil.
