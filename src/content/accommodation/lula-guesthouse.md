Guests can enjoy the modern amenities of a cozy 1-bedroom apartment, which is equipped with a flat-screen TV, washing machine, and a kitchenette that boasts a dishwasher and toaster, all complemented by complimentary WiFi. This makes for an ideal setting for travelers seeking a blend of home comfort and the ease of digital connectivity.

Situated 82 km from Ioannina Airport, Lula Guesthouse offers a peaceful base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Gjirokastër and its surroundings, ensuring a memorable stay for those who venture into this captivating part of the world.
