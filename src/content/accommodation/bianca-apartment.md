This air-conditioned apartment offers ample space for relaxation and entertainment, boasting a balcony that provides a peaceful outdoor retreat. With two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a well-equipped kitchen complete with an oven, guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures that entertainment options are always at hand.

For those interested in delving into the local culture and history, the Durres Amphitheatre is located just 19 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich past. Additionally, the House of Leaves museum, situated 49 km from the apartment, presents an intriguing exploration of historical narratives. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 44 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements for guests.

With the added benefits of private parking on site and complimentary WiFi, BIANCA Apartment stands out as a top selection for travelers looking for a blend of leisure, culture, and convenience in their accommodation choice.
