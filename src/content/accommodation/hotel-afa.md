Each room at Hotel Afa is a haven of relaxation, featuring air conditioning, a private bathroom, and complimentary WiFi to ensure a comfortable stay. For those who need to stay connected or get some work done, a desk and a flat-screen TV are standard inclusions. Select accommodations boast city views and come with a balcony, offering a perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploration. Convenience is further enhanced with the inclusion of a fridge in every room.

Guests can start their day on the right note with a delightful continental breakfast served daily, providing the energy needed for a day of adventure or relaxation.

The hotel's reception is always ready to assist with local information, ensuring guests make the most of their visit to the area.

Located merely 500 meters from Coco Beach and 600 meters from Ksamil Beach 7, Hotel Afa's location allows easy access to some of the area's most beautiful beaches. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Hotel Afa a convenient base for travelers.
