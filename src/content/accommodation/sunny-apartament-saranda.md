This air-conditioned apartment doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a home away from home. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a spacious terrace that boasts stunning mountain views, making it an ideal spot for morning coffees or evening relaxation. The apartment features a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for unwinding, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment needs. The kitchenette is equipped with modern amenities including an oven and a microwave, catering to guests' culinary desires. The convenience of an in-apartment bathroom with a shower, complemented by fresh towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot to relax and socialize. Adventure seekers and nature lovers will appreciate the proximity to Maestral Beach and the historic Butrint National Park, promising days filled with exploration and discovery.

Located 97 km from Ioannina Airport, Sunny Apartament Saranda is not just an accommodation choice but a gateway to experiencing the enchanting city of Sarandë in comfort and style.
