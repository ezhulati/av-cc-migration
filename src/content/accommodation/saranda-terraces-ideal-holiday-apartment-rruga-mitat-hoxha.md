The apartment itself is a haven of space and comfort, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a living area that is both functional and inviting. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, ensuring that guests have access to their favorite shows even while away from home. The kitchen does not disappoint, being fully equipped with modern appliances including an oven, microwave, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience to the overall experience.

For those intrigued by the natural beauty and historical significance of the area, Butrint National Park is within a manageable distance, located 18 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity. Furthermore, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 98 km away, facilitates relatively easy access for international travelers.

SARANDA TERRACES Ideal Holiday Apartment Rruga Mitat Hoxha is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the vibrant life and beauty of Sarandë, making it an excellent choice for those looking to explore this enchanting part of Albania.
