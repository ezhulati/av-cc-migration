This air-conditioned haven, located merely 100 meters from Sarande Main Beach and within easy reach of Saranda Bay Beach, ensures a stay that combines relaxation with the allure of the Albanian coast. The proximity to Butrint National Park, 18 km away, adds a touch of adventure for nature enthusiasts and history buffs alike.

Guests are treated to a spacious layout in the two-bedroom apartment, which is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, fridge, and washing machine. The addition of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy and exclusivity.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the convenience of an on-site bar elevates the experience, providing a perfect setting to unwind after a day of exploration.

Situated 98 km from Ioannina Airport, Blue Sea Apartment offers a blend of accessibility and tranquility, making it an ideal spot for both short getaways and extended stays. This property promises a memorable stay in Sarandë, where comfort meets convenience in a stunning coastal backdrop.
