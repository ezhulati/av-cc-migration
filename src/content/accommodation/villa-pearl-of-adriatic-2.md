The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a living space adorned with a flat-screen TV, satellite channels, and air conditioning for utmost comfort. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen, invites guests to prepare their meals with ease, featuring a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, toaster, and fridge. Meals can be savored in the outdoor dining area, offering garden views that add a touch of nature to every dining experience. The addition of a private entrance and soundproofing ensures an undisturbed stay, making it an ideal retreat for those seeking privacy and peace.

For those looking to venture beyond the comforts of Villa Pearl of Adriatic 2, Skanderbeg Square is located 49 km away, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, just 8 km from the property, presents an opportunity for outdoor enthusiasts to explore. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 44 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements for guests.

In summary, Villa Pearl of Adriatic 2 stands out as a distinguished choice for travelers aiming to immerse themselves in the beauty of Golem, offering a perfect mix of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty.
