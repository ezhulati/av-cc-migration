Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the convenience of free private parking and the assurance of full-day security, making for a worry-free experience from the get-go. The property also boasts a seamless connection to the digital world with complimentary WiFi access throughout.

Elevating the standard of city accommodations, the apartment features a spacious layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is never far away. The two bathrooms, complete with a bidet and shower, are stocked with fresh bed linen and towels, adding a touch of home-like comfort. For added convenience, a laundry service is readily available.

The apartment's terrace presents a serene spot to take in the breathtaking mountain views, offering a peaceful retreat amidst the city's hustle and bustle. For those moments of relaxation, the on-site lounge provides a cozy haven.

Practical needs are also met with a minimarket located within the premises, ensuring essentials are always within reach.

Situated 58 km from Podgorica Airport, "New Apartment in the City Center" promises an accessible and hassle-free stay for both leisure and business travelers looking to explore Shkodër and its surroundings.
