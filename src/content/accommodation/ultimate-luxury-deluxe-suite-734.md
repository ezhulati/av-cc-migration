This suite is a haven of modern amenities and comfort. Guests are greeted with a spacious, air-conditioned apartment that boasts a tastefully furnished bedroom, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and kettle for those home-cooked meals. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet and hairdryer, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the property's attention to detail and commitment to guest comfort.

For those who might need to pick up a quick snack or essential items, the convenience of an on-site minimarket adds an extra layer of ease to the stay. While the allure of the nearby beaches is undeniable, guests also have the opportunity to explore further afield with the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car located 47 km away, offering breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience.

Situated 37 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Ultimate Luxury Deluxe Suite 734 simplifies travel logistics with its offered paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless arrival and departure experience.
