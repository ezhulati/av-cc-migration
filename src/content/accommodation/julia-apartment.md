A mere 43 kilometers from the thrill of the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and 39 kilometers from the historic heart of Skanderbeg Square, Julia Apartment positions itself as a gateway to exploring the rich tapestry of the area's attractions. The accommodation boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a kitchen equipped with an oven and microwave for those who enjoy a home-cooked meal, and a bathroom featuring a bidet, hair dryer, and washing machine for utmost convenience. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of towels and bed linen, adding a touch of home comfort to the experience.

Views of the serene inner courtyard offer a peaceful retreat from the day's adventures. For those looking to delve deeper into the local culture, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 4.7 kilometers away, while the historical significance of the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located 39 kilometers from the doorstep. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 33 kilometers away, Julia Apartment ensures a seamless journey for international and domestic travelers alike, making it an exceptional choice for those seeking to uncover the charms of Durrës.
