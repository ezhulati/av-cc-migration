With its own bar, the hotel stands out as a convenient spot for guests to unwind after a day of sun-soaked adventures. The allure of IONIAN HOTEL is further enhanced by its outdoor swimming pool, where guests can enjoy a refreshing dip, and the availability of room service and complimentary WiFi across the premises, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with connectivity.

For those intrigued by cultural exploration, the historic Butrint National Park is located just 3 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. Additionally, for international travelers, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service to Corfu International Airport, located 93 km away, makes IONIAN HOTEL an accessible haven of comfort and hospitality in the heart of Ksamil.
