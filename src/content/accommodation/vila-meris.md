The accommodation boasts a thoughtfully designed space that includes three cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that features both a dishwasher and an oven for those who love to cook. The villa's two bathrooms come complete with a bidet, adding a touch of convenience and luxury. One of the villa's highlights is its balcony, offering breathtaking garden views that promise a tranquil backdrop for morning coffees or evening relaxation. For those chillier evenings, the fireplace becomes a focal point, offering warmth and a cozy atmosphere. Additionally, the outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy meals al fresco while soaking in the serene surroundings.

Privacy is paramount at Vila MeriS, with a private entrance ensuring guests feel truly at home. The convenience extends to complimentary WiFi and on-site parking, making stays hassle-free for those who come by car.

Situated 41 km from the Rock of Kavaje and 44 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Vila MeriS serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich history and natural beauty of the area. For travelers flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 29 km away, making Vila MeriS an accessible haven for both local and international guests seeking a peaceful escape with the comforts of home.
