Guests at Da Luz Boutique Hotel are treated to air-conditioned rooms that boast not only a sleek design but also practical features such as a wardrobe, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private balcony. The en-suite bathrooms, equipped with either a bath or shower, add an extra layer of privacy and convenience. For those who enjoy leisurely mornings or quiet evenings, each room also includes a cozy seating area.

The hotel doesn't stop at comfortable accommodations. An American breakfast is served daily, providing a perfect start to the day. The on-site restaurant and bar offer a delightful dining experience without the need to venture far. For relaxation, the shared lounge is a welcoming space for guests to unwind.

Entertainment is never far away, with the hotel's entertainment staff ready to ensure guests have a memorable stay. Additionally, the convenience of room service means that guests can enjoy the hotel's offerings at any time.

Located just 1.2 km from Saranda City Beach and a short drive from the enchanting Butrint National Park, Da Luz Boutique Hotel is ideally situated for guests looking to explore the natural beauty and historical sites of the area. La Petite Beach, another nearby attraction, is easily accessible at just 2.1 km away. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is 98 km from the hotel, making it a manageable gateway for international visitors.

In summary, Da Luz Boutique Hotel provides a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and service, making it a top recommendation for travelers to Sarandë.
