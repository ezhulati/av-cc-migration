The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a living space that is both functional and inviting. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen does not disappoint, being fully equipped with an oven, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals.

One of the highlights of Apartment x4 Lungomare is undoubtedly its balcony, offering breathtaking sea views and furnished for outdoor enjoyment. The property also features views of an inner courtyard, adding a touch of tranquility to the overall experience.

Situated a mere 2.4 km from Vjetër Beach, and in close proximity to notable landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, guests are perfectly positioned to delve into the rich cultural tapestry of the area. For those traveling from afar, the convenience of being 152 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal.

Apartment x4 Lungomare stands as a testament to the allure of Vlorë, offering a stay that promises not just a place to rest, but a place to create memories, making it a top recommendation for those planning their next coastal getaway.
