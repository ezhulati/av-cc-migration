Located a mere stone's throw away from the iconic Skanderbeg Square, less than 1 kilometer to be exact, this property stands out for its commitment to a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy atmosphere for all guests. The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring a spacious bedroom, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for endless entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen comes complete with a microwave, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, catering to all culinary needs. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort to the stay.

Privacy and tranquility are paramount at Two Steps Apartments 3, with soundproofing and a private entrance for each accommodation unit. This thoughtful design ensures that guests can enjoy their stay without any disturbances.

For those keen to explore Tirana's rich cultural tapestry, the apartment is conveniently situated near several key attractions. The Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and National Museum of History Albania are all within easy reach, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local heritage.

Accessibility is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 14 kilometers away, making Two Steps Apartments 3 an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Albania's vibrant capital.
