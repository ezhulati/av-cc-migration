Boasting a generous layout, ALFA Loft features two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living area complete with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchenette equipped with all the essentials, including a fridge. The apartment's highlight is undoubtedly its terrace, offering sweeping city views that guests can enjoy from the comfort of an outdoor dining area. For those chillier evenings, the warmth of the indoor fireplace provides a perfect retreat.

Adding to the convenience, the property includes complimentary amenities such as private parking and WiFi, ensuring a hassle-free stay. For quick bites or essential groceries, guests will find a coffee shop and a minimarket within easy reach.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks, including the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania and the National Museum of History Albania, both located less than 3 kilometers away. Furthermore, the former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the scenic Rock of Kavaje are easily accessible, adding to the list of must-visit sites during the stay.

Situated just 14 kilometers from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, ALFA Loft presents an ideal base for both short-term visits and extended stays, offering a blend of modern amenities and prime location to ensure an unforgettable experience in Tirana.
