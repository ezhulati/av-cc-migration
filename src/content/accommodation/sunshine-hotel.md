Accommodations at Sunshine Hotel are thoughtfully designed to ensure comfort and convenience. Each air-conditioned room boasts a private balcony offering panoramic views of the city, complemented by modern amenities including free WiFi, a private bathroom, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The attention to detail in the room's design and the provision of essential amenities underscore the hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction.

The hotel's on-site restaurant serves a delightful continental breakfast, providing a perfect start to the day with a variety of delicious options to suit all tastes. The combination of excellent dining, comfortable rooms, and exceptional service makes Sunshine Hotel a standout choice for visitors to Ksamil.

Located just 700 meters from the sandy shores of Ksamil Beach and within close proximity to Coco Beach and Sunset Beach, the hotel offers easy access to some of the area's most beautiful beaches. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is conveniently situated 93 km away, making Sunshine Hotel an accessible gateway for international travelers.

With its strategic location, comprehensive facilities, and unwavering dedication to guest experience, Sunshine Hotel is not just a place to stay-it's a destination in its own right for those exploring the enchanting region of Ksamil.
