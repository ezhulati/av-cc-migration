Each apartment is a haven of comfort, featuring a balcony that invites guests to enjoy the city's ambiance. The modern amenities include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and hair dryer, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. Select units also boast a well-appointed kitchen, complete with kitchenware, perfect for those who prefer to prepare their own meals. To further enhance the guest experience, Apartments Fabio provides bed linen and towels in all units, adding a touch of convenience to the stay.

Situated just 2.7 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and with Corfu International Airport 92 km away, Apartments Fabio stands out as an ideal base for exploring the natural and historical wonders of the region.
