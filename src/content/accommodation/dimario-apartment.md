With its thoughtful layout, the apartment boasts two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, a fully equipped kitchen for those culinary adventures, and a bathroom designed with comfort in mind. Guests can enjoy the added luxury of a balcony, perfect for sipping morning coffee while soaking in the serene garden views. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complemented by complimentary WiFi to keep guests connected and entertained throughout their stay.

For those arriving by air, the journey from Ioannina Airport is a scenic 97 km drive, making Dimario Apartment an accessible escape for both local and international travelers. This property, committed to a smoke-free environment, is ideally positioned 1.7 km from the quaint La Petite Beach, promising a stay that is both relaxing and invigorating.

Dimario Apartment emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of home comfort and the excitement of Sarandë's coastal life.
