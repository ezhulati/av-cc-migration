The hotel prides itself on offering complimentary WiFi across the premises, catering to the needs of both leisure and business travelers. For families or groups, the availability of family rooms adds an extra layer of convenience, ensuring a comfortable stay for all parties involved.

Each room at Sol Hotel is thoughtfully equipped with essential amenities to enhance the guest experience. From a wardrobe for personal belongings to a private bathroom stocked with a hairdryer, the attention to detail is evident. Entertainment and comfort are not overlooked, with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning standard in all accommodations. Select rooms also boast a balcony, offering guests a private outdoor space to relax and unwind.

For those concerned with connectivity and productivity, the inclusion of a desk and kettle in every unit underscores the hotel's commitment to meeting the diverse needs of its guests.

Located 58 km from Podgorica Airport, Sol Hotel presents a viable option for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and thoughtful amenities in Shkodër.
