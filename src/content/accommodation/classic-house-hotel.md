The hotel prides itself on its array of amenities, including a shared kitchen and attentive room service, ensuring a stay that is as comfortable as it is enjoyable. Each room is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV boasting satellite channels, a refrigerator, and a kettle. The attention to detail extends to the provision of a shower, complimentary toiletries, and a desk in every room, catering to the needs of the modern traveler. For those desiring the comforts of home, select rooms offer a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher, an oven, and a microwave, alongside a spacious wardrobe.

Located a mere 43 km from the historic Monastery Saint Naum and 70 km from Kastoria National Airport, Classic House Hotel stands as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of the region.
