Guests are welcomed into spacious accommodations that boast not only a flat-screen TV for entertainment but also come equipped with air conditioning and heating to ensure comfort in any season. The private bathrooms add an extra layer of privacy and convenience.

The farm stay distinguishes itself with a family-friendly restaurant that prides itself on serving delectable local cuisine for both lunch and dinner. It's an opportunity for guests to savor the flavors of the region in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

For those looking to unwind, Sirena Agrotizem doesn't disappoint. The property features a lush garden that offers a picturesque view and a tranquil spot for relaxation. Children are not forgotten, with outdoor play equipment available to keep them engaged and entertained.

Situated a mere 7.9 km from the Rock of Kavaje and within reach of the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the farm stay serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of the area. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 44 km away, access to and from this tranquil retreat is hassle-free.

Sirena Agroturizem stands out as a perfect getaway for those seeking a blend of pastoral beauty and accessibility to Albania's coastal and historical sites, all while enjoying the comforts of home.
