The mansion boasts a meticulously designed apartment that caters to the modern traveler's needs. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with air-conditioned spaces, complemented by a balcony that offers both garden and city views. The proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, just over a kilometer away, adds a touch of historical intrigue to the stay.

Attention to detail is evident in the apartment's amenities, which include a bedroom with cozy bed linen, a bathroom stocked with towels, and a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV offering streaming services. The dining area and a fully equipped kitchen allow guests the freedom to prepare their meals, while the terrace, boasting mountain views, and a patio that serves as an outdoor dining area, invite relaxation in a picturesque setting. The commitment to guest comfort is further underscored by the apartment's soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle.

For those looking to explore Tirana, 25 Green Retreat Mansion facilitates adventures with a car rental service. The property's location is a gateway to exploring the city's cultural landmarks, including Saint Paul Cathedral, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 16 km away, the mansion also offers a convenient paid airport shuttle service, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

25 Green Retreat Mansion is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing Tirana's vibrant culture and scenic beauty, all while enjoying the comforts of a thoughtfully appointed home away from home.
