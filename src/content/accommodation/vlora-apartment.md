Guests are welcomed into a space where every detail is catered to ensure a relaxing stay. The apartment boasts two elegantly appointed bedrooms, a modern living room designed for leisure, and a dining area perfect for enjoying meals with a view. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, invites guests to prepare their favorite meals just the way they like them. For added convenience, the apartment comes with complimentary WiFi and private parking, ensuring that every aspect of your stay is hassle-free.

The attention to detail extends to the in-room amenities, including fresh fruits, towels, and bed linen, all provided to enhance the comfort of your stay. The property prides itself on being a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and fresh atmosphere for all guests.

Located just 2.8 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and a mere 3 km from the vibrant Independence Square, Vlora Apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë. For those traveling further afield, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km away, making it a convenient option for international travelers.

Vlora Apartment stands out as a serene retreat that promises an unforgettable stay, where the beauty of Vlorë's beaches meets the comfort of home.
