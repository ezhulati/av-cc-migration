The property prides itself on its array of family-friendly rooms, complemented by an inviting outdoor fireplace that adds a touch of warmth to the cool seaside evenings. For those who prefer the comfort of their own space, select apartments come equipped with air conditioning, a dining area, and a cozy seating area, complete with a cable flat-screen TV for entertainment. Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the well-appointed kitchens available in some units, featuring an oven, fridge, and stovetop, perfect for preparing home-cooked meals.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, guests are invited to unwind in the on-site bar or lounge, offering a relaxed atmosphere for socializing or simply enjoying a quiet evening. For outdoor enthusiasts, the apartment complex features a picturesque picnic area, ideal for spending a day soaking up the sun and the scenic surroundings.

Strategically positioned, Amelia Apartments is a mere 600 meters from both Lori Beach and the vibrant Ksamil Beach 9, offering ample opportunities for beachside relaxation and water activities. Moreover, the historic Butrint National Park is just 3 km away, promising a fascinating excursion for history buffs and nature lovers alike. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 92 km from the property, making Amelia Apartments an accessible haven for both local and overseas visitors.

In summary, Amelia Apartments stands out as a top-tier destination for those in pursuit of a memorable stay in Ksamil, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, comfort, and convenience.
