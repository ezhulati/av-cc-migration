Following its comprehensive renovation in 2022, the apartment presents a modern retreat, equipped with high-speed WiFi, ensuring guests stay connected during their visit. The smoke-free environment promises a clean and healthy stay, catering to the preferences of today's discerning traveler.

The interior of this air-conditioned one-bedroom apartment is thoughtfully designed to meet the needs of both short and long-term guests. It boasts a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their own meals, alongside a cozy seating area, a dining space for intimate meals, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. Additional amenities, including a bidet and a dressing room, add a touch of luxury to the living experience.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the apartment's proximity to notable attractions such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, making it an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Tirana. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, with the apartment offering a convenient paid airport shuttle service.

Luxury Rent Apartment 10 is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the vibrant life and history of Tirana, making it a top recommendation for travelers in search of a memorable city break.
