This apartment distinguishes itself with its generous space, featuring a balcony that offers a breath of fresh air, two cozy bedrooms for a restful night's sleep, a welcoming living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen complete with a fridge for those who enjoy preparing their meals. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a pleasant stay. Importantly, the entire apartment is a smoke-free zone, catering to the comfort of all guests.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is accessible throughout the premises, keeping guests connected with the world or planning their next adventure in Durrës. For convenience, a supermarket is located within easy reach, making grocery runs effortless.

Situated 400 meters from the picturesque Durres Beach, the apartment invites guests to immerse themselves in the local beach culture. Additionally, the property is conveniently located near several key attractions, including the Rock of Kavaje, 4.2 km away, and the historical Durres Amphitheatre, 7.2 km away. For those interested in Albania's history, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the House of Leaves are both within a manageable distance of 39 km.

Travelers will find the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 33 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze. N&E Apartment 1 in Durres, Beach, stands out as a stellar accommodation choice for those looking to explore the enchanting city of Durrës and its captivating coastline.
