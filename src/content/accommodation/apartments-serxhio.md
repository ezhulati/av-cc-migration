Accommodations are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring a fully equipped kitchen, a cozy dining area, and a living space complete with two sofas and satellite TV for those moments of relaxation. The convenience of an en-suite bathroom with a shower is standard in every apartment, ensuring privacy and comfort.

For those looking to explore local flavors, a variety of restaurants are conveniently located nearby, offering a taste of traditional Albanian cuisine alongside international favorites. Additionally, the proximity to local agencies means that arranging excursions to the historic Butrint Park is a breeze, offering a glimpse into the region's rich heritage.

Transportation is readily available, with a local bus stop directly adjacent to the apartments and a bus station just 300 meters away. For those venturing further, the port, offering lines to Corfu and its international airport, is a mere 550 meters from Apartments Serxhio, making it an ideal base for both local exploration and beyond.
