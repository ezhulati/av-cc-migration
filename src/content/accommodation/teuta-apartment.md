The highlight of Teuta Apartment is its spacious layout, which includes a bedroom, a living room with a flat-screen TV, and an equipped kitchen boasting a dishwasher and an oven. The terrace provides stunning sea views, adding a touch of serenity to the stay. Upon arrival, guests are welcomed into the apartment through a private entrance and greeted with complimentary fruits, chocolates, or cookies, making for a warm and inviting first impression. Additional conveniences include provided bed linen, towels, and a laundry service, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free experience.

For those who appreciate the little extras, the property hosts a coffee shop and a minimarket, perfect for grabbing a quick bite or essentials without having to venture far. Families will be pleased to find outdoor play equipment, making it an ideal option for guests with children.

Located just a few steps from Durres Beach, Teuta Apartment offers easy access to sun, sand, and sea. For those looking to explore further, Skanderbeg Square is 37 km away, while Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 31 km from the accommodation, making it an excellent base for exploring the region's attractions.

In summary, Teuta Apartment provides a comprehensive package of comfort, convenience, and thoughtful touches, making it a highly recommended option for travelers visiting Durrës.
