The apartment itself presents a generous layout, featuring a balcony that commands breathtaking sea views. Within its walls, guests will discover two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room complete with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen outfitted with a dishwasher and an oven. The modern bathroom, complete with a walk-in shower, adds to the comfort, while towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided. The added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity.

For those seeking leisure on-site, a bar caters to refreshment needs without the necessity to venture far. The surrounding area of Llogara invites adventurous spirits to explore its beauty through hiking, while families will appreciate the children's playground available at Le Bellevue Apartment, ensuring entertainment for its younger guests.

Positioned 168 km from Ioannina Airport, the apartment serves as an ideal retreat for those looking to immerse themselves in the tranquility and natural beauty of Llogara, promising an unforgettable stay marked by comfort and convenience.
