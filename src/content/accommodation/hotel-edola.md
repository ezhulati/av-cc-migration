Each room and studio at Hotel Edola is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. Guests can enjoy the serene sea views from their own private balconies, a perfect backdrop for any vacation. The accommodations are well-appointed, with televisions for entertainment and private bathrooms equipped with showers for a refreshing end to a day in the sun. For those desiring a touch more independence, the studios offer kitchenettes, allowing the option of home-cooked meals.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Hotel Edola provides practical amenities such as luggage storage, ensuring a hassle-free stay. For adventurers looking to explore further, the Port of Sarandë is just 2 km away, with ferry services to Corfu Airport, making travel arrangements as breezy as the sea air.

Hotel Edola emerges as a standout choice for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and the allure of the Albanian Riviera.
