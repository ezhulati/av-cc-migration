Guests are welcomed into a serene oasis, with each accommodation offering a private balcony that opens up to tranquil garden views or, for some, the captivating expanse of the sea. The interiors are thoughtfully designed for a home-away-from-home experience, featuring fully equipped kitchens, comfortable dining areas, and the modern comfort of flat-screen TVs. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, equipped with showers, and the provision of essential amenities such as bed linen and towels, enhancing the overall convenience and comfort of the stay.

The property boasts a sun-drenched terrace, inviting guests to unwind and soak in the peaceful atmosphere, with the added benefit of complimentary WiFi throughout, ensuring that guests remain connected and entertained. 

A mere 500 meters from Lori Beach and a short drive to the historical marvels of Butrint National Park, Vila Ester's location offers an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural treasures of the area. For those arriving from afar, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 92 km away, adds an element of ease to travel arrangements.

Vila Ester stands out as a premier destination for those desiring a tranquil retreat with the comforts of modern living, making it a top recommendation for an unforgettable stay in Ksamil.
