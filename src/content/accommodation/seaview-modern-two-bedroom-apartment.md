The apartment itself is a haven of space and modern amenities. With two well-appointed bedrooms, it caters perfectly to families or groups looking for a serene getaway. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV, while the kitchen doesn't skimp on conveniences, offering a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster. This makes self-catering a breeze, allowing guests to enjoy home-cooked meals with a view. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of hotel-like luxury to the self-sufficient setup.

Privacy and security are paramount at SeaView Modern Two Bedroom Apartment. Guests will appreciate the private entrance and the peace of mind that comes with full-day security, ensuring a secluded and safe environment throughout their stay. Additionally, the apartment is designed to be accessible, featuring facilities for disabled guests, making it a welcoming option for all.

For those looking to explore the local area or venture further afield, the apartment conveniently offers a car rental service on-site. A minimarket within the premises also means essentials are always within reach. The location is ideal for beach enthusiasts, with Saranda City Beach and Saranda Bay Beach just a short distance away, 1.5 km and 1.6 km respectively. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 98 km from the accommodation.

In summary, SeaView Modern Two Bedroom Apartment stands out as a stellar accommodation choice, combining the comforts of home with the luxuries of a beachfront location. Its thoughtful amenities and prime position make it a compelling option for those looking to soak up the sun and sea of Sarande.
