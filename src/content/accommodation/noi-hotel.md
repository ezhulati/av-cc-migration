Accommodations at NOI HOTEL are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with essential amenities such as a desk for those needing to stay connected, a safety deposit box for valuables, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a private balcony and bathroom with a shower in every room enhances the sense of privacy and relaxation. Additionally, ample storage is provided with the inclusion of a wardrobe in all units.

For guests arriving from afar, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 92 km away, makes NOI HOTEL an accessible retreat for international travelers seeking the serene beauty and charm of Ksamil. With free WiFi available throughout the property, guests can easily share their experiences or stay in touch with loved ones. NOI HOTEL stands out as a preferred choice for those seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and connectivity in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
