The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, boasting air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and on-site parking at no extra cost. Security is a priority, with full-day monitoring, alongside amenities such as luggage storage and currency exchange services, ensuring a worry-free stay for all guests.

Featuring a balcony that offers breathtaking views of the sea, the apartment includes a bedroom, a spacious living room, a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances like an oven and a toaster. The bathroom is well-appointed with a bidet, and for those who prefer dining al fresco, there's an outdoor dining area that presents a picturesque backdrop of the mountains. Convenience is further enhanced with the provision of bed linen, towels, and an ironing service.

For guests looking to explore the local area or venture further afield, a car rental service is readily available on-site. The apartment's strategic location also places it within easy reach of Sarande Main Beach and the historic Butrint National Park, offering a mix of leisure and cultural activities. For essentials or quick snacks, a minimarket on the premises adds to the convenience.

Situated 97 km from Ioannina Airport, Comfort 2 Apartment stands out as a compelling choice for those looking to experience the best of Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
