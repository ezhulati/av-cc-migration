Boasting a generous layout, the apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for those leisurely evenings in, a fully equipped kitchen for culinary adventures, and two pristine bathrooms, each with a shower and hairdryer, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.

For those intrigued by local culture and history, the iconic Kuzum Baba is located just 2.1 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's heritage. Furthermore, for guests arriving from afar, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 150 km from the doorstep of The Confluence Apartment, ensures a seamless travel experience to this enchanting city.

In essence, The Confluence Apartment not only promises a stay that is as relaxing as it is enriching but also positions itself as a gateway to the captivating sights and sounds of Vlorë.
