The accommodation boasts a generously sized one-bedroom layout, complete with a living room that is both spacious and inviting. Guests can enjoy leisurely moments in front of the flat-screen TV, offering a wide range of cable channels for entertainment. The apartment doesn't skimp on practicalities either; a fully equipped kitchen awaits those who prefer to prepare their meals, adding a touch of home-away-from-home comfort. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet further enhances the living experience, ensuring convenience at every turn.

For those keen on exploring the surroundings, Apartment Durres serves as an ideal base. The property is conveniently located 37 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, offering a perfect opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the local culture and history. Adventure seekers will find the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within a 41 km radius, promising breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 7.6 km away, presenting another must-visit landmark. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is situated 32 km from the apartment, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

Apartment Durres stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the allure of the Albanian coast.
