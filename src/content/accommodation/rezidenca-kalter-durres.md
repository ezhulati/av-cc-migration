Guests are treated to accommodations equipped with flat-screen TVs and cozy seating areas, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Each unit features a fully equipped kitchenette, complete with a fridge, stovetop, and kettle, offering the convenience of home-cooked meals. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a shower and complimentary toiletries adds to the comfort, while select units also offer the luxury of a furnished balcony, inviting guests to enjoy their morning coffee or evening relaxation outdoors.

Strategically located, Rezidenca Kalter Durres is a convenient base for travelers, with the historic Port of Durres just 16 km away and the capital city, Tirana, reachable within 43 km. For those arriving by air, Tirana Airport is 45 km from the property, with airport shuttle services available for a smooth transition to this tranquil retreat.

Free WiFi and private parking are the cherries on top, ensuring that guests enjoy not only the beauty and calm of Rezidenca Kalter Durres but also the utmost in convenience and connectivity during their stay.
