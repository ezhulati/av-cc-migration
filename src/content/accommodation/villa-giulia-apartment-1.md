This accommodation is thoughtfully appointed with a bedroom, a bathroom, and comes complete with essentials such as bed linen and towels. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV and a dining area, leading to a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their meals. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the terrace, offering breathtaking sea views, perfect for dining al fresco while admiring the mountainous horizon. The design of the apartment ensures guests enjoy utmost privacy and tranquility, thanks to a private entrance and soundproofing features.

For those venturing from afar, Ioannina Airport is the closest air link, situated 102 km from the property, making Villa Giulia Apartment 1 an accessible escape for international and domestic travelers alike.
