Guests are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their experience, including complimentary WiFi and private parking. The sun terrace beckons for leisurely afternoons under the sky, while the on-site snack bar caters to cravings with ease.

Accommodations at VILA AARON are thoughtfully appointed with air conditioning, cozy seating areas, and flat-screen TVs equipped with streaming services for a touch of home. The soundproof units ensure a peaceful retreat, complete with a safety deposit box, and a private bathroom that includes a bidet, hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries. For added comfort, each unit is also furnished with quality bed linen and towels.

Situated just 5.8 km from the Rock of Kavaje and within easy reach of the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, VILA AARON offers an ideal base for exploring the rich history and natural beauty of the area. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 32 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Durrës.
