Each unit at Apartament Leko is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned spaces that open up to private balconies for serene moments under the Albanian sky. The well-equipped kitchens and cozy living areas ensure a home-away-from-home experience, while the inclusion of modern amenities such as free WiFi and televisions cater to the needs of the contemporary traveler. The convenience of private bathrooms and ample wardrobe space adds to the appeal, making it a suitable choice for families and groups alike.

A short drive from the property reveals the natural beauty of Maestral Beach and the historical allure of Butrint National Park, making it easy for guests to immerse themselves in the rich culture and stunning landscapes Albania is known for. With Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, Apartament Leko serves as a convenient gateway for international and domestic visitors.

In summary, Apartament Leko stands out as a welcoming retreat in Sarandë, offering well-appointed accommodations that promise a memorable stay for those looking to explore the wonders of this enchanting city and its surroundings.
