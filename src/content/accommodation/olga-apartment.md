Guests will find themselves in a generously sized apartment that boasts a balcony for soaking in the garden views. The living space is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a kitchen that comes well-equipped, including a minibar for those evening refreshments. Entertainment needs are met with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable stay.

For those concerned about connectivity, Olga Apartment does not disappoint, offering complimentary WiFi throughout the property. Additionally, the convenience of air conditioning is available to ensure a pleasant environment regardless of the weather outside.

Travelers looking to explore beyond the local beaches will find Ioannina Airport 97 km away, making this apartment a strategic location not just for beach lovers but also for those looking to explore the wider region. Olga Apartment stands out as a top recommendation for those visiting Sarandë, offering both the comforts of home and the ease of access to the city's most sought-after attractions.
