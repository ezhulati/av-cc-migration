This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it offers a haven of space and privacy. Guests are greeted with a generously sized duplex that features a balcony offering city views, two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with modern appliances. The inclusion of two bathrooms, each with a bidet, ensures convenience and comfort for all guests. Moreover, the provision of towels and linens, available upon request, adds an extra layer of ease to the stay.

Connectivity is seamless within this accommodation, thanks to complimentary WiFi accessible throughout the premises. Those looking to explore the local area will find Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach merely 200 meters away, inviting guests for leisurely strolls or refreshing swims. The property also prides itself on offering a terrace for guests to unwind and a private entrance that guarantees an exclusive and undisturbed experience.

For those intrigued by Albania's historical narrative, the former residence of Enver Hoxha is within reach, presenting an opportunity to delve into the country's past. Additionally, the convenience of being located 35 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport facilitates a hassle-free arrival and departure, making Elysee - Duplex Apartment near the beach - Shkembi i Kavajes a compelling choice for both short and extended stays in Durrës.
