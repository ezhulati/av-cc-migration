The highlight of the accommodation is its well-appointed apartment, which features a spacious bedroom, a modern bathroom, and a living area that opens up to a breathtaking balcony view of the sea. The apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV boasting cable channels, a dining space for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to whip up their own culinary delights. The inclusion of an air-conditioning system, a shower, and a dressing room within the unit ensures a stay that combines luxury with the comforts of home.

Outdoor enthusiasts will appreciate the garden and barbecue facilities, perfect for leisurely afternoons, as well as the unique outdoor fireplace that adds a touch of warmth to the evenings. Additionally, the property boasts an outdoor dining area, allowing guests to enjoy their meals amidst the beauty of Ksamil's natural landscape.

Families visiting Studio Apartments Rizarjo will find the accommodation welcoming and thoughtfully prepared for young guests, with the provision of a baby safety gate to ensure peace of mind during their stay.

Situated within easy reach of popular attractions such as Sunset Beach, Ksamil Beach 7, and Bora Bora Beach, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the beauty of the region. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is located 93 km away, making Studio Apartments Rizarjo a convenient choice for both local and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Ksamil.
