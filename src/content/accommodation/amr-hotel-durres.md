Each room at AMR Hotel - Durres is thoughtfully appointed with a flat-screen TV featuring satellite channels, alongside a safety deposit box for personal security. Select accommodations offer captivating city views, with every room extending the luxury of a private balcony. Comfort is further enhanced with the provision of quality bed linen and towels, making for a restful night's sleep.

Morning risers are greeted with a choice of buffet, à la carte, or continental breakfast options, setting the stage for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's multilingual staff, fluent in Catalan, English, Spanish, and French, stands ready at the reception to offer tailored advice, ensuring guests make the most of their stay.

Located in the heart of Durrës, AMR Hotel is conveniently positioned near popular attractions such as Durres Beach, Golem Beach, and the iconic Rock of Kavaje. For those venturing further, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 36 km away, making the hotel an ideal base for both local and international travelers.

In summary, AMR Hotel - Durres presents a compelling option for those in pursuit of a coastal retreat that does not compromise on amenities or service. Its strategic location, coupled with a range of thoughtful facilities, positions it as a standout choice for visitors to Durrës.
