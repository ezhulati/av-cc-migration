Guests are welcomed into a world where each room serves as a sanctuary of relaxation, equipped with essential modern amenities including a desk, a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a private bathroom complete with a shower and complimentary toiletries. For those desiring a little extra, select rooms also feature a balcony, offering a private space to unwind and soak in the surroundings. Comfort is further accentuated with the provision of soft bed linen and fluffy towels, ensuring a restful night's sleep.

The start of each day at Nobbu Hotel Ksamil is greeted with a choice between a hearty buffet or a lighter continental breakfast, catering to all tastes and preferences. While the hotel prides itself on its serene location and comprehensive amenities, it also understands the needs of the modern traveler, offering free WiFi throughout the premises and a paid airport shuttle service for those coming from or heading to Corfu International Airport, which is 93 km away.

With its strategic location, a stone's throw from some of Ksamil's most beautiful beaches and attractions, combined with its unwavering commitment to guest comfort and satisfaction, Nobbu Hotel Ksamil stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to explore the beauty of this coastal paradise.
