This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious haven complete with a terrace that boasts breathtaking mountain views. Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, the apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with a toaster and a fridge, catering to guests' culinary needs, while the inclusion of two bathrooms, complete with a bidet, ensures convenience and comfort. Additional comforts include towels and bed linen, a seating area, and a charming fireplace, making it a home away from home.

Guests of Apartment Krenar Voda benefit from the added perks of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a hassle-free stay. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is located 93 km away, making international travel accessible.

Apartment Krenar Voda emerges as a top choice for travelers who value both the tranquility of a beachside getaway and the comforts of a fully equipped living space.
