The apartment itself is a spacious haven, featuring a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views, ensuring guests can soak in the beauty of the Albanian Riviera from the comfort of their accommodation. It is well-appointed with a bedroom, a living room that invites relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and an equipped kitchen complete with an oven and toaster for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The convenience of a bathroom with a shower, along with the option to request towels and linens, adds to the comfort of the stay. To elevate the experience further, the apartment offers additional in-room amenities such as wine or champagne, catering to the finer tastes of its guests.

Located 96 kilometers from Ioannina Airport, XhuliAl's Apartment is accessible yet feels like a world away from the hustle and bustle, promising a serene getaway with the allure of the Albanian coast. With free WiFi included, guests can stay connected while escaping the everyday. This property stands out as a compelling choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and tranquility of Sarandë.
