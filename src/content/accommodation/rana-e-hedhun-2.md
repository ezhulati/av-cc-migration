Guests will find themselves comfortably accommodated in an apartment that features a single bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for all culinary needs, and a bathroom designed with modern amenities. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, making evenings in a pleasure. For those who prioritize a smoke-free environment, this accommodation meets the mark, ensuring a pleasant atmosphere for all guests.

Located just 2.7 km from the inviting shores of Shëngjin Beach, the apartment presents an ideal base for beach lovers. Additionally, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 51 km away, making Rana e Hedhun 2 an accessible haven for both local and international travelers.
