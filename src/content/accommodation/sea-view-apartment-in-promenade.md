This apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest but a space to live. With a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony that invites the sea breeze right into the living space, it's designed for both short and long stays. The kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with modern appliances including a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and fridge, catering to every culinary need. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, ensuring a comfortable stay.

While the allure of the apartment itself is undeniable, its location further enhances its appeal. A mere 39 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, it offers an excellent base for exploring the rich culture and history of the area. For those seeking adventure, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is located 43 km away, offering breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience. Additionally, the Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, is just 12 km from the apartment, making for an easy day trip. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 33 km away, making SEA VIEW APARTMENT IN PROMENADE an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers looking for a memorable stay in Durrës.
