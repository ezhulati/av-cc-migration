The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring a bedroom, a bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services. The dining area and a fully equipped kitchenette allow for home-cooked meals, while the terrace offers breathtaking sea views, adding to the overall serene experience. For added convenience, an ironing service is available upon request.

Guests will appreciate the on-site facilities, which include a restaurant, an elevator for easy access, and an ATM. The commitment to guest satisfaction is further underscored by the provision of complimentary WiFi throughout the property, ensuring that both leisure and business travelers stay connected. For those needing essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises serves as a handy resource.

Located a mere 500 meters from Golem Beach and 2.2 km from Durres Beach, the apartment's location allows easy access to some of the area's most beautiful coastlines. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 37 km away, with the apartment offering a paid airport shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

Beach View Apartment, Golem Durres, thus stands as a testament to the fusion of modern amenities and strategic location, promising an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
