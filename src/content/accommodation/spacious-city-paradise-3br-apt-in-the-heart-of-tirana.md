The apartment itself is a study in spacious and modern living, featuring three well-appointed bedrooms and a bathroom equipped with essential amenities such as bed linen and towels. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. A dining area and a fully equipped kitchen cater to all culinary needs, whether it's a quick breakfast or a full dinner. Adding to the charm is a balcony that presents tranquil street views, inviting guests to enjoy a moment of peace amidst the city's hustle and bustle. The entire property is a non-smoking zone, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those keen on delving into Tirana's cultural scene, the apartment is conveniently located near the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, Reja - The Cloud, and the iconic Pyramid of Tirana. Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 17 km away, making Spacious City Paradise - 3BR Apt in the Heart of Tirana not only a comfortable retreat but also a gateway to the vibrant life and culture of Albania's capital.
