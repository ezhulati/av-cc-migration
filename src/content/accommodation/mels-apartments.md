The accommodations at Mels Apartments are designed with families and groups in mind, featuring spacious family rooms that promise a comfortable stay. Each apartment is a testament to thoughtful convenience, boasting a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven, ensuring that guests feel right at home. The inclusion of a seating area, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine adds layers of comfort and practicality to the living space. Bathrooms are modern and private, equipped with a bidet and a hairdryer, catering to all personal care needs.

Connectivity is seamless throughout the premises, with complimentary WiFi available to all guests. Select units elevate the experience further with the addition of a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to unwind. The comfort of guests is paramount, with every apartment featuring air conditioning and ample wardrobe space, ensuring a pleasant and organized stay.

For those traveling to Sarandë by air, the convenience of Ioannina Airport, located 98 kilometers away, makes Mels Apartments an accessible destination for international and domestic visitors alike.
