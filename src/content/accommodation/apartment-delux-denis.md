This air-conditioned haven offers guests a serene retreat with its well-appointed separate bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable stay.

For those looking to venture further, Skanderbeg Square is easily accessible, located just 2.4 km away, offering a glimpse into the cultural heartbeat of Tirana. Adventure seekers will appreciate the proximity to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 7.1 km from the apartment, providing an opportunity for an unforgettable aerial view of the city. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 13 km away, making Apartment Delux Denis an ideal base for both short visits and extended stays in this captivating city.

With free WiFi available throughout the property and private parking on site, guests are assured of both connectivity and security. Apartment Delux Denis represents a blend of comfort, convenience, and accessibility, making it a top recommendation for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the charm and dynamism of Tirana.
