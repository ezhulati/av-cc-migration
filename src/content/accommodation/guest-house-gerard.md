The accommodations at Guest House Gerard are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each air-conditioned unit is equipped with essential amenities such as a wardrobe, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, balcony, and a private bathroom with a shower. For those who prefer to cater to their culinary needs, select units boast a well-fitted kitchen, complete with an oven and stovetop. The attention to detail extends to the provision of bed linen and towels in every unit, ensuring a comfortable stay.

One of the highlights of staying at Guest House Gerard is the delightful breakfast available daily. Guests can start their day with a buffet featuring an array of dishes, complemented by a selection of fruits and cheese, catering to diverse tastes and preferences.

Beyond the comfort of their rooms, guests are invited to unwind in the shared lounge area or explore the serene garden. The guest house also offers barbecue facilities for those who enjoy outdoor dining. With the convenience of free private parking, full-day security, and complimentary WiFi, guests are assured a worry-free stay.

For travelers looking to explore beyond Ksamil, Guest House Gerard provides a paid airport shuttle service to Corfu International Airport, located 92 km away, adding an extra layer of convenience to the travel experience.

In summary, Guest House Gerard stands as a testament to the perfect balance of accessibility, comfort, and hospitality, making it a highly recommended choice for travelers visiting Ksamil.
