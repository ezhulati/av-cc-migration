The resort's dining experience is elevated by an on-site restaurant and bar, complemented by the luxury of a hot tub, ensuring relaxation is never far away. For those seeking seamless service, Limni Resort does not disappoint, with a 24-hour front desk, efficient airport transfer services, a helpful tour desk, and the convenience of free WiFi across the property.

Accommodations at Limni Resort are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air conditioning, flat-screen TVs with cable channels, refrigerators, kettles, and essential amenities like hairdryers and desks. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms equipped with slippers, and select rooms enhance the stay with balconies and captivating pool views. Every room is meticulously prepared with fresh bed linen and towels, ensuring a restful night's sleep.

Morning at the resort begins with a choice of buffet or à la carte breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The surrounding area of Ksamil invites guests to engage in a variety of activities such as hiking, fishing, and snorkeling, making every day an adventure.

Limni Resort is ideally positioned near popular attractions including Ksamil Beach 9, Bora Bora Beach, and Ksamil Beach 7, with Corfu International Airport located 93 km away, making it a convenient choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and exploration in the heart of Ksamil.
