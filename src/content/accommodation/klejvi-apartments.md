Each apartment at Klejvi is a haven of comfort, featuring air-conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a private balcony, presenting serene garden views that promise relaxation. The kitchenettes are thoughtfully equipped, catering to those who appreciate the option to prepare their own meals. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of flat-screen TVs in every unit, ensuring that guests have access to their favorite shows after a day of exploration.

The attention to detail extends to the bathrooms, which are fitted with a bidet, and the provision of free WiFi throughout the property, allowing guests to stay connected with ease. Select apartments elevate the experience further with the addition of a terrace, offering an ideal spot for a morning coffee or an evening glass of wine under the stars.

For those intrigued by history and nature, the proximity to Butrint National Park, just 3.8 km away, makes Klejvi Apartments an excellent base for discovering the area's rich heritage. Moreover, with Corfu International Airport 94 km away, the apartments serve as a convenient gateway for international travelers seeking the beauty and tranquility of Ksamil.

Klejvi Apartments not only promises a stay filled with comfort and convenience but also offers a glimpse into the serene lifestyle of Ksamil, making it a top choice for travelers in search of a peaceful getaway by the sea.
