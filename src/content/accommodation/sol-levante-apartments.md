The apartment is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities to ensure a seamless experience. It features a cozy bedroom, a bathroom stocked with essential linens and towels, and a living space that includes a flat-screen TV compatible with streaming services for entertainment at any time. The fully equipped kitchen caters to guests' culinary needs, making it easy to prepare meals just the way they like. Adding to the charm is a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views, perfect for morning coffees or evening relaxation. The patio, furnished for comfort, offers a splendid view of the mountains, enhancing the tranquil ambiance.

For those concerned about privacy and peace, Sol Levante Apartments does not disappoint. The accommodation boasts a private entrance and is designed with soundproofing, ensuring a quiet, undisturbed stay. Families traveling with little ones will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a baby safety gate, making it a safe environment for children.

Situated 18 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport 98 km away, the location of Sol Levante Apartments serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural and natural heritage of the area. The convenience of free WiFi and private parking adds to the hassle-free experience, making it a compelling choice for those looking to explore Sarandë in comfort and style.
