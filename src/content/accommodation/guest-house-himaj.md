Security is paramount at Guest House Himaj, with full-day monitoring and a currency exchange service available on-site for international visitors. The attention to guest comfort is evident in the design of each room. Equipped with air conditioning, soundproofing, and a private bathroom with a shower, the units promise a serene and comfortable environment. Moreover, allergy sufferers will find solace here, as all accommodations are allergy-free and include a safety deposit box for added peace of mind.

For those looking to enjoy moments of tranquility, the guest house's garden offers a perfect retreat. Despite its serene location, Guest House Himaj remains connected to the wider region, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport situated 117 km away. The property facilitates travel arrangements by offering a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring guests can navigate their arrival and departure with ease.

In summary, Guest House Himaj emerges as a distinguished option for visitors to Berat, blending essential amenities with thoughtful extras to create a memorable stay.
