The apartment itself is a spacious haven, equipped with a bedroom, a bathroom, and an inviting living space that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels for entertainment. The dining area is thoughtfully paired with a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests the convenience of home-cooked meals. Moreover, the terrace opens up to stunning mountain vistas, adding to the apartment's charm. Comfort is prioritized with air conditioning, a seating area, a washing machine, and an additional bathroom featuring a shower. Guests can also enjoy al fresco dining in the outdoor area, enhancing the overall stay experience.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, visitors can explore notable attractions such as Rozafa Castle Shkodra and Lake Skadar, located 29 km and 30 km away, respectively. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest, situated 82 km from the accommodation.

Velipoj Appartamento distinguishes itself not only through its impressive amenities but also by offering a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests. With its combination of comfort, convenience, and captivating views, this property stands as a top choice for travelers seeking a memorable escape in Velipojë.
