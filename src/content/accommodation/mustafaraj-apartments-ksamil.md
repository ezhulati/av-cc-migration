The apartments boast stunning views of the Ionian Sea and the distant shores of Corfu, setting the scene for a tranquil escape. With kitchen facilities and private bathrooms in every unit, guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The addition of shared barbecue facilities encourages a sense of community and offers a delightful option for dining al fresco.

Just a brief walk away, visitors will find a selection of restaurants, bars, and grocery stores, ensuring convenience is always at hand. The heart of Ksamil village, with its quaint bus stop offering routes to Sarandë, is merely 200 metres from the apartments, making exploration a breeze.

For those intrigued by history and culture, the renowned archaeological site of Butrint is less than 5 km away, promising an enriching excursion. Meanwhile, the vibrant city of Sarandë, located 15 km from the apartments, presents a perfect opportunity for day trips and experiencing the local lifestyle.

Mustafaraj Apartments Ksamil stands out as a peaceful haven amidst lush surroundings, offering both the tranquility of the seaside and the allure of nearby attractions. It's an ideal choice for travelers seeking a harmonious blend of relaxation and adventure.
