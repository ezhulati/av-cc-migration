The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and elegance, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven. The living space is adorned with a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, complemented by a bathroom outfitted with a bath. One of the highlights of staying at Sunset Views is the opportunity to dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area, where meals are enjoyed against the backdrop of breathtaking sea views. The addition of a fireplace elevates the ambiance, making it an ideal setting for both relaxation and intimate gatherings.

For those concerned with connectivity, the promise of complimentary WiFi and the convenience of free private parking are notable perks. The entire property is a smoke-free zone, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

Situated 143 km from Ioannina Airport, Sunset Views offers a blend of accessibility and seclusion, making it a coveted destination for those looking to unwind by the sea. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the comfort of a well-furnished apartment, this property promises a memorable stay in the picturesque setting of Himare.
