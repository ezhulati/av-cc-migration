This apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest; it provides a home away from home. With two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom stocked with essentials, and a living space designed for relaxation, guests can truly unwind. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, and a fully equipped kitchen ensures every need is met. The highlight, however, is the balcony that opens up to breathtaking sea views, promising serene mornings and tranquil evenings.

Privacy is paramount at Fancy Summer Sea View Apartment, underscored by the private entrance that allows guests to come and go as they please. Despite its secluded feel, the property remains connected, offering free WiFi and air conditioning to keep guests comfortable and in touch.

For those looking to explore beyond Sarandë, the nearest airport in Ioannina is within reach, and the apartment facilitates travel arrangements with a paid airport shuttle service. This blend of convenience, comfort, and captivating views makes Fancy Summer Sea View Apartment a must-visit destination for discerning travelers.
