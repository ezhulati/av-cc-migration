The property prides itself on modern amenities, including an elevator for easy access and round-the-clock security, ensuring a worry-free stay. Connectivity is never an issue, with complimentary WiFi enveloping guests in every corner of the premises. Designed with families in mind, the apartments feature spacious family rooms, making it an ideal retreat for those traveling with loved ones.

Each apartment is a testament to thoughtful living spaces, boasting terraces and fully equipped kitchens with ovens, ready to cater to any culinary endeavor. The living areas are furnished with comfortable seating and flat-screen TVs, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Convenience is further amplified with in-unit washing machines and private bathrooms, complete with bidets and slippers, adding a touch of home comfort. The highlight for many, however, is the balcony that opens up to an outdoor dining area, offering mesmerizing city views, inviting guests to dine under the stars.

Situated less than 1 km from the serene La Petite Beach and a leisurely 12-minute walk from the picturesque Maestral Beach, the location of Seaside Apartments Sarande could not be more perfect for beach enthusiasts. For those looking to venture further, Ioannina Airport is within a manageable distance, 96 km away, making it a convenient gateway for international travelers.

In summary, Seaside Apartments Sarande stands as a beacon of comfort and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay with its blend of modern amenities, strategic location, and thoughtful touches designed to make every guest feel at home.
