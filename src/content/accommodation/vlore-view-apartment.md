This generously sized apartment is not just about the views; it features two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances including an oven and a toaster, catering to all culinary needs. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with complimentary towels and bed linen, adds to the convenience. Emphasizing privacy and security, the apartment boasts a private entrance and is safeguarded by round-the-clock security, offering peace of mind for guests.

For those with an adventurous spirit, the apartment's location is ideal for engaging in snorkeling and cycling, with the added option to relax in the lush garden. Cultural landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are within easy reach, located 4.7 km and 4.9 km away, respectively. For international travelers, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 154 km away, making Vlore View Apartment a compelling choice for both local and overseas visitors looking for a memorable stay in Vlorë.
