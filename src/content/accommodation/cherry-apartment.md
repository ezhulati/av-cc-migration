This property doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious sanctuary complete with a balcony that presents captivating city views. The apartment is well-appointed with a bedroom, a cozy living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a microwave and toaster for those morning or late-night cravings. Additionally, the convenience of a bathroom equipped with a bidet adds to the comfort of the stay. For those concerned about health and safety, it's worth noting that the accommodation maintains a strict non-smoking policy.

For guests looking to explore the area at their own pace, Cherry Apartment facilitates this adventure by offering a car rental service directly from the premises. This feature is particularly beneficial for those eager to discover the city's must-visit sites, such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, all located within close proximity.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi available throughout the property. Moreover, Skanderbeg Square is just 600 meters away, making it an ideal spot for guests to immerse themselves in the local culture and vibrancy of Tirana.

For travelers arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 14 km away, with the apartment offering a paid airport shuttle service for a seamless transfer experience.

In summary, Cherry Apartment stands as a beacon of comfort, convenience, and connectivity, making it an excellent choice for those looking to experience the best of Tirana.
