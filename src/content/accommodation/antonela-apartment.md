Guests are welcomed into a world of modern amenities within this air-conditioned apartment, which boasts a spacious balcony that leads to a serene terrace - ideal for morning coffees or evening relaxation. The living space is thoughtfully designed with a fully equipped kitchen, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, complementing the non-smoking environment that prioritizes guest comfort.

For those looking to delve deeper into Tirana's attractions, Antonela Apartment is conveniently located near the Grand Park of Tirana, the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, and the iconic Pyramid of Tirana, making it an excellent base for city exploration. Connectivity is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 16 km away, ensuring smooth travel arrangements for international guests.

While the apartment offers the luxury of privacy, it doesn't skimp on convenience, providing free WiFi throughout the property and the option for private parking at a nominal fee. Antonela Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those desiring a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of Tirana's charm.
