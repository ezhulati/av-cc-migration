The accommodations at EDGEWATER are designed with comfort and family in mind, featuring spacious family rooms that open up to a patio for an immersive experience of the locale. Each apartment is equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and hair dryer, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. The highlight of each unit is undoubtedly the balcony, which comes with an outdoor dining area and offers panoramic views of the city, providing the perfect backdrop for a memorable meal.

Beyond the confines of the apartments, guests are invited to unwind in the lush garden, a tranquil oasis that promises a moment of peace amidst the excitement of Sarandë. For those looking to explore, Sarande Main Beach is conveniently located just 1.6 km away, offering a day of sun-soaked leisure, while the historic Butrint National Park, 19 km from the property, offers a glimpse into the area's rich cultural heritage.

Situated within reach of Ioannina Airport, 99 km away, EDGEWATER Apartments stands out as an accessible retreat for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and charm of Sarandë, making it an ideal choice for both short escapes and extended stays.
