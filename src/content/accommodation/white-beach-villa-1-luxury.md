Upon entering the 2-bedroom villa, guests are greeted by a spacious living room, adorned with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for those leisurely evenings in. The villa boasts a fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and a microwave, ensuring that every culinary need is met with ease. The inclusion of two bathrooms, complete with bathrobes, adds a touch of luxury to the stay. For those looking to celebrate or simply enjoy a quiet night in, the villa provides wine or champagne and fruits, setting the stage for memorable moments.

Privacy and convenience are paramount, with a private entrance ensuring guests can come and go as they please. The villa doesn't stop at just providing a comfortable stay; it caters to all needs with a dressing room, laundry service, and full-day security, ensuring peace of mind throughout the stay.

For those looking to enjoy the outdoors, the villa complex features a picnic area, ideal for a day spent soaking up the sun and enjoying the fresh air.

Situated within easy reach of local attractions, Rozafa Castle Shkodra is 29 km away, and Lake Skadar is just a bit further at 30 km, making White Beach Villa 1 - Luxury an excellent base for exploring the surrounding area. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 82 km from the villa, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

White Beach Villa 1 - Luxury stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, promising an unforgettable stay in Velipojë.
