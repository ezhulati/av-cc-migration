The apartment is well-appointed with a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen including a toaster and fridge for those who prefer to prepare their meals, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, and the convenience of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with provided towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of guests. Notably, the apartment is designed to be non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate area, VIP Beach is a short drive away, offering another splendid option for sun-seekers, while the historic Butrint National Park, 16 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for a cultural excursion. Crown Apartment is also conveniently situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, making it accessible for international travelers.

Outdoor dining options at the apartment allow guests to enjoy their meals al fresco, making the most of the Mediterranean climate. With free WiFi included, guests can easily stay connected, whether for leisure or work. Crown Apartment stands out as an ideal spot for those looking to experience the best of Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with the beauty of the Albanian Riviera.
