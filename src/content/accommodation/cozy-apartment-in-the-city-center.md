Elevating the experience of city living, the apartment boasts a thoughtful layout that includes a spacious bedroom, a welcoming living room with a cable flat-screen TV for entertainment, an equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their own meals, and a pristine bathroom equipped with a bidet and shower. The addition of a terrace provides an intimate setting for guests to enjoy the breathtaking mountain scenery that Shkodër has to offer.

Understanding the needs of the modern traveler, the property ensures convenience at every turn, featuring amenities such as a lift for easy access and a minimarket on-site for any last-minute needs. Guests will appreciate the added touches of comfort, including the option to request towels and linens for a nominal fee, and the privacy afforded by a separate entrance to the accommodation.

Located a mere 58 km from Podgorica Airport, the Cozy Apartment in the City Center stands out as an ideal lodging option for those looking to explore the vibrant city of Shkodër while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed home away from home.
