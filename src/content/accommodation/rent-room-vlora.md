This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home. With its spacious layout, guests can unwind in a two-bedroom haven featuring a cozy living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a balcony that offers tranquil street views. The kitchen comes fully equipped with an oven and a fridge, catering to those who prefer to prepare their meals. Additionally, the presence of two bathrooms, each with a bidet, ensures convenience for all guests. While towels and linens are available upon request, the commitment to comfort is evident in every detail.

The convenience extends beyond the apartment's walls, with complimentary WiFi and on-site parking ensuring a hassle-free experience. Notably, the property maintains a non-smoking environment, reflecting its dedication to providing a clean and healthy stay.

For those keen to explore the local sights, Kuzum Baba is just 2.5 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's historical significance. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 150 km away, facilitates easy travel arrangements for international guests.

Rent Room Vlora stands as a testament to thoughtful accommodation that combines strategic location, ample amenities, and a commitment to guest comfort, making it a compelling choice for visitors to Vlorë.
