The apartment is thoughtfully equipped with a bedroom that promises restful nights, a kitchen complete with an oven and fridge for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a living area designed for relaxation, featuring a flat-screen TV. The inclusion of a bathroom with a walk-in shower adds to the comfort of the stay. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the opportunity to dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area, where guests can savor their meals against the backdrop of breathtaking sea views. Additionally, the availability of a laundry service adds an element of convenience to the experience.

For those inclined to explore the surroundings, Keta Apartment is ideally positioned for engaging in activities such as cycling around Golem, offering a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. The property's location also provides easy access to notable landmarks, with Skanderbeg Square located 48 km away, and the Rock of Kavaje just 6.3 km from the doorstep. For travelers arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 42 km from the apartment, making Keta Apartment an accessible and appealing choice for both domestic and international visitors.
