The property boasts a shared kitchen and a charming picnic area, making it an ideal choice for families and groups. The highlight of Hotel Jaho is undoubtedly its garden-view apartments, which come equipped with air conditioning, a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony, and a private bathroom with a bidet. Adding to the allure, each unit features a patio adorned with outdoor furniture, perfect for basking in the serene environment. The apartments are also fitted with heating facilities, ensuring a comfortable stay regardless of the season.

For culinary enthusiasts preferring to stay in, the option to grill on the barbecue presents a delightful opportunity to enjoy a meal under the stars. Adventure seekers will appreciate the convenience of car rental services arranged by the apartment, opening up possibilities to explore the surrounding area, including fishing adventures.

Situated 32 km from the Rock of Kavaje and 43 km from the Durres Amphitheatre, Hotel Jaho serves as an excellent base for exploring the rich cultural heritage of the region. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 68 km away, making the property easily accessible for international travelers.

Hotel Jaho stands as a testament to the perfect blend of homely comfort and the promise of adventure, making it a top choice for those looking to experience the best of Kavajë.
