Each room at Hotel Metali extends an invitation to experience Sarandë from a unique vantage point, with balconies offering city views that capture the essence of the locale. For those desiring a glimpse of the Ionian Sea, select rooms elevate the experience with breathtaking sea views. The presence of a flat-screen TV and a fridge in every room adds a layer of comfort, making it easy for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

The hotel's commitment to service is evident through its 24-hour front desk and room service, catering to guests' needs at any hour. The on-site bar presents a cozy spot for evening relaxation or a casual meeting point.

Situated a mere 17 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park, Hotel Metali serves as an ideal base for adventurers keen to delve into the region's rich history and natural beauty. Its proximity to Sarande Main Beach, Saranda City Beach, and La Petite Beach offers beach lovers a variety of options for sun, sea, and sand. Moreover, with Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, the hotel remains accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Hotel Metali stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, perfectly positioned for those eager to explore the vibrant city of Sarandë and its surrounding attractions.
