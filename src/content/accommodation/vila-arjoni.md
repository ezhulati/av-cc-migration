The accommodation boasts a cozy 1-bedroom setup, complemented by a living room that features a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The kitchen is fully equipped, catering to the needs of those who prefer to prepare their own meals, while the bathroom comes complete with soft bathrobes, adding a touch of homely comfort.

Located a mere 2.8 km from the scenic Saranda Bay Beach and within easy reach of the historic Butrint National Park, 18 km away, VILA ARJONi serves as an ideal base for explorers keen to discover the natural and cultural treasures of the area. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 98 km from the property, making it a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Sarandë.
