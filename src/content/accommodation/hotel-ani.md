Accommodations at Hotel Ani are designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Each unit is equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a refrigerator, and a kettle, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and enjoyable. The inclusion of a shower, complimentary toiletries, and a wardrobe in the private bathrooms adds to the thoughtful touches. Notably, rooms boast not only a private bathroom complete with a bath and hairdryer but also offer captivating city views. The provision of a dishwasher in all units underscores the hotel's commitment to guest convenience.

For those looking to explore beyond the sandy shores, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 6 kilometers away, while the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is accessible at 48 kilometers. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 42 kilometers from the hotel, facilitates ease of travel for international guests.

Hotel Ani stands out for its blend of strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and thoughtful accommodation features, making it a compelling choice for travelers seeking a harmonious balance between leisure and exploration.
