This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it offers guests a glimpse into a serene lifestyle with its breathtaking mountain and pool views. The space is thoughtfully designed to ensure comfort and relaxation, featuring a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, and a balcony that presents stunning sea views. The inclusion of modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and a microwave, and a bathroom with a bidet, ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxuries of a resort. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience, while the seating area and fireplace invite guests to unwind in style.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, the Apartment at Bougainville Resort is perfectly positioned just 14 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park, offering an easy gateway to one of Albania's most treasured natural and historical sites. Additionally, the property's non-smoking policy ensures a fresh and welcoming environment for all guests.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Ioannina Airport located 96 km away, making the apartment a feasible choice for both local and international travelers. Whether it's the allure of the beach, the comfort of the apartment, or the adventure that awaits in the surrounding areas, the Apartment at Bougainville Resort stands out as a top choice for those seeking an unforgettable Sarandë experience.
