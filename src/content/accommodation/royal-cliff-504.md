The apartment itself is a haven of modern living, featuring air-conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It boasts a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen ready for culinary adventures with an oven and kettle, and a bathroom outfitted with a shower and hairdryer, ensuring all the comforts of home. For entertainment, a flat-screen TV is at guests' disposal in a smoke-free environment.

For those looking to explore the local area or needing essentials, a minimarket on the premises provides utmost convenience. Additionally, Royal Cliff 504 facilitates exploration and ease of travel with airport transfer services and car rental options available, making it effortless for guests to venture beyond.

Located just 2.1 km from the lively Golem Beach and within a manageable distance of 42 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, the apartment serves as an ideal base for both beach lovers and culture enthusiasts. Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the nearest airport, is located 36 km away, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures.

Royal Cliff 504 stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to experience the best of Durrës, offering a blend of convenience, comfort, and connectivity.
