The apartment itself is a haven of tranquility and convenience, offering air-conditioned accommodation complete with a welcoming balcony and complimentary WiFi. Situated 5.5 km from the scenic Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and a mere 1.9 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, guests are perfectly positioned to experience both the natural beauty and historical depth of Tirana.

Accommodating up to guests in its spacious layout, the apartment boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a sleek bathroom equipped with a bidet, and a living area that invites relaxation with its flat-screen TV. The fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and toaster, caters to culinary enthusiasts wishing to prepare home-cooked meals, while the inclusion of a washing machine adds an extra layer of convenience for longer stays.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of quality bed linen, towels, and laundry service, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. For those arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 13 km away, making the Modern Cozy Apartment an accessible and attractive option for both domestic and international travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and culture in Albania's captivating capital.
