Accommodations at Villa Mucho are thoughtfully designed with guest convenience in mind. Each unit boasts a fully equipped kitchenette, complete with a dining area, refrigerator, and stovetop, perfect for those who enjoy the flexibility of preparing their own meals. The presence of air conditioning, a safety deposit box, and a flat-screen TV in every room adds an extra layer of comfort and security to the guest experience. For those seeking a bit more luxury, select units feature a terrace adorned with outdoor furniture, providing an ideal setting for enjoying the sea views and soaking in the tranquil atmosphere of Sarandë.

Strategically located, Villa Mucho offers easy access to the serene La Petite Beach, just 2 km away, and the picturesque Maestral Beach, located 2.2 km from the property. For guests traveling from afar, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is 98 km away, making Villa Mucho an accessible choice for both local and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Sarandë.
