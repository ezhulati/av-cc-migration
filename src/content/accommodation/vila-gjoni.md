Accommodations at Vila Gjoni are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with essential amenities such as a wardrobe, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a restful and enjoyable stay. The private bathrooms come complete with a bidet and hairdryer, while select units elevate the experience with a patio or balcony offering captivating city views. For added convenience, all rooms are provided with fresh bed linen and towels.

The hotel's prime location allows easy access to the picturesque La Petite Beach, less than 1 km away, and Sarande Main Beach, a leisurely 16-minute walk from the premises. For those looking to explore beyond the city, Ioannina Airport is situated 97 km from Vila Gjoni, making it a convenient base for both local and international travelers.
