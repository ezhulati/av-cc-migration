The establishment is particularly family-friendly, boasting spacious family rooms and an engaging children's playground, making it an ideal choice for travelers with little ones in tow. Each apartment within the complex is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels for those moments of relaxation. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen and dining area in every unit adds a touch of home, complemented by the convenience of a safety deposit box and a private bathroom that features a walk-in shower, hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries. Select apartments elevate the experience further with either a balcony or patio, offering breathtaking mountain views.

For guests who prefer the comfort of home-cooked meals, the option to utilize the on-site barbecue facilities is a welcome feature.

Located just 3.7 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and within a manageable distance of 94 km from Corfu International Airport, Villa Zotaj not only promises a memorable stay but also ensures ease of travel to and from the property. This review highlights Villa Zotaj as a top-tier choice for travelers seeking a blend of leisure and adventure in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
