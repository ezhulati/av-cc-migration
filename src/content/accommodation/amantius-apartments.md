The highlight of AMANTIUS APARTMENTS is undoubtedly its air-conditioned accommodation, boasting a balcony that invites guests to bask in the serene surroundings. The apartment is thoughtfully laid out with a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and microwave, ensuring all the comforts of home. The inclusion of a bathroom with a shower, complemented by fresh towels and bed linen, adds to the apartment's appeal.

Further enhancing the guest experience is the apartment's terrace, offering breathtaking sea views, and an outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals in the open air. The property's garden provides a peaceful haven for relaxation, allowing guests to unwind in a natural setting.

Located 129 km from Ioannina Airport, AMANTIUS APARTMENTS presents an ideal base for exploring the beauty of Borsh and its surroundings, promising a stay that combines relaxation with the allure of the Albanian coast.
