Privacy is a paramount feature at Elite Apartments Sarande, with each apartment boasting a private entrance. The air-conditioned accommodations are thoughtfully equipped with a fully functional kitchen, including a dining area, fridge, kettle, and stovetop, ensuring guests feel right at home. The convenience of a coffee machine, flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and complimentary WiFi enhances the stay, while select units also offer the luxury of a balcony with captivating city views. To complete the experience, all units are furnished with quality bed linen and towels, underscoring the attention to detail and guest comfort.

For those arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the property, making Elite Apartments Sarande an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers. This apartment complex not only promises a stay marked by comfort and privacy but also positions guests perfectly to soak in the vibrant life and natural beauty of Sarandë.
