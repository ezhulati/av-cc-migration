New Brilant Apartment 02 is not just about its prime location; the apartment itself is a haven of comfort and style. It boasts a spacious layout with one bedroom, ensuring a restful stay. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for relaxing evenings in. The kitchen is a dream for those who love to cook, featuring modern appliances including an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge. The balcony, adorned with outdoor furniture, offers breathtaking mountain views, providing an ideal backdrop for morning coffees or evening relaxation. It's important to note that the apartment is both non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful stay.

For those eager to explore, New Brilant Apartment 02 is conveniently located near several of Tirana's attractions. The National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, National Museum of History Albania, and Toptani Shopping Centre are all within close proximity. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

In summary, New Brilant Apartment 02 is a standout option for those looking to experience the best of Tirana, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views.
