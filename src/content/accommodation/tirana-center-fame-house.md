This property excels in providing a serene retreat amidst the city's hustle and bustle, thanks to its spacious layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that offers tranquil street views. The apartment is equipped with modern conveniences such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven, and two bathrooms featuring walk-in showers. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home-like comfort, making stays here all the more pleasant.

For guests valuing their privacy and peace, Tirana Center Fame House does not disappoint. The apartment boasts soundproofing and a private entrance, ensuring a secluded and uninterrupted experience. Additionally, the convenience of free private parking, an elevator, and complimentary WiFi enhances the overall guest experience.

The vicinity of the apartment is dotted with popular attractions, including the House of Leaves, Clock Tower Tirana, and Et'hem Bey Mosque, inviting guests to explore the rich cultural tapestry of Tirana. For those needing essentials or quick snacks, a minimarket on the premises serves as a handy resource.

Located 13 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the property also offers a paid airport shuttle service, adding to the seamless travel experience for guests. Tirana Center Fame House stands as a testament to thoughtful accommodation that combines the comfort of modern living with the charm of Tirana's vibrant surroundings.
