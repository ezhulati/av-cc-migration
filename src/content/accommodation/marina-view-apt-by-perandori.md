The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and bathrooms, complete with all necessary linens and towels. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at guests' fingertips. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to every culinary need, while the outdoor dining area invites guests to dine al fresco against a backdrop of stunning mountain scenery. The added convenience of a private entrance underscores the property's commitment to privacy and exclusivity.

For those who might need to pick up essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises adds an extra layer of convenience. Situated just a short drive from the historical Independence Square (2.6 km) and the iconic Kuzum Baba (3.1 km), Marina View APT by Perandori is perfectly positioned for guests looking to explore the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making it a manageable journey to this coastal retreat.

Marina View APT by Perandori stands out as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of modern amenities, privacy, and stunning natural beauty in Vlorë.
