Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the unique benefit of a private entrance, setting the stage for a stay marked by exclusivity and privacy. The complex prides itself on its attentive 24-hour front desk service, ensuring all needs are met promptly, from private check-in and check-out to currency exchange services, adding an extra layer of convenience to the guest experience.

The accommodations at Apartment Dejvi are designed with comfort and functionality in mind. Each air-conditioned unit is well-equipped with essentials such as a wardrobe, coffee machine, fridge, stovetop, and safety deposit box, not to mention the added luxuries of a flat-screen TV, terrace, and a private bathroom complete with a bidet. The attention to detail extends to the provision of bed linen and towels in every unit, alongside a patio featuring an outdoor dining area and serene garden views, ensuring a homely atmosphere.

For those who enjoy al fresco dining, the barbecue facilities and private balconies offer the perfect setting for a memorable meal under the stars. Alternatively, the well-appointed kitchens and dining areas within each apartment provide a cozy option for indoor dining.

The opportunity for relaxation continues with the beautifully maintained garden, inviting guests to unwind in a tranquil setting. The location of Apartment Dejvi is equally appealing, with Saranda Bay Beach less than 1 km away and Saranda City Beach just a short 1.7 km journey. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is located 98 km from the complex, making it a convenient base for exploring the wider region.

In summary, Apartment Dejvi emerges as a standout accommodation choice in Sarandë, perfectly blending modern amenities with the natural beauty of its surroundings to offer guests an unforgettable stay.
