This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious haven of relaxation. Guests can enjoy the luxury of two bedrooms and two bathrooms, ensuring ample space for families or groups. The inclusion of bed linen and towels, coupled with a flat-screen TV and a dining area, adds to the comfort of the stay. The fully equipped kitchen invites guests to prepare their meals, offering a taste of home while away. Moreover, the balcony presents an opportunity to soak in the city views, adding a touch of serenity to the morning coffee or evening unwind.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is accessible throughout the property, ensuring guests stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventures in Sarandë. For those concerned about health and wellness, the accommodation proudly maintains a non-smoking environment, promoting a clean and fresh atmosphere.

Accessibility is further enhanced by the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 96 km away, making Tomas Apartment a convenient choice for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Tomas Apartment stands out for its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay in Sarandë, whether one is here to explore the natural beauty, historical sites, or simply enjoy the local beaches.
