This air-conditioned apartment offers a serene retreat with its well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes modern amenities such as a dishwasher and a kettle. The bathroom is equipped with a bidet and comes with complimentary slippers, ensuring guests enjoy both comfort and convenience. To enhance the stay, towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, emphasizing the property's attention to detail.

For those interested in exploring local attractions, Gisi's Bazaars is perfectly situated near Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, offering guests a glimpse into the local culture and shopping scene. Connectivity is also a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 16 km away, facilitating easy arrivals and departures.

With its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment coupled with free WiFi access throughout the property, Gisi's Bazaars caters to the needs of modern travelers, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and connected.
