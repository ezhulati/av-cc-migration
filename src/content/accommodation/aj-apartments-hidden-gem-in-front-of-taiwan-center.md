This apartment boasts an array of amenities designed to enhance the stay of every guest. With air conditioning, free WiFi accessible throughout the premises, and a balcony offering breathtaking mountain views, comfort is at the forefront of AJ Apartments' offerings. The accommodation features a well-appointed bedroom, a bathroom stocked with essential linens and towels, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their own dishes. Additionally, the apartment is non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful and comfortable stay.

For those keen on exploring local attractions, AJ Apartments is conveniently located near several points of interest. The Et'hem Bey Mosque, National Gallery of Arts Tirana, and the iconic Reja - The Cloud are all within easy reach. Moreover, the property is just 600 meters from Skanderbeg Square, offering guests a glimpse into the heart of Tirana's bustling city life. For travelers arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 15 km away, making AJ Apartments an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in this dynamic city.
