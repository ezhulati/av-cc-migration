The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, including a bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a terrace that overlooks a lush garden. The kitchen comes fully equipped with modern appliances, including a dishwasher and an oven, ensuring guests can feel at home. The inclusion of a hot tub in the bathroom adds a touch of luxury, making it the perfect place to unwind after a day of adventure. For convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available in the apartment, and the added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy.

For those looking to venture further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is located 48 km away, offering breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience. The Rock of Kavaje, another notable attraction, is just 5.7 km from the apartment. Travelers will find the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport conveniently located 38 km away, making Lovely Golem Durres Apartment an accessible and attractive option for both short and long-term stays.
