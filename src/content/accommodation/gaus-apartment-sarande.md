The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom with a shower. The inclusion of air conditioning and free WiFi throughout the apartment ensures that guests enjoy the comforts of home in a serene setting. The added convenience of a 24-hour front desk and an elevator adds to the seamless experience of staying at Gaus Apartment Sarande.

For those keen to explore beyond the immediate comforts of their accommodation, La Petite Beach awaits at a distance of 1.6 km, while the serene Maestral Beach is just 1.8 km away. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the apartment, making Gaus Apartment Sarande an accessible retreat for both local and international guests.

In essence, Gaus Apartment Sarande offers a tranquil escape in Sarandë, providing guests with the perfect base to explore the beauty of the Albanian Riviera, all while ensuring the utmost in comfort and convenience.
