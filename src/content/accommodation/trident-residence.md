Each apartment at Trident Residence is a testament to comfort and elegance, boasting air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. The kitchens are fully equipped, inviting guests to create their own culinary delights in a dining area designed for memorable meals. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV in each unit, while the private bathrooms come complete with a bidet, plush bathrobes, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a pampering experience. For added convenience, all units are provisioned with high-quality bed linen and towels.

The property's garden offers a tranquil retreat, a perfect spot for guests to unwind and soak in the serene atmosphere.

For those looking to explore, Vjetër Beach is a mere 3 km away, offering a quieter slice of Vlorë's stunning coastline. The historic Kuzum Baba is also within easy reach, located just 3.7 km from the residence, promising a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 153 km from Trident Residence, ensures a seamless journey to this exquisite destination.

Trident Residence is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the enchanting experiences that Vlorë has to offer, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.
