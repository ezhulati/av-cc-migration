The accommodation options at Ion Sea View cater to a variety of preferences, with select units boasting terraces or balconies that present breathtaking sea views, complemented by outdoor dining areas for an unforgettable al fresco experience. For those sensitive to noise, soundproof units are available, ensuring a peaceful retreat amidst the lively surroundings.

Strategically positioned, Ion Sea View is a mere 500 meters from the allure of Ksamil Beach 7 and within easy reach of the natural wonders of Butrint National Park, located just 3 km away. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km from the property, making Ion Sea View an accessible haven for both local and overseas visitors.

With its blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views, Ion Sea View is an excellent choice for families and solo travelers alike, promising a stay that is as relaxing as it is memorable.
