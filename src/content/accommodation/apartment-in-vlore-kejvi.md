Following its 2016 renovation, the apartment presents a modern retreat, boasting a spacious balcony that offers guests a private outdoor space to relax. Inside, the air-conditioned space is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of contemporary travelers. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is always at hand, while the provision of free WiFi keeps guests connected throughout their stay.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free experience. For those looking to celebrate or simply enjoy a quiet night in, the apartment also offers the option of wine or champagne, adding a touch of luxury to the stay.

Located 2.2 km from the inviting Vlore Beach and 150 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Apartment in Vlorë Kejvi stands out as an accessible and attractive option for both short and long-term visitors to this vibrant city. Its smoke-free policy further underscores its commitment to providing a pleasant and healthy environment for its guests.
