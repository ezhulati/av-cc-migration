Boasting an expansive layout, the apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, ensuring ample space for families or groups. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful touches that make this place feel like home, including cozy bed linen, plush towels, and a balcony that offers serene pool views. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for entertainment after a day of exploration. A fully equipped kitchen and a dedicated dining area invite guests to prepare and enjoy meals with a view of the garden.

Convenience is key at Family Vacation Big House, with complimentary WiFi and on-site parking adding to the hassle-free experience. The property maintains a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and fresh atmosphere for all guests.

For those needing essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises caters to everyday needs. Additionally, the shared lounge area provides a relaxing space for guests to unwind and socialize.

Adventure seekers will find the Rock of Kavaje, located just 4.6 km away, an intriguing spot to visit, while history enthusiasts will be drawn to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, 39 km from the apartment. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 33 km away, making Family Vacation Big House an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a memorable coastal retreat in Durrës.
