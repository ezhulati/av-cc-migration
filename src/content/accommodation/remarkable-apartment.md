The facility is equipped with modern amenities including an elevator, a shared kitchen for those who prefer to cook their meals, and a currency exchange service, adding an extra layer of convenience for international guests. Each apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of every traveler, featuring a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, a flat-screen TV with access to streaming services, and a private bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower, bathrobes, and a hairdryer. For those with specific needs, allergy-free units are available, ensuring a comfortable stay for every guest.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, guests will find themselves in close proximity to some of the city's most notable attractions, including the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Rinia Park, and the House of Leaves. For those traveling by air, the convenience is extended with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located just 16 km away, making Remarkable Apartment an ideal base for exploring the vibrant city and its surroundings.
