Each room at Aleks Holiday Rooms is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, boasting private balconies that open up to refreshing garden views. The interiors are equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms complete with baths, creating a home away from home atmosphere. The addition of a cozy seating area in each unit further enhances the living experience, providing a perfect nook for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

The property itself invites guests to bask in the tranquility of its garden, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle. For those looking to explore beyond the confines of the guest house, Coco Beach awaits just 1 km away, while the historic Butrint National Park, a treasure trove of ancient ruins, is only 3.4 km distant. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, located 93 km from the premises.

Aleks Holiday Rooms stands out not only for its prime location and impeccable accommodations but also for the added conveniences it offers, such as complimentary WiFi and private parking, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and exploration in Ksamil.
