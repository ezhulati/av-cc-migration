The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting a bedroom equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring satellite channels, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The fully equipped kitchen is a culinary enthusiast's dream, offering a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster for those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals. Additionally, the convenience of outdoor dining allows guests to savor their creations against the backdrop of Sarandë's stunning landscape. Soundproofing and a bath add an extra layer of privacy and relaxation, making it an ideal setting for both rest and rejuvenation.

For those who appreciate a good view, the property does not disappoint, offering breathtaking mountain vistas that promise to enchant. An on-site bar provides a cozy spot for evening relaxation or a casual meet-up.

Situated a mere 1.6 km from Saranda City Beach and 18 km from the historic Butrint National Park, guests have the luxury of exploring the local area's rich cultural and natural attractions with ease. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is 98 km away, making Modern One Bedroom Apartment Saranda Terraces an accessible haven for both domestic and international visitors.

In summary, Modern One Bedroom Apartment Saranda Terraces stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those seeking a blend of serenity, convenience, and comfort in the heart of Sarandë.
