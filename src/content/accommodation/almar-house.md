The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It boasts a layout that includes one bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a cozy night in if they choose to stay indoors. Importantly, Almar House is committed to providing a smoke-free environment, enhancing the air quality and comfort for all guests.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is accessible throughout the property, allowing guests to easily plan their next day's adventure or share their experiences with loved ones back home. For those venturing from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making Almar House a feasible option for international travelers.

In summary, Almar House offers a blend of location, convenience, and comfort, making it an attractive option for visitors to Vlorë looking for a peaceful stay close to the beach and local attractions.
