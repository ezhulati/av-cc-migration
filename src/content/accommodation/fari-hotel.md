The hotel elevates the morning experience by offering a diverse breakfast menu, with options ranging from continental to Italian and American, catering to a variety of palates. Beyond the confines of the hotel, Golem Beach awaits at a short 500-meter stroll, offering a perfect spot for sun-seekers, while the picturesque Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach is just 2.4 km away, ideal for those looking to explore the local coastline further.

For travelers flying in, the convenience is unmatched with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 41 km from the premises, making Fari Hotel an accessible retreat for both international and domestic visitors. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and attentive service, including room service and an on-site bar, Fari Hotel stands out as a prime lodging option for those visiting Golem.
