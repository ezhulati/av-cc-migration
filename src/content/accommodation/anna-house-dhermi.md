Each apartment at Anna House Dhermi is designed with guest privacy and comfort in mind, featuring a private entrance, air conditioning, soundproofing, and ample storage with a wardrobe. The private bathrooms come equipped with a bath, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience. For those desiring a glimpse of the sea or additional outdoor space, select units present the luxury of a balcony or patio, offering picturesque sea views that promise to elevate the stay.

The attention to detail extends to the provision of bed linen and towels in all units, ensuring a hassle-free stay for families and groups alike. Situated 159 km from Ioannina Airport, the property is a manageable distance for international travelers, making it an accessible retreat for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle.

Anna House Dhermi emerges as a distinguished option for guests in search of tranquility, comfort, and the beauty of Dhërmi, promising an unforgettable stay with every visit.
