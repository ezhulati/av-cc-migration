Guests are welcomed into a world of elegance and comfort in this spacious apartment, which boasts a modern design complemented by a suite of high-end appliances. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV offering satellite channels, while the kitchen is a culinary enthusiast's dream, featuring a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster. The inclusion of wine or champagne adds a touch of luxury, ensuring a memorable stay.

The apartment's thoughtful layout includes a comfortable bedroom, meticulously furnished to provide a restful night's sleep. High-quality towels and bed linen further enhance the guest experience, emphasizing the property's commitment to comfort and luxury.

For those keen to explore Tirana's cultural landmarks, the apartment's location offers easy access to notable attractions such as the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, the Pyramid of Tirana, and Saint Paul Cathedral. Additionally, the proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha invites guests to delve into the country's rich history.

With the convenience of free private parking, complimentary WiFi, and a non-smoking environment, Luxury Condo Apartment @Air Albania Stadium caters to the needs of modern travelers. Situated 17 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment serves as an ideal base for both short-term visits and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
