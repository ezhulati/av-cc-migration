Each apartment at Villa Alfred is thoughtfully equipped with a kitchenette, balcony, and cozy seating area, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The convenience of a private entrance, coupled with air conditioning, soundproofing, and a desk in every unit, caters to both leisure and business travelers. The added luxury of a private bathroom with a bidet and heating facilities in all units underscores the property's attention to guest comfort.

For those looking to explore the local area, Villa Alfred is ideally positioned just 1.4 km from the serene La Petite Beach and 17 km from the historic Butrint National Park, making it an excellent base for adventurers and culture enthusiasts alike.

Practical amenities such as an on-site minimarket and car rental service enhance the stay, providing everything needed right at guests' fingertips. Moreover, for those arriving from afar, the property's paid airport shuttle service from Ioannina Airport, located 97 km away, offers a hassle-free transition to this tranquil retreat.

With its blend of convenience, comfort, and accessibility, Villa Alfred stands out as a top accommodation choice, promising an unforgettable stay in Saranda.
