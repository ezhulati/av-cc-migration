Located a mere 44 km from the serene Ohrid Lake Springs, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty surrounding Korçë. For those intrigued by historical and spiritual sites, the Monastery Saint Naum is just 43 km away, promising a journey into the region's rich cultural tapestry.

The accommodation itself is thoughtfully designed with a single bedroom, making it perfect for solo travelers or couples. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen offers guests the freedom to prepare their own meals, adding a touch of home. The bathroom is well-appointed, ensuring a comfortable stay. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

An added convenience is the dressing room, providing a private space for guests to change and refresh in comfort. For those traveling by air, the Kastoria National Airport is located 71 km away, making "Beautiful apartment in the heart of the city!" a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, this apartment in Korçë stands out as a delightful retreat that combines the essence of city living with the tranquility of nearby natural attractions, making it a must-visit for those looking to experience the best of both worlds.
