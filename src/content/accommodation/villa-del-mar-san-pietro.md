The villa itself presents a picturesque setting with its balcony offering sweeping mountain views. It is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a microwave. The presence of two bathrooms, each with a bidet, adds to the convenience. The outdoor dining area, overlooking the lush garden, provides an idyllic spot for guests to savor their meals amidst nature.

For those seeking moments of relaxation, the villa's in-house lounge serves as the perfect retreat. Additionally, the convenience of an on-site minimarket caters to any last-minute needs or cravings.

Adventure enthusiasts will appreciate the availability of a car rental service directly from the villa, opening up opportunities to explore the surrounding area or partake in diving excursions nearby.

Significant landmarks such as the Rock of Kavaje and the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha are within accessible distances, enriching the stay with cultural exploration opportunities. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 31 km away, ensures ease of travel for international guests.

Villa del Mar San Pietro stands out as an exemplary choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of adventure in the heart of Durrës.
