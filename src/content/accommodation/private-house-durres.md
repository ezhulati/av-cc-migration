The villa's interior is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. With air conditioning throughout, the space includes two cozy bedrooms, a living room that invites relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen ready for culinary adventures with an oven and a coffee machine, and a bathroom equipped with both a bidet and a bath. The addition of a private entrance underscores the villa's commitment to guest privacy and comfort.

Strategically located, Private House Durres is within reach of notable attractions. The Dajti Ekspres Cable Car stands 43 km away, offering an adventure for those keen to explore the heights, while the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 12 km from the villa, presenting an opportunity for memorable excursions. History enthusiasts will find the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha 40 km away, and the ancient Durres Amphitheatre just 2 km from the doorstep, promising a dive into the rich tapestry of the area's past.

For guests arriving by air, the convenience is unparalleled with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 34 km away, making Private House Durres an ideal choice for both short getaways and extended stays. This villa stands out as a prime accommodation option for those looking to experience the charm and history of the region while enjoying the comforts of a home away from home.
