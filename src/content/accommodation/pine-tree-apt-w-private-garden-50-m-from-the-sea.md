The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting two bedrooms and a living room that opens up to breathtaking garden views. Modern amenities are not spared, with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom with a walk-in shower enhancing the comfort of your stay. The outdoor dining area invites guests to savor their meals amidst the natural beauty surrounding them, while the added security and private entrance ensure peace of mind throughout the visit.

For those who seek convenience within their reach, the property hosts a coffee shop and a minimarket, catering to everyday needs without the necessity to venture far. Adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts will find Golem's offerings of cycling, fishing, and hiking particularly appealing. Moreover, the Pine-Tree Apt w/ Private Garden 50 m from the Sea is equipped with barbecue facilities and a lush garden, perfect for leisurely days spent outdoors.

The location is ideally positioned for exploring the local area, with Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach less than 1 km away and Mali I Robit Beach accessible within an 18-minute walk. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being just 39 km away makes this apartment a compelling choice for those seeking both relaxation and adventure in Golem.
