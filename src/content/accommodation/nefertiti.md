Guests at Nefertiti are treated to air-conditioned rooms, ensuring a restful retreat from the day's adventures. Each unit is equipped with a private bathroom and the added benefit of complimentary WiFi, catering to the needs of modern travelers.

For those interested in exploring the area's rich history, the apartment is conveniently situated 39 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and just 6.5 km from the ancient Durres Amphitheatre. Moreover, the property's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 33 km away, makes it an accessible haven for international guests.

Nefertiti not only offers family-friendly accommodations but also serves as a gateway to the diverse attractions of Durrës, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience the best of what the region has to offer.
