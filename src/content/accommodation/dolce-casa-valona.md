This property stands out for its commitment to providing a seamless stay, offering free WiFi across the premises and the added convenience of being just 700 meters from the inviting shores of Vlore Beach. The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It boasts 2 separate bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, catering to all the needs of its guests. The inclusion of 2 bathrooms adds an extra layer of privacy and convenience, while a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment needs are met. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 151 km from Dolce Casa Valona, making it a manageable distance for international visitors. This apartment not only offers a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the rich culture and stunning landscapes of Vlorë, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and exploration.
