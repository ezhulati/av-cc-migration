Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy the luxury of free WiFi throughout their stay. The hotel prides itself on its impeccable service, featuring room service and a 24-hour front desk ready to cater to guests' needs around the clock. Families will find solace in the thoughtfully designed family rooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for all.

Accommodations at Hotel Esmerald Ksamil are designed with guest comfort in mind, boasting a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment, and a private bathroom equipped with a hairdryer and a bidet. Select rooms offer captivating city views from the privacy of a balcony, creating the perfect backdrop for a morning coffee or an evening unwind. The essence of convenience is captured in every room, all of which are air-conditioned and include a desk for those needing to stay productive.

Guests can start their day on the right note with a choice of a buffet or continental breakfast, offering a variety of options to suit every palate. The hotel's prime location places guests within a leisurely stroll from Paradise Beach, merely 400 meters away, and Lori Beach, just 500 meters from the doorstep. For those looking to explore beyond, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 92 km away, making Hotel Esmerald Ksamil an ideal base for both local and international travelers.

Hotel Esmerald Ksamil stands out as a top choice for those seeking a memorable stay in Ksamil, combining the warmth of hospitality with the comforts of home.
