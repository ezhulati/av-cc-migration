For those arriving by air or planning to explore the area at their own pace, the apartment simplifies logistics with its airport transfer services and the availability of a car rental service. The interior of Sion Saranda Apartment 21 is designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area equipped with a satellite TV for entertainment, and a kitchen that leaves nothing to be desired. With a dishwasher, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge at guests' disposal, the apartment ensures all the comforts of home are at hand. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of convenience.

Situated a mere 800 meters from Maestral Beach and within easy reach of the historical Butrint National Park, 16 km away, the apartment serves as an ideal base for both beach lovers and culture enthusiasts. The nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is located 96 km away, making Sion Saranda Apartment 21 an accessible retreat for international travelers.

In summary, Sion Saranda Apartment 21 stands out as a top-tier accommodation option for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Sarandë, offering a seamless blend of location, luxury, and leisure.
