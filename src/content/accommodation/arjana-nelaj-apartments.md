Guests are treated to the luxury of free private parking, a boon for those arriving by car, ensuring a hassle-free start to their stay. The accommodations, enveloped in the tranquility of a well-manicured garden, promise a refreshing retreat. Each air-conditioned unit is meticulously designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring private bathrooms and complimentary WiFi to keep guests connected. For those desiring a touch of exclusivity, select rooms boast terraces, offering a private slice of paradise.

The allure of Arjana & Nelaj Apartments is further enhanced by its proximity to Butrint National Park, a mere 2.7 km away, inviting guests to explore the rich tapestry of history and nature. Additionally, the convenience of being 92 km from Corfu International Airport positions the apartments as an ideal gateway for international travelers.

In essence, Arjana & Nelaj Apartments stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, making it a top recommendation for those venturing to Ksamil.
