This apartment isn't just about its prime location; it boasts breathtaking views of the sea and the cityscape, visible from its inviting balcony. Inside, guests will find a spacious layout that includes a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a kitchen equipped with modern amenities such as an oven and a toaster. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience to the stay, ensuring guests feel at home.

For those looking to venture further, the apartment facilitates exploration with its car rental service, allowing guests to discover the hidden gems of Sarandë at their own pace. And for a seamless travel experience, a paid airport shuttle service to Ioannina Airport, located 96 km away, is available upon request.

With free WiFi enveloping the property, guests can stay connected and share their memorable experiences without a hitch. The Lovely One Bedroom Apartment stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Sarandë.
