Guests will find themselves in a haven of relaxation within the 1-bedroom apartment, which is thoughtfully designed to cater to all needs. The living room, equipped with a flat-screen TV, offers a cozy retreat for entertainment or relaxation. Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the fully equipped kitchen, complete with a minibar, perfect for preparing meals or enjoying a quiet drink. The inclusion of a bathroom with a hot tub adds a touch of luxury, promising a soothing end to a day of exploration.

The convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi enhances the appeal of Aria Apartments, making it an ideal choice for those traveling by car or in need of staying connected. Situated 50 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and within easy reach of the Rock of Kavaje, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the local area's attractions. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 44 km away, ensures smooth travel arrangements for international guests.

Aria Apartments Qerret Albania not only offers a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the enchanting landscapes and rich history of Golem and its surroundings, making it a compelling choice for discerning travelers.
