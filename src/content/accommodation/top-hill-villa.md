A mere stroll from the property reveals the beauty of Lori Beach and the inviting sands of Ksamil Beach 9, both less than a kilometer away, making it an ideal spot for beach enthusiasts. The villa prides itself on its family-friendly rooms and the added delight of a picnic area, perfect for those leisurely family afternoons.

Each air-conditioned unit is a testament to comfort and convenience, boasting a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those home-cooked meals, and a balcony to soak in the serene views. Select apartments elevate the experience further with a terrace, inviting guests to enjoy their morning coffee or evening relaxation outdoors. Convenience is key, with every unit featuring a kettle and a desk, catering to both leisure and business travelers.

For those looking to explore the area at their own pace, Top Hill Villa offers a car rental service, adding a layer of ease to discovering the local sights. The historic Butrint National Park is a short drive away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage.

Situated 92 km from Corfu International Airport, the villa also provides a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a hassle-free journey to and from the accommodation. Top Hill Villa emerges as a top-tier choice for those seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and exploration in the heart of Ksamil.
