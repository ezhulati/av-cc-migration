The accommodation itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort, boasting air-conditioned spaces throughout, including three well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a terrace that offers breathtaking sea views. The villa is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a fully outfitted kitchen featuring an oven and a microwave, and three bathrooms, one of which includes a luxurious hot tub. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available in the villa, ensuring a comfortable stay. Guests will also appreciate the added touches of a seating area and a fireplace, perfect for relaxing evenings.

For those looking to soak up the sun, Villa Angela is conveniently located near several popular beaches, including Ri Beach, Vlore Beach, and the Beach at Government Villas. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 154 km away, making Villa Angela an accessible retreat for both local and international guests.

In summary, Villa Angela offers a perfect blend of privacy, luxury, and convenience, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience the best of Vlorë in a serene and comfortable setting.
