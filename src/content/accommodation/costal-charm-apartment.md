The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort. Guests are greeted with a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a fridge. The inclusion of a balcony offering panoramic pool views adds a touch of luxury, allowing guests to dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area while enjoying the serene garden views. The commitment to guest comfort is further evidenced by the apartment's soundproofing and private entrance, ensuring a tranquil and undisturbed stay.

For those who relish the outdoors, the apartment boasts a private beach area and a garden, inviting guests to unwind in a picturesque setting. The pool, with its inviting view, serves as a perfect retreat for relaxation and leisure.

Adding to the convenience, the property is a non-smoking establishment, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for all guests. The on-site bar offers a cozy spot for evening drinks or socializing with fellow travelers.

Located a mere 1.9 km from Golem Beach, the apartment is also within easy reach of notable attractions such as Skanderbeg Square and the Rock of Kavaje, making it an ideal base for exploring the surrounding area. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 42 km away, ensuring easy access to and from the property.

Costal Charm Apartment stands as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of privacy, comfort, and convenience in the heart of Golem.
