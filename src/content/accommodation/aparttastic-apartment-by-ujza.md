Situated merely a stone's throw away from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and within easy walking distance of the Toptani Shopping Centre, Aparttastic is an ideal base for explorers keen to immerse themselves in the local scene. Just 500 meters from the vibrant Skanderbeg Square, guests find themselves in the perfect spot to dive into Tirana's rich history and bustling social life.

The apartment itself is a sanctuary of modern living, featuring air conditioning for those warm Albanian days, a separate bedroom for privacy, a living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom. Entertainment is readily available through the flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have all the comforts of home at their fingertips.

For those interested in the arts and history, Aparttastic is conveniently located near the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, and the National Museum of History Albania, making it easy to indulge in the country's rich cultural offerings.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures for international guests. Aparttastic - Apartment by Ujza stands out as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of historical exploration and modern comfort in the heart of Tirana.
