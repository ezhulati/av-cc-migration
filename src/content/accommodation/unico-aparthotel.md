Each air-conditioned unit within the complex is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a wardrobe, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a terrace for an elevated sense of comfort. The private bathrooms come with a bidet, ensuring a blend of convenience and luxury. For those who prefer to cater to their culinary needs, select rooms boast a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, fridge, and stovetop, alongside a cozy seating area, making it a perfect retreat for families or longer stays.

The presence of an on-site bar offers a convenient spot for guests to unwind with a drink. Additionally, the lush garden provides a serene oasis for relaxation, inviting guests to take a moment to breathe and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Unico ApartHotel is ideally situated within walking distance of La Petite Beach and a short drive from VIP Beach, offering guests a variety of beach experiences to choose from. For travelers exploring the region, Ioannina Airport is located 96 km away, making the hotel a convenient base for both short and extended visits to Sarandë. Unico ApartHotel promises a memorable stay, where comfort meets convenience in a stunning coastal setting.
