The Sea Scape Apartment is a haven of space and comfort, boasting a generously sized layout that includes a bedroom, a living room with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and toaster, and a bathroom featuring a bidet. The highlight of the apartment is undoubtedly its balcony, offering breathtaking sea views that promise to enchant guests from dawn till dusk. Designed with the guest's well-being in mind, the apartment is both allergy-free and non-smoking, ensuring a comfortable stay for all.

For those looking to enjoy a bite without venturing far, the property hosts a snack bar and has a minimarket on-site for added convenience. Additionally, guests seeking to explore the surrounding area can take advantage of the car rental service available, providing an easy way to discover local attractions such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, located just a few kilometers away.

Situated 153 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Sea Scape Apartment offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views, making it a top choice for travelers to Vlorë looking for a memorable stay by the sea.
