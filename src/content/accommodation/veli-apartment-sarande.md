Upon arrival, guests are greeted with a private entrance, a thoughtful touch that underscores the establishment's commitment to privacy and ease. Inside, the family-friendly rooms are a testament to modern living, with tiled floors paving the way to a realm of amenities. Each unit is equipped with a fully functional kitchen, complete with an oven and dining area, making meal preparation a breeze. Entertainment and connectivity are at guests' fingertips, thanks to the flat-screen TV with satellite channels and air conditioning for those warmer days. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a bidet and hair dryer adds an extra layer of convenience.

For those captivated by urban landscapes, select units offer mesmerizing city views, complemented by the charm of a private balcony - a perfect spot for morning coffees or evening reflections. The thoughtful provision of a desk in all units caters to the needs of business travelers or guests who like to stay connected.

Strategically located, VELI Apartment Sarande is a stone's throw away from the vibrant Saranda City Beach, just 1.4 km away, and the serene Saranda Bay Beach, located 1.8 km from the premises. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 98 km away, making the apartments an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors.

In summary, VELI Apartment Sarande stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, offering a seamless blend of home-like amenities and strategic location, making it an ideal choice for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.
