This apartment is equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine, making it perfect for both short and extended stays. The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven and fridge, complemented by towels and bed linen, adding to the comfort of home away from home. The inclusion of air conditioning and heating ensures a pleasant environment, regardless of the season, while the private bathroom adds an extra layer of privacy.

Located just 2.2 km from the bustling Durres Beach, Studio Apartment Margo serves as a peaceful haven away from the crowds yet close enough to enjoy the vibrant beach life when desired. For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Skanderbeg Square is within a 43 km reach, and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offers an adventurous escape 47 km away. Convenience is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 37 km from the apartment, with a paid airport shuttle service available to ensure a hassle-free arrival and departure.

Studio Apartment Margo stands out as a meticulously appointed accommodation, offering both the tranquility of a beachfront getaway and the comforts of modern living, making it a compelling choice for travelers to Durrës.
