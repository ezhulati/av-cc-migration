The residence itself presents a harmonious blend of comfort and elegance, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that opens up to breathtaking sea views. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom outfitted with a bidet, ensures that guests have all the comforts of home at their fingertips. The outdoor dining area, overlooking lush garden views, provides an idyllic setting for meals under the open sky, while the private entrance to the accommodation enhances the sense of seclusion and privacy.

For those seeking moments of relaxation amidst nature, the property's garden offers a tranquil retreat. Located just 8 km from the Rock of Kavaje and 50 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Villa Pearl of Adriatic serves as an ideal base for exploring the surrounding area. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently 44 km away, making the holiday home an accessible escape for both local and international travelers seeking a peaceful beachfront getaway.
