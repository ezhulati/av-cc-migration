This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its spacious layout that includes two bedrooms, a living room, and a balcony boasting serene garden views. The inclusion of modern amenities such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, and a bathroom with a bidet, ensures guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. Additionally, the provision of bed linen, towels, and an ironing service adds a touch of convenience to the overall experience.

Guests of Tirana Apartment 2 benefit from complimentary private parking, ensuring ease of access for those exploring by car. The availability of free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk service further enhances the stay, providing both connectivity and peace of mind around the clock. It's noteworthy that this property maintains a non-smoking environment, catering to the comfort and well-being of all guests.

For those who prefer to have essentials within easy reach, the apartment houses a minimarket, adding to the convenience factor. Moreover, its proximity to popular attractions such as the Toptani Shopping Centre, National Gallery of Arts Tirana, and Saint Paul Cathedral makes it a strategic spot for culture enthusiasts and shoppers alike.

Situated 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Tirana Apartment 2 is not only accessible but stands as a testament to the blend of convenience, comfort, and culture, making it a compelling choice for visitors to Tirana.
