The property prides itself on its comprehensive amenities, including an outdoor fireplace that adds a touch of warmth to the cool evenings. Guests benefit from the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking, ensuring a hassle-free stay. The chalet itself is a haven of comfort, featuring a spacious bedroom, a welcoming living room with a satellite flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, and a bathroom that caters to both bath and shower preferences. The inclusion of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity, inviting guests to unwind in a space that feels uniquely their own.

For those who relish the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, the on-site coffee shop serves as a delightful spot to start the day. Additionally, the option of packed lunches caters to explorers eager to maximize their time in the great outdoors. Speaking of exploration, Guest House Bashaj is ideally positioned for guests to engage in a variety of activities. Whether it's the tranquility of fishing, the thrill of canoeing, or the challenge of hiking, the surrounding area offers ample opportunities for adventure. Moreover, the chalet's car rental service facilitates further exploration of the scenic locale.

Located 193 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the property also offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring smooth transitions to and from the retreat. Guest House Bashaj stands out as a prime choice for those in pursuit of both relaxation and adventure, encapsulating the essence of a memorable stay.
