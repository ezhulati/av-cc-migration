The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a balcony that offers breathtaking sea views. Designed to accommodate the needs of modern travelers, the apartment features a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven. The convenience of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with the thoughtful provision of towels and bed linen, ensures a comfortable stay. Guests will also appreciate the delightful touch of complimentary fruits, adding a personal touch to their experience.

For those needing to pick up essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises offers added convenience, ensuring everything needed for a relaxing stay is at hand.

Located 47 km from the historical site of Ancient Fanoti and with Ioannina Airport 97 km away, Saranda Nik Apartment is not only accessible but stands as a gateway to exploring the rich history and culture of the region. This apartment is a top recommendation for those seeking a serene getaway with the comforts of home at their fingertips.
