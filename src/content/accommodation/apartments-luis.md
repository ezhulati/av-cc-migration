Each apartment at Apartments Luis is designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a private entrance that ensures a seamless and personalized stay. The interiors boast a modern aesthetic, equipped with air conditioning for those warm summer days, complimentary private parking, and access to free WiFi, ensuring guests stay connected and comfortable throughout their visit.

The living spaces are thoughtfully appointed, with each unit housing a balcony that presents an urban view, a fully equipped kitchenette complete with a fridge for those who prefer to prepare their meals, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. Entertainment and convenience are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV and a washing machine, while the private bathroom with a shower adds an extra layer of privacy. To enhance the homely feel, the apartments are also furnished with fresh bed linen and towels.

For those with an adventurous spirit, Apartments Luis offers proximity to a variety of activities, with windsurfing being a popular choice among guests eager to explore the vibrant marine life of Ksamil. Additionally, the property is conveniently located less than 1 km from Bora Bora Beach and a mere 2.6 km from the historical Butrint National Park, making it an ideal base for guests looking to immerse themselves in the natural and cultural wonders of the region.

With Corfu International Airport 92 km away, Apartments Luis stands as a gateway for international travelers to discover the enchanting allure of Ksamil, making it a top recommendation for those in search of a memorable stay in one of Albania's most picturesque locales.
