The accommodation itself is a testament to comfort and convenience, boasting a balcony that offers serene views of a quiet street, perfect for those seeking a peaceful getaway. Inside, guests will find a well-appointed living space that includes a bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a microwave, catering to all culinary needs. The inclusion of a bathroom with a shower adds to the practicality of the apartment.

For those who might need to pick up essentials or snacks, there's a minimarket on the premises, adding an extra layer of convenience to the stay. Adventure seekers and culture enthusiasts will appreciate the apartment's proximity to the Rock of Kavaje, just 8.1 km away, and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 50 km from the property. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 44 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service to ensure a hassle-free transfer.

Holiday In 817 is not just an accommodation; it's a gateway to exploring the beauty and history of Golem and its surroundings, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.
