Each unit at Ksamil Holiday is thoughtfully designed to enhance the guest experience, featuring terraces or balconies that present breathtaking sea and mountain vistas. The comfort of air conditioning, a cozy seating area, satellite flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen ensure a home-away-from-home atmosphere. Guests can enjoy meals in the open air, thanks to patios equipped with outdoor dining areas that overlook the lake. The convenience of private bathrooms, complete with a bath and hair dryer, underscores the attention to detail in every aspect of the accommodation.

For those looking to explore the area, Ksamil Holiday facilitates both bicycle and car rental services, making it effortless to venture out. Sunset Beach is just 1.3 km away, offering another spot for relaxation and sunbathing, while the historical Butrint National Park is a mere 3.3 km from the property, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich cultural heritage of the region. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, located 93 km away, with paid airport shuttle service available for a seamless travel experience.

Ksamil Holiday stands out as an ideal choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
