Located a mere 400 meters from the iconic Skanderbeg Square, the apartment offers a spacious living area, including a bedroom equipped with a flat-screen TV for those relaxing evenings in. The kitchen doesn't disappoint, being fully equipped with modern appliances such as an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs and preferences. The addition of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to guest privacy and convenience.

For those keen to explore Tirana's rich cultural tapestry, the apartment is ideally situated near several key attractions. The Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and National Museum of History Albania are all within easy reach, promising enriching experiences just a short walk away. Moreover, for guests arriving or departing by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 14 km away, making Tirana Centre ComfortInn Apartments an excellent base for discovering the delights of Tirana.
