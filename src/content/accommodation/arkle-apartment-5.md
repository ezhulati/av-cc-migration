This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its air-conditioned, spacious layout that includes two bedrooms, a living room, and a balcony boasting city views. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances such as a dishwasher and an oven, along with a bathroom featuring a bidet, ensures guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The convenience is further enhanced by the provision of towels and bed linen.

Beyond the comforts of the apartment, guests benefit from practical amenities including an elevator for easy access and a minimarket for all the essentials. The commitment to guest satisfaction is evident with complimentary WiFi available throughout the property, ensuring connectivity and convenience for all guests.

Arkle Apartment 5 is not just about the amenities within but also its proximity to Tirana's most sought-after attractions. Guests find themselves within easy reach of the Et'hem Bey Mosque, Reja - The Cloud, and the House of Leaves, offering a deep dive into the local culture and history. Additionally, the apartment is conveniently located near the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Rinia Park, and the Clock Tower Tirana, making it an ideal base for exploration.

For those traveling to or from the city, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 15 km away, making Arkle Apartment 5 a strategic choice for both short and long-term stays. This apartment stands out as a top recommendation for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Tirana while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed home.
