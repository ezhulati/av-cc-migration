For those arriving by air, the convenience is extended with airport transfer services, and for the explorers eager to roam the picturesque surroundings, a car rental service awaits to facilitate your adventures.

The villa itself is a sanctuary of modern living, featuring a single bedroom complemented by a bathroom, ensuring privacy and tranquility. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with quality bed linen and towels provided. Entertainment and connectivity are guaranteed through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, while the fully equipped kitchen invites guests to feel at home, preparing meals at their leisure. Perhaps the crown jewel of Wood House Berat is its balcony, offering serene mountain views that promise a backdrop of natural beauty to every moment spent there.

This accommodation prides itself on being a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and fresh atmosphere for all guests. Located 116 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Wood House Berat positions itself as an accessible escape for both local and international visitors seeking a blend of modern amenities and natural beauty.
