The villa's design prioritizes space and luxury, featuring three elegantly appointed bedrooms and three modern bathrooms, complete with all necessary linens and towels. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, catering to a wide range of viewing preferences. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen provide everything needed for an in-house dining experience, while the terrace offers breathtaking mountain views, perfect for those who appreciate nature's beauty.

One of the villa's standout features is its allergy-free and non-smoking environment, making it an ideal choice for guests with health sensitivities. The outdoor dining area, overlooking the lush garden, offers a unique opportunity to enjoy meals in a picturesque setting.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures that essentials are always within reach. Additionally, the villa complex includes a picnic area, inviting guests to enjoy leisurely days outdoors surrounded by natural beauty.

Located just 14 km from Skanderbeg Square and 18 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Secret Stone Villa serves as a perfect base for exploring the local attractions. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 25 km away, making travel to and from the villa straightforward for visitors.

With its thoughtful amenities and strategic location, Secret Stone Villa stands out as a top choice for those seeking a tranquil retreat with the convenience of city access.
