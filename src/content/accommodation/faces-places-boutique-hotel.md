Each guest room at Faces & Places Boutique Hotel is a testament to modern comfort, featuring a flat-screen TV with an array of satellite channels, a secure safety deposit box, and a private bathroom stocked with essentials. Select rooms elevate the experience further with private balconies and sweeping city views, ensuring every stay is as unique as the guests themselves. Comfort is paramount, with every room meticulously prepared with quality bed linen and towels.

Mornings at Faces & Places Boutique Hotel begin with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's prime location places guests within easy reach of notable attractions such as the enigmatic Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the historic Tanners' Bridge, and the bustling Toptani Shopping Centre, making it an ideal base for both leisure and adventure.

Accessibility is effortless, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, ensuring smooth transitions from travel to tranquility. Faces & Places Boutique Hotel stands out as a beacon of hospitality, offering a refined stay in the heart of Tirana.
