The apartment is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a dining area perfect for intimate meals. The kitchenette is thoughtfully equipped with a fridge, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, the living room offers a cozy space for relaxation. Guests will appreciate the inclusion of towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable stay. It's noteworthy that PUMI Home is a non-smoking establishment, catering to the comfort and well-being of all guests.

For those traveling with pets, this apartment welcomes furry friends, ensuring no member of the family is left behind. The Rock of Kavaje, located 11 km away, offers a unique natural attraction, while the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, 39 km from the property, provides a glimpse into the country's history. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 33 km away, making PUMI Home an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers seeking a peaceful escape with the convenience of nearby attractions.
