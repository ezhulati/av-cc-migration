The apartment itself is generously sized, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their own meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a relaxing stay.

For those looking to explore Sarandë and its surroundings, Romina's Apartments facilitates this adventure by offering both bicycle and car rental services, providing guests with the freedom to roam at their leisure.

Nature lovers will find Butrint National Park, located 18 km away, a must-visit for its rich history and natural beauty. Additionally, the accommodation is conveniently positioned 98 km from Ioannina Airport, making it accessible for international and domestic travelers.

With free WiFi available throughout the property, guests can stay connected and share their experiences with loved ones. Romina's Apartments emerges as a top selection for those desiring a blend of serenity, accessibility, and the comforts of home while exploring the vibrant city of Sarandë.
