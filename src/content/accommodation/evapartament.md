Guests at EVApartament enjoy the luxury of free WiFi accessible throughout the premises, coupled with the convenience of on-site private parking. The commitment to a non-smoking environment ensures a clean and healthy stay for all guests.

The accommodation itself is a study in modern living, featuring an air-conditioned apartment with a separate bedroom, ensuring privacy and tranquility. The fully equipped kitchen, boasting an oven and toaster, invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV adds an element of leisure, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

For those looking to venture further, the Rock of Kavaje is 45 km away, offering an adventurous day trip, while the Bunk'Art 1 Museum lies just 1.9 km from the apartment, presenting an opportunity to delve into the area's rich history. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is only 16 km away, making EVApartament an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in Tirana.
