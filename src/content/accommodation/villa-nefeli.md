The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort. Boasting a spacious terrace that offers tranquil garden views, the accommodation includes a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a microwave. The inclusion of a modern bathroom with a shower, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of a hotel. The added feature of a private entrance underscores the property's commitment to guest privacy and convenience.

Located 118 km from Ioannina Airport, Villa Nefeli represents an accessible escape for both local and international travelers seeking a peaceful getaway amidst the beauty of Vlorë County.
