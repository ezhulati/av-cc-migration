This apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days. Guests will appreciate the added convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi, ensuring a hassle-free stay. Emphasizing a smoke-free environment, E&G Apartments 1 prioritizes the comfort and well-being of its guests.

Accommodation at E&G Apartments 1 includes a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a bathroom that complements the living space. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, making it easy to unwind after a day of exploration.

For those interested in venturing beyond the beach, Skanderbeg Square is located 47 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich history and culture of the area. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, just 5.7 km from the apartment, presents a unique natural attraction. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 41 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements for guests.

E&G Apartments 1 represents an excellent choice for travelers who value both the tranquility of beachside living and the convenience of nearby attractions.
