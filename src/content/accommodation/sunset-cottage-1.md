The accommodation boasts a tastefully furnished air-conditioned interior, featuring two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with modern appliances such as an oven and kettle. The presence of a bathroom equipped with either a bath or shower, alongside a hairdryer, ensures guests' comfort and convenience. Moreover, the apartment extends its living space outdoors with a terrace that serves as a delightful outdoor dining area, enhancing the overall experience with a touch of alfresco elegance. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to ensuring guest privacy.

For those moments of leisure, the property's garden offers a tranquil retreat, inviting guests to unwind amidst its verdant embrace. Further enriching the stay, Lori Beach is accessible within a short 600-meter stroll, while the historic Butrint National Park awaits exploration just 3 kilometers away. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is located 92 kilometers from Sunset Cottage 1, making it a convenient option for those coming from afar.

In summary, Sunset Cottage 1 stands out as an idyllic haven for guests looking to enjoy the natural beauty and tranquility of Ksamil, backed by thoughtful amenities and a location that puts the best of the area within easy reach.
