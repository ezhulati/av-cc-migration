The highlight of this accommodation is undoubtedly its spacious apartment, which features a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, the apartment includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen comes fully equipped with an oven and a fridge, catering to guests' culinary needs, while the bathroom is fitted with a bidet. Adding to the comfort are the provided towels and bed linen, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience with an added touch of luxury.

For convenience, guests will find a minimarket on the premises, ensuring that essentials are always within reach. Those interested in exploring the local culture and history will appreciate the apartment's proximity to Independence Square and Kuzum Baba, located just a short drive away. Furthermore, for international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is accessible, situated 151 km from the property, making Views on Vlore - Lungomare an ideal base for both domestic and overseas visitors looking to explore the enchanting city of Vlorë.
