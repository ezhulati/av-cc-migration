Located a mere 8.2 km from the iconic Rock of Kavaje and within easy reach of historical landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre, this property serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of the area. Moreover, the bustling heart of the city, Skanderbeg Square, is only 50 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity.

The apartment itself features two cozy bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable stay. Additionally, the apartment offers delightful garden views, adding a touch of tranquility to the ambiance.

For those interested in further exploration, the House of Leaves and Rinia Park are both located approximately 49 km from the apartment, promising engaging cultural experiences. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 46 km away, making travel arrangements a breeze.

5th Floor Apartment Sea View Qerret stands out as a splendid retreat for those looking to enjoy the beauty of Golem and its surroundings, offering both the comforts of home and the excitement of adventure.
