Each apartment at AngelX is thoughtfully designed to ensure guests enjoy a comfortable stay, featuring air-conditioned rooms that come with complimentary private parking and WiFi access. The living spaces are equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a private bathroom that includes a bidet, shower, and hair dryer for convenience. The kitchen, a highlight for those who prefer to prepare their own meals, is fully fitted with a dishwasher, an oven, and a microwave, with the thoughtful addition of fruits to welcome guests upon their arrival. For added convenience, all units are furnished with a fridge.

For those minor necessities or quick snacks, a minimarket on the premises caters to guests' needs without the need to venture far. The property also boasts a garden where guests can unwind and soak in the peaceful surroundings, offering a perfect spot for relaxation after a day of exploring.

AngelX Apartments is ideally situated for guests eager to explore Tirana's rich cultural landscape, with the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, Pyramid of Tirana, and Reja - The Cloud all within close proximity. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 15 km away, making AngelX Apartments an excellent choice for both short and extended stays in the city.
