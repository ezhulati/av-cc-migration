The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain views. Designed to accommodate a variety of guest needs, it includes a bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge. The convenience extends to a bathroom fitted with a bidet, and the provision of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of comfort. For those who appreciate the outdoors, the property also offers an outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals in the fresh air.

For guests looking to explore the area, EDEN Beach Apartment Vlore facilitates this with a car rental service, making local travel both easy and accessible. The apartment's location is ideal for beach lovers, with Vlore Beach just 600 meters away and Vjetër Beach within a short 2.9 km distance. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 153 km away, making EDEN Beach Apartment Vlore a convenient and desirable choice for both short and long-term stays in Vlorë.
