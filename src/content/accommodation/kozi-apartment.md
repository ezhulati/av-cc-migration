The apartment offers a serene retreat with its air-conditioned spaces featuring a bedroom that opens up to a balcony, presenting tranquil garden views. The presence of a fully equipped kitchen adds a touch of home, while entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring a comfortable stay in a smoke-free environment.

For those looking to explore the area on two wheels, Kozi Apartment facilitates this adventure with a bicycle rental service, inviting guests to discover the local landscape at their own pace. Situated a mere 2.5 km from Vjetër Beach, the property also serves as a convenient base for beach enthusiasts.

Cultural landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are within easy reach, located just over 3 km away, making it an ideal choice for travelers keen on immersing themselves in the local heritage. For international visitors, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, connecting Kozi Apartment to the wider world.

In summary, Kozi Apartment stands out as a compelling choice for those seeking a blend of beachfront bliss, comfort, and convenience in Vlorë.
