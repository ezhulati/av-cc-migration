This apartment doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a home away from home. With two cozy bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those homemade meal cravings, and a welcoming living space complete with a flat-screen TV, guests are guaranteed a stay that blends relaxation with the comforts of modern living. The inclusion of air conditioning ensures a cool retreat from the sun-soaked days by the beach.

Adding to the appeal is the apartment's commitment to a smoke-free environment, ensuring a fresh and healthy living space for all guests. The convenience extends to the provision of free private parking, making arrivals and departures as smooth as the gentle sea breeze. The private balcony presents an intimate space for guests to unwind and soak in the tranquil atmosphere of Ksamil.

For those intrigued by the rich tapestry of history and nature, Butrint National Park lies a mere 2.8 km away, offering an easy escape to explore the ancient ruins and lush landscapes. And for travelers venturing from afar, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 92 km away, underscores Klodi Apartment as a strategically positioned haven for both local and international visitors.

In essence, Klodi Apartment stands out as a beacon of comfort, convenience, and serene living in the picturesque setting of Ksamil, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience the best of the Albanian Riviera.
