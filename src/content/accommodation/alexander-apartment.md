Located less than 1 km from the serene Bora Bora Beach and just an 11-minute stroll from the picturesque Ksamil Beach 7, Alexander Apartment positions guests perfectly to explore the best of the area's coastal beauty. The apartment itself boasts two separate bedrooms and two bathrooms, making it an ideal choice for families or groups seeking both privacy and space. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dining area and fridge, allows for easy meal preparation, while the living room is a cozy retreat with its flat-screen TV. The thoughtful addition of fruits as an in-room amenity adds a personal touch to the welcoming atmosphere.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate comforts of the apartment, nearby attractions include the vibrant Ksamil Beach, the relaxing Coco Beach, and the stunning Sunset Beach, each offering a unique experience. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Alexander Apartment a convenient base for both local and overseas visitors. This property stands out as a top recommendation for those seeking to immerse themselves in the beauty and charm of Ksamil.
