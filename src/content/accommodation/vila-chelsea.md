Upon arrival, visitors are greeted by a welcoming 24-hour front desk, ready to assist with any needs, including luggage storage. Each guest room at Vila Chelsea opens up to a private balcony, allowing for moments of quiet reflection or a breath of fresh air.

The accommodations are thoughtfully appointed with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment, and a fridge and kettle for in-room refreshments. The attention to detail extends to the bathrooms, equipped with a bidet, bathrobes, and a refreshing shower, enhancing the overall experience.

For those in pursuit of picturesque views, select rooms offer the added delight of sea vistas, promising a visual treat right from the comfort of one's room. The proximity to Ksamil Beach, less than 1 km away, and the leisurely 15-minute walk to Coco Beach, positions Vila Chelsea as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty of the area.

Situated 94 km from Corfu International Airport, the hotel serves as a convenient gateway for international travelers seeking a peaceful escape in Ksamil. Vila Chelsea is not just a place to stay; it's a serene sanctuary where every detail is curated for an unforgettable experience.
