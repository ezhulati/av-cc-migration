This apartment doesn't just boast a strategic location; it offers a haven of relaxation and convenience. Guests are welcomed into a space featuring two cozy bedrooms, a bathroom, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs. Entertainment and connectivity are ensured with free WiFi throughout the premises and a flat-screen TV for those quiet nights in.

Just 300 meters from the inviting Shëngjin Beach, Alkets Apartment promises an experience that combines the ease of access to stunning landscapes with the comforts of modern living. For those flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 49 km away, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, Alkets Apartment stands out as a top recommendation for those looking to explore Shëngjin's charms while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed home away from home.
