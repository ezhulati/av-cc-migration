A mere 700 meters from both the National Gallery of Arts Tirana and the historic Clock Tower, and a brief journey from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the city. The apartment's proximity to key attractions, including Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, makes it an ideal base for those looking to immerse themselves in the local scene.

This spacious one-bedroom apartment is not just about location; it's a haven of comfort. Guests can enjoy a range of modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven, fridge, washing machine, and stovetop. The added convenience of a private entrance ensures a sense of privacy and exclusivity.

With free WiFi throughout the property and a scenic balcony that invites guests to relax and take in the city's vibrant atmosphere, Amazing View & Location -Central is a non-smoking establishment that promises a memorable stay in Tirana. For those arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 14 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

In summary, Amazing View & Location -Central stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of local charm in the heart of Tirana.
