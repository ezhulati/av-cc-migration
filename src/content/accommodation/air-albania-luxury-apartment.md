This apartment doesn't just promise space; it delivers with two bedrooms, a generous living room, and a balcony that invites guests to breathe in the city's atmosphere from the comfort of their temporary home. The kitchen is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a toaster and a fridge, catering to all culinary needs, whether it's a quick breakfast or a leisurely dinner. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have access to global media and local broadcasts alike. Emphasizing a commitment to comfort, the entire apartment is a smoke-free environment.

Connectivity is never an issue, with complimentary WiFi for all guests and the option for private parking available at a nominal fee, making it effortless for those traveling with vehicles. For those keen to explore, the apartment is conveniently located near cultural landmarks such as the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, the intriguing Pyramid of Tirana, and the interactive art installation, Reja - The Cloud. For international visitors, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a manageable 17 km away, making Air Albania Luxury Apartment an ideal base for both short visits and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
