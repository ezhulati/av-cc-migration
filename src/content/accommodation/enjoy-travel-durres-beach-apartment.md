The apartment itself is a study in comfort and efficiency, featuring a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living area complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a well-equipped kitchenette. The dining area allows guests to enjoy home-cooked meals, while the inclusion of a modern bathroom with a shower and the convenience of a washing machine ensures all the comforts of home are at hand. For those in need of it, towels and linens are available upon request, adding an extra layer of convenience to your stay.

Dining at the Enjoy Travel Durres Beach Apartment is an experience in itself, with an on-site restaurant that serves a variety of meals ranging from traditional dinners to leisurely brunches, not to mention cocktails and high tea. It's a perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploring.

Families will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a babysitting service, allowing parents to enjoy some well-deserved downtime knowing their little ones are in good hands. The apartment complex also boasts a picnic area, offering a delightful opportunity for guests to enjoy al fresco dining amidst the beauty of Durrës.

Situated a mere 42 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just over 6 km from the Rock of Kavaje, the apartment serves as an excellent base for exploring the natural and historical sights of the area. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 33 km away, guests will find both arrival and departure to be a breeze.

Enjoy Travel Durres Beach Apartment stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of local flavor during their stay in Durrës.
