This property distinguishes itself with its stunning lake views, which guests can enjoy from the comfort of a holiday home equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and complimentary WiFi. The thoughtful inclusion of a seating area, desk, and a bathroom ensures a stay that combines both relaxation and functionality. The terrace, adorned with outdoor furniture, offers a tranquil garden view, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventures, while the private entrance to the accommodation underscores a commitment to guest privacy.

Culinary experiences at The Mussel House do not disappoint, with a continental breakfast available to kickstart the day. The convenience of an in-house restaurant and a bar adds to the allure, ensuring that guests have a variety of dining options at their fingertips.

For those with a penchant for the outdoors, the proximity to Butrint National Park, just 5.7 km away, presents an opportunity to immerse in the natural beauty of the area. Additionally, the property is a haven for fishing enthusiasts, offering easy access to nearby spots.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Corfu International Airport located 95 km away, making The Mussel House a compelling choice for both local and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Ksamil.
