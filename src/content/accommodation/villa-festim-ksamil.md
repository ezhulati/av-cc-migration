Each apartment at Villa Festim Ksamil is thoughtfully designed with a cozy seating area, a state-of-the-art flat-screen TV featuring satellite channels, and a private bathroom equipped with plush bathrobes, a bidet, and both a bath and shower. The highlight of each unit is undoubtedly the balcony, which presents an outdoor dining area set against the backdrop of breathtaking mountain scenery. To further enhance the guest experience, the apartments are stocked with fresh bed linen and towels.

Morning risers at Villa Festim Ksamil are greeted with an array of breakfast options, ranging from à la carte to continental, featuring warm dishes, local delicacies, and pancakes. The on-site coffee shop offers a perfect start to the day, while the convenience of a minimarket caters to any additional needs.

For those seeking adventure or relaxation, Villa Festim Ksamil does not disappoint. The property facilitates bike and car rentals, encouraging guests to explore the surrounding area, known for its excellent cycling and fishing spots. Families will appreciate the children's playground and picnic area, ensuring entertainment for all ages.

Located just 1.1 km from Coco Beach and a short drive from the historical Butrint National Park, 3.4 km away, Villa Festim Ksamil offers an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural treasures of the region. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km from the property, making Villa Festim Ksamil a compelling choice for those seeking a memorable stay in Ksamil.
