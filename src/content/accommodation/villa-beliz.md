This property stands out not only for its prime location but also for the amenities it offers. Guests are welcomed into air-conditioned accommodations, ensuring a comfortable retreat from the bustling city life. The convenience of private parking on site, coupled with complimentary WiFi, adds to the seamless experience of staying at Villa Beliz. Emphasizing a smoke-free environment, the guest house ensures a pleasant stay for all guests.

Accommodations at Villa Beliz are thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV, and the provision of towels and bed linen underscores the guest house's attention to detail and commitment to comfort.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and attractions, Villa Beliz does not disappoint. The guest house is a short distance from notable landmarks such as the National Museum of History Albania, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, making it a convenient base for exploration. Moreover, the proximity to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 4.4 km away, offers an opportunity for an unforgettable adventure overlooking the city.

Travelers will appreciate the ease of access to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 13 km away, making Villa Beliz an excellent choice for both short visits and longer stays in Albania's captivating capital.
