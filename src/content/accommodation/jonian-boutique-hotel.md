Security and guest convenience are paramount, with around-the-clock security, efficient luggage storage, and currency exchange services ensuring a worry-free stay. Each air-conditioned unit is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a terrace, alongside a private bathroom featuring a shower. The commitment to guest comfort is evident in the provision of free WiFi across the premises, with select rooms further enhancing the experience with private balconies. The attention to detail extends to the essentials, with quality bed linen and towels readily available in all units.

Mornings at Jonian Boutique Hotel promise a delightful start with a continental breakfast menu that includes favorites like pancakes and cheese, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation.

For those looking to venture beyond the immediate comforts of the hotel, Butrint National Park offers a natural escape just 16 km away, while Ioannina Airport, located 96 km from the property, facilitates ease of travel to and from the hotel. Jonian Boutique Hotel thus positions itself as an ideal base for both leisure and exploration in Sarandë, promising an experience that is as memorable as it is comfortable.
