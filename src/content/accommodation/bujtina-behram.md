Accommodations at Bujtina Behram are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is furnished with a practical desk, while select rooms elevate the experience with enchanting city views. Privacy and convenience are paramount, as evidenced by the inclusion of a private bathroom equipped with a shower, complimentary toiletries, and the added touch of a hairdryer in every unit. Entertainment needs are not overlooked, with a TV provided in each room for guests' enjoyment.

For travelers flying into the region, Kastoria National Airport serves as the nearest gateway, located 71 km from the hotel, making Bujtina Behram an accessible haven for both domestic and international visitors. This hotel stands out as a prime accommodation choice, blending essential amenities with a touch of luxury for a memorable stay in Korçë.
