Each apartment is a testament to thoughtful design, featuring a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views, ensuring guests can soak in the serene ambiance from the privacy of their accommodation. The interiors are equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment, a well-appointed kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their meals, and a private bathroom that includes a shower, slippers, and a hair dryer, catering to the needs of the modern traveler. The provision of bed linen and towels in each unit adds a touch of convenience, making for a hassle-free stay.

Situated a short drive from the tranquil La Petite Beach and the historic Butrint National Park, New Luxury Apartments offers an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural treasures of the region. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 98 km away, making the apartments accessible for both domestic and international visitors.

In summary, New Luxury Apartments stands out as a sophisticated haven for those who appreciate the finer details, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views, making it a top recommendation for an unforgettable stay in Sarandë.
