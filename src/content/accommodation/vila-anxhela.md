Accommodations at Vila Anxhela are thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. Each apartment boasts a balcony that opens up to breathtaking mountain vistas, providing a perfect backdrop for relaxation. The interiors are equipped with air conditioning, ensuring comfort regardless of the weather outside. For entertainment, a satellite flat-screen TV is available, alongside complimentary WiFi, making it easy for guests to stay connected. The convenience of free private parking adds to the hassle-free experience.

The living spaces are furnished with a seating area, where guests can unwind after a day of exploration. The kitchenette, complete with a fridge and stovetop, allows for the preparation of simple meals, offering a taste of home away from home. The private bathroom, stocked with complimentary toiletries, ensures a refreshing start and end to each day. Additionally, every unit features a patio with an outdoor dining area, inviting guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the serene garden views. Soundproofing in all units guarantees a peaceful night's sleep.

For those who enjoy outdoor cooking, barbecue facilities are available, providing an ideal setting for a memorable evening under the stars.

Located just a short distance from the allure of Ksamil Beach 7 and the historical wonders of Butrint National Park, Vila Anxhela serves as a perfect base for exploring the natural and cultural treasures of the area. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is within reach, located 93 km away, making Vila Anxhela an accessible haven for both local and overseas visitors seeking a tranquil escape in Ksamil.
