The apartment itself is a study in spacious luxury, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms. Guests will find every need catered to, from the provision of fresh bed linen and towels to the convenience of a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for those evenings in. The dining area offers a welcoming space for meals, while the fully equipped kitchen invites guests to whip up culinary delights at their leisure. Perhaps the crown jewel of the apartment is its balcony, offering unobstructed sea views that promise to enchant at any time of day.

For those looking to venture further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car presents an opportunity for adventure 46 km away, and the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 500 metres from the property, offering a glimpse into the natural beauty of the area. Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 36 km away, provides convenient access for international travelers, making Modern Sea View Apartment For a Perfect Holiday an ideal choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the call of the sea.
