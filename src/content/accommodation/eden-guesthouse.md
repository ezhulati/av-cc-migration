The property boasts a tranquil garden setting, providing a peaceful retreat amidst the urban landscape. With its commitment to non-smoking accommodations, EDEN GUESTHOUSE ensures a clean and healthy environment for all guests. The convenience of private parking on-site, coupled with complimentary WiFi, adds to the seamless experience offered.

Accommodations at EDEN GUESTHOUSE are notably spacious, featuring a three-bedroom apartment that opens up to a balcony with picturesque garden views. The living space is thoughtfully designed with a flat-screen TV and a comfortable seating area, inviting guests to relax and unwind. The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances, including a dishwasher and an oven, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, two well-appointed bathrooms, complete with a bidet, enhance the comfort of the stay.

For those eager to explore the surroundings, the apartment is conveniently located just 2.4 km from the vibrant Skanderbeg Square and a mere 2.1 km from the iconic Pyramid of Tirana. Adventure seekers will appreciate the proximity to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, situated 7.1 km away, offering an opportunity to witness breathtaking views of the city from above. Further afield, the Rock of Kavaje awaits at a distance of 43 km, promising an intriguing excursion.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures for international guests. EDEN GUESTHOUSE stands out as a premier choice for those desiring a blend of tranquility, convenience, and a touch of adventure in Tirana.
