The apartment boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring a restful night's sleep for all guests. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a microwave, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and an array of kitchenware, invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like.

Adding to the appeal is a charming balcony that offers a space for relaxation and a terrace that provides an ideal spot for soaking up the local atmosphere. The commitment to a non-smoking environment ensures a fresh and pleasant stay for all guests.

For those traveling by air, the convenience of being located just 43 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Sunny City Center Apartment an excellent choice for both short and extended stays in Lezhë.
