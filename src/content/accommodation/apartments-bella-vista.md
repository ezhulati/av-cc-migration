Each air-conditioned apartment at Bella Vista is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring a cable TV for entertainment and a balcony for enjoying the gentle sea breeze. The kitchenette, equipped with a dining area, allows guests the freedom to prepare meals as they wish, while the private bathrooms are fitted with a shower and bidet, adding to the overall sense of privacy and comfort.

For those who prefer not to cook, the on-site restaurant serves up delectable dishes that cater to a variety of tastes, and the bar is the perfect spot for a refreshing drink after a day in the sun.

Strategically located, Bella Vista Apartments is a mere 7.8 km from the Port of Durres, making it an ideal base for exploring the area. Tirana, with its vibrant culture and attractions, is only 42 km away, and for those traveling by air, Tirana Airport is conveniently 38 km from the property.

With its blend of convenience, comfort, and exclusive beach access, Apartments Bella Vista stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay in Durrës.
