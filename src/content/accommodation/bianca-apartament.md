The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom stocked with essential linens and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to all culinary needs, while the balcony presents breathtaking mountain views, adding a touch of tranquility to the stay. Notably, the accommodation ensures guest privacy with a dedicated entrance.

For those keen on exploring the local sights, Bianca Apartament facilitates sightseeing tours and offers a car rental service, making it effortless to venture out. The property's proximity to notable landmarks, including Skanderbeg Square and the Rock of Kavaje, further enhances its appeal to travelers. Additionally, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 42 km away, ensuring easy access for international guests.

In essence, Bianca Apartament is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the beauty and culture of Golem, making it a top choice for discerning travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.
