The apartment itself is a spacious sanctuary, equipped with two bedrooms and a single bathroom, all meticulously designed for comfort and convenience. Guests can enjoy the luxury of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. The balcony, offering breathtaking sea views, adds a touch of tranquility to the stay. Notably, the apartment is soundproof, features a walk-in shower, and is both allergy-free and non-smoking, catering to the needs and preferences of all guests.

Situated in close proximity to popular attractions such as Mali I Robit Beach, Golem Beach, and Qerret Beach, Pine Apartment 2 plus 1 is ideally located for those looking to explore the area's natural beauty. For travelers, the convenience is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 41 km away, making it an accessible option for both local and international visitors.

Pine Apartment 2 plus 1 stands out as a prime choice for those seeking a blend of modern amenities, comfort, and a prime location for their next getaway.
