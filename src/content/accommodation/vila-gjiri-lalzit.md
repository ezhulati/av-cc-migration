The property is well-equipped to cater to the needs of its guests, featuring a 24-hour front desk and ample parking space. The holiday home itself is a cozy haven, boasting a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a living area designed for relaxation. The highlight, however, is the bathroom with a walk-in shower, ensuring a refreshing start to each day.

One of the most captivating aspects of Vila Gjiri Lalzit is its terrace. Here, guests can enjoy breathtaking sea views, complemented by comfortable outdoor furniture, making it an ideal spot for morning coffees or evening reflections. The added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy for guests.

For those looking to unwind, the property houses a bar that promises leisurely evenings. Convenience is further amplified by the presence of a minimarket on-site. Families visiting Vila Gjiri Lalzit will find the outdoor play equipment a delightful addition, ensuring the little ones are entertained, while adults can enjoy the outdoor fireplace and picnic area for memorable days under the sun.

Situated within reach of notable attractions, the holiday home is 47 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and 40 km from the Rock of Kavaje, offering guests a chance to explore the local area. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 28 km away, making Vila Gjiri Lalzit an accessible and appealing choice for both domestic and international visitors seeking a tranquil escape by the sea.
