This generously sized apartment boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their own meals. The inclusion of a modern bathroom adds to the convenience, ensuring a comfortable stay. Entertainment needs are also met with a flat-screen TV, complemented by free WiFi available throughout the property, keeping guests connected during their stay.

For those looking to venture further, the Port of Bar is a scenic 48 km drive away, offering an easy day trip option. Additionally, the proximity to Podgorica Airport, located just 59 km from the apartment, facilitates stress-free travel arrangements for international guests.

This apartment represents an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local culture of Shkodër.
