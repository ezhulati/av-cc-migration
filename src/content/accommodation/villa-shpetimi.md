Guests at Villa Shpetimi are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The property boasts free private parking, ensuring ease of access for those arriving by car, and a 24-hour front desk, providing round-the-clock assistance to meet all guest needs. Complimentary WiFi throughout the premises allows visitors to stay connected and share their experiences with loved ones.

Accommodations at Villa Shpetimi are thoughtfully appointed to ensure a comfortable stay. Select units feature a private bathroom and a balcony, offering guests a peaceful retreat to unwind after a day of exploration. The apartment complex caters to families with spacious family rooms, making it an excellent choice for those traveling with children.

Dining at Villa Shpetimi is a delightful experience, with a buffet and continental breakfast served each morning. The offerings include a variety of fruits, juices, and cheeses to start the day on a refreshing note. The on-site restaurant, specializing in Greek cuisine, welcomes guests for lunch and dinner, promising a culinary journey with every meal.

For those looking to explore the local area, sightseeing tours are readily available, providing an opportunity to discover the rich history and natural beauty surrounding the property. Butrint National Park, a site of significant archaeological interest, is just a short drive away, offering a fascinating excursion for history enthusiasts.

Located 92 km from Corfu International Airport, Villa Shpetimi facilitates travel arrangements with a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless journey to and from the property.

In summary, Villa Shpetimi stands out as a premier choice for travelers to Ksamil, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and culinary delights, all set against the backdrop of Albania's stunning coastal landscape.
