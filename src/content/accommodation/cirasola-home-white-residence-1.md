The apartment itself is a haven of space and comfort, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, two modern bathrooms, and the convenience of bed linen and towels. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, ensuring guests are never short of viewing options. The heart of this residence is undoubtedly its fully equipped kitchen and dining area, complemented by an outdoor dining space on the terrace. Here, guests can savor their meals against the backdrop of breathtaking sea and mountain views, adding a touch of magic to every dining experience.

Designed with the discerning traveler in mind, Cirasola Home - White Residence 1 is both non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful and comfortable stay. The allure of Butrint National Park, located just 15 km away, invites guests to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and historical richness of the area. Furthermore, with Ioannina Airport 95 km away, the apartment serves as a convenient gateway for international travelers.

In summary, Cirasola Home - White Residence 1 offers an exceptional blend of location, luxury, and leisure, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience the best of Sarandë in a home-like setting.
