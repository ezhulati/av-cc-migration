The apartment is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests will find themselves in an air-conditioned space that features a cozy bedroom, a sleek flat-screen TV for entertainment, a dining area for intimate meals, and a living room designed for relaxation. The kitchen does not disappoint, being fully stocked with a fridge and all the essentials needed for a home-cooked meal. While the property maintains a non-smoking policy to ensure the comfort and well-being of all guests, it goes the extra mile by offering towels and linens for a nominal fee, adding a touch of convenience to the overall experience.

Situated 43 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just 4.3 km from the Rock of Kavaje, Garden View! Seaside Apartment is ideally located for those looking to explore the natural beauty and attractions of the area. For travelers flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 33 km away, making access to this coastal retreat both easy and stress-free. While private parking is available for those with vehicles, it comes at an additional charge, ensuring peace of mind for guests wishing to explore the region at their leisure.

In summary, Garden View! Seaside Apartment offers a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and scenic beauty, making it a standout option for those wishing to experience the best of Durrës from a tranquil, beachfront location.
