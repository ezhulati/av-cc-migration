The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, boasting a balcony that offers serene garden views, a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a well-equipped kitchen. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, ensuring relaxation is on the agenda after a day of exploration. The inclusion of air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and private parking adds layers of ease to the guest experience, all within a smoke-free environment.

For those looking to stock up on essentials or prepare a beach picnic, a supermarket is conveniently located near the property. Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to local landmarks, with Kuzum Baba and Independence Square just a short drive away, offering a glimpse into the rich history and vibrant spirit of Vlorë.

Situated 152 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Alimaj Apartments Marina represents an accessible escape for both domestic and international travelers, promising a stay that combines the comforts of home with the allure of the Albanian Riviera.
