The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, boasting two cozy bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with modern amenities, and the convenience of bed linen and towels. The living space is designed for relaxation and entertainment, featuring a flat-screen TV and a dining area that invites guests to unwind. The kitchen is fully equipped, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals, while the balcony offers tranquil garden views, setting the stage for peaceful mornings or evenings. The air-conditioned unit includes a shower and a dressing room, enhancing the comfort of the stay. Additionally, an outdoor dining area is available, perfect for those who enjoy al fresco dining.

For those keen on exploring the local area, Beach Apartments Durres serves as an ideal base. The apartment is not only a haven for relaxation but also a gateway to engaging activities such as fishing in the surrounding waters of Durrës.

Significant landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are within accessible distances, 38 km and 42 km respectively, offering guests the opportunity to delve into the rich cultural and natural heritage of the region. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 33 km away, ensures convenient travel arrangements for international guests.

Beach Apartments Durres stands as a compelling choice for travelers seeking a harmonious blend of modern amenities, comfort, and strategic location, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Durrës.
