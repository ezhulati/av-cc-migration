Shtepi Pushimi me Qera stands out for its strategic position, just 2.5 km from the bustling Golem Beach and within a reasonable distance from notable landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 41 km and 45 km away respectively. The historic Durres Amphitheatre and the intriguing House of Leaves are also easily accessible, promising a rich cultural experience.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air-conditioned spaces thoughtfully designed for relaxation. Guests are welcomed into a space that boasts a fully equipped kitchen, a cozy bedroom, and a bathroom, all complemented by a balcony that offers serene garden views. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

For those keen on exploring the local area, the Rock of Kavaje is just 1.5 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's natural beauty. Additionally, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located 42 km from the apartment, presenting an opportunity for a deeper dive into Albania's historical narrative.

Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, located 36 km from the apartment, making Shtepi Pushimi me Qera an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers looking for a memorable stay in Durrës.
