The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting a balcony that offers unobstructed sea views. Guests will find themselves at home with two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and the modern comforts of a flat-screen TV. The kitchen is fully equipped, featuring a dishwasher and an oven, ready to cater to any culinary desire. The two bathrooms, complete with a bidet, add a touch of convenience, while the option to request towels and linens is available for those seeking extra comfort. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to privacy and exclusivity.

For those keen to explore, Kuzum Baba is just 3 km away, offering a cultural excursion, while Independence Square is slightly further at 3.3 km, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local ambiance. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making ANISA Vlore an accessible retreat for both local and global visitors seeking a tranquil escape by the sea.
