Guests are welcomed into a beautifully appointed apartment that boasts a harmonious blend of comfort and style. The space is thoughtfully designed with a cozy bedroom, a well-equipped kitchenette featuring a dishwasher and an oven, and a comfortable seating area perfect for unwinding. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a modern bathroom with a bidet adds to the apartment's appeal. Moreover, the provision of towels and bed linen ensures a hassle-free stay.

One of the apartment's highlights is its terrace, offering guests a splendid vantage point to soak in the stunning garden views and the captivating sunset over the sea. The ambiance of the property is further enhanced by its commitment to a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and fresh setting for all guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square and the Rock of Kavaje presents an excellent opportunity for day trips. Additionally, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 41 km away, making Blue Apartment Golem Sunset an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers.

In summary, Blue Apartment Golem Sunset provides a perfect blend of scenic beauty, comfort, and convenience, making it an ideal choice for those in search of a peaceful beachfront getaway.
