The inclusion of bed linen and towels in the units underscores the guest house's commitment to providing a home-away-from-home experience.

Whether it's the allure of historical sites or the call of the majestic mountains, this guest house offers a welcoming and serene haven for travelers venturing into the heart of Shkodër.
