Guests at Hotel-Restorant Pepushaj are treated to well-appointed rooms that come with the convenience of air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable stay regardless of the weather. The provision of complimentary private parking and WiFi adds to the hassle-free experience, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and connectivity.

The attention to detail in each unit is evident, with amenities such as a wardrobe for personal belongings. Select rooms elevate the comfort level further with a shared bathroom equipped with a bidet, slippers, a hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring guests feel pampered and well-cared for. The inclusion of bed linen and towels in the units underscores the guest house's commitment to providing a home-away-from-home experience.

Situated merely 44 km from the Millennium Bridge and with Podgorica Airport within easy reach, Hotel-Restorant Pepushaj serves as a perfect base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural wonders of the region. Whether it's the allure of historical sites or the call of the majestic mountains, this guest house offers a welcoming and serene haven for travelers venturing into the heart of Shkodër.
