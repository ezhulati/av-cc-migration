Each apartment within Portafortuna is a testament to modern living, equipped with air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. The living area, anchored by a sleek flat-screen satellite TV, invites guests to unwind in style. The convenience of an equipped kitchenette and a dining table ensures that meals can be prepared and enjoyed with ease. The attention to detail extends to the bathrooms, where guests are treated to a refreshing shower experience, complemented by complimentary toiletries.

Beyond the confines of its apartments, Portafortuna enhances the guest experience with an on-site restaurant and bar, perfect for those evenings when local cuisine calls. For everyday essentials, a grocery shop is just a short stroll away. Adventure seekers will find Butrint National Park, located 10 km from the property, an irresistible draw with its rich tapestry of history and nature.

Connectivity is a breeze, with the Ferry Port offering lines to Corfu just 3 km away, and Corfu Airport within a manageable distance of 38 km. The convenience of free private parking on-site is the cherry on top for travelers arriving by car.

In summary, Portafortuna Apartments emerges as a compelling choice for those in pursuit of a blend of self-sufficiency, comfort, and accessibility, all set against the picturesque backdrop of Sarandë's coastal charm.
