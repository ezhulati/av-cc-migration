The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms that promise restful nights. The living space is anchored by a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. An equipped kitchen offers the freedom to craft meals at any time of day, while the inclusion of a bathroom equipped with either a bath or shower adds an extra layer of comfort to the stay.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, a terrace invites guests to bask in the serene atmosphere, and a garden provides a tranquil escape, offering a perfect spot for relaxation.

Strategically located, Lume Apartments Orikum places guests within easy reach of the area's most sought-after beaches. Baro Beach lies just 2.2 km away, offering a sandy retreat, while Nettuno Beach and Orikum Beach are merely 1.6 km and 1.7 km away, respectively, promising days filled with sun-soaked leisure. For those intrigued by historical sites, Kuzum Baba is a mere 23 km from the property, offering a glimpse into the region's rich past.

For travelers flying in, the convenience is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 168 km from the apartments, making Lume Apartments Orikum an accessible haven for both domestic and international visitors.
