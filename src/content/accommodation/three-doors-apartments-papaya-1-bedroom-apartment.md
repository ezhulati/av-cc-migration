The Papaya apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest; it ensures a secure and personalized experience with full-day security and private check-in and check-out services. Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy complimentary WiFi access throughout their stay.

Designed with comfort in mind, the apartment features a cozy bedroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and air conditioning to ensure a pleasant environment regardless of the weather. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home, making it an ideal retreat after a day of exploration.

For those moments when you need something quick and convenient, a minimarket on the premises caters to your basic needs without having to step out.

The location of the Papaya apartment is unbeatable for those eager to dive into the local culture and history. Attractions such as the House of Leaves, Rinia Park, and the Clock Tower of Tirana are within close proximity, inviting guests to discover the city's rich past and lively present. Moreover, the Et'hem Bey Mosque, a significant cultural landmark, is just a stone's throw away.

For travelers flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 14 km journey, making the Three Doors Apartments' Papaya 1-bedroom apartment a convenient and desirable choice for your next visit to Tirana.
