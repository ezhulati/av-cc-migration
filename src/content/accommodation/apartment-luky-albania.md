This beachfront gem is not just about its stunning location; it also excels in providing a seamless stay experience with amenities such as complimentary WiFi, an elevator for easy access, and financial services including an ATM and currency exchange right on the premises. The commitment to guest convenience is evident.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It houses a bedroom, a living room, and a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances like an oven and a kettle, ensuring guests feel right at home. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet and shower, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the thoughtful touches designed to enhance the stay experience. Importantly, the property maintains a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and fresh atmosphere for all guests.

For those looking to explore the local area or venture further afield, the apartment facilitates this with a car rental service. Additionally, the convenience of having a minimarket on-site cannot be overstated, allowing guests to easily procure essentials without having to leave the comfort of the apartment complex.

Located a short drive from the serene Qerret Beach and the historic Skanderbeg Square, Apartment Luky Albania offers a strategic base for exploring the rich cultural and natural attractions of the region. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 40 km away, making the apartment readily accessible for international travelers.

In summary, Apartment Luky Albania presents an inviting option for those looking to enjoy the beauty of Albania's coast, offering a harmonious blend of location, comfort, and convenience.
