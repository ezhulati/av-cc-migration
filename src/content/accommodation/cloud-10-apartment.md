The apartment itself is a study in spaciousness and comfort, featuring a single bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring that guests have access to their favorite shows and movies at their leisure. The kitchenette, complete with an oven and fridge, invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like them, while the inclusion of a washing machine adds an extra layer of convenience. The bathroom, equipped with a shower, is complemented by the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

Located a mere 3 km from Independence Square, Cloud 10 Apartment offers a perfect base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë. For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making the apartment a worthy consideration for both local and international visitors seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to the beach.
