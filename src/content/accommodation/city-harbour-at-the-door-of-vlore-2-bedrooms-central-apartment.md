This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a living experience. Guests are treated to a spacious layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. The inclusion of modern amenities such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxuries of a holiday. The convenience of having a lift, a minimarket on the premises, and complimentary WiFi throughout the property adds to the seamless experience.

The outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy meals with a view, making every dining experience memorable. With the property being a non-smoking zone, cleanliness and freshness are guaranteed, ensuring a pleasant environment for all guests.

Located 2.9 km away from the serene Ri Beach and with the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 150 km away, the City & Harbour at the Door of Vlorë 2 Bedrooms Central Apartment is not just a place to stay but a gateway to exploring the beauty and culture of Vlorë. Whether it's the allure of the beaches or the charm of the city, this apartment serves as the perfect base for an unforgettable adventure in Albania.
