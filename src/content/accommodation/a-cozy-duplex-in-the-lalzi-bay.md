The villa itself is a haven of comfort, boasting a terrace that offers serene garden views, three well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven. The presence of two bathrooms, each with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the property's attention to guests' comfort and convenience. Adding to the sense of privacy and security, the villa features a private entrance and is safeguarded by round-the-clock security.

Culinary delights await at the villa's family-friendly restaurant, where guests can savor American cuisine for both lunch and dinner, promising a delightful dining experience without having to leave the comfort of this tranquil oasis.

For families, "A Cozy Duplex in the Lalzi Bay" is a paradise, equipped with a children's playground and a barbecue area, ensuring entertainment for all ages. Whether it's building sandcastles on the beach or exploring the nearby attractions such as the Rock of Kavaje and the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, there's no shortage of activities to fill your days.

Situated a mere 29 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, this duplex offers an accessible escape to the beauty of Albania's coast. "A Cozy Duplex in the Lalzi Bay" is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises unforgettable memories for those who visit.
