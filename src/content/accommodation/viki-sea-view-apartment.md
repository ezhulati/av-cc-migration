The apartment boasts a spacious layout, complete with air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It features a well-appointed terrace that promises serene mornings and tranquil evenings overlooking the sea. The inclusion of free WiFi ensures that guests remain connected, whether for leisure or work.

Accommodation at Viki Sea View Apartment includes a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen ready to handle any culinary endeavor with a dishwasher and an oven, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. The presence of a flat-screen TV adds a touch of modernity, while the bathroom, equipped with a bidet, underscores the apartment's attention to comfort and convenience. Towels and bed linen, provided for guests, add an extra layer of ease to the stay.

For those who enjoy dining al fresco, the property features an outdoor dining area, inviting guests to savor their meals with a view. Additionally, the convenience of having a bar on-site means refreshments are never far away.

Located a brief drive from Golem Beach and within reasonable distance from the historic Skanderbeg Square, Viki Sea View Apartment serves as a perfect base for exploring the rich offerings of the area. For international travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 36 km away, makes arrivals and departures a breeze.

Viki Sea View Apartment stands out as a compelling choice for those in search of a serene beachfront getaway that doesn't compromise on the comforts of home.
