Guests at Old Town Inn are treated to well-appointed, air-conditioned rooms that promise comfort and convenience. Each room is equipped with essential amenities including a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a balcony. The private bathrooms, featuring showers, add an extra layer of privacy and relaxation to the guest experience.

For those looking to explore the spiritual side of Pogradec, the Monastery Saint Naum is a mere 8.4 km away, while the Saint Nicholas Church is located 19 km from the inn. Travelers will find the nearest airport, Ohrid Airport, 47 km away, making Old Town Inn an accessible choice for both domestic and international visitors.

Old Town Inn invites guests into a shared lounge, a communal space designed for relaxation and socializing, emphasizing the property's commitment to creating a welcoming atmosphere. This non-smoking inn not only promises a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of Pogradec and its surroundings.
