The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting three generously sized bedrooms, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to every culinary need with an oven and microwave. The inclusion of a washing machine and two well-appointed bathrooms, one featuring a bidet, adds to the convenience, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience in a smoke-free environment.

For those looking to explore the local area or ensure their pantry is stocked without venturing far, the apartment complex offers an on-site minimarket. Additionally, guests looking to explore Tirana's sights can take advantage of the car rental service directly from the apartment, making visits to notable attractions such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, and the Grand Park of Tirana effortlessly accessible.

Situated just 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Miku Apartment also provides a paid shuttle service, ensuring smooth transitions to and from the accommodation. This property stands out not only for its prime location and comprehensive amenities but also for its commitment to providing a seamless and enriching stay for all who walk through its doors.
