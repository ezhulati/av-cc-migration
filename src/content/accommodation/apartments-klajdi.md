The accommodations at Apartments Klajdi are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned spaces that boast terraces with mountain vistas. Each unit is equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and a hairdryer, alongside a balcony that offers an outdoor dining area with garden views, adds to the appeal. Guests can also expect the convenience of bed linen and towels in every unit.

The 24-hour front desk, shared kitchen facilities, and currency exchange service underscore the property's commitment to guest satisfaction. For those eager to explore the surroundings, Apartments Klajdi does not disappoint. The property is ideally located for guests interested in diving, fishing, and hiking, with car rental services available for those looking to venture further afield.

Notably, Saranda Bay Beach is within easy reach, located just 1.9 km away, while the historical Butrint National Park is 17 km from the property, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 97 km away.

In summary, Apartments Klajdi stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those wishing to explore Sarandë's natural beauty and cultural landmarks, offering a comfortable and convenient base for all types of travelers.
