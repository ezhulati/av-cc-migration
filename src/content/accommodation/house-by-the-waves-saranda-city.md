The property itself is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring air conditioning and complimentary WiFi that extends throughout the premises, ensuring guests stay connected and comfortable during their stay. The holiday home boasts a thoughtful layout, comprising 2 well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal retreat for families or groups seeking a blend of relaxation and home-like amenities.

Guests will appreciate the non-smoking environment, which underscores the property's commitment to providing a clean and healthy stay. For those intrigued by cultural exploration, the Butrint National Park, a site of immense historical significance, is merely 17 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity to immerse oneself in the rich heritage of the region.

Accessibility is further enhanced by the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 97 km from House by the Waves-Saranda City, making it a convenient option for international travelers. This holiday home not only promises a memorable stay by the picturesque waves of Sarandë but also serves as a gateway to the enchanting beauty and cultural depth of the surrounding area.
