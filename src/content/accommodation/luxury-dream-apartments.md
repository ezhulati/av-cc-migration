The apartments themselves are a testament to modern living, equipped with air conditioning, comfortable seating areas, and flat-screen TVs complete with streaming services. The inclusion of a fully outfitted kitchen, dining area, and essential appliances like an oven, microwave, and toaster, along with a kettle, ensures that every culinary need is met with ease. Safety and comfort are paramount, with each unit boasting a safety deposit box and a private bathroom featuring a walk-in shower, hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries. The provision of bed linen and towels adds a touch of home.

Dining at Luxury Dream Apartments is an adventure in itself, with a variety of breakfast options available each morning, including local specialties, pancakes, and fruits. The on-site restaurant welcomes guests for dinner, lunch, brunch, and cocktails, promising a family-friendly atmosphere that caters to every palate.

For those eager to explore, the property is ideally situated within reach of sightseeing tours, and adventurous guests can try their hand at fishing in the nearby surroundings. The convenience of a car rental service arranged by the apartments enhances the exploratory experience.

Located just 1.5 km from Saranda City Beach and 1.7 km from Saranda Bay Beach, Luxury Dream Apartments stands as a gateway to the beauty of the region. With Ioannina Airport 98 km away, the property also offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless travel experience.

Luxury Dream Apartments not only promises a stay filled with comfort and convenience but also serves as a perfect base for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and adventure of Sarande.
