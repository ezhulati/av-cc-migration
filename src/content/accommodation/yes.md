The hotel boasts a meticulously designed apartment that caters to the needs of modern travelers. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with the apartment's air-conditioned spaces, featuring a cozy bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, an equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their own meals, and a bathroom complete with a bath and hair dryer. The addition of a private entrance ensures an exclusive and undisturbed stay.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy complimentary WiFi throughout their stay. For those arriving by car, private parking is available, ensuring a hassle-free experience from arrival to departure.

Strategically positioned, Yes Hotel is within walking distance of several of Tirana's must-see attractions, including the House of Leaves, Rinia Park, and the Clock Tower Tirana. For guests looking to venture further, the Rock of Kavaje is an accessible 43 km drive. The Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 15 km away, adds to the convenience, making Yes Hotel an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers aiming to discover the charm and vibrancy of Tirana.
