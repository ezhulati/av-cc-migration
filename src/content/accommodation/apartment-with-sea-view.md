This one-bedroom haven is equipped with modern amenities designed for the discerning traveler. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi, a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine for longer stays. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped with a dishwasher and oven, making self-catering a breeze. To add to the comfort, the apartment is furnished with towels and bed linen, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

Located just 16 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park, this property offers an ideal base for explorers keen to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and historical richness of the area. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is within a manageable distance, 96 km away, making Apartment with Sea View a prime choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a blend of beachside bliss and cultural exploration in Sarandë.
