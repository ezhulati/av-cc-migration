The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious balcony that offers breathtaking sea views. It is perfectly equipped for both short and long stays, featuring a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen fitted with a microwave and fridge. The inclusion of air conditioning and a private bathroom with a shower adds to the comfort of guests.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy complimentary WiFi access throughout their stay. The convenience of a lift and the exclusivity of private check-in and check-out services underscore the property's commitment to guest satisfaction. For those needing essentials or quick snacks, a minimarket on the premises caters to these needs efficiently.

Situated a mere 7.1 km from the Rock of Kavaje and within easy reach of the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Wonderful 641 serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of the area. Moreover, its proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 32 km away, complemented by the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, ensures seamless travel arrangements.

In summary, Wonderful 641 is not just an accommodation but a gateway to experiencing the vibrant city of Durrës, offering guests a blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views.
