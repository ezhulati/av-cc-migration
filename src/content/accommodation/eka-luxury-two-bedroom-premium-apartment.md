The apartment itself is a haven of luxury and tranquility, boasting two elegantly furnished bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, ensuring ample space and privacy for all guests. Attention to detail is evident in the provision of quality bed linen, towels, and the inclusion of a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to all culinary needs, while the balcony offers breathtaking sea views, perfect for unwinding.

Designed with the guest's comfort in mind, the apartment features soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life. Additionally, the property is allergy-free and maintains a strict non-smoking policy, catering to the health and well-being of all guests.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises allows for easy access to essentials. The apartment's location also provides easy access to notable attractions, including the Rock of Kavaje and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha. For those looking to explore further, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 33 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for seamless travel arrangements.

EKA Luxury Two-Bedroom Premium Apartment not only promises a stay marked by luxury and convenience but also provides a secure environment with full-day security and a currency exchange service, ensuring a worry-free and enjoyable experience for all guests.
