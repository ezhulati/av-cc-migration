The apartment itself is a study in comfort and convenience, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It houses two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a kitchen equipped with modern amenities including an oven and kettle, ensuring guests feel right at home. The presence of two well-appointed bathrooms, complete with a bidet and hairdryer, alongside the provision of towels and bed linen, adds a touch of thoughtfulness to the stay. Moreover, guests can enjoy serene inner courtyard views, a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life.

For those needing essentials or a quick snack, the convenience of an on-site minimarket is a notable perk. The location is also strategically positioned for exploring, with the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car located 42 km away, offering breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, a mere 4.9 km from the apartment, promises an adventure for nature lovers.

Situated 32 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Apartment by the Adriatic Sea ensures seamless travel arrangements with its paid airport shuttle service, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and exploration in Durrës.
