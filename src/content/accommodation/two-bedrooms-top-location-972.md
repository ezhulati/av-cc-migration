The apartment itself is a haven of space and tranquility, boasting a balcony that offers views of a quiet street, ensuring guests can enjoy peace and privacy. With two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen that comes complete with an oven and a fridge, guests have everything they need at their fingertips. The inclusion of air conditioning and a private bathroom with a shower adds to the comfort of the stay.

What sets Two Bedrooms, Top Location 972 apart is not just its facilities but also the thoughtful services provided. The property ensures a seamless experience with private check-in and check-out, and the convenience of a minimarket on-site. Complimentary WiFi throughout the property allows guests to stay connected, while the non-smoking environment ensures a pleasant stay for all.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate area, the Rock of Kavaje is an 8.5 km drive away, and the historic Durres Amphitheatre is within 19 km, making for easy day trips. Additionally, the property is located 50 km from the iconic Skanderbeg Square, offering a perfect blend of beachside serenity and cultural exploration.

Travelers arriving by air will find the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport conveniently located 44 km away, with the property offering a paid airport shuttle service for a hassle-free transfer.

Two Bedrooms, Top Location 972 represents an ideal choice for those in search of a comfortable, well-equipped stay in Golem, combining the convenience of modern amenities with the charm of its picturesque surroundings.
