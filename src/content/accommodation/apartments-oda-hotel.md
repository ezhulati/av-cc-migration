Each apartment within this complex is a testament to thoughtful design, boasting air-conditioned spaces that open up to balconies or terraces with enchanting garden views. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of a flat-screen TV, a well-equipped kitchen, and a private bathroom complete with a bidet, hairdryer, and complimentary toiletries. The inclusion of bed linen and towels in every unit adds a touch of convenience, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

Beyond the confines of these well-appointed accommodations, guests are invited to unwind in the tranquil garden, a perfect spot for relaxation after a day of exploration. The proximity to Rana e Hedhun Beach, just 2 km away, offers an easy escape to one of the area's natural wonders, while the historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra, 30 km distant, beckons history enthusiasts for a visit.

For those concerned about logistics, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 83 km from the hotel, making Apartments Oda Hotel an accessible haven for both local and international travelers. With its combination of strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and serene ambiance, Apartments Oda Hotel stands out as a distinguished option for those seeking a memorable stay in Velipojë.
