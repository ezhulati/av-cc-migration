This air-conditioned haven boasts a welcoming balcony that opens up to tranquil garden views, providing a peaceful retreat after a day of adventure. The interior is thoughtfully designed with two cozy bedrooms, a spacious living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a microwave, catering to all culinary needs. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with a bath, towels, and bed linen, adds to the convenience, ensuring a comfortable stay.

Privacy is paramount at AG Beach Apartment, with a private entrance that guarantees guests their own space and freedom. The property prides itself on being a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and healthy atmosphere for all guests.

For those intrigued by history and culture, the Rock of Kavaje and the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha are within easy reach, offering a glimpse into the region's rich past. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 39 km away, provides hassle-free travel arrangements for international visitors.

AG Beach Apartment stands out not only for its prime location and the array of amenities but also for offering the luxury of complimentary WiFi and private parking on site, making it a top pick for travelers looking for a seamless and enjoyable stay in Golem.
