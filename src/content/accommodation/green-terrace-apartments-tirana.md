The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living and modern design, featuring four well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a terrace that presents captivating city views. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven, and four bathrooms with walk-in showers, underscores the property's commitment to providing a comfortable and self-sufficient living space. Moreover, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of thoughtful hospitality. Designed as an allergy-free and non-smoking environment, it caters to the well-being and comfort of all guests.

For those moments of relaxation, the shared lounge area serves as a perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploring Tirana's attractions. Speaking of which, the apartment's location is unbeatably convenient, lying in close proximity to notable landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, making Green Terrace Apartments Tirana an accessible and appealing choice for both short and long-term stays.

In essence, Green Terrace Apartments Tirana offers a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and connectivity, making it an excellent accommodation choice for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the cultural tapestry of Tirana.
