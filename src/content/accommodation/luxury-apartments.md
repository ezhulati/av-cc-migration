A mere 2.2 kilometers from the historic Kuzum Baba and just under 2 kilometers from the bustling Independence Square, Luxury Apartments positions guests perfectly to explore the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë. The accommodations themselves are thoughtfully designed, featuring two cozy bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their own meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, making it easy for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

For travelers flying in, the convenience of being 150 kilometers from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal, making Luxury Apartments a prime choice for both domestic and international visitors looking for a blend of comfort, location, and amenities in Vlorë.
