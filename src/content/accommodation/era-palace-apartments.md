The accommodations at Era Palace are designed with families in mind, featuring spacious family rooms that promise a comfortable stay. Select units elevate the experience further, offering balconies where guests can unwind and seating areas equipped with flat-screen TVs for entertainment, all while enjoying the benefits of air conditioning and heating. For those with sensitivity concerns, allergy-free options are available, ensuring a comfortable environment for every guest.

For guests interested in exploring the local culture and history, Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are both 16 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich heritage of the area.
