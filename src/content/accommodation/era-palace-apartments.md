The accommodations at Era Palace are designed with families in mind, featuring spacious family rooms that promise a comfortable stay. Select units elevate the experience further, offering balconies where guests can unwind and seating areas equipped with flat-screen TVs for entertainment, all while enjoying the benefits of air conditioning and heating. For those with sensitivity concerns, allergy-free options are available, ensuring a comfortable environment for every guest.

Strategically located, the apartment is a short stroll from the vibrant Al Breeze Beach, just 400 meters away, and the serene Nettuno Beach, located 1.2 km from the premises. For guests interested in exploring the local culture and history, Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are both 16 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich heritage of the area.

For travelers flying in, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 165 km from Era Palace Apartments, makes this property an accessible retreat for both domestic and international visitors.
