Elevating the guest experience, the apartment is equipped with modern amenities including complimentary WiFi, a television for entertainment, a washing machine for longer stays, and a kitchenette complete with a fridge and kitchenware, ensuring all the comforts of home are at your fingertips.

For those looking to explore local culinary delights, a variety of dining options are conveniently located near the property, offering a taste of the local cuisine without venturing far.

Positioned just 1.8 km from the serene Saranda Bay Beach and a scenic drive from the historic Butrint National Park, the apartment serves as an ideal base for guests eager to explore the natural and cultural treasures of the area. With the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, 98 km away, planning your arrival and departure is a breeze.

"Have the Best Holidays in Our Apartment" not only promises a stay that is as comfortable and convenient as it is memorable but also provides easy access to lifts, a minimarket, and currency exchange services on-site, ensuring a hassle-free holiday experience in the enchanting city of Sarandë.
