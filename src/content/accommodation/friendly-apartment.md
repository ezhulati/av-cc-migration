The apartment itself is a haven of relaxation and convenience. Guests are welcomed into a spacious 2-bedroom setup, complete with a cozy living room that boasts a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped with modern appliances including an oven and a microwave. The inclusion of a bathroom stocked with bathrobes, alongside the thoughtful touch of complimentary fruits, chocolates, or cookies, ensures a comfortable stay. Moreover, the property prides itself on being allergy-free, catering to the needs of every guest.

For leisure, the apartment offers a bar and lounge area perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The convenience of a grocery delivery service and an on-site minimarket further enhances the guest experience, making every stay as effortless as possible. Additionally, a car rental service is available for those looking to venture further afield.

Located a mere 58 km from Podgorica Airport, Friendly Apartment not only promises a memorable stay in Shkodër but also ensures ease of access for international travelers. This property is an excellent choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of local charm.
