The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom designed for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring guests have access to their favorite shows and movies. The kitchen doesn't skimp on amenities either; it's fully equipped with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, catering to every culinary need. Perhaps one of the most delightful features is the outdoor dining area, where guests can savor their meals against the backdrop of captivating sea views.

Privacy and comfort are paramount at Alpha Lux Apartment, underscored by the inclusion of a private entrance and soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of daily life. For those venturing from afar, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km away, making the apartment accessible for international and domestic travelers alike. In essence, Alpha Lux Apartment stands out as a sophisticated base for exploring the enchanting city of Sarandë and its surrounding natural wonders.
