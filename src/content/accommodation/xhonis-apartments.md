The accommodation boasts a well-appointed two-bedroom apartment, featuring a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and stovetop, ensuring guests feel right at home. The living space is designed for relaxation, with a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The apartment is thoughtfully soundproofed, guaranteeing a peaceful night's sleep. Additionally, conveniences such as bed linen, towels, and laundry service are meticulously provided, enhancing the overall stay experience.

For those eager to explore the surroundings, Coco Beach awaits just 1 km away, offering a splendid beach experience. Meanwhile, the historical allure of Butrint National Park is merely 3.4 km from the doorstep, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich cultural heritage of the area. For international travelers, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service to Corfu International Airport, located 93 km away, is an added perk.

Xhonis Apartments not only promises a comfortable and enjoyable stay but also serves as a gateway to the natural beauty and historical wonders of Ksamil, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and adventure seekers.
