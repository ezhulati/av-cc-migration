Each room at Fishta Hotel & Apartments is thoughtfully designed with a balcony and a private bathroom, providing a personal oasis of comfort. Guests can start their day on a high note with a buffet breakfast served each morning, a delightful experience that caters to various tastes and preferences. The lounge area, accessible to all guests, serves as a perfect spot for relaxation after a day of exploration.

Located just 31 km from Shkodër and 87 km from Tirana Airport, the hotel's location makes it an ideal base for exploring the surrounding area. Whether it's the convenience, the comfort, or the attentive services, Fishta Hotel & Apartments is a compelling choice for those visiting Velipojë.
