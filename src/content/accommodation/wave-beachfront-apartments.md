Guests at Wave Beachfront Apartments are treated to accommodations that boast balconies with sweeping sea views, creating a serene backdrop for their stay. Each apartment is thoughtfully equipped with a seating area, a cable flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven, fridge, stovetop, and kettle, ensuring all the comforts of home are at hand. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a shower and hair dryer, along with the provision of bed linen and towels, underscores the aparthotel's attention to detail.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Wave Beachfront Apartments offers a car rental service, making local attractions such as Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car easily accessible. Located 39 km and 43 km away respectively, these landmarks are within reach for a day trip. Additionally, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 33 km away, ensures a hassle-free travel experience.

With its blend of prime location, comprehensive amenities, and thoughtful services, Wave Beachfront Apartments stands out as a top accommodation choice for those visiting Durrës, promising a stay that is as relaxing as it is memorable.
