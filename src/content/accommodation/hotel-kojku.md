The hotel distinguishes itself with its commitment to non-smoking rooms, ensuring a clean and fresh environment. Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is available throughout the property, allowing guests to stay connected with the world or plan their next adventure in Durrës. For those seeking the sun and sea, Hotel Kojku boasts a private beach area, a mere 400 meters from Durres Beach and a short distance from the serene Golem Beach, offering a perfect retreat for beach lovers.

Accommodations at Hotel Kojku are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is a sanctuary of tranquility, equipped with a wardrobe, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of entertainment. The private bathrooms are stocked with complimentary toiletries, ensuring a refreshing start to the day. Select rooms also feature balconies, offering guests a private space to unwind and soak in the surroundings.

Beyond the comforts of the hotel, guests will find themselves conveniently located to explore the wider region. The historic Skanderbeg Square is within reach, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of Albania, while the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car presents an opportunity for an unforgettable aerial view of the landscape. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 36 km away, with the hotel providing a paid shuttle service for a smooth transition to and from the property.

Hotel Kojku stands out as a beacon of hospitality, offering a blend of convenience, comfort, and access to the natural and cultural highlights of Durrës. Whether it's basking on the private beach, exploring local attractions, or simply enjoying the amenities on offer, guests are sure to find a memorable escape at this distinguished hotel.
