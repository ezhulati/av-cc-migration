Guests at Blissful Apartments are treated to stunning sea views that can be enjoyed from the privacy of their balconies. Each apartment is thoughtfully designed with a cozy seating area, a cable flat-screen TV for entertainment, and air conditioning to ensure a comfortable stay regardless of the weather. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, toaster, fridge, stovetop, and kettle, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. The convenience of a private bathroom, equipped with a walk-in shower and hairdryer, adds to the comfort of the stay. Moreover, the provision of bed linen and towels in all units underscores the apartment complex's attention to detail.

For those concerned about security, Blissful Apartments addresses these concerns with full-day security and the added convenience of a lift. Free WiFi and private parking are the cherries on top, ensuring that guests have a hassle-free experience from the moment they arrive.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, adventure seekers will find Maestral Beach within an easy 800-meter reach, while the historic Butrint National Park is just 16 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich cultural heritage. For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 96 km from the property.

Blissful Apartments thus presents itself as an ideal lodging option for those looking to explore Sarandë's charms, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and security.
