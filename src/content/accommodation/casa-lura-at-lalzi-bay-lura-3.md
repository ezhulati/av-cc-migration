Additionally, the convenience of being just 28 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport ensures a hassle-free arrival and departure, making Casa Lura at Lalzi Bay, Lura 3 an ideal choice for discerning travelers in search of a memorable Albanian getaway.
