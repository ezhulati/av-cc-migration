Vista View Residence B is not just about its enviable position; it's a haven of comfort and convenience. The apartment is equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a pleasant environment regardless of the weather outside. Guests are treated to the luxury of a private balcony, complimentary private parking, and high-speed WiFi, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers. The presence of a lift, round-the-clock security, and guest services dedicated to organizing tours elevate the stay experience, ensuring every need is met with professionalism and care.

The interior of the apartment is thoughtfully designed to maximize comfort and aesthetic appeal. It features a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances such as a dishwasher and an oven. The inclusion of a bathroom with a shower, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the convenience. Moreover, the entire apartment is a non-smoking zone, maintaining a fresh and clean atmosphere.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty, Vista View Residence B serves as the perfect base. Activities like fishing offer a glimpse into the leisurely pace of life in Sarandë. The apartment's proximity to notable attractions, including Maestral Beach and the historic Butrint National Park, provides ample opportunity for exploration and adventure.

Situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, Vista View Residence B is accessible yet feels worlds away from the hustle and bustle, offering guests a serene retreat with all the comforts of home. Whether seeking a peaceful escape by the sea or an adventurous exploration of Sarandë's charms, Vista View Residence B is a choice that promises the best of both worlds.
