The property stands out for its thoughtful amenities, including complimentary private parking and WiFi, ensuring a hassle-free stay for every guest. Families and groups will appreciate the spacious family rooms, designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Each air-conditioned unit boasts a fully equipped kitchen, providing guests with the freedom to prepare meals to their liking. Select units also feature a private bathroom complete with a bidet, slippers, a hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries, adding an extra layer of comfort to the stay. For those sensitive to noise, soundproof options are available, ensuring a peaceful and uninterrupted rest.

Taulant doesn't stop at offering a comfortable stay; it also facilitates exploration and adventure. With both bicycle and car rental services on-site, guests have the freedom to explore Sarandë and its surroundings at their own pace. Whether it's a leisurely ride to the nearby Maestral Beach, located just 400 meters away, or a drive to the more secluded VIP Beach, 1.9 km from the property, adventure is never far off.

Situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, Taulant is not only accessible but stands as a beacon of relaxation and convenience for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Sarandë. This property promises a memorable stay, blending the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
