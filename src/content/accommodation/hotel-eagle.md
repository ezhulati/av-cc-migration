Each room at Hotel Eagle extends an invitation to relaxation with its own balcony, providing a personal oasis where guests can unwind. The modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV and a hairdryer, add a touch of convenience to the tranquil ambiance.

Morning at Hotel Eagle begins with a culinary delight, as guests are treated to a choice of continental, Italian, or vegetarian breakfasts. The on-site restaurant further tantalizes the taste buds with an array of dishes from Greek, Italian, and pizza cuisines, accommodating a variety of dietary preferences with vegetarian, halal, and vegan options upon request.

Beyond the comforts of their rooms, guests can explore the lush garden or enjoy a refreshing drink at the bar. The hotel's commitment to service shines through with room service, a 24-hour front desk, and currency exchange facilities, ensuring a seamless and worry-free experience.

Situated conveniently close to Bora Bora Beach and Lori Beach, each just 800 meters away, and with Corfu International Airport 92 km from the doorstep, Hotel Eagle serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and cultural richness of the region. Whether seeking a peaceful retreat or an adventurous exploration, Hotel Eagle promises an unforgettable stay in Ksamil.
