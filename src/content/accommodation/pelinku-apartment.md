The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, boasting a balcony that invites guests to enjoy their own private outdoor space. Inside, the accommodation includes a bedroom, a living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen complete with a minibar, catering to the needs of those who prefer to prepare their own meals. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have all the comforts of home at their fingertips.

Situated 32 km from the serene Lake Skadar and with the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 84 km away, Pelinku Apartment positions itself as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region.
