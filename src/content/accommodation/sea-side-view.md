Strategically positioned, Sea Side View is a mere 41 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, making it an ideal base for guests eager to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the area. Additionally, the property is conveniently located 45 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering adventurous guests the chance to experience breathtaking aerial views of the region. For those intrigued by natural landmarks, the Rock of Kavaje is just 1.3 km away, while the inviting sands of Golem Beach are within a 2.4 km reach.

The apartment itself is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It features a single bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their own meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are well catered for with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have access to their favorite shows and movies during their stay.

For history enthusiasts, the former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located 42 km from the apartment, offering a unique glimpse into Albania's past. Meanwhile, the Durres Amphitheatre, an 11 km journey from the property, presents an opportunity to immerse oneself in the ancient history of the region. For ease of travel, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, situated 36 km from Sea Side View, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, Sea Side View is not just an accommodation; it's a gateway to exploring the enchanting city of Durrës, offering comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury to all who stay.
