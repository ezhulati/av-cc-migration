The property distinguishes itself with its thoughtful amenities, including complimentary private parking and exclusive private entrances to each apartment, ensuring a seamless and personalized experience for every guest. Designed with families in mind, the apartment complex boasts spacious family rooms, making it a perfect retreat for those traveling with loved ones.

Attention to detail is evident in every corner of Sea Rooftop Apartment. Each unit is equipped with a private bathroom featuring a bidet, alongside the modern conveniences of free WiFi. Select rooms elevate the guest experience further with the addition of a terrace, inviting guests to unwind with a view. Comfort is guaranteed with air conditioning and ample wardrobe space provided in all units, ensuring a restful and organized stay.

For those eager to explore, Mali I Robit Beach is located just 1.8 km away, offering another scenic spot for relaxation and leisure. The cultural heartbeat of the region, Skanderbeg Square, is 44 km from the property, presenting an opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the local heritage. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 38 km away, making Sea Rooftop Apartment an accessible haven for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Sea Rooftop Apartment stands as a testament to thoughtful hospitality and strategic location, promising an unforgettable stay for those venturing to the shores of Golem.
