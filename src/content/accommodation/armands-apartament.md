This property prides itself on providing a smoke-free environment complemented by the luxury of private parking on the premises, along with complimentary WiFi access for all guests. The charm of Golem Beach is less than 1 km away, making Armand's Apartment an ideal base for beach lovers and explorers alike.

Guests are welcomed into a spacious setting, featuring a balcony that offers a tranquil space to unwind. The apartment boasts two cozy bedrooms, a comfortable living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring a pleasant stay.

For those intrigued by history and culture, the former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located 47 km away, while the Durres Amphitheatre, a testament to ancient architecture, is just 16 km from the apartment. Additionally, the convenience of being 40 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal for international travelers.

Armand's Apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to exploring the rich tapestry of Golem and its surroundings, offering a perfect blend of accessibility, privacy, and comfort.
