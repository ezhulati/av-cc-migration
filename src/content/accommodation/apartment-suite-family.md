The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and convenience, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, and not one, but two bathrooms, each equipped with a bidet and a shower. The inclusion of a balcony offers guests the luxury of sea views right from the comfort of their accommodation. Designed with the modern traveler in mind, the apartment features a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen complete with an oven and a fridge, and the added convenience of a washing machine. Air conditioning throughout ensures a comfortable stay regardless of the weather outside.

For those looking to venture further, Lake Skadar awaits at a distance of 44 km, offering a natural escape for nature enthusiasts. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 49 km away, provides ease of travel for international guests. Apartment Suite Family emerges as a compelling choice for those in search of a blend of scenic beauty, comfort, and convenience on their next getaway.
