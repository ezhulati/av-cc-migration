Occupying a prime location, this non-smoking apartment is a mere 1.3 km from the tranquil Maestral Beach, promising guests an escape into serenity. The accommodation boasts a generously sized layout, complete with a balcony that offers breathtaking sea views. Designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, the apartment includes two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchenette is well-equipped with an oven and toaster, perfect for those who enjoy preparing their meals. Additionally, the convenience of a private bathroom with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, enhances the comfort of the stay.

Adding to the allure, the apartment features a private entrance, ensuring an exclusive and undisturbed experience for guests. Those with a penchant for outdoor activities will find the surrounding area of Gjashtë ripe for exploration, with fishing listed among the popular pastimes.

For those looking to venture further, VIP Beach is accessible within a short 2.2 km drive, while the historic Butrint National Park awaits 16 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. For guests arriving from afar, the nearest airport is in Ioannina, located 96 km from the accommodation, with the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service available upon request.

Albania Dream Apartment 31 stands out as an exemplary choice for travelers who value both the tranquility of a beachside retreat and the accessibility to local attractions, making it an ideal base for an unforgettable stay in Gjashtë.
