Accommodations at Gioia Resort are thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, while the inclusion of plush towels and fine bed linen ensures a comfortable and restful night's sleep. Each guest house is designed with a cozy seating area and a warming fireplace, creating a perfect blend of luxury and homeliness.

For those looking to explore the surrounding area, the resort provides bike hire services, allowing guests to venture into the picturesque landscapes at their leisure. Additionally, the presence of a charming coffee shop and bar on the premises offers a delightful space for relaxation and socializing.

Travelers seeking further convenience will appreciate the car rental service available, facilitating easy exploration of the region's attractions. Located just 90 km from Kastoria National Airport, Gioia Resort stands as an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors, promising an unforgettable stay amidst the natural beauty of Korçë County.
