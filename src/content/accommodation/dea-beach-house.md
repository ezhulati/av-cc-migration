The apartment boasts an array of amenities designed to cater to the needs of its guests. With two bedrooms, a bathroom, and an inclusion of bed linen and towels, it ensures a hassle-free stay. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, complemented by a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a terrace that offers a tranquil garden view. The outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy their meals amidst the beauty of their surroundings, while the added benefits of a private entrance and soundproofing underscore a dedication to privacy and peace.

For those looking to explore the area, DEA Beach House facilitates this with a convenient car rental service. The property's location offers easy access to notable attractions, including the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 47 km away, and the Rock of Kavaje, 40 km distant. Furthermore, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 28 km away, makes DEA Beach House an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers.

In summary, DEA Beach House stands as a testament to the perfect blend of location, comfort, and amenities, making it an ideal choice for those in pursuit of a memorable stay in Durrës.
