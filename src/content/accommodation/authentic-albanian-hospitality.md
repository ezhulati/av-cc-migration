The property prides itself on its spacious layout, featuring three well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with essential linens and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining space, and a fully stocked kitchen. The addition of a balcony offering tranquil garden views adds a touch of serenity to the stay. Upon entering through a private entrance, guests are greeted with thoughtful touches such as fresh fruits and an assortment of chocolates or cookies, enhancing the feeling of a home away from home in a non-smoking and soundproof environment.

For those looking to explore the local cuisine or needing a quick snack, a coffee shop is available on-site, along with the convenience of packed lunches. The guest house is ideally situated for cycling enthusiasts, providing options for bike and car rentals to explore the picturesque surroundings. A relaxing garden invites guests to unwind in a peaceful setting.

Situated 43 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just 12 km from the Rock of Kavaje, Authentic Albanian Hospitality offers an excellent base for exploring the area's attractions. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 34 km away, with the guest house offering a paid shuttle service for ease of travel. This property not only promises a memorable stay but also delivers an authentic experience of Albanian hospitality and charm.
