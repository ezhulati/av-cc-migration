The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, boasting a generous layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. Guests can expect to find a flat-screen TV for their entertainment needs, alongside an equipped kitchen that features an oven and a microwave, catering to those who prefer to prepare their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom with a shower adds to the comfort of the stay, ensuring all the conveniences of home are at hand.

Privacy and tranquility are paramount at Anxhelo's Apartment, underscored by soundproofing and a private entrance that guarantees a secluded and uninterrupted experience. The commitment to a smoke-free environment further enhances the air of exclusivity and well-being.

Situated 42 km from the historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra and 43 km from the scenic Lake Skadar, the apartment serves as an ideal base for explorers keen to delve into the region's rich cultural and natural heritage. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 48 km away, ensures seamless connectivity for international travelers.

With the added benefits of complimentary WiFi and private parking, Anxhelo's Apartment stands out as a compelling choice for those in pursuit of a peaceful retreat by the sea, coupled with the ease of modern living.
