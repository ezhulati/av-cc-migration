The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring a bedroom, a bathroom, and an inviting living space. It comes fully furnished with modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a comfortable stay. The inclusion of bed linen and towels adds a touch of home. One of the highlights is the balcony, offering views of a quiet street, perfect for enjoying a peaceful morning or a relaxing evening. The outdoor dining area further enhances the experience, allowing guests to savor their meals amidst the gentle breeze of Sarandë.

Privacy and tranquility are paramount at Arkadia Saranda, underscored by the apartment's private entrance and soundproofing features. This ensures a secluded and uninterrupted retreat for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle.

Located 48 km from the ancient site of Fanoti and 96 km from Ioannina Airport, the apartment not only promises a restful stay but also ease of access to explore further afield. Arkadia Saranda stands out as a meticulously designed accommodation, offering security, comfort, and a strategic location for an unforgettable visit to Sarandë.
