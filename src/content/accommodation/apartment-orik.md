The layout of Apartment Orik is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of its guests, featuring a generously sized bedroom, a welcoming living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy the art of cooking, and a well-appointed bathroom. Entertainment needs are not overlooked, with a flat-screen TV available for guests' enjoyment.

For those intrigued by local culture and history, the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, both 18 km away, offers a perfect opportunity for exploration. Additionally, the convenience of being 167 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport facilitates both the beginning and the end of the travel experience, making Apartment Orik an attractive option for those visiting the area.
