The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with families in mind, offering spacious family rooms. Inside, guests will find all the essentials for a comfortable stay, including a desk, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom stocked with fresh bed linen and towels. Select units also come equipped with a well-appointed kitchen, featuring a refrigerator, a stovetop, and kitchenware, catering to those who prefer the option of preparing their own meals.

Beyond the comfort of their rooms, guests are invited to unwind in the lush garden, a peaceful oasis perfect for relaxation. For those looking to explore the local area, La Petite Beach is conveniently located just 500 meters away, while the picturesque Maestral Beach is a short 600-meter stroll from the property. For travelers arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 96 km from Hotel Andrea.

Hotel Andrea stands out as a welcoming haven for visitors to Sarandë, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and scenic beauty, making it an ideal choice for both short and extended stays.
