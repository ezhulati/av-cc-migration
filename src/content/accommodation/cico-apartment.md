The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, ensuring a comfortable stay. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of towels and bed linen, adding a touch of home-like comfort. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to privacy and ease of access.

Connectivity is seamless, with complimentary WiFi enveloping the premises, ensuring that guests remain connected with the world or plan their next day's adventures in Tirana. A mere 2.3 km away, Skanderbeg Square invites visitors to immerse themselves in the local culture and social scene.

For those keen on exploring further, Cico Apartment is conveniently situated near Saint Paul Cathedral, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, offering a diverse mix of cultural, shopping, and artistic experiences. The Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 17 km away, provides effortless connectivity for international travelers, making Cico Apartment an ideal base for both short-term visits and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
