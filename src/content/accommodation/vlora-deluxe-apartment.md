Positioned a mere 1.5 km from the vibrant Coco Bongo Beach and within easy reach of the city's historical landmarks, Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort, with the apartment boasting a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those culinary adventures, and a bathroom that caters to all your refreshment needs. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, ensuring that leisure moments are well spent.

For those venturing from afar, the convenience of being located 156 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal, making Vlora Deluxe Apartment a compelling choice for both domestic and international travelers. This accommodation stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Vlorë.
