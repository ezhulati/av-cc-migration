This accommodation doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious sanctuary equipped with a bedroom, a bathroom, and essential amenities including bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The presence of a fully equipped kitchen and a dining area adds a touch of home, while the terrace invites guests to dine al fresco against a backdrop of stunning city and sea views. Soundproofing and a private entrance underscore the apartment's commitment to guest privacy and comfort.

For those looking to explore the area at their own pace, the availability of a car rental service directly from the apartment adds an element of ease to travel plans. Positioned a mere kilometer from Saranda City Beach and within striking distance of the historical Butrint National Park, the apartment serves as an ideal base for both relaxation and adventure. With Ioannina Airport 96 km away, it also offers a feasible option for international travelers.

Saranda 3D Sea View Apartment is not just about the views; it's about providing a serene, well-appointed space for guests to create lasting memories in the heart of Sarandë.
