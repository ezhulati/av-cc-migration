The apartments at Rezidenca Primavera are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air conditioning, cozy seating areas, and flat-screen TVs with satellite channels for entertainment. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen and dining area in each unit allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, while the safety deposit box and private bathroom with a bidet and hair dryer add an extra layer of convenience. The highlight of each apartment is undoubtedly the balcony, furnished to enable guests to relax and soak in the surroundings in comfort. Storage needs are met with spacious wardrobes provided in all units.

Catering to families, the property boasts an on-site restaurant, coffee shop, and bar, ensuring that dining options are readily available without having to venture far. Younger guests will delight in the children's playground, making it an ideal choice for those traveling with kids. For those looking to explore the area, Rezidenca Primavera Apartments offers both bicycle and car rental services, facilitating easy access to nearby attractions.

Situated just steps away from the pristine Lalëz Bay Beach and within a manageable distance from Skanderbeg Square, the location of Rezidenca Primavera Apartments allows guests to experience the best of Durrës. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 30 km away, adds to the convenience, making it a compelling choice for both short and extended stays.
