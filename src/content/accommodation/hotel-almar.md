Each accommodation option at Hotel AlMar is meticulously designed for comfort, boasting balconies that offer guests breathtaking views of the sea. The interiors are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV for entertainment, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. For those seeking the convenience of home-cooked meals, the apartments come with a fully equipped kitchen. Additionally, private bathrooms are stocked with complimentary toiletries, adding an extra touch of luxury to the guest experience.

Situated just 2.6 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park, Hotel AlMar offers an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and historical richness of the area. Whether visiting for leisure or seeking a tranquil retreat, guests will find Hotel AlMar to be an unparalleled choice for their stay in Ksamil.
