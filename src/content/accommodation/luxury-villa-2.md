The villa's interior is thoughtfully designed, boasting a spacious layout that includes three well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for culinary adventures, and two modern bathrooms. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have all the comforts of home at their fingertips.

Outside, the villa extends its luxury with a lush garden perfect for unwinding in tranquility, alongside a private beach area reserved exclusively for guests, offering a slice of paradise on the doorstep. For those looking to explore, the property is conveniently located just 1.3 km from the scenic Beach at Government Villas and 1.5 km from the vibrant Coco Bongo Beach, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 156 km away, making Luxury Villa 2 an ideal base for both relaxation and adventure in the heart of Vlorë.
