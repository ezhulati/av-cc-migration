The apartments are designed to cater to the modern traveler's every need. Each unit is thoughtfully equipped with essential amenities such as a washing machine, a flat-screen TV, a comfortable sofa, and ample wardrobe space. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a bidet, alongside the luxury of free WiFi, underscores the apartment complex's commitment to guest comfort. For those seeking an elevated experience, select rooms also feature a terrace, providing an additional space to relax and soak in the surroundings. Notably, all units are soundproof, promising a serene retreat amidst the bustling city.

For convenience at your doorstep, the property hosts a minimarket, ensuring daily necessities are easily accessible. Further enhancing the guest experience, Gregor Apartments 1 offers both bicycle and car rental services, facilitating exploration of Sarandë's enchanting sights. The proximity to popular attractions such as Saranda City Beach, Sarande Main Beach, and La Petite Beach positions guests perfectly to discover the beauty of the area. With Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, the apartments serve as an ideal base for both local and international travelers.

The 24-hour front desk, lift access, and currency exchange services are testament to the apartment complex's dedication to guest satisfaction, making Gregor Apartments 1 a standout option for those seeking a memorable stay in Sarandë.
