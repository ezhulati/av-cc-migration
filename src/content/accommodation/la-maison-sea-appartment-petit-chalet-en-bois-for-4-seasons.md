The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom stocked with essential linens and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for endless entertainment. The dining area seamlessly connects to a fully equipped kitchen, while the terrace invites guests to soak in the breathtaking sea views. Soundproofing, parquet floors, and a cozy fireplace ensure that every stay is as tranquil as it is luxurious. Additionally, the convenience of on-site parking, a solarium, and round-the-clock front desk service underscores the property's commitment to guest satisfaction.

For those who enjoy outdoor activities, the apartment does not disappoint. Guests have access to barbecue facilities for a delightful culinary experience, a garden for relaxation, and a picnic area for leisurely afternoons under the sun. A minimarket on the premises ensures that essentials are always within reach.

Beyond the comforts of the apartment, the surrounding area offers its own charms. Mali I Robit Beach is just a short drive away, offering another spot for sun-seekers, while the historic Skanderbeg Square lies 44 km from the property, perfect for day trips. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 38 km away, with the option of a paid airport shuttle service provided by the property.

In summary, LA MAISON - Sea Apartment & Petit Chalet en Bois for 4 SEASONS is more than just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises a memorable experience for those who visit, making it a highly recommended choice for travelers to the Golem area.
