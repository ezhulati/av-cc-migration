The establishment prides itself on offering top-notch services, including a 24-hour front desk and round-the-clock security, ensuring guests' safety and peace of mind. The added convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout the property allows visitors to stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventures in Tirana.

For those looking to explore the city's rich history and culture, Pietro Guesthouse 2 is ideally situated. A mere 1.5 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, it also lies in close proximity to the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves, making it a perfect base for cultural exploration.

Moreover, the guesthouse caters to the needs of the adventurous traveler, offering both bicycle and car rental services. This makes it incredibly convenient for guests to venture further afield or navigate the city at their own pace.

Situated 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Pietro Guesthouse 2 ensures a seamless experience for international and domestic travelers alike. Whether visiting for business or leisure, guests are guaranteed a memorable stay, underscored by the guesthouse's commitment to comfort and hospitality.
