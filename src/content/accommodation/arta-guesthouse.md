Guests are treated to the comfort of modern amenities, including access to a private balcony, complimentary private parking, and high-speed WiFi, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and connected. The proximity to notable landmarks such as the Rock of Kavaje and the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 4.9 km and 39 km away respectively, adds to the appeal for those looking to delve into the area's intriguing history.

Each accommodation unit at Arta Guesthouse is thoughtfully equipped with a private bathroom featuring a shower, catering to the needs of modern travelers seeking privacy and convenience. The homestay's location is further enhanced by its accessibility, with the Durres Amphitheatre just 6.9 km away and the House of Leaves 38 km away, offering guests a glimpse into Albania's rich historical narrative.

For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 32 km from the homestay, making Arta Guesthouse an excellent choice for both international visitors and locals alike. With its blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to cultural landmarks, Arta Guesthouse stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking an authentic and relaxing Albanian experience.
