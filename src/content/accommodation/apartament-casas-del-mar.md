Apartament Casas del Mar stands out for its generous amenities, including complimentary WiFi and on-site parking, ensuring a hassle-free stay for all guests. The commitment to a smoke-free environment further enhances the clean and welcoming atmosphere of the property, located a mere 41 km from the scenic Dajti Ekspres Cable Car.

Guests are treated to a spacious living area within the apartment, which boasts two well-appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, ensuring ample space and privacy. The inclusion of bed linen and towels adds a touch of home comfort, while the flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services promises endless entertainment options. The dining area and fully-equipped kitchen offer the freedom to enjoy home-cooked meals, and the balcony presents serene inner courtyard views, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises caters to guests' everyday needs, adding to the self-sufficient charm of Apartament Casas del Mar. The property's location is also ideal for those looking to explore beyond the beach, with the Rock of Kavaje and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha within easy driving distance. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 31 km away, makes arrivals and departures a breeze.

Apartament Casas del Mar is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the rich culture and stunning landscapes of Durrës, offering a memorable retreat for travelers seeking both adventure and relaxation.
