This air-conditioned accommodation doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a home away from home. With two generously sized bedrooms, two well-appointed bathrooms, and a fully equipped kitchen that boasts a dining area and oven, guests are invited into a space designed for comfort and ease. The living room, anchored by a flat-screen TV, provides a cozy nook for relaxation, while the inclusion of towels and bed linen ensures a hassle-free stay. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores a commitment to privacy and convenience.

For those eager to delve deeper into Tirana's offerings, Square INN Komuna is conveniently situated near several popular points of interest. The Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, Pyramid of Tirana, and Rinia Park are all within proximity, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local culture. Furthermore, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 kilometers away, and the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, the property ensures seamless connectivity for international travelers.

In summary, Square INN Komuna stands out as a thoughtful blend of location, comfort, and convenience, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience Tirana in a truly immersive way.
