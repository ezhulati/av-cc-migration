Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the promise of modern amenities in a picturesque setting. Each air-conditioned unit is thoughtfully equipped with a desk, coffee machine, fridge, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with a bidet. The addition of a terrace or patio in every room, offering an outdoor dining area and sweeping garden views, ensures that relaxation is never more than a few steps away. Complimentary bed linen and towels underscore the guest house's commitment to comfort.

Dining at ROOMS EMILIANO is an experience in itself, with the on-site restaurant welcoming guests for dinner, lunch, and high tea. The family-friendly atmosphere makes it a perfect spot for guests of all ages to enjoy a meal together.

For those traveling with children, the guest house does not disappoint. A well-equipped children's playground and a picnic area provide ample opportunities for family fun and outdoor enjoyment.

Situated 49 km from the Rock of Kavaje and 31 km from the House of Leaves, ROOMS EMILIANO Castle of Kruja serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of the area. The convenience of being just 18 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, coupled with the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, adds to the appeal for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, ROOMS EMILIANO Castle of Kruja offers a welcoming blend of historical charm, modern amenities, and family-friendly facilities, making it a compelling choice for travelers looking to explore the beauty and heritage of Krujë.
