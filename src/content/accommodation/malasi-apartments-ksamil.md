Each apartment in this cozy complex boasts a private balcony, presenting breathtaking mountain views that promise to enchant guests from the moment they arrive. The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with comfort in mind, featuring air conditioning, soundproofing, and a private bathroom equipped with a shower. For those who delight in crafting their own meals, select rooms come with a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a stovetop.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Malasi Apartments Ksamil invites guests to take advantage of the outdoor barbecue and garden area, perfect for relaxing or dining al fresco when the weather is favorable.

The property's location also serves as an ideal base for exploring the surrounding area, including the historic Butrint National Park, located just 3.6 km away. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km from the apartments, making it a convenient option for those coming from afar.

With its combination of comfortable accommodations, stunning views, and a location that puts the best of Ksamil at your doorstep, Malasi Apartments Ksamil stands out as a top choice for visitors to this beautiful part of the world.
