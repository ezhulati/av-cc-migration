Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the convenience of free WiFi and complimentary private parking, alongside a commitment to service demonstrated by a 24-hour front desk, an elevator for easy access, and currency exchange services. The apartment itself is a haven of space and luxury, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms, two modern bathrooms, and the added comforts of bed linen and towels. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area perfect for gatherings, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to dine in. The balcony, offering breathtaking views of the sea, becomes the perfect backdrop for enjoying a glass of wine or champagne in privacy, thanks to the apartment's soundproofing and non-smoking policy.

For those eager to explore, the apartment's location serves as an ideal base for sightseeing tours in the surrounding area. La Petite Beach awaits at a short distance of 1.4 km, while the natural wonders of Butrint National Park are just 17 km away. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the property.

New Beach Apartment not only promises a stay filled with relaxation and scenic beauty but also ensures a memorable experience in the heart of Sarandë for those who choose to make it their temporary home.
