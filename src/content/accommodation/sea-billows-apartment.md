This spacious apartment doesn't just promise comfort; it delivers it with three well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and two modern bathrooms, ensuring a hassle-free stay. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air-conditioned interiors and the convenience of free WiFi throughout their stay. The addition of a balcony offers a private outdoor space to relax and soak in the city's vibrant atmosphere.

For those traveling by car, the availability of free private parking is a welcome feature, removing any worries about the safety of their vehicle. The property prides itself on being a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and fresh experience for all guests.

Located 95 kilometers from Ioannina Airport, Sea Billows Apartment is accessible yet comfortably distanced from the hustle and bustle, making it a serene haven for travelers seeking both adventure and tranquility in Sarandë. With a flat-screen TV available for entertainment, guests have everything they need for a memorable stay.
