The accommodation itself is a testament to space and comfort, featuring two distinct bedrooms, a single bathroom, and a living area that is both inviting and functional. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dining area and refrigerator, ensures that guests have everything they need to feel at home. Entertainment needs are also well catered to with a flat-screen TV available for guest use.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate comforts of their accommodation, La Petite Beach awaits at a distance of 1.4 km, offering a more secluded beach experience. Meanwhile, the historical allure of Butrint National Park is accessible within a 17 km drive, presenting an opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the rich cultural heritage of the area.

Conveniently located, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is 97 km away, making Family Rooms Sarande an accessible option for both domestic and international travelers. This property stands out as an ideal base for those eager to explore the beauty and charm of Sarandë, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to key attractions.
