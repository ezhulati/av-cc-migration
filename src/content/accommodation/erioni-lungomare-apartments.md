The apartment itself is a spacious haven, featuring one bedroom and two bathrooms, ensuring ample space for relaxation. Guests will appreciate the modern comforts of a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the terrace, offering unobstructed sea views that promise to enchant every morning.

Beyond the allure of the ocean, the apartment provides a unique vantage point to appreciate the surrounding mountain views, adding a touch of natural beauty to the stay. For those looking to explore, the historic Kuzum Baba is a mere 3.4 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage.

Connectivity is seamless with complimentary WiFi, ensuring guests can share their experiences or stay in touch with loved ones. For those venturing from afar, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being 152 km away makes Erioni Lungomare Apartments an accessible retreat.

In summary, Erioni Lungomare Apartments stands out as an idyllic destination for those seeking the comfort of modern amenities while being cradled by the natural beauty of Vlorë's coastline.
