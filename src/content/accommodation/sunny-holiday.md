The accommodations at Sunny Holiday are thoughtfully appointed with modern comforts and conveniences. Each unit boasts air conditioning, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment at your fingertips, along with essentials such as a toaster, kettle, bidet, hair dryer, and ample wardrobe space. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, complete with a bath, and select rooms feature a fully equipped kitchen, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. Complimentary bed linen and towels underscore the property's commitment to guest satisfaction.

Beyond the confines of their rooms, guests are invited to take advantage of the outdoor fireplace and picnic area, perfect for relaxing evenings or casual dining under the stars. The panoramic views of the sea and mountain from the property add a touch of natural beauty to the experience.

For those looking to explore the local area or venture further afield, Sunny Holiday facilitates this with an on-site car rental service. The property's strategic location also places guests within easy reach of Saranda City Beach, just 2.9 km away, and the historic Butrint National Park, 19 km from the doorstep. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 99 km away.

Adding to the convenience, guests can enjoy the on-site bar, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration or beach activities.

In summary, Sunny Holiday stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those visiting Sarandë, offering a harmonious blend of location, amenities, and comfort. Whether it's basking in the natural beauty of the surroundings, enjoying the privacy and convenience of the well-appointed units, or exploring the rich tapestry of the local area, Sunny Holiday caters to a wide range of preferences and needs.
