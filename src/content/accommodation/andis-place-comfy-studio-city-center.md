This recently updated studio apartment, with its roots tracing back to 1990, offers a modern retreat with the charm of historical architecture. Guests are treated to a suite of amenities including air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that combines the ease of modern living with the warmth of home. The space is strictly non-smoking, promoting a clean and healthy environment, and is located a brief journey from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, adding a touch of historical intrigue to the visit.

The accommodation boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment needs, and a kitchenette equipped with a toaster and fridge, catering to the culinary desires of guests. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the property's attention to detail and commitment to guest comfort.

For those keen to explore, Andi's Place is within easy reach of notable attractions such as the National Museum of History Albania, the House of Leaves, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, making it an ideal base for cultural aficionados. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 13 km away, offers convenient travel options for international guests.

Andi's Place-Comfy Studio-City Center stands out as a meticulously designed space that caters to the needs of modern travelers, offering a seamless blend of historical charm and contemporary amenities in the vibrant city of Tirana.
