Guests at Rezidenca Luma are treated to the luxury of a garden-view balcony, complemented by the practicality of complimentary private parking and WiFi, ensuring a stay that is both relaxing and connected. The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a separate bedroom for privacy, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchenette for culinary adventures, and a bathroom that caters to all your needs. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, making every moment inside as enjoyable as those spent exploring the surroundings.

For those intrigued by cultural landmarks, the Rock of Kavaje is merely 1.6 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history. Further afield, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha stands 44 km away, presenting an opportunity for a deeper dive into Albania's past. The heart of Albanian culture, Skanderbeg Square, is located 43 km from the apartment, while the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 47 km away, offers breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience.

Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 38 km from the property, making Rezidenca Luma an accessible haven for both domestic and international travelers. This property does not just offer a place to stay; it provides a starting point for adventures, a peaceful retreat, and a slice of the local lifestyle, all wrapped up in one delightful package.
