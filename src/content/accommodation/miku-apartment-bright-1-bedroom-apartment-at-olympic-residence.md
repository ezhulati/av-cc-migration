The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and accessibility. It features a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a bathroom, and a balcony that offers serene garden views - a rare find in the bustling city. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen ensures that guests have everything they need at their fingertips. Moreover, the property prides itself on being non-smoking and offers facilities to accommodate disabled guests, ensuring a comfortable stay for all.

Adding to the convenience, the apartment boasts a range of on-site amenities. A minimarket on the premises means essentials are never far away. For those looking to explore the wider area, the availability of a car rental service directly from the apartment opens up endless possibilities for adventure and exploration.

Connectivity is seamless, with free WiFi available throughout the property, and the peace of mind that comes with full-day security and an elevator adds to the overall experience. Located just 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Miku Apartment at Olympic Residence stands out as an accessible and inviting option for both short-term and extended stays in Tirana.
