The establishment prides itself on its 24-hour front desk service and a shared kitchen, catering to the needs of guests at any hour. The allure of city views can be enjoyed, adding to the charm of this accommodation.

For those looking to unwind, the on-site bar and lounge offer the perfect setting for relaxation after a day of exploration. Situated at a convenient distance, Mali I Robit Beach is accessible within less than 1 km, and Golem Beach is just a 14-minute walk away, making Haka Guesthouse an ideal base for beach enthusiasts.

Furthermore, the guesthouse is located 40 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, with a paid airport shuttle service available, ensuring smooth transitions to and from the property. Haka Guesthouse stands out as a top selection for travelers who value both the tranquility of their lodging and the adventure that lies beyond.
