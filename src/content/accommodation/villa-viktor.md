Select rooms elevate the experience further with additional seating areas, ensuring a comfortable stay. To make guests feel at home, each unit is thoughtfully supplied with fresh bed linen and towels.

For those looking to explore the local area, Villa Viktor is ideally located. The property is a haven for fishing enthusiasts and those eager to immerse themselves in the outdoors, with car hire services readily available for further exploration. A picnic area within the complex offers the perfect setting for a leisurely day under the sun.

Beyond its immediate comforts, Villa Viktor serves as an excellent base for discovering nearby attractions. La Petite Beach is within easy reach, located just 1.9 km away, while the historic Butrint National Park is a mere 17 km from the doorstep, promising a blend of natural beauty and ancient history. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 97 km from the accommodation.

Villa Viktor thus presents itself as an ideal choice for travelers seeking a peaceful beachside escape with the comforts of home and the convenience of easy access to Sarandë's most beloved sights.
