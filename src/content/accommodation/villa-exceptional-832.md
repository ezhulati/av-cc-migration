Located conveniently close to Mali I Robit Beach and within easy reach of the scenic Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, Villa Exceptional 832 serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty of the area. For those interested in venturing further, the historic Skanderbeg Square lies 47 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the region, while the Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, is just 5.3 km from the property.

For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is situated 41 km from the apartment, with Villa Exceptional 832 facilitating travel arrangements through its paid airport shuttle service. This non-smoking accommodation not only prioritizes the comfort and well-being of its guests with its array of amenities but also ensures a seamless and worry-free stay with its complimentary WiFi access. Villa Exceptional 832 stands out as a remarkable choice for those in pursuit of a tranquil beachfront getaway, coupled with the convenience of modern living.
