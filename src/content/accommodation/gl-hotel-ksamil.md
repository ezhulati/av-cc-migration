The hotel prides itself on its array of amenities, including a welcoming terrace, complimentary private parking, and a lively bar, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. For those looking for a light bite or a refreshing drink, the on-site snack bar serves as an ideal spot.

Accommodations at GL Hotel Ksamil are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room boasts a balcony, offering a private outdoor space for guests to enjoy. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of modern conveniences such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and connected. The en-suite bathrooms are well-appointed with showers, free toiletries, and hairdryers, adding to the overall experience of effortless luxury.

The hotel enhances the start of each day with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of adventure or relaxation. The multilingual staff, proficient in Greek and English, are always available at the reception to assist with any inquiries or recommendations, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable stay for all guests.

Situated a mere 3.7 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and within reach of Corfu International Airport, 93 km away, GL Hotel Ksamil serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural and historical wonders of the region. This hotel not only promises a memorable stay but also delivers an experience that is as enriching as it is relaxing.
