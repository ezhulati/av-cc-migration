Equipped with complimentary WiFi, the apartment boasts a contemporary living space complete with a flat-screen TV, washing machine, and a kitchen outfitted with an oven and microwave. For added comfort, guests will find towels and bed linen readily available, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

Strategically located, Cosy Urban Central Apartment is within close proximity to some of Tirana's most sought-after attractions, including Skanderbeg Square, the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the National Museum of History Albania. For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 12 km away, making it an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in the city.
