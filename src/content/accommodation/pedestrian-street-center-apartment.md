The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living and modern conveniences, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and two bathrooms, ensuring ample space for guests. Comfort is further enhanced by the provision of quality bed linen and towels. Entertainment and relaxation are catered for with a flat-screen TV, offering a wide range of satellite channels, situated in a cozy dining area. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen, invites guests to prepare their meals, while the balcony offers a serene spot to enjoy the city's panoramic views. The commitment to guest privacy and comfort is evident in the apartment's design, featuring a private entrance and soundproofing, creating a tranquil urban retreat.

For those everyday essentials, the convenience of a minimarket on the premises adds to the appeal of this accommodation. Located 59 km from Podgorica Airport, the apartment also simplifies travel arrangements by offering airport transfers and a car rental service, making exploration of the surrounding area effortless.

Pedestrian Street Center Apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing Shkodër in unparalleled comfort and style.
