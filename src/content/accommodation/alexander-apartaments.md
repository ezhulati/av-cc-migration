Each apartment at Alexander Apartments is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. The space boasts a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The inclusion of a luxurious bathroom, outfitted with a hot tub and plush bathrobes, adds a touch of indulgence to the stay.

For those interested in exploring the local area, Alexander Apartments is conveniently located just 1.9 km from the tranquil Ri Beach, offering an easy escape to the seaside. Further afield, guests can discover the historical significance of Independence Square, only 2.5 km away, or marvel at the panoramic views from Kuzum Baba, situated 3 km from the property.

Accessibility is seamless, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport positioned 151 km away, making Alexander Apartments an ideal base for both short-term visits and extended stays in Vlorë. This apartment stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
