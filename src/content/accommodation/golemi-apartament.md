This spacious apartment is not just about its prime location; it offers guests a home away from home with its well-appointed amenities. Featuring two cozy bedrooms, a living room that invites relaxation with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and stovetop, the apartment ensures a comfortable stay. The inclusion of two bathrooms equipped with a bidet and a shower, along with a washing machine, adds to the convenience, making it suitable for both short and extended stays. The air-conditioned space also boasts a terrace and mountain views, providing a tranquil setting for guests to unwind.

Privacy and comfort are paramount at Golemi Apartament, highlighted by the accommodation's private entrance. Situated just 800 meters from Golem Beach and a short drive from the Rock of Kavaje, the apartment serves as a gateway to the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the area. For those looking to explore further, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is also within reach, located 46 km from the apartment.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 40 km away. The property thoughtfully offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless travel experience for guests. Golemi Apartament stands as a testament to the perfect balance of scenic beauty, comfort, and convenience, making it a must-visit destination for discerning travelers.
