The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, boasting a generous terrace that invites guests to unwind, a cozy bedroom for restful nights, a living room designed for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen that caters to culinary needs. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have access to their favorite shows and movies during their stay.

For those keen to explore the local area, SKY Apartment serves as a perfect base. It is conveniently located near Vlore Beach, offering a chance to experience the city's vibrant seaside atmosphere. Additionally, the historical Independence Square and the iconic Kuzum Baba are within easy reach, providing a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Vlorë.

Travelers will find the journey to and from the apartment facilitated by its proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 150 km away, making SKY Apartment an accessible retreat for both domestic and international visitors. This property stands out as a prime choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and charm of Vlorë, promising a memorable stay with every visit.
