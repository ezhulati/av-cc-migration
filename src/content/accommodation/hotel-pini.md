Each room at Hotel Pini is thoughtfully designed to ensure maximum comfort, featuring a private balcony, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of air conditioning in every room guarantees a refreshing retreat from the warm Mediterranean climate. Private bathrooms equipped with showers add to the convenience, ensuring a pleasant stay.

For those intrigued by history and nature, the ancient Lëkurësi Castle is merely 2.5 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's rich past. Further afield, Butrint National Park, located within 18 km, presents a unique opportunity to explore a harmonious blend of lush natural landscapes and ancient Greek ruins.

Travelers concerned about accessibility need not worry, as Corfu Airport is approximately 35 km away. The journey to this international gateway is made effortless with ferry services available from the Port of Sarandë, a mere 200 meters from Hotel Pini. This proximity to key transport links underscores the hotel's appeal to both international visitors and locals alike, making it an ideal choice for anyone seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and cultural exploration in Sarandë.
