Each room at Bujtina Oxhaku is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring essential amenities such as a wardrobe, air conditioning, and a TV. The private bathrooms are equipped with showers and slippers, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience. Select rooms also boast balconies, offering guests a private outdoor space to relax and unwind.

The hotel prides itself on its exceptional customer service, with a multilingual staff proficient in Greek, English, and Italian available around the clock at the front desk to assist guests in planning their stay and making the most of their visit to Korçë.

For those interested in exploring the local area, the Monastery Saint Naum is just 43 km away, making for an easy day trip. Additionally, Bujtina Oxhaku is conveniently situated 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, facilitating stress-free travel arrangements for both domestic and international guests. This hotel emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of tranquility, convenience, and attentive service in the heart of Korçë.
