This air-conditioned apartment boasts a well-appointed bedroom, complemented by a bathroom equipped with modern amenities including a bidet, a bath, and a hair dryer, ensuring a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi throughout the property, and the inclusion of a flat-screen TV adds a touch of home comfort. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further enhances the appeal of this accommodation.

For those interested in cultural excursions, the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, Saint Paul Cathedral, and the historic Tanners' Bridge are located nearby, offering a deep dive into the local heritage and art scene. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, Cosy Urban Retreat offers both a serene escape and a convenient connection to wider explorations.
