Accommodations at UJVARA HOTEL are a testament to modern comfort, with each air-conditioned room providing guests with complimentary WiFi and a flat-screen TV for their entertainment needs. Select rooms elevate the experience further, offering tranquil garden views and the luxury of a private balcony, ensuring a stay that is as refreshing as it is comfortable.

For those looking to explore the vibrant surroundings of Golem, the hotel serves as an ideal base. Activities such as cycling allow guests to immerse themselves in the local landscape, offering a unique blend of adventure and tranquility. Moreover, UJVARA HOTEL caters to the needs of business travelers with efficiency, offering essential services like faxing, photocopying, and ironing to ensure a seamless stay.

Situated conveniently close to both Qerret Beach and Golem Beach, and just a 42 km drive from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, UJVARA HOTEL is perfectly positioned for guests to explore the beauty of the Albanian coast. Whether it's for leisure or business, this hotel promises an experience that combines the best of local charm with impeccable service.
