Each guest room is a haven of comfort, equipped with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a private balcony. The bathrooms are well-appointed, featuring both a bath and a bidet, ensuring a refreshing start or end to your day. What sets these rooms apart is the breathtaking view of the sea and mountains, promising a serene backdrop to your stay.

The hotel prides itself on exceptional guest services, including a 24-hour front desk ready to cater to your needs at any hour. For families, the children's playground is a delightful touch, ensuring entertainment for the little ones. Additional amenities such as dry cleaning and ironing services are available upon request, complemented by the convenience of free parking.

For those looking to explore the local area, a grocery shop is conveniently located just 150 meters away. The hotel's proximity to notable landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, both within 6.4 km, makes it an excellent base for exploring the rich history and culture of Vlorë.

Hotel Ble Ble Vlore is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to a memorable experience, combining the comforts of home with the beauty and excitement of Albania's coastal city.
