The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a bathroom, complete with all the essentials such as bed linen and towels. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The dining area and a fully-equipped kitchen provide all the necessities for a home-cooked meal, which can be enjoyed in the outdoor dining area, offering views of the lush garden. For those who love grilling, a barbecue is available for a delightful dining experience under the stars.

Beyond the comforts of the apartment, guests will find themselves conveniently located to explore the wider area. The historic Skanderbeg Square lies 47 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich culture of the region, while the Rock of Kavaje, a mere 5.8 km from the property, presents an opportunity for an adventurous day out. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 41 km away, making Marina Apartment an accessible haven for both local and international visitors.

With its combination of serene beachfront location, comprehensive amenities, and proximity to local attractions, Marina Apartment stands out as an exceptional choice for those looking to experience the best of Mali i Robit.
