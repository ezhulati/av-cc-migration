The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting an air-conditioned space that includes a bedroom, a living room with a flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a fridge. The inclusion of towels and bed linen ensures a hassle-free stay, while the private entrance adds an extra layer of privacy for discerning guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate comforts of their accommodation, Maestral Beach lies a mere 2.9 km away, offering another picturesque spot for relaxation and leisure. Further afield, the historical allure of Butrint National Park, located 18 km from the apartment, invites guests to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of Albania's ancient past.

Convenience is further underscored by the apartment's proximity to Ioannina Airport, situated 98 km away, with the added benefit of a paid airport shuttle service to ensure seamless travel arrangements. Panorama Sea View Apartment not only promises a stay marked by luxury and comfort but also serves as a gateway to the enchanting landscapes and cultural heritage of Sarandë.
