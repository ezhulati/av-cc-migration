The apartments are equipped with modern amenities, including satellite flat-screen TVs, washing machines, and fully equipped kitchens with dishwashers and ovens, ensuring a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of a holiday retreat. For those who appreciate attention to detail, the option to request towels and linens is available, enhancing the convenience factor.

One of the unique features of VISI Apartments is the outdoor fireplace and barbecue area, inviting guests to enjoy memorable evenings under the stars. The property prides itself on being a non-smoking establishment, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

Situated within easy reach of other popular beaches like Bora Bora Beach and Paradise Beach, visitors have the luxury of exploring the area's coastal gems. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making VISI Apartments an accessible escape for international travelers.

With its combination of prime location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a tranquil beachfront experience, VISI Apartments is a standout destination for those seeking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Ksamil.
