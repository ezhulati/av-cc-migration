The accommodation itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring a balcony that offers a personal outdoor space for guests to enjoy. The layout includes a comfortable bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen that caters to the culinary needs of its occupants. Entertainment is also taken care of, with a flat-screen TV available for guests' viewing pleasure.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, La Petite Beach awaits at a distance of 1.2 km, offering another spot for sun-seekers, while the historic Butrint National Park, located 17 km away, provides a cultural excursion for history enthusiasts.

Travelers will find the nearest airport in Ioannina, situated 97 km from the apartment, making Elisabeth Apartments 3 an accessible retreat for both domestic and international visitors. This property not only promises a stay that is rich in comfort and convenience but also positions guests perfectly to explore the best of what Sarandë has to offer.
