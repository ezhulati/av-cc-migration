Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the convenience of free private parking and the seamless connectivity of complimentary WiFi. The property elevates the guest experience further by offering a concierge service, currency exchange, and an elevator, ensuring a hassle-free stay from start to finish.

The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to dine in, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, catering to a wide range of tastes and preferences. Importantly, the entire accommodation is a non-smoking environment, maintaining a fresh and welcoming atmosphere.

For outdoor enthusiasts and those seeking relaxation alike, Appartement Santa does not disappoint. The property includes a terrace perfect for soaking in the views, a bar for evening leisure, and barbecue facilities for a delightful culinary experience. Moreover, a minimarket on-site adds an extra layer of convenience for any last-minute needs.

Wellness enthusiasts will find solace in the variety of packages available, aimed at rejuvenating the body and spirit. The adventurous can explore Sarandë's picturesque surroundings through cycling, hiking, and walking tours, with outdoor play equipment available for younger guests.

Notably, VIP Beach and Flamingo Beach are within easy reach, located 400 meters and 700 meters away, respectively, offering ample opportunities for sunbathing and water sports. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is 96 km away, making Appartement Santa an accessible haven for both local and international visitors.

In essence, Appartement Santa stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, promising a memorable stay with its blend of scenic beauty, thoughtful amenities, and a welcoming atmosphere.
