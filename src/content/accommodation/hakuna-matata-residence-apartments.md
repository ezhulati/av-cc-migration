Guests are greeted with stunning sea views from private balconies, while an infinity pool and well-manicured garden add to the serene atmosphere. The property ensures convenience with complimentary private parking and WiFi, making it an effortless retreat for those looking to disconnect and rejuvenate.

Accommodations at Hakuna Matata are thoughtfully appointed with air conditioning, cozy seating areas, and flat-screen TVs with cable channels for those leisurely moments indoors. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, and modern appliances such as an oven, toaster, fridge, coffee machine, and kettle, cater to the needs of guests preferring home-cooked meals. Moreover, the safety deposit box, private bathroom complete with a bidet, bathrobes, and slippers, underscore the villa complex's commitment to guest comfort and security. The provision of bed linen and towels in all units further exemplifies the attention to detail.

For those looking to unwind with a drink, the on-site bar serves as a perfect spot. The property's proximity to Coco Bongo Beach and the historic Kuzum Baba offers guests an opportunity to explore the local area's attractions. Despite its secluded feel, Hakuna Matata Residence & Apartments remains accessible, located 157 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, ensuring a seamless travel experience to and from the property.

In essence, Hakuna Matata Residence & Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, promising an unforgettable stay with its blend of natural beauty, comprehensive amenities, and impeccable service.
