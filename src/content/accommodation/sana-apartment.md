Guests at Sana Apartment are treated to a secure and personalized experience, thanks to around-the-clock security and the luxury of private check-in and check-out services. The apartment itself is a haven of space and tranquility, featuring a balcony that offers a serene spot to unwind. Inside, two bedrooms and a living room provide ample space for relaxation, while a well-equipped kitchen caters to those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have everything they need at their fingertips. Moreover, the commitment to a non-smoking and soundproof environment underscores the apartment's dedication to guest comfort and satisfaction.

For those keen to explore, Sarande Main Beach is just a short 1.5 km journey away, offering another picturesque locale for sun-seekers. Meanwhile, the historical allure of Butrint National Park, located 17 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for adventure and exploration. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 97 km from Sana Apartment, making it a convenient option for those flying in.

In summary, Sana Apartment not only promises a stay marked by comfort and convenience but also positions itself as a gateway to the natural and cultural riches of Sarandë.
