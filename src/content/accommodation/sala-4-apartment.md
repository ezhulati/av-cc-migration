Guests will find the air-conditioned space thoughtfully arranged with a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that boasts modern appliances such as an oven and a coffee machine. The bathroom is well-appointed with a shower and complimentary slippers, enhancing the comfort of one's stay. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided in the apartment, along with a thoughtful touch of fresh fruits for guests to enjoy.

For those needing to pick up essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises caters to those needs without having to venture far.

Located strategically, the apartment is just 58 km away from Podgorica Airport, making it an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers.
