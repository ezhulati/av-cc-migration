Strategically located, the apartment is within easy reach of notable landmarks, including the historic Skanderbeg Square and the thrilling Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, situated 38 km and 42 km away, respectively. For those intrigued by natural wonders, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 10 km journey, offering an escape into the area's natural beauty.

Accommodation at Vintage Sea-View Apartment is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable stay.

Cultural enthusiasts will find the apartment's location advantageous, with the Durres Amphitheatre only 1.4 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history. Additionally, the former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the tranquil Rinia Park are both accessible, located 38 km from the apartment, promising a blend of historical exploration and leisure.

For travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being just 32 km away means less time commuting and more time experiencing the vibrant life and scenic views that Durrës has to offer. Vintage Sea-View Apartment in Centre Durres stands out as an ideal lodging option for those looking to immerse themselves in the culture, history, and natural beauty of Durrës.
