A mere stone's throw away, at 43 km, guests can discover the Rock of Kavaje, while the poignant Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument and the lush Rinia Park are accessible within a 2.6 km and 2.8 km radius, respectively. The iconic Skanderbeg Square, a testament to the city's vibrant culture and history, is just 3.3 km from the doorstep, promising an enriching experience for those keen to immerse themselves in local life.

The apartment itself is a sanctuary of space and light, boasting a balcony that offers a serene spot to unwind. It comprises two elegantly appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen that caters to culinary enthusiasts. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complemented by the convenience of free WiFi throughout the premises. A notable feature is the private entrance, ensuring an exclusive and undisturbed stay.

For nature lovers, the Grand Park of Tirana is a mere 2.8 km away, offering a green escape, while the Pyramid of Tirana, located 3 km from the residence, presents an architectural marvel worth exploring. Accessibility is seamless, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, making Appartamento in Residenza Esclusiva an ideal base for both leisure and exploration in this dynamic city.
