Guests are treated to the comfort of modern amenities in a picturesque setting. Each apartment at Doci Seven Hotel is equipped with complimentary WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine, ensuring a stay that combines the conveniences of home with the luxury of hotel service. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a fridge and kitchenware, invites guests to prepare their own meals, which can be enjoyed in the outdoor dining area against a backdrop of breathtaking mountain views. The promise of comfort is fulfilled with air conditioning, heating, and a private bathroom in every accommodation.

For those seeking adventure, the hotel's location offers an abundance of activities. From diving in the crystal-clear waters of Velipojë to exploring the surrounding natural and historical sites, there is no shortage of experiences to enrich your stay.

Conveniently located 84 kilometers from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Doci Seven Hotel stands as an ideal destination for travelers looking for a blend of relaxation, adventure, and cultural immersion in one of Velipojë's most picturesque settings.
