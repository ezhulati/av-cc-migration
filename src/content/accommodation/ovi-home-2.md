Moreover, the proximity to Reja - The Cloud adds an artistic touch to the array of nearby attractions.

Guests of Ovi Home 2 are treated to modern comforts within a cozy setting. The apartment boasts a well-appointed kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, ensuring that home-cooked meals are a delightful option. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV and complimentary WiFi allows for relaxing evenings in, while the convenience of a washing machine offers an added layer of ease for longer stays. The thoughtful provision of towels and bed linen underscores the attention to detail that makes a stay here truly carefree.

Adding to the allure, this accommodation ensures privacy with its own separate entrance, allowing guests to come and go as they please, truly making it a home away from home. For those looking to explore the wider area, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the iconic Pyramid of Tirana, and the lush Rinia Park are also within close proximity. And with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, Ovi Home 2 represents the perfect base for both short visits and extended stays in this dynamic city.
