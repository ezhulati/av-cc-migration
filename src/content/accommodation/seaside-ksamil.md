The accommodations at Seaside Ksamil are thoughtfully designed to cater to diverse needs and preferences. Select apartments come equipped with air conditioning, a dining area, and a cozy seating area complemented by a satellite flat-screen TV for those moments of relaxation. For guests seeking to immerse themselves in the breathtaking natural beauty of Ksamil, some units feature balconies that offer an outdoor dining area and panoramic sea views, providing the perfect backdrop for a memorable stay.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, Seaside Ksamil invites guests to unwind in the shared lounge area, fostering a sense of community and relaxation. The property's prime location places guests within easy reach of popular attractions such as Lori Beach, Ksamil Beach 9, and Bora Bora Beach, offering a plethora of options for exploration and leisure.

For travelers venturing from afar, the convenience of being located 93 km from Corfu International Airport adds to the appeal of Seaside Ksamil, making it a compelling choice for those seeking a tranquil yet accessible beachfront escape in Ksamil.
