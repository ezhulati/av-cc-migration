Guests at Home Shkodra are treated to modern conveniences including complimentary WiFi, ensuring connectivity is never an issue. The holiday home features a cozy 1-bedroom layout, equipped with a flat-screen TV and a washing machine for all your laundry needs. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped and including a minibar, perfect for those who enjoy preparing their meals or simply want a cold beverage at hand. Moreover, visitors can wake up to serene garden views, adding a touch of nature to their stay.

For those traveling by air, Podgorica Airport is conveniently located just 57 km away, making Home Shkodra an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers alike.
