This air-conditioned apartment offers a serene retreat with its spacious layout, featuring two bedrooms, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen, and a modern bathroom. Guests can enjoy the added luxury of a balcony, perfect for sipping morning coffee or unwinding in the evening breeze. Complimentary WiFi and a flat-screen TV ensure that entertainment and connectivity are readily available.

Ola Bicycle Apartment stands out for its proximity to Tirana's most notable attractions. Just a brief walk away, visitors can find themselves at Rinia Park, Saint Paul Cathedral, and the intriguing House of Leaves. The historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is also within easy walking distance, offering a glimpse into Albania's past.

For those looking to delve deeper into the city's offerings, the Pyramid of Tirana, Reja - The Cloud, and the poignant Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument are nearby, making it effortless for guests to immerse themselves in the local culture and history.

Situated 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment ensures a hassle-free journey to and from the city, making it an ideal base for both short visits and extended stays. Ola Bicycle Apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the best of Tirana, combining comfort, convenience, and a touch of adventure for an unforgettable visit.
