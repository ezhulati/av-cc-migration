Guests are welcomed into a spacious apartment that not only boasts a balcony with breathtaking sea views but also includes a well-appointed living room. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances such as an oven and microwave, and a bathroom complete with a bidet, ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of a holiday retreat. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience to the experience, while city views present a delightful urban contrast.

For those who appreciate the ease of access to essentials, the on-site minimarket serves as a handy resource. The apartment's proximity to notable landmarks, including the Rock of Kavaje, just 7.4 km away, and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, 38 km distant, invites guests to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the area. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located just 32 km away, Superior 581 further enhances guest convenience by offering a paid airport shuttle service, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, Superior 581 offers a harmonious blend of convenience, comfort, and scenic beauty, making it a compelling choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the charm of Durrës while enjoying the comforts of a thoughtfully appointed beachfront apartment.
