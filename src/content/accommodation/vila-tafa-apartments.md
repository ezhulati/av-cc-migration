Vila TAFA Apartments boasts recently updated accommodations, ensuring a comfortable and stylish stay. Each air-conditioned unit is a haven of tranquility, overlooking serene street scenes, and comes equipped with complimentary WiFi. The interiors feature elegant parquet flooring, a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven, a cozy dining area, and a living space adorned with a flat-screen TV offering streaming services. The private bathrooms are designed with modern walk-in showers and include thoughtful touches like slippers for added comfort. Select apartments further enhance the experience with either a balcony or patio, offering splendid views of the surrounding mountains or the vibrant cityscape.

Guests will appreciate the convenience of having a minimarket on the premises, making it easy to stock up on essentials or grab a quick snack. The property is also within easy reach of several of Tirana's most notable attractions, including the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the intriguing Reja - The Cloud, and the iconic Pyramid of Tirana. For those interested in shopping and culture, the Toptani Shopping Centre and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana are nearby, promising a fulfilling day out.

Situated just 16 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Vila TAFA Apartments offers a seamless experience for both short and long-term visitors. Whether in town for business or leisure, guests will find this apartment complex to be an ideal base for their adventures in Tirana.
