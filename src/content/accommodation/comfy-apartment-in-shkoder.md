The highlight of this accommodation is its spacious layout, featuring a balcony that offers serene views of a quiet street, perfect for those moments of relaxation. The apartment is well-appointed with a cozy bedroom, a living area designed for relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and an oven, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. The convenience of having a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Notably, this apartment is an allergy-free and non-smoking environment, ensuring a healthy and comfortable experience for all guests.

For those minor necessities or quick snacks, the apartment houses a minimarket, adding an extra layer of convenience to your stay. Moreover, the property facilitates airport transfers and offers a car rental service, making travel arrangements seamless for exploring the surrounding area or for smooth arrivals and departures. Podgorica Airport is located 58 km away, making it a reasonably accessible option for travelers.

Comfy Apartment in Shkoder presents itself as an ideal accommodation choice for those in search of a comfortable, well-equipped, and conveniently located stay in Shkodër, whether they are in town for leisure or business.
