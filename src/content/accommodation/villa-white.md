Guests at Villa White are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their stay. Each room is a sanctuary of comfort, equipped with air conditioning, a desk for those needing to stay connected, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a private bathroom stocked with fresh bed linen and towels. The addition of a terrace in every room offers guests a unique vantage point of the city's pulse, while select accommodations present breathtaking sea views, complemented by the charm of a private balcony. Convenience is further amplified with the inclusion of a fridge in every room, ensuring refreshments are always within reach.

Beyond the confines of its well-appointed rooms, Villa White extends its hospitality through a suite of services. The property prides itself on offering free WiFi across all areas, ensuring guests remain connected with the world, alongside complimentary private parking for those arriving by car. The presence of a welcoming bar, room service, a 24-hour front desk, and currency exchange facilities underscores the hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction and convenience.

For travelers venturing from afar, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 93 km away, underscores Villa White's appeal as a gateway to the enchanting beauty of Ksamil and its surroundings.
