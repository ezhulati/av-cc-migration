Each apartment at Holiday Apartments Altini is thoughtfully equipped with a balcony adorned with outdoor furniture, perfect for soaking in the serene garden views. Inside, guests are greeted with a sleek flat-screen TV and a cozy bathroom featuring a refreshing shower. The kitchenette, complete with a refrigerator, stovetop, and kitchenware, alongside a convenient dining table, ensures that guests have everything they need to prepare and enjoy their meals just the way they like.

The allure of the nearest beach, merely a 6-minute stroll away, adds to the appeal of staying at Holiday Apartments Altini. Moreover, the provision of complimentary parking further enhances the convenience for those exploring the area by car.

In summary, Holiday Apartments Altini offers a blend of convenience, comfort, and natural beauty, making it a prime choice for those looking to experience Ksamil from a cozy, home-like base.
