Guests will appreciate the thoughtful amenities provided, including complimentary WiFi and on-site parking, ensuring a hassle-free experience from start to finish. Accessibility is a breeze with the inclusion of an elevator and the added convenience of an ATM on the premises.

The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a balcony that offers picturesque city views. It is well-appointed with a bedroom, a cozy living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a fridge. The bathroom comes with a bath, adding an extra layer of comfort to your stay. Notably, the apartment is designed to be non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful retreat for all guests.

For those needing essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the property provides utmost convenience.

Situated a mere 2.8 km from Ri Beach and 2.3 km from Independence Square, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring Vlorë's rich history and natural beauty. For travelers flying in, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making the Lovely Large Summer Apartment an accessible choice for both domestic and international visitors seeking a memorable seaside escape in Vlorë.
