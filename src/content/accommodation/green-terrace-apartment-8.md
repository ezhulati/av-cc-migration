This apartment is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a fully equipped kitchen ready for culinary adventures, and a cozy living room ideal for unwinding after a day of exploration. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, free WiFi accessible throughout the premises, and private parking on site adds layers of convenience for every guest.

Attention to detail is evident in the private entrance and around-the-clock security, providing a sense of privacy and safety. The bathroom is well-appointed with a bidet and hair dryer, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those keen to immerse themselves in the local culture, Green Terrace Apartment 8 is conveniently located near significant landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Museum of History Albania. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, this apartment stands out as an accessible and attractive option for both short and long-term visitors.
