Boasting a generous layout, the apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that offers captivating sea views. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances such as a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom complete with a bidet, underscores the property's commitment to providing a comfortable and convenient stay.

For those looking to explore the area at their own pace, the apartment facilitates a car rental service, adding an extra layer of convenience to your stay. Major attractions, including Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, lie within accessible distances, 41 km and 45 km away respectively, making it easy for guests to plan day trips and adventures. Furthermore, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 35 km away, is complemented by the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring smooth transitions to and from the property.
