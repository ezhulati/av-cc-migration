The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their own meals, and a modern bathroom. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures that entertainment needs are well catered to, making it an ideal retreat after a day of exploration.

Adding to the appeal, guests can enjoy the fresh air and views from their own private balcony or spend leisurely evenings on the terrace. The commitment to a non-smoking environment throughout the property ensures a pleasant stay for all guests.

For those intrigued by the natural beauty and historical significance of the region, Butrint National Park is merely 17 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity. Furthermore, with Ioannina Airport located 97 km from the apartment, guests will find their travel needs conveniently met.

Serxho Apartment Sarande stands out as a welcoming base for visitors to Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with a location that makes exploring the surrounding beauty a breeze.
