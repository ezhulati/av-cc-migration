Guests are treated to air-conditioned accommodations that boast not only a balcony with sweeping sea views but also a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. Each apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven, toaster, fridge, stovetop, and essential kitchenware, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The convenience of a coffee machine and kettle adds a touch of home comfort, while the private bathroom comes with a shower and hairdryer, ensuring privacy and ease.

The property prides itself on its family-friendly rooms and the added bonus of a terrace, where guests can soak up the sun or enjoy a quiet evening under the stars. For those who value connectivity and convenience, free WiFi and private parking are available, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

Beyond the confines of Chill Out Apartment, guests will find Mango Beach and Maestral Beach within walking distance, offering more opportunities for seaside relaxation and adventure. For those looking to explore beyond Sarandë, Ioannina Airport is located 96 km away, making it a convenient gateway for international travelers.

With its thoughtful amenities and prime location, Chill Out Apartment stands out as a top accommodation choice for those visiting Sarandë, promising a stay that is as relaxing as it is memorable.
