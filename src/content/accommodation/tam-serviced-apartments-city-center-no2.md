Guests at TAM Serviced Apartments City Center No2 are treated to a seamless experience, thanks to the 24-hour front desk, round-the-clock security, and the convenience of complimentary WiFi access across the premises. The property prides itself on ensuring privacy and ease, with services such as private check-in and check-out, alongside dry cleaning facilities, enhancing each stay.

The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and guest comfort. It boasts a spacious layout that includes one bedroom, a bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower, and a living area that merges seamlessly with a fully equipped kitchen. The inclusion of a balcony offering serene street views, coupled with soundproofing, ensures a tranquil retreat from the city's hustle and bustle. For entertainment, guests can enjoy a flat-screen TV complete with streaming services. Additionally, the apartment is designed to be allergy-free and strictly non-smoking, catering to the health and well-being of all guests.

For those looking to explore Tirana on wheels, the apartment facilitates both bicycle and car rental services, making it effortless to visit nearby attractions such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Museum of History Albania, and the House of Leaves. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 13 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

In essence, TAM Serviced Apartments City Center No2 stands as a beacon of modern living and convenience, offering a perfect blend of home-like comfort and the luxuries of a premium accommodation choice in Tirana.
