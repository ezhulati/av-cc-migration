Each apartment at "Home By the Sea" is a testament to modern living, boasting air-conditioned spaces that ensure a cool retreat from the sun-soaked adventures outside. Guests are welcomed into a world of convenience with private entrances leading to family-friendly rooms, designed to cater to the needs of every traveler. The heart of each unit is a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a refrigerator, making self-catering a breeze. The living areas are furnished thoughtfully, featuring comfortable seating, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine for longer stays. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, each equipped with a walk-in shower, ensuring a refreshing start and end to each day.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is available throughout the property, keeping guests connected with the world. For those who desire a little extra, select rooms boast terraces, offering private outdoor spaces to relax and soak in the serene surroundings.

"Home By the Sea" is not just about the immediate comforts. Its location places guests within a 45 km radius of Skanderbeg Square and a short drive from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, inviting explorers to venture beyond the beach and immerse themselves in the rich culture and breathtaking landscapes Albania has to offer. "Home By the Sea - HBS BRAND NEW" emerges as a beacon of tranquility and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay for those looking to experience the best of Golem's coastal charm.
