Accommodations at Hotel Virginia are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen cable TVs and refrigerators. Each room boasts a furnished balcony, allowing guests to savor the fresh sea breeze from the privacy of their accommodations. The en-suite bathrooms are well-appointed with showers, bidets, slippers, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a comfortable and refreshing stay.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Hotel Virginia enhances the guest experience with its on-site bar and restaurant, where delicious meals and refreshing drinks can be enjoyed. Families will appreciate the indoor play area, a thoughtful addition that keeps younger guests entertained.

Strategically located, the hotel is a mere 1.6 km from the Port of Durres and 3.5 km from the historic Durres Amphitheatre, making it an ideal base for exploring the local sights. For those arriving by air, Tirana Airport is 41 km away, with airport shuttle services available for a smooth transition to and from the hotel.

Hotel Virginia stands out as a top-tier choice for travelers to Durrës, offering a harmonious blend of convenience, comfort, and leisure facilities, all set against the backdrop of the stunning Adriatic coast.
