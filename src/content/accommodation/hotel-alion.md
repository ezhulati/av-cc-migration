Guests at Hotel Alion start their day on the right note with a continental breakfast, a delightful spread available each morning. For those looking to explore the rich history and vibrant culture of the area, the hotel's reception desk stands ready to organize various excursions and daily trips to the nearby towns of Durres, Kruje, and Tirana, albeit at an additional surcharge, promising an unforgettable exploration of Albania's treasures.

Strategically located, Hotel Alion is just 3.2 km away from the bustling Port of Durres and 5 km from the scenic Rock of Kavaje, making it an ideal base for both leisure and adventure. For guests arriving by air, the convenience is further enhanced by the availability of a paid airport shuttle service from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 34 km from the hotel. With its thoughtful amenities and strategic location, Hotel Alion stands out as a premier choice for those seeking to discover the charm and beauty of Durrës and beyond.
