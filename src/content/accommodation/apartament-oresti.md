Apartament Oresti stands out for its commitment to guest convenience and comfort. The property boasts a range of facilities designed to make each stay as enjoyable as possible. Guests have access to a shared kitchen, perfect for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a shared lounge, which serves as a cozy gathering space. The entire apartment benefits from complimentary WiFi, ensuring that guests stay connected during their visit.

The accommodation itself is a testament to modern living, featuring a spacious bedroom, a well-appointed living room, and a balcony that offers stunning views of the garden. The apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV, an oven, a microwave, and a bathroom complete with a bidet, catering to all the needs of modern travelers. For added convenience, the unit is air-conditioned and includes a bath and a dressing room. A laundry service is also available, adding to the hassle-free experience.

Located 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, Apartament Oresti is not only accessible but also serves as the perfect base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Korçë and its surroundings. Whether visiting for leisure or business, guests will find this apartment to be the ideal choice for a memorable stay.
