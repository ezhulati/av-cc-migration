The apartment itself is a study in modern comfort, boasting a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. Guests can unwind in a spacious living room, stay entertained with a flat-screen TV, and prepare meals in a fully equipped kitchen that includes both an oven and a microwave. The convenience of having a bathroom with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the property's attention to detail in ensuring a hassle-free stay. Moreover, the apartment offers captivating city views, adding another layer to its appeal.

For those needing essentials or quick snacks, the on-site minimarket is a welcome feature, ensuring that everything needed for a comfortable stay is within easy reach. While the allure of the beach is undeniable, guests also have the opportunity to explore further afield with Skanderbeg Square located 41 km away and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offering scenic rides at 45 km from the apartment. For international travelers, the convenience of being 36 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is enhanced by the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

Nouveau 623 stands out not only for its prime location and comprehensive amenities but also for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests. This property is not just an accommodation; it's a gateway to experiencing the coastal charm of Durrës in unparalleled comfort and style.
