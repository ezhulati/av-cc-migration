The apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of its guests, featuring two separate bedrooms, a bathroom, and a living room adorned with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dining area and microwave, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals in the comfort of their own space. Additionally, the duplex ensures a restful stay with soundproofing and a private entrance for maximum privacy.

Located just a stone's throw away from the inviting sands of Durres Beach, the property positions itself as an ideal base for beach lovers. Meanwhile, those looking to explore further afield will find Skanderbeg Square within a manageable distance of 38 km. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 32 km away, making Luxury Sea View Duplex an accessible haven for both local and international travelers.
