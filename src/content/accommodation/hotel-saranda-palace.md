Accommodations at Hotel Saranda Palace are designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Each air-conditioned room features a balcony, allowing guests to enjoy their own private outdoor space. Modern amenities, including a flat-screen cable TV and a refrigerator, are standard in every room, ensuring a stay that blends relaxation with the comforts of home. The en suite bathrooms are equipped with showers, complimentary toiletries, and hairdryers, with select rooms offering captivating sea views.

Guests of Hotel Saranda Palace are treated to complimentary sun loungers and parasols at the hotel's private beach, making it easy to enjoy the coastal beauty without any additional cost. The front desk offers a luggage storage service and a safe for valuables, adding an extra layer of convenience to the guest experience.

Located just 850 meters from the Port of Sarandë, the hotel provides easy access to ferry lines to the Island of Corfu, making it an ideal base for exploring the wider region. Corfu International Airport is a mere 35 km away, ensuring smooth travel arrangements for international guests.

Hotel Saranda Palace emerges as a top choice for discerning travelers, offering a blend of leisure and luxury in the heart of Sarandë.
