The accommodation is thoughtfully designed, featuring a bedroom, a bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels for entertainment. The dining space, alongside a fully equipped kitchen, allows guests the freedom to prepare and enjoy their meals. Perhaps most captivating is the balcony, offering breathtaking sea views, perfect for dining al fresco while soaking in the city's ambiance. The addition of a private entrance and soundproofing ensures an intimate and undisturbed stay.

For convenience, the property includes a snack bar and a minimarket, catering to guests' immediate needs. Additionally, a shared lounge area provides a space for relaxation and socializing.

Located near Saranda City Beach, La Petite Beach, and Maestral Beach, Teuta Apartament is ideally situated for guests eager to explore the local coastline. With Ioannina Airport 95 km away, the apartment serves as a comfortable and accessible base for both short and extended stays in Sarandë.
