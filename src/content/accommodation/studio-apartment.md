The property shines with its thoughtful amenities, offering guests a seamless stay with free WiFi accessible across the premises. Currila Beach, another picturesque locale, is conveniently located just 1.9 km from the doorstep, promising serene beach days within easy reach.

Designed with comfort in mind, the apartment features a single bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals, and a well-appointed bathroom, all within a smoke-free environment. This makes it an ideal choice for solo travelers or couples looking for a cozy getaway.

For those drawn to historical sites, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 11 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich past. Additionally, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha stands 39 km from the apartment, presenting an intriguing excursion for history buffs. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest air travel hub, located 33 km away, facilitating easy arrivals and departures.

Studio Apartment in Durrës emerges as a compelling choice for guests aiming to balance leisurely beach days with the allure of Albania's cultural and historical treasures.
