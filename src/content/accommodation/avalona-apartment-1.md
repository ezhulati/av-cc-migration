This apartment is a haven of tranquility, offering guests a soundproof environment equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a restful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle. The living space is thoughtfully designed, featuring one cozy bedroom, a bathroom stocked with fresh bed linen and towels, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV with streaming services, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen allows guests to feel at home, while the option to dine outdoors with a mountainous backdrop adds a touch of luxury to the stay. The thoughtful touch of providing fruits as an additional in-room amenity further enhances the guest experience.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local area, Avalona Apartment 1 does not disappoint. The property is conveniently located within a building that houses a lift and a minimarket, ensuring essentials are always within reach. Free WiFi throughout the apartment allows guests to stay connected, whether planning the day's activities or sharing holiday snaps with loved ones.

Adventure seekers will find plenty to keep them entertained, with diving and cycling opportunities available in the vicinity of Vlorë. For those willing to venture a little further, the mystical Kuzum Baba is just 3 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's spiritual heritage.

Situated 150 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Avalona Apartment 1 represents an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers. This apartment not only promises a stay filled with comfort and convenience but also serves as a gateway to the enchanting landscapes and vibrant culture of Vlorë.
