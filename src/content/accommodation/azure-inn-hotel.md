The hotel prides itself on its commitment to service, featuring a 24-hour front desk ready to cater to guests' needs, efficient airport transfer services, and ample luggage storage space. Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is available throughout the premises, ensuring guests stay connected with the world or share their memorable experiences instantly.

Rooms at Azure Inn Hotel are designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is a sanctuary of relaxation, equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and complimentary toiletries, making for a refreshing start or end to the day. Select rooms also boast balconies, offering guests their own private vista to enjoy the serene surroundings. Comfort is further enhanced with the provision of quality bed linen and towels in every room.

The hotel's location is ideal for beach enthusiasts, with Flamingo Beach just 500 meters away and the scenic Santa Quaranta Beach within a 1.1 km reach. For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 98 km from the hotel, making Azure Inn Hotel an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors seeking a tranquil escape by the sea.
