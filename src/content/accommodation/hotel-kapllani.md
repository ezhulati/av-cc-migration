Guests at Hotel Kapllani are treated to a variety of room options, each featuring a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV for a touch of home comfort. Select accommodations also offer captivating city views, adding an extra layer of enjoyment to the stay. The attention to detail extends to the provision of bed linen and towels in every room, highlighting the hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Hotel Kapllani enhances the guest experience with its on-site restaurant and bar, perfect for relaxing evenings or casual meetings. The hotel's terrace offers an additional space for guests to unwind in the open air. Practical services such as room service, a 24-hour front desk, and currency exchange are readily available, ensuring a hassle-free visit.

For those looking to explore beyond Berat, the hotel is conveniently located 118 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, with a paid shuttle service available to facilitate travel arrangements. Hotel Kapllani stands out as a prime choice for both leisure and business travelers, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and quality service.
