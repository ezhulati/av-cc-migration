Each holiday home within the complex is designed to maximize comfort and enjoyment, featuring a balcony that opens up to breathtaking garden views. The presence of an outdoor fireplace adds a unique touch to the beachfront living experience. Guests are welcomed around the clock, thanks to the 24-hour front desk service, and the convenience of free private parking and WiFi is a given.

Accommodations are thoughtfully equipped with a terrace that offers sweeping sea views, a cozy seating area for relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge for those who love to cook. The private bathroom comes with a shower, ensuring privacy and convenience. Moreover, each unit includes a patio with an outdoor dining area, where guests can enjoy meals against a backdrop of mountain views. Air conditioning and a desk in all units cater to the needs of both leisure and business travelers.

For dining and leisure, the complex features an on-site bar and provides barbecue facilities perfect for enjoying the warmer months outdoors. Families will appreciate the children's playground and picnic area, making it an ideal choice for guests of all ages.

Situated close to other popular beaches like Radhimë Beach and Baro Beach, and with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 164 km away, Novruzi Housing Complex serves as a perfect base to explore the beauty of Vlorë and its surroundings. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the comfort of a home away from home, this complex promises a memorable stay.
