Each room at Aragosta Hotel is a testament to modern luxury, equipped with comfortable seating areas, flat-screen TVs, and minibars for a touch of indulgence. The private bathrooms elevate the experience further, featuring hydromassage showers, complimentary toiletries, and hairdryers, with the thoughtful addition of slippers.

Culinary enthusiasts will delight in the hotel's restaurant and wine bar, a local favorite renowned for its exquisite Italian and Mediterranean cuisine, including fresh fish dishes. The selection of wines is impressive, promising a perfect pairing for every palate.

For those visiting on business or hosting events, Aragosta offers a fully equipped conference and meeting room, complete with state-of-the-art audio and video technology. Additional services such as laundry and ironing are available upon request, ensuring every need is met.

The hotel's private beach, equipped with parasols and sun loungers, offers an exclusive escape to sunbathe, swim, or simply unwind with the soothing sounds of the sea. Located just a stone's throw from the water's edge, Aragosta Hotel & Restaurant serves as an ideal base for beach lovers and those looking to explore the scenic coastline.

Beyond its immediate surroundings, the hotel is conveniently situated for further adventures. The vibrant city of Tirana is a mere 35 km away, with Tirana Airport only 20 km from the hotel, making it an accessible choice for both local and international travelers.

In summary, Aragosta Hotel & Restaurant is more than just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises a memorable experience, blending modern comforts with the natural beauty of its beachfront location and the rich flavors of its culinary offerings.
