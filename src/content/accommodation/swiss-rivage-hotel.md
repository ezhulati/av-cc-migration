Accommodations at this 4-star hotel are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms equipped with complimentary WiFi and private bathrooms. For those who appreciate a view, select rooms offer picturesque cityscapes, adding an extra layer of enjoyment to the stay.

Culinary needs are well catered to with a daily breakfast selection that spans continental, Italian, and vegetarian options, ensuring a delightful start to the day for every guest.

Situated just 1.5 km from Radhimë Beach and a short 1.7 km from Kevi Beach, Swiss Rivage Hotel is ideally located for beach enthusiasts and explorers alike. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 162 km away, making the hotel a convenient choice for both local and overseas visitors.

With its combination of prime location, comprehensive amenities, and attentive service, Swiss Rivage Hotel emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay in Vlorë.
