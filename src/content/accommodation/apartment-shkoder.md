The apartment, enveloped in tranquility with its garden views, presents a well-appointed living space. It is equipped with air conditioning for comfort in every season. The layout includes two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a kitchen that comes fully equipped with modern amenities such as a dishwasher and a coffee machine, catering to all culinary needs. The presence of two bathrooms, each featuring a walk-in shower and slippers, adds a layer of convenience and luxury to the guest experience. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, making it easy to unwind after a day of exploration.

Adding to the allure of Apartment Shkoder is the thoughtful inclusion of a private entrance, ensuring that guests enjoy an undisturbed and intimate stay. For those looking to explore beyond the city, Podgorica Airport is conveniently located just 59 km away, making this apartment an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Shkodër.
