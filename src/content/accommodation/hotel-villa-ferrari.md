Guests are greeted with impeccable service around the clock, thanks to the 24-hour front desk and thoughtful luggage storage options. The rooms at Hotel Villa Ferrari are designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Each guest room is equipped with a private bathroom, featuring a shower and hairdryer, ensuring a refreshing stay. The modern amenities don't end there; a flat-screen TV and air conditioning are standard in all rooms, with select accommodations offering the added luxury of a balcony. To enhance the comfort of your stay, each room is also furnished with quality bed linen and towels.

For those eager to explore Tirana's rich cultural tapestry, Hotel Villa Ferrari is ideally positioned near several attractions that capture the essence of the city. Skanderbeg Square, the House of Leaves, and Rinia Park are all within close proximity, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local culture and history. Moreover, the convenience of being just 13 km away from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Hotel Villa Ferrari a prime choice for both leisure and business travelers seeking a memorable and hassle-free experience in Albania's capital.
