The villa, with its air-conditioned spaces, presents a harmonious blend of comfort and style. It features a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a kitchen equipped with an oven and a coffee machine, ensuring guests feel at home. The bathroom is fitted with a walk-in shower and slippers, adding a touch of elegance to the experience. The outdoor dining area and the warmth of the fireplace provide perfect spots for relaxation, regardless of the season. Moreover, the villa boasts a private entrance, offering unparalleled privacy.

For those looking to unwind, the villa's lounge area serves as an ideal spot. Additionally, the convenience of an on-site minimarket caters to any last-minute needs. For the adventurous, a bicycle rental service is available, inviting guests to explore the picturesque surroundings at their own pace.

Situated 58 km from Podgorica Airport, Historical Center City House is not just a place to stay but a gateway to experiencing the charm and history of Shkodër, making it a top choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.
