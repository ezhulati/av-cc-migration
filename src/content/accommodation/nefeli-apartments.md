Each apartment at Nefeli is a haven of relaxation, equipped with air conditioning to provide a cool retreat from the Mediterranean warmth. The convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi adds to the seamless stay experience. The interiors boast a thoughtful layout, including a terrace that presents breathtaking sea views, a cozy seating area for unwinding, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a fridge for those who love to prepare their meals. The private bathroom comes with a shower and a hairdryer, ensuring guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay.

What sets Nefeli Apartments apart is the attention to detail in ensuring guest comfort. All units are designed to be allergy-free and soundproof, promising a peaceful and uninterrupted stay. The addition of a balcony with an outdoor dining area in every apartment allows guests to enjoy their meals against the backdrop of stunning city views.

For those keen to explore beyond the comforts of their apartment, Butrint National Park offers a glimpse into Albania's rich history and natural beauty, located just 15 km away. Furthermore, the property is conveniently situated 95 km from Ioannina Airport, making it easily accessible for international travelers.

Nefeli Apartments stands as a testament to thoughtful hospitality, where every aspect of the guest experience is catered to with precision and care.
