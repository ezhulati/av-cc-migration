Each meticulously designed unit at Villa Anastasia Ksamil boasts a terrace that frames breathtaking sea views, enhancing the dining experience with the backdrop of the Ionian Sea. The interiors are thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a satellite flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a kitchen that comes complete with an oven, fridge, and coffee machine, ensuring guests feel right at home. The addition of a private bathroom with a bidet in every unit adds a touch of comfort and privacy.

For those who prefer dining under the sky, an outdoor dining area is available, allowing guests to savor their meals amidst the villa's tranquil garden setting. Convenience is further amplified with an on-site minimarket, providing easy access to essentials and snacks.

Adventure and relaxation seamlessly blend at Villa Anastasia Ksamil, where guests can engage in local activities such as fishing, exploring the nearby Paradise Beach, or delving into the historical allure of Butrint National Park, located just a short drive away. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 92 km from the villa, making it a feasible option for those coming from afar.

Villa Anastasia Ksamil stands as a testament to the perfect balance of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, promising an unforgettable stay for families and leisure travelers alike.
