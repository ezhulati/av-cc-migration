The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a balcony that offers serene garden views. It is well-appointed with a bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen that comes complete with modern appliances including an oven and a toaster. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with complimentary towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of guests. Notably, the apartment also offers views of the inner courtyard, enhancing the sense of tranquility.

For those who appreciate the outdoors, the apartment complex features a picnic area, perfect for enjoying a day under the sky. The proximity to Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, just 1.7 km away, and the historical Skanderbeg Square, 46 km distant, provides ample opportunity for exploration and enjoyment. Additionally, the convenience of being 41 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Lovely Apartment Near the Beach an attractive option for both domestic and international travelers.
