The highlight of Te Nana Home is its spacious apartment, which boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring ample space for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The apartment doesn't fall short on practicalities either; a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and microwave, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals. Additionally, the convenience of a washing machine and two bathrooms with walk-in showers adds to the comfort of the stay. For added luxury, towels and bed linen are provided, ensuring a touch of home. It's worth noting that the apartment is both non-smoking and soundproof, promising a serene retreat from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

For those looking to explore the surrounding area, Te Nana Home offers a shuttle service, and a bicycle rental service is available for an eco-friendly way to sightsee. With Podgorica Airport just 58 km away, the apartment serves as an ideal base for both local and international travelers.
