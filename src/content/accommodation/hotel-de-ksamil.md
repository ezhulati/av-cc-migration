Accommodations at Hotel de Ksamil are designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is thoughtfully equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for a homely feel. The private bathrooms come with a shower and hairdryer, ensuring a refreshing start to the day. For those desiring a bit more, selected rooms feature a patio, offering a private oasis to unwind.

The hotel's location is unbeatable for beach enthusiasts. Paradise Beach is a mere 300 meters away, while the tranquil Lori Beach is just a 500-meter stroll. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 93 km from the hotel, making Hotel de Ksamil an accessible haven for both local and overseas visitors.

With round-the-clock front desk service, airport transfers, room service, and complimentary WiFi, guests are assured a stay that combines the ease of modern amenities with the charm of Ksamil's coastal beauty.
