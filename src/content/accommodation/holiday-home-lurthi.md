The accommodations at Holiday Home Lurthi are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring flat-screen TVs for entertainment and private bathrooms equipped with showers and hair dryers for convenience. The kitchens in each unit are fully furnished with a dishwasher, an oven, and a fridge, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. Moreover, the provision of bed linen and towels in each apartment underscores the complex's attention to detail and commitment to guest comfort.

For those interested in exploring the local area, the historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra lies 42 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history, while the natural beauty of Lake Skadar is just 44 km from the property. Additionally, the convenience of being located 49 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Holiday Home Lurthi an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers.

With its strategic location, well-appointed accommodations, and thoughtful amenities, Holiday Home Lurthi emerges as a top-tier lodging option for families and individuals alike, promising a memorable stay by the Albanian coast.
