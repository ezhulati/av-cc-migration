The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for unwinding after a day of exploration, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals, and a bathroom. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV and complimentary WiFi ensures that guests remain connected and entertained throughout their stay. Additionally, the balcony offers serene garden views, providing a tranquil backdrop for morning coffees or evening relaxation.

Situated a mere 46 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, and with the House of Leaves and Rinia Park both 49 km away, the 5th Floor Apartment 2+1 Qerret stands as an ideal base for both short-term visits and extended stays. Offering a smoke-free environment, this property caters to those who prioritize a clean and healthy living space. For travelers looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and culture of Golem while enjoying the comforts of home, this apartment emerges as a distinguished choice.
