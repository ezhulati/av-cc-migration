The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days. It houses a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a microwave, catering to all culinary needs. The bathroom is modern and convenient, boasting a walk-in shower and providing slippers for added comfort. Guests will find the apartment well-appointed with towels and bed linen, and can enjoy delightful garden views that promise a tranquil backdrop to their stay.

For moments of relaxation, the garden offers a peaceful retreat where guests can unwind and soak in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Situated 46 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and just 5 km from the iconic Rock of Kavaje, 2D Apartment 2 GOLEM serves as an ideal base for exploring the local area. Moreover, the property is conveniently located 41 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it an accessible choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a blend of convenience and comfort by the sea.
