Accommodations at Villa Shpetim Zogaj are thoughtfully designed, with terraces that frame breathtaking mountain vistas, satellite flat-screen TVs for entertainment, and kitchens that are both modern and well-equipped. The privacy of a bathroom with a bidet in every unit, alongside a safety deposit box and complimentary WiFi, ensures a stay that is as secure as it is comfortable. Select rooms elevate the experience further with the addition of a patio. The villa's outdoor dining area invites guests to savor meals under the open sky, adding a memorable touch to dining experiences.

Located a mere 800 meters from Sunset Beach and within easy reach of Coco Beach, the villa's position allows guests to effortlessly explore the area's coastal beauty. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is situated 93 km away, making Villa Shpetim Zogaj a convenient gateway to the enchanting surroundings and beyond.
