The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities and homely comfort. Guests are treated to a well-equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and microwave, ensuring that home-cooked meals are a delightful option during their stay. The living space is thoughtfully designed with a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, making it an ideal spot for relaxation after a day of exploration. The convenience of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, adds to the apartment's appeal.

For those looking to venture a bit further, La Petite Beach is just 1.2 km away, while the serene Maestral Beach is located 1.4 km from the property. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 97 km away, facilitates easy access for international travelers.

Sarrande stands out as a perfect retreat for those desiring a blend of city life and beachside relaxation, all from the comfort of a spacious and well-appointed apartment in the center of Sarandë.
