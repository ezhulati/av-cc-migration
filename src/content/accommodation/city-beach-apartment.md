This non-smoking property doesn't just boast an enviable location; it offers guests a spacious sanctuary complete with two bedrooms, a bathroom, and an array of modern amenities. From the comfort of air conditioning to the convenience of free WiFi, every detail has been thoughtfully considered. The apartment is well-appointed with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The fully equipped kitchen invites guests to whip up their favorite meals, adding a touch of home to their holiday. Moreover, the balcony presents an opportunity to enjoy captivating city views, making every moment spent indoors just as memorable as those outdoors.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, VIP Beach is a mere 2.1 km away, offering a more exclusive seaside experience. Meanwhile, the allure of Butrint National Park, located 16 km from the apartment, calls to adventurers and history buffs alike. And with Ioannina Airport 96 km away, City Beach Apartment stands out as a remarkably accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, City Beach Apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the best of Sarandë, offering comfort, convenience, and captivating views, all wrapped up in a welcoming beachfront package.
