The apartment itself is a haven of space and comfort, boasting air-conditioned interiors that include a well-equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, ensuring guests feel right at home. The living area is designed for relaxation, featuring a flat-screen TV and comfortable seating, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The highlight, however, is the private terrace. Furnished and inviting, it offers unparalleled views of the sea, allowing guests to soak in the serene atmosphere from the comfort of their accommodation.

Accommodating up to two guests in its one-bedroom layout, the apartment also includes a modern bathroom fitted with a bidet, adding an extra layer of convenience. For those concerned about connectivity, free WiFi is available throughout the property, ensuring guests can stay connected with the world or plan their next adventure in Sarandë.

Situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, Clearwater View represents an excellent choice for travelers looking for a blend of scenic beauty, comfort, and convenience in one of Sarandë's most sought-after locations.
