The apartment itself is a haven of tranquility and space, boasting a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain views. It is well-appointed with a bedroom, a cozy living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a fridge, catering to all culinary needs. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bath, along with towels and bed linen, ensures a comfortable and hassle-free stay. Importantly, the entire accommodation is designated as non-smoking, maintaining a clean and healthy environment for guests.

Free WiFi blankets the property, keeping visitors connected with the world, while its proximity to Skanderbeg Square, only 1.2 km away, places guests in the heart of Tirana's cultural and social hub. Additionally, the apartment is surrounded by must-visit sites such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, offering a rich tapestry of activities and experiences.

For those traveling by air, the convenience is further enhanced by the close proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 14 km away, making EQ Apartaments a highly accessible retreat. Whether it's the allure of the city's landmarks, the comfort of a spacious apartment, or the serene views that one seeks, EQ Apartaments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice in Tirana.
