The highlight of Comfort 528 is undoubtedly its apartment offerings, which feature a spacious terrace that opens up to breathtaking sea views. Each apartment is thoughtfully designed with a separate bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances, including a microwave and fridge, ensuring guests can prepare meals with ease. The inclusion of a bidet in the bathroom, along with towels and bed linen, adds a touch of home-like comfort. For dining al fresco, an outdoor dining area is available, enhancing the experience of meals with a view.

For those needing essentials or a quick snack, the convenience of an on-site minimarket means guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

Located just 1.9 km from the serene Golem Beach and within a reasonable distance of 42 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, Comfort 528 serves as an ideal base for exploring the area's attractions. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 36 km away, makes it a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a beachfront retreat in Durrës. Whether it's for the stunning sea views, the comprehensive amenities, or the prime location, Comfort 528 stands out as a top choice for travelers in search of a memorable stay.
