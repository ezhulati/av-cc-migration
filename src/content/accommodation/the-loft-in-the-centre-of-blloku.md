This air-conditioned apartment offers guests a seamless stay with its array of modern amenities. It boasts a bedroom equipped with a flat-screen TV that includes streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped with a dishwasher, an oven, and even a washing machine, catering to all domestic needs. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. It's worth noting that the apartment maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a fresh environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore Tirana on wheels, The Loft provides both bicycle and car rental services, making it effortless to venture around the city's attractions. Points of interest such as Reja - The Cloud, Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, and Rinia Park are conveniently located nearby, enriching the stay with cultural and historical insights. Additionally, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 15 km away, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest comfort, The Loft in the centre of Blloku is an exemplary choice for travelers aiming to experience Tirana in style and convenience.
