The apartments are a haven of comfort, each equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV for a touch of modernity. Privacy and convenience are guaranteed with a private bathroom in every unit, complete with a bidet and plush bathrobes. Free WiFi is available throughout, with select rooms boasting the added luxury of a terrace. Attention to detail is evident, with each unit thoughtfully appointed with crisp bed linen and soft towels, ensuring a restful night's sleep.

For those looking to explore the area at their own pace, a car rental service is conveniently available on-site. The allure of Coco Bongo Beach lies just 1.7 kilometers away, offering a vibrant beach experience, while the historic Kuzum Baba is within a 7-kilometer reach, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local culture. Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest gateway, located 156 kilometers from Seaside Apartments, making it a manageable distance for both international and domestic travelers.

Seaside Apartments stands out as a distinguished choice for those in pursuit of a memorable stay, combining the essence of coastal living with the comforts of home.
