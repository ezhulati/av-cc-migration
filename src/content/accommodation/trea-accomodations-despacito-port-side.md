Each apartment is a testament to thoughtful design, featuring a terrace for soaking in the surroundings, a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven for home-cooked meals, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, and the inclusion of a washing machine adds a touch of home comfort. Privacy and convenience are paramount, with each unit housing a private bathroom outfitted with a bidet and a hair dryer. Select apartments elevate the experience further with breathtaking sea views, while essentials like a kettle, air conditioning, and ample wardrobe space are standard across all units.

Situated merely 1.6 km from the picturesque Vlore Beach and 2.3 km from the historic Independence Square, TreA Accommodations - Despacito Port Side offers an ideal base for exploring the vibrant surroundings. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, with the property providing a convenient paid airport shuttle service. This apartment complex not only promises a memorable stay but also delivers a perfect blend of location, comfort, and amenities.
