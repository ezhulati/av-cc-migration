The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with essential amenities, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining space, and a fully equipped kitchen. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking on site adds to the comfort of the stay. The balcony, offering views of the bustling city, serves as a perfect spot for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

For those looking to explore beyond the city's borders, Mediterranean Sea Apartment city center facilitates adventure with its car and bicycle rental services. Additionally, the provision of airport transfers ensures a hassle-free arrival and departure experience.

Situated 39 km from Skanderbeg Square and 43 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, the apartment positions guests perfectly to discover the wider region. Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the nearest airport, is conveniently located 33 km away, making Mediterranean Sea Apartment city center an excellent choice for travelers seeking both the vibrancy of the city and the tranquility of the coast.
