This non-smoking apartment facility stands out for its commitment to providing a seamless stay experience, highlighted by complimentary WiFi, air conditioning, and the added luxury of a bar on-site. Guests can also rely on the convenience of a 24-hour front desk, ready to assist at any hour.

Accommodation at 2V1 - 21 Dhjetori AM is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. The 1-bedroom apartment features a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, creating a perfect environment for relaxation. The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances, including an oven and a toaster, catering to guests' culinary needs. Additionally, the inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a hair dryer adds to the convenience.

For those keen to explore, the apartment is in close proximity to several of Tirana's attractions. The historical House of Leaves, the lush Rinia Park, and the iconic Clock Tower of Tirana are all within easy reach. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 14 km away, ensuring a hassle-free travel experience to and from the property.

2V1 - 21 Dhjetori AM emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local culture and attractions of Tirana.
