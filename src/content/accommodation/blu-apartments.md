Each apartment at Blu Apartments is a haven of relaxation, boasting private balconies that frame garden views, cozy seating areas for unwinding, and cable flat-screen TVs for entertainment. The heart of each unit is a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips. The privacy and comfort extend into the bathrooms, outfitted with bidets and hair dryers for a home-like experience. Select apartments elevate the living experience further, offering terraces that feature outdoor dining areas and breathtaking sea views, perfect for those tranquil evenings under the stars.

For those who need to stay connected or get some work done, a desk and kettle in each unit add a touch of convenience to the serene ambiance. Beyond the confines of these exquisite apartments, guests can explore the lush garden or gather in the shared lounge, fostering a sense of community and relaxation.

Situated 39 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 43 km from the adventurous Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Blu Apartments serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich culture and natural beauty of the area. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 33 km away, guests enjoy easy access to and from this tranquil retreat.

Blu Apartments stands out as a sanctuary for those seeking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Durrës, offering a blend of privacy, comfort, and scenic beauty that caters to the needs of modern travelers.
