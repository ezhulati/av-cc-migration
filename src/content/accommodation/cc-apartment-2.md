The apartment itself is a study in spaciousness and modern amenities. Guests will find themselves at home in a two-bedroom setup, complemented by a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels for those evenings in. The kitchen doesn't skimp on conveniences, featuring a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, ensuring that every culinary need is met with ease. Additionally, the inclusion of a bidet and a dressing room in the unit adds a touch of luxury and practicality to the living space. Perhaps most enticing is the promise of sea views, offering a serene backdrop to a stay here.

For those incidental needs or quick snacks, the presence of a minimarket on the premises is a thoughtful touch, adding to the overall ease of staying at CC Apartment 2. While the allure of the surrounding beaches and cultural sites is undeniable, the apartment's location also makes it a convenient base for explorers, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 153 km away, ensuring that arrivals and departures are as stress-free as the stay itself.

In summary, CC Apartment 2 stands out as a top-tier accommodation option in Vlorë, blending modern comforts with unbeatable proximity to both the beach and city attractions, making it an ideal spot for both relaxation and adventure.
