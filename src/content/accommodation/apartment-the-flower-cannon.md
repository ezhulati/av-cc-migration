This air-conditioned accommodation doesn't just offer a place to stay; it presents a home away from home with its spacious terrace and complimentary WiFi access.

Those who choose to stay here will find themselves in a beautifully appointed apartment featuring two cozy bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and not one but two modern bathrooms.

Entertainment needs are also met with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

Located just 33 km from Corfu Town, Apartment The Flower Cannon stands out as an ideal base for exploring the beauty and charm of the surrounding area.
