This apartment doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a slice of home with its thoughtfully furnished spaces. Guests are treated to a cozy bedroom, a clean and well-appointed bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living area that boasts a flat-screen TV with streaming services for those evenings in. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to every culinary need, while the balcony presents captivating city views, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure.

Privacy and comfort are paramount at Ashley Home 2, with a private entrance ensuring guests can retreat to their personal haven with ease. Upon arrival, visitors are welcomed with fresh fruits, a small but telling gesture of the hospitality that awaits them. For those conscious about their health and environment, the accommodation's non-smoking policy ensures a clean and fresh atmosphere throughout their stay.

Located conveniently, Ashley Home 2 is not far from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the ancient Durres Amphitheatre, inviting guests to delve into the rich tapestry of Albania's past. For travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 32 km away, adds to the appeal, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

Ashley Home 2 stands out as a compelling choice for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and a touch of home in Durrës.
