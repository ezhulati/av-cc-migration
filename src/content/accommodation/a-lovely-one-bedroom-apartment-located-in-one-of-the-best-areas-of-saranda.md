This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it's a haven of comfort and convenience. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a private balcony, ideal for savoring morning coffees or evening cocktails against the backdrop of Sarandë's vibrant cityscape. The living space is thoughtfully designed, featuring a well-equipped kitchen complete with an oven and toaster for those who delight in crafting meals. Entertainment is readily available with a TV set for relaxing evenings in.

For those looking to explore the wider area, the apartment facilitates adventure with a car rental service, ensuring the best of Sarandë and its surroundings are within easy reach. And for guests arriving from afar, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service from Ioannina Airport, located 97 km away, makes the journey to this coastal retreat as seamless as possible.

With free WiFi throughout the property and Saranda City Beach just 300 meters away, this apartment stands as a beacon for travelers seeking the perfect blend of relaxation, convenience, and adventure in one of Sarandë's finest locales.
