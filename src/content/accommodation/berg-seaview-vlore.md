The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen comes complete with modern appliances including an oven, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, catering to all culinary needs. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort to the stay. Guests are also treated to serene lake views, further enhancing the apartment's appeal.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the convenience of an on-site bar cannot be overstated.

Location-wise, Berg Seaview Vlore is perfectly positioned less than 1 km from the inviting sands of Vlore Beach, and a mere 3.8 km from the historic Kuzum Baba, offering guests a mix of leisure and exploration opportunities. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 153 km away, making the apartment accessible for both local and foreign visitors.

In summary, Berg Seaview Vlore stands out as a top-tier accommodation option, providing guests with a harmonious blend of modern amenities, stunning views, and a strategic location for an unforgettable stay in Vlorë.
