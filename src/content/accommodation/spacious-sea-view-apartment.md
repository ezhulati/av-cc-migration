The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, ensuring ample room for families or groups. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful touches such as high-quality bed linen, plush towels, and a terrace that presents breathtaking sea views, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a cozy dining area, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-cooked meal, making it an ideal setting for both short and long stays.

Adding to the comfort, the apartment features a private entrance and soundproofing, providing a peaceful and undisturbed experience. For those looking to explore beyond the city, the historic Butrint National Park is just 17 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage.

Located 97 km from Ioannina Airport, Spacious Sea View Apartment serves as a perfect base for both leisure and adventure in the beautiful city of Sarandë, promising an unforgettable stay with its blend of convenience, comfort, and stunning views.
