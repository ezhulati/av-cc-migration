One of the highlights of this property is its terrace, complemented by a balcony, allowing guests to soak in the vibrant atmosphere of Vlorë without leaving the comfort of their accommodation. Despite being a haven for smokers, the apartment maintains a non-smoking policy indoors, ensuring a fresh and welcoming environment for all guests.

For those who appreciate the convenience of having refreshments on hand, the on-site bar serves a variety of drinks. 

Situated just over 2 kilometers from the picturesque Ri Beach, and in close proximity to notable landmarks such as Independence Square and Kuzum Baba, guests at Seafront Apartment are perfectly positioned to explore the rich history and natural beauty of the area. Moreover, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 151 kilometers away, the apartment serves as an accessible getaway for both local and international travelers.

Seafront Apartment not only promises a stay that combines the comfort of modern living with the charm of Vlorë's coastal lifestyle but also offers an ideal base for those eager to explore the enchanting surroundings of this Albanian gem.
