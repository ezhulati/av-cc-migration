Each apartment at Crown Deluxe Apartments is a testament to comfort and style, boasting private entrances that lead into spaces filled with natural light. The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchens are well-equipped, catering to guests' every need. Select units elevate the experience further with patios offering panoramic city views, while others delight with terraces or balconies that frame the breathtaking sea and mountain landscapes.

The attention to detail extends to the provision of crisp bed linen and plush towels in every unit, ensuring a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxuries of hotel living. Guests are invited to unwind in the lush garden, a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life.

Located just a short distance from the allure of Saranda Bay Beach and the historical wonders of Butrint National Park, Crown Deluxe Apartments serves as an ideal base for explorers. With Ioannina Airport 98 km away, the journey to this haven of relaxation is a seamless experience, promising an unforgettable stay in one of Sarandë's most cherished accommodations.
