Guests are welcomed into a world of comfort and convenience, with each air-conditioned apartment boasting a private balcony that opens up to serene garden views. The space is thoughtfully designed with a blend of modern and homely touches, featuring three cozy bedrooms, a spacious living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook. The inclusion of three well-appointed bathrooms, complete with a bidet and bath, adds to the convenience, ensuring a comfortable stay for families and groups. For those in need of it, towels and linens are available upon request, adding an extra layer of convenience to the stay.

Privacy and security are paramount at Stefan Vasili, with each apartment offering a private entrance and the reassurance of full-day security, allowing guests to relax in peace and comfort. Situated 97 km from Ioannina Airport, the property is an accessible getaway for both local and international travelers seeking a blend of city excitement and natural tranquility. Stefan Vasili stands out as a preferred choice for those looking to experience the best of Sarandë in a comfortable, secure, and welcoming environment.
