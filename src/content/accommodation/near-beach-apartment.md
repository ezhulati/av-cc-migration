The apartment, a breath of fresh air with its modern renovations, boasts a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, and a balcony offering serene views of the inner courtyard. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV, while the kitchen comes fully furnished with an oven and a microwave, catering to all culinary needs. The bathroom features a walk-in shower, enhancing the comfort of your stay. Additional amenities such as bed linen, towels, and laundry service are thoughtfully provided, ensuring a hassle-free experience.

Situated 47 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and within easy reach of the Rock of Kavaje, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the local sights. The Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is also accessible, located 47 km away, offering a glimpse into the country's past. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 41 km away, making the Near Beach Apartment an excellent choice for both leisure and exploration in the heart of Golem.
