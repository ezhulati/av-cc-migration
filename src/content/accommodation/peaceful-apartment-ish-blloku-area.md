This meticulously appointed one-bedroom apartment is a haven of convenience, offering air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and a host of modern amenities. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge, ensuring a stay that blends comfort with the ease of home. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a thoughtful touch to the well-furnished space.

Situated a mere 400 meters from the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the apartment serves as a gateway to exploring Tirana's rich history. Other nearby attractions include the Pyramid of Tirana, Reja - The Cloud, and the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, each offering a unique glimpse into the city's diverse heritage and contemporary art scene.

For those arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 17 km away, making Peaceful Apartment Ish-Blloku Area an accessible choice for international travelers seeking a blend of tranquility and urban exploration in Albania's bustling capital.
