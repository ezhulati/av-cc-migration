This ground-floor apartment is not just about its prime location; it's a haven of comfort and style. With air-conditioning, free WiFi, and a private balcony that offers captivating city views, guests are assured of a stay that combines relaxation with the convenience of modern living. The apartment boasts a spacious layout, featuring a bedroom, a living area with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, and a soundproof environment ensuring a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life.

The fully equipped kitchen is a highlight, catering to guests' culinary needs with an oven, toaster, washing machine, and fridge. The outdoor dining area invites guests to savor their meals while soaking in the sea views, adding a touch of magic to every dining experience. It's important to note that this apartment is committed to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests.

Accessibility is also a key feature, with Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, making Azzur Luxury Apartment a convenient option for international travelers. Whether it's for the terrace that offers a breath of fresh air or the apartment's close proximity to must-visit beaches and parks, this property promises a memorable stay for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and vibrancy of Sarandë.
