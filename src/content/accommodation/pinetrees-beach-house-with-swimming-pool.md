The property prides itself on being a smoke-free environment, providing a clean and healthy atmosphere for all guests. The holiday home is thoughtfully designed with two cozy bedrooms and a bathroom, equipped with essential amenities such as bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The dining area and a fully equipped kitchen allow guests to prepare and enjoy their meals with ease. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the terrace, offering stunning pool views, where guests can dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area, surrounded by lush garden views.

For those seeking tranquility, the garden on the property serves as a perfect spot to unwind. The strategic location of PineTrees Beach House also means that guests have easy access to nearby attractions. Mali I Robit Beach is less than 1 km away, offering another splendid option for beach enthusiasts, while Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach is a mere 14-minute walk, presenting a chance to explore the local coastline further. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being just 41 km away adds to the appeal.

PineTrees Beach House with Swimming Pool is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that offers a memorable escape with its blend of comfort, privacy, and natural beauty.
