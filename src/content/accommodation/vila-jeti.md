The villa complex doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a home away from home with its well-appointed family rooms. Each unit is designed with comfort in mind, featuring a wardrobe for personal belongings, a balcony to enjoy the fresh sea breeze, a cozy seating area for relaxation, and a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. The convenience of air conditioning and a private bathroom in every unit adds to the comfort of guests, while a desk in each room ensures that those who need to stay connected or work can do so with ease.

For those who enjoy a drink under the sun or stars, the on-site bar serves as a perfect spot to unwind.

Located just 1.4 km from the vibrant Bora Bora Beach and a mere 3 km from the historical Butrint National Park, Vila Jeti offers an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural treasures of the area. Moreover, with Corfu International Airport 92 km away, it provides a feasible getaway for international travelers seeking the tranquility of Ksamil's shores.

With its combination of prime location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a peaceful retreat, Vila Jeti stands as a distinguished choice for those planning their next coastal getaway.
