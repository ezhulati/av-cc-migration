This one-bedroom apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a slice of home with its air-conditioned spaces, complemented by modern amenities. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a refrigerator, making self-catering a breeze. The inclusion of a washing machine, alongside the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the thoughtful touches designed to ensure a hassle-free stay.

Entertainment and connectivity are well catered for, with free WiFi and a cable flat-screen TV available for guests' use, ensuring that staying in touch or winding down after a day of exploration is effortlessly enjoyable.

For those arriving by car, the availability of private parking, albeit at an additional charge, adds an extra layer of convenience to the experience.

The apartment's location is a treasure trove for culture enthusiasts and shoppers alike, with the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the Clock Tower Tirana just a stone's throw away. Additionally, the proximity to historical sites such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and Tanners' Bridge, along with the modern Toptani Shopping Centre, offers a perfect blend of past and present.

For travelers flying into the city, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a manageable 16 km away, making 41° Bs Pazari City Center Comfort an accessible gateway to the delights of Tirana.
