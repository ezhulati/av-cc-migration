Each apartment at Majki Beach Apartments is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring balconies that offer breathtaking views, fully equipped kitchens for those who love to cook, and cozy seating areas perfect for unwinding. The presence of modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, washing machines, and private bathrooms with walk-in showers adds a layer of luxury, ensuring guests feel at home. The thoughtful inclusion of microwaves, fridges, stovetops, and kettles, along with essential bed linen and towels, underscores the property's commitment to guest satisfaction.

Culinary delights await at the on-site restaurant, where a blend of continental and Full English/Irish breakfast options greet guests each morning. The menu, rich with local specialties, fresh pastries, and pancakes, caters to a variety of tastes and preferences. The family-friendly dining venue extends its offerings to include dinner, lunch, brunch, and an enticing cocktail selection, promising memorable dining experiences throughout the day.

For those eager to explore, Majki Beach Apartments is ideally located near sightseeing tours, with a car rental service available to facilitate adventures beyond the beach. The proximity to Saranda City Beach, just 1.6 km away, and the historic Butrint National Park, 18 km distant, invites exploration and discovery. Additionally, the property eases travel concerns with a paid airport shuttle service to Ioannina Airport, located 98 km away, ensuring a seamless journey from arrival to departure.

Majki Beach Apartments stands out as a distinguished destination for those in pursuit of relaxation, culinary excellence, and the allure of the Albanian coast.
