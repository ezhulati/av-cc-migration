The highlight of Tanja Apartments is undoubtedly its spacious accommodations, boasting balconies that offer breathtaking sea views. Each apartment is thoughtfully designed with comfort in mind, featuring a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with modern amenities such as a toaster and a fridge. The convenience of a walk-in shower, along with the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. The added touch of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy and exclusivity for guests.

Situated a mere 1.7 km from the picturesque Saranda Bay Beach and within easy reach of Maestral Beach, Tanja Apartments serves as an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. For those inclined towards exploring the rich cultural heritage of the area, the historic Butrint National Park is just 18 km away, offering a glimpse into the ancient past of this enchanting region. Moreover, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 98 km away, provides convenient access for international travelers.

In summary, Tanja Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and charm of Sarandë, offering a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and scenic beauty.
