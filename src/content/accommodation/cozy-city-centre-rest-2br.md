Strategically located, the property is within walking distance of Tirana's most celebrated attractions. The National Gallery of Arts, the iconic Clock Tower, and the historic Et'hem Bey Mosque are all less than a kilometer away, with Skanderbeg Square just a 700-meter stroll from the doorstep. For those who prioritize connectivity, the entire apartment boasts complimentary WiFi, ensuring guests stay connected with the world while enjoying their urban escape.

The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom outfitted with a shower and hairdryer, ensuring a comfortable stay. Emphasizing a smoke-free environment, Cozy City Centre Rest 2BR caters to the health and well-being of all guests.

For culture enthusiasts and shoppers alike, the apartment's proximity to the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History, and the Toptani Shopping Centre offers a diverse itinerary just steps away. Moreover, with the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 14 km away, guests enjoy the ease of travel to and from this charming abode.

In summary, Cozy City Centre Rest 2BR stands out as an ideal accommodation for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant culture of Tirana while enjoying the comforts of a home-like setting.
