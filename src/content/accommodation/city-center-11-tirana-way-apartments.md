This air-conditioned, one-bedroom apartment doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a home away from home. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and microwave, ensuring all the comforts of home are at their fingertips. The provision of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of convenience to the stay.

For those keen on exploring local culinary delights, a variety of dining options await just steps from the apartment. Moreover, its strategic location places guests within easy reach of some of Tirana's most sought-after attractions. The National Museum of History Albania, House of Leaves, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania are all within proximity, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the cultural heartbeat of the city.

Situated 42 km from the Rock of Kavaje and a brief walk from the Clock Tower Tirana and Et'hem Bey Mosque, the property also offers a glimpse into the country's historical and architectural marvels. Additionally, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located just 1.6 km away, providing an intriguing peek into Albania's past.

For those traveling by air, the convenience of being just 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes City Center 11 - Tirana Way Apartments a compelling choice for both short and extended stays. With its blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to key attractions, this apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation option for visitors to Tirana.
