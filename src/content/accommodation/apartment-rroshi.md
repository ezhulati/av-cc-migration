With complimentary WiFi enveloping the entire property, guests are guaranteed seamless connectivity, making it an ideal spot for both leisure and business travelers. The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, featuring a private balcony that offers a slice of tranquility in the bustling city. It houses two elegantly appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-cooked meal. Entertainment needs are well catered to with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have access to their favorite shows and movies in the comfort of their accommodation.

The commitment to a smoke-free environment underscores the property's dedication to providing a healthy and refreshing living space for all guests. For those looking to explore beyond the city's borders, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is located 97 km away, making Apartment Rroshi an accessible gateway to the wonders of Sarandë and beyond.

In essence, Apartment Rroshi stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and connectivity, all wrapped up in the charming ambiance of Sarandë's scenic landscape.
