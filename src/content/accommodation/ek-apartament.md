The apartment itself is a spacious haven featuring a balcony that invites guests to bask in the breathtaking surroundings. It houses two bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a well-equipped kitchen, ensuring a stay that feels like home. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV for those moments of indoor relaxation.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the convenience of an on-site bar enhances the experience, offering a perfect setting for unwinding after a day of exploration. Additionally, a garden provides a tranquil space for guests to relax and soak in the natural beauty of the area.

Located just a short distance from notable landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, E&K Apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich culture and history of Vlorë. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is accessible, located 152 km away, making E&K Apartment a compelling choice for both local and international visitors seeking a memorable stay in Vlorë.
