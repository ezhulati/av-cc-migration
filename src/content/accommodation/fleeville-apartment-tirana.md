The apartment itself is a testament to spacious and comfortable living, featuring a balcony that offers captivating city views. Guests can enjoy the luxury of two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and an oven. The modern bathroom, complete with a walk-in shower, adds a touch of elegance to the space. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available in the apartment, complementing the serene street views that contribute to a peaceful stay.

Situated a mere 6.3 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Fleeville Apartment Tirana serves as an ideal base for exploring the city. Attractions within easy reach include Rinia Park, Reja - The Cloud, and the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, making it a perfect choice for those keen on discovering Tirana's rich cultural heritage. For travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 15 km away, ensures a hassle-free arrival and departure experience.
