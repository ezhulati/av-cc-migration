This beachfront gem boasts a garden, air conditioning, and the luxury of stepping out onto a balcony to embrace the sea breeze. Guests enjoy complimentary WiFi, ensuring that both leisure and business travelers stay connected. For those looking to explore the area or needing a ride from the airport, Navigator 525 simplifies travel logistics with its airport transfer and car rental services.

The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring two bedrooms, a living room with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and an equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and a fridge. The terrace offers breathtaking sea views, inviting guests to unwind and soak in the tranquil atmosphere. The property maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those who appreciate the convenience of having refreshments within reach, an on-site bar serves a variety of drinks. The location is also ideal for beach lovers, with Golem Beach just 2.5 km away. Cultural enthusiasts will find Skanderbeg Square, located 41 km from the apartment, worth a visit. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 36 km away, making Navigator 525 an accessible haven for both domestic and international guests.

In summary, Navigator 525 offers a harmonious blend of location, comfort, and amenities, making it a compelling choice for travelers visiting Durrës.
