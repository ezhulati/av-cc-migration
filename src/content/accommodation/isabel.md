The apartment itself is a haven of relaxation, featuring a spacious terrace that boasts breathtaking sea views. Guests can unwind in a cozy bedroom, lounge in a well-appointed living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and prepare meals in a kitchenette that comes complete with an oven and a fridge. The convenience of an on-site bathroom with a bidet adds to the apartment's appeal, ensuring a stay that combines home comfort with the luxury of beachfront living.

For those in need of essentials or a quick snack, the apartment's minimarket serves as a handy resource. While the tranquility of Isabel offers a perfect escape, adventure seekers will find Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within a reasonable distance, offering a glimpse into the rich culture and breathtaking landscapes of the area. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 36 km away, makes Isabel an accessible retreat for international travelers.

In summary, Isabel presents an idyllic setting for guests looking to enjoy the serene beauty of Durrës' coastline, with the added convenience of modern amenities and easy access to local attractions.
