This non-smoking property, located a mere 2.3 km from the vibrant Golem Beach, features a tastefully decorated 1-bedroom apartment. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV, providing entertainment after a day of exploration. A fully equipped kitchen allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, while the inclusion of a bathroom with a hairdryer adds to the convenience.

For those looking to venture further, Skanderbeg Square is 41 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich history and culture of the area. The Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 45 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for an unforgettable aerial view of the surroundings. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 35 km away, ensures ease of travel for international guests.

Julia Luxury Apartment stands out as an ideal accommodation choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury during their stay in Durrës.
