A stone's throw away from significant historical landmarks, Dhome me qera is conveniently situated 2.2 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and a short stroll of 1.6 km will lead guests to both the Clock Tower Tirana and the Et'hem Bey Mosque, enriching their stay with cultural immersion. The National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, another gem, is located just 1.4 km away, offering a taste of Albania's rich artistic heritage.

For those who appreciate the convenience of location, the apartment is within close proximity to the House of Leaves, Rinia Park, and the National Museum of History Albania, making it an ideal base for exploring the city's key attractions. Moreover, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is only 12 km away, ensuring easy arrivals and departures.

Guests will be pleased to find that Dhome me qera is a non-smoking accommodation, ensuring a clean and healthy environment during their stay. Whether in town for leisure or exploration, this apartment stands out as a top selection for those who value comfort, location, and a smoke-free atmosphere.
