Upon entering the air-conditioned abode, visitors are greeted by a thoughtfully designed space comprising two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs with an oven and a coffee machine. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet and plush bathrobes adds a touch of luxury to the stay, ensuring guests feel pampered throughout their visit. The holiday home's private entrance underscores the exclusive experience, welcoming guests with a selection of fresh fruits as a warm gesture of hospitality.

For those who appreciate convenience, the presence of a minimarket on the premises is a notable perk, allowing for easy access to essentials without the need to venture far. Adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts staying at Sea La Vie will find themselves delighted by the array of activities available in the surrounding area of Qarr, with fishing being a popular pastime. Moreover, the property boasts a picnic area, perfect for those wishing to enjoy a leisurely day outdoors amidst the scenic beauty of the locale.

Situated a mere 30 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and 125 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Sea La Vie offers an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural wonders of the region. This holiday home stands out as a prime choice for travelers in search of a peaceful getaway that does not compromise on accessibility or the comforts of home.
