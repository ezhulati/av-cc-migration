Situated just a stone's throw from cultural hotspots such as the Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Museum of History Albania, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the vibrant city. For those looking to venture further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is a mere 4.3 km away, while the Rock of Kavaje stands 44 km from the doorstep, promising adventures beyond the urban landscape.

The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and style, featuring two separate bedrooms, a bathroom, and a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, ensuring a cozy night in after a day of exploration. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dining area and dishwasher, invites guests to prepare their meals with ease, making it a perfect choice for both short and extended stays. Towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience.

Notably, the Grand New Bazaar Apartment prioritizes the well-being of its guests with non-smoking and soundproof accommodations, ensuring a peaceful and healthy stay. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently located 15 km away, facilitating stress-free travel arrangements.

In summary, Grand New Bazaar Apartment stands out as a sophisticated option for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and proximity to Tirana's cultural and historical treasures.
