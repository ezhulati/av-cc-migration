The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a living area that merges modernity with comfort. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring guests are never far from their favorite shows or movies. The kitchen doesn't shy away from impressing either, with a full suite of appliances including a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, toaster, and fridge. It's a space where culinary skills can truly come to life, supported by the provision of essential towels and bed linen for a hassle-free stay. For those with a taste for the finer things, the apartment thoughtfully includes options for wine or champagne, adding a touch of elegance to the ambiance.

Situated a manageable distance of 58 km from Podgorica Airport, the ORA Central Duplex Apartment offers a blend of accessibility and serenity, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both the vibrancy of city life and the comforts of home.
