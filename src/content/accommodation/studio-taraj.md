Each apartment at Studio Taraj is thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. A fully equipped kitchen awaits those who prefer home-cooked meals, complete with a dining table for enjoying them. Entertainment is readily available through the flat-screen TV, offering a wide range of satellite channels. The convenience of a private bathroom is enhanced with the inclusion of a bidet, hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries. Air conditioning ensures a comfortable environment year-round, while select units boast the added perk of a balcony for those who love to bask in the outdoor ambiance. Storage needs are easily met with the inclusion of a wardrobe in every unit.

For those intrigued by the natural beauty and historical significance of the area, Butrint National Park is a mere 2.9 km away, promising an enriching excursion. Furthermore, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 93 km from the premises, makes Studio Taraj an accessible retreat for international travelers.

In essence, Studio Taraj stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, offering a serene escape with all the comforts of home, making it an ideal spot for those looking to explore the beauty of Ksamil and its surroundings.
