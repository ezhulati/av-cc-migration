Each apartment at The Gwen is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, featuring balconies that present breathtaking sea views. The interiors are equipped with flat-screen TVs, kitchens that cater to every culinary need, and private bathrooms stocked with essentials including a hair dryer. For added convenience, guests will find ample storage space in the wardrobes and a kettle for their morning or evening brew.

The property doesn't just stop at providing excellent rooms; it extends an invitation to tranquility with its serene garden. It's a perfect spot for guests to unwind after a day of beach adventures or city exploration.

For those looking to venture a bit further, La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach are within easy reach, located 2 km and 2.1 km away respectively. And for the travelers concerned about their journey to and from the airport, Ioannina Airport is situated 97 km from The Gwen, making it accessible yet comfortably distant from the hustle and bustle of airport traffic.

The Gwen stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, offering a blend of home-like amenities and easy access to some of Sarandë's most sought-after beaches and attractions. It's an ideal choice for families and travelers seeking a blend of relaxation and adventure in the picturesque setting of Sarandë.
