The apartments themselves are a haven of modern amenities and home comforts. Each unit boasts a balcony that presents either a captivating city view or a tranquil sea and quiet street vista, setting the stage for memorable mornings and evenings. The interiors are thoughtfully designed with a fully equipped kitchen, including a fridge, making self-catering a breeze. The living areas are furnished with comfortable seating, complemented by a flat-screen TV for entertainment. Convenience is further enhanced with the inclusion of a washing machine and a private bathroom, complete with a shower and hair dryer, in every apartment. The promise of privacy and ease is fulfilled with private entrances to all units, which also feature air conditioning and ample wardrobe space.

For those keen on exploring the outdoors, Flori Apartments serves as an ideal base for embarking on hiking adventures in the surrounding areas of Sarandë. The property ensures that guests can easily connect with the natural and cultural offerings of the region.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Ioannina Airport located 96 km away, making Flori Apartments a practical choice for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Flori Apartments stands out for its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a comfortable stay, whether one is looking to explore the natural beauty of Sarandë or simply enjoy a peaceful retreat by the sea.
