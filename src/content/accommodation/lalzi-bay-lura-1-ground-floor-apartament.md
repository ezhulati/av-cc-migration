The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a living room designed for relaxation, and the modern convenience of air conditioning, ensuring a pleasant stay regardless of the weather. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV caters to the entertainment needs of guests, while the fully equipped kitchen allows for the preparation of home-cooked meals, adding a touch of homeliness to the holiday experience.

One of the property's highlights is its serene garden, providing a tranquil space for guests to unwind and soak in the natural beauty of the surroundings. The view of the garden from the apartment adds an extra layer of tranquility to the stay.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, the property is conveniently located within driving distance of notable attractions such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the awe-inspiring Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering breathtaking views of the area. The Rock of Kavaje, another must-visit landmark, is also within reach.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 29 km away, making Lalzi Bay, Lura 1 ground floor apartment an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and exploration.
