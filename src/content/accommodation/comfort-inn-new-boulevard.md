The apartment itself is a haven of modern living, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a dining area perfect for a cozy meal in. The kitchen is fully equipped, boasting an oven among other amenities, ensuring guests have everything they need at their fingertips. The living room provides a spacious area to unwind, while the bedroom promises a restful night's sleep. For added convenience, towels and linens are available upon request. It's important to note that this apartment is both non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful stay for all guests.

For those needing to pick up essentials without venturing far, a minimarket on the premises offers the utmost in convenience. Additionally, the outdoor fireplace area presents an inviting spot to relax and enjoy the ambiance of the apartment complex.

Located near several of Tirana's most sought-after attractions, including the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the area. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, with the property providing a paid shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

Comfort Inn (New Boulevard) is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the vibrant heart of Tirana, offering both the comforts of home and the excitement of the city.
