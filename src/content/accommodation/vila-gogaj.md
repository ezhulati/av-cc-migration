Each apartment at Vila Gogaj is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning, complimentary private parking, and Wi-Fi access, ensuring a seamless stay. The interiors boast a balcony that opens up to tranquil garden views, a cozy seating area for relaxation, a cable flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge for those who love to cook. The private bathroom comes with a bidet and plush bathrobes, adding a touch of luxury to the experience. Additionally, guests can enjoy al fresco dining in the patio area, which offers inner courtyard views, enhancing the charm of each unit. For convenience, all units include a wardrobe.

Situated within easy reach of notable attractions, Vila Gogaj is just 29 km from the historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra and 31 km from the scenic Lake Skadar, making it an ideal base for explorers. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 83 km away, ensuring easy access for international guests. Vila Gogaj stands out as a delightful retreat for those looking to enjoy the beauty and tranquility of Velipojë.
