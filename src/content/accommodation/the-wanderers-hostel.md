Beyond the comfort of the accommodations, The Wanderers Hostel boasts an on-site bar, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The hostel doesn't stop at providing a place to rest; it also offers essential services such as ironing and laundry, ensuring that guests' needs are fully catered to. With the bonus of complimentary parking, guests arriving by car have one less thing to worry about.

Location-wise, the hostel is ideally situated for those looking to explore the local sights. Rozafa Castle Shkodra is just a short 3.1 km journey away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich history. For nature enthusiasts, Lake Skadar is within a 3.9 km reach, presenting an opportunity to experience the natural beauty of the region. Additionally, for those traveling to or from the capital, Tirana Airport is conveniently located 74 km from the hostel, making The Wanderers Hostel a strategic base for exploring Shkodër and beyond.
