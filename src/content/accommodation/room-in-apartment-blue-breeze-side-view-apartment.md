The guest house itself is a haven of space and light, featuring a balcony that serves as the perfect spot for morning coffees or evening relaxation. Inside, guests are treated to a fully equipped kitchenette, complete with an oven, toaster, and kettle, ensuring all the comforts of home are at their fingertips. The provision of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of convenience, making for a hassle-free stay.

From the windows of the Blue Breeze Side View Apartment, the city unfolds in a panorama of urban charm, while the proximity to Sarande Main Beach and the enchanting Butrint National Park - just 1.4 km and 17 km away respectively - places guests in the heart of the region's natural and cultural treasures. For those arriving from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the property, making it a reachable destination for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, the Blue Breeze Side View Apartment stands out as a stellar choice for those seeking a serene retreat that does not compromise on location or views. Whether it's for the allure of the Albanian coast or the exploration of historical sites, this guest house is a launchpad for all kinds of adventures in Sarandë.
