Each apartment at Duka Apts is a testament to modern living, equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and complimentary WiFi. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of a private bathroom in every unit, complete with a shower and hair dryer, ensuring a comfortable stay. Select rooms further enhance the experience with the addition of a balcony, inviting guests to soak in the serene surroundings. Comfort is prioritized, with every unit outfitted with quality bed linen and towels.

For everyday essentials, guests need not venture far as the apartment complex houses its own minimarket. The option to unwind in the lush garden adds a touch of relaxation to the stay, offering a peaceful retreat from the day's activities.

Exploration is encouraged, with La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach located 2.1 km and 2.2 km away, respectively, presenting perfect opportunities for beach enthusiasts to revel in the coastal beauty. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 98 km from Duka Apts, making it a convenient base for both local and international visitors.
