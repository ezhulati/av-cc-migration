The property distinguishes itself with its beachfront allure, providing guests with unfettered access to a private balcony that boasts breathtaking sea views. The air-conditioned retreat is thoughtfully designed, featuring a single bedroom complemented by a fully equipped kitchenette, ensuring a comfortable and self-sufficient stay. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience to the experience.

Guests of this holiday home are treated to an exclusive outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals al fresco while soaking in the panoramic views. The provision of free WiFi and private parking further enhances the appeal, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and connectivity.

Situated within easy reach of notable landmarks, the property is just 1.5 km from the main Radhimë Beach and a short drive from the historic Independence Square and the picturesque Kuzum Baba, both located 13 km away. For those looking to explore further, Kevi Beach is also nearby, merely 1.7 km from the doorstep.

For international visitors, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 162 km away, making First Room of Cozy Beachside Haven - Panoramic Radhime Retreat with Balcony Dining an accessible haven for both local and global travelers in search of a tranquil beachside retreat.
