Connectivity is a breeze with complimentary WiFi enveloping guests in the warmth of home-like comfort, while the option for airport transfers and car rental services adds a layer of ease to exploring the vibrant surroundings. The interior of Sion Saranda Apartment 22 is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, featuring a spacious layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area with a TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen. Here, culinary enthusiasts can find everything from an oven and toaster to a washing machine, fridge, and an array of kitchenware, making self-catering an enjoyable endeavor.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. For those eager to explore beyond the immediate comforts of their apartment, Maestral Beach awaits less than a kilometer away, with VIP Beach offering its exclusive sands a little over two kilometers from the doorstep. For international travelers, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 96 kilometers away, underscores the apartment's appeal as a gateway to experiencing the enchanting city of Sarandë.

Sion Saranda Apartment 22 stands out as an exemplary choice for those desiring a stay that combines the freedom of self-catering accommodation with the allure of Sarandë's most beloved beaches and attractions, all wrapped up in the convenience of a central location.
