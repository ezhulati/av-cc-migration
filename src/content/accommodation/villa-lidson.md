The villa's design caters to both privacy and communal living, featuring five bedrooms and six bathrooms, making it an ideal choice for families or groups. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with streaming services, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a terrace overlooking the sea enhances the comfort and convenience of guests. The air-conditioned interior, complete with a seating area, washing machine, and soundproofing, ensures a serene and comfortable stay. The villa's bathrooms, equipped with a hot tub and a bidet, add a touch of luxury to the experience.

Dining at Villa LIDSON is a culinary adventure, with an on-site restaurant serving dinner, lunch, brunch, and cocktails. The option to engage in activities such as table tennis and darts, along with a children's playground and a picnic area, caters to guests of all ages, ensuring a memorable stay.

Strategically located near Golem Beach, Mali I Robit Beach, and Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, Villa LIDSON positions guests perfectly to explore the natural beauty of the area. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 41 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

Villa LIDSON stands out not only for its luxurious amenities and prime location but also for its commitment to guest security, offering full-day security and a private entrance. This makes it a top choice for those seeking a secure, private, and indulgent seaside escape.
