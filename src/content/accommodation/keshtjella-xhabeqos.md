The charm of Keshtjella Xhabeqos extends to its accommodations, where every room is designed with comfort in mind. Guests can expect to find well-appointed units equipped with private bathrooms and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that is as restful as it is connected. Select rooms elevate the experience further, offering balconies that open up to refreshing garden views.

For those looking to unwind closer to their home away from home, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot for relaxation and socializing. 

The location of Keshtjella Xhabeqos also serves as a gateway to explore notable attractions. The historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the natural beauty of the Rock of Kavaje are within easy reach, as are the House of Leaves and Rinia Park, offering insights into the local culture and leisure options. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 31 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

In summary, Keshtjella Xhabeqos stands out as a thoughtful choice for those looking to enjoy the best of Tirana's natural and historical sites, without compromising on comfort and accessibility.
