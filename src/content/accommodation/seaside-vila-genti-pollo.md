The property boasts a unique feature: a private entrance, ensuring a seamless and exclusive experience for guests. Designed with families in mind, Seaside Vila Genti Pollo offers spacious family rooms, making it an ideal stay for those traveling with loved ones.

Each unit at the aparthotel is a haven of relaxation, offering a terrace or balcony that presents breathtaking views of the sea or the serene quiet street. The rooms are equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, a seating area, a cable flat-screen TV, and a fully-equipped kitchen. The convenience of a private bathroom, complete with a shower and hair dryer, is complemented by the thoughtful inclusion of free WiFi, bed linen, and towels, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those looking to explore the vibrant life in and around Ksamil, Seaside Vila Genti Pollo serves as the perfect base. Activities such as snorkeling promise an adventurous day out. Moreover, the proximity to Butrint National Park, just 2.8 km away, offers guests a chance to immerse themselves in the rich history and natural beauty of the area.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Corfu International Airport located 92 km away, making Seaside Vila Genti Pollo a convenient option for international travelers. Whether it's the allure of the beaches, the comfort of the accommodations, or the promise of adventure, Seaside Vila Genti Pollo stands out as a top-tier choice for those visiting Ksamil.
