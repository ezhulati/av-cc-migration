Upon entering, guests are welcomed into air-conditioned rooms that are a testament to comfort and convenience, each equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private balcony for soaking in the breathtaking sea views. The inclusion of a small refrigerator and a dining area in each room adds a touch of home, ensuring that guests' needs are met with the utmost attention to detail. The private bathrooms, whether featuring a bath or shower, uphold the hotel's commitment to providing a refreshing and rejuvenating stay.

The amenities at Hotel GRINT extend beyond the confines of its rooms. A 24-hour front desk ensures that guests' inquiries and requests are addressed at any hour, while the presence of a garden and terrace invites moments of reflection and relaxation in the embrace of nature. The shared lounge and luggage storage facilities further exemplify the hotel's dedication to creating a seamless and hassle-free experience for all who walk through its doors.

For those arriving by car, the convenience of complimentary parking removes any worries about the logistics of their stay. Located just 10 km from the historical landmarks of Durres' Port and Durres Amphitheatre, and a mere 43 km from Tirana International Airport, Hotel GRINT stands as a gateway to exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of the region.

With its strategic location, comprehensive facilities, and commitment to guest satisfaction, Hotel GRINT is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises memorable experiences for every traveler.
