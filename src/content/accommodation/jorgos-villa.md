Upon arrival, visitors are greeted by a lush garden setting, complete with barbecue facilities, ideal for those leisurely evenings under the stars. The villa prides itself on offering a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and fresh stay for all guests. With the added convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi, staying connected and mobile is effortlessly seamless.

The interior of Jorgos Villa does not disappoint, boasting a spacious layout that includes two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen, complete with an oven and toaster, invites guests to prepare home-cooked meals, which can be enjoyed in the outdoor dining area, offering views of the garden that inspire tranquility and relaxation.

Families visiting Jorgos Villa will find the outdoor play equipment a delightful addition, ensuring the little ones are entertained while adults can unwind. The inclusion of an outdoor fireplace and a dedicated picnic area invites guests to make the most of the outdoors, whether it's roasting marshmallows or enjoying a meal amidst nature.

Situated 130 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the villa remains accessible for international travelers, making it an ideal choice for those looking to explore this picturesque region of Albania. Jorgos Villa stands out as a welcoming haven for families, couples, and solo travelers alike, promising a stay that combines the comforts of home with the beauty of the Albanian landscape.
