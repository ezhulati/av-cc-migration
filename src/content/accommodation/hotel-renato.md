The accommodations at HOTEL RENATO are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of modernity. The private bathrooms are stocked with complimentary toiletries, ensuring a refreshing start to the day. For those seeking a little extra, select rooms boast balconies offering a tranquil space to unwind.

Mornings at HOTEL RENATO begin with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's proximity to notable attractions like Ksamil Beach 9, just 400 meters away, and the serene Lori Beach, a short 600-meter stroll, makes it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. Moreover, the historic Butrint National Park is within a 3 km reach, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage.

For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, located 92 km from the hotel, making HOTEL RENATO a convenient choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and exploration in Ksamil.
