The apartment itself is a spacious haven, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, two modern bathrooms, and an array of amenities designed to make guests feel at home. From the crisp bed linen and plush towels to the state-of-the-art flat-screen TV with satellite channels, every detail has been thoughtfully considered. The fully equipped kitchen and dining area invite guests to prepare and enjoy meals with a view, while the air-conditioned interiors ensure comfort at any time of the year. The highlight, however, is the terrace, offering panoramic sea views that promise to enchant.

For those traveling with little ones, the apartment thoughtfully includes a baby safety gate, ensuring peace of mind for parents. Outdoor dining options add an extra layer of convenience and charm to the experience, allowing guests to savor meals against the backdrop of Sarandë's stunning landscape.

Situated 18 km from the lush expanses of Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport 98 km away, Apartment Panorama also offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring easy access to and from this coastal sanctuary. Whether seeking a peaceful escape by the sea or an adventure in the surrounding natural beauty, Apartment Panorama stands out as a premier choice for discerning travelers.
