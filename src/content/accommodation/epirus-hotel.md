A standout feature of this hotel is its inviting terrace, where guests can unwind under the natural shade of straw umbrellas. This spot becomes even more appealing with the hotel's restaurant nearby, ready to tantalize your taste buds with an array of traditional Albanian and Italian dishes.

For those who like to have essential amenities within easy reach, the Epirus Hotel does not disappoint. A mere 30 meters away, guests will find a supermarket for all their needs, while a bakery and pharmacy are just a short 100-meter stroll. Explorers eager to delve into the local culture will appreciate the proximity to the Sarandë Museum in the town center, located just 1 km from the hotel.

Transportation is conveniently accessible, with the local bus station about 500 meters away, making exploration beyond the hotel's immediate surroundings a breeze. The Sarandë Ferry Port, situated 800 meters from the hotel, offers adventurous guests the opportunity to venture to the Island of Corfu, with Ioannis Kapodistrias Airport just a 45-minute boat ride away.

In summary, the Epirus Hotel presents a compelling choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural immersion. Its thoughtful amenities and strategic location make it an ideal base for both relaxation and exploration.
