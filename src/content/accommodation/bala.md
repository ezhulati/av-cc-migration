This charming apartment boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their own meals, and a modern bathroom, providing all the comforts of home. Its balcony offers a serene spot to unwind after a day of adventures.

Beyond its immediate surroundings, Bala serves as an ideal base for exploring further afield. The enchanting Golem Beach is merely 2.5 km away, while the historic heart of Albania, Skanderbeg Square, is accessible at 41 km. For those intrigued by Albania's recent history, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located 42 km from the apartment, offering a glimpse into the country's past.

Adventure seekers will appreciate the proximity to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 45 km away, promising breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience. Meanwhile, the ancient Durres Amphitheatre, one of the city's most significant archaeological sites, is just 11 km away, inviting guests to step back in time.

Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, located 36 km from Bala, making it a hassle-free choice for both international and domestic travelers. Whether it's the allure of the beach, the call of historical exploration, or simply the need for a comfortable and convenient place to stay, Bala offers a compelling option for discerning guests visiting Durrës.
