Accommodations at Tea Residence are thoughtfully designed, featuring balconies that provide a breath of fresh air, fully equipped kitchens for those who love to cook, and dining areas for intimate meals. The presence of a flat-screen TV in each unit ensures entertainment is never far away, while private bathrooms equipped with a bidet and hairdryer add an extra layer of convenience. Select units elevate the experience further with terraces that offer outdoor dining options and captivating sea views, complemented by the provision of bed linen and towels for a hassle-free stay.

The property boasts an on-site bar perfect for evening relaxation, and guests can enjoy the communal barbecue facilities during the warmer months. The seasonal outdoor pool is a refreshing escape, while outdoor play equipment is available for younger guests, ensuring a delightful stay for all ages.

Located a significant distance from the hustle and bustle, Tea Residence is 46 km from Skanderbeg Square and 50 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering a tranquil escape with the convenience of being within reach of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 31 km away. This makes Tea Residence, Gjiri i Lalzit Apartments, a compelling choice for those looking to enjoy the tranquility of Durrës while being conveniently connected to the broader region.
