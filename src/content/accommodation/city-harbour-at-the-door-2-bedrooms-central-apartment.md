This air-conditioned apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest; it invites guests into a spacious haven of relaxation. With two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen boasting an oven and fridge, it caters to all the comforts of home. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of convenience. What sets this apartment apart is its terrace and patio, offering stunning sea views and an idyllic setting for outdoor dining, ensuring memorable moments under the Vlorë sky.

Privacy and tranquility are paramount here, with soundproofing and a private entrance allowing guests to escape the hustle and bustle of the city. Additionally, the convenience of a minimarket on the premises means essentials are always within reach.

Situated a mere 2.4 km from Independence Square and close to the historical Kuzum Baba, guests are never far from exploring the rich heritage of the area. For those looking to venture further, Ri Beach awaits at a distance of 2.9 km, promising a serene beach day out.

For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making the City & Harbour at the Door 2 Bedrooms Central Apartment an accessible retreat for both local and global visitors seeking the ultimate Vlorë experience.
