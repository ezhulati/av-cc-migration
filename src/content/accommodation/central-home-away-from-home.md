The accommodation boasts an air-conditioned apartment that is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It features a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and a coffee machine, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. The bathroom is well-appointed with a bidet and bathrobes, adding a touch of luxury to the stay. For convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available in the apartment, and the added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy.

For those looking to explore Shkodër and its surroundings, Central Home Away From Home offers both bicycle and car rental services, providing a hassle-free solution for travel arrangements. Additionally, the property is located 58 km from Podgorica Airport, with a paid airport shuttle service available, making it a convenient option for international travelers.

Central Home Away From Home stands out as a compelling choice for guests who value privacy, convenience, and the comforts of home while exploring the vibrant city of Shkodër.
