Guests are treated to a tranquil garden view from this air-conditioned haven, which also boasts a welcoming terrace and the convenience of complimentary WiFi. The commitment to a smoke-free environment ensures a refreshing stay for all visitors.

The apartment itself is a marvel of space and modern amenities. It features two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring ample room for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen leaves nothing to be desired, fitted with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster, making self-catering a breeze. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort.

Outdoor dining becomes an experience in its own right, with a dedicated area set against the backdrop of the apartment's lush garden. For convenience, a minimarket on the premises caters to guests' immediate needs.

The allure of the Luxury Ground Floor Apartment is further enhanced by its private beach area, offering an exclusive retreat by the sea. This feature alone positions the apartment as a top pick for beach lovers and those seeking a peaceful escape.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre, promising enriching day trips. Moreover, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 28 km away, facilitating easy arrivals and departures.

In summary, the Luxury Ground Floor Apartment in the Bay of Lalzi presents an unparalleled accommodation choice, promising a blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural immersion for a memorable stay near Durrës.
