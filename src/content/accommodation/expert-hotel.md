Accommodations at Expert Hotel are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is a haven of relaxation, equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV boasting satellite channels, a refrigerator, and a kettle for your morning brew. The presence of a shower, complimentary toiletries, and a desk adds to the room's functionality. Privacy and comfort are paramount, with every room featuring a private bathroom outfitted with a hairdryer. Select accommodations elevate the experience further with balconies and captivating sea views, ensuring a memorable stay. The commitment to guest satisfaction is evident in the provision of quality bed linen and towels in all rooms.

Situated a mere 1.3 km from the picturesque Qerret Beach and 1.5 km from the vibrant Golem Beach, Expert Hotel's location allows guests to explore the coastal beauty of the area with ease. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 42 km away, making Expert Hotel an accessible haven for both local and global visitors seeking a peaceful escape by the sea.
