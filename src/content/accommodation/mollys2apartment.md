The apartment boasts a generously sized bedroom, a well-appointed bathroom, and an array of comforts including bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for those leisurely evenings in. The heart of this accommodation is its fully equipped kitchen and dining area, perfect for those who enjoy the intimacy of a home-cooked meal. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony, offering breathtaking lake views that promise to start every morning on a note of tranquility.

For those who relish dining al fresco, an outdoor dining area is available, overlooking a peaceful street scene, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the local ambiance. The added convenience of a private entrance ensures an undisturbed and personal experience throughout the stay.

Situated 39 km from both Port Ohrid and the iconic Church of St. John at Kaneo, and with Ohrid Airport only 45 km away, Mollys2apartment not only offers a tranquil escape but also serves as a perfect base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of the region. This property stands out as an ideal accommodation choice for those in pursuit of comfort, privacy, and the beauty of Pogradec's natural surroundings.
