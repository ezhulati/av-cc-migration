Following a recent renovation, guests are welcomed into a spacious setting, thoughtfully furnished with a comfortable bedroom, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, and a balcony that invites relaxation. The fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, caters to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, along with delightful touches such as chocolates or cookies to make guests feel at home.

The apartment's commitment to non-smoking ensures a clean and healthy environment. For those needing essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises offers a range of items without the need to venture far.

Attractions nearby, such as Rinia Park, the Clock Tower of Tirana, and the Et'hem Bey Mosque, are easily accessible, enriching the stay with cultural experiences and leisure opportunities. The Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 15 km away, allows for hassle-free arrivals and departures, making Cozy 1-Bedroom near the center of Tirana a convenient and comfortable choice for both short visits and extended stays in the heart of Tirana.
