Each guest at Hotel Dollari is treated to a personal beach tent, ensuring a private and serene beach experience. The rooms, equipped with satellite TV, a minibar, and a convenient bathroom with a shower, promise a stay that blends relaxation with the comforts of home.

Situated just a short drive from the heart of Durrës, Hotel Dollari offers a peaceful retreat with easy access to the city's rich history, including the ancient Roman amphitheatre and the historic King Zogu's Palace. Despite its tranquil beachfront location, the hotel ensures that guests have the ease of exploration, with a bus stop directly in front that offers rides into the city center.

For those traveling by car, the hotel provides complimentary parking, making it a hassle-free choice for guests. Additionally, its proximity to Durrës Bus Station and Tirana Airport, located just 3 km and 35 km away respectively, adds to the convenience for both local and international travelers.

Hotel Dollari represents an ideal choice for those seeking a comfortable and culturally rich stay by the Albanian coast.
