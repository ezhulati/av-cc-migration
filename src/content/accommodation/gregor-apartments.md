Upon entering the apartments, guests are greeted with a harmonious blend of functionality and style. Each unit boasts a terrace that offers breathtaking sea views, setting the stage for unforgettable sunsets. The living spaces are thoughtfully designed with a seating area and a satellite flat-screen TV, creating a homely atmosphere. The kitchen comes fully equipped with an oven, microwave, toaster, fridge, stovetop, and a coffee machine, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like.

For those who appreciate the finer details, the apartments are meticulously fitted with quality bed linen and towels, adding a touch of luxury to the stay. The convenience of guests is paramount, as evidenced by the 24-hour front desk, lift access, and currency exchange services available on-site.

The surrounding area of Gregor Apartments offers a plethora of activities, with fishing being a popular pastime. The option to hire a car directly from the apartment complex opens up opportunities to explore Sarandë and its environs. For moments of tranquility, the property's garden provides a serene escape.

Located merely 700 meters from Saranda City Beach and within easy reach of the Butrint National Park, 15 km away, Gregor Apartments positions itself as an ideal base for both relaxation and adventure. The nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is 96 km away, making the apartments accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.
