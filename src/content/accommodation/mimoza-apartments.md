Each apartment is meticulously designed with tiled floors and boasts a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, dining area, and modern appliances such as a microwave, fridge, stovetop, coffee machine, and kettle. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The convenience of a private bathroom, complete with a shower and complimentary slippers, adds to the comfort of the stay. For added convenience, all units are fitted with air conditioning and a wardrobe, ensuring a pleasant and organized living space.

Located strategically, Mimoza Apartments is a mere 1.2 km from Sarande Main Beach and 1.8 km from the serene La Petite Beach, making it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is 97 km away, making the apartments a convenient choice for both local and international travelers.
