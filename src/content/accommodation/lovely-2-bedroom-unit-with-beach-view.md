The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. It houses two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen boasting an oven and a fridge for those who prefer to prepare their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Additionally, guests have the luxury of dining al fresco in the outdoor dining area provided.

For relaxation, the property offers a charming garden where guests can unwind and a bar on-site for enjoying evening drinks.

The location is unbeatable, with Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach just 60 meters away, offering another picturesque beach option. For those willing to explore a bit further, Mali I Robit Beach is located 2.5 km away, presenting another beautiful setting for beach lovers. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 38 km from the apartment, making Lovely 2 Bedroom Unit with Beach View an ideal choice for both local and international guests seeking a tranquil beachfront escape.
