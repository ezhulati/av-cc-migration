This beachfront gem boasts a modern 1-bedroom apartment equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a cool and comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a private balcony, where the sounds of the sea create a soothing backdrop. The apartment is well-appointed with contemporary amenities including a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a minibar, catering to all the needs of the modern traveler.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises allows guests to easily grab essentials without having to venture far. While the allure of the beach is undeniable, those looking to explore further will find Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within accessible distances, offering a glimpse into the rich culture and breathtaking landscapes of the area.

Located just 36 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Magic Sea is not only easily accessible but also serves as a perfect base for both relaxation and adventure in the heart of Durrës. Whether it's basking in the sun on the nearby Golem Beach or exploring local attractions, Magic Sea promises a memorable stay filled with comfort and convenience.
