This accommodation doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a serene retreat. Guests are welcomed into a spacious three-bedroom apartment that boasts modern amenities and thoughtful touches. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV, while the kitchen comes fully stocked with an oven, toaster, and even a washing machine, ensuring all the comforts of home are at guests' fingertips. The presence of two well-appointed bathrooms, complete with bidets, adds a layer of convenience for families or groups.

Privacy and tranquility are paramount at Salt Life Apartments. Each unit features a private entrance and is designed with soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful stay devoid of external disturbances. The addition of air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and the convenience of free private parking further enhance the guest experience, making it a hassle-free haven.

For those keen to explore the rich tapestry of the region, the apartment serves as an ideal base. The historic Butrint National Park is just 13 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's storied past, while the ancient site of Fanoti lies 47 km from the doorstep, promising an intriguing excursion. Moreover, with Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, access to and from the property is relatively straightforward for international travelers.

Salt Life Apartments represents a blend of strategic location, thoughtful amenities, and serene living spaces, making it a compelling choice for those looking to explore Sarandë's charms while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed home away from home.
