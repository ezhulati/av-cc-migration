Accommodations at Grand Blue Fafa Resort & SPA are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen satellite TVs and private balconies for breathtaking views. The attention to detail extends to the bathrooms, where guests are treated to either a luxurious bath or a refreshing shower, complemented by complimentary toiletries, slippers, and a hairdryer for a touch of home.

Culinary experiences at the resort are highlighted by the in-house restaurant's expertise in Italian cuisine, offering a delightful journey through authentic flavors. The bar serves as a perfect retreat for guests to unwind with a refreshing drink after a day of exploration or relaxation.

Strategically located, the resort is a mere 43 km from Tirana and just 10 km from Durres, making it an ideal base for guests looking to explore the surrounding areas. Tirana Airport is conveniently 40 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements for international guests.

Grand Blue Fafa Resort & SPA not only promises a memorable stay with its array of amenities and exceptional service but also positions itself as a gateway to the enchanting beauty of Golem and beyond.
