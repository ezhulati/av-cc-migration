Boasting a generous layout, The "Peace-Up" Apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring ample space for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with modern appliances including an oven, microwave, washing machine, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home-like comfort to the stay.

Privacy is paramount at The "Peace-Up" Apartment, with a private entrance that guarantees guests their own secluded haven. Located a mere 58 km from Podgorica Airport, the apartment serves as an ideal base for travelers seeking both tranquility and convenience in the heart of Shkodër.
