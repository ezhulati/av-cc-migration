Guests at Melina Apartment are treated to a seamless experience, thanks to the inclusion of modern facilities such as an elevator and the convenience of private check-in and check-out services. The promise of free WiFi throughout ensures that connectivity is never an issue, whether for leisure or work.

The apartment itself is a study in spaciousness and comfort, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a bathroom equipped with essential linens and towels. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV offering satellite channels, positioned in a cozy dining area. The heart of the apartment, a fully equipped kitchen, invites guests to feel at home, while a balcony presents breathtaking sea views, adding a touch of serenity to the stay. Notably, the apartment features a private entrance and soundproofing, ensuring a tranquil and undisturbed experience.

For those eager to explore, sightseeing tours are conveniently accessible near the property, promising adventures and local discoveries. Melina Apartment is also ideally situated for beach enthusiasts, with Maestral Beach and VIP Beach just a short distance away. For travelers arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 95 km from the apartment.

In summary, Melina Apartment offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and connectivity, making it an excellent choice for those looking to experience Sarandë in a relaxed and homely atmosphere.
