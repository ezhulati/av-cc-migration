This charming apartment boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom designed with comfort in mind. Guests can unwind while enjoying garden views or stay entertained with the available flat-screen TV. Despite its tranquil setting, the apartment ensures that guests remain connected to the pulse of the city, with the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the vibrant Skanderbeg Square located just 50 km away.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the House of Leaves and Rinia Park, each less than 50 km from the property, offering a glimpse into the rich history and lush greenery of the area. For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 46 km away, ensuring a smooth transition to and from this coastal sanctuary.

Apartment Near The Beach, Qerret, stands out as a non-smoking property that combines the essence of a peaceful beachside stay with the accessibility to major attractions, making it a compelling choice for both leisure and adventure seekers.
