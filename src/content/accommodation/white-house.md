This property distinguishes itself by providing guests with modern amenities such as complimentary WiFi and private parking, ensuring a hassle-free stay. The holiday home, a non-smoking establishment, is also conveniently located just 1.8 km from the bustling Saranda City Beach, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the local beach scene.

Accommodation at White House is thoughtfully designed with two separate bedrooms, a bathroom, and a living room, catering to the needs of families and groups seeking a comfortable retreat. The inclusion of a garden and a terrace further enhances the appeal, offering serene spots for relaxation and social gatherings.

For those traveling to or from the area, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 98 km away, adds an extra layer of convenience, making White House an attractive option for both domestic and international travelers.
