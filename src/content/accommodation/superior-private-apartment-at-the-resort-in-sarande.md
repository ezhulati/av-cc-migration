The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, boasting a balcony that offers stunning sea views. It is well-appointed with a bedroom, a cozy living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher and an oven, ensuring guests feel right at home. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds to the comfort, while the private entrance and 24-hour security ensure peace of mind and exclusivity.

For those who prefer the convenience of on-site shopping, a minimarket within the apartment complex caters to everyday needs.

Location-wise, Superior Private Apartment is ideally situated close to several of Sarandë's most sought-after beaches, including Maestral Beach, La Petite Beach, and VIP Beach, making it a perfect base for beach lovers. Additionally, for guests looking to explore beyond the city, Ioannina Airport is within a manageable distance, located 96 km away.

In summary, Superior Private Apartment at the Resort in Sarande is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the best of Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with the luxuries of a top-tier holiday destination.
