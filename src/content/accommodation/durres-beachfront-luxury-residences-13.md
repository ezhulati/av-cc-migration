Each apartment at Durres Beachfront Luxury Residences 13 is designed to maximize the stunning sea views, with balconies that invite guests to relax and soak in the scenery. The interiors are thoughtfully appointed with a seating area, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a microwave, toaster, fridge, and stovetop, along with a kettle for those morning or evening refreshments. The convenience extends to the private bathrooms, equipped with a bidet and hairdryer, ensuring a comfortable stay. Moreover, the provision of bed linen and towels in each unit adds an extra layer of comfort.

For those looking to unwind without straying far, the apartment complex does not disappoint. It boasts an on-site restaurant, coffee shop, and bar, catering to a variety of tastes and preferences. The highlight, however, is the pool with a view, offering a tranquil setting for guests to relax and enjoy the panoramic vistas.

Situated a mere 500 meters from Golem Beach and 2.4 km from Durres Beach, the location of Durres Beachfront Luxury Residences 13 is ideal for beach enthusiasts looking to explore the local coastline. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 37 km away, makes it a convenient choice for international travelers.

Durres Beachfront Luxury Residences 13 is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises a memorable experience, combining the comforts of home with the luxury of a high-end resort.
