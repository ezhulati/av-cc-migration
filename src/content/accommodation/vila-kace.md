Accommodations at Vila Kace are thoughtfully designed with modern travelers in mind. Each room is outfitted with a flat-screen TV complete with streaming services, ensuring guests are entertained and connected throughout their stay. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and plush bathrobes, adding a touch of luxury to the experience. For those who prefer a breath of fresh air, select rooms boast balconies that offer a vantage point of the surrounding tranquility. Comfort is a priority, with all units featuring air conditioning and a desk, catering to both leisure and business travelers.

The presence of an on-site bar adds a convenient option for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

Situated 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, Vila Kace presents an accessible escape for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and calm of Korçë. With its blend of modern amenities and prime location, this guest house is an ideal choice for families and solo travelers alike seeking a memorable stay.
