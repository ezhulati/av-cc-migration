This smoke-free property boasts a generously sized one-bedroom layout, complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen is ready to cater to any culinary need, featuring an oven, fridge, washing machine, and stovetop. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, allowing for a hassle-free stay.

Situated merely 1.4 km away from the charming La Petite Beach and within a 17 km radius of the enchanting Butrint National Park, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the natural beauty and historical richness of the area. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km away, making Center Apartment Saranda an ideal base for both local and international travelers.

In summary, Center Apartment Saranda stands out as a top selection for guests who value both the comforts of home and the excitement of exploring Sarandë's coastal charm.
