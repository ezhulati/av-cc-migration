The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting air conditioning for those warm Shkodër days and nights. It features a well-appointed bedroom, a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a fridge, catering to the needs of guests preferring home-cooked meals. The living room, spacious and inviting, is perfect for relaxation or entertaining, with a private entrance that adds an extra layer of privacy. To elevate the experience further, guests are treated to the option of enjoying wine or champagne within the comfort of their accommodation, all in a smoke-free environment.

For those looking to explore the vibrant surroundings, Room 446, Center is ideally situated. Guests have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local culture and landscape, with cycling among the popular activities to engage in around Shkodër. Additionally, the property facilitates exploration and ease of travel through its shuttle and car rental services, ensuring guests can navigate the area with ease.

Located a mere 58 km from Podgorica Airport, Room 446, Center offers a seamless experience for both arriving and departing guests, making it an excellent choice for travelers in search of a modern, secure, and well-equipped base in Shkodër.
