Guests at Rozafa Palace Hotel are treated to a host of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The hotel boasts a welcoming terrace, complimentary private parking, and a lively bar, ensuring that relaxation and socialization are always within reach. For those arriving from afar, the convenience of airport transfers, a 24-hour front desk, and luggage storage services make for a hassle-free experience. Moreover, the entire property is enveloped in free WiFi, allowing guests to stay connected with ease.

Accommodations at Rozafa Palace Hotel are thoughtfully appointed to ensure a restful night's sleep. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a desk, a safety deposit box for valuables, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a private balcony offers a personal oasis where guests can unwind in solitude. Private bathrooms, complete with showers, along with the provision of fresh bed linen and towels, underscore the hotel's commitment to comfort.

The hotel's location also serves as a gateway to exploring Sarandë's vibrant coastline, with Flamingo Beach, Santa Quaranta Beach, and VIP Beach all within easy reach. For those venturing further, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km away, making Rozafa Palace Hotel an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Rozafa Palace Hotel presents an inviting option for those in pursuit of a serene getaway or an adventurous exploration of Sarandë's natural and cultural treasures.
