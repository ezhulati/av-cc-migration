This air-conditioned villa, a haven of tranquility, boasts a balcony that opens up to serene garden views, ensuring a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life. The accommodation is strictly non-smoking, maintaining a fresh and clean environment for all guests. Its proximity to Sarande Main Beach, just 1.1 km away, adds to its appeal for beach lovers and casual swimmers alike.

Designed with space and comfort in mind, the villa features three generously sized bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal setting for families or groups seeking a home-away-from-home experience.

For those traveling to or from the region, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is located 97 km away, making Villa K & N a convenient base for both short and extended stays in Sarandë.
