The apartment itself is a study in spacious living, offering two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and two modern bathrooms, catering to all the needs of its guests. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, making downtime enjoyable for everyone.

For those intrigued by the natural beauty and historical richness of the area, Butrint National Park is accessible, located just 16 km away, promising an adventure for nature lovers and history buffs alike. Additionally, the convenience of Ioannina Airport being 96 km away facilitates easy travel arrangements for international guests.

Joti Apartments 2 not only provides a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the enchanting experiences Sarandë has to offer.
