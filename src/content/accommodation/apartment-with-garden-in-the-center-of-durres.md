The apartment itself is a haven of comfort and convenience, boasting a spacious, air-conditioned living space complete with a terrace for enjoying the fresh air and free WiFi to keep guests connected. The view from the balcony is a tranquil garden scene, setting the stage for a peaceful retreat. Inside, the apartment is equipped with a modern kitchen, featuring an oven and toaster for home-cooked meals, alongside a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a bathroom with a bidet. Towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those needing essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises adds an extra layer of convenience. Guests are also invited to unwind in the lush garden, offering a serene escape from the hustle and bustle.

Adventurous guests will find the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car 42 km away, offering breathtaking views from above, while the Rock of Kavaje, 11 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for exploration. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 33 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

The Apartment with Garden in the center of Durres is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the beauty and culture of Durrës, promising a memorable getaway for all who visit.
