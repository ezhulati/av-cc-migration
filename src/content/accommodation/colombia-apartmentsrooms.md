The accommodations at Colombia Apartments&Rooms are designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for those moments of relaxation. The safety deposit box, private bathroom complete with a bidet, slippers, and hair dryer, alongside a well-stocked minibar, fridge, coffee machine, and kettle, ensure a stay that combines convenience with luxury. The provision of bed linen and towels adds a touch of home.

Culinary experiences at Colombia Apartments&Rooms are tailored to suit every palate, with the family-friendly restaurant offering an array of Italian dishes for dinner, lunch, and high tea. Guests have the option to start their day with either an à la carte or Italian breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation.

Understanding the needs of guests traveling with children, the property offers a babysitting service, ensuring a worry-free stay for parents. Additionally, the availability of both bicycle and car rental services on-site encourages guests to explore Durrës and its surroundings at their own pace.

With a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, full-day security, and complimentary WiFi, Colombia Apartments&Rooms prioritizes guest safety and convenience, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of modern amenities and prime location. Just 34 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, this apartment complex serves as a perfect base for both short and extended stays in Durrës.
