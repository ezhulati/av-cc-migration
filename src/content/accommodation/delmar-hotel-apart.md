The property prides itself on its array of accommodations, each boasting essential amenities such as a desk, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom, ensuring a stay that marries both relaxation and productivity. Select units elevate the experience further with a fully equipped kitchen, catering to those who prefer a home-cooked meal even while away from home. The commitment to guest comfort is evident in the soundproofing of all units, promising a tranquil retreat from the world outside.

Morning at DelMar Hotel-Apart greets guests with a choice of breakfast options, ranging from a hearty Full English/Irish to a lighter vegetarian fare, catering to diverse palates and dietary preferences. The convenience of an on-site minimarket adds a layer of ease to the stay, allowing for last-minute needs or local indulgences.

For those drawn to the allure of the outdoors, the surrounding area of Golem offers a playground of activities, with fishing being a popular pastime. Beyond the immediate beachfront, Golem Beach and Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach beckon just a short walk away, inviting exploration and relaxation alike.

Situated a manageable distance from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, DelMar Hotel-Apart stands as a beacon for travelers seeking a serene beachside escape coupled with the comforts of modern amenities and the warmth of attentive service.
