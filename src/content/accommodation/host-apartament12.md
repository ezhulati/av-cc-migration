The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring a spacious balcony that offers a serene retreat. The interior boasts a tastefully furnished living space, complete with air conditioning for those warm Tirana days. Guests will find a fully equipped kitchen at their disposal, perfect for those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals while traveling. The living area, anchored by a sleek flat-screen TV, provides a cozy setting for relaxation after a day of exploration. Additionally, the convenience of a private entrance adds an extra layer of privacy to the guest experience.

For those looking to venture further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is located just 4 km away, offering breathtaking views of the city from above. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, 44 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for a scenic day trip. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 15 km away, ensuring easy arrivals and departures.

With free WiFi available throughout the property, guests at Host Apartment12 are well-equipped to stay connected and share their experiences. Whether in Tirana for culture, shopping, or simply to unwind, this apartment offers a comfortable and convenient base for all types of travelers.
