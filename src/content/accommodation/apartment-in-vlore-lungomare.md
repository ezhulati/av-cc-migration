The apartment itself is a haven of space and modern amenities. Guests are welcomed into an air-conditioned environment that boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with both a bidet and a shower, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds a touch of home comfort to the seaside escape. Additionally, an outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy meals al fresco, making the most of the apartment's scenic surroundings.

Strategically located, the property is just 2.1 km away from the serene Ri Beach, offering an easy escape to one of the area's most beautiful coastlines. For those interested in exploring the local culture and history, Independence Square is within 2.6 km, and the historic site of Kuzum Baba is 3 km away, providing a glimpse into the rich heritage of Vlore. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 151 km from the apartment, ensures a straightforward journey to this coastal retreat.

Apartment in Vlore-Lungomare stands out as an exceptional choice for those looking to experience the best of Vlore's beaches and cultural sites, all while enjoying the comforts of a thoughtfully designed and well-situated accommodation.
