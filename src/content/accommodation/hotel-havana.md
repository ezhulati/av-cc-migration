Guests at Hotel Havana are treated to well-appointed rooms, each featuring a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of modernity. The private bathrooms are equipped with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, adding an extra layer of comfort. For those desiring a view, select rooms also come with a balcony, providing a perfect setting to unwind. The attention to detail extends to the provision of quality bed linen and towels in every room, ensuring a pleasant and comfortable stay.

Beyond the rooms, Hotel Havana caters to the needs of its guests with a range of services including room service, a tour desk, and currency exchange, making it a convenient base for exploring the local area. The hotel's location is ideal for beach lovers, with VIP Beach just a bit further afield, and for those interested in cultural excursions, Butrint National Park is within easy reach.

For travelers arriving from afar, the nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, located 96 km away, making Hotel Havana an accessible destination for both domestic and international visitors. This hotel combines the essentials of a comfortable stay with the charm of Sarandë's coastal life, making it a compelling choice for those looking to experience the best of the region.
