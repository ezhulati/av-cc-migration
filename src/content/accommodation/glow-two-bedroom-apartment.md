The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring two cozy bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with fresh bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels for entertainment. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to guests' culinary needs, while the balcony presents breathtaking mountain views, perfect for unwinding. Designed with privacy and tranquility in mind, the accommodation boasts a private entrance and soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful stay.

For those keen to explore, Ksamil Beach 9 is conveniently located less than 1 km away, and the historical Butrint National Park is just 2.9 km from the doorstep. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Glow Two Bedroom Apartment an accessible and attractive option for both local and overseas visitors.
