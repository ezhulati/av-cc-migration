The apartment itself is a testament to modern comfort and convenience, boasting air conditioning for those warm summer days. Guests will find themselves at home with a flat-screen TV, a cozy dining area, and a kitchen that comes fully equipped with an oven and a fridge, catering to all culinary needs. The addition of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy and exclusivity, making each stay feel personalized.

For those traveling to or from the area, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 82 km away, adds an extra layer of convenience, making Solhouse a compelling choice for both short and long-term stays. This property not only promises a restful and comfortable visit but also serves as a gateway to the rich cultural and natural beauty of Gjirokastër and its surroundings.
