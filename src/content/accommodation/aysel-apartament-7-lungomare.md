The apartment itself is a spacious sanctuary, equipped with a bedroom that promises restful nights. The living space is adorned with a flat-screen TV, and the kitchen comes fully stocked with modern appliances including an oven, toaster, washing machine, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. For those seeking added ease, towels and linens are available upon request. Guests will also delight in the garden views that add a touch of nature to their stay.

For explorers eager to venture beyond, the apartment offers a car rental service, making local excursions seamless. Noteworthy landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are within easy reach, located 2.7 km and 3 km away respectively. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km from the apartment, making Aysel Apartment 7 Lungomare an accessible gateway to the charms of Vlorë.
