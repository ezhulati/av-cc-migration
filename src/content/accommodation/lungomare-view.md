The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring an array of cable channels. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped with modern appliances such as an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. The highlight, however, is the outdoor dining area, where guests can relish their meals against the backdrop of mesmerizing sea views.

For those concerned about connectivity, Lungomare View ensures peace of mind with complimentary WiFi throughout the property. Additionally, the commitment to a non-smoking environment promotes a clean and healthy stay for all guests. Its proximity to the Beach at Government Villas, just 1.6 km away, adds another layer of appeal for beach enthusiasts.

Travelers planning their visit can also take comfort in knowing that Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 154 km away, making Lungomare View an accessible retreat for both local and international guests. This property doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides an experience, blending convenience, comfort, and the beauty of Vlorë into an unforgettable stay.
