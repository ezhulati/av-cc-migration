The apartment itself is a haven of space and tranquility, boasting air-conditioned interiors that include a well-equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and microwave, ensuring that guests feel right at home. The living area is designed for relaxation, featuring a flat-screen TV and comfortable seating, alongside a bathroom that comes with a modern bidet. One of the highlights is undoubtedly the balcony, offering mountain views and outfitted with outdoor furniture, perfect for enjoying the serene surroundings. The addition of soundproofing and a private entrance underscores the apartment's commitment to privacy and peace.

For those with an adventurous spirit, the area around Apartment Albania does not disappoint. Activities such as diving, cycling, and fishing are readily available, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the region. Moreover, the property boasts a picnic area, ideal for those wishing to enjoy a day outdoors in the scenic setting.

Located 18 km from Independence Square and with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 167 km away, Apartment Albania positions itself as a gateway for both local exploration and ease of travel. This apartment not only promises a stay filled with comfort but also offers a launchpad for adventures in and around Orikum.
