For those who prefer the convenience of home-cooked meals, select apartments feature a well-appointed kitchenette complete with a dishwasher and all necessary kitchenware. Comfort is further enhanced with the provision of quality bed linen and towels in every unit.

Located merely 700 meters from Saranda City Beach and a bit further from the serene La Petite Beach, Vila 31 serves as an ideal base for exploring the local coastline. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 97 km away, making Vila 31 an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors.
