The villa itself presents a tranquil haven, boasting a balcony that opens up to enchanting garden views. Inside, guests are treated to a cozy setup comprising two bedrooms, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a toaster, perfect for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the villa's appeal, making it a home away from home. Importantly, the accommodation maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

One of the villa's highlights is the opportunity for outdoor dining, allowing guests to enjoy their meals amidst the tranquility of the property's surroundings. Additionally, the garden offers a peaceful spot for relaxation, inviting guests to unwind in the midst of nature.

Located 119 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Tourists Home Likaj presents an accessible escape for those looking to explore the beauty of Berat while enjoying the comforts of a private villa. This property is an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of relaxation and convenience in their accommodation.
