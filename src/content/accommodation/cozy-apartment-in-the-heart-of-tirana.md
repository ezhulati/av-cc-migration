This non-smoking, one-bedroom haven is equipped with modern amenities designed to cater to the needs of contemporary travelers. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air conditioning, free WiFi, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The inclusion of a washing machine and a fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and toaster, ensures a home-away-from-home experience. The provision of towels and bed linen further enhances the comfort of stay, while the private entrance underscores the apartment's commitment to privacy.

Strategically positioned less than 1 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the apartment is a stone's throw away from Rinia Park, Reja - The Cloud, and the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, making it an ideal base for those keen to delve into Tirana's cultural and historical landscape. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 15 km away, ensuring easy access to and from this charming retreat.

Cozy Apartment in the Heart of Tirana stands out as a top recommendation for travelers who prioritize location, comfort, and a touch of local charm in their accommodation choices.
