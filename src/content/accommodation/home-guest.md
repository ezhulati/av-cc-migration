A mere 3.8 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just over 3 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Home Guest positions itself as a gateway to exploring the rich tapestry of Tirana's cultural and natural attractions. The establishment prides itself on being a smoke-free environment, catering to the health and comfort of all guests.

Accommodation at Home Guest is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each apartment boasts two bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their own meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

For those interested in delving into the local culture and history, Home Guest is conveniently situated near several of Tirana's most visited landmarks, including Tanners' Bridge and the National Museum of History Albania. Shopping enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to Toptani Shopping Centre, offering a variety of retail options.

Ease of access is further enhanced by the accommodation's location, just 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making Home Guest an ideal option for both international visitors and those traveling within Albania.
