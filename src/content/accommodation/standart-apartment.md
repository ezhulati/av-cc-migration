This apartment is strategically located, making it an ideal base for exploring the area. It's a comfortable distance from notable landmarks, including the historic Skanderbeg Square and the thrilling Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, both within 47 kilometers. For those intrigued by local history, the Rock of Kavaje is just 1.4 kilometers away, and the Durres Beach, known for its captivating sunsets, is 2.4 kilometers from the property.

The interior of Standart Apartment is thoughtfully designed for relaxation and convenience. It features a cozy bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, an equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their meals, and a bathroom complete with a bidet. The non-smoking policy ensures a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

For visitors interested in exploring the cultural heritage of the area, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre are significant sites, located 43 km and 12 km away, respectively. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 37 km away, makes Standart Apartment a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Standart Apartment stands out as a welcoming haven for guests looking to experience the beauty and history of Durrës, offering both the comforts of home and the excitement of exploration.
