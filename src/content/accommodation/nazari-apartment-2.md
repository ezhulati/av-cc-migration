Having undergone a recent renovation, Nazari Apartment 2 combines modern amenities with a touch of historical charm, dating back to its original construction in 2003. Guests will appreciate the convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout their stay, ensuring they remain connected and comfortable. The Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, lies 42 km away, while the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania is merely 600 metres from the doorstep, placing guests in the cultural epicenter of Tirana.

This one-bedroom apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It features a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen is fully equipped with a microwave and toaster, allowing guests to prepare meals with ease. The bathroom boasts a walk-in shower, adding an extra layer of convenience. For those with specific needs, the apartment is allergy-free and maintains a strict non-smoking policy.

Additional services such as towel and linen provision are available, ensuring a hassle-free stay. Situated near attractions like the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, National Museum of History Albania, and House of Leaves, guests are perfectly positioned to dive into Albania's storied past. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, making Nazari Apartment 2 an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Tirana.
