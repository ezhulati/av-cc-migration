The hotel prides itself on its commitment to a smoke-free environment, further enhancing the quality of the guest experience. Accommodations at NORGE HOTEL are thoughtfully furnished, featuring flat-screen TVs and hairdryers, catering to the needs of modern travelers.

Strategically located, the hotel is just a brief walk from the enchanting Paradise Beach and a mere 600 meters from the vibrant Puerto Rico Beach. For those intrigued by history and nature, Butrint National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is only 3.6 km away, offering an easy escape to one of Albania's most treasured landscapes. For international guests, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km from the hotel, making NORGE HOTEL an accessible haven for both local and global visitors seeking a peaceful yet convenient getaway.
