The studio prides itself on its array of amenities, including complimentary WiFi, ensuring guests stay connected during their travels. The space is thoughtfully furnished with a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen, featuring a refrigerator and stovetop, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. Moreover, the provision of fresh towels and bed linen underscores the studio's commitment to comfort and convenience. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

For those looking to venture beyond the confines of their cozy retreat, the studio offers a car rental service, facilitating easy exploration of Tirana's must-visit sites. The apartment is a stone's throw away from significant landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Museum of History Albania, making it an ideal base for cultural enthusiasts. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 14 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

In essence, Modern Central Studio stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those desiring a harmonious blend of accessibility, comfort, and a touch of home in Albania's bustling capital.
