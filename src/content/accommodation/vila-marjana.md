Guests at Vila Marjana are treated to accommodations that blend functionality with comfort. Each apartment is air-conditioned and comes equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The convenience of a fridge and coffee machine in each unit adds a touch of home, while the inclusion of a bidet, hair dryer, and desk speaks to the thoughtful touches provided. For those seeking a view, select units boast balconies that offer serene mountain vistas, creating a perfect backdrop for relaxation. Privacy and comfort are paramount, with each unit featuring a private bathroom and ample wardrobe space, complemented by the provision of bed linen and towels.

The start of each day at Vila Marjana is marked by a continental breakfast offering a variety of choices, from fresh pastries and pancakes to fruits, catering to diverse tastes and preferences. The on-site bar presents an inviting space for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

Beyond the comforts of their apartments, guests have the opportunity to engage with the surrounding area through sightseeing tours available nearby. The option to relax in the garden offers a peaceful retreat from the day's activities.

Located a mere 1.8 km from La Petite Beach and within easy reach of Butrint National Park, 18 km away, Vila Marjana serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and historical richness of the region. With the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, located 94 km away, the complex remains conveniently accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

With its combination of well-appointed accommodations, desirable location, and attentive services, including 24-hour front desk, full-day security, and currency exchange, Vila Marjana represents a compelling choice for those visiting Sarandë.
