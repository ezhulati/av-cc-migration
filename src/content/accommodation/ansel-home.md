Guests at Ansel Home are treated to an array of amenities designed to ensure a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. Each apartment boasts a balcony offering breathtaking sea views, a cozy seating area for unwinding, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The presence of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and toaster, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals. The convenience of a private bathroom, outfitted with a shower and complimentary toiletries, adds to the comfort.

For those who prefer outdoor spaces, select units feature a terrace, perfect for enjoying the serene surroundings. The inclusion of a coffee machine in all units ensures that mornings at Ansel Home start with the invigorating aroma of freshly brewed coffee. The practical addition of a desk caters to the needs of business travelers or anyone requiring a dedicated workspace.

The convenience extends beyond the apartment's walls, with a minimarket on the premises, ensuring that essentials are always within reach. For guests arriving by air, the Ioannina Airport is located 96 km away, making Ansel Home accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

Ansel Home not only offers a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the captivating beauty of Sarandë and its surroundings, promising an unforgettable experience for all who visit.
