Guests at Villa Ermis are treated to a luxurious stay in a holiday home that boasts air-conditioning for those warm summer days, two cozy bedrooms, and a spacious living room, ensuring ample space for relaxation and entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen and two well-appointed bathrooms add a touch of home comfort to the beachside experience.

The charm of Villa Ermis is further enhanced by its beautiful garden and inviting balcony, where guests can unwind and soak in the picturesque surroundings. The addition of a terrace provides an ideal spot for evening relaxation or morning contemplation with a view. Convenience is paramount, with free private parking and complimentary WiFi ensuring a hassle-free stay.

Situated 162 km from Ioannina Airport, Villa Ermis is accessible yet feels like a secluded paradise, making it a highly recommended choice for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
