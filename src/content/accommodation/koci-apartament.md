Within its walls, Koci Apartament boasts a generously sized living space, complete with a bedroom that promises restful nights. The living area is anchored by a sleek flat-screen TV, while the kitchen is a model of modern convenience, outfitted with an oven, fridge, washing machine, and stovetop. The provision of towels and bed linen underscores the apartment's commitment to comfort.

For those eager to explore, Flamingo Beach and Heaven Beach are mere moments away, offering unique coastal experiences just 1.7 km and 1.8 km from the doorstep, respectively. Further afield, the enchanting Butrint National Park invites exploration at 11 km away, with Ancient Fanoti beckoning at 46 km for the historically inclined.

Situated 99 km from Ioannina Airport, Koci Apartament positions itself as an ideal base for both short-term escapes and longer stays, promising a blend of relaxation and adventure in a setting that feels both intimate and inviting.
