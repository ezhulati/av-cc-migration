This property stands out for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, coupled with modern amenities that cater to the needs of today's traveler. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and the convenience of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, washing machine, towels, and bed linen ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the elegance of upscale accommodation.

Situated a brief 13-minute walk from the iconic Clock Tower Tirana and only 1.6 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, l.l maison is perfectly located for guests eager to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the city. The apartment is also in close proximity to other notable attractions, including the National Museum of History Albania, the House of Leaves, and the tranquil Rinia Park, making it an ideal base for those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and history.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 13 km away, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free arrival and departure experience. Whether visiting for leisure or business, l.l maison offers an unparalleled stay in Tirana, promising not just a place to rest, but a gateway to the city's most cherished experiences.
