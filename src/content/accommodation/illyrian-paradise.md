The accommodation at ILLYRIAN Paradise is thoughtfully designed to ensure a comfortable and private stay. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a fully equipped kitchenette, complete with a fridge and kettle, in a spacious air-conditioned apartment that also features a cozy living room. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of towels and bed linen, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. Additionally, the apartment boasts a private entrance and soundproofing, offering an oasis of privacy in a bustling world.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the area, Butrint National Park is just 8.1 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity. Despite its secluded feel, ILLYRIAN Paradise remains easily accessible, with Corfu International Airport located 98 km away, making it a convenient choice for international travelers.

ILLYRIAN Paradise stands out as a testament to the harmonious blend of natural beauty and comfortable living, making it a must-visit destination for those seeking a peaceful escape in Kalive Gjoka.
