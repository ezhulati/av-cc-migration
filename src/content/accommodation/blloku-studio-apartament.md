This air-conditioned apartment offers a cozy retreat with its well-appointed bedroom, inviting living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs with a microwave and kettle. The bathroom is modern and includes a walk-in shower and hairdryer, ensuring guests enjoy both comfort and convenience. Essential amenities such as towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, enhancing the stay experience.

Adding to the appeal is the apartment's private entrance, which guarantees guests privacy and a sense of exclusivity during their stay. Free WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests remain connected, whether for leisure or work.

For those keen to explore Tirana's cultural scene, the apartment is conveniently located near several attractions, including the Pyramid of Tirana, Reja - The Cloud, and the poignant Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument. With Rinia Park, Saint Paul Cathedral, and the House of Leaves also within walking distance, guests have the city's rich history and vibrant present at their fingertips.

Situated 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Blloku Studio Apartment offers both accessibility and a tranquil haven for visitors to Tirana, making it an excellent choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the city's charm.
