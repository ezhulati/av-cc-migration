The interior of the apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to all needs. It houses a bedroom, a living room adorned with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen boasting an oven and a toaster for those who love to cook. The bathroom comes with a bidet, adding a touch of luxury to the everyday routine. One of the highlights is the terrace, offering an outdoor dining area where guests can savor their meals against the backdrop of the city's skyline. Additionally, an ironing service is available, ensuring that guests' attire is always crisp and presentable.

Located in a vibrant area of Tirana, Plis Apartment Myslym Shyri is within proximity to several attractions that capture the essence of the city. Guests are just a stone's throw away from the historic Skanderbeg Square, the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the lush greenery of Rinia Park. For those traveling by air, the convenience is unparalleled, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport merely 14 km away. This apartment stands out as an ideal spot for those eager to explore the city's rich culture and history while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed accommodation.
