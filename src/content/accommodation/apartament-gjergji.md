This holiday home is a haven of relaxation and convenience, featuring air-conditioned accommodation that caters to every need. The interior boasts a bedroom, a bathroom, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. Linens and towels are thoughtfully provided, ensuring a comfortable stay. The terrace, offering sea views, and a patio that serves as an outdoor dining area, elevate the experience, allowing guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the picturesque landscape.

Privacy is paramount at Apartament Gjergji, with a private entrance to the accommodation ensuring a secluded retreat. The property also includes a garden where guests can unwind and reconnect with nature. For convenience, a minimarket on-site means essentials are always within reach.

Situated 16 km from the historic Butrint National Park and 50 km from Ancient Fanoti, the holiday home serves as a perfect gateway for those eager to explore Albania's rich history and natural beauty. With Ioannina Airport 95 km away, it remains easily accessible for international travelers.

Apartament Gjergji stands out as a top choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of adventure in the heart of Sarandë.
