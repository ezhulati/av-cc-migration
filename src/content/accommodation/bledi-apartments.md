Each apartment at Bledi is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning to ensure a pleasant stay regardless of the weather. The provision of complimentary private parking and WiFi adds to the seamless experience, allowing guests to stay connected and explore with ease. The interiors are thoughtfully designed, featuring a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain vistas, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a dining area for intimate meals. The kitchen is well-appointed, catering to the culinary needs of guests, while the private bathroom comes complete with a shower and hair dryer, ensuring a home-like comfort. Notably, an oven is available in all units, adding a touch of convenience for those who prefer home-cooked meals.

For those intrigued by the allure of nature and history, Butrint National Park is a mere 15 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. Further afield, Ancient Fanoti is located 50 km from the apartments, presenting an opportunity for adventurous explorers. The nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is 95 km away, making Bledi Apartments an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers alike.

Bledi Apartments stands as a testament to the charm and tranquility of Sarandë, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a memorable stay in this picturesque locale.
