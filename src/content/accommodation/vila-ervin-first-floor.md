The accommodations are thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a kitchenette complete with a fridge, stovetop, and kitchenware, catering to guests' culinary needs. The inclusion of a private bathroom, outfitted with slippers and a hairdryer, adds a touch of home-like comfort, while the property's mountain views promise a scenic backdrop to a memorable stay.

For those looking to unwind, Vila Ervin First Floor doesn't disappoint. An on-site bar serves as a cozy spot for evening relaxation, and a garden offers a tranquil retreat for moments of peace. Situated a mere 1.1 km from Lori Beach and 2.4 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park, the guest house is perfectly positioned for guests eager to explore the natural beauty of the area.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Corfu International Airport located 92 km away, ensuring that arrival and departure are as smooth as possible. Vila Ervin First Floor is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the captivating charm of Ksamil, making it an ideal choice for discerning travelers.
