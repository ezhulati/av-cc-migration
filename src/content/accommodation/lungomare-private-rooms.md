The accommodations at Lungomare are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, flat-screen TVs with cable channels for in-room entertainment, and essential amenities such as a fridge and kettle. Comfort is further enhanced with plush bathrobes, a wardrobe for personal belongings, and private bathrooms equipped with showers and hair dryers. For guests who prefer to cater to their own culinary needs, select rooms boast fully equipped kitchenettes, adding a touch of home to the travel experience.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Lungomare Private Rooms extends its hospitality outdoors with a terrace that invites guests to unwind and soak in the serene atmosphere. An outdoor fireplace provides a cozy gathering spot on clear evenings, perfect for sharing stories or simply enjoying the tranquil surroundings.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures that guests have easy access to essentials without having to venture far. The property's garden offers a peaceful retreat, ideal for moments of reflection or leisurely afternoons spent in the company of nature.

Situated a mere 700 meters from Ri Beach and 2.8 km from Vjetër Beach, Lungomare Private Rooms serves as an ideal base for beach enthusiasts and those eager to explore the coastal beauty of Vlore. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 153 km away, the property remains accessible for both domestic and international travelers, promising a memorable stay marked by comfort, convenience, and the allure of the Albanian Riviera.
