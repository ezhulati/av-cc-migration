Boasting a prime location, the Renovated Condo is merely moments away from the city's heart, with Kuzum Baba and Independence Square located just a few kilometers away. This ensures guests are perfectly placed to explore the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë.

The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities and thoughtful touches. Guests will appreciate the air-conditioned space, complete with a cozy bedroom, a pristine bathroom stocked with fresh towels and bed linen, and a living area that invites relaxation. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services means entertainment is always at hand.

Culinary enthusiasts will be delighted by the fully equipped kitchen and dining area, providing everything needed to whip up delicious meals. Moreover, the terrace offers a serene spot to enjoy city views or dine al fresco on the patio, furnished for guests' comfort.

For those who value convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures essentials are never far away. And with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 150 km away, the Renovated Condo serves as a perfect base for both local exploration and further travels.

In summary, the Renovated Condo in Vlorë presents a compelling choice for travelers looking for a blend of modern amenities, convenience, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local culture.
