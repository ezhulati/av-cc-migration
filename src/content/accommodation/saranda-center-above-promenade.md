This property boasts an air-conditioned apartment that is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a kettle, ensuring guests feel right at home. The inclusion of two bathrooms equipped with showers and hair dryers adds to the convenience, making it ideal for families or groups. The highlight, however, is the balcony. Offering breathtaking sea views and furnished for comfort, it invites guests to relax and soak in the serene atmosphere.

For those intrigued by history and nature, the proximity to Butrint National Park, just 16 km away, presents an opportunity to explore Albania's rich heritage. Additionally, with Ioannina Airport located 96 km from the apartment, access to and from the property is relatively straightforward for international travelers.

Saranda Center (above promenade) stands out not only for its exceptional location and amenities but also for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a pleasant stay for all guests. Whether it's waking up to the gentle sound of waves or enjoying a quiet evening on the balcony under the stars, this property promises a memorable stay in the heart of Sarandë.
