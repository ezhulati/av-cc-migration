Located just a short 600-meter stroll from Saranda City Beach, this property positions itself as a non-smoking haven for beach lovers and city explorers alike. The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a bathroom, complete with bed linen, towels, and a terrace that presents breathtaking sea views. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to whip up their own culinary delights.

One of the standout features of this apartment is the outdoor dining area, allowing guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the city's vibrant atmosphere. To elevate the experience further, the apartment offers the option of wine or champagne, perfect for toasting to a memorable stay.

For beach enthusiasts, La Petite Beach is conveniently located less than 1 km away, while Maestral Beach is a mere 13-minute walk, offering ample opportunities for sunbathing and relaxation. For those looking to explore beyond Sarandë, Ioannina Airport is 96 km away, making Orange Ap. 2 Bed - City Centre Sarande! an excellent base for both local and regional adventures.
