Accommodations at Vila 1926 are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring shared bathrooms equipped with slippers and fresh bed linen to ensure a restful night. The hostel goes above and beyond by offering a shared kitchen, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their own meals, a convenience especially appreciated by those who enjoy the comforts of home while traveling.

The 24-hour front desk and currency exchange services underscore the hostel's commitment to guest satisfaction, ensuring a hassle-free stay from check-in to departure. For those looking to explore the spiritual and historical side of the region, the Monastery Saint Naum is a mere 43 km away, making for an easy day trip.

Situated 72 km from Kastoria National Airport, Vila 1926 Hostel is not only accessible but stands as a beacon of hospitality and comfort in Korçë. Whether it's the strategic location, the array of on-site amenities, or the warm, inviting atmosphere, this hostel is a top pick for travelers venturing into this picturesque part of the world.
