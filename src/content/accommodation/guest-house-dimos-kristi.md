Each room at Guest House Dimos Kristi is thoughtfully equipped with a television, inviting guests to unwind in comfort. The kitchen, complete with an oven, fridge, and essential kitchenware, caters to those who prefer the homely touch of preparing their own meals. The inclusion of a private bathroom, adorned with bathrobes and slippers, adds a layer of luxury to the experience. Moreover, the guest house takes pride in offering essential amenities such as bed linen, towels, and a laundry service, ensuring a carefree stay.

For those needing to pick up essentials or snacks, a convenient minimarket on the premises meets this need effortlessly. Situated a mere 89 km from Ioannina Airport, Guest House Dimos Kristi represents an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of home away from home.
