The accommodations at Aria&Erika Apartments are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air-conditioned spaces that promise a respite from the Mediterranean heat. For those who value privacy and the option to prepare their own meals, select units boast fully equipped kitchens, adding a touch of home to the travel experience.

Families will find the spacious family rooms an ideal setup for a comfortable stay, ensuring that everyone has ample room to relax and unwind.

Situated 18 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport 98 km away, Aria&Erika Apartments not only offers easy access to local attractions but also serves as a convenient gateway for further adventures in the region.
