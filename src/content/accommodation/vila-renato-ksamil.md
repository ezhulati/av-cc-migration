Each apartment at Vila Renato Ksamil is a haven of comfort, boasting tiled floors and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a refrigerator, making self-catering a breeze. The dining area, coupled with a flat-screen TV featuring a wide array of cable channels, ensures guests feel at home. The private bathrooms with showers add a touch of convenience, while the provision of bed linen and towels underscores the attention to detail. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony present in every unit, offering breathtaking lake views that promise to enchant guests.

The garden area provides a tranquil space for relaxation, inviting guests to unwind amidst nature's embrace. For those eager to explore, Bora Bora Beach is located less than 1 km away, with Coco Beach also within easy walking distance, offering a perfect opportunity to experience the local beach culture.

Situated 93 km from Corfu International Airport, Vila Renato Ksamil not only promises a memorable stay but also ensures ease of access, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in the heart of Ksamil.
