This apartment is not just about its prime location; it offers a spacious layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that serves as the perfect spot for morning coffees or evening relaxation, all while gazing at the expansive sea. The inclusion of modern amenities such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom with a bidet, ensures guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience, making it a home away from home.

Privacy is paramount at Spectacular SeaView Apartment, with a private entrance that guarantees guests their own secluded haven. The addition of a minimarket on the premises is a thoughtful touch, offering ease and accessibility for any last-minute needs or snacks.

For those looking to explore, the apartment is conveniently located 2 km from the lively Coco Bongo Beach and a scenic drive away from Kuzum Baba, offering a glimpse into the local culture and natural beauty. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 156 km away, making it accessible yet comfortably distant from the hustle and bustle of airport traffic.

Spectacular SeaView Apartment stands as a testament to the perfect blend of location, comfort, and privacy, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience Vlorë in all its glory.
