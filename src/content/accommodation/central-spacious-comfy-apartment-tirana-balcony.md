This air-conditioned one-bedroom apartment doesn't just promise space; it delivers it, along with a host of amenities designed to make stays as comfortable as possible. Guests will find themselves enjoying the luxury of a private balcony, the convenience of free WiFi, and the security of a private entrance, all within a smoke-free environment. The living room, equipped with a flat-screen TV, invites relaxation, while the fully equipped kitchen, complete with a microwave and fridge, makes dining in an easy choice. The inclusion of a hairdryer, towels, and bed linen adds a touch of home.

Situated close to notable landmarks such as the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, Clock Tower Tirana, and Et'hem Bey Mosque, and only 1.7 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, guests are perfectly positioned to immerse themselves in the local culture and history. The apartment is also conveniently located near the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, National Museum of History Albania, and Toptani Shopping Centre, catering to a variety of interests.

For those venturing further, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, making CENTRAL - Spacious Comfy Apartment TIRANA an ideal base for both short city breaks and longer explorations of Albania's capital. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest comfort, this apartment stands out as a top accommodation choice for discerning travelers.
