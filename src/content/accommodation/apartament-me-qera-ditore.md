A mere 3.3 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, the property offers easy access to one of Tirana's most breathtaking natural attractions, while the bustling Skanderbeg Square lies just 2.7 km away, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local lifestyle.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Albanian afternoons, a separate bedroom for privacy, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and microwave, catering to all culinary needs. The inclusion of towels and bed linen ensures a hassle-free stay, emphasizing the non-smoking environment for the comfort of all guests.

For those interested in exploring the local area, Tanners' Bridge is within a 1.7 km reach, and the Toptani Shopping Centre is 2.4 km away, perfect for guests looking to indulge in a bit of retail therapy. Additionally, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 17 km from the apartment, adds an extra layer of ease to travel arrangements.

With free WiFi available throughout the property, guests can stay connected and share their experiences with friends and family or plan their next day's adventure in Tirana. Apartament me qera ditore stands out as a comfortable, conveniently located, and well-equipped option for travelers seeking to experience the best of Tirana.
