This air-conditioned haven doesn't just promise a comfortable stay; it delivers with a spacious layout that includes 2 bedrooms, a bathroom, and a balcony offering tranquil garden views. The apartment is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and the convenience of a private entrance underscore the property's commitment to privacy and comfort.

Situated a mere 400 meters from the serene Mali I Robit Beach and within proximity to notable landmarks like the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre, the apartment serves as an ideal base for those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and history. Moreover, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 41 km away, accessibility is never an issue.

For travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of local charm, the Family Apartment in Golem emerges as a distinguished choice. Offering private parking on site and complimentary WiFi, this non-smoking property ensures that every aspect of your stay is catered to, making it a compelling option for those planning their next getaway to Golem.
