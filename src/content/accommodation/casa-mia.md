Accommodations at Casa Mia are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring private bathrooms equipped with a bidet and hairdryer, alongside the modern amenity of free WiFi access. Select rooms boast the added luxury of city views, ensuring a memorable stay. Each room is meticulously prepared with fresh bed linen, emphasizing the hostel's attention to detail and dedication to guest satisfaction.

Strategically located, Casa Mia is conveniently close to notable landmarks, including Independence Square, which is just 700 meters away, and Kuzum Baba, located 1.5 km from the property. For guests arriving from afar, the hostel is accessible from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 149 km away, with the added convenience of a paid airport shuttle service available upon request.

Casa Mia presents a compelling choice for travelers eager to explore the vibrant city of Vlorë, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and thoughtful amenities.
