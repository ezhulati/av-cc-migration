Guests of the villa are treated to a spacious layout that includes two bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with essentials such as bed linen and towels, and a living area that's both modern and cozy, thanks to a flat-screen TV with streaming services. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen ensure that every meal feels like home, with the added benefit of soundproofing and a private entrance for unparalleled privacy.

The on-site amenities further enhance the stay, with a family-friendly restaurant offering outdoor dining options, a coffee shop for those morning or afternoon pick-me-ups, and barbecue facilities perfect for enjoying the warmer months. The 24-hour front desk ensures assistance is always available, making every aspect of the stay seamless and worry-free.

For those looking to explore the surrounding area, Pacific 2 Bedroom Villa Palase does not disappoint. The villa offers bicycle rental services, making it easy for guests to enjoy cycling, fishing, and hiking in the picturesque surroundings. Sightseeing tours are also available, providing an excellent opportunity to immerse oneself in the local culture and natural beauty.

Located 168 km from Ioannina Airport, this villa is a convenient option for both domestic and international travelers, promising a memorable stay that combines the comfort of home with the beauty and excitement of Palasë.
