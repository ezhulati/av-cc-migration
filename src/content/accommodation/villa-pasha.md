The villa itself is a testament to modern living, featuring two tastefully appointed bedrooms, two well-equipped bathrooms, and the convenience of bed linen and towels. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, complemented by a dining area for intimate meals. The fully equipped kitchen caters to culinary enthusiasts, while the terrace offers breathtaking mountain views, setting the stage for moments of tranquility.

Privacy is paramount at Villa Pasha, with a private entrance that adds an extra layer of exclusivity to the accommodation. The garden invites guests to unwind in a peaceful setting, further enhancing the villa's appeal.

Located strategically, Villa Pasha is just 5.6 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 5.8 km from the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, making it an ideal base for explorers keen to delve into the city's rich history. For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 19 km away, ensuring ease of access for international guests. Villa Pasha stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a harmonious blend of luxury and location in Tirana.
