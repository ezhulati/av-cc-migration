Each room at Hotel Palma is a testament to comfort and convenience, boasting a balcony that invites guests to enjoy their own private slice of Durrës. The air-conditioned spaces are adorned with homelike furnishings, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Entertainment and connectivity are ensured with satellite TV, while the inclusion of a private bathroom equipped with a shower and complimentary toiletries adds an extra layer of convenience to the guest experience.

The commitment of Hotel Palma to guest satisfaction is further evidenced by its 24-hour reception, ready to attend to needs and inquiries at any hour. Additionally, the provision of complimentary on-site parking addresses the practical concerns of those traveling by car. Its location is notably advantageous, being just a 30-minute drive from both the vibrant heart of Tirana city center and Tirana Airport, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers seeking a blend of convenience and comfort in Durrës.
