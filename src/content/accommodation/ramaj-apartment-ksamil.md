This property offers air-conditioned accommodations that come with the added luxury of a balcony, providing stunning garden views that promise a serene retreat. The apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring a spacious layout that includes two bedrooms, a living room, and a well-equipped kitchen, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. Entertainment needs are met with the provision of a flat-screen TV, and the commitment to a non-smoking environment ensures the comfort of all guests.

Convenience is further enhanced by the availability of a minimarket on-site, making it easy for guests to grab essentials without having to venture far. For those traveling with children, the apartment goes above and beyond by offering both indoor and outdoor play areas, ensuring that younger guests are entertained. Additionally, an outdoor fireplace provides a cozy spot for guests to unwind after a day of cycling or exploring the surrounding area.

Transportation needs are seamlessly addressed with airport transfer services and the option to rent bicycles, allowing guests to explore at their own pace. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making Ramaj Apartment Ksamil an accessible choice for international travelers.

In summary, Ramaj Apartment Ksamil stands as a testament to thoughtful accommodation that prioritizes guest comfort, convenience, and the opportunity to immerse in the local culture and natural beauty of Ksamil.
