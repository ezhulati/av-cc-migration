The property boasts a meticulously designed layout featuring two elegantly appointed bedrooms, a spacious living room, and a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances including a dishwasher and an oven. Each space within the villa is thoughtfully curated to enhance guest comfort, from the air-conditioning and soundproofing to the cozy fireplace, ensuring a stay that is both relaxing and rejuvenating. The villa's thoughtful design extends to its exterior, with a patio offering serene pool views, setting the stage for tranquil mornings or peaceful evening reflections.

Privacy and security are paramount at LT Luxury Villa, with a private entrance for guests and round-the-clock security measures in place, providing peace of mind throughout the stay. For those who enjoy outdoor living, the villa does not disappoint, offering barbecue facilities and a sun terrace, perfect for unwinding under the sky or hosting intimate gatherings.

Situated within easy reach of notable attractions, including the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and the Rock of Kavaje, and only 30 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, LT Luxury Villa serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural and natural landscape of the area.

In summary, LT Luxury Villa stands out as a sanctuary of luxury and privacy, offering an unparalleled stay experience for those seeking to immerse themselves in the beauty and tranquility of Rrushkull.
