This apartment stands out for its commitment to providing a seamless stay, featuring complimentary WiFi and private parking at no additional cost. Despite its proximity to the city's bustling center, guests can enjoy a tranquil retreat in the air-conditioned spaces, a mere 5.2 km away from the scenic views offered by the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and a comfortable distance of 45 km from the natural beauty of the Rock of Kavaje.

Accommodation at Deluxe Apartment 1 is thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, boasting a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are also catered to with the provision of a flat-screen TV.

For those keen to explore, the apartment is conveniently positioned near several of Tirana's attractions, including the serene Saint Paul Cathedral, the bustling Toptani Shopping Centre, and the culturally enriching National Gallery of Arts Tirana. Moreover, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is easily accessible, located just 16 km away, making Deluxe Apartment 1 an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Albania's vibrant capital.
