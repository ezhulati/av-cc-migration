Accommodations at Hotel Kaceli are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms equipped with modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs and refrigerators. Select rooms boast the added luxury of a balcony, providing a private outdoor space for guests to enjoy. The thoughtful inclusion of a seating area and desk in every room ensures a comfortable and productive stay, whether for business or leisure.

Guests at Hotel Kaceli are treated to a sumptuous buffet and continental breakfast each morning, starting the day on a high note with a range of tasty options. The hotel's commitment to convenience extends to its services, including room service, a 24-hour front desk, and complimentary WiFi throughout the property. For those with vehicles, the availability of free private parking removes any parking woes.

Exploring the surrounding area is made effortless with the hotel's shuttle service, inviting guests to discover the local sights and sounds. Situated 115 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Hotel Kaceli is a choice destination for travelers seeking a blend of serenity, service, and accessibility in the heart of Berat.
