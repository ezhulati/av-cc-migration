The apartment itself is a haven of relaxation and entertainment. Guests can enjoy a spacious layout that includes a fully equipped kitchen boasting modern appliances like an oven, microwave, and toaster. The living area is designed for leisure, offering a cozy seating and dining area, complemented by a PS3 for gaming enthusiasts. The bedroom promises a restful night's sleep, while the bathroom features a walk-in shower and bidet, emphasizing comfort and convenience.

One of the apartment's highlights is its terrace, offering guests a serene spot to unwind and soak in the picturesque surroundings. The addition of a private entrance and soundproofing ensures an undisturbed and intimate experience.

Located at a distance from key attractions, EKA Luxury One-Bedroom Apartment is 39 km from Skanderbeg Square and 43 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, making it an ideal base for exploring the area. For those arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 33 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for easy transfers.

In summary, EKA Luxury One-Bedroom Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, providing guests with a blend of luxury, privacy, and convenience, perfect for those looking to explore Durres and its surroundings.
