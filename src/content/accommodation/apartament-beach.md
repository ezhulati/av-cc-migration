Apartament Beach stands out for its thoughtful amenities, including air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, a balcony to enjoy the serene surroundings, complimentary private parking, and free WiFi to keep guests connected. The apartment is a cozy retreat after a day of exploration, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for home-cooked meals, and a bathroom stocked with essentials. Entertainment is also on hand with a flat-screen TV available for guests' enjoyment.

For those intrigued by history and culture, the apartment is conveniently positioned. It's a mere 38 km from the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and just a bit further from the heart of the city, Skanderbeg Square. The Durres Amphitheatre, another must-visit landmark, is only 6.5 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich past. Adventure seekers will appreciate the proximity to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 42 km away, providing breathtaking views of the region. Additionally, the Rock of Kavaje, a local natural wonder, is just 5.5 km from the doorstep.

For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 32 km from the apartment, making Apartament Beach an ideal base for both short getaways and extended stays in Durrës. Whether it's the allure of the beach, the call of historical sites, or simply the need for a comfortable and convenient place to stay, Apartament Beach offers a well-rounded experience for all who visit.
