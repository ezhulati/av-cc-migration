This air-conditioned apartment, boasting a welcoming balcony and complimentary WiFi, positions itself as an ideal retreat for those eager to explore the enchanting surroundings. Situated within easy reach of La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach, and not far from the historical wonders of Butrint National Park, guests are perfectly placed to enjoy a variety of experiences.

Inside, the apartment reveals a spacious layout featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring a restful night's sleep. Entertainment is readily available with a TV equipped with satellite channels, while the fully equipped kitchen caters to all culinary needs. Guests will find an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge at their disposal, making self-catering a breeze. Additional comforts include a shower, a dressing room, a cozy seating area, and even a fireplace, adding a touch of warmth to cooler evenings.

For those arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 98 km away, making the apartment accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, this apartment in Sarandë offers a delightful base for visitors looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of the Albanian Riviera, combining the comforts of home with the allure of the Mediterranean.
