This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it offers a sanctuary of comfort and convenience. Guests are welcomed into a space thoughtfully designed with a bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen, and a modern bathroom, complemented by the presence of a flat-screen TV for those moments of relaxation.

City Center 6 - Tirana Way Apartments stands out as an ideal lodging for those eager to experience the essence of Tirana, offering both the comforts of home and the excitement of exploration right at its doorstep.
