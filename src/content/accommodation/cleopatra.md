Additionally, the heart of the city, Skanderbeg Square, is located 38 km away, presenting a perfect opportunity for guests to explore the vibrant local life.

Guests are welcomed into a meticulously designed air-conditioned apartment that promises a home-away-from-home experience. The apartment boasts a modern aesthetic with its flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen, ensuring both comfort and convenience during the stay.

For those keen on exploring further, the Durres Amphitheatre, a testament to the rich history of the area, is situated just 6.5 km from the hotel. Meanwhile, the House of Leaves, located 38 km away, offers a deep dive into the intriguing past of the region. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, located 33 km from Cleopatra, making it an accessible option for international and domestic travelers alike.

Cleopatra Hotel stands out as an exemplary choice for those in pursuit of relaxation, convenience, and a touch of adventure during their visit to Durrës.
