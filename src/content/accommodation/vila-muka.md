Each apartment at Vila Muka is thoughtfully designed to enhance guest comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, and a balcony to enjoy the serene views. Entertainment and practicality are well catered for with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom equipped with a shower and hair dryer in every unit. For those who prefer to prepare their meals, select apartments boast a well-equipped kitchen, complete with a toaster, stovetop, and kitchenware, making it an ideal choice for families or longer stays. The provision of bed linen and towels in the units adds a touch of home.

The garden offers a peaceful oasis for guests to unwind, further enhancing the appeal of Vila Muka as a relaxing getaway. Its location is unbeatably convenient for beach lovers, with Paradise Beach also just 600 meters away, and Lori Beach slightly further at 800 meters, making it easy for guests to explore the area's coastal beauty. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making Vila Muka an accessible option for those coming from afar.

Vila Muka presents a compelling option for those in search of a comfortable, well-situated base to explore the enchanting surroundings of Ksamil, promising a stay that combines relaxation with the allure of the Albanian Riviera.
