Guests at Liora's Place are treated to a garden that invites relaxation and contemplation. The accommodation itself boasts air-conditioning, ensuring a comfortable stay regardless of the weather outside. For those arriving by car, the convenience of private parking on-site is a notable perk, alongside complimentary WiFi that keeps guests connected during their stay. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the property maintains a strict non-smoking policy.

The apartment within Liora's Place is generously sized, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms that promise restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen does not disappoint, being fully equipped with an oven and microwave, alongside a washing machine, making it ideal for both short and extended stays. The inclusion of a bathroom with a shower, complemented by fresh towels and bed linen, adds to the convenience. Moreover, the charm of a fireplace enhances the ambiance, making it a cozy retreat during cooler evenings.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, Kastoria National Airport is conveniently located 71 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements.

Liora's Place, with its thoughtful amenities and prime location, stands as a compelling choice for travelers seeking a blend of tranquility and convenience in Korçë.
