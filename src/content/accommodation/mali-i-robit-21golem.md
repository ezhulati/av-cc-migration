The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting a balcony that offers breathtaking sea views. It is perfectly designed to accommodate the needs of its guests, featuring two cozy bedrooms, a living room for relaxation, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is well-equipped with a fridge and stovetop, catering to those who prefer to prepare their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet and the thoughtful touch of offering wine or champagne elevate the living experience, making it feel like a home away from home.

For those who might need to pick up essentials or prefer to cook their meals, a convenient minimarket is located within the apartment complex, adding an extra layer of ease to the stay.

Situated at a comfortable distance from significant landmarks, the apartment is 48 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre, offering a perfect base for explorers keen to delve into the rich history of the area. Moreover, the Rock of Kavaje is merely 6.4 km away, presenting an opportunity for guests to explore the natural beauty of the surroundings. For travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 42 km away, ensures smooth travel plans to and from the accommodation.

Mali i Robit 2+1, Golem, thus stands as an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the opportunity to explore the enchanting beauty of Golem and its surroundings.
