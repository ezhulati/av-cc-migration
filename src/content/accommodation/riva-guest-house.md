Guests are welcomed into a spacious air-conditioned apartment, thoughtfully designed with two separate bedrooms to ensure privacy and comfort. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with slippers, alongside a cozy seating area and a living room, adds a touch of home. Entertainment needs are well catered to with a flat-screen TV boasting satellite channels, ensuring guests are never far from global happenings or local culture. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's dedication to guest privacy and ease of access.

Situated a manageable distance from Ioannina Airport, merely 84 km away, RIVA Guest House positions itself as an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of tranquility, comfort, and convenience amidst the natural beauty of the surrounding area.
