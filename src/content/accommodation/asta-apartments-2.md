The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain views. It is well-appointed with a bedroom, a cozy living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment needs, and a kitchen equipped with modern appliances including an oven and a toaster. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of convenience, complemented by captivating city views that can be enjoyed from various vantage points within the apartment.

Strategically positioned, ASTA Apartments 2 is merely 1.7 km away from the historic Independence Square and a brief drive to the iconic Kuzum Baba, making it an ideal base for explorers keen on immersing themselves in the local culture and history. For those venturing further, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is accessible, located 150 km from the accommodation, ensuring that arrivals and departures are as smooth as the stay.

In essence, ASTA Apartments 2 stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay for those visiting the vibrant city of Vlorë.
