The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a generous layout that includes a bedroom, a living area with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony for soaking in the city's vibrant atmosphere. The kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs, whether it's a quick breakfast or a full-fledged dinner. Complimentary towels and bed linen add a touch of luxury, ensuring guests feel at home.

For those looking to explore, the apartment's location does not disappoint. The historic Durres Amphitheatre is just 7 km away, offering a glimpse into the city's rich past, while the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha stands 39 km away, presenting an intriguing cultural excursion. Convenience is further enhanced by a nearby supermarket, making daily essentials easily accessible.

Adventure seekers can venture further to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 42 km from the apartment, for breathtaking views, or explore the natural beauty of the Rock of Kavaje, merely 4.3 km away. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 33 km from the doorstep, Durres Summer Apartment ensures a seamless travel experience, making it an ideal choice for both short getaways and extended stays in this enchanting city.
