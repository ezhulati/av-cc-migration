The layout of Orange Apartment includes a cozy bedroom and a living room, designed to accommodate the needs of both solo travelers and families. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, while the kitchenette, complete with a microwave and fridge, allows guests the freedom to prepare their own meals. Additionally, the convenience of a washing machine and the option to request towels and bed linen underscore the property's attention to guest comfort.

For those looking to explore the area, Orange Apartment does not disappoint. A car rental service is offered directly from the apartment, making local excursions hassle-free. Anglers and outdoor enthusiasts will find the proximity to prime fishing spots an added bonus.

Situated just a short walk from the picturesque Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach and within easy reach of Mali I Robit Beach, the apartment's location is ideal for beachgoers. Furthermore, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 39 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements for international visitors.

Orange Apartment stands out as a welcoming haven for those eager to experience the beauty and tranquility of Golem and its surrounding beaches.
