The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with essential linens and towels, and a living space that includes a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped, catering to guests' culinary needs, whether they choose to cook a meal or simply enjoy a coffee. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony, offering breathtaking sea views that promise a serene backdrop for meals in the outdoor dining area, with the added bonus of mountain vistas.

For those concerned with allergies or seeking a smoke-free environment, Main Square Apartment ensures a comfortable stay. The property also caters to relaxation and convenience with an in-house bar and a nearby minimarket, making it easy for guests to unwind or grab necessities without venturing far.

Located a brief drive from Durres Beach and within manageable distance from Skanderbeg Square, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the area. For guests arriving by air, the convenience is extended with a paid airport shuttle service from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 33 km away.

Main Square Apartment not only offers a stay but an experience, with its blend of comfort, convenience, and thoughtful amenities, making it a standout option for travelers to Durrës.
