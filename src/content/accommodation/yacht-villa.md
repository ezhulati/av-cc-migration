The accommodations at Yacht Villa are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned spaces that open up to balconies offering serene city views. Each apartment is thoughtfully equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV, ensuring entertainment is always within reach. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home, making stays here effortlessly comfortable.

For those arriving by car, the availability of on-site parking is a welcome feature, along with an electric vehicle charging station, catering to the needs of the modern traveler.

Yacht Villa's location is ideally positioned for guests eager to explore Sarandë's most beloved beaches, including Sarande Main Beach, La Petite Beach, and Maestral Beach. For those looking to venture further, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km away, making Yacht Villa an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors.

In summary, Yacht Villa offers a harmonious blend of accessibility, comfort, and scenic beauty, making it a top recommendation for those planning a visit to Sarandë.
