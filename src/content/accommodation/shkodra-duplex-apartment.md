The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful living spaces, boasting a generously sized bedroom, a well-appointed bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living area that merges seamlessly with a dining space. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring guests are never far from the comforts of home. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped to cater to every culinary need. Perhaps the crown jewel of this accommodation is its terrace, offering guests a private vantage point from which to soak in the majestic mountain views.

Located a mere 58 km from Podgorica Airport, the Shkodra Duplex Apartment presents an ideal retreat for those seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and a touch of luxury during their stay in Shkodër.
