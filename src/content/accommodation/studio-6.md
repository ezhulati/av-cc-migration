Guests at Studio 6 are treated to modern amenities designed to ensure a seamless stay. The apartment features complimentary WiFi, allowing visitors to stay connected with ease. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen is well-equipped with a refrigerator and stovetop, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience to the lodging experience.

From the comfort of Studio 6, guests can enjoy captivating city views, adding an urban flair to the beachside getaway. For quick essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises offers added convenience.

The apartment's location is not only ideal for beach enthusiasts but also for those keen to explore Vlorë's cultural landmarks. Independence Square is located 3.4 km away, offering a glimpse into the city's rich history, while the historic Kuzum Baba stands 4 km from the apartment, promising a cultural excursion.

For travelers arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 151 km from Studio 6, making it a feasible option for international visitors.

Studio 6 presents itself as a smart choice for those looking to experience the best of Vlorë, combining the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
