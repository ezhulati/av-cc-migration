Each room at Villa Domenico Korce is thoughtfully equipped with a desk, ensuring a seamless blend of relaxation and productivity for guests. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and complimentary toiletries, enhancing the comfort of the stay. For entertainment and convenience, all rooms feature a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. Select accommodations also boast a balcony, offering guests a private outdoor space to unwind. Storage needs are effortlessly met with the inclusion of a wardrobe in every unit.

The property's location offers an added advantage for explorers, with the Monastery Saint Naum located just 43 km away. For those traveling by air, Kastoria National Airport is within a manageable distance of 71 km, making Villa Domenico Korce an ideal base for both leisure and exploration in the region.
