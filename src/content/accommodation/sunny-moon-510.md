The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, boasting an air-conditioned bedroom that promises a restful night's sleep, complemented by a bathroom equipped with fresh bed linen and towels. The living space is thoughtfully designed with a flat-screen TV and a dining area, leading to a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs. Perhaps the most enchanting feature is the terrace, offering tranquil street views and an outdoor dining area where guests can savor the local ambiance.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate beachfront, Golem Beach is a short drive away, offering another picturesque setting for sun-seekers. The cultural heart of the region, Skanderbeg Square, is accessible at 41 km, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local heritage. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 36 km from Sunny Moon 510, making it an accessible gateway for international travelers.

Sunny Moon 510 distinguishes itself not only through its prime location and comprehensive amenities but also through its dedication to ensuring a smoke-free environment, catering to the comfort and well-being of all guests. This accommodation is a top recommendation for those in pursuit of a tranquil beachside escape with all the comforts of home.
