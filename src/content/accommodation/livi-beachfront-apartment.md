This apartment stands out for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests. Located merely 6.3 km from the iconic Rock of Kavaje, it positions itself as a serene retreat amidst the vibrant life of Durrës.

The accommodation boasts an air-conditioned space that includes a bedroom, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen with a toaster, catering to all culinary needs. The bathroom is well-appointed with a bidet and slippers, adding a touch of luxury to the everyday routine. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

Guests will find a variety of dining options in close proximity, offering a taste of local and international cuisine. For those interested in exploring the historical aspects of the area, the former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre are both within a manageable distance, located 39 km and 5 km away, respectively.

Livi Beachfront Apartment is not just about its prime location; it's about providing a memorable stay that combines the tranquility of beachfront living with the ease of access to significant landmarks. With the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, only 33 km away, it serves as an ideal base for both leisure and exploration in this beautiful region of Albania.
