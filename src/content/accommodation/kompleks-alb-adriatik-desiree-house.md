Each apartment at Kompleks Alb Adriatik Desiree' House is designed to be a home away from home, featuring a balcony that offers a breath of fresh air, a cozy seating area for relaxation, and a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and microwave, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals. The private bathrooms are modern and equipped with walk-in showers and hair dryers, ensuring a comfortable stay. For added convenience, all units are provided with fresh bed linen and towels.

For those looking to explore the local area or simply enjoy a leisurely ride, the aparthotel offers a bicycle rental service. Additionally, a minimarket on the premises ensures that guests have easy access to essentials without having to venture far.

Located merely 400 meters from Mali I Robit Beach and a short 2.1 km from the picturesque Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, the property is perfectly positioned for guests to explore the local coastline. For travelers arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 41 km away, making Kompleks Alb Adriatik Desiree' House an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in the heart of Kryemëdhej.
