Guests at Casa Mia Twin Towers are greeted with a host of thoughtful amenities designed to make their stay as comfortable and hassle-free as possible. The property boasts modern facilities including an elevator for easy access and the convenience of private check-in and check-out services, ensuring a personalized experience. The commitment to guest comfort is further underscored by the provision of complimentary WiFi throughout the premises, allowing visitors to stay connected with ease.

The highlight of Casa Mia Twin Towers is undoubtedly its spacious apartment, which is thoughtfully designed to accommodate the needs of modern travelers. The apartment features two elegantly appointed bedrooms and two well-equipped bathrooms, ensuring ample space and privacy for guests. The inclusion of bed linen and towels adds a touch of home-like comfort, while the flat-screen TV caters to entertainment needs. The fully equipped kitchen offers guests the freedom to prepare their own meals, adding an element of convenience to their stay. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony, which presents breathtaking mountain views, offering a tranquil retreat for guests to unwind.

For those seeking leisure within the property, an on-site bar provides the perfect setting to relax and socialize over drinks.

Situated a mere 42 km from the historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra and 44 km from the picturesque Lake Skadar, Casa Mia Twin Towers serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural wonders of the region. Moreover, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 49 km away, the property ensures ease of travel for international guests.

Casa Mia Twin Towers is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the beauty and charm of Shëngjin, making it a top choice for discerning travelers seeking a memorable escape by the sea.
