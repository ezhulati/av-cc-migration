The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and microwave for those home-cooked meals, and a spacious living area with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of four bathrooms, each fitted with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the property's commitment to guest comfort.

For those looking to explore, Sarande Main Beach is conveniently located just 1.2 km away, while the charming La Petite Beach can be found 1.6 km from the doorstep. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 97 km away, makes Villa All Season an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers.

With the added benefits of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, guests at Villa All Season are assured a seamless and relaxing stay, making it a standout option for anyone visiting Sarandë.
