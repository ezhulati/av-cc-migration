The apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a kitchen that boasts an oven and fridge, catering to all the needs for a home-away-from-home experience. Emphasizing a clean and healthy living space, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

Strategically positioned, Brand New Apartment Tirana is within proximity to notable landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square, the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the National Museum of History Albania, making it an ideal choice for those keen to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the city. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 13 km away, ensuring a smooth transition to and from this exquisite retreat.
