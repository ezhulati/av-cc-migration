The apartment, a non-smoking haven located just 2.6 km from the scenic Mali I Robit Beach, offers guests a tranquil retreat with its air-conditioned spaces and private balcony, promising a blend of comfort and privacy. The accommodation includes a cozy 1-bedroom layout, featuring a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen ready for culinary adventures with a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom adorned with plush bathrobes for that extra touch of luxury.

For those who appreciate the convenience of on-site amenities, a minimarket within the apartment complex ensures essentials are always within reach.

Explorers looking to venture beyond the comforts of Shehapi Apartment will find the Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, just 12 km away, while the historic Durres Amphitheatre awaits 23 km from the property. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 48 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

Shehapi Apartment stands out as an exemplary choice for guests desiring a blend of modern amenities, comfort, and the natural beauty of Kavajë's coastline.
