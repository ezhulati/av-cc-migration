The apartment is a beacon of modern amenities in a smoke-free environment, boasting air conditioning for those warm summer days. Guests can enjoy the ease of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a refrigerator, ensuring that home-cooked meals are never off the menu. The inclusion of a washing machine adds a layer of convenience for longer stays, while the provision of towels and bed linen underscores the attention to detail that makes Lakeside Family Heaven stand out.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can stay in touch with the world through complimentary WiFi. Entertainment needs are also catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it easy to unwind after a day of exploration.

Strategically located, the property is within easy reach of significant landmarks such as Port Ohrid, the Church of St. John at Kaneo, and the mystic Cave Church Archangel Michael, all approximately 38 km away. The Early Christian Basilica, another gem in the region's crown, is also situated at a similar distance, offering a glimpse into the area's rich historical tapestry.

For those looking to delve deeper into the spiritual heritage of the region, the Monastery Saint Naum is just 6.6 km away, while the Saint Nicholas Church stands 17 km from the doorstep. The nearest airport, Ohrid Airport, is 45 km away, making Lakeside Family Heaven an accessible haven for both domestic and international travelers.

Lakeside Family Heaven is not just an apartment; it's a gateway to the cultural and natural wonders of Pogradec, offering a cozy, well-appointed base from which to embark on a memorable exploration of the area.
