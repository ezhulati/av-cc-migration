Guests at Roni Apartment are treated to a serene retreat, thanks to its strategic position that combines breathtaking beach proximity with the ease of modern living. The property boasts a welcoming terrace that invites guests to unwind and soak in the tranquil atmosphere. Adding to the convenience, complimentary WiFi and on-site parking are available, ensuring a hassle-free stay. For those keen on exploring the local sights and sounds, the apartment offers car hire services, providing an easy gateway to the surrounding area's attractions.

The interior of Roni Apartment does not disappoint, offering a tastefully air-conditioned space that includes two separate bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV with streaming services ensures that entertainment is always at guests' fingertips, making it a perfect setting for both relaxation and leisure. The accommodation is strictly non-smoking, maintaining a fresh and inviting environment for all guests.

Situated 41 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 45 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Roni Apartment serves as a comfortable base for those looking to explore beyond the beach. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 36 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

Roni Apartment stands out as a top-tier choice for those in search of a memorable stay in Durrës, offering the perfect mix of beachfront bliss and accessibility to local attractions.
