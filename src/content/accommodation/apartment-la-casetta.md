Each unit within La Casetta is thoughtfully designed with a kitchen and patio, ensuring guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The addition of a terrace in select apartments elevates the experience, allowing visitors to enjoy the fresh air without leaving the comfort of their accommodation. Essential amenities such as air conditioning, a television, and a private bathroom equipped with both a shower and bathrobes, alongside complimentary bed linen and towels, underscore the complex's commitment to guest comfort.

For those who appreciate the convenience of on-site amenities, La Casetta does not disappoint with its cozy coffee shop, perfect for starting the day with a freshly brewed cup or unwinding in the afternoon.

Strategically positioned, La Casetta offers easy access to some of Tirana's most notable attractions. The historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is just 2.6 km away, while the Rock of Kavaje stands at a distance of 44 km, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history and natural beauty. Shopping enthusiasts will find the Toptani Shopping Centre within a 1.2 km reach, and the iconic Clock Tower of Tirana is a mere 1.3 km away. Additional nearby points of interest include the Tanners' Bridge, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Museum of History Albania, making it an ideal base for exploring the city's cultural landmarks.

For guests arriving by air, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being only 15 km away ensures a hassle-free travel experience to and from Apartment La Casetta. This property stands out as a top selection for those looking to immerse themselves in the sights and sounds of Tirana while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed apartment.
