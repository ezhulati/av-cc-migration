The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a well-appointed bedroom, a modern bathroom, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. Guests can expect the comfort of air conditioning, the convenience of complimentary WiFi accessible throughout the premises, and the luxury of private parking on site. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchenette ensures that guests have everything they need for a self-sufficient stay.

For those arriving by air, the convenience of airport transfers is a thoughtful touch, and the availability of a car rental service on the premises adds an extra layer of ease for explorers keen to discover the local area.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, guests are invited to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty. The area around Gjashtë is ripe for exploration, with activities such as fishing offering a glimpse into the local way of life.

With Maestral Beach less than a kilometer away and VIP Beach within easy reach, beach enthusiasts will find themselves spoilt for choice. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 96 km from the accommodation.

Albania Dream Apartment 32 not only promises a stay that is as relaxing as it is enriching but also positions itself as a gateway to the enchanting landscapes and vibrant life of Gjashtë.
