This homestay distinguishes itself with modern amenities in a cozy setting. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, allows for the preparation of home-cooked meals, adding to the feeling of a home away from home. The air-conditioned unit also boasts a walk-in shower and a dressing room, ensuring a comfortable and private stay.

The charm of Albora Guest House is further enhanced by its garden and balcony, offering a serene spot for morning coffees or evening relaxation. An outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy meals under the sky, making the most of the property's peaceful surroundings.

For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 107 km away, making the guest house accessible for international visitors seeking a tranquil escape in the heart of this vibrant region. With its thoughtful amenities and prime location, Albora Guest House stands out as a top choice for travelers looking for a blend of exploration and relaxation.
