This apartment doesn't just boast a strategic location; it offers guests a comfortable and spacious living space. With a balcony that provides serene garden views, a bedroom that promises restful nights, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, the apartment ensures a home-away-from-home experience. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV adds a touch of modern convenience, catering to the entertainment needs of guests.

For those concerned about connectivity and convenience, Koko Beach Apartment addresses these needs by offering complimentary WiFi and on-site parking, ensuring that guests stay connected and mobile. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further enhances the comfort and health of all guests.

While the allure of the beach is a significant draw, the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks adds to its appeal. Skanderbeg Square, a hub of cultural and historical significance, is located 44 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich heritage of the area. For adventure seekers, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 48 km from the apartment, provides an unforgettable experience with its breathtaking views. Additionally, the convenience of being 38 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Koko Beach Apartment an excellent choice for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Koko Beach Apartment - Golem Durres stands as a testament to comfortable, convenient, and captivating accommodation, making it a top recommendation for those planning a visit to the area.
