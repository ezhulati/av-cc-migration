Each room at Bujtina Apsus is thoughtfully equipped with a desk, ensuring that both leisure and business travelers have a space to work or plan their day. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a hairdryer, alongside in-room amenities such as a TV and air conditioning, underscores the hotel's commitment to guest comfort. Select rooms further enhance the stay experience with the addition of a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to unwind. For added convenience, a fridge is provided in all units, catering to the needs of guests preferring to keep snacks and beverages close at hand.

Located 117 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Bujtina Apsus presents a compelling option for those visiting Berat, combining essential amenities with thoughtful touches to ensure a memorable stay.
