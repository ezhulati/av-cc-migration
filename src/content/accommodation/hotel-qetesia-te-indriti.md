Each room at Hotel Qetesia-Te Indriti is a haven of relaxation, boasting air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and private bathrooms, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and connected. The added luxury of a balcony with a breathtaking mountain view in every room invites guests to start their day with a picturesque panorama.

Culinary experiences at the hotel are a delight, with a restaurant that prides itself on serving a variety of local dishes. For those looking to relax with a drink, the on-site bar offers a selection of cocktails in a cozy setting.

The attentive staff at Hotel Qetesia-Te Indriti are dedicated to making guests' stays memorable, offering personalized advice and assistance at the reception.

For those eager to explore the surrounding area, the hotel serves as an ideal base. The historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra is located 29 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history, while the natural beauty of Lake Skadar is just 31 km from the hotel. For international travelers, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 82 km away, making Hotel Qetesia-Te Indriti an accessible choice for both local and international visitors seeking a peaceful retreat by the Albanian coast.
