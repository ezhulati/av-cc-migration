The apartment itself is a haven of space and luxury, boasting a generous terrace that commands stunning sea views. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room complete with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring modern appliances such as an oven and a microwave. The convenience of a walk-in shower, air conditioning, a bidet, and a dressing room adds to the apartment's appeal, while soundproofing and a private entrance ensure a serene and undisturbed stay.

Culinary enthusiasts will delight in the on-site family-friendly restaurant, where dinner and lunch showcase the flavors of Greek cuisine. For those eager to explore, the area offers ample opportunities for walking tours, with bike and car rental services readily available to guests wishing to venture further.

Families will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a baby safety gate, making DOWN TOWN LUXURY Apartment an ideal choice for guests of all ages. Situated within easy reach of La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach, and with Ioannina Airport 97 km away, this accommodation stands out as a perfect base for exploring the enchanting surroundings of Sarandë.
