This air-conditioned studio apartment is a haven of comfort, offering guests a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a fridge, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further enhances the quality of stay for all guests.

For those looking to explore the local area, Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are conveniently located within a short driving distance, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the region. Additionally, the property's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 152 km away, facilitates ease of travel for international guests.

With its combination of stunning sea views, modern amenities, and a strategic location close to both the beach and local attractions, Noi's Sea View Studio stands out as a top accommodation choice for visitors to the area.
