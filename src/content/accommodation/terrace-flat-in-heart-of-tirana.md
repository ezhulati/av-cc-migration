Boasting a generous layout, the apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with essential amenities, and a living space adorned with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, which can be enjoyed on the terrace that offers breathtaking mountain and city views. The promise of free WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests remain connected during their stay.

For those keen on exploring, the apartment's location does not disappoint. It is conveniently located 600 meters from Tanners' Bridge and 700 meters from Skanderbeg Square, placing guests in the heart of Tirana's cultural and historical hub. Shopping enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to Toptani Shopping Centre, while art lovers can easily access the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana. Additionally, the Terrace Flat is just 15 km away from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it an accessible option for international travelers.

In essence, Terrace Flat in the Heart of Tirana stands out as an ideal accommodation for those looking to immerse themselves in the city's rich history and culture, all while enjoying the comforts of a home-like setting.
