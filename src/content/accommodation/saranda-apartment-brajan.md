Occupying a coveted position merely 2 kilometers from the quaint La Petite Beach, this apartment is not just about its prime location. Guests are treated to a generously sized living space that boasts a balcony offering breathtaking sea views. The interior is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a microwave, catering to all culinary needs. The presence of two well-appointed bathrooms with showers adds to the comfort and convenience.

For those who appreciate the joy of dining al fresco, the outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy their meals against the backdrop of the sea, making every dining experience memorable.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, adventure seekers will find Butrint National Park, a site of significant historical and natural beauty, located just 18 kilometers away. For guests traveling by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 98 kilometers from the accommodation, making Saranda Apartment Brajan an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors.

In summary, Saranda Apartment Brajan emerges as a top-tier accommodation choice, blending the allure of beachfront living with the comforts of home, all while offering a strategic base from which to explore the enchanting surroundings of Sarandë.
