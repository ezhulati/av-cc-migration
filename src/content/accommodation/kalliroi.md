Each apartment at Kalliroi is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities to ensure a relaxing stay. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air-conditioned spaces, private bathrooms, and the entertainment of flat-screen TVs, all complemented by the charm of a personal balcony.

For those arriving from afar, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, situated 93 km away, makes Kalliroi an accessible haven for international travelers. With its strategic location, comprehensive facilities, and the promise of a serene getaway, Kalliroi stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for visitors to the picturesque region of Ksamil.
