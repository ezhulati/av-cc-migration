This apartment is equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable stay regardless of the season. The presence of an elevator and round-the-clock security underscores the property's commitment to guest convenience and safety. Moreover, guests can enjoy the luxury of staying connected with complimentary WiFi accessible throughout the premises.

The non-smoking apartment features a cozy bedroom, complemented by a living room that boasts a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for those evenings when guests prefer a quiet night in. The kitchenette is thoughtfully equipped with a fridge and stovetop, catering to the needs of those who enjoy preparing their meals. Additionally, the bathroom is well-appointed with slippers, and both towels and bed linen are provided, adding an extra layer of comfort to the stay.

Guests are treated to captivating city views, adding to the charm of this accommodation. While the Rock of Kavaje is located 40 km away, the historical House of Leaves is much closer, at just 2.7 km, making it an easy visit for those interested in exploring the local culture and history. For travelers flying in or out, the convenience is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, which is only 11 km away.

Studio Apartment Bela is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the vibrant life and beauty of Tirana, making it an excellent choice for both short visits and extended stays.
