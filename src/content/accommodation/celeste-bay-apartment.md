The apartment itself is a spacious haven featuring a bedroom designed for relaxation, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV offering satellite channels, and a kitchen that leaves no culinary desire unmet. With an oven, fridge, washing machine, stovetop, and an array of kitchenware at guests' disposal, the space is perfect for those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals. The sea views from the patio, complemented by outdoor furniture, make for an idyllic setting to unwind. Additional conveniences include the provision of bed linen, towels, and a laundry service, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

For those needing to pick up essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises caters to these needs with ease.

Situated 7.2 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and 7.4 km from the bustling Independence Square, Celeste Bay Apartment offers a serene escape with the convenience of being close to major attractions. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 156 km away, making it a manageable distance for a getaway to remember.

Celeste Bay Apartment stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury during their stay in Vlorë.
