The apartment complex is designed with privacy in mind, featuring a dedicated entrance for each apartment. It caters to families with its spacious family rooms and an outdoor fireplace that adds a touch of warmth to the evening gatherings.

Each apartment at Vila SUN 2E is a testament to thoughtful amenities and comfort. Guests can expect air-conditioned spaces, cozy seating areas, and flat-screen TVs equipped with streaming services for entertainment at any time. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, and private bathrooms with luxurious bathrobes, a bidet, and a hairdryer elevate the living experience. Select units boast terraces or balconies, offering serene lake views, while essentials like a desk and kettle meet the needs of both leisure and business travelers.

Families will appreciate the children's playground, ensuring the little ones are entertained, while the proximity to fishing spots and a lush garden invites guests to relax and engage with the outdoors.

Vila SUN 2E is ideally located near popular attractions, including Ksamil Beach 9, Bora Bora Beach, and Ksamil Beach 7, making it a convenient base for exploring the area's natural beauty. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making the apartment complex accessible for those coming from afar.

In summary, Vila SUN 2E offers a comprehensive package of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it a compelling choice for travelers to Ksamil.
