The apartment doesn't just offer space; it provides a home away from home with its two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a stay-in meal, and two modern bathrooms. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complementing the free WiFi that spans the entire property, keeping guests connected and entertained throughout their stay.

Security and convenience are paramount at Apartament Egzoni City Center, with full-day security and an elevator service ensuring peace of mind and ease of access for all guests. Additionally, the presence of a minimarket on-site adds an extra layer of convenience, catering to any last-minute needs or cravings.

Located 119 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment offers a seamless connection to and from the city, making it an ideal base for exploring Berat and beyond.
