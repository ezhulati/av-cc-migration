The apartments at Kondo Stays are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring balconies that showcase stunning mountain vistas, cozy seating areas for relaxation, and flat-screen TVs for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, toaster, fridge, stovetop, and kettle, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. The convenience of having bed linen, towels, and a private bathroom with a bidet and hairdryer adds to the comfort of the stay.

For those who need to pick up essentials or prefer to cook their meals, a minimarket on the premises offers added convenience. Active guests will appreciate the opportunity to play tennis at the property, and the availability of car hire services opens up the possibility to explore Vlorë and its surroundings at one's own pace.

Situated 1.2 km from the popular Vlore Beach and a bit further from Ri Beach, Kondo Stays - City Center Apartment positions itself as a strategic base for beach lovers and explorers alike. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, with the property providing a paid airport shuttle service for a smooth transition to and from the accommodation.

In summary, Kondo Stays - City Center Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to experience the best of Vlorë, combining the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
