A stone's throw away from cultural landmarks such as Tanners' Bridge, Clock Tower Tirana, and the historic Et'hem Bey Mosque, guests find themselves immersed in the rich tapestry of Tirana's history and culture. The intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is also within close proximity, offering a unique glimpse into Albania's past.

The apartment itself boasts a harmonious blend of comfort and functionality, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy the art of cooking, and a bathroom that ensures relaxation after a day of exploration. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding.

For those keen on exploring the arts, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania offers captivating performances nearby. History enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the National Museum of History Albania, while shoppers can delight in the Toptani Shopping Centre's diverse offerings. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, making Deluxe Apartment No 9 an ideal base for both short visits and extended stays in Tirana.
