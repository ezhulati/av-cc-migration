The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and privacy, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days. It boasts a layout that includes one separate bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a well-appointed bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it a perfect home away from home.

For those who appreciate the outdoors, the property offers a tranquil garden space where guests can unwind and soak in the serene atmosphere.

Its proximity to Golem Beach, just 500 meters away, and Qerret Beach, located 2.7 km from the premises, positions Shell Home as a prime location for guests eager to explore the coastal beauty of the area. Moreover, the convenience of being 40 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal, making Shell Home an excellent choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in Golem.
