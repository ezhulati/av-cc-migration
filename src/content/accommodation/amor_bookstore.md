Guests at Amor\_Bookstore are treated to the simple comforts of home, with towels and bed linen thoughtfully provided to enhance their stay. For those looking to explore beyond the local beaches, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offers breathtaking views and is located just 43 km away. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, is only 11 km from the bed and breakfast, making it an easy day trip for those inclined to explore the natural beauty of the area.

Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 33 km from Amor\_Bookstore, making it a hassle-free choice for both international and domestic travelers seeking a peaceful retreat in Durrës.
