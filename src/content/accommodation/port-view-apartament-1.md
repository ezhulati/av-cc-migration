Each apartment is a showcase of thoughtful amenities and home comforts. Guests are treated to breathtaking sea views from private balconies, ensuring a tranquil backdrop to their stay. The interiors boast flat-screen TVs for entertainment, well-equipped kitchens for those who love to cook, and private bathrooms complete with baths, slippers, and hair dryers for a touch of pampering. The presence of an oven, toaster, fridge, kettle, and stovetop in every unit makes self-catering a breeze.

Located 38 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 42 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Port View Apartment 1 serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of the area. For international travelers, the convenience of being just 32 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal.

Port View Apartment 1 stands out as a perfect fusion of location, comfort, and amenities, making it a highly recommended stay for those venturing to Durrës.
