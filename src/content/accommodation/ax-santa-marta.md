This one-bedroom apartment doesn't just boast a strategic location; it also offers a sanctuary of comfort with its air-conditioned spaces, ensuring a cool retreat from the bustling city life. Guests are treated to breathtaking views of both the mountain and the city, providing a serene backdrop to their stay. The apartment is a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy space for all guests.

A&X Santa Marta is equipped with modern amenities to cater to the needs of the contemporary traveler. With complimentary WiFi, guests can easily stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventures in Tirana. The apartment features a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a washing machine for convenience, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like them. For those looking for added ease, towels and linens are available upon request.

The apartment's location is a gateway to exploring Tirana's most sought-after attractions. Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania are all within easy reach, offering a diverse mix of historical, cultural, and retail experiences. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 15 km away, making A&X Santa Marta an accessible and convenient choice for both short and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
