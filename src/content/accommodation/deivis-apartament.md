The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are well catered to with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

For those traveling to or from Ioannina, the nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, located 97 km from the apartment, making it a convenient option for international and domestic travelers alike. Deivis Apartment presents an ideal lodging choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Sarandë while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed home away from home.
