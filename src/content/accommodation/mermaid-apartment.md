The apartment itself is a haven of tranquility and convenience. Guests are treated to a spacious layout that includes a bedroom with sea views, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with a dishwasher and an oven, making meal preparation a breeze. Additionally, the apartment features a bathroom complete with a bidet, while allergy-free accommodations ensure a comfortable stay for all guests. Towels and bed linen add a touch of home.

For those who need to pick up essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises caters to all basic needs. The Mermaid Apartment is ideally located for guests eager to explore, with sightseeing tours readily available and cycling opportunities in the surrounding area. For further exploration, car rental services can be arranged directly through the apartment.

Noteworthy attractions near the Mermaid Apartment include the pristine Durres Beach, the scenic Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, and the iconic Rock of Kavaje. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 35 km away, with the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service offered by the property.

The Mermaid Apartment stands out as a perfect blend of relaxation and exploration, making it an unforgettable stay for those visiting Durrës.
