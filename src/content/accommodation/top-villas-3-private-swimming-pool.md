Located a mere 26 km from the vibrant Skanderbeg Square, this non-smoking property offers a unique blend of seclusion and accessibility. The villa itself is a testament to spacious living and modern amenities. Guests are treated to air-conditioned interiors, including a bedroom that promises restful nights. The presence of a satellite flat-screen TV, dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen cater to all entertainment and culinary needs. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home, while the private entrance underscores the villa's commitment to guest privacy.

The outdoor garden invites guests to unwind in a natural setting, offering a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of daily life. For those looking to explore the surroundings, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is located 30 km away, offering an adventure into the heights, whereas the Rock of Kavaje, 24 km from the villa, presents a geological marvel worth visiting. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 19 km away, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures.

Top Villas 3 Private Swimming Pool stands out as a sanctuary for those seeking a blend of relaxation, privacy, and convenience in the heart of Rubjekë.
