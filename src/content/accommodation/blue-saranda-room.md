The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a separate bedroom for privacy, and a living room designed for relaxation. Adding to the convenience is the inclusion of a balcony, perfect for enjoying the serene evenings or the vibrant mornings of Sarandë.

For the adventurous at heart, the Butrint National Park lies 17 km away, offering a dive into the rich tapestry of history and nature. Meanwhile, the Ioannina Airport, located 97 km from the accommodation, facilitates ease of travel to and from the destination.

Blue Saranda Room stands out as an exemplary choice for those in pursuit of a memorable stay in Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
