This apartment distinguishes itself with its airy, well-appointed spaces, including a bedroom that promises restful nights, a living room designed for relaxation, and a balcony that offers captivating city views. The inclusion of modern amenities such as air conditioning, free WiFi accessible throughout the premises, and private parking enhances the stay experience. The kitchen, equipped with an oven and microwave, invites guests to prepare meals at their leisure, while the outdoor dining area provides a unique setting for meals under the sky.

The non-smoking property, a stone's throw away from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, ensures privacy and tranquility with its private entrance, making it an ideal retreat for those looking to unwind. Moreover, its proximity to notable attractions like the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves, enriches the cultural tapestry available to guests.

For those arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 14 km away, making Green Terrace Apartment 7 an accessible haven for both short-term visitors and long-term explorers looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Tirana.
