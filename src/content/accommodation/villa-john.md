The property prides itself on its round-the-clock front desk service and the convenience of an on-site minimarket, catering to all guest needs at any hour. The aparthotel's accommodations are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a modern bathroom equipped with both a bath and a shower, and the added luxury of slippers for that home-away-from-home feeling. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, in a completely non-smoking environment.

Dining at Villa John is an experience in itself, with the family-friendly restaurant welcoming guests for meals throughout the day, including dinner, lunch, and brunch. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beach or the comfort of the aparthotel's facilities, Villa John stands out as a top-tier choice for those visiting Borsh. For travelers flying in, Ioannina Airport is the nearest hub, located 123 km away, making Villa John an accessible retreat for both domestic and international visitors.
