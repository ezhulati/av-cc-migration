The property prides itself on facilitating a hassle-free stay, providing complimentary WiFi and private parking. For those looking to explore the area or needing assistance with airport transfers, Elite Apartments goes the extra mile with its car rental service and convenient transport arrangements.

Accommodations at Elite Apartments are designed with guest comfort in mind. Each apartment boasts a private balcony that opens up to tranquil garden views, a cozy seating area for relaxation, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, microwave, toaster, fridge, and stovetop, ensures that guests have everything they need to feel at home. Moreover, the inclusion of a kettle, along with provided bed linen and towels, adds to the thoughtful touches that make a stay here truly comfortable.

Strategically located, the property is not far from other attractions, with Dajti Ekspres Cable Car 47 km away and the Rock of Kavaje 41 km away, offering guests ample opportunities to explore the surrounding area. For international travelers, the convenience of being 28 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is an added advantage.

Elite Apartments stands as a testament to the perfect marriage of convenience, comfort, and natural beauty, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience Durrës in a memorable way.
