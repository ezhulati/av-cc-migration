The apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring a spacious layout that includes one bedroom, one bathroom, and a terrace that offers breathtaking mountain views. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen, making it a home away from home. Additionally, the unit's soundproofing ensures a peaceful stay, while the inclusion of a bidet adds a touch of luxury.

For dining, guests have the convenience of an on-site family-friendly restaurant that serves a variety of meals, including dinner, lunch, and high tea, catering to diverse palates and preferences.

Families will appreciate the apartment's child-friendly amenities, such as a children's playground and a picnic area, making it an ideal choice for those traveling with little ones.

Situated in proximity to notable attractions such as Skanderbeg Square, the National Museum of History Albania, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, Old Train Station Tirana offers guests the opportunity to explore the rich cultural heritage of Tirana with ease. The property is also conveniently located just 13 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, facilitating stress-free travel arrangements.

With its combination of strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and thoughtful touches, Old Train Station Tirana stands as a compelling option for those looking to experience the best of Tirana in a comfortable and welcoming setting.
