The property boasts a selection of apartments, each equipped with a balcony that presents captivating city views. Inside, guests are treated to a modern living space that includes a cable flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a well-equipped kitchen, ensuring all the comforts of home are at hand. The private bathrooms come complete with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, adding an extra layer of convenience. For those chillier evenings, the in-room fireplace provides a cozy retreat.

Beyond the confines of the apartments, guests can enjoy the seasonal outdoor swimming pool, set amidst a well-maintained garden, offering a perfect oasis for relaxation. The provision of free private parking and complimentary WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests' needs are well catered to, from the moment of arrival.

For those looking to explore the local area, Apartments Mario is ideally situated. Paradise Beach is a mere 400 meters away, while Lori Beach is within a 600-meter distance, inviting guests to explore the natural beauty of the surroundings. Additionally, the property is located 92 km from Corfu International Airport, making it easily accessible for international travelers.

The on-site bar and the availability of sightseeing tours further enhance the guest experience, offering leisure and adventure in equal measure. Families visiting with little ones will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a baby safety gate, ensuring a worry-free stay for parents.

In summary, Apartments Mario presents a compelling option for those in search of a comfortable, convenient, and well-appointed base to explore the enchanting area of Ksamil.
