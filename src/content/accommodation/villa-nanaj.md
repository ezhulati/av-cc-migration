Each apartment at Villa Nanaj is designed with guest privacy and comfort in mind, featuring a private entrance that ensures a secluded and personalized stay. The interiors are thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, a cable flat-screen TV, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. The inclusion of a well-fitted kitchenette and a private bathroom stocked with free toiletries adds a touch of convenience, allowing guests to feel fully at ease.

The charm of Villa Nanaj is further enhanced by its outdoor spaces. Balconies and patios in each unit offer not just city views but also an outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals in the open air or simply soaking in the ambiance of Ksamil.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Butrint National Park is located just 2.6 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich history and natural beauty. Additionally, the property's proximity to Corfu International Airport, 92 km away, makes it a practical choice for international travelers.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and thoughtful design, Villa Nanaj stands out as a top accommodation choice for those visiting Ksamil, whether for leisure or exploration.
