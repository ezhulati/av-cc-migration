This apartment doesn't just boast a prime location; it offers a spacious living environment complete with a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and the convenience of a fully equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher and fridge. The inclusion of two bathrooms, each with a walk-in shower, enhances the living experience, ensuring ample space and privacy for all guests. Modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, a washing machine, and a hairdryer are thoughtfully provided, catering to the needs of the contemporary traveler. Moreover, the apartment is designed to be non-smoking and soundproof, promising a peaceful and comfortable stay.

For those arriving by car, the added perk of complimentary on-site parking, along with free WiFi, ensures that every aspect of your stay is as hassle-free as possible. And for the explorers eager to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of Albania, Butrint National Park awaits just 15 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity.

Located 95 km from Ioannina Airport, the Lovely 2 Bedroom Apartment stands as a testament to the beauty and allure of Sarandë, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a memorable stay by the sea.
