The accommodations at Endy Apartments are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom, ensuring a stay that combines convenience with luxury. The inclusion of a desk, bed linen, towels, and a fridge adds to the homely feel, while the balcony offers breathtaking sea views, allowing guests to soak in the beauty of their surroundings.

Adding to the appeal is the property's commitment to convenience, highlighted by the provision of complimentary private parking for those arriving by car. The lush garden setting provides a tranquil backdrop, perfect for unwinding after a day of beach exploration or cultural excursions.

For travelers venturing from afar, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 165 kilometers away, makes Endy Apartments an accessible haven for both international and domestic visitors. This blend of comfort, location, and thoughtful amenities positions Endy Apartments as a standout option for those seeking a memorable stay in Orikum.
