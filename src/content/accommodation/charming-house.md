This air-conditioned apartment boasts a welcoming balcony, providing a perfect setting for morning coffees or evening relaxation. Inside, guests will find a cozy living room, a bedroom promising restful nights, and a well-equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort to the stay.

For those drawn to spiritual sites, the Monastery Saint Naum is merely 8.4 km away, while the Saint Nicholas Church stands 19 km from the doorstep. Accessibility is also a highlight, with Ohrid Airport located 47 km away, making Charming House a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a peaceful escape in the heart of Pogradec.
