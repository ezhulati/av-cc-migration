The apartment boasts a well-appointed layout featuring two bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge, and a cozy seating area for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, and the convenience of a bathroom with a bidet adds to the comfort of the stay. One of the highlights of Ales' Apartment is the balcony, offering guests breathtaking sea views complemented by outdoor furniture for an enhanced experience. Additionally, the apartment provides captivating pool views, adding another layer of tranquility to the ambiance.

For those looking to unwind, the on-site bar serves as a perfect spot to relax and socialize. The convenience of a minimarket on the premises ensures that guests' needs are promptly met, making for a hassle-free stay.

Situated 46 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just 600 metres from the Rock of Kavaje, Ales' Apartment is ideally located for guests eager to explore the surrounding attractions. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 36 km away, making the apartment a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Ales' Apartment stands out as a delightful retreat for those in pursuit of a peaceful beachfront holiday, complete with all the comforts of home and easy access to local landmarks.
