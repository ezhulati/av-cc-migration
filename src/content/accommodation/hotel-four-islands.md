Guest accommodations at Hotel Four Islands are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, and a balcony offering captivating city views. The inclusion of a private bathroom, flat-screen TV, and essential linens and towels ensures a comfortable stay. For those desiring a touch more independence, select rooms boast a kitchenette complete with a minibar and stovetop, alongside a convenient fridge.

Morning at Hotel Four Islands begins with a delightful culinary journey. Guests can choose from a variety of breakfast options, including a buffet, continental, and vegetarian selections, catering to diverse tastes and dietary requirements.

The hotel's location is ideal for beach enthusiasts and explorers alike. Bora Bora Beach is a mere 800 meters away, while the lesser-known gem, Ksamil Beach 7, is also within easy reach. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Hotel Four Islands an accessible haven for those looking to experience the beauty and tranquility of Ksamil.
