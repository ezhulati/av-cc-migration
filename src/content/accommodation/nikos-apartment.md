The property boasts a well-appointed apartment featuring a single bedroom, a balcony for enjoying the gentle Mediterranean breeze, and two bathrooms, providing ample space and privacy for guests. The kitchen comes fully equipped, catering to those who prefer the comforts of home-cooked meals while on vacation. For added convenience, complimentary WiFi envelops the premises, allowing guests to stay connected with the world or plan their next adventure in Vlorë.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. Moreover, Nikos Apartment is committed to providing a smoke-free environment, promoting a clean and healthy atmosphere for all guests.

Located a mere 2.5 km from the historic Independence Square and close to the iconic Kuzum Baba viewpoint, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the rich cultural heritage of Vlorë. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is 150 km away, making Nikos Apartment an ideal base for both local exploration and broader adventures in the region.

In summary, Nikos Apartment offers a blend of convenience, comfort, and cleanliness, making it an excellent choice for travelers to Vlorë seeking a memorable stay.
