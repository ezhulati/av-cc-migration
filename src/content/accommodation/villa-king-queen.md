The interior of Villa KING & QUEEN is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of its visitors. The home boasts two elegantly appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, providing ample space and privacy. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area and oven, invites guests to prepare and enjoy their meals with a view. The living room, a cozy retreat, is perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

For those who appreciate the outdoors, the property's garden offers a tranquil setting to relax. The holiday home's strategic location also places guests within easy reach of other popular beaches, including the Beach at Government Villas and Liro Beach, further enriching the vacation experience.

Situated 155 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Villa KING & QUEEN stands out as an exceptional choice for those in pursuit of comfort, privacy, and scenic beauty during their stay in Vlorë.
