Each air-conditioned unit in Summer Dreams is thoughtfully equipped with essentials such as a wardrobe, kettle, oven, fridge, and safety deposit box, ensuring a stay that combines the ease of home with the luxury of vacation. Entertainment is guaranteed with a flat-screen TV, while the private bathroom with a shower adds an extra layer of privacy. Select apartments elevate the experience further, boasting terraces or patios that offer serene views of the surrounding mountains or lush gardens. Complimentary bed linen and towels underscore the complex's commitment to comfort.

The option to prepare meals in a well-appointed kitchen presents a convenient alternative to dining out, perfect for those evenings when guests prefer the intimacy of a home-cooked meal. The property's garden invites moments of relaxation and tranquility, allowing guests to unwind in a serene outdoor setting.

Adventure seekers and culture enthusiasts will find the location of Summer Dreams exceptionally convenient, with Bora Bora Beach just 1.1 km away and the historic Butrint National Park a short 2.6 km journey. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, located 92 km from the property, making Summer Dreams an accessible haven for both local and overseas visitors.
