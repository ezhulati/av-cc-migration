Connectivity is a breeze at Vila Mihasi, thanks to the complimentary WiFi available throughout the property. Social gatherings are elevated with the hotel's own bar and barbecue facilities, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. For those seeking entertainment, the hotel's dedicated staff are on hand to ensure an engaging stay, complemented by the convenience of an on-site ATM.

Comfort meets functionality in each guest room, which is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of modernity. The private bathrooms, complete with fresh bed linen and towels, ensure a private and comfortable experience. Additionally, each room features a terrace, offering captivating views of the cityscape, while a fridge in every room adds an extra layer of convenience.

Guests at Vila Mihasi can start their day with a choice of continental or vegetarian breakfast options, catering to diverse dietary preferences. The hotel's prime location means that the stunning Sunset Beach is also less than a kilometer away, with Coco Beach just a leisurely 13-minute walk from the property. For those looking to explore beyond Ksamil, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Vila Mihasi an ideal base for both local and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in this picturesque region.
