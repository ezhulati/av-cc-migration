The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, boasting a balcony that offers breathtaking sea views. Guests can enjoy the comfort of a well-appointed living room, a bedroom that promises restful nights, and the convenience of a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a fridge. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, a bathroom with a walk-in shower, and the provision of towels and bed linen further enhance the living experience. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's dedication to privacy and exclusivity.

For those intrigued by the allure of nature and history, the Butrint National Park, a treasure trove of ancient ruins set amidst lush landscapes, is merely 15 km away. Additionally, the property facilitates travel arrangements with a paid airport shuttle service to Ioannina Airport, located 95 km from the apartment, ensuring a hassle-free connection for international and domestic travelers alike. Moon Sea View Apartment not only promises a memorable stay in Sarandë but also serves as a gateway to exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of the region.
