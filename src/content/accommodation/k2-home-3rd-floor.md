This third-floor apartment doesn't just offer a strategic location; it provides guests with a spacious, air-conditioned haven complete with a balcony to enjoy the city's ambiance. The inclusion of free WiFi and the option for private parking (available for an additional charge) adds to the convenience factor, making it an attractive option for both short and long stays.

Accommodation at K2 HOME is designed with comfort in mind. The apartment boasts a well-equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and a fridge, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals. The living space is complemented by a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The bathroom, fitted with a bidet, ensures a refreshing start or end to the day. Notably, the entire apartment is a non-smoking environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests.

For those looking to venture further, the Rock of Kavaje is 39 km away, offering a unique day trip opportunity. Meanwhile, the House of Leaves museum is just 4.4 km from the apartment, presenting a fascinating glimpse into the country's history. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, located 10 km from K2 HOME - 3rd floor, facilitating easy arrivals and departures.

In summary, K2 HOME - 3rd floor stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for visitors to Tirana, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and accessibility to the city's key attractions.
