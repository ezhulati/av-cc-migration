Offering a spacious, air-conditioned haven with the added luxury of a balcony, Comfy Condo doesn't skimp on amenities. Guests can enjoy the freedom of a fully equipped kitchen, making it an ideal choice for those who prefer the comforts of home-cooked meals while traveling. The inclusion of two bathrooms adds an extra layer of convenience, particularly for groups or families. Entertainment needs are well catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring relaxation is on the agenda after a day of exploration.

The provision of complimentary WiFi and private parking is a testament to the property's commitment to guest convenience, allowing for seamless connectivity and ease of travel. Situated a comfortable distance from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the picturesque Rock of Kavaje, Comfy Condo serves as a perfect base for those keen to delve into Albania's cultural and natural landscapes.

Notably, the condo is in close proximity to several of Tirana's key attractions, including the Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana. This proximity ensures that guests have a plethora of options for entertainment, shopping, and cultural enrichment within easy reach.

For travelers flying into Tirana, the convenience is further amplified by the property's location, just 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

In summary, Comfy Condo offers a compelling mix of strategic location, thoughtful amenities, and the comforts of modern living, making it an excellent choice for travelers seeking a hassle-free stay in Tirana.
