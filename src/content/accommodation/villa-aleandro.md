The villa itself is a masterpiece of design and functionality, featuring three elegantly appointed bedrooms, two modern bathrooms, and an array of amenities including bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to every culinary need, while the terrace offers breathtaking sea views, perfect for dining al fresco in the outdoor dining area amidst the serene garden views. The added benefits of a private entrance and soundproofing ensure an undisturbed and intimate experience for guests.

For those who enjoy outdoor activities, Villa Aleandro does not disappoint, providing a picnic area and a barbecue for a delightful outdoor experience. Its prime location places guests within easy reach of popular attractions such as Paradise Beach, Ksamil Beach 9, and Lori Beach, making it an ideal base for exploring the beauty of the surrounding area. Furthermore, for guests arriving from afar, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service to Corfu International Airport, located 92 km away, is available.

In summary, Villa Aleandro stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and charm of Ksamil, offering a perfect blend of luxury, privacy, and convenience.
