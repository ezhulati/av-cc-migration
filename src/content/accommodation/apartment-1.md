Strategically located, guests find themselves a mere 2.1 km from the inviting sands of Golem Beach, with the historic Skanderbeg Square and the scenic Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within 41 and 45 km respectively. The intriguing Rock of Kavaje is conveniently situated just 1 km away, adding a touch of exploration to the stay, while the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is accessible at 42 km, offering a glimpse into the country's past.

Each air-conditioned unit within the property is designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a balcony to enjoy the fresh air and perhaps a morning coffee or an evening glass of wine.

For those who enjoy a good drink without the need to venture far, an on-site bar caters to guests' preferences, providing a cozy spot to unwind after a day of beach exploration or sightseeing.

Notably, the Apartment 1 is also conveniently located 11 km from the historical Durres Amphitheatre, offering a cultural excursion for those interested in the rich history of the area. The House of Leaves stands 41 km away, presenting another intriguing visit for history enthusiasts. For ease of travel, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a manageable 36 km from the accommodation, making Apartment 1 an ideal base for both leisure and exploration in Durrës.
