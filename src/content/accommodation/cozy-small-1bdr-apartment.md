This inviting apartment is distinguished by its spacious layout, featuring a welcoming balcony that offers a tranquil space for guests to unwind. The interior boasts a tastefully furnished living room, a cozy bedroom designed for restful nights, and a well-equipped kitchen that caters to the culinary needs of its guests. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complementing the homely ambiance of the apartment. Moreover, guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi throughout their stay, ensuring they remain connected and entertained.

For those keen on exploring the local culture, the apartment is conveniently located near Skanderbeg Square, the Clock Tower of Tirana, and the historic Et'hem Bey Mosque, each within a short distance. The proximity to these landmarks offers guests an unparalleled opportunity to immerse themselves in the city's rich heritage.

Ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay, the apartment is a strictly non-smoking environment, catering to the well-being of all guests. For travelers arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 12 km away, making the Cozy Small 1Bdr Apartment an accessible haven for both short and extended stays in the heart of Tirana.
