Guests are welcomed into a generously sized apartment that boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-cooked meal. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is always at hand. The apartment's design prioritizes guest comfort, featuring air conditioning throughout and offering the luxury of a private balcony for moments of relaxation.

For those arriving by car, the convenience of complimentary private parking is a notable perk, alongside the availability of free WiFi, ensuring that both connectivity and convenience are guaranteed. It's also worth noting that Ilirjan Apartment is a smoke-free environment, catering to those who prioritize wellness and clean living spaces.

Notably, the apartment is within manageable distance from other attractions and essentials. The Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, is 40 km away, while the House of Leaves museum is just 6.2 km from the property, offering a glimpse into the country's history. For guests' travel needs, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 11 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

Ilirjan Apartment stands as a testament to the blend of comfort, convenience, and thoughtful amenities, making it an excellent choice for travelers looking to explore Tirana's charms.
