Boasting a generous layout, the apartment features three well-appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, complete with bed linen and towels for a seamless stay. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for relaxing evenings in. The dining area and a fully-equipped kitchen offer the freedom to prepare and enjoy meals in a homely setting. Perhaps the most captivating aspect is the terrace, offering breathtaking mountain views, where guests can savor their meals in an outdoor dining space with a picturesque backdrop of the lake.

Designed with health and comfort in mind, the apartment is both allergy-free and strictly non-smoking, ensuring a pleasant environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the comforts of Luis Penthouse, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 119 km away, making it an accessible option for international travelers.
