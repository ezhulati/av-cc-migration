Guests at Delight Apartments are treated to a seamless stay, thanks to amenities such as complimentary WiFi and on-site private parking. The presence of an elevator and currency exchange services on the premises further enhances the guest experience, making every stay as effortless as it is enjoyable.

Accommodations at Delight Apartments are designed with guest comfort in mind. Each unit boasts a balcony that invites guests to bask in the breathtaking surroundings. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, and the dining area allow for home-cooked meals away from home. Entertainment is readily available through the flat-screen TV in each unit, while the air conditioning ensures a comfortable environment regardless of the weather. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with a bidet and a hair dryer, and for added convenience, bed linen and towels are provided in all units. Select apartments further elevate the experience with the inclusion of a terrace.

For those looking to explore the area at their own pace, a car rental service is conveniently available directly from the apartment complex.

Delight Apartments is ideally located for both beach enthusiasts and culture seekers. Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach is a mere 2.2 km away, offering another picturesque spot for relaxation and leisure. Meanwhile, the historic Skanderbeg Square in the heart of the capital is 46 km away, presenting an opportunity for a memorable day trip. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is situated 41 km from the accommodation, making Delight Apartments an accessible and attractive option for both domestic and international visitors.
