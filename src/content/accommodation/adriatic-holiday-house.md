A mere 1.2 kilometers from the inviting sands of Qerret Beach and slightly further from the bustling Golem Beach, this property positions itself as an ideal retreat for beach enthusiasts and those looking to escape the city's clamor. The apartment itself is a spacious haven featuring a balcony that invites the sea breeze right into the living quarters. It houses a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a kitchen equipped with modern amenities including an oven and a fridge, ensuring guests feel at home. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

For those intrigued by local culture and history, Skanderbeg Square is within a manageable distance, located 48 kilometers away, offering a deep dive into the area's heritage. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, a mere 6.2 kilometers from the residence, presents a unique natural attraction. Accessibility is hardly an issue, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 42 kilometers away, making Adriatic Holiday House a convenient and compelling choice for both domestic and international travelers.

In essence, Adriatic Holiday House emerges as a standout accommodation, promising a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to natural beauty, making it a must-visit for those charting their next coastal adventure.
