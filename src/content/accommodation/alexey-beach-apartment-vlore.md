Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, it features a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge. The convenience of a private bathroom with a shower and a private entrance adds to the sense of exclusivity.

For those looking to explore the local area, Alexey Beach Apartment Vlore does not disappoint.
