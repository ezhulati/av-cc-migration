The apartment's thoughtful design includes a private entrance, enhancing the sense of privacy and exclusivity. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of luxury, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. For those looking to explore Vlorë and its surroundings, Apartment Lion Fountain 14 facilitates this adventure by offering both bicycle and car rental services, making it effortless to venture beyond the immediate beachfront.

Located conveniently close to Vlore Beach and within easy reach of the historic Kuzum Baba viewpoint, the apartment serves as an ideal base for guests eager to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty. Independence Square, a notable landmark, lies just 8.4 km away, inviting guests to delve into the rich history of the area.

For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 157 km away, ensuring that Apartment Lion Fountain 14 remains accessible for both domestic and international guests seeking a serene and picturesque escape on the Albanian coast.
