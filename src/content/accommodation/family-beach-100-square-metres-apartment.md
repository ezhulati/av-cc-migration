This generously sized apartment boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available, ensuring a comfortable stay.

Situated 1.6 km from the inviting Golem Beach and 17 km from the Durres Amphitheatre, the apartment is perfectly positioned for guests to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the area. The House of Leaves museum, a testament to the country's history, is also within a manageable distance of 47 km. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 42 km away, making Family Beach 100 Square Metres Apartment an accessible and appealing option for both domestic and international travelers.
