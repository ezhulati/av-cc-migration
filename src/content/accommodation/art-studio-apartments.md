Guests can enjoy the luxury of air-conditioned accommodations, each equipped with a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for home-cooked meals, and a terrace to soak in the surroundings. Whether visiting with family or on a solo adventure, the apartment's family rooms cater to all configurations of travelers.

Strategically located, Art Studio Apartments is a mere 500 meters from the bustling Durres Beach and 2.4 km from the picturesque Golem Beach, offering a variety of coastal experiences. For those traveling to and from the area, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 36 km away, making Art Studio Apartments an ideal base for exploring the beauty of Durrës and beyond.
