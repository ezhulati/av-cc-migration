The accommodations at Konak I Skanderbeg Square are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring kitchenettes for a home-like experience. The availability of free WiFi across the premises ensures that guests stay connected, whether for leisure or work. Selected rooms elevate the experience further with the addition of balconies, offering a private outdoor space to unwind. All units boast air conditioning and flat-screen TVs, ensuring a stay marked by relaxation and entertainment.

Beyond the doors of Konak I Skanderbeg Square, guests can explore additional points of interest, including the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, National Museum of History Albania, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, making it an ideal base for cultural aficionados. For those traveling to and from Tirana, the convenience is underscored by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 14 km away, ensuring smooth transitions to and from the city.

Konak I Skanderbeg Square not only offers a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the rich history and vibrant culture of Tirana, making it a compelling choice for discerning travelers.
