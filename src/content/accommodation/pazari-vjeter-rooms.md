Each room at Pazari Vjeter Rooms is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a cozy seating area for relaxation and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment. The presence of air conditioning and a desk in every room underscores the hotel's commitment to providing a seamless experience, whether for leisure or business.

For added convenience, the hotel boasts a 24-hour front desk and room service, ensuring all needs are met promptly, any time of the day or night. Located 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, Pazari Vjeter Rooms stands out as an accessible retreat for those looking to immerse themselves in the charm and culture of Korçë.
