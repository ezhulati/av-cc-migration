The apartment boasts a well-appointed balcony that offers breathtaking sea views, creating the perfect backdrop for a morning coffee or an evening unwind. Inside, guests will find a cozy bedroom, a spacious living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a stovetop - ideal for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of convenience and comfort to the stay. Moreover, the apartment offers captivating city views, adding another layer of charm to the experience.

For daily essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises caters to guests' needs without the need to venture far. Those looking to relax and soak in the tranquil atmosphere will find solace in the property's garden, a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle.

Situated a mere 1.9 km from Ri Beach and 2.6 km from Independence Square, Appartamento Laura con vista Lungomare serves as an ideal base for exploring the local area's attractions. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km away, making the apartment a convenient choice for both domestic and overseas visitors seeking a memorable stay in Vlorë.
