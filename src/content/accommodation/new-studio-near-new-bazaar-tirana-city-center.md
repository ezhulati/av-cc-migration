Boasting a strategic position, the property is merely 4.1 km from the scenic Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and a brief 1.6 km journey from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha. Guests will find themselves in close proximity to the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the Clock Tower Tirana, and the Et'hem Bey Mosque, with the famed Skanderbeg Square just 700 meters away.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning for those warm Tirana days. It features a well-appointed bedroom, a kitchen complete with a toaster and fridge, and a cozy seating area. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, and the bathroom comes fitted with a bidet, ensuring a comfortable stay. Towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience. Additionally, guests will appreciate the added privacy afforded by the apartment's private entrance.

For those looking to explore, the apartment is conveniently located near Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, making it easy to immerse oneself in the local culture. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 16 km away, New Studio near New Bazaar - Tirana City Center is an ideal choice for both short visits and longer stays, promising an unforgettable experience in the heart of Tirana.
