The property is distinguished by its family-friendly atmosphere, complete with a picnic area for leisurely outdoor dining. Each apartment is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring patios with tranquil garden views, satellite flat-screen TVs for entertainment, and well-equipped kitchens for those who prefer home-cooked meals. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, outfitted with bidets, plush bathrobes, and hair dryers, ensuring a comfortable stay. Safety is also a priority, with units equipped with deposit boxes for peace of mind.

For guests who delight in the charm of al fresco dining, the option to grill on the barbecue is available, adding a touch of home to the vacation experience. Select units elevate the stay further with terraces and captivating sea views, inviting guests to unwind in the beauty of their surroundings.

Situated 4.1 km from the historical Kuzum Baba and 150 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Villas Apartments SHAHAJ facilitates exploration and travel with its paid airport shuttle service. This property stands out as a perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and natural beauty, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Vlorë.
