The interior of Elite Apartment is a blend of comfort and modernity, featuring a generously sized bedroom, a well-appointed bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living space that boasts a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment at any time of the day. The dining area seamlessly connects to a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests to prepare meals with ease. Perhaps one of the apartment's most charming aspects is its balcony, offering captivating city views and an outdoor dining area perfect for enjoying the local ambiance.

For those seeking a blend of adventure and relaxation, the apartment's location does not disappoint. It is situated a manageable 41 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering an opportunity for an unforgettable aerial view of the area, and just 7.9 km from the Rock of Kavaje, a landmark worth exploring. Convenience is further underscored by its proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 31 km away, making Elite Apartment an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in Durrës.
