Accommodations at Area Hotel are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a stay that combines both productivity and relaxation. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with complimentary toiletries, alongside the provision of fresh bed linen and towels, adds a touch of luxury to the guest experience. Select rooms further elevate the stay with the addition of a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to soak in the surroundings.

Mornings at Area Hotel begin on a high note with a continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. Guests are invited to bask in the sunshine on the hotel's sun terrace, a perfect spot for leisurely afternoons.

The hotel's prime location places guests within easy reach of local attractions, including the serene Sunset Beach, the vibrant Coco Beach, and the exotic Bora Bora Beach, making it an ideal base for those keen to explore the area's natural beauty. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 93 km away, with the hotel offering a paid airport shuttle service for seamless travel arrangements.

Area Hotel emerges as a top choice for discerning travelers, promising a blend of convenience, comfort, and access to the breathtaking landscapes of Ksamil.
