The interior of the apartment does not disappoint, featuring a generously sized bedroom, a well-appointed bathroom, and the inclusion of essential linens and towels. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area poised for memorable meals, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to every culinary need. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the terrace, offering breathtaking sea views, complemented by an outdoor dining area that allows guests to dine against a backdrop of mountain vistas, adding an element of tranquility to the dining experience. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores the apartment's commitment to guest privacy.

Situated just a stone's throw away from Vjetër Beach and a short drive from Vlore Beach, the location of Duplex Apartment Front Sea ensures that guests are never far from the soothing embrace of the sea. For those traveling from afar, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 151 km away, makes this apartment a convenient choice for both short and extended stays. Duplex Apartment Front Sea stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Vlorë.
