The highlight of the hotel is undoubtedly its outdoor swimming pool, complete with a pool bar and an exhilarating water slide, offering both relaxation and fun for all ages. For families, the addition of a kids' pool guarantees that younger guests are not left out of the aquatic enjoyment.

Accommodations at Absolute Hotel Ksamil are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring flat-screen TVs with satellite channels for entertainment in every room. The convenience of a private bathroom equipped with a bath and a hair dryer, alongside the benefit of complimentary WiFi, ensures that guests' needs are met with the utmost attention to detail. Moreover, the option of soundproof units speaks to the hotel's commitment to providing a serene and undisturbed retreat.

An on-site bar adds to the allure, offering a cozy spot for guests to unwind after a day of exploration or sunbathing.

The hotel's location is ideal for beach enthusiasts, with Paradise Beach just 600 meters away and Lori Beach a short 700 meters distance, making it easy for guests to experience the area's renowned coastal beauty. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making Absolute Hotel Ksamil a convenient base for both local and international travelers.

In summary, Absolute Hotel Ksamil stands as a testament to thoughtful hospitality, where every detail is curated to enhance the guest experience. From its strategic location to its comprehensive amenities, this hotel promises a stay that is both enjoyable and unforgettable.
