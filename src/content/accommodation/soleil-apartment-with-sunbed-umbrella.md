Positioned just a short distance from the heart of Vlorë, the apartment is a mere 3.2 km from the historic Independence Square and a comfortable 2.5 km journey to the popular Vlore Beach. For those seeking a smoke-free environment, Soleil Apartment ensures a clean and fresh setting, further enhanced by the ease of elevator access.

Guests are treated to a generously sized apartment featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a modern bathroom stocked with essential linens and towels, and the added luxury of a bidet and a dressing room for ultimate comfort. The living space is designed for relaxation and entertainment, equipped with a flat-screen TV offering streaming services, a cozy dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen for those moments when dining in feels just right. The highlight, however, is the private balcony, where breathtaking sea views await, promising serene mornings and enchanting evenings under the Vlorë sky.

Air-conditioned for your comfort, the apartment ensures a pleasant stay regardless of the season. For those interested in exploring, the historic Kuzum Baba is just 3.7 km away, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history.

For international travelers, the journey to Soleil Apartment is made easy with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 151 km away, ensuring that this exquisite beachfront retreat is within reach for a memorable getaway in Vlorë.
