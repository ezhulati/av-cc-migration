Designed with accessibility in mind, Hotel Alexis ensures a hassle-free stay for all guests, providing amenities such as complimentary WiFi, ample private parking, and specially adapted facilities for disabled guests. Each apartment within the complex is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a terrace that opens up to majestic mountain views. The promise of a home away from home is fulfilled with a fully equipped kitchen, an al fresco dining area, and a cozy bathroom equipped with a shower, complemented by the provision of fresh bed linen and towels.

The allure of Hotel Alexis is further enhanced by its well-maintained garden, complete with a barbecue area, inviting guests to enjoy leisurely outdoor gatherings. For those with an adventurous spirit, the surrounding area offers excellent hiking trails.

Situated in close proximity to a selection of coveted beaches, including Paradise Beach, Lori Beach, and Bora Bora Beach, Hotel Alexis serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty of the region. Moreover, the convenience of being located 92 km from Corfu International Airport adds to its appeal for international travelers seeking a tranquil escape by the sea.
