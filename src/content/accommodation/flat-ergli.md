The accommodation boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a bathroom equipped with all essentials, and a living space that includes a flat-screen TV with a variety of cable channels. The dining area seamlessly connects to a fully equipped kitchen, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony, offering breathtaking sea views that promise to enchant every guest.

Adding to the appeal, Flat Ergli is equipped with an escalator, enhancing accessibility and convenience for all guests. The outdoor space is thoughtfully designed with a garden and barbecue facilities, perfect for leisurely afternoons. Families will particularly appreciate the children's playground, ensuring entertainment for the little ones.

Located a mere 55 km from Tirana International Airport Mother Teresa, Flat Ergli positions itself as an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers. This apartment stands out as a delightful haven for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and scenic beauty.
