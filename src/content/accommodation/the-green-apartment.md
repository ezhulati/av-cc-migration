The Green Apartment presents a cozy retreat with its air-conditioned one-bedroom layout, designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. Guests will find themselves at home with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen complete with a fridge and kitchenware for those who love to cook, and the convenience of a washing machine. The private bathroom comes with a shower, ensuring privacy and comfort. Adding to the sense of exclusivity is the apartment's private entrance, allowing guests to come and go as they please.

Strategically positioned, the apartment is a gateway to exploring the rich history and culture of the area. The former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre are within a manageable distance, offering a glimpse into Albania's storied past. For those looking to venture further, Skanderbeg Square and the Rock of Kavaje are also accessible, promising an adventure for the curious traveler. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 42 km away, making The Green Apartment an ideal choice for both domestic and international visitors seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and exploration.
