The accommodation, an air-conditioned apartment, is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It features a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen with essentials like a fridge and coffee machine, making it perfect for those who prefer a home-cooked meal even while on vacation. The inclusion of a sleek bathroom equipped with a shower and slippers adds a touch of comfort. Entertainment needs are well catered to with a flat-screen TV that comes with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Moreover, the apartment offers captivating city views, allowing guests to soak in the urban landscape of Tirana.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures that essentials are always within reach.

Urban Chill Tirana is ideally situated for guests eager to explore the city's cultural offerings, with the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the House of Leaves, and the Clock Tower Tirana all within close proximity. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, with the property providing a paid shuttle service for effortless airport transfers.

In summary, Urban Chill Tirana stands out as a sophisticated retreat for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury in the vibrant city of Tirana.
