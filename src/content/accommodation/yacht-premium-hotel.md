Guests of Yacht Premium Hotel are treated to the pinnacle of comfort in air-conditioned rooms, each thoughtfully equipped with a desk, coffee machine, minibar, and safety deposit box for personal security. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV and a private balcony, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The private bathrooms, complete with showers, add a touch of privacy and convenience, while select rooms offer captivating city views, enhancing the experience. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of a kettle in all rooms, alongside plush bed linen and towels, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and satisfying.

The hotel's strategic location places guests at the heart of Sarandë's most sought-after attractions, including Saranda City Beach, La Petite Beach, and the picturesque Saranda Bay Beach, making it an ideal choice for travelers keen on exploring the local sights and sounds. For those venturing further, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km away, making Yacht Premium Hotel a convenient base for both local and international travelers.

With its commitment to exceptional service, evidenced by the availability of room service, a 24-hour front desk, complimentary WiFi, concierge service, and luggage storage space, Yacht Premium Hotel ensures a seamless and memorable stay for all guests.
