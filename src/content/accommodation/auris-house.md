Boasting a spacious balcony that presents breathtaking sea views, the apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It features a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and an equipped kitchen that includes a toaster and a fridge, allowing guests to prepare their meals with ease. The inclusion of a walk-in shower in the bathroom adds a touch of luxury to the overall experience.

One of the highlights of Auri's House is the added layer of privacy afforded by its private entrance, ensuring a secluded and intimate stay for guests. The entire property is serviced by free WiFi, ensuring that guests remain connected throughout their stay.

For those looking to explore local dining options, a variety of restaurants and cafes are conveniently located near the apartment, offering a taste of the local cuisine.

Situated 3.3 km from the historical Kuzum Baba and 3.5 km from the vibrant Independence Square, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the rich cultural heritage of Vlorë. For travelers arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making Auri's House an accessible retreat for both domestic and international visitors.

In summary, Auri's House provides a delightful escape for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and culture of Vlorë, offering all the comforts of home in a picturesque seaside setting.
