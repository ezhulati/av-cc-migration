Accommodations at Hotel Ceko feature air-conditioned rooms, each equipped with a balcony that offers a personal outdoor space for guests to enjoy. The inclusion of cable TV and a fridge in every room adds a touch of home comfort, while the private bathrooms equipped with showers ensure privacy and convenience.

Dining at the hotel is a delightful experience, thanks to the on-site a la carte restaurant complemented by a terrace for al fresco dining. Guests can also take advantage of the free WiFi and parking, making their stay hassle-free from arrival to departure.

For those looking to unwind, the hotel's bar presents the perfect setting for relaxing evenings after a day of exploration. The 24-hour front desk ensures that guests' needs are attended to at any hour, adding to the seamless service experience.

Situated just 15 km from the UNESCO-protected ancient city of Butrint and the Butrint National Park, Hotel Ceko serves as an ideal base for cultural excursions. Moreover, the proximity to Corfu Airport, accessible via ferry from the Port of Sarandë, approximately 40 km away, makes it a convenient choice for international travelers.

In summary, Hotel Ceko offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural proximity, making it a standout option for those visiting Sarandë.
