The villa itself is a spacious retreat, boasting six well-appointed bedrooms, two cozy living rooms, and a balcony that offers stunning pool views. It's equipped with a flat-screen TV, a kitchen that includes both an oven and a microwave, and two bathrooms featuring a bidet, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The outdoor dining area and the warmth of an indoor fireplace provide perfect settings for relaxation, regardless of the season.

Security is paramount at Villa Shefit, with full-day security and a private entrance ensuring guests' peace of mind. The villa is also designed with families in mind, offering outdoor play equipment and a baby safety gate for younger guests. For those looking to explore the outdoors, the villa is conveniently located near cycling trails, with an outdoor fireplace to welcome guests back from their adventures.

Situated 38 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and within proximity to other attractions like the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and the Rock of Kavaje, Villa Shefit serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural and natural landscape of the area. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is just 30 km away, making it easily accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

With its thoughtful amenities and prime location, Villa Shefit is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that offers a memorable experience for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and culture of Durres County.
