This generously sized apartment boasts three well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and two modern bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for both families and groups of friends. The inclusion of air conditioning, a balcony to enjoy the gentle sea breeze, complimentary private parking, and free WiFi adds to the appeal, making it an excellent choice for those prioritizing both amenities and privacy.

Located 5.8 km from Independence Square and with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 155 km away, Vila Joana presents an accessible retreat for international travelers and those looking to explore the broader region of Vlorë. Whether it's the tranquility of a quiet location or the proximity to beautiful beaches and cultural landmarks, Vila Joana caters to a diverse range of preferences, promising a memorable stay in one of Albania's most charming coastal cities.
