The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities and home comforts. Guests are welcomed into a space that boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring a restful night's sleep for all. The heart of the apartment, a fully equipped kitchen featuring a fridge and stovetop, invites guests to prepare meals at their leisure, adding a homely feel to the dining experience. The living area, complete with a TV and comfortable seating, provides the perfect setting for unwinding after a day of adventure. A contemporary bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower, along with the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, ensures that every need is met with the utmost attention to detail.

Adding to the appeal, the apartment offers the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking, making it an ideal choice for those traveling by car. The commitment to a smoke-free environment further enhances the comfort and well-being of all guests.

Situated just 600 meters from Liro Beach and 7 km from the iconic Independence Square, the location of Holiday 2-Bedroom Apartment in Vlora is unbeatable. For those looking to explore further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 156 km away, making this apartment a great base for both local and international travelers.

In summary, Holiday 2-Bedroom Apartment in Vlora is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the best of Vlorë, combining the freedom and space of apartment living with the thoughtful touches and conveniences of a premium accommodation choice.
