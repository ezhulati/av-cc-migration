Inside, the apartment reveals a cozy yet elegant layout, featuring a single bedroom that promises restful nights. The kitchen is well-equipped with an oven and toaster, catering to guests' culinary needs, while the living area, adorned with a flat-screen TV and comfortable seating, offers a perfect spot for unwinding. The bathroom, complete with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the suite's attention to comfort and convenience. Additionally, an outdoor dining area enhances the appeal, providing an idyllic setting for meals under the sky.

Situated within easy reach of notable attractions such as Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, Golem Beach, and the Rock of Kavaje, Lulu Suites - Moka serves as an ideal base for guests keen to explore the natural beauty and cultural highlights of the area. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 37 km away, ensures smooth travel arrangements for international visitors. Lulu Suites - Moka thus presents itself as a compelling choice for those in search of a comfortable, convenient, and captivating stay in Golem.
