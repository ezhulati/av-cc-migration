Strategically located, the apartment is a mere 2.8 km away from the intriguing House of Leaves and only 1.9 km from the enriching National Museum of History Albania, making cultural excursions effortlessly accessible. For those interested in Albania's history, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is also within easy reach, located just 3.6 km away.

The accommodation itself boasts a tastefully furnished bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a relaxing stay.

For those keen to explore the green spaces of Tirana, Rinia Park is conveniently situated 3 km from the apartment, offering a tranquil escape from the urban environment. Additionally, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, a cornerstone of the country's cultural scene, is just 3.1 km away.

Ease of travel is guaranteed with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located only 12 km from Apartament Tirane, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible. This apartment not only promises a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the rich tapestry of experiences that Tirana has to offer.
