Each air-conditioned apartment within The Quiet Place is designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven, and a cozy seating area. The convenience of a washing machine and the luxury of a private bathroom, equipped with a bidet, bath, and shower, ensure a home-away-from-home experience. For those who love to dine al fresco, an outdoor dining area is available, enhancing the enjoyment of meals amidst the beauty of the garden.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, The Quiet Place offers a picnic area, inviting guests to enjoy leisurely days in the open air, making it an ideal setting for both relaxation and adventure. The Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, is located 33 km away, while the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is 41 km from the property, offering a glimpse into the region's past. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 32 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze for international and domestic travelers alike.

With its thoughtful amenities, strategic location, and peaceful atmosphere, The Quiet Place stands out as a premier choice for those visiting the area, promising a stay that is both comfortable and memorable.
