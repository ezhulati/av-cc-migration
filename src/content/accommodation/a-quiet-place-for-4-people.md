The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and privacy, featuring two tastefully decorated bedrooms that promise a restful night's sleep. The space is thoughtfully equipped with a modern kitchen, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their own meals in a home-away-from-home setting. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is never far away, making downtime enjoyable for everyone.

Located just a short drive from the scenic Saranda City Beach, 2.6 km away, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the local area. For those eager to immerse themselves in nature, the Butrint National Park is within easy reach, located 18 km away, offering a day of exploration and discovery in one of Albania's most treasured landscapes.

For travelers arriving by air, the Ioannina Airport is situated 98 km from the property, making "A Quiet Place for 4 People" an accessible destination for both domestic and international visitors seeking a tranquil escape in Sarandë.
