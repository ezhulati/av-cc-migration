The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It houses two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a coffee machine, perfect for those mornings when guests prefer to dine in. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet and hairdryer, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the apartment's attention to detail. For a touch of luxury, wine or champagne is available, adding an extra layer of indulgence to the stay.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Comfort Apartament serves as an excellent base. Skanderbeg Square, a notable landmark, is located 39 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich history and culture of the area. Additionally, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 42 km from the apartment, provides an adventurous escape with its breathtaking views. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 33 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

In summary, Comfort Apartament, Near the Beach, is not just about its prime location; it's about creating a memorable experience. With its thoughtful amenities and strategic position close to both the beach and key attractions, it stands as a compelling choice for travelers to Durrës.
