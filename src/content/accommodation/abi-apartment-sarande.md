The ground-floor apartment is notably spacious, featuring a bedroom equipped with a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen comes complete with modern appliances including an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. The highlight for many guests is the opportunity to dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area, where the backdrop of the sea enhances every meal.

For convenience, the apartment includes free private parking and complimentary WiFi, ensuring that guests stay connected and mobile with ease. The addition of a sun terrace and the assurance of a 24-hour front desk service further enhance the guest experience, providing both leisure and support at any hour.

Practical amenities on-site include a minimarket, ensuring essentials are always within reach. Additionally, for those looking to explore the area, a car rental service is available directly from the apartment, offering a seamless start to any adventure.

Situated a short drive from La Petite Beach and the renowned Butrint National Park, ABI APARTMENT SARANDË is ideally positioned for guests eager to explore the natural beauty and historical richness of the region. For those traveling further afield, the property offers a paid airport shuttle service to Ioannina Airport, located 97 km away, adding a layer of convenience to the travel experience.

In summary, ABI APARTMENT SARANDË presents itself as a comprehensive solution for travelers looking for a serene beachfront retreat with all the comforts of home, coupled with easy access to Sarandë's most captivating attractions.
