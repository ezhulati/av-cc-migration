The apartment itself is a study in spaciousness and functionality, boasting two separate bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dining area and oven. The inclusion of a living room with a flat-screen TV, along with towels and bed linen, ensures a home-away-from-home experience. Guests also have the luxury of an outdoor dining area, perfect for those balmy evenings.

For those planning to explore the city's sights, Black Diamond in City Center does not disappoint. The provision of packed lunches for day trips adds a thoughtful touch to the stay. Moreover, the property facilitates both bicycle rentals and car hire services, making it effortless for guests to venture out and discover Tirana's attractions, such as Skanderbeg Square, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania.

Located just 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Black Diamond in City Center is not only easily accessible but also serves as an ideal base for both leisure and adventure in Albania's bustling capital.
