Guests are welcomed into a world of comfort with private entrances to each apartment, ensuring a seamless and personalized experience. The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with air conditioning, cozy seating areas, and flat-screen TVs with satellite channels for entertainment. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen and dining area in each unit allows guests the freedom to prepare their own meals, while the option to request towels and linens adds an extra layer of convenience. The private bathrooms, complete with slippers, a shower, and a hairdryer, ensure a comfortable stay.

For dining, the on-site restaurant caters to families and offers a delightful menu of Mediterranean dishes, available for lunch, dinner, and high tea. This family-friendly establishment ensures that every meal is a memorable part of the stay.

Situated 42 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just 6.6 km from the Rock of Kavaje, Peace Apartments is ideally located for exploring the local area. For those flying in, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 32 km away, with the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service offered by the property.

In summary, Peace Apartments stands as a beacon of comfort and convenience, offering a serene retreat with all the comforts of home, making it an ideal choice for families and travelers looking to explore the beauty of Durrës and its surroundings.
