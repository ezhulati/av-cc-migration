Belle, Littleparis stands out for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests. Positioned conveniently 43 km from the historic Monastery Saint Naum, it offers an ideal base for explorers looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and history.

Guests are treated to a luxurious stay in this air-conditioned apartment, which boasts a cozy bedroom and a lavish bathroom equipped with both a hot tub and a bath, promising relaxation and rejuvenation. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV complemented by streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure. The addition of a fireplace adds a touch of warmth and elegance to the accommodation, enhancing the overall experience.

With the convenience of free WiFi and private parking, guests can enjoy the ease of connectivity and security during their stay. For those looking to venture further, Kastoria National Airport is merely 71 km away, making Belle, Littleparis an excellent choice for both local and international travelers seeking a blend of comfort, luxury, and accessibility in Korçë.
