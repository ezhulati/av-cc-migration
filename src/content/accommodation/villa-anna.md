Each unit at Villa Anna is meticulously equipped with a private bathroom, ensuring privacy and ease for all guests. Select rooms elevate the experience further, offering balconies for a breath of fresh air or garden views for a tranquil outlook.

For those looking to satisfy their daily needs without venturing far, a minimarket on the premises provides a range of essentials and snacks. Located 95 km from Ioannina Airport, Villa Anna serves as an accessible haven for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the natural and cultural richness of the area.
