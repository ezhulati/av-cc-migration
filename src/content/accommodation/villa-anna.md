Accommodations at Villa Anna are thoughtfully designed with comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms that ensure a restful escape from the day's adventures. Guests have the convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, making it easy to stay connected and plan the day's explorations.

Each unit at Villa Anna is meticulously equipped with a private bathroom, ensuring privacy and ease for all guests. Select rooms elevate the experience further, offering balconies for a breath of fresh air or garden views for a tranquil outlook.

For those looking to satisfy their daily needs without venturing far, a minimarket on the premises provides a range of essentials and snacks. Additionally, Villa Anna facilitates exploration and adventure beyond its doors with an available car rental service, inviting guests to discover the beauty of Sarandë and its surroundings at their own pace.

Located 95 km from Ioannina Airport, Villa Anna serves as an accessible haven for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the natural and cultural richness of the area.
