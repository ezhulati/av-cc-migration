The hotel's accommodations are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with essential amenities such as a desk, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private balcony, perfect for taking in the breathtaking mountain views. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a bidet and a wardrobe in all units adds an extra layer of convenience to the guest experience.

Adding to the appeal is the hotel's terrace, where guests can unwind and soak up the serene atmosphere, and a bar that serves a variety of refreshing drinks. Room service is also available, ensuring that guests' needs are promptly met.

For those looking to explore beyond the hotel, Bujtina Vasili's location makes it an ideal base. The nearest airport, Kastoria National Airport, is 72 km away, making the hotel easily accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Bujtina Vasili offers a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and scenic beauty, making it a must-visit destination for travelers to Korçë.
