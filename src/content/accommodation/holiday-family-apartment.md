Boasting a generous layout, the apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and an inviting terrace that presents stunning sea views. The kitchen comes fully equipped, catering to guests' culinary needs with a refrigerator, while the bathroom is complete with a bidet, enhancing the comfort of the stay. Beyond its walls, guests can also enjoy captivating city views, adding to the apartment's charm.

Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, located 41 km from Holiday Family Apartment, making it an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers. This property stands out as an ideal spot for families and groups looking for a blend of relaxation and exploration in Golem.
