The apartment itself is a haven of space and comfort, boasting air-conditioned interiors that are thoughtfully designed to accommodate the needs of modern travelers. Guests are welcomed into a world of convenience with two well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen featuring essentials like a toaster and fridge, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment needs are met, while the bathroom, complete with a bidet, adds an extra layer of comfort. The highlight, however, is the balcony that invites guests to enjoy outdoor dining against the backdrop of captivating sea views, making every meal an experience to remember.

Privacy and exclusivity are paramount at Christos Apartment 17, with a private entrance setting the tone for a stay defined by tranquility and relaxation. The provision of fruits upon arrival is a thoughtful touch that underscores the apartment's commitment to guest satisfaction. Moreover, the strict non-smoking policy ensures a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore local culinary delights, a variety of dining options lie in close proximity, offering a taste of Sarandë's vibrant food scene. And with Ioannina Airport just 97 km away, the journey to and from Christos Apartment 17 is as hassle-free as it gets, making it an ideal base for both short getaways and extended stays in this enchanting part of Albania.
