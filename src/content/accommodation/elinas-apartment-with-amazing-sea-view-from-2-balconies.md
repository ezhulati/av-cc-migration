This property distinguishes itself with modern amenities, including an elevator and an ATM, complemented by complimentary WiFi accessible throughout the premises. Emphasizing a smoke-free environment, Elinas Apartment ensures a clean and healthy stay for all guests.

The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen boasting an oven and a fridge. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a shower, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. The added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy for guests.

For convenience, the property hosts a snack bar and a minimarket, catering to guests' immediate needs. Those looking to explore Sarandë and its surroundings will find cycling a delightful option. Additionally, the apartment offers outdoor play equipment and a sun terrace, perfect for relaxation and leisure.

Located a mere 2.7 km from Maestral Beach and 18 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park, Elinas Apartment serves as an ideal base for adventurers and culture enthusiasts alike. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is the nearest airport, located 98 km away, making it a convenient choice for international and domestic travelers seeking a serene coastal escape in Sarandë.
