This air-conditioned haven is not just about its prime location. Guests are treated to a living space that marries comfort with breathtaking scenery. The apartment features two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a balcony that serves as a window to the Adriatic's mesmerizing sea views. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances including a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom with a shower ensures a stay that combines convenience with luxury. Towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, enhancing the homely feel of the apartment.

Privacy is paramount at Sunny Flat, with a private entrance that guarantees a secluded and uninterrupted experience for guests. The promise of free WiFi throughout the property ensures that connectivity needs are met, whether for leisure browsing or staying in touch with loved ones.

For those venturing from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the apartment, making Sunny Flat an accessible retreat for international and domestic travelers alike.

Sunny Flat in Sarandë invites guests to immerse themselves in a setting that is both serene and stimulating, offering a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Whether it's soaking in the scenic views from the balcony, exploring the vibrant city life, or lounging on the nearby beaches, this apartment is a choice accommodation for those seeking an unforgettable Sarandë experience.
