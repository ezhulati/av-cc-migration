Each apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs, featuring a balcony that offers a personal outdoor retreat. Inside, guests are treated to a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The convenience of a well-equipped kitchenette allows for the preparation of meals in the comfort of one's own space, while the private bathroom, complete with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, adds an extra layer of comfort. For those needing to stay connected or get some work done, a desk is provided in every unit.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, guests will find La Petite Beach just 1.8 km away, offering a more secluded spot for sunbathing and relaxation. For the adventurers, the historic Butrint National Park is a mere 17 km journey, presenting an opportunity to immerse in the rich cultural heritage of the region. With Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, Deda Apartments serves as a convenient base for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Deda Apartments stands as a beacon of hospitality in Sarandë, offering well-appointed accommodations that blend convenience, comfort, and accessibility, making it an ideal choice for those looking to experience the best of this vibrant coastal city.
