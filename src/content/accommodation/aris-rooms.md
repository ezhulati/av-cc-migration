The inn prides itself on being a smoke-free environment, promoting a clean and healthy atmosphere for all guests. Situated merely 400 meters from the scenic Prinos Beach and a short drive from the captivating Maracit Beach, Aris Rooms positions itself as a perfect base for beach lovers and explorers alike.

Accommodations at Aris Rooms are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room features a balcony, offering a private outdoor space for guests to enjoy the fresh sea breeze. The inclusion of air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a fridge in every room adds a touch of home comfort, ensuring a pleasant and convenient stay. Select units also boast city views, providing a unique perspective on the local surroundings.

For those looking to venture beyond the beach, the inn is located 142 km from Ioannina Airport, making it accessible for international and domestic travelers seeking a tranquil escape on the Albanian coast.
