Accommodation at Traditional House Shapllo is thoughtfully designed, featuring a four-bedroom apartment that is both spacious and inviting. The kitchen is well-equipped with an oven and toaster, catering to guests' culinary needs. The living space includes a cozy seating area, complemented by a TV for entertainment. Two bathrooms, equipped with showers, ensure convenience for guests. The highlight, however, is the terrace. Offering breathtaking mountain views, it is furnished, inviting guests to relax and soak in the natural beauty surrounding them. The apartment is also well-appointed with essentials such as bed linen, towels, and offers housekeeping services, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is located 82 km away, making Traditional House Shapllo an accessible destination for travelers seeking tranquility and a touch of tradition in their accommodation choice. Additional conveniences such as private check-in and check-out, bicycle parking, and currency exchange services underscore the property's commitment to guest satisfaction and convenience.
