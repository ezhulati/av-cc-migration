This air-conditioned haven offers a sanctuary with its well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their meals, and a pristine bathroom, catering to the needs of modern travelers. The commitment to a non-smoking environment underscores the property's dedication to providing a comfortable and healthy stay.

Beyond the doorstep of Apartment City Center, guests find themselves enveloped in the rich tapestry of Tirana's attractions. The proximity to the Toptani Shopping Centre invites a modern retail experience, while the National Gallery of Arts and Saint Paul Cathedral beckon with cultural enrichment. For those looking to explore further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offers breathtaking views, located just a short drive away.

Connectivity is seamless, with free WiFi enveloping the premises, ensuring that guests remain linked to the world beyond. For explorers venturing from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 15 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

Apartment City Center stands out as a beacon for those desiring to immerse themselves in the dynamic tapestry of Tirana, offering a blend of comfort, culture, and convenience.
