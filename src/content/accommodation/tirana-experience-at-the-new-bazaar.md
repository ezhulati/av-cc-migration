The property distinguishes itself with its garden and mountain views, providing a serene backdrop to its well-appointed apartments. Each apartment is designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring a private entrance that ensures a seamless and private experience. The interiors boast a modern aesthetic, equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV complete with streaming services for entertainment at any time of the day. The inclusion of a fully functional kitchen, dining area, and essential appliances such as an oven, fridge, stovetop, coffee machine, and kettle, underscores the property's commitment to a home-away-from-home experience. The convenience is further enhanced by the provision of private bathrooms equipped with slippers, a walk-in shower, and a hairdryer, alongside complimentary bed linen and towels in all units.

Guests can enjoy the added luxury of a terrace to unwind, a 24-hour front desk for round-the-clock assistance, and the benefit of complimentary WiFi throughout the property, ensuring connectivity and convenience are never compromised.

Strategically positioned, Tirana Experience at the New Bazaar is within close proximity to notable attractions such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Tanners' Bridge, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, making it an ideal base for those keen to explore the historical and cultural offerings of Tirana. For those traveling further afield, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 15 km away, facilitating easy arrivals and departures.

In summary, Tirana Experience at the New Bazaar stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for visitors to Tirana, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and access to the city's rich tapestry of attractions.
