Guests at Coral Apartments are treated to a seamless stay experience, thanks to the thoughtful amenities provided. Each apartment boasts a balcony that offers a private slice of Sarandë's charming ambiance. The interiors are designed for both short and long stays, featuring a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher, an oven, a toaster, and a fridge, ensuring guests feel right at home. The living spaces are adorned with a flat-screen TV, a comfortable seating area, and a desk, catering to both leisure and business needs.

The convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi adds to the appeal, making it an effortless choice for travelers arriving by car or those in need of constant connectivity. The inclusion of air conditioning, a washing machine, and a private bathroom with a bidet and hair dryer in every unit underscores the property's commitment to guest comfort.

Situated 18 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport 98 km away, Coral Apartments not only offers a gateway to the natural and historical wonders of the region but also ensures a hassle-free travel experience.

For those in search of a stay that combines the comforts of home with the excitement of travel, Coral Apartments in Sarandë presents itself as an impeccable choice.
