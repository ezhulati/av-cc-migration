Inside, guests will find a well-appointed living space that includes a bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen outfitted with modern appliances such as a dishwasher and an oven. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with towels and bed linen, ensures a comfortable and hassle-free stay.
