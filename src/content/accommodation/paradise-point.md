Guests at Paradise Point are treated to rooms boasting balconies that open up to captivating city views, enhancing the experience of every stay. The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring private bathrooms equipped with showers and complimentary toiletries. For entertainment and connectivity, each room is outfitted with a flat-screen TV and free WiFi, catering to the needs of the modern traveler.

The multilingual staff at Paradise Point stand ready to offer valuable insights and recommendations, ensuring guests fully explore and appreciate the local culture and attractions. Their proficiency in Greek, English, and Italian allows for seamless communication and an enriched guest experience.

Located 72 km from Kastoria National Airport, Paradise Point serves as an ideal base for exploring the enchanting surroundings of Korçë, promising an unforgettable stay marked by convenience, comfort, and exceptional hospitality.
