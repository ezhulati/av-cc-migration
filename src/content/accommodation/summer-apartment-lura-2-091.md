The apartment itself presents a harmonious blend of comfort and style, featuring a bedroom, a living room, and a terrace that boasts garden views. Guests can enjoy the modern comforts of a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchenette with essential appliances including a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower. The provision of towels and bed linen enhances the home-away-from-home experience, while the private entrance to the accommodation underscores the commitment to guest privacy.

For those who prefer to stay in, a convenient minimarket on the premises caters to everyday needs. Meanwhile, the adventurous at heart will find Durrës a haven for activities, especially cycling, promising an engaging experience of the local landscape.

Strategically positioned, Summer Apartment Lura 2 - 091 is a mere 43 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 46 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering a perfect base to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the area. For international travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 28 km away, adds to the appeal, making it an accessible retreat for both local and global visitors.
