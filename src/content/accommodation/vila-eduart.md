The accommodations at Vila Eduart are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air-conditioned units that boast not only a desk and safety deposit box for those mixing work with pleasure but also a flat-screen TV and balcony for leisurely moments. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a bidet in each unit, alongside a private entrance, ensures a blend of convenience and privacy. To further enhance the comfort of your stay, each unit is furnished with fresh bed linen and towels.

Morning risers are greeted with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The property's terrace offers a tranquil setting for guests to unwind, while the on-site lounge provides a cozy corner for socializing or simply enjoying a moment of solitude. For added convenience, a minimarket is available on the premises, catering to any last-minute needs or cravings.

Situated just 1.3 km from the inviting Coco Beach and a short drive from the historic Butrint National Park, Vila Eduart serves as an ideal base for guests looking to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the area. For those venturing further afield, Corfu International Airport is located 94 km away, making international travel a breeze.

Vila Eduart represents a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and location, making it a top choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay in Ksamil.
