Guests at Fenix Hotel are treated to a host of thoughtful amenities designed to enhance their stay. The hotel boasts a selection of well-appointed rooms, each featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, ample storage with a wardrobe, and the convenience of a fridge. Entertainment and relaxation are ensured with a flat-screen TV, while the private balcony offers captivating views of the cityscape. Ensuring a comfortable stay, each room is also equipped with fresh bed linen, towels, and a private bathroom.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Fenix Hotel offers a range of facilities to cater to the needs of its guests. The property includes a shared lounge for socializing, complimentary private parking for those arriving by car, and a terrace that invites guests to unwind in the open air. The hotel's commitment to service is evident with its 24-hour front desk, ATM, and currency exchange services, ensuring a hassle-free experience for international travelers.

For those looking to explore the natural beauty of the area, Butrint National Park is a mere 2.6 km away, offering a perfect opportunity for an adventurous day out. Additionally, for guests traveling from afar, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km from the hotel, making Fenix Hotel a convenient choice for both domestic and international visitors.

In summary, Fenix Hotel stands out as a welcoming retreat in Ksamil, offering a harmonious blend of location, amenities, and service, poised to make any stay memorable.
