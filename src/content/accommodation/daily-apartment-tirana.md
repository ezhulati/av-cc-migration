The apartment itself is a study in spacious living, boasting a separate bedroom, a bathroom, and a living room adorned with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen is fully equipped, featuring a dining area and oven, catering to guests' culinary needs. While the accommodation is strictly non-smoking, it does offer the option of towels and linens for a small additional cost, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those looking to enjoy a drink without straying far, an on-site bar serves as a cozy spot to relax. Moreover, the availability of a car rental service directly from the apartment adds a layer of convenience for guests eager to explore Tirana's sights and sounds.

Notably, the apartment is strategically located, with the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car just 4.2 km away, offering breathtaking views of the city from above. The historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is also within easy reach, located 3.2 km away. For guests traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 14 km from Daily Apartment Tirana, with the added benefit of a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless travel experience.

Daily Apartment Tirana stands out as a thoughtful blend of comfort, convenience, and accessibility, making it an ideal choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed home away from home.
