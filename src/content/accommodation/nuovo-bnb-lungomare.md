The apartment is a testament to modern living, featuring a sleek bedroom, a living space adorned with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a contemporary bathroom complete with a bidet, all while ensuring a smoke-free environment. Guests are treated to tranquil street views, providing a peaceful retreat after a day of exploration.

Strategically located, Nuovo bnb Lungomare is within easy reach of the historic Kuzum Baba viewpoint and the iconic Independence Square, making it an ideal base for guests eager to discover the cultural heartbeat of Vlorë. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is accessible, ensuring that this haven is well-connected for international travelers.

In essence, Nuovo bnb Lungomare stands out as a sophisticated haven for guests looking for a blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural immersion in the heart of Vlorë.
