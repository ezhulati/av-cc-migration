The guest house presents a meticulously designed villa that boasts a terrace with captivating mountain views. The layout includes two cozy bedrooms, dual living spaces adorned with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring modern appliances such as a microwave and toaster. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet, along with thoughtful touches like complimentary fruits, enhances the comfort of the stay.

Outdoor enthusiasts will appreciate the villa's barbecue facilities and the tranquil garden, perfect for leisurely afternoons or intimate gatherings. Despite its peaceful location, Baka Guest House remains within easy reach of notable attractions. Skanderbeg Square is located 38 km away, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history, while the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 42 km away, promises an adventure with its scenic rides. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 32 km from the property, ensuring a smooth transition to and from this idyllic escape.

Baka Guest House stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of tranquility, convenience, and a touch of luxury during their visit to Durrës.
