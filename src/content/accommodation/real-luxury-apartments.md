Guests are welcomed into a world of modern amenities, where each air-conditioned apartment boasts two separate bedrooms, ensuring privacy and space for all occupants. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen caters to those who prefer the comforts of home-cooked meals, while the presence of two bathrooms adds an extra layer of convenience. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

The allure of Real Luxury Apartments is further enhanced by its generous offering of a balcony and terrace, inviting guests to soak in the vibrant atmosphere of Sarandë from the comfort of their accommodation. The provision of complimentary private parking and WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests enjoy a hassle-free stay, focusing on making the most of their visit to this enchanting city.

Situated 17 km from the natural wonders of Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport located 97 km away, Real Luxury Apartments serves as an ideal base for both leisure and adventure. This non-smoking property not only promises a memorable stay but also delivers on the expectation of real luxury in every aspect.
