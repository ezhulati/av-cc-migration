Guests are welcomed into a space where convenience meets modernity. The apartment is air-conditioned, featuring two cozy bedrooms that promise a restful night's sleep. Entertainment and relaxation are at your fingertips with a satellite flat-screen TV and a comfortable dining area, perfect for enjoying meals prepared in the fully equipped kitchen. The inclusion of a fridge and a spacious living room further enhances the home-away-from-home experience.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate comforts of Alvi Apartment, Skanderbeg Square is located 50 km away, offering a deep dive into the local culture and history. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, a mere 8.4 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for adventure and exploration. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 44 km away, making travel arrangements to and from the accommodation a breeze.

With both free WiFi and on-site parking available at no extra charge, and a strict non-smoking policy to ensure the comfort and well-being of all guests, Alvi Apartment stands out as an exemplary choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure on their next getaway.
