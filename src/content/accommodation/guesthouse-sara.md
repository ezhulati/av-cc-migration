The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a generous layout that includes a well-appointed bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a balcony that invites guests to unwind in the fresh air. The heart of the home, the kitchen, leaves nothing to be desired, featuring a full suite of appliances including an oven, microwave, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, perfect for those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals while traveling. To further enhance the experience, the apartment is furnished with soft towels and crisp bed linen, adding a touch of luxury to the stay.

For those traveling from afar, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 150 km away, makes Guesthouse Sara an accessible retreat for international visitors. This accommodation is not just a place to stay but a gateway to exploring the rich culture and breathtaking landscapes of Vlorë, promising an unforgettable experience for all who visit.
