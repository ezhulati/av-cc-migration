The highlight of Orest Guest House is undoubtedly its spacious apartment, which features a balcony that presents tranquil garden views. The apartment is thoughtfully designed with a separate bedroom, a cozy living room, and a well-equipped kitchen, complete with a minibar, ensuring guests feel right at home. Entertainment needs are well catered to with the provision of a flat-screen TV.

Located just 2 km from the captivating Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, the guest house serves as an ideal base for beach lovers. Moreover, those keen to explore further afield will find Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within accessible distances, at 45 km and 49 km respectively. For travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being just 39 km away adds to the appeal of Orest Guest House as a choice accommodation in Golem.
