The apartment distinguishes itself with a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen boasting an oven and microwave, alongside a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. The presence of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is always at hand, while the bathroom, complete with a bidet, adds a touch of modernity. For those who value their privacy, the accommodation also provides a private entrance, enhancing the sense of exclusivity.

Guests will find themselves in close proximity to several of Tirana's cultural landmarks, including the National Museum of History Albania, the intriguing House of Leaves, and the prestigious National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, making it an ideal base for explorers keen to immerse themselves in the local culture. Furthermore, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 12 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest comfort, Modern 1 Bedroom City Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to experience the best of Tirana.
