This air-conditioned apartment doesn't just offer a convenient location; it comes with a balcony that provides a serene spot to unwind, complemented by complimentary WiFi to keep guests connected. The interior boasts a sleek design, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen. Whether it's whipping up a quick breakfast with the provided oven and toaster or doing laundry with ease thanks to the washing machine, guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot to relax after a day of sightseeing. Moreover, 2V1 - 21 Dhjetori is surrounded by must-visit attractions, including Rinia Park, the House of Leaves, and the whimsical Reja - The Cloud installation.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, ensuring smooth travel plans to and from the city. Whether it's the allure of nearby historical sites like the Clock Tower Tirana, Et'hem Bey Mosque, and the National Museum of History Albania, or the comfort and convenience offered by the apartment itself, 2V1 - 21 Dhjetori emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking an immersive Tirana experience.
