Guests are welcomed into a spacious apartment that boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a bathroom stocked with essential linens and towels, and a living area anchored by a flat-screen TV. The fully equipped kitchen invites guests to feel at home, while the balcony offers an unparalleled view of the sea, with the city's silhouette on the horizon.

For everyday needs, a minimarket on the premises ensures convenience is just a few steps away.

Explorers looking to uncover the area's secrets will find the Rock of Kavaje within a short 6.2 km journey, while the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is accessible at 39 km. Infinite 680 is also conveniently located 33 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, with a paid shuttle service available to ensure seamless arrivals and departures.

Infinite 680 is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the rich culture and breathtaking landscapes of Durrës, offering comfort, convenience, and captivating views, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.
