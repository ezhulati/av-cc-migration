This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a slice of home with its spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room adorned with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony that offers tranquil street views. The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances including an oven and a fridge, ensuring guests can prepare meals with ease. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds to the convenience, while the private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to privacy.

Beyond the comfort of the apartment, guests will appreciate the practical amenities that enhance the stay experience. The property boasts a lift for easy access, a minimarket for essentials, and the indispensable free WiFi that covers the entire property, ensuring that both leisure and business travelers stay connected.

Located just 1.6 km from the iconic Kuzum Baba and with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 150 km away, Redsun City Center Apartment is not only accessible but also a non-smoking haven for those who value cleanliness and fresh air.

For those in search of a comfortable, well-appointed base in Vlorë, Redsun City Center Apartment offers a compelling blend of home-like amenities and a location that puts the best of the city within easy reach.
