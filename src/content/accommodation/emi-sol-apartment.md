The apartment itself is a haven of tranquility and comfort. Guests are greeted by a beautifully designed space that opens onto a balcony, offering serene garden views. The air-conditioned apartment boasts a spacious bedroom and a fully equipped kitchen, ensuring a stay that combines the warmth of home with the luxury of hotel living. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, perfect for relaxing evenings in.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, guests can explore the well-maintained garden, soak up the sun on the terrace, or enjoy a refreshing drink at the bar. For those needing essentials or a quick snack, a convenient minimarket on the premises caters to all basic needs.

Situated 7.2 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and 7.4 km from Independence Square, Emi Sol Apartment offers an excellent base for those looking to delve into the rich history and culture of the area. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 156 km away, making it a manageable distance for both short and extended stays.

Emi Sol Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering a blend of location, comfort, and amenities that cater to the needs of modern travelers. Whether it's for a leisurely beach getaway or a cultural exploration of Vlorë, this property promises an unforgettable stay.
