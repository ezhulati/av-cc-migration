Accommodations at Helia Hotel are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV boasting an array of satellite channels, alongside essential amenities such as a desk for those needing to stay productive. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, with select rooms offering captivating city views that add a touch of charm to your stay. Comfort is paramount, with air conditioning and ample wardrobe space standard in all guest rooms.

Mornings at Helia Hotel are greeted with a delightful buffet breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's prime location not only places guests within easy reach of Sarandë's beautiful beach but also offers convenient access to Corfu Town, just 28 km away, and Glyfada, 35 km distant, for those looking to venture further.

Helia Hotel stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of style, service, and location in Sarandë.
