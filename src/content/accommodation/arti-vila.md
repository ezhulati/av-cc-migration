The property boasts a well-appointed holiday home that features a spacious terrace overlooking the sea, designed to accommodate the modern traveler's needs. The home includes a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances such as an oven and a microwave. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, alongside an air-conditioned unit that comprises a bath and a dressing room, ensures a comfortable stay. Notably, the holiday home prides itself on being allergy-free and strictly non-smoking, catering to the well-being of all guests.

For those looking to unwind, Arti Vila offers a variety of leisure options. Guests can enjoy moments of relaxation in the bar or lounge area, and for convenience, a minimarket is available on-site. The property is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with activities like fishing, canoeing, and hiking readily accessible. Moreover, families will appreciate the children's playground and the barbecue facilities, promising enjoyable moments for all ages.

Situated merely 500 meters from Lori Beach and a kilometer from Bora Bora Beach, the location is ideal for beach lovers. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 92 km away, making Arti Vila an accessible choice for a memorable getaway.

With its comprehensive amenities, including a free shuttle service, a shared kitchen, and luggage storage, Arti Vila stands out as a top choice for those seeking a blend of adventure and relaxation in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
