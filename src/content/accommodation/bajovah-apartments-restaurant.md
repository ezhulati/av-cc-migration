Guests at Bajovah Apartments & Restaurant are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The property boasts an array of family-friendly rooms, each featuring a private bathroom with a shower and complimentary toiletries. Select accommodations also offer a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. All units are thoughtfully appointed with bed linen and towels, adding a touch of home-like comfort.

The in-house restaurant presents a culinary journey, inviting guests to savor delicious meals without leaving the comfort of the property. Additionally, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot for evening relaxation or a casual meet-up over drinks.

For those looking to explore the local area, Bajovah Apartments & Restaurant serves as an ideal base. The property is conveniently located near notable attractions, including the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, 8.3 km away, and the Rock of Kavaje, 37 km away. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 11 km from the property, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

With the added conveniences of complimentary private parking and free WiFi, Bajovah Apartments & Restaurant ensures a hassle-free experience for all guests. Whether visiting for leisure or business, this property offers a welcoming atmosphere, modern amenities, and a prime location, making it a top choice for travelers to Tirana.
