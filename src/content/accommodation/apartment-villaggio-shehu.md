Rated at four stars, each apartment at Villaggio Shehu is designed to be a home away from home. Guests can expect to find a private entrance leading to a space that boasts a balcony with breathtaking sea views, a cozy seating area for relaxation, and a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchenette, equipped with a fridge and stovetop, allows for the preparation of meals at any time of the day, while the private bathroom comes with a bath for soaking after a long day of exploration. For added comfort, air conditioning and a wardrobe are standard in all units.

For those peckish moments, a snack bar is on-site, and a minimarket is conveniently located for any essentials or last-minute needs.

Adventure seekers and leisure lovers alike will find Apartment Villaggio Shehu to be an ideal base for engaging in local activities such as cycling and fishing, exploring the natural beauty and vibrant culture of Vlorë.

Situated just 12 km from the historical Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, and 161 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, this accommodation offers both tranquility and easy access to local landmarks, making it a compelling choice for travelers looking to experience the best of Vlorë.
