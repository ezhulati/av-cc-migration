Guests can enjoy the convenience of modern amenities including free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine. The kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with a dishwasher and oven, catering to those who prefer a home-cooked meal even while away from home.

For those looking to explore beyond the city, Butrint National Park offers a rich historical and natural experience, located just 18 km from the apartment. Additionally, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is 98 km away, making The Hill Sea View Apartment an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers alike.

This apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the vibrant life and beauty of Sarandë, promising an unforgettable experience for all who visit.
