This apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It features a single bedroom, but don't let that fool you; the space is ample and welcoming. The kitchen is fully equipped, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. After a day of exploring, guests can unwind in the living area and catch up on their favorite shows on the flat-screen TV provided.

For those interested in the rich history of Tirana, the House of Leaves museum is a mere 2.9 km away, offering insightful exhibits into the country's past. Rinia Park, a perfect spot for a leisurely stroll or a picnic, is just 3.2 km from the apartment. Additionally, the convenience of being 11 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes this apartment an ideal choice for both short and long-term stays.

This non-smoking property ensures a comfortable and serene environment, making it a perfect retreat for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in the heart of Tirana.
