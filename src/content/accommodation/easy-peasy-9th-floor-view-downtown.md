The accommodation prides itself on its commitment to a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and fresh setting for all guests. The air-conditioned apartment is thoughtfully designed with a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a fridge, catering to all the needs of modern travelers. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet and slippers, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds a touch of home comfort to the urban retreat.

For those looking to explore the city at their own pace, Easy Peasy offers a convenient car rental service right from the premises. This feature is perfect for guests eager to discover nearby attractions such as the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the bustling Toptani Shopping Centre, and the prestigious National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania.

Situated just 4.4 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, guests have the unique opportunity to easily venture into the natural landscapes surrounding the city. Moreover, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 14 km away, the apartment stands as an ideal choice for both international visitors and those seeking a memorable urban escape.

Easy Peasy - 9th Floor View Downtown is not just an accommodation; it's a gateway to experiencing the city's culture, history, and natural beauty, all from the comfort of a home-like setting.
