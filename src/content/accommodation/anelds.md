This pet-friendly haven doesn't just welcome guests but their furry companions too, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore Sarandë with their pets. The convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests remain connected and entertained throughout their stay.

Featuring a generously sized layout, the apartment includes two cozy bedrooms, a living room designed for relaxation, and a balcony that presents captivating city views. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV caters to the entertainment needs of guests, while the fully equipped kitchen, complete with a fridge, invites them to create culinary delights at their leisure. The bathroom, equipped with a bidet, adds an extra layer of comfort and convenience to the living space.

For those eager to explore beyond the city's beaches, Butrint National Park lies just 16 km away, offering a dive into the area's rich history and natural beauty. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 96 km from Aneld's, facilitates easy travel arrangements for international guests.

Aneld's Apartment stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of urban excitement and beachside relaxation in Sarandë, promising a stay that is as comfortable as it is memorable.
