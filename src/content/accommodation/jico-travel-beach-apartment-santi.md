This property stands out for its thoughtful amenities, including air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and the convenience of an in-house minimarket. Guests will appreciate the added ease provided by the lift, making every floor easily accessible.

The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a terrace that invites guests to unwind while gazing at the expansive sea. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen complete with an oven and a fridge, and two bathrooms, each with a bidet, ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxuries of a getaway.

For those keen to explore, the Beach at Government Villas is just 2 km away, offering another spot for relaxation and leisure. The historical site of Kuzum Baba is also within reach, located 4 km from the apartment, providing an opportunity to delve into the local culture and history.

Situated 153 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Jico Travel - Beach Apartment Santi represents an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers looking for a serene beachfront retreat in Vlorë.
