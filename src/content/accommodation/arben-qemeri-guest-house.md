Each room at Arben Qemeri Guest House is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, equipped with air conditioning for those warm Berat days, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of modernity. The private bathrooms come complete with a bidet and slippers, adding a layer of luxury to the stay. Moreover, the presence of both seating and dining areas in all units underscores the guest house's commitment to creating a homely atmosphere.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot to unwind after a day of exploring the city's rich history. And while the allure of Berat's local attractions is undeniable, the guest house's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 118 km away, makes it a practical choice for international travelers.

In essence, Arben Qemeri Guest House stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience, offering a blend of traditional charm and modern amenities that cater to the needs of every guest.
