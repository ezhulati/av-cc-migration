Accommodations at KRISLI HOTEL are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room boasts a balcony, offering a private outdoor space for guests to enjoy. The presence of a private bathroom equipped with a shower in every room, alongside complimentary WiFi, ensures a stay that is both comfortable and connected. Select rooms elevate the experience further with breathtaking sea views, allowing guests to wake up to the tranquil beauty of Sarandë. Convenience is paramount, with all units featuring air conditioning and a desk, catering to both leisure and business travelers.

For those traveling to or from the area, Ioannina Airport is located 120 km away, making KRISLI HOTEL an accessible choice for international and domestic visitors alike.
