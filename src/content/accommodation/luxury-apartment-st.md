The apartment itself is a testament to contemporary living, boasting an air-conditioned space that includes a bedroom, a living room with a flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring a fridge. Guests can also enjoy city views, adding another layer to the apartment's appeal. The inclusion of free WiFi and access to a private balcony ensures that guests have all they need for a comfortable stay.

Situated 2.8 km from the popular Durres Beach and within a reasonable distance from notable landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Luxury Apartment ST offers an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of the area. For those traveling by air, the convenience of being just 33 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes this apartment an attractive option.

Luxury Apartment ST stands out as a sophisticated retreat that promises an unforgettable stay in Durrës, blending the allure of the sea with the comforts of home.
