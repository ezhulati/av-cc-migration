This exquisite apartment boasts a spacious bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring satellite channels, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes modern appliances such as a dishwasher and toaster. For added convenience, a washing machine is available, alongside essential services like bed linen, towels, and ironing. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of luxury to the overall comfortable and well-appointed accommodation.

Beyond the comfort of the apartment, guests are invited to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty. The area is ripe for exploration, with opportunities for fishing and leisurely strolls along the beach, allowing visitors to connect with the local way of life.

For those interested in venturing further, Arteg Apartments is strategically positioned, with Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within a reasonable distance, offering a glimpse into Albania's rich history and breathtaking landscapes. The property is also conveniently located 36 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it easily accessible for international travelers.

With its stunning sea and city views, comprehensive amenities, and prime location, Arteg Apartments - Full Sea View is a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of relaxation and adventure in Durrës.
