The accommodations at SEA VIEW SUITES Lungomare are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air-conditioned spaces that boast balconies with breathtaking sea views. Each apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a kitchen that comes complete with a microwave, fridge, coffee machine, and kettle, ensuring guests feel right at home. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which are outfitted with baths, bathrobes, and hair dryers, adding a touch of luxury to the experience. For added convenience, bed linen and towels are provided in every unit, ensuring a comfortable stay.

Guests of SEA VIEW SUITES Lungomare can also enjoy the convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, making it easy to stay connected and plan day trips around Vlorë. The on-site coffee shop offers a cozy spot for guests to relax and savor their favorite beverages, adding to the property's appeal.

Located just 2.6 km from Independence Square and 3.1 km from the historic Kuzum Baba, the property serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural heritage of the area. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km away, with the property offering a paid airport shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

SEA VIEW SUITES Lungomare is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises an unforgettable escape by the sea, where every detail is crafted for guest satisfaction.
