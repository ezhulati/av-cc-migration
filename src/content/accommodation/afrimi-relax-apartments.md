Each apartment at Afrimi Relax Apartments is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of entertainment. The attention to detail is evident in the private bathrooms, equipped with both shower and bathrobes, ensuring a luxurious stay. The inclusion of a terrace and seating area in every unit allows guests to soak in the breathtaking views at their leisure.

The convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi adds to the allure, making it an effortless choice for families and solo travelers alike. For those keen to explore beyond the beach, the historic Butrint National Park is just 13 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage, while the ancient ruins of Fanoti are within a 47 km drive.

Situated 97 km from Ioannina Airport, Afrimi Relax Apartments stands out as a beacon of relaxation and comfort, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Sarandë's coastal paradise.
