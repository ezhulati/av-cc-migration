Each apartment is thoughtfully furnished with cable TV and a kitchenette, complete with a dining table for those intimate meals prepared in the comfort of one's temporary home. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which are not only equipped with showers but also stocked with complimentary toiletries and towels, adding a touch of luxury to the everyday.

For guests arriving by car, the convenience of free on-site parking eliminates any worries about the safety of their vehicle. Moreover, the property facilitates the exploration of Ksamil and its stunning environs by offering boat and scooter rentals, organized seamlessly through the owner.

Dining options are conveniently located just 130 meters away, where a bar and restaurant await to serve local delicacies and refreshing drinks. For those looking to venture further, the Apartments Cerro is ideally situated, with a bus station only 150 meters away and a ferry port 14 km distant, making it an excellent base for exploring the area. Notably, the awe-inspiring Syri i kaltër, a natural water spring phenomenon, lies within a reachable distance of 31 km, promising an unforgettable excursion.

Adding to its appeal, the property is a mere 4 km from the historic Butrint National Park, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of history and nature that defines this region. Apartments Cerro emerges not just as a place to stay but as a gateway to the myriad of experiences that Ksamil has to offer.
