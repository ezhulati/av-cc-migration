The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, equipped with a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV hosting a variety of satellite channels. Culinary enthusiasts will be delighted to find a fully equipped kitchen at their disposal, complete with an oven, microwave, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, making self-catering a breeze. An added layer of comfort is provided by the accommodation's private entrance, ensuring a secluded and personal experience for every guest.

For those looking to explore beyond the city's bounds, Podgorica Airport is conveniently located just 58 km away, making MISHEL House an ideal base for both short stays and longer explorations in this captivating region.
