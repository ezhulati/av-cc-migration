The accommodations at Studio Eno Ksamil are designed with comfort and privacy in mind. Select units boast their own entrances, ensuring a secluded stay, and are equipped with essentials such as a desk and wardrobe. For those who appreciate a view, some rooms offer terraces that gaze out onto the majestic mountains, complemented by modern amenities including satellite flat-screen TVs and air conditioning. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms fitted in each unit, enhancing the overall guest experience.

Families and groups will find the spacious family rooms and the on-site barbecue facilities ideal for creating memorable moments together. Moreover, the property's garden offers a tranquil setting for relaxation or leisure activities.

For adventurers and nature enthusiasts, the proximity to Bora Bora Beach and the renowned Butrint National Park, just a few kilometers away, presents an opportunity to explore the natural beauty of the area. Additionally, the option to engage in fishing near the apartment adds an element of excitement to the stay.

Located 93 km from Corfu International Airport, Studio Eno Ksamil serves as a convenient base for exploring the surrounding region, promising an unforgettable stay marked by comfort, beauty, and adventure.
