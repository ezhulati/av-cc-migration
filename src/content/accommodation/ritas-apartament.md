Guests at Ritas Apartment are treated to a suite of amenities designed to make their stay as effortless as it is enjoyable. The apartment boasts a modern living room equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring satellite channels, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and toaster, invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like them, adding a touch of home to their travel experience. The convenience of air conditioning, coupled with complimentary WiFi and on-site parking, further enhances the appeal of this accommodation, making it a standout choice for both short and long stays.

The apartment's commitment to a smoke-free environment ensures a fresh and inviting atmosphere, while the quiet street views offer a peaceful backdrop for relaxation. For those looking to explore beyond the city's beaches, Butrint National Park lies 18 km away, promising adventures amidst historical ruins and lush landscapes. Moreover, with Ioannina Airport located 98 km from the apartment, guests have a straightforward route to their serene Sarandë sanctuary.

Ritas Apartment stands out as a prime choice for travelers in search of a comfortable, well-appointed base from which to explore the enchanting city of Sarandë and its surrounding natural beauty.
