Accommodations at HOTEL VILA 15 - CENTER are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room boasts essential amenities such as a wardrobe, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a restful stay. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and complimentary toiletries, while select rooms also feature balconies for guests to enjoy the surrounding views. Practical conveniences like desks and kettles in every room add to the hotel's appeal for both leisure and business travelers.

Guests can start their day on a high note with a choice of buffet, continental, or American breakfast options served daily. The hotel's proximity to popular landmarks, including Tanners' Bridge, Saint Paul Cathedral, and Toptani Shopping Centre, makes it an ideal base for exploring the city's rich cultural tapestry.

For those arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 16 km away, with the hotel offering a convenient paid airport shuttle service. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and attentive services such as a concierge, tour desk, and currency exchange, HOTEL VILA 15 - CENTER stands as a testament to Tirana's hospitality, promising an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
