LETI APARTMENTS 21 ST stands out for its proximity to notable landmarks, including the Et'hem Bey Mosque, the National Museum of History Albania, and the intriguing Reja - The Cloud installation. Additionally, the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is just 2.1 km away, with the scenic Rock of Kavaje located 43 km from the premises, offering a perfect blend of historical exploration and natural beauty.

Guests are welcomed into a spacious one-bedroom apartment, where comfort meets style. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV boasting an array of cable channels, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The apartment's kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with modern appliances including an oven, a stovetop, and a washing machine, catering to all culinary needs. Complimentary towels and bed linen add a touch of luxury to the stay, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

For those keen to explore, LETI APARTMENTS 21 ST is conveniently located near the House of Leaves, Rinia Park, and the Clock Tower Tirana, making it an ideal base for discovering the city's attractions. The Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 14 km away, ensuring easy access for international and domestic travelers alike.

LETI APARTMENTS 21 ST emerges as a top recommendation for those seeking to immerse themselves in the culture and beauty of Tirana, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to the city's most cherished sights.
