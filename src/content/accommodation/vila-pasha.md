Boasting a selection of accommodations, each with air conditioning and a private bathroom stocked with complimentary toiletries, VILA PASHA ensures a comfortable stay for all guests. Selected rooms further enhance the experience with private balconies, offering garden views that promise relaxation and a sense of escape from the everyday. Complimentary WiFi throughout the property keeps guests connected, while the option of free private parking and car hire services provides the ultimate convenience for those eager to explore Sarandë and its surroundings.

The property's garden invites guests to unwind and soak in the peaceful atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for relaxation after a day of adventure. For those looking to venture further, the ancient ruins of Fanoti are within a 46-kilometer journey, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history.

Situated 99 kilometers from Ioannina Airport, VILA PASHA represents an accessible haven for travelers seeking both relaxation and exploration in the picturesque setting of Sarandë.
