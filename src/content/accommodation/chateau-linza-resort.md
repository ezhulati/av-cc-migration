Guests have the pleasure of starting their day with a choice of breakfast options, ranging from a hearty buffet to Full English/Irish or American styles, each promising to satisfy a variety of tastes and preferences.

For those eager to explore, Chateau Linza Resort serves as an ideal base, located just 5.7 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and a short 5.9 km journey from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, only 18 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

Select accommodations at the resort offer the added luxury of a terrace, presenting guests with stunning views of the cityscape, making every stay at Chateau Linza Resort an experience to remember.
