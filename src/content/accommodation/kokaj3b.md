Guests at Kokaj3B are greeted with thoughtfully appointed rooms, each featuring essential amenities such as a wardrobe and a private bathroom equipped with a shower and slippers. The promise of connectivity is kept with complimentary WiFi access, and select rooms elevate the experience with breathtaking mountain views, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Strategically located, the hotel is a stone's throw away from notable landmarks including the Clock Tower in Podgorica and the Parliament of Montenegro, both situated 26 km away. The Natural History Museum and the venerable St. George Church, along with the iconic Millennium Bridge, are also within a 27 km radius, offering guests a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of the region.

Kokaj3B prides itself on exceptional guest services, including a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage space, and currency exchange, ensuring a hassle-free stay for international and domestic travelers alike. Just 26 km from Podgorica Airport, the hotel serves as an ideal base for exploring the enchanting landscapes and historical sites of Montenegro.
