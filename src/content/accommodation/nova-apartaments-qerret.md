Guests can enjoy the luxury of connectivity with free WiFi accessible throughout the premises, ensuring that the world is at their fingertips, even while basking in the tranquility of this serene getaway. The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a dining area perfect for intimate meals. The kitchen is fully equipped, boasting an oven among other essentials, catering to the culinary whims of every guest. The living room, with its cozy ambiance, invites guests to relax after a day of exploration.

Adding to the charm of Nova Apartaments Qerret is the balcony, which serves as an idyllic outdoor dining area, offering guests the opportunity to dine under the stars. The provision of a private entrance underscores the apartment's commitment to privacy and exclusivity.

For those keen on exploring the local area, the Rock of Kavaje is located just 8.5 km away, while the historical Durres Amphitheatre is within a 19 km reach. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, located 44 km from the property, making Nova Apartaments Qerret an accessible and appealing choice for both domestic and international travelers.
