The villa itself is a testament to modern comfort and privacy, featuring three well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen with state-of-the-art appliances including a dishwasher and microwave, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. The presence of two bathrooms, each with a bidet, adds an extra layer of convenience. The outdoor dining area invites guests to savor their meals against the backdrop of stunning natural scenery, while the private entrance to the accommodation guarantees an exclusive experience.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty, Villa Cristal does not disappoint. The property is ideally situated for guests to engage in a variety of activities, from fishing adventures in the surrounding waters of Vlorë to leisurely afternoons spent barbecuing on the terrace.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Ioannina Airport located 162 km away, making Villa Cristal an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers. This villa stands out as a perfect blend of luxury, privacy, and natural beauty, promising an unforgettable stay for anyone looking to explore the enchanting coast of Vlorë.
