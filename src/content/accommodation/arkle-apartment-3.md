The strategic location of Arkle Apartment 3 places guests within a stone's throw from significant landmarks, including the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, just 700 meters away, and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 6.5 km from the premises for those inclined towards adventure and panoramic city views.

Accommodations at Arkle Apartment 3 are thoughtfully designed to cater to families and groups, featuring spacious family rooms that promise a comfortable stay. Each unit is well-appointed with a cozy seating area, a dining space, and a fully equipped kitchenette boasting an array of cooking facilities such as an oven, microwave, fridge, and stovetop, ensuring guests feel right at home. The provision of bed linen and towels in all units adds a touch of convenience to the stay.

For those keen on exploring the local culture and attractions, Arkle Apartment 3 is conveniently located near several popular points of interest. Guests can easily access the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, the iconic Pyramid of Tirana, and Reja - The Cloud, making it an ideal base for those looking to immerse themselves in the city's rich history and contemporary art scene.

Completing the appeal of Arkle Apartment 3 is its proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 16 km away, ensuring a hassle-free arrival and departure for international and domestic travelers alike.
