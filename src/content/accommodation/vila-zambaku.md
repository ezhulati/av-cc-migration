The accommodation at Vila Zambaku doesn't just promise comfort; it delivers it with style. Guests are treated to an air-conditioned apartment that boasts a harmonious blend of convenience and elegance. The space is thoughtfully designed with a bedroom, a bathroom equipped with fresh bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV and a dining space. The fully equipped kitchen invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like, while the balcony offers a charming city view, perfect for enjoying a morning coffee or an evening glass of wine.

Notably, Vila Zambaku is committed to providing a serene environment for all guests, as evidenced by its non-smoking and soundproof rooms. This ensures a peaceful stay, free from the hustle and bustle of the outside world.

For those arriving from afar, the Corfu International Airport is located 93 km away, making Vila Zambaku an accessible retreat for international travelers. With private parking on site and complimentary WiFi, guests are assured of both convenience and connectivity throughout their stay.

In summary, Vila Zambaku stands out as a delightful haven in Ksamil, offering a blend of tranquility, comfort, and accessibility that caters to the needs of modern travelers. Whether one is looking to explore the natural beauty and historical sites of the area or simply unwind by the beach, Vila Zambaku provides the perfect backdrop for a memorable getaway.
