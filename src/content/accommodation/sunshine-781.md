The highlight of Sunshine 781 is undoubtedly its spacious apartment, which boasts a terrace that opens up to breathtaking sea views. Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, the apartment features two cozy bedrooms, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and microwave, ensuring guests feel right at home. The inclusion of a bathroom with a shower, along with towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay in this non-smoking accommodation.

Located 38 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, Sunshine 781 offers more than just a beachside retreat. It is a gateway to exploring the rich cultural tapestry of the area. For those looking to venture further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is 41 km away, offering an adventure into the scenic heights, while the Rock of Kavaje, located just 7.1 km from the property, presents a fascinating natural attraction. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 32 km away, making Sunshine 781 an accessible haven for both international and domestic travelers seeking a tranquil escape by the sea.
