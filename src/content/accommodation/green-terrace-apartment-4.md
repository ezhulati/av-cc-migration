Designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, the apartment boasts a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a bathroom, and a living area that is both functional and stylish. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen offer the freedom to dine in, while the terrace presents an inviting space to enjoy panoramic city views.

Upon entering Green Terrace Apartment 4 through its private entrance, guests are greeted with a welcoming touch of complimentary fruits, setting the tone for a pleasant stay. The commitment to comfort extends to the apartment being non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life.

For added convenience, a minimarket on the premises caters to guests' everyday needs. The apartment's location is unbeatable, with proximity to notable attractions such as Skanderbeg Square, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the House of Leaves. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 15 km away, making Green Terrace Apartment 4 an ideal base for exploring Tirana's rich cultural landscape.
