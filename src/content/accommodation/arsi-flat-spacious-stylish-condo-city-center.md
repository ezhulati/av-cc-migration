The condo boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, each designed to provide a restful night's sleep after a day of exploring. The living space is anchored by a sleek flat-screen TV, perfect for those moments of relaxation. A fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, fridge, and washing machine, adds a touch of home, allowing guests to prepare meals at their leisure. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available, highlighting the property's attention to detail and commitment to guest comfort. Importantly, ARSI FLAT is a non-smoking accommodation, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all.

Situated just 49 km from the Port of Bar and 58 km from Podgorica Airport, the condo's location makes it an ideal base for travelers looking to explore the wider region or those in transit. Whether it's the allure of Shkodër's city center or the ease of travel to surrounding areas, ARSI FLAT offers a compelling blend of style, space, and strategic location.
