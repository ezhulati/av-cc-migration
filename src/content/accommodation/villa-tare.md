The kitchen is fully equipped, catering to guests' culinary needs with an oven, fridge, and even a washing machine, making it ideal for both short and long stays. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of convenience to the living experience.

For those who find solace in nature, the property's garden offers a tranquil retreat, a perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploration. Villa Tare's location is strategically positioned, with Ri Beach just 1 km away for those eager to explore the local coastline further. Vjetër Beach is also within easy reach, located 2.2 km from the apartment, offering another picturesque spot for relaxation and leisure.

Significantly, the apartment is not too far removed from cultural landmarks, with Kuzum Baba and Independence Square only a short drive away, making it an excellent base for guests keen on exploring the historical aspects of Vlorë. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 152 km away, ensures that Villa Tare is both accessible and a compelling choice for those visiting the beautiful coast of Albania.
