Each room in the villa complex is thoughtfully equipped with air conditioning, ensuring comfort regardless of the season. For those who value privacy and convenience, free private parking and complimentary WiFi are available, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers. The attention to detail is evident in the accommodations, with all units featuring a flat-screen TV for entertainment needs. Bathrooms come standard with a bidet, and select rooms elevate the living experience with a fully equipped kitchen, including an oven and a fridge, catering to the needs of those who prefer to prepare their own meals.

For guests looking to explore the surroundings, Guest House Tian offers a car rental service, providing an easy way to venture out to nearby attractions. The villa is conveniently located just 3.5 km from the historic Durres Amphitheatre and a short drive from the intriguing House of Leaves. Additionally, its proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, which is 31 km away, makes it an accessible option for international travelers.

Guest House Tian represents an excellent choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of history in their accommodations. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beach or the cultural exploration in Durrës, this villa serves as a perfect base for an unforgettable stay.
