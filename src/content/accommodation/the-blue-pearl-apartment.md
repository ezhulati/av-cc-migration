Guests at The Blue Pearl Apartment are treated to a seamless blend of modern comforts and natural beauty. The apartment boasts a well-appointed balcony that offers serene garden views, creating an ideal backdrop for relaxation. Inside, the space is thoughtfully designed with a cozy bedroom, a spacious living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a stovetop. The inclusion of an en-suite bathroom with a shower adds to the convenience, ensuring a comfortable stay. Notably, the property maintains a non-smoking environment, catering to the preferences of health-conscious guests.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests enjoy complimentary access to high-speed WiFi and the benefit of free private parking. For those looking to explore the area or needing assistance with travel arrangements, The Blue Pearl Apartment provides valuable services such as airport transfers and car rental options.

Beyond the comforts of the apartment, the location serves as a gateway to explore notable attractions. The Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is located 42 km away, offering an adventurous escape with breathtaking views, while the Rock of Kavaje, a mere 6.8 km from the property, presents a unique natural landmark worth visiting. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being just 32 km away makes The Blue Pearl Apartment an excellent choice for both short and extended stays in Durrës.
