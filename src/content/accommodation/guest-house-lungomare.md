The charm of Guest House Lungomare is further accentuated by its thoughtful amenities, including a flat-screen TV for entertainment and the provision of towels and bed linen, ensuring a hassle-free stay. The guest house's allure is magnified by captivating garden views, inviting guests to unwind in the peaceful surroundings.

For those who appreciate the beauty of nature, the property's garden offers a perfect spot to relax and rejuvenate. Situated merely 600 meters from the pristine Vlore Beach, the guest house presents an ideal base for beach lovers. Moreover, the proximity to Vjetër Beach and Ri Beach, located 1.9 km and 2.1 km away respectively, provides ample opportunities for guests to explore the scenic coastline.

Accessibility is seamlessly addressed, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 152 km away, ensuring that Guest House Lungomare is within reach for international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Vlorë.
