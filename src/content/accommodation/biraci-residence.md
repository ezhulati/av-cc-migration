Guests at Biraci Residence are treated to a seamless blend of modern amenities and home-like comfort across all units. Each apartment is equipped with a kettle, ensuring a warm cup is never out of reach. The presence of a balcony in every unit invites guests to enjoy their meals al fresco or simply bask in the serene sea vistas. Entertainment and comfort are guaranteed with the inclusion of a cable flat-screen TV and air conditioning, while the provision of a wardrobe and a private bathroom in each unit adds a touch of privacy and convenience.

Connectivity is a breeze at Biraci Residence, with free WiFi enveloping the property, ensuring guests stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventures in Ksamil Beach, located just 200 meters away. For those arriving from afar, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km from the aparthotel, making Biraci Residence an accessible retreat for international travelers.

Biraci Residence stands out as a distinguished choice for those in pursuit of a tranquil getaway, where the beauty of Ksamil's shores meets the comfort of modern living.
