Accommodations at Seashell Beach Rooms are thoughtfully designed to enhance guest comfort, featuring seating areas, flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, and private bathrooms equipped with hairdryers and bidets. Select rooms boast city views, but all are adorned with balconies, air conditioning, and desks, catering to both leisure and business travelers.

Morning risers are greeted with a variety of breakfast options, ranging from continental to Full English/Irish and Italian, promising a delightful start to the day. The hotel's prime location offers easy access to popular attractions such as Durres Beach, Golem Beach, and the Rock of Kavaje, making it an ideal base for exploring the area's rich offerings.

For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 36 km away, with the hotel providing a paid airport shuttle service for seamless transfers. Seashell Beach Rooms stands out as a top choice for visitors to Durrës, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and warm hospitality.
