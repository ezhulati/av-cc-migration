The accommodations at Apartments Margarita are thoughtfully designed to ensure a restful experience. Each unit boasts a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, and a private bathroom equipped with modern necessities including a hair dryer and bidet. The highlight for many, however, is the balcony that comes with each apartment, offering an outdoor dining area and breathtaking mountain views, perfect for those serene moments at sunrise or sunset.

For families, the children's playground is a delightful addition, ensuring the little ones are entertained. The convenience of a 24-hour front desk and currency exchange service on-site further adds to the seamless experience Apartments Margarita promises.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, guests are encouraged to explore the surrounding area, which is rich with attractions. Coco Beach, Bora Bora Beach, and Sunset Beach are all within close proximity, offering a variety of beach experiences. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Apartments Margarita an accessible retreat for international travelers.

In essence, Apartments Margarita is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that offers a tranquil escape, with all the comforts of home and the added luxury of a beachside location. Whether it's relaxing in the garden, enjoying the private beach, or exploring the nearby attractions, there's something for everyone at this esteemed property.
