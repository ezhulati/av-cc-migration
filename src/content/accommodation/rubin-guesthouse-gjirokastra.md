The guesthouse boasts a generously sized apartment that is a haven of peace, with its balcony offering serene street views. The apartment is well-appointed, featuring two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and an oven for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The presence of a bathroom with a walk-in shower adds to the comfort, while the inclusion of towels and bed linen ensures a hassle-free stay. Guests can also relax in the seating area or enjoy the warmth of the fireplace, making it a perfect setting for both leisure and relaxation.

Situated 82 km from Ioannina Airport, Rubin Guesthouse Gjirokastra is conveniently located for travelers seeking a blend of convenience and charm during their stay in Gjirokastër.
