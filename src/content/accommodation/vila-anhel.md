The accommodations at Vila Anhel are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each unit is equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV boasting an array of cable channels. The convenience of a kitchenette, complete with a fridge, stovetop, and essential kitchenware, alongside a kettle, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals. The dining area serves as a perfect spot for enjoying home-cooked meals. Private bathrooms are well-appointed with a hair dryer, shower, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a refreshing start to the day. To add to the comfort, bed linen and towels are graciously provided in each unit.

Beyond the confines of their rooms, guests are invited to unwind in the serene garden, offering a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Located a mere 800 meters from Coco Beach and less than a kilometer from the famed Ksamil Beach 7, Vila Anhel positions itself as an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 93 km away, making Vila Anhel an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers.

In summary, Vila Anhel stands out as a top choice for those in search of a comfortable, well-equipped stay in Ksamil, promising an unforgettable experience with its blend of convenience, comfort, and accessibility.
