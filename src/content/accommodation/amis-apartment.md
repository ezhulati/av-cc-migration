Equipped with complimentary WiFi, the apartment ensures a seamless stay with its array of modern amenities including a television, washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge. The added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy for guests.

The option to unwind in the garden adds a touch of tranquility to the overall experience at Ami's Apartment. Its strategic location not only provides easy access to Saranda Bay Beach and Maestral Beach, both within a 2.5 km radius but also ensures connectivity with the wider region, thanks to the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 98 km away.

For those in pursuit of a blend of relaxation, convenience, and exploration, Ami's Apartment stands out as an accommodation of choice in Sarandë.
