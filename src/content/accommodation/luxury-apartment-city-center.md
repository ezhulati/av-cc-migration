The apartment complex is a short distance from significant landmarks, including the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 4.5 km away, and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, just 1.7 km from the doorstep. For those looking to explore the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the area, the Rock of Kavaje is accessible at 43 km.

Accommodations at Luxury Apartment City Center are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each unit boasts air conditioning for comfort, a private bathroom for convenience, and a well-equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher, oven, microwave, and toaster. The attention to detail extends to the provision of a coffee machine and a wardrobe in all units, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. For an additional touch of ease, towels and linens are available upon request.

Connectivity is seamless with complimentary WiFi throughout the property, and for guests arriving by car, private parking is available for a nominal fee.

The location is unbeatable for culture enthusiasts and shoppers alike, with the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, National Museum of History Albania, and Toptani Shopping Centre all within easy reach. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 14 km away, making Luxury Apartment City Center an ideal base for exploring Tirana and beyond.
