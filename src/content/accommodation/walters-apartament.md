Beyond the private comforts, Walters Apartment extends its hospitality through shared spaces, including a kitchen and a lounge area, fostering a sense of community among guests. The property's strategic location places it a mere 6.1 km from the Rock of Kavaje and an engaging 6.7 km from the historical Durres Amphitheatre, offering a blend of leisure and cultural exploration. Further afield, the apartment is conveniently positioned 39 km from the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the vibrant Skanderbeg Square, with the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car 42 km away, inviting guests to venture into the scenic heights of the area.

For those arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 33 km from the apartment, ensuring a smooth transition to and from this inviting haven. Walters Apartment stands out as a non-smoking establishment, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for all guests. This property not only promises a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of Durrës and its surroundings.
