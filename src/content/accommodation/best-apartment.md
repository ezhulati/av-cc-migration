The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, equipped with a bedroom, bathroom, and essential amenities including bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to guests' culinary needs, while the terrace, offering mountain views, provides a perfect backdrop for relaxation. The added luxury of a patio with lake views and outdoor furniture enhances the stay, ensuring memorable moments of tranquility. The feature of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to privacy.

For convenience, Best Apartment includes a 24-hour front desk, lift access, and currency exchange services. Leisure activities are well catered for, with an on-site bar and a bicycle rental service, inviting guests to explore the surroundings at their own pace.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the Church of St. John at Kaneo and the Cave Church Archangel Michael, located 39 km and 41 km away, respectively. With Ohrid Airport just 45 km away, Best Apartment presents itself as an accessible retreat for both leisure and adventure in the heart of Pogradec.
