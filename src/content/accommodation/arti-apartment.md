This generously sized apartment is designed to cater to every need, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a modern bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living area that boasts a flat-screen TV with a variety of cable channels. The dining space flows seamlessly into a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests the freedom to craft their own culinary experiences. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony, offering breathtaking mountain views, an ideal backdrop for morning coffees or evening relaxation.

Attention to detail is evident in the thoughtful amenities provided, including air conditioning for those warmer days, a bidet, and a dressing room for added convenience. The apartment's design prioritizes guest comfort, with soundproofing ensuring a peaceful stay and a private entrance offering that extra sense of seclusion.
