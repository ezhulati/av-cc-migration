The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting air-conditioned interiors that open up to a balcony offering sweeping views of the sea and cityscape. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and toaster, making in-stay dining both convenient and enjoyable. The living space is thoughtfully designed with a comfortable seating area and a TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The bathroom is modern and well-appointed, featuring a walk-in shower, while towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

Adding to the appeal is the apartment's commitment to a smoke-free environment, coupled with the added benefit of a private entrance, ensuring utmost privacy and peace for guests. For those looking to venture further, Butrint National Park is accessible at 16 km, offering a glimpse into the area's rich history and natural beauty. Moreover, with Ioannina Airport 96 km away, the accommodation serves as a convenient gateway for international travelers.

Albania Dream Holidays Accommodation stands out as an exemplary choice for those seeking to immerse themselves in the charm and vibrancy of Sarandë, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views.
