The rooms at Apartment Artemis are thoughtfully appointed with essential amenities including a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom, along with the added touches of bed linen and towels for a hassle-free stay. For those who prefer to cater to their culinary needs, select units feature a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, a toaster, and a stovetop, alongside standard conveniences like a kettle. Additionally, the presence of heating facilities in each unit guarantees a cozy and comfortable environment, regardless of the season.

Situated 144 km from Ioannina Airport, Apartment Artemis not only offers ease of access for travelers but also serves as a perfect base to explore the enchanting surroundings of Himare. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beaches or the comfort of the well-equipped accommodations, this apartment complex promises an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
