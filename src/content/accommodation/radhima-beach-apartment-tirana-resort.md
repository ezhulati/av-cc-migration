This property prides itself on offering guests a hassle-free experience, providing complimentary WiFi and on-site parking without any additional charges. The commitment to a smoke-free environment further enhances the quality of stay for all guests, ensuring a clean and healthy atmosphere.

Accommodation at Radhima Beach Apartment-Tirana Resort is generously sized, featuring a two-bedroom apartment that boasts a balcony with breathtaking sea views. The living space is thoughtfully designed with comfort in mind, including a cozy living room, a television for entertainment, and an equipped kitchen complete with a fridge. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds an extra layer of convenience.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot to unwind after a day of adventures. The resort's location also makes it an ideal base for outdoor enthusiasts. Whether it's cycling along the scenic coast or hiking through the nearby trails, there's no shortage of activities to engage in. Additionally, guests can take advantage of the serene garden for a moment of relaxation.

Situated 16 km from both Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, the resort offers a peaceful retreat that's conveniently close to some of the area's notable attractions. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 165 km away, making Radhima Beach Apartment-Tirana Resort an accessible option for both domestic and international visitors looking for a tranquil beachfront escape.
