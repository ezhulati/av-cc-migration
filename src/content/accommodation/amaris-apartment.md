The apartment itself is a testament to modern living and convenience, featuring a single bedroom, a bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower, and a dressing room, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests. The living space is adorned with bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment, a dining area for intimate meals, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a balcony that offers breathtaking sea views. Designed with the guests' health in mind, the apartment is both allergy-free and strictly non-smoking.

Situated 42 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just 7.2 km from the Rock of Kavaje, Amaris Apartment is perfectly located for explorers keen to discover the area's natural beauty. For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 32 km away, making Amaris Apartment an ideal choice for travelers seeking both convenience and a touch of luxury by the sea.
