Guests are welcomed into a private haven, with each apartment boasting its own entrance, ensuring a seamless and personalized experience. The accommodations at Vila Megi-Rent Room are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms, complimentary private parking, and high-speed WiFi access at no extra cost.

The living spaces are thoughtfully appointed, with each unit presenting a balcony that invites natural light and fresh air. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, alongside a dedicated dining area, allows guests the freedom to prepare and enjoy their meals in the comfort of their apartment. The seating area, anchored by a flat-screen TV, provides a cozy nook for relaxation. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which are outfitted with a bidet and slippers for added comfort.

In a commitment to creating a welcoming environment for all, the apartments at Vila Megi-Rent Room are meticulously maintained as allergy-free zones. The provision of bed linen and towels in each unit underscores the establishment's dedication to guest convenience and satisfaction.

Located 3.6 km from the iconic Kuzum Baba and with the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 150 km away, Vila Megi-Rent Room serves as an ideal base for exploring Vlorë and beyond. This property not only promises a stay marked by tranquility and ease but also positions itself as a gateway to the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of the region.
