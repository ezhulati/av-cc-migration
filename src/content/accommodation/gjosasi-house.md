The accommodation boasts an air-conditioned environment, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. It features a well-equipped kitchen with a fridge, a cozy dining area, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The addition of a garden and terrace offers guests a peaceful spot to relax and soak in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

For those flying in, Ioannina Airport is located 114 km away, making Gjosasi House an accessible option for international and domestic visitors alike.
