Following a recent renovation, the apartment boasts proximity to historical and cultural landmarks, including being just 1 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and a short stroll, 800 metres, to the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument. For those intrigued by the allure of the Adriatic, the Rock of Kavaje is 46 km away, presenting an opportunity for a memorable day trip.

Guests will find the apartment generously spacious, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a fridge. Convenience is further enhanced with the inclusion of a washing machine and a dedicated dressing room, offering ample space for guests to store and change their attire. The bathroom is modern and includes a bidet, with towels and bed linen readily available for a comfortable stay.

The apartment's location is ideally positioned for exploring Tirana's rich tapestry of attractions, with Saint Paul Cathedral, the iconic Pyramid of Tirana, and the Toptani Shopping Centre all within easy reach. For those arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 17 km from the property, ensuring a smooth transition to and from this captivating city.

Appartamento con due camere da letto stands out as a meticulously designed space that caters to the needs of modern travelers, making it an excellent choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the cultural heartbeat of Tirana.
