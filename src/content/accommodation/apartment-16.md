The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It boasts a single, elegantly furnished bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen ready for culinary adventures, and a bathroom that caters to all the essentials. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with a variety of cable channels, ensuring guests are never short of options for relaxation.

Situated 17 kilometers from the ancient Durres Amphitheatre and close to the evocative House of Leaves, Apartment 16 serves as an ideal base for those wishing to delve into Albania's rich history and cultural heritage. Moreover, with Golem Beach just 1.7 kilometers away, guests have the luxury of choosing between a tranquil beach day or an insightful trip to nearby historical landmarks.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 42 kilometers away, making Apartment 16 a convenient choice for international travelers. This non-smoking property not only promises a memorable stay by the sea but also an opportunity to immerse oneself in the local culture and history of Golem and its surroundings.
