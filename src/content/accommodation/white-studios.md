Each accommodation at WHITE STUDIOS is thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with an array of cable channels, and complimentary WiFi to keep guests connected. The convenience of in-room amenities such as a toaster, kettle, and a selection of bed linen and towels adds a touch of home. The private bathrooms, equipped with a shower and hairdryer, ensure a comfortable and refreshing stay.

For those desiring a picturesque view, select units boast terraces that offer breathtaking sea vistas, allowing guests to unwind in the beauty of their surroundings. The added charm of outdoor furniture invites moments of relaxation and contemplation under the open sky.

Situated 143 km from Ioannina Airport, WHITE STUDIOS presents an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers. With its blend of family-friendly accommodations and the allure of the nearby beaches, this aparthotel stands as a testament to the beauty and hospitality that awaits on this stretch of coast.
