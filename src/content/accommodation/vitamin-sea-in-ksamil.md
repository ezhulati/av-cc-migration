This apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest but a space to live, featuring a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, and a well-equipped kitchen with modern amenities such as a toaster and a fridge. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures guests have entertainment at their fingertips, while the air-conditioning provides a cool retreat from the warm Mediterranean climate. The bathroom is well-appointed with a shower, catering to the needs of modern travelers.

One of the highlights of this property is its terrace, offering breathtaking sea views that promise to enchant guests from dawn till dusk. The balcony, adorned with outdoor furniture, overlooks a lush garden, providing a perfect backdrop for a morning coffee or an evening unwind. The added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy, making guests feel like they have their own personal oasis.

For those concerned about connectivity and convenience, Vitamin Sea does not disappoint. Guests enjoy the perks of complimentary WiFi and private parking, ensuring a hassle-free stay from arrival to departure. Although the nearest airport, Corfu International Airport, is 93 km away, the journey is well worth the distance, promising an escape that combines the tranquility of a beachside retreat with the accessibility of city attractions.

Vitamin Sea in Ksamil is more than just an accommodation; it's a gateway to a memorable experience, offering the perfect blend of privacy, comfort, and scenic beauty.
