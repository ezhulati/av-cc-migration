This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its air-conditioned spaces, including two well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and two modern bathrooms. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures guests can enjoy a cozy night in after a day of sightseeing.

One of the standout features of DeanSiar Apartment is the balcony, which offers a private outdoor space for guests to relax and unwind. The convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi throughout the property adds to the hassle-free experience, ensuring that guests can stay connected and navigate the area with ease.

For those interested in local culture and history, the proximity to Kuzum Baba, just 2.5 km away, provides an excellent opportunity for exploration. Furthermore, the property's non-smoking policy ensures a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

Situated 149 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, DeanSiar Apartment is accessible for international travelers, making it an appealing choice for both short and long stays in Vlorë.
