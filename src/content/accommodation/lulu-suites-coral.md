The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and functionality, boasting air-conditioned spaces complemented by a private balcony that invites guests to bask in the serene environment. Connectivity is never an issue, thanks to complimentary WiFi access throughout the premises. For those arriving by car, the convenience of on-site parking is a welcome feature, and the availability of car hire services opens up endless possibilities for exploration and adventure in the surrounding area.

Accommodation at Lulu Suites - Coral is generously proportioned, featuring a spacious bedroom designed to ensure restful nights. The living area is equipped with a flat-screen TV, offering a range of streaming services for entertainment at any time of day. A fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, allows guests the freedom to prepare meals as they wish, while the inclusion of a washing machine adds an extra layer of convenience for longer stays. The apartment also includes two well-appointed bathrooms, each with a bidet, ensuring privacy and comfort for all guests.

Outdoor dining is elevated to a new level with the property's dedicated area, perfect for enjoying meals under the open sky. For those looking to venture further, Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are within accessible distances, offering unique experiences and breathtaking views. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 37 km away, makes Lulu Suites - Coral an ideal choice for both international travelers and those looking to explore the local area.

In summary, Lulu Suites - Coral stands out as a sophisticated haven for guests desiring a blend of leisure and exploration in the heart of Golem.
