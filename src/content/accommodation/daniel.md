This one-bedroom apartment doesn't skimp on amenities. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a washing machine for longer stays, and a fully equipped kitchen that even includes a minibar for those late-night cravings or early morning refreshments.

Located merely 2.6 km from the vibrant Skanderbeg Square, Daniel Apartment offers an excellent base for exploring the city's cultural heart. For those looking to venture further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car provides breathtaking views and is only 7.4 km away. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 13 km from the apartment, ensures a hassle-free arrival and departure for international guests. Daniel Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for both its strategic location and its comprehensive array of in-room amenities.
