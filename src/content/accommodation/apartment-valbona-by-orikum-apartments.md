This air-conditioned haven provides more than just a place to rest; it's a spacious sanctuary equipped with two bedrooms, ensuring ample space for families or groups. The living area, adorned with a flat-screen TV, invites guests to unwind in comfort, while the fully equipped kitchen caters to culinary needs with an oven, fridge, washing machine, and stovetop. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience, making it feel like a home away from home.

Privacy is paramount at Apartment Valbona, with a private entrance that underscores the accommodation's commitment to a secluded and intimate guest experience. The added benefits of complimentary WiFi and private parking on site further enhance the appeal, ensuring that every aspect of the stay is seamless.

For those venturing from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 168 km away, making the apartment an accessible escape for international travelers seeking the tranquility and beauty of Orikum.
