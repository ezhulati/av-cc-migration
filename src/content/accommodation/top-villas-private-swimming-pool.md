Each villa within the complex is a testament to modern living, equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels for entertainment. The inclusion of a fully furnished kitchen, dining area, safety deposit box, and a private bathroom with a bidet, hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries underscores the attention to detail in ensuring a comfortable stay. The presence of an oven, microwave, toaster, and coffee machine adds a touch of home, while the option to dine al fresco elevates the experience.

For those looking to make the most of the sunny weather, the property's barbecue facilities are a delightful bonus. Adventure seekers will appreciate the opportunity to explore the surrounding area through diving and cycling, with car rental services readily available for convenience.

Situated a mere 30 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and 24 km from the Rock of Kavaje, Top Villas Private Swimming Pool serves as an ideal base for exploring the local attractions. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 19 km away, ensures ease of travel for international guests. This villa complex stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of leisure and exploration in a picturesque setting.
