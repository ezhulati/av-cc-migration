Each apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring a private balcony that invites guests to unwind with a view. The heart of each unit is a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a refrigerator, making self-catering a breeze. The living area, adorned with a flat-screen TV and comfortable seating, provides a cozy retreat after a day of sun and surf. Convenience is further enhanced with the inclusion of a washing machine and a sleek, private bathroom equipped with a bath and hairdryer. For those who need to stay connected or get some work done, air conditioning and a dedicated desk space are standard in all units.

Situated a mere 2.4 km from the tranquil Mali I Robit Beach and within easy reach of the historic Skanderbeg Square, 44 km away, Armando Apartments serves as an ideal base for exploring the local area. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 38 km away, adds to the appeal for international travelers looking for a seamless travel experience.

Armando Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering both the privacy of a personal apartment and the convenience of prime beachfront location.
