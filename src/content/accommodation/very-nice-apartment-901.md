Guests are welcomed into a sophisticated air-conditioned one-bedroom apartment, thoughtfully furnished with a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals. The living space is designed for relaxation, featuring a cozy seating area and a flat-screen TV for entertainment.

For explorers eager to venture beyond the comforts of their apartment, a car rental service is readily available, offering a perfect opportunity to discover the local area at one's own pace. The iconic Skanderbeg Square lies 45 km away, presenting a cultural excursion, while the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 48 km from the apartment, offers an adventure with breathtaking views. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 39 km away, with the apartment providing an option for a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures.

Very Nice Apartment 901 emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and the allure of the Albanian coast.
