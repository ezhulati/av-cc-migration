Guests at Qielli Mes Pallateve are treated to the luxury of complimentary WiFi and private parking, a rarity in the bustling cityscape. The property prides itself on offering a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests. Situated merely 4.8 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and 2.8 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, it serves as an ideal base for those looking to immerse themselves in the city's rich history and natural beauty.

The accommodation itself is a testament to modern living, featuring air-conditioned spaces that promise a serene retreat from the city's hustle and bustle. Each apartment is thoughtfully designed with a bedroom, a fully equipped kitchenette for those who love to whip up their own meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are well catered to with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

For those keen on exploring, Qielli Mes Pallateve is conveniently located near several of Tirana's most sought-after attractions, including the National Museum of History Albania, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the House of Leaves. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 13 km away, adds to the appeal for international travelers looking for an accessible and hassle-free experience in Albania's vibrant capital.

In summary, Qielli Mes Pallateve stands as a beacon of comfort and convenience, offering a perfect blend of modern amenities and prime location for travelers eager to explore the best of Tirana.
