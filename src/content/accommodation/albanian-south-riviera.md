The property is conveniently located within proximity to popular attractions, including Radhimë Beach and Nettuno Beach, which are just 1.1 km and 1.5 km away, respectively. For the convenience of its guests, Albanian South Riviera is equipped with modern amenities such as an elevator, round-the-clock security, and currency exchange services.

Each air-conditioned unit at this esteemed apartment complex is designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, and a balcony that offers a personal outdoor space. Additionally, a microwave in each unit ensures that guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

Understanding the needs of its guests, Albanian South Riviera goes the extra mile by offering packed lunches, perfect for those planning to explore the surrounding area or embark on day trips.

For beach enthusiasts, Orikum Beach is a mere 2.8 km away, while the historic Kuzum Baba can be found 16 km from the property. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 165 km away, making Albanian South Riviera an accessible and attractive option for both local and international visitors.
