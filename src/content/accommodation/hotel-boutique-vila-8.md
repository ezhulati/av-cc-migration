Each room at Hotel Boutique Vila 8 is thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Work and relaxation are equally catered to with well-appointed desks and air conditioning in every room. The inclusion of a private bathroom, equipped with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, adds a touch of luxury to the stay. For those who delight in the small pleasures, select rooms boast balconies, offering a private space to unwind. The attention to detail extends to the provision of crisp bed linen and plush towels, enhancing the overall experience.

Located just a short drive from the vibrant Durres Beach and the historic Skanderbeg Square, Hotel Boutique Vila 8 serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich tapestry of Durrës. For guests arriving from afar, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 34 km away, makes for a convenient travel connection.

Hotel Boutique Vila 8 emerges as a beacon of comfort and elegance, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Durrës.
