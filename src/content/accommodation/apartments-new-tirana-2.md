A stone's throw from cultural landmarks, the apartment finds itself 1.3 km from the evocative Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, and a leisurely stroll from the House of Leaves. History enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the National Museum of History Albania, a mere 16-minute walk away, while those keen on exploring the life of Enver Hoxha can find his former residence just 1.2 km from the doorstep.

The living space is thoughtfully designed, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for culinary adventures, and a bathroom that caters to rejuvenation routines. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

For those eager to immerse themselves in local culture, Rinia Park, Et'hem Bey Mosque, and the iconic Reja - The Cloud are conveniently located near the apartment, inviting guests to discover Tirana's vibrant community life. Moreover, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 14 km away, ensures seamless travel arrangements for international guests.

Apartments New Tirana 2 stands out as an exemplary choice for those looking to experience the dynamic city of Tirana, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural immersion.
