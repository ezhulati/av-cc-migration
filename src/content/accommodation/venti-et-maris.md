Each room at Venti et Maris is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning and free WiFi to ensure a stay that combines relaxation with connectivity. The inclusion of a TV and a fridge in every room adds a touch of home, while select accommodations boast captivating city views, allowing guests to soak in the vibrant atmosphere of Ksamil from the comfort of their rooms.

The hotel's strategic location places guests at the doorstep of Ksamil's most sought-after attractions, including the pristine Ksamil Beach, the lively Coco Beach, and the picturesque Bora Bora Beach. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 94 km away, making Venti et Maris an ideal base for both local and international travelers.

With its combination of thoughtful amenities, prime location, and attentive service, Venti et Maris stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay in Ksamil.
