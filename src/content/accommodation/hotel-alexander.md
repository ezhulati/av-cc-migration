Accommodations at Hotel Alexander are especially welcoming for families, offering spacious family rooms designed with both comfort and style in mind. Mornings here start on a high note with a delightful buffet breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation.

The dedication of Hotel Alexander's staff is evident around the clock. The 24-hour front desk is always at the ready, offering insightful information to enhance guests' stays and ensure a seamless experience in Ksamil.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the area, Butrint National Park is a mere 2.8 km away, promising an easy escape to one of Albania's most cherished landscapes.
