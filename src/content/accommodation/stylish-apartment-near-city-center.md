The apartment is a haven of modern amenities, featuring complimentary WiFi that keeps guests connected, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine for convenience. The kitchen is thoughtfully equipped with a fridge and stovetop, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen ensures a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 15 km away, adds to the seamless travel experience for international guests.

Stylish Apartment Near City Center stands out as an exemplary choice for visitors to Tirana, offering both the tranquility of a private residence and the excitement of city exploration right at its doorstep.
