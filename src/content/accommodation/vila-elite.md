The hotel itself is a haven of comfort, boasting a serene garden, BBQ facilities, and captivating mountain views that can be enjoyed from the terrace. Vila Elite prides itself on its commitment to guest satisfaction, offering round-the-clock front desk service and complimentary WiFi to ensure a seamless stay.

Accommodations at Vila Elite are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment. The inclusion of a kitchen and dining area in each unit adds a touch of home, while the private bathroom comes complete with complimentary toiletries, a bidet, and a shower. For added convenience, a fridge is provided in all units, catering to the needs of guests who prefer to prepare their own meals or store snacks.

For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Vila Elite offers a paid airport shuttle service to Corfu International Airport, located 92 km away. This service ensures a hassle-free connection for international travelers, making Vila Elite an accessible and appealing choice for visitors to Ksamil.
