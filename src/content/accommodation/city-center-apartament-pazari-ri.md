The accommodation boasts an air-conditioned apartment that is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It features a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven, ensuring guests feel at home. The bathroom is well-appointed with a bidet and slippers, enhancing the comfort of the stay. Notably, the apartment offers a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a private entrance to ensure guests' privacy.

For those looking to explore the city at their own pace, the apartment provides a convenient car rental service. Its proximity to popular attractions such as the Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, positions it as an ideal base for cultural excursions and shopping sprees. Moreover, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is merely 15 km away, making City Center Apartament, Pazari Ri a favorable option for both short and extended stays in Tirana.
