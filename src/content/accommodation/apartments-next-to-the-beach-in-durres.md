The air-conditioned apartments ensure a comfortable retreat from the warm Mediterranean climate, with family rooms available to accommodate groups of all sizes. Each unit boasts a private bathroom and complimentary WiFi, ensuring that guests' needs are met with the highest standards of hospitality. Select apartments further enhance the experience by offering terraces, allowing visitors to soak in the breathtaking surroundings in privacy.

Situated 39 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and a scenic drive from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 42 km away, guests have the opportunity to explore the rich cultural tapestry of the region. For those looking to delve into the natural beauty of Albania, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 6.1 km from the property, presenting a perfect excursion opportunity. Convenience is also paramount, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located just 33 km away, making Apartments Next to the Beach in Durres an ideal base for both leisure and exploration in this vibrant area.
