This beachfront gem boasts a spacious apartment equipped with modern amenities to ensure a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. Guests will find themselves in a haven featuring a bedroom with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven, and the convenience of a washing machine. The bathroom comes with a bidet, and for those in need, towels and linens are available upon request. The highlight, perhaps, is the outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals with a view.

Adding to the convenience, a minimarket on the premises means essentials are never far away. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 82 km away, making Seaside Sun and Fun an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers.

With its commitment to non-smoking, air-conditioned comfort, free private parking, and complimentary WiFi, this property doesn't just promise a stay by the sea; it offers a gateway to the tranquility and beauty of Velipojë, wrapped in the luxury of modern amenities.
