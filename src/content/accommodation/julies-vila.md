The property is distinguished by its lush garden surroundings, creating an oasis for guests seeking a peaceful escape. Each room at Julies Vila is a haven of comfort, boasting balconies that invite guests to bask in the serene atmosphere. The accommodations are equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms complete with hair dryers, ensuring a stay that marries comfort with convenience. For those in need, the guest house thoughtfully offers the option to include towels and linens for a nominal supplement, while the inclusion of a toaster in every unit adds a touch of home.

For adventurers eager to explore the natural beauty of the region, Butrint National Park lies just 2.8 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich history and breathtaking landscapes. Moreover, the guest house's location makes it an ideal base for travelers, with Corfu International Airport located 92 km away, ensuring easy access for international guests.

Julies Vila represents an ideal choice for families and travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of home, all within the beautiful setting of Ksamil's most sought-after beaches.
