Security and accessibility are paramount here, with around-the-clock security and an elevator enhancing guest experiences, alongside the benefit of complimentary WiFi across the premises. For those looking to explore the city or needing assistance with their travel arrangements, Miku Apartment facilitates both airport transfers and car rental services, making exploration a breeze.

The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living and modern amenities. Guests are treated to two elegantly appointed bedrooms and two bathrooms, ensuring privacy and comfort. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen cater to every need, while a balcony offering city views adds a touch of charm to the stay. It's a non-smoking environment, catering to those who prioritize wellness and clean living.

For quick essentials or a snack, the on-site minimarket is a convenient addition, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

Situated within proximity to notable landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, Miku Apartment offers an ideal base for those eager to delve into the rich history and vibrant culture of Tirana. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, the apartment stands as a beacon of comfort and convenience for both international and domestic travelers.
