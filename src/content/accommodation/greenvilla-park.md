Accommodations at GreenVilla Park are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each apartment is equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a cool retreat from the warm Mediterranean climate. The convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi access further enhances the guest experience. Living spaces are generously appointed with a terrace or, in some cases, a balcony or patio offering captivating city views. The presence of outdoor furniture invites guests to unwind in their private outdoor space. Inside, a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a refrigerator, and a cozy seating area provide the comforts of home, while a flat-screen TV and a washing machine add a touch of modern convenience. Private bathrooms are stocked with essentials, including bath amenities and slippers, ensuring a pampered stay. For added convenience, bed linen and towels are provided in all units.

The on-site minimarket is a notable amenity, offering guests the ease of accessing snacks and essentials without having to leave the comfort of the complex.

For those looking to explore beyond the property, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offers breathtaking views from 42 km away, and the Rock of Kavaje, a mere 4.7 km distance, presents a natural wonder worth visiting. GreenVilla Park is also conveniently located 33 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers.

In summary, GreenVilla Park presents a compelling choice for those seeking a harmonious blend of convenience, comfort, and accessibility in the heart of Durrës.
