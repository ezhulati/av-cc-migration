This air-conditioned apartment doesn't just boast a prime location; it offers guests a glimpse into city life with its stunning city views, all from the comfort of a private balcony. The inclusion of free WiFi and a non-smoking environment ensures a stay that combines modern necessities with health-conscious living.

Space is a luxury here, with two generously sized bedrooms providing ample room for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and a toaster, invites guests to prepare their meals, while the convenience of a washing machine and a bathroom equipped with a bidet adds to the comfort of the stay. The private entrance to the accommodation underscores the sense of exclusivity and privacy.

For those keen to explore, 2V1 - Myslym Shyri is ideally situated near several of Tirana's attractions, including the Clock Tower, Et'hem Bey Mosque, and the artistic installation, Reja - The Cloud. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is just 14 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

2V1 - Myslym Shyri emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of local flavor during their stay in Tirana.
