The apartment boasts a generously sized bedroom, ensuring a comfortable stay for its guests. Entertainment and relaxation are readily available, thanks to a flat-screen TV equipped with a variety of cable channels. For those who prefer to dine in, the kitchen does not disappoint. It is fully outfitted with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster, catering to every culinary need.

Guests will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of free WiFi, keeping them connected during their stay. Moreover, the commitment to a non-smoking environment ensures a fresh and welcoming atmosphere for all.

Conveniently located, Sweet Living Apartment 2 is just 58 km from Podgorica Airport, making it an accessible choice for travelers seeking comfort and convenience in Shkodër.
