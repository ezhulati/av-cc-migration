Spanning a generous layout, the apartment at Blue Bay features a terrace that invites relaxation, a cozy bedroom for restful nights, a welcoming living room for leisurely downtime, and a well-appointed kitchen for those inspired to cook with local ingredients. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complementing the comfortable and smoke-free environment.

For explorers eager to venture beyond the comfort of Blue Bay, the enchanting Butrint National Park lies just 15 km away, promising a day filled with natural beauty and historical wonders. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 95 km from the property, facilitates a hassle-free journey to this idyllic escape.
