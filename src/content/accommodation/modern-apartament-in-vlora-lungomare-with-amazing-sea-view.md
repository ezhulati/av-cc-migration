The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting a layout that includes one bedroom, two living rooms, and a terrace overlooking the sea. It's equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom complete with a bidet. The addition of a private entrance and soundproofing ensures that guests enjoy utmost privacy and tranquility during their stay.

Beyond the confines of this beachfront property, Vjetër Beach awaits at a distance of 2.8 km, offering a different flavor of the Albanian coast. For those intrigued by historical sites, Kuzum Baba is just 3.9 km away, presenting an opportunity to explore local heritage. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 153 km from the apartment, making it a feasible getaway destination.

With its combination of modern comforts, stunning views, and prime location, Modern Apartment in Vlora Lungomare is a top choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay in Vlorë.
