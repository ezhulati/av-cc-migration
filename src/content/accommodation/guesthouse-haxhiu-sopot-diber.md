The property prides itself on providing a range of amenities designed to enhance the guest experience, including complimentary private parking, a shared kitchen, and high-speed WiFi, ensuring both convenience and connectivity throughout the stay. The availability of a shuttle service, arranged by the attentive on-site staff, adds an extra layer of comfort for guests seeking to explore the surrounding area or requiring transportation assistance.

Accommodation at Guesthouse "Haxhiu" - Sopot, Diber, is characterized by its spacious layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room complete with a TV for entertainment, and a balcony that offers breathtaking mountain views. The villa is equipped with a kitchen that includes a fridge and kitchenware, catering to guests' culinary needs, alongside two bathrooms equipped with modern facilities such as a bidet, shower, washing machine, bathrobes, and slippers. The presence of a fireplace further enhances the homely atmosphere, making it an ideal setting for relaxation.

For those keen on outdoor activities, the villa's location in Bulqizë serves as a perfect base for hiking enthusiasts. Additionally, the property boasts a picnic area, providing an idyllic setting for guests to enjoy a day outdoors amidst nature.

Travelers looking for seamless connectivity will appreciate the villa's proximity to Ohrid Airport, located 83 km away, with the option of a paid airport shuttle service available for a hassle-free journey to and from the property.

In summary, Guesthouse "Haxhiu" - Sopot, Diber, offers a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it an appealing choice for travelers seeking a peaceful retreat with easy access to cultural sites and outdoor adventures.
