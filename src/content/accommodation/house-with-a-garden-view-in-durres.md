The property, which prides itself on being a smoke-free environment, is conveniently situated 48 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, providing an excellent starting point for adventurers looking to explore the scenic beauty of the area. The House of Leaves and the National Museum of History Albania are also accessible, both located 43 km away, adding to the list of nearby attractions that guests can enjoy.

This air-conditioned holiday home is designed with comfort in mind, featuring two cozy bedrooms that open up to a balcony offering picturesque garden views. The inclusion of towels and bed linen ensures a hassle-free stay, while the seating area and fireplace invite guests to unwind and relax in a homely atmosphere.

The property's garden is a highlight, offering a tranquil space for guests to relax and soak in the natural beauty of the surroundings. With the convenience of free WiFi and private parking, visitors will find everything they need for a memorable stay.

For those flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 29 km away, making the House with a Garden View in Durres an accessible and inviting destination for travelers seeking a blend of cultural exploration and serene relaxation.
