The accommodations at Vila Ervin Ksamil are designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Each apartment is a retreat featuring terraces that open up to breathtaking mountain views, ensuring guests start their day with a dose of natural beauty. The interiors are thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including satellite flat-screen TVs, dining areas, well-appointed kitchens, and private bathrooms complete with bidets and hair dryers. For those desiring a closer connection to the sea, select units offer balconies or patios that boast direct sea views, allowing guests to soak in the tranquil ambiance right from their rooms. Moreover, the provision of bed linen and towels in all units adds an extra layer of convenience for guests.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, Vila Ervin Ksamil offers a range of facilities to enhance the stay experience. Guests can enjoy leisurely moments in the lush garden, unwind at the bar, or engage in friendly barbecue gatherings, making it an ideal spot for both relaxation and socializing.

For explorers eager to discover the local beaches, Lori Beach and Bora Bora Beach are within easy reach, located just 1.1 km and 1.2 km away, respectively. Additionally, the property's proximity to Corfu International Airport, situated 92 km away, facilitates convenient travel arrangements for international guests.

In essence, Vila Ervin Ksamil emerges as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering a blend of scenic beauty, comfort, and accessibility, making it a compelling pick for families and solo travelers alike in search of a memorable Ksamil beachfront experience.
