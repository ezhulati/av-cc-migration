Each apartment at Golem is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a balcony that offers a personal outdoor space. The inclusion of air conditioning and heating guarantees a pleasant environment, regardless of the season. The living spaces are modern and inviting, with an open-plan dining and lounge area equipped with satellite TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, boasting an oven and stovetop for those who enjoy preparing their meals.

For those looking to relax without straying far, the shared swimming pool provides a serene oasis, while the bar serves as a cozy spot to enjoy a refreshing drink. Additionally, Golem Apartments facilitates bicycle and car rental services, making it effortless for guests to explore the surrounding area.

Situated 15 km from the vibrant Durrës city center, visitors have the opportunity to delve into local history at the Durrës Amphitheatre. The Port of Durrës, also 15 km away, offers a glimpse into the maritime life of the area. For those traveling by air, the convenience of being 40 km from Tirana International Airport adds to the appeal of Golem Apartments as a well-positioned base for both leisure and exploration.
