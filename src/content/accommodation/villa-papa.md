Positioned merely 400 meters from the inviting sands of Ksamil Beach, Villa Papa offers a unique ground-floor villa experience. The villa boasts three well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring ample space for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, caters to the culinary needs of guests, making it feel like a home away from home. The balcony, adorned with outdoor furniture, presents a splendid view of the sea, inviting guests to bask in the beauty of their surroundings. The addition of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy.

For those keen on immersing themselves in local culture and nightlife, Villa Papa is ideally located to offer walking tours and pub crawls in the vicinity. The property also boasts a lush garden, perfect for those moments of quiet contemplation or leisurely enjoyment.

Further enriching the appeal of Villa Papa is its proximity to Coco Beach, just 700 meters away, and the allure of Ksamil Beach 7, less than 1 km from the villa. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km away, making Villa Papa an accessible haven of relaxation and comfort.

In summary, Villa Papa stands as a testament to the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Ksamil.
