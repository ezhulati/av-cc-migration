Boasting breathtaking garden views, Appartement mer Ionienne is a mere 13 kilometers from the lush expanses of Butrint National Park and 47 kilometers from the historical marvels of Ancient Fanoti. The highlight, however, is its proximity to the tranquil Flamingo Beach, merely 100 meters away, ensuring that the soothing sounds of the sea are just a few steps from your doorstep.

This spacious, air-conditioned apartment is designed with the discerning traveler in mind, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms and direct access to a balcony that offers unparalleled sea views. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a fully equipped kitchen, making it easy to whip up a meal while basking in the coastal ambiance. Entertainment is readily available with a featured TV, complementing the serene environment for a truly relaxing stay.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is available throughout the property, ensuring that guests can stay in touch with loved ones or manage work commitments seamlessly. Moreover, the commitment to a non-smoking environment underscores the property's dedication to providing a clean and healthy stay for all guests.

For those arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is located 97 kilometers away, making Appartement mer Ionienne an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers. Whether it's the allure of the beaches, the call of historical sites, or simply the need for a peaceful getaway, this apartment serves as the perfect base for an unforgettable Sarandë experience.
