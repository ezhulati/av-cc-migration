This apartment, strictly non-smoking, is perfectly positioned 1.8 km from the lively Saranda City Beach, offering guests a serene retreat close to the city's bustling center. The accommodation boasts a thoughtfully designed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their meals, and a modern bathroom, ensuring a comfortable and self-sufficient stay. Entertainment needs are well catered for with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, complemented by free WiFi to keep guests connected.

For travelers arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 98 km from the apartment, making Relax Apts Saranda 2 an accessible haven for both local and international visitors. This apartment not only offers a place to rest but also serves as a perfect base from which to explore the rich tapestry of Sarandë's beaches, cultural sites, and natural beauty.
