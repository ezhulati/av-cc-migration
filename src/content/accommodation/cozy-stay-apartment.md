This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its thoughtful amenities and comforts. The space is well-appointed with a single bedroom, complemented by a flat-screen TV for entertainment needs. The kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with modern appliances including a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and fridge, catering to all culinary desires. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

Guests can also enjoy the convenience of an on-site bar, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The apartment's location is a gateway to discovering Tirana's cultural heartbeat, with the National Museum of History Albania, National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the intriguing House of Leaves all within close proximity. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, making Cozy Stay Apartment an ideal choice for both short visits and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
