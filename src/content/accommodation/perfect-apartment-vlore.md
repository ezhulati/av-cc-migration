The apartment itself is a testament to luxury living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a balcony with breathtaking mountain views. It features a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances such as a dishwasher and an oven. The inclusion of two bathrooms with walk-in showers adds a layer of convenience, while the private entrance sets the stage for an intimate and welcoming atmosphere. Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the thoughtful touch of wine or champagne and fresh fruits, making for a memorable start to their stay.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of high-quality bed linen, towels, and a laundry service, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free experience. Dining options are readily available at the on-site restaurant, which caters to a variety of tastes with its dinner, lunch, brunch, and cocktail offerings.

Families will find Perfect Apartment Vlore particularly appealing, thanks to its child-friendly amenities, including an indoor play area, outdoor play equipment, and a baby safety gate. The outdoor fireplace and picnic area present perfect settings for relaxation and making cherished memories.

Located just 1.5 km from Vjetër Beach and 152 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, this apartment complex is not only accessible but also serves as an ideal base for exploring the beauty of Vlorë. With its combination of luxurious accommodations, comprehensive amenities, and a stunning location, Perfect Apartment Vlore stands as a distinguished destination for discerning travelers.
