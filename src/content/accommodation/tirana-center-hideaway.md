The accommodations at Tirana Center Hideaway boast air-conditioned rooms with parquet flooring, adding a touch of elegance to the modern traveler's stay. Each unit is well-appointed with a fully equipped kitchen, including a fridge and dining area, ensuring guests feel right at home. Entertainment is readily available through flat-screen TVs equipped with streaming services, and the convenience of a private bathroom with shower and complimentary slippers enhances the overall guest experience. Free WiFi is accessible throughout the property, with select rooms offering the added luxury of a balcony, perfect for soaking in the city's ambiance.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of bed linen and towels in each apartment, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. For those looking to explore, the property is a stone's throw away from notable attractions such as the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, Clock Tower Tirana, and Et'hem Bey Mosque. The National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, National Museum of History Albania, and Toptani Shopping Centre are also within close proximity, making it effortless for guests to immerse themselves in the local culture and retail therapy.

Situated 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Tirana Center Hideaway facilitates ease of travel with its paid airport shuttle service, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible. This property not only promises a memorable stay but also serves as a gateway to the vibrant life and history of Tirana, making it a must-visit destination for discerning travelers.
