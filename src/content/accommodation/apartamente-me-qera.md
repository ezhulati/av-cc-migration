Apartamente me Qera doesn't just boast an enviable location; it offers a suite of amenities designed to make every stay as comfortable as possible. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free private parking, an essential for those exploring by car. Each apartment is a haven of modern comforts, featuring free WiFi, a washing machine, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of home. Air conditioning ensures a cool retreat from the warm Pogradec sun, while private bathrooms add an extra layer of privacy. Select units also offer balconies, providing a perfect spot for a morning coffee or evening relaxation.

Further afield, yet still within reach, the Church of St. John at Kaneo and the Cave Church Archangel Michael invite exploration, located 39 km and 41 km from the property, respectively. For guests traveling by air, the convenience of Ohrid Airport, just 45 km away, makes Apartamente me Qera an excellent choice for both short and extended stays in Pogradec. Whether it's the allure of historical sites or the promise of serene lake views, this property offers a strategic base from which to explore all that the region has to offer.
