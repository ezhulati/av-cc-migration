The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms and a modern bathroom, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests. The heart of the home is the fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dining area and toaster, making meal preparation a breeze. The adjacent living room, boasting a flat-screen TV, provides a cozy spot for relaxation after a day of sun and sand.

Guests will appreciate the thoughtful touches that make this apartment feel like home, including the provision of towels and bed linen. The added convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi allows for a worry-free vacation experience.

Situated 50 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, the Lovely Beach Front Apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the local area. For those interested in cultural excursions, the Rock of Kavaje and the Durres Amphitheatre are within easy reach, located 8.4 km and 19 km away, respectively.

With its beachfront location, offering direct access to a balcony where the sea breeze can be savored, this non-smoking property promises a memorable stay for beach lovers and explorers alike. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently 44 km away, ensuring smooth travel arrangements for international and domestic guests.
