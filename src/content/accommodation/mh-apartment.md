The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring a bedroom, a bathroom, and inclusive of bed linen and towels for a seamless stay. Entertainment needs are well catered to with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The dining area and a fully-equipped kitchen offer guests the freedom to dine in comfort, while the balcony presents an inviting cityscape view, adding a touch of tranquility to the urban experience.

Situated merely 1.2 km from the iconic Skanderbeg Square, MH Apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring Tirana's rich cultural tapestry. Attractions within easy reach include the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves, each offering a unique glimpse into the nation's heritage. For those traveling by air, the convenience is further amplified with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located just 13 km away, making MH Apartment a compelling choice for discerning travelers aiming to immerse themselves in the vibrancy of Tirana.
