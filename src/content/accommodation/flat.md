Designed with privacy and ease in mind, the apartment boasts a seamless private check-in and check-out process, alongside secure bicycle parking for those looking to explore the city on two wheels. The interior is thoughtfully appointed with a cozy bedroom, a well-equipped kitchenette featuring an oven and fridge, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a comfortable seating area, and a bathroom equipped with either a bath or shower. The added luxury of a private entrance ensures a secluded and personal experience for every guest.

For those eager to venture beyond the apartment, both bicycle and car rental services are readily available, providing the perfect opportunity to discover Tirana's hidden gems. The apartment's prime location offers easy access to the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, just 3 km away, and the picturesque Rock of Kavaje, located 43 km from the property. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is only 13 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

This apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the heart of Tirana, combining the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
