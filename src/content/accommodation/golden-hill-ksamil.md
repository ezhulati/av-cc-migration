Each room at Golden Hill Ksamil is thoughtfully appointed to ensure a restful stay. Guests can expect to find a well-equipped desk, a private bathroom with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, and the added comfort of air conditioning. For entertainment and relaxation, a flat-screen TV is standard in every room, while select accommodations also feature a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to unwind. The convenience of an in-room fridge adds a touch of home, ensuring refreshments are always within reach.

The hotel's prime location places guests within a mere kilometer of both Coco Beach and Ksamil Beach, making it an ideal base for exploring the local seaside attractions. For those venturing further, Corfu International Airport is located 94 km away, connecting travelers to and from this idyllic destination.

Golden Hill Ksamil stands out as a preferred choice for visitors to Ksamil, offering a blend of modern amenities and prime location, all wrapped up in a welcoming atmosphere that promises a memorable stay.
