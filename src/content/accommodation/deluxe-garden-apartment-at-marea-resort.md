The apartment itself is a spacious haven, accommodating guests with two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom complete with essential linens and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for endless entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen and dining area provide all the necessities for a home-cooked meal, which can be enjoyed on the patio overlooking the lush garden. For those chillier evenings, the fireplace offers a cozy retreat.

Privacy and security are paramount, with the apartment featuring a private entrance and round-the-clock security. The outdoor dining area invites guests to savor meals amidst the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Dining at the resort is a delightful experience, with the family-friendly restaurant serving a variety of meals from dinner and brunch to cocktails and high tea, catering to all tastes and preferences.

Families will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of outdoor play equipment and a baby safety gate, ensuring a stress-free stay for parents. Additionally, the convenience of an on-site car rental service opens up endless possibilities for exploration.

Located 49 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and just 7.2 km from the Rock of Kavaje, Deluxe Garden Apartment at Marea Resort is not only a sanctuary of relaxation but also a gateway to the rich cultural tapestry of the area. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 43 km away, making it a convenient option for both domestic and international travelers.
