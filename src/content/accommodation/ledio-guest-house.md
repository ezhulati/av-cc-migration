Guests at Ledio Guest House are treated to the comfort of air-conditioned accommodations, each boasting a fully equipped kitchen, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with luxurious bath, bathrobes, and slippers. The attention to detail extends to the provision of a desk in every unit, catering to the needs of both leisure and business travelers.

The property itself is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring a lush garden that invites guests to unwind in a peaceful setting. The addition of a private balcony, complimentary private parking, and free WiFi throughout the premises ensures a stay that is as convenient as it is relaxing.

Situated 38 km from Port Ohrid and a mere 39 km from the iconic Church of St. John at Kaneo, Ledio Guest House offers an ideal base for those eager to delve into the region's historical sites. With Ohrid Airport only 45 km away, accessibility is yet another highlight of this charming guest house.

Ledio Guest House stands out as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of exploration in the heart of Pogradec.
