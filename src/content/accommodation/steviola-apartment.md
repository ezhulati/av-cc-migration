The accommodation prides itself on providing a seamless stay, featuring air-conditioned units complemented by complimentary WiFi and the bonus of private parking on the premises. Families and groups will appreciate the spacious family rooms, designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Each unit boasts a private bathroom, complete with a bidet and hair dryer, ensuring a hassle-free experience. For guests who prefer to cater to their culinary needs, select rooms come with a fully equipped kitchen, including a handy toaster.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Steviola Apartment offers a bicycle rental service, inviting guests to explore the surrounding area at their own pace. Whether it's a leisurely ride to the nearby Rinia Park, a visit to the intriguing House of Leaves, or a quick jaunt to the Clock Tower Tirana, the apartment's location serves as the perfect base for adventure.

For those traveling to or from the city, the convenience of being just 16 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal of Steviola Apartment. This property stands out as an excellent choice for travelers looking for a blend of comfort, convenience, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local culture.
