Each room at Cathedral Boutique Hotel is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a balcony for those who appreciate a private outdoor space. The accommodations are well-appointed with modern necessities such as air conditioning, a desk for those needing to stay productive, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. Select rooms elevate the experience further with the addition of a patio, offering an ideal setting for a serene retreat. Moreover, all rooms boast a seating area, ensuring a comfortable and spacious environment for relaxation.

For travelers arriving by air, the proximity to Kastoria National Airport, located 70 km away, adds an element of convenience to the stay at Cathedral Boutique Hotel. This hotel stands out as a compelling choice for families and individuals alike, promising an experience that combines modern comforts with easy access to the region's natural and cultural attractions.
