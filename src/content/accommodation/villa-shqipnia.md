Accommodations at Villa Shqipnia are thoughtfully equipped to ensure a comfortable stay. Each room is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a desk for travelers who need to catch up on work, and a shared bathroom that maintains cleanliness and comfort. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV and bed linen in every room. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the terrace, offering a breathtaking mountain view that promises to start every morning on a high note. For added convenience, guests will find a wardrobe and a coffee machine in their rooms, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

The hostel also prides itself on offering a shared kitchen, allowing guests to prepare their meals and interact with fellow travelers, adding to the communal atmosphere that makes Villa Shqipnia stand out. Additionally, the availability of a currency exchange service on-site simplifies financial transactions for international guests, making their stay as seamless as possible.

Located 66 km from Podgorica Airport, Villa Shqipnia is conveniently positioned for travelers coming in or out of the country. This hostel is more than just a place to rest; it's a gateway to the rich culture and breathtaking landscapes of Shkodër, promising an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
