Guests at StartApart CityCenter 2 are treated to a range of thoughtful amenities designed to enhance their stay. The property is equipped with an elevator and round-the-clock security, ensuring a worry-free experience. Additionally, the convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout the premises allows guests to stay connected with ease.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days. It boasts a terrace equipped with an outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals with a view. Entertainment is readily available thanks to a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, catering to a variety of viewing preferences. Importantly, the accommodation maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

Practical needs are not overlooked, with a minimarket on-site providing essentials without the need to venture far. For added convenience, the property offers luggage storage space, room service, and currency exchange, catering to the diverse needs of its guests.

Located 33 kilometers from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, StartApart CityCenter 2 also offers a paid airport shuttle service, simplifying travel arrangements for arriving and departing guests.

In summary, StartApart CityCenter 2 presents a compelling option for travelers who value both the tranquility of a beachside retreat and the comforts of modern amenities. Its strategic location, coupled with a comprehensive suite of services, makes it a standout choice for an unforgettable stay.
