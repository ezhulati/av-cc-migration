Guests are greeted with impeccable service around the clock, thanks to the 24-hour front desk, alongside thoughtful amenities such as a lift and currency exchange services, catering to every need. The accommodations at Star Holiday Apartments are designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Each unit is outfitted with air conditioning, satellite TV, a fridge, and a kettle, alongside essentials like a shower, hair dryer, and a desk, making it a home away from home.

For those who enjoy the art of cooking, the fully equipped kitchenette, complete with a stovetop and kitchenware, allows for the preparation of meals just the way one likes. The promise of privacy and comfort is fulfilled with each apartment featuring a private bathroom and fresh bed linen, ensuring a restful night's sleep.

Situated 44 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 48 km from the scenic Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Star Holiday Apartments offers a perfect base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of the area. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 39 km away, the apartments present an ideal choice for travelers seeking both convenience and tranquility during their visit to Golem.
