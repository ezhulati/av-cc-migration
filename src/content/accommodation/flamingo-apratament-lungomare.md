The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a balcony that invites guests to bask in the serene views. Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, the apartment features a single bedroom, a cozy living room, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-cooked meal. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV, and for those who prefer a smoke-free environment, the entire accommodation is non-smoking.

For guests looking to explore the local area, bike hire is conveniently offered on-site, providing an eco-friendly option to discover the nearby attractions. Additionally, the presence of an on-site bar adds a social dimension, allowing guests to unwind with a drink without having to venture far.

Those with a penchant for exploring further afield will appreciate the car rental service available, making visits to the nearby Vjetër Beach or the historic Kuzum Baba effortlessly accessible. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 153 km away, ensuring a relatively straightforward journey to this coastal retreat.

Flamingo Apartment Lungomare stands out not only for its exceptional location but also for its thoughtful array of amenities, making it an attractive option for those looking to experience the best of Vlorë's coastal charm.
