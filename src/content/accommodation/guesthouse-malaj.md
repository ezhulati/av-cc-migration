Upon entering the apartment, visitors are greeted with an expansive layout that includes two bedrooms, two living rooms, a fully equipped kitchenette, and two bathrooms, making it an ideal space for families or groups. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, and the added benefit of a balcony allows for private outdoor relaxation.

For those concerned about health and wellness, it's noteworthy that the entire accommodation is designated as non-smoking.

Located a manageable distance from Podgorica Airport, just 58 km away, GuestHouse Malaj offers a blend of accessibility and serene living, promising a memorable stay in one of Shkodër's most inviting accommodations.
