Guests at Oyster Apartments are treated to well-appointed accommodations that boast not only a balcony for soaking in the surroundings but also modern amenities such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower. The convenience of in-room refrigerators adds to the appeal, ensuring that refreshments are always at hand.

For those looking to explore beyond the local beaches, Butrint National Park offers a natural escape just 17 km away, promising adventures amidst historical ruins and lush landscapes. Additionally, the property facilitates travel arrangements with a paid airport shuttle service to Ioannina Airport, located 97 km away, making both arrivals and departures a breeze.

Oyster Apartments presents an ideal lodging option for families and solo travelers alike, promising a stay that is as relaxing as it is memorable.
