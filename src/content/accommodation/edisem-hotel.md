The accommodations at Edisem Hotel are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a stay that blends relaxation with productivity. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and hairdryers, and select rooms even feature balconies for those who appreciate a breath of fresh air from the comfort of their room. To further enhance the guest experience, all rooms are provided with quality bed linen and towels.

Strategically positioned, Edisem Hotel is not only close to the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, just 5.7 km away, but also offers easy access to the natural beauty of the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 9.3 km from the hotel. For those looking to explore beyond the city, the Rock of Kavaje awaits at a distance of 39 km. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 11 km away, making Edisem Hotel an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Tirana.
