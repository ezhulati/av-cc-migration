Positioned just 50 km from the Port of Bar, The White Villa offers a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and fresh setting throughout the stay. The villa itself boasts an impressive layout with four generously sized bedrooms, three well-appointed bathrooms, and a selection of amenities including bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of home comfort. The heart of this villa is its fully equipped kitchen, perfect for those who enjoy preparing their own meals, while the terrace provides an idyllic spot to take in the garden views.

Adding to the sense of exclusivity, guests will appreciate the private entrance to the villa, allowing for a personal and secluded experience. For those looking to explore beyond the villa, Podgorica Airport is conveniently located 67 km away, making The White Villa an ideal choice for both local and international travelers seeking a blend of luxury, comfort, and privacy.
