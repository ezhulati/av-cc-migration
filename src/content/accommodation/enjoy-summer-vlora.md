This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious, air-conditioned haven complete with a balcony for those serene morning coffees or tranquil evening reflections. The inclusion of free WiFi ensures that guests remain connected, whether for leisure browsing or sharing their holiday experiences with loved ones.

Accommodation here is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a bedroom that promises a restful night's sleep, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom that caters to all your refreshment needs. Entertainment is also on hand with a flat-screen TV, perfect for those relaxing nights in.

For those keen to explore the local culture and history, Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are both less than 3 km away, making for an easy and enriching day out. And for guests arriving from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km away, ensuring that Enjoy Summer Vlora is within reach for international travelers seeking the charm and beauty of Vlorë.
