The highlight of Villa Stergjo is its spacious, air-conditioned villa, which boasts a private balcony overlooking a serene garden. The villa is thoughtfully designed with a single bedroom and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a self-sufficient stay. Entertainment needs are also covered, thanks to the inclusion of a flat-screen TV.

Located 119 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Villa Stergjo presents an accessible retreat for those looking to explore the beauty of Berat or seeking a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle.
