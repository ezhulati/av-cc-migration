The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air conditioning for those warm Albanian afternoons, complemented by the convenience of an elevator and the assurance of full-day security. Guests will appreciate the complimentary WiFi access that spans the entire property, ensuring connectivity and convenience throughout their stay. It's worth noting that Oro Apartments Tirana upholds a non-smoking policy, promoting a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

Accommodation at Oro Apartments Tirana is designed with guest comfort in mind. The apartment includes a bedroom, a bathroom equipped with fresh bed linen and towels, and a living area that boasts a flat-screen TV and a dining space. The fully equipped kitchen invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like, while the balcony offers a picturesque view of the city, perfect for enjoying a morning coffee or an evening glass of wine. For those concerned with privacy and noise, the apartment features a private entrance and soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful and uninterrupted stay.

Situated a mere 1.9 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and equally close to the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, guests are perfectly positioned to delve into Albania's rich history. For those looking to venture further, the Rock of Kavaje stands 43 km away, offering an adventurous day trip option. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km from the property, making Oro Apartments Tirana an ideal choice for both short visits and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
