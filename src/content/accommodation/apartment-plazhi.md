The space itself is thoughtfully designed, featuring an air-conditioned layout that includes a separate bedroom, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, making it a perfect retreat for relaxation after a day of exploring.

Situated conveniently, Apartment Plazhi is not only less than a kilometer from the inviting sands of Vlore Beach but also offers easy access to other local gems such as Vjetër Beach and Ri Beach, located 1.2 km and 2.3 km away, respectively. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km from the property, making it an accessible option for both domestic and international visitors.

Apartment Plazhi presents an ideal choice for travelers looking for a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to Vlorë's beautiful coastline.
