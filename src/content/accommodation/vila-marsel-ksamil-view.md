The villa, a mere 3.4 km away from the enchanting Butrint National Park, is a sanctuary of relaxation and privacy. Guests are treated to a seamless connection with the digital world through complimentary WiFi, ensuring that they remain plugged in, should they choose to. The 3-bedroom haven is thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a minibar, catering to all the comforts of home while offering garden views that promise a tranquil backdrop to a memorable stay.

Strategically located, Vila Marsel - Ksamil View is in close proximity to some of the area's most sought-after attractions. Ksamil Beach, Coco Beach, and Sunset Beach are all within easy reach, offering guests a variety of options for sun, sand, and sea. For those traveling from afar, the convenience of Corfu International Airport being just 93 km away simplifies travel plans.

In essence, Vila Marsel - Ksamil View stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those desiring a peaceful retreat with the added benefit of being close to nature and beautiful beaches, making every stay an unforgettable experience.
