Accommodations at Arameras Beach Resort are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring seating areas that invite relaxation and select units boasting balconies that offer breathtaking sea views. The resort's sun terrace serves as the perfect backdrop for unwinding and soaking in the sun's warmth.

For those looking to explore the area's natural beauty, Arameras Beach Resort is ideally situated near several popular attractions, including the tranquil Paradise Beach, the picturesque Lori Beach, and the secluded Pema e Thate Beach. Additionally, the resort is conveniently located 94 km from Corfu International Airport, making it an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers seeking a peaceful getaway by the sea.
