Featuring air-conditioned accommodations complete with a private pool, this villa not only promises comfort but also delivers on the promise of a picturesque garden view from its own balcony. The inclusion of a terrace, alongside complimentary private parking and WiFi, ensures that guests' needs are catered to with the utmost attention to detail.

The villa itself is a spacious abode, boasting four well-appointed bedrooms, each designed to offer the ultimate in comfort and relaxation. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV and a fireplace, creating a cozy atmosphere for guests. The kitchen is a culinary enthusiast's dream, fully equipped with modern appliances including a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and fridge. Towels and bed linen, provided for guests' convenience, add an extra layer of luxury to the experience.

For those looking to unwind, the villa's garden offers a tranquil setting where one can relax and enjoy the serene surroundings.

Situated 67 km from Podgorica Airport, The Luxury Villa is not just a place to stay but a destination to experience. Whether it's the allure of a private pool or the charm of a garden view, this property ensures a memorable stay for all who visit.
