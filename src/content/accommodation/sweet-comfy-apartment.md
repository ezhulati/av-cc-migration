A mere stone's throw from the enchanting La Petite Beach and within easy reach of the historic Butrint National Park, 17 km away, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the natural and cultural treasures of the area. For those intrigued by ancient civilizations, the ruins of Ancient Fanoti are accessible, located 49 km from the apartment, offering a unique glimpse into the past.

The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring ample space for relaxation. The living area is equipped with a flat-screen TV, offering a range of cable channels for guests' entertainment. Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, fridge, washing machine, and stovetop, providing everything needed for a comfortable self-catering stay.

For travelers arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 94 km from the property, making Sweet Comfy Apartment an accessible choice for both domestic and international visitors. This accommodation stands out as an ideal base for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and history of Sarandë, offering a blend of modern amenities and prime location.
