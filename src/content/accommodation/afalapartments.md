The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those culinary adventures, and three modern bathrooms, ensuring ample space and privacy for all guests. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal retreat for both families and groups of friends.

For beach enthusiasts, Santa Quaranta Beach is within easy reach, less than 1 km away, promising golden sands and crystal-clear waters. Meanwhile, adventurers can venture a little further to Butrint National Park, located 13 km from the property, for a day immersed in nature and history. With Ioannina Airport 97 km away, Afalapartments serves as a convenient and picturesque base for exploring the enchanting coast of Sarandë and beyond.
