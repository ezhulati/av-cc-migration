This smoke-free property is also a stone's throw away from the natural beauty of Lake Skadar, located 46 km away, making it an ideal spot for nature enthusiasts and those looking to unwind in a tranquil setting. The apartment itself boasts a well-appointed balcony that offers breathtaking sea views, providing a perfect backdrop for a morning coffee or an evening relaxation session.

Inside, the apartment is equipped with a cozy bedroom, a spacious living room, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable and entertaining stay. The kitchen comes fully equipped with an oven and a fridge, catering to the culinary needs of guests who prefer to prepare their meals. Additionally, the bathroom features a bidet, and the air-conditioned unit includes a luxurious bath and a dressing room, adding an extra layer of comfort to the guest experience. The unique offering of mountain views further elevates the appeal of Bujar's Apartment.

For those concerned about connectivity and travel logistics, the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently located just 51 km away, making Bujar's Apartment an accessible and compelling choice for both domestic and international travelers.
