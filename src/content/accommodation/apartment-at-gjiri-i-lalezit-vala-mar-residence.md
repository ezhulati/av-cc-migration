The apartment itself is a haven of comfort and style, boasting a spacious layout that includes a balcony offering breathtaking sea views. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes modern amenities such as a toaster and a fridge. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with towels and bed linen, adds to the thoughtful touches that make a stay here truly relaxing. The added benefit of a private entrance ensures an exclusive and undisturbed experience.

Dining options are readily available, with an on-site restaurant providing delicious meals and a bar for evening relaxation. The apartment's location also offers easy access to notable attractions, including the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre, making it an ideal base for exploring the rich history and culture of the area.

Situated conveniently 34 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the Apartment at Gjiri i Lalezit, Vala Mar Residence is not just a place to stay but a destination to experience the unparalleled beauty and tranquility of Hamallaj.
