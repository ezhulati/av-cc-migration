The establishment prides itself on its commitment to guest security and satisfaction, providing around-the-clock front desk service and full-day security. Accommodations at Eleanna are designed to cater to a variety of needs and preferences, featuring units equipped with cable TV, air conditioning, and a wardrobe. Select apartments elevate the experience further with a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, and a private bathroom that includes both a bidet and a hair dryer.

Morning delights await guests with a continental breakfast selection that includes pancakes, fresh fruits, and juice, ensuring a delightful start to the day. For those who enjoy outdoor activities, the apartment complex does not disappoint, offering a barbecue and a well-maintained garden for leisure and relaxation.

Located conveniently close to other popular beaches, Mango Beach and Santa Quaranta Beach are both within a 400-meter radius, making it easy for guests to explore the local coastline. For travelers arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest, situated 97 km from the property, making Apartments & Hotel Eleanna an accessible and attractive option for both domestic and international visitors.
