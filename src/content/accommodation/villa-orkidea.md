Guests at Villa Orkidea are treated to a visual feast of garden and city views, creating a tranquil backdrop for a relaxing stay. The property prides itself on its attentive services, including a 24-hour front desk, ensuring a seamless experience from private check-in to check-out, along with the convenience of currency exchange on-site.

Accommodations at Villa Orkidea are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning and a satellite flat-screen TV, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and entertaining. The private bathrooms come with a walk-in shower and slippers, adding a touch of luxury to the experience. Select rooms elevate the stay further with the addition of a terrace, offering a private outdoor space to unwind. Complimentary WiFi, along with bed linen and towels, underscore the guest house's commitment to convenience and connectivity.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, Butrint National Park lies 18 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich history and natural beauty. With Ioannina Airport located 98 km from the property, Villa Orkidea serves as an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Sarandë.
