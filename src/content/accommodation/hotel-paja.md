Additionally, the convenience of a fridge in every room adds a touch of home, ensuring refreshments are always at hand.

For travelers concerned with allergens, Hotel Paja offers a breath of fresh air, being an allergy-free environment. This detail underscores the hotel's dedication to providing a comfortable and inclusive stay for all guests.

Located 93 km from Corfu International Airport, the hotel ensures that even those coming from afar can easily access this haven of relaxation. With complimentary WiFi and private parking, guests are afforded the luxuries of connectivity and convenience from the moment they arrive.

Hotel Paja stands out not just for its prime location and modern amenities but also for its ability to create a welcoming atmosphere that caters to the needs of today's travelers. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beaches or the call of historical exploration at Butrint National Park, Hotel Paja serves as a perfect gateway to the treasures of Ksamil.
