The hotel prides itself on its air-conditioned rooms, each equipped with an LCD TV featuring satellite channels, and a fridge for personal use. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers, ensuring a refreshing start or end to the day.

For those keen to explore the local cuisine and nightlife, a variety of restaurants and bars are just a short stroll away, promising an authentic taste of Tirana's vibrant culture. Additionally, the National History Museum, located 300 meters from the hotel, offers a deep dive into the rich heritage of the region.

Convenience is further underscored by the hotel's proximity to essential travel links; a bus station is located 500 meters away, making exploration beyond the city limits a breeze. For international travelers, Tirana Airport is a mere 13 kilometers from the hotel, ensuring smooth transitions to and from this charming city.

Centrum Hotel not only positions itself as a gateway to the treasures of Tirana but also as a haven of comfort and convenience, making it a top recommendation for visitors to this dynamic city.
