The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, equipped with a single bedroom, a fully furnished kitchen including a dishwasher and an oven, and a living space with a flat-screen TV. The bathroom is modern, boasting a walk-in shower, while towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The added benefit of a private entrance underscores the accommodation's commitment to guest privacy.

For casual dining, a snack bar is on-site, complemented by a minimarket for any essentials or quick bites. Guests looking to enjoy the outdoors will appreciate the picnic area, perfect for leisurely days under the sun.

Situated a considerable distance from the hustle and bustle, Skanderbeg Square is 43 km away, and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is 47 km from the apartment, making for easy day trips. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 28 km away, making Gjiri i Lalzit Lura 3 Apartment Toni an accessible and attractive option for both short and extended stays.
