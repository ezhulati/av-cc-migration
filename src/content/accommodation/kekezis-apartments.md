Each unit is thoughtfully equipped with a private bathroom, ensuring privacy and comfort. Select accommodations at Kekezis Apartments further enhance the guest experience with the addition of a terrace, providing a perfect setting for a morning coffee or an evening wind-down.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, Butrint National Park lies 17 km away, promising an adventure into nature and history. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 97 km from the property, facilitates ease of travel for international guests. Kekezis Apartments stands out as an ideal spot for visitors to Sarandë, offering both the delights of the city and the tranquility of a comfortable retreat.
