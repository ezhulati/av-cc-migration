The property is distinguished by its array of family rooms, each boasting a private balcony where guests can soak in the serene surroundings. Inside, the rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring an array of cable channels, a kitchenette that caters to all basic culinary needs, and a private bathroom stocked with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries. Comfort is further ensured with the provision of quality bed linen and towels, making for a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of a hotel.

Beyond the confines of their rooms, guests are invited to unwind in the lush garden, a peaceful retreat that adds to the charm of Saranda Rooms. For those looking to explore the local beaches, Santa Quaranta Beach and VIP Beach are conveniently located just over a kilometer away, offering a variety of seaside experiences.

For travelers arriving from afar, the property is situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, with a paid shuttle service available to ensure a seamless journey to this coastal haven. Saranda Rooms stands as a testament to the allure of Sarandë, offering a stay that is as relaxing as it is memorable.
