The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring a single bedroom and bathroom, complemented by essentials such as bed linen and towels. Entertainment and relaxation are ensured with a flat-screen TV offering satellite channels, while the dining area and fully equipped kitchen invite guests to feel at home. The addition of a balcony presents breathtaking mountain views, setting the scene for a serene retreat. Upon arrival, guests are welcomed into their private sanctuary with delightful treats, including fruits and chocolates or cookies, emphasizing the apartment's non-smoking environment.

Beyond the confines of Aria Apartment, Berat beckons with its array of activities, particularly cycling, allowing guests to explore the picturesque surroundings at their leisure.

For those traveling to or from the city, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 118 km away, ensures a seamless journey. Aria Apartment thus stands as a prime choice for travelers in search of a blend of local charm and modern amenities in Berat.
