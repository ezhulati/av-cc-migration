Each apartment at Vila Ballaxhia is a testament to thoughtful design, boasting balconies that open up to refreshing garden vistas. Inside, guests are treated to a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a fridge, ensuring that home-cooked meals are never off the menu. The dining area provides a cozy nook for meals, while the inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is always at hand. Air conditioning in every room guarantees a respite from the Mediterranean warmth, making every moment inside as comfortable as it is enjoyable.

Privacy and convenience are paramount, as evidenced by the provision of free private parking and complimentary WiFi throughout the premises. The attention to detail extends to the personal touches in each unit, where guests will find their private bathrooms equipped with a bidet, and fresh bed linen and towels readily available, ensuring a stay that is as carefree as it is comfortable.

Located just 700 meters from the inviting sands of Ksamil Beach 9 and a short drive from the historical allure of Butrint National Park, Vila Ballaxhia positions itself as an ideal base for explorers keen to uncover the natural and cultural treasures of the region. For those venturing further afield, Corfu International Airport is within a manageable distance, making Vila Ballaxhia a compelling choice for international travelers seeking a blend of exploration and relaxation.

In summary, Vila Ballaxhia stands out as a premier choice for those in pursuit of a peaceful getaway, where the beauty of Ksamil's landscapes can be enjoyed in comfort and style.
