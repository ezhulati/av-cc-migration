The accommodation itself boasts an air-conditioned environment, ensuring a comfortable stay regardless of the weather outside. It features two elegantly appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a well-maintained bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it a home away from home.

Guests can start their day on a high note with a diverse breakfast selection that caters to various dietary preferences, including American, vegan, and halal options.

For those traveling to or from the area, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 137 km away, making Diarla Home an ideal choice for both short and long stays.
