Guests are treated to a generously sized apartment that opens up to a balcony offering serene sea vistas. The accommodation includes a bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a kitchen fitted with modern appliances including an oven and a toaster. The inclusion of a bathroom with a hot tub and the availability of laundry services add layers of convenience, ensuring a stay that is as comfortable as it is enjoyable.

For those looking to explore, Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are within a short driving distance, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history. Furthermore, Belleview Apartment 5 facilitates exploration beyond its immediate surroundings by offering a paid airport shuttle service to and from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 152 km away.

In essence, Belleview Apartment 5 stands out as an exemplary choice for travelers who prioritize location, comfort, and the freedom to explore Vlorë's coastal beauty at their own pace.
