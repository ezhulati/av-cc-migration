Guests stepping into EMOJI House are greeted by a tastefully decorated space that combines comfort with convenience. The air-conditioned apartment features a cozy bedroom, a sleek flat-screen TV, and a dining area perfect for enjoying meals. The kitchen is fully equipped, catering to all culinary needs with a microwave among other essentials, while the living room provides a relaxing retreat. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

EMOJI House prides itself on being a non-smoking and soundproof property, offering peace and privacy. For those looking to explore the city or venture further, the apartment facilitates airport transfers and provides a bicycle rental service, making transportation a breeze.

For everyday needs, a minimarket on the premises adds to the list of conveniences, ensuring guests have everything they need at their fingertips. Located just 2.5 km from Skanderbeg Square and a short distance from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, EMOJI House serves as an ideal base for exploring Tirana. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently 13 km away, making arrivals and departures smooth and stress-free.

EMOJI House stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and access to Tirana's rich cultural tapestry.
