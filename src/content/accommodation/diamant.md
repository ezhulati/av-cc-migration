The apartment itself is a study in modern living, featuring a single bedroom that promises restful nights. The heart of the space is a fully equipped kitchen, catering to guests' culinary needs, whether it's a quick breakfast or a leisurely dinner. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have access to their favorite shows and movies during their stay.

For those intrigued by the rich tapestry of history and nature, Butrint National Park lies just 18 km away, offering an easy getaway to one of Albania's most cherished cultural and natural landmarks. Furthermore, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 98 km from Diamant, facilitates smooth travel arrangements for international visitors.

Diamant stands out not only for its prime location and well-appointed accommodations but also for its commitment to a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests. This apartment is a top pick for travelers who prioritize both the vibrancy of Sarandë's coastal life and the tranquility of a private retreat.
