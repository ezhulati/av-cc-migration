Located a mere 700 meters from the pristine sands of Ri Beach, this apartment is a haven for guests who appreciate the tranquility of a smoke-free environment. The layout is generously spacious, featuring a bedroom, a living area adorned with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen boasting modern appliances such as an oven and a fridge. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bath, alongside the provision of towels and bed linen, ensures a home-away-from-home experience. The terrace, offering panoramic sea views, and an outdoor dining area, invites guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the serene atmosphere.

For added convenience, a minimarket on the premises caters to the immediate needs of guests, allowing for a focus on relaxation and enjoyment. The property's garden is a tranquil retreat for those looking to unwind in a natural setting.

The apartment's location is ideal for explorers and beach lovers alike, with Vlore Beach less than 1 km away, offering easy access to sun, sea, and sand. For those interested in local culture, Kuzum Baba is just 3.8 km from the property, presenting an opportunity to experience the area's rich heritage. For international travelers, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 153 km away, making Seaview Terrace Penthouse Top Loft & Box Garage a compelling choice for both domestic and overseas visitors seeking a memorable stay in Vlorë.
