Having undergone a recent renovation, Main Street Apartment retains its original 1970s architecture while providing contemporary amenities and comforts. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi throughout their stay in this strictly non-smoking property, situated merely 1.8 km from the bustling Skanderbeg Square.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes modern appliances such as a toaster and a coffee machine. The inclusion of a walk-in shower and plush bathrobes in the bathroom adds a touch of luxury, ensuring a relaxing and comfortable stay. For those who appreciate the finer details, towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience.

An added bonus is the apartment's outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals al fresco while soaking in the local atmosphere. 

For those keen to explore, Main Street Apartment is ideally positioned near several of Tirana's attractions, including the artistic Reja - The Cloud, the historic Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, and the serene Et'hem Bey Mosque. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, this apartment stands out as an accessible and inviting option for both short and long-term visitors to Albania's vibrant capital.
