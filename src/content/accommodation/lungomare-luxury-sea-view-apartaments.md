The apartments are designed with the discerning traveler in mind, featuring air conditioning, flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, refrigerators, kettles, and walk-in showers. The attention to detail extends to the provision of slippers, desks, and private bathrooms equipped with bidets and hair dryers. Free WiFi enhances the convenience factor, with select rooms offering the luxury of a terrace. Comfort is further assured by the inclusion of quality bed linen and towels in every unit.

For those looking to explore the local dining scene, a variety of options are conveniently located near the apartments. Adventure seekers and leisure travelers alike will find Lungomare Luxury Sea View Apartments perfectly positioned to enjoy a range of activities, from hiking and diving to fishing. The availability of bicycle rentals and access to a private beach area on-site encourage guests to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and recreational opportunities that Vlorë has to offer.

Notably, the property is situated within easy reach of Vjetër Beach and the historic Kuzum Baba, making it an ideal base for exploring the area's attractions. For international travelers, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, with the apartments facilitating airport transfers and offering a car rental service to ensure a seamless travel experience.

Lungomare Luxury Sea View Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, promising a blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience for an unforgettable stay in Vlorë.
