The guest house prides itself on its array of family rooms, each equipped with air conditioning, a fully outfitted kitchenette, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom that includes a bidet, a hairdryer, and free toiletries. The convenience of having a desk in every unit cannot be overstated, catering to the needs of both leisure and business travelers.

A continental breakfast awaits guests each morning, setting the stage for a day of exploration or relaxation. For those moments of downtime, Joni Apartments offers a variety of spaces to unwind, from its inviting terrace to the tranquil garden and a shared lounge area, ensuring every guest finds their perfect spot to relax.

Situated a short stroll from Paradise Beach and less than a kilometer from Lori Beach, the guest house is ideally positioned for guests to discover the local beaches' natural beauty. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 92 km away, making Joni Apartments an accessible retreat for those coming from afar.

With its 24-hour front desk and full-day security, guests are assured a worry-free stay, making Joni Apartments a standout choice for those seeking a blend of hospitality, comfort, and scenic beauty in Ksamil.
