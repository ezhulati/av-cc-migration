This air-conditioned haven boasts a spacious layout, including two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests. The living space is enhanced by a balcony that opens up to tranquil street views, allowing visitors to soak in the local ambiance. The apartment is equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster, catering to all culinary needs. Additionally, the provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience to the experience.

Emphasizing guest privacy, Seaside Escape Apartment features a private entrance, ensuring a secluded and uninterrupted stay. Despite its peaceful location, the property maintains a strict non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is located 98 km away, making Seaside Escape Apartment an accessible destination for international and domestic travelers alike. This property stands out as a compelling choice for those in search of a comfortable, well-situated base to explore the beauty of Sarandë and its surroundings.
