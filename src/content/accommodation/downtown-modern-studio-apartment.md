This air-conditioned studio apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV, washing machine, and a kitchenette that includes a fridge and minibar, ensuring guests feel at home. The addition of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy, making it an ideal retreat for those looking to unwind after a day of exploration.

For those interested in exploring the local culture and history, the apartment is conveniently located near several of Tirana's most notable attractions, including the House of Leaves, Clock Tower Tirana, and Et'hem Bey Mosque. Additionally, the property is just a stone's throw away from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, offering a unique glimpse into the country's past.

Guests will appreciate the thoughtful amenities such as the picnic area and the availability of sightseeing tours, which can be arranged nearby. Moreover, for those traveling from afar, the property provides a paid airport shuttle service from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 13 km away, ensuring a seamless travel experience.

Downtown Modern Studio Apartment not only offers a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the rich cultural tapestry of Tirana, making it a compelling choice for discerning travelers.
