The apartment at Nautical 657 is notably spacious, offering guests a well-appointed living space that includes a bedroom, bathroom, and the luxury of a balcony boasting captivating sea views. The inclusion of modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a fully equipped kitchen enhances the home-away-from-home experience, while thoughtful touches like provided bed linen and towels underscore the property's attention to guest comfort.

For those who appreciate the convenience of on-site amenities, the presence of a minimarket within the apartment complex is a welcome feature, allowing for easy access to essentials during their stay.

Situated a manageable distance from notable landmarks, Nautical 657 is just 7.5 km from the Rock of Kavaje and a scenic drive from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 37 km away. The property's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, which is 31 km away, is complemented by the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, adding an extra layer of convenience for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Nautical 657 presents itself as an idyllic destination for those in pursuit of a tranquil beachside escape, combined with the comforts and conveniences of modern living.
