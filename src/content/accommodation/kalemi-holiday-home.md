The accommodations at Kalemi are thoughtfully designed to cater to families, with select units boasting private entrances that lead into spaces adorned with a desk and wardrobe for personal belongings. For those seeking a touch of luxury, options are available with terraces, satellite flat-screen TVs, and the comfort of air conditioning and heating, ensuring a pleasant environment regardless of the season. Every unit is meticulously equipped with a private bathroom, highlighting the property's attention to guest comfort and privacy.

Beyond the confines of their rooms, visitors are invited to unwind in the lush garden, offering a tranquil oasis for relaxation. The allure of Bora Bora Beach beckons from less than a kilometer away, promising sun-soaked days by the sea. Meanwhile, the natural wonders of Butrint National Park are just 3 km from the doorstep, presenting an opportunity for exploration and adventure. For those traveling from afar, Corfu International Airport is located 93 km from the holiday home, making Kalemi an accessible haven for both local and international guests seeking a picturesque escape in Ksamil.
