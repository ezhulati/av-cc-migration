The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring cable channels, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs with an oven and microwave. The inclusion of a washing machine adds a touch of convenience, while the bathroom, complete with a bidet, ensures a comfortable stay. Notably, the apartment is designed with comfort in mind, featuring air conditioning, soundproofing, and even a cozy fireplace, making it an ideal retreat in any season. The added benefit of a private entrance further enhances the sense of privacy and exclusivity.

For those traveling to or from the area, the proximity to Podgorica Airport, located 58 km away, is a significant advantage. The apartment also offers a paid airport shuttle service, adding an extra layer of convenience for guests.

With its comprehensive amenities, including free WiFi throughout the property, a lift for easy access, and shared facilities such as a lounge and kitchen, Sweet Living Apartment is more than just a place to stay-it's a home away from home.
