The property boasts a well-maintained garden and a terrace, complemented by the convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi. With a commitment to guest satisfaction, Niks Apartments ensures round-the-clock assistance thanks to its 24-hour front desk, alongside a shared kitchen that caters to the culinary needs of its guests.

Accommodation at Niks Apartments is generously spacious, featuring a three-bedroom layout equipped with a flat-screen TV hosting satellite channels, a fully stocked kitchen including an oven and toaster, and the added convenience of a washing machine. The presence of two bathrooms with showers adds to the comfort. The outdoor dining area, overlooking the serene garden, provides an idyllic setting for meals.

For those looking to make the most of the sunny weather, the property's barbecue facilities are a delightful bonus. The adventurous at heart will find no shortage of activities in Ksamil, with hiking trails nearby. Moreover, diving, cycling, and fishing opportunities abound in the vicinity, with Niks Apartments ready to facilitate car rental services for further exploration.

Located just 500 meters from Puerto Rico Beach and a short drive of 3.7 km from Butrint National Park, the apartments serve as an ideal base for exploring the local area. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Niks Apartments a convenient and attractive option for both domestic and overseas visitors.
