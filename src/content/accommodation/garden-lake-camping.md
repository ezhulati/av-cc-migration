Located strategically, Garden Lake Camping is not just close to the vibrant Ksamil Beach but also a short distance from the enchanting Butrint National Park, making it an ideal base for explorers. Moreover, the proximity to Bora Bora Beach, just 600 meters away, adds another layer of appeal to this charming destination.

One of the highlights of staying at Garden Lake Camping is the opportunity to enjoy the serene lake views right from the patio, which is thoughtfully equipped with outdoor furniture for guests' comfort. The commitment to providing a peaceful environment is further evidenced by the property's non-smoking policy and the inclusion of a private entrance for each accommodation, ensuring utmost privacy and a sense of exclusivity.

For those who appreciate the finer things in life, the on-site coffee shop and bar offer a cozy spot to unwind. The luxury tent doesn't stop there; it invites guests to relax and socialize around an outdoor fireplace or enjoy a laid-back afternoon in the picnic area, making it a perfect setting for both relaxation and social gatherings.

Accessibility is also a breeze, with Corfu International Airport located just 93 km away, making Garden Lake Camping an accessible escape for international travelers.

In essence, Garden Lake Camping redefines the camping experience by blending the allure of the great outdoors with the comforts of modern amenities, making it a must-visit destination for those looking to immerse themselves in nature without forgoing luxury.
