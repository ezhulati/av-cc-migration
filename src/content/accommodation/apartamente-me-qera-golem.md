The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area with a television for entertainment, and a balcony that invites guests to relax and enjoy the local scenery. The kitchen is fully equipped, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals, with an oven, microwave, and washing machine at their disposal. An outdoor dining area further enhances the appeal, offering a unique opportunity to dine al fresco while soaking in the ambient surroundings.

Security is a top priority at Apartamente me Qera Golem, with full-day security and an elevator for easy access, providing peace of mind for guests throughout their stay.

For those eager to explore, Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach is within easy reach, located just 2.3 km away, offering another spot for sun-seekers and beach lovers. The historic Skanderbeg Square is a bit farther afield, 46 km away, but well worth the trip for those interested in the rich history and culture of the area. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, making Apartamente me Qera Golem an accessible retreat at just 41 km away.

In summary, Apartamente me Qera Golem presents an inviting option for guests looking for a blend of comfort, convenience, and the chance to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and cultural richness of Durrës.
