The property boasts a spacious villa that is a masterpiece of comfort and style, featuring a balcony that offers breathtaking mountain views. With three elegantly furnished bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen, guests are assured of a stay that combines luxury with the comforts of home. The villa's air-conditioned interior includes a seating area, washing machine, and two bathrooms complete with walk-in showers and slippers, ensuring a blend of convenience and indulgence. Notably, the villa prioritizes privacy and tranquility with its private entrance and soundproofing.

Chateau Sukaj enhances the guest experience with thoughtful amenities, including free WiFi, complimentary private parking, and accessible facilities for disabled guests. The outdoor space is equally impressive, featuring a patio and an outdoor fireplace that invite relaxation and social gatherings.

For those seeking leisure on-site, a coffee shop and a minimarket add to the convenience of the stay. Families will appreciate the child-friendly amenities, such as a kids' pool, an indoor play area, and outdoor play equipment, making it an ideal choice for guests of all ages.

Adventurous guests will find the villa's location perfect for exploring the surrounding area, with opportunities for hiking and bike tours readily available. Chateau Sukaj also offers a car rental service, facilitating easy access to nearby attractions, including the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Rock of Kavaje.

Situated merely 5 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, with a paid airport shuttle service available, Chateau Sukaj stands out as a convenient and luxurious option for both leisure and adventure in the heart of Laknas.
