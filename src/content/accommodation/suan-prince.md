The apartment is a haven for those who appreciate the finer details, featuring a modern 1-bedroom layout that is strictly non-smoking. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with amenities such as free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped with an oven to cater to all culinary needs. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

Location-wise, Suan Prince couldn't be more ideally placed. La Petite Beach awaits at a mere 1 km away, offering another picturesque spot for relaxation and sunbathing, while the vibrant Sarande Main Beach is just a bit further at 1.2 km, promising lively beachside activities and eateries. For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the apartment, making it a feasible option for international visitors.

In summary, Suan Prince presents itself as a prime accommodation choice for those looking to explore Sarandë's charming coastal lifestyle, with its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay.
