The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring two separate bedrooms, a bathroom, and a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal setting for both relaxation and entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dining area and oven, invites guests to prepare and enjoy their meals with ease. The outdoor dining area further enhances the experience, allowing guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the tranquil atmosphere.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures that essentials are always within reach.

Strategically positioned, Beach House Gjiri i Lalzit is not far from notable attractions such as the Rock of Kavaje, 41 km away, and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 44 km from the property. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 29 km away, makes this apartment an accessible and appealing choice for both domestic and international travelers.

In essence, Beach House Gjiri i Lalzit stands out as a meticulously designed haven, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and the beauty of Albania's coastal landscape, making it a must-visit destination for those in search of a peaceful getaway.
