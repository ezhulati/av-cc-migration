Guests at Kristal Apartments are treated to accommodations that boast not only a balcony with serene garden views but also access to a well-maintained garden and barbecue facilities, perfect for leisurely evenings. The highlight of each apartment is undoubtedly the thoughtful provision of amenities designed to ensure a stay that is as comfortable as it is memorable. From air-conditioned rooms that promise a respite from the heat to the convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi, every detail has been considered.

The living spaces are characterized by their bright, airy feel, complemented by a terrace that offers breathtaking mountain views. Comfort is further enhanced by a seating area and a cable flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have a cozy spot to unwind after a day of exploration. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, microwave, fridge, stovetop, and essential kitchenware, along with the added luxuries of a coffee machine and kettle, invites guests to prepare and enjoy their meals just the way they like.

For those who value privacy and convenience, each apartment includes a private bathroom equipped with a shower and hairdryer, ensuring that guests' needs are met without compromise. The provision of bed linen and towels in all units underscores the apartment complex's commitment to guest comfort.

Beyond the confines of Kristal Apartments, guests will find Paradise Beach just 700 meters away, offering another splendid option for sun-seekers. The proximity to Butrint National Park, only 2.6 km away, presents an opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the rich history and natural splendor of the region. For those arriving from afar, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km from the property, making Kristal Apartments an accessible haven for international and domestic travelers alike.
