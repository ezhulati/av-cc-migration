The property stands out for its thoughtful amenities, including complimentary private parking and access to a shared kitchen, making it an ideal choice for families and groups. The presence of family rooms, coupled with a dedicated picnic area, ensures that guests have ample space to unwind and connect with nature.

Accommodations at TAFA A. are designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Select units boast their own private entrances, enhancing the sense of exclusivity and privacy. The interiors are thoughtfully appointed with a dining area, a cozy fireplace, and a fully equipped oven, catering to the culinary needs of guests. The charm of shared bathrooms is elevated by the provision of free WiFi across the premises, ensuring that guests stay connected. For those seeking a touch of luxury, certain rooms offer the added delight of a terrace, along with the indulgence of a coffee machine and a sweet treat of chocolates or cookies.

Situated 124 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, TAFA A. offers a blend of convenience and serenity, making it a compelling choice for travelers exploring the vibrant region of Fier.
