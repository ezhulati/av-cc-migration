Accommodations at Villa CACA are designed with the modern family in mind, featuring select units equipped with cable flat-screen TVs, fully outfitted kitchens complete with ovens, and private bathrooms adorned with baths and plush bathrobes, ensuring a stay that combines luxury with the comforts of home.

For those who prefer to dine in or need essentials, a convenient minimarket on the premises caters to guests' needs without the need to venture far. The property doesn't stop at just providing a place to stay; it invites guests to experience leisure in its purest form. A well-maintained garden equipped with a barbecue stands ready for guests to enjoy a serene afternoon or an intimate evening under the stars.

Anglers and outdoor enthusiasts will find the nearby fishing opportunities irresistible, adding a touch of adventure to their stay. For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Villa CACA is conveniently located less than a kilometer from the enchanting Ksamil Beach 9 and a short 2.9 km journey from the historical Butrint National Park, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage.

Accessibility is seamless, with Corfu International Airport situated 93 km away, making Villa CACA an accessible retreat for international travelers seeking tranquility and convenience in one of Ksamil's most picturesque settings.
