This spacious apartment is thoughtfully appointed with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. It features one bedroom, a bathroom, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV and a dining space. Designed with a non-smoking policy, the apartment ensures a clean and healthy environment for all guests.
