The accommodation itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring a balcony that offers tranquil garden views, two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-away-from-home experience. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV for guests' enjoyment.

For those looking to explore the vibrant surroundings of Vlorë, Bjora Home serves as the perfect base. The area is ripe with opportunities for hiking and discovering the local landscape. Noteworthy landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are within easy reach, located 4.7 km and 4.9 km away, respectively. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 154 km from the property, making Bjora Home an accessible retreat for both local and foreign visitors seeking the ultimate Vlorë experience.
