Guests can look forward to relaxing in air-conditioned rooms, each equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers, hairdryers, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a comfortable stay. For those needing to catch up on work, desks are provided in every room.

Dining at Hotel Jurgen is a delightful experience, thanks to its on-site Italian restaurant. The restaurant boasts a spacious terrace, perfect for enjoying meals outdoors. Additionally, the hotel features a welcoming bar and a serene garden, offering guests multiple spots to unwind.

The convenience extends beyond the hotel's amenities with a bus stop located merely 25 meters away, providing easy access to the center of Tirana, 15 km from the hotel. For guests arriving by car, the provision of free private parking is a significant plus.

Hotel Jurgen also excels in service, with a 24-hour front desk ready to assist guests at any hour. Whether it's a late-night check-in or early morning queries, the staff's dedication to guest satisfaction is evident.

In summary, Hotel Jurgen emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and quality service near Tirana International Airport. Its blend of thoughtful amenities, strategic location, and attentive service makes it a standout option for both short and extended stays.
