The generously sized apartment is thoughtfully designed with a balcony that invites natural light and fresh air, creating a welcoming atmosphere. It boasts a comfortable bedroom, a cozy living area, and a kitchen equipped with a refrigerator and all necessary kitchenware, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The air-conditioned space is meticulously maintained, featuring modern amenities such as a bidet and a dressing room, all within a smoke-free environment.

For those needing to stock up on essentials, a convenient minimarket is located on-site, with a supermarket also nearby, making grocery shopping a breeze. Families visiting Lando Apartment will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of an indoor play area and a baby safety gate, ensuring a safe and enjoyable stay for guests with children. Additionally, the apartment offers a bicycle rental service, providing an eco-friendly option for exploring the surrounding area.

Situated just 57 km from Podgorica Airport, Lando Apartment presents an ideal base for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of adventure in Shkodër.
