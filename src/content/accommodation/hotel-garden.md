Accommodations at Hotel Garden are thoughtfully equipped with cable TV and a refrigerator, ensuring comfort and convenience. The en-suite bathrooms are a testament to the hotel's attention to detail, featuring showers, complimentary toiletries, and fresh towels for a rejuvenating experience.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Hotel Garden provides ample amenities to enhance the stay of every guest. Complimentary garage parking removes any worries about vehicle security, while the array of on-site activities promises entertainment for all ages. Guests can engage in lively table tennis matches, partake in fishing adventures, or watch as their children delight in the playground.

Situated just 11 kilometers from the vibrant town of Vlorë, which houses a ferry port, Hotel Garden offers an ideal base for exploring the local area or venturing further afield. Whether seeking a peaceful retreat or an adventure-laden holiday, Hotel Garden stands out as a premier choice for travelers to Radhimë.
