Accommodations at Villa Idrizi are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for a homely feel. The private bathrooms come with a bidet and slippers, adding a touch of luxury to the stay. For those seeking extra space, selected rooms boast a cozy seating area, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Moreover, all units are furnished with quality bed linen and towels, emphasizing the guest house's attention to detail.

Situated a short stroll away from La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach, Villa Idrizi offers an ideal base for beach lovers. The property is also conveniently located 96 km from Ioannina Airport, making it easily accessible for international travelers. Whether visiting for leisure or seeking a peaceful retreat, Villa Idrizi stands out as a top choice for those looking to experience the best of Saranda.
