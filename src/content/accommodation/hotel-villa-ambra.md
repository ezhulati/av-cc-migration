Guests are welcomed into rooms that blend comfort with functionality, each equipped with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of bed linen and towels, enhancing the stay experience. Select accommodations delight with breathtaking sea views, inviting guests to unwind on their private balconies. The presence of a fridge in every room adds a touch of home, ensuring refreshments are always at hand.

For those seeking adventure or relaxation, Hotel Villa Ambra is perfectly positioned. The surrounding area is a playground for activities such as fishing, snorkeling, and cycling, with the beachfront offering a sublime spot to relax and soak in the sun. Further afield, the allure of Ksamil Beach 7 awaits at a mere 1.5 km distance, while the historical marvels of Butrint National Park are just 3.9 km away. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, located 94 km from the hotel, making Hotel Villa Ambra an accessible haven for both local and global visitors seeking a memorable stay by the sea.
