Each room at Luani ARTE is a testament to elegance and comfort, boasting air conditioning for those warm summer days, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment at any time. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, each equipped with either a bath or a shower, complemented by a bidet, a hairdryer, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a seamless stay.

The on-site bar and restaurant offer guests the convenience of delicious dining options without the need to leave the comfort of the hotel. The presence of a 24-hour front desk underscores the property's commitment to guest service, ready to assist at any hour of the day or night.

For those looking to explore beyond the hotel's comforts, Luani ARTE facilitates airport shuttles for an additional charge, connecting guests to Tirana Airport, located 85 km away, with ease. This feature adds an extra layer of convenience for international travelers or those on tight schedules.

In summary, Luani ARTE stands out as a premier choice for visitors to Shkodër, offering a harmonious blend of luxury accommodations, wellness facilities, and impeccable service, all designed to create an unforgettable stay.
