This air-conditioned sanctuary features a spacious bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those inspired to cook, and a bathroom designed with comfort in mind. Guests can unwind while enjoying their favorite shows on the flat-screen TV provided in the apartment. With free WiFi enveloping the entire property, staying connected or sharing your experiences with friends and family is effortless. The balcony serves as a perfect nook for morning coffees or evening reflections, offering a tranquil space to start or end your day.

For travelers arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 96 km away, making Beach Apartment an accessible haven for both domestic and international visitors. Emphasizing a smoke-free environment, this accommodation ensures a clean and healthy stay for all guests.

In summary, Beach Apartment stands out as a premier choice for those in search of a peaceful beachside getaway in Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with the beauty and excitement of the Albanian Riviera.
