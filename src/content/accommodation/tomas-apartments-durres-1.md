This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a fully immersive living experience. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, making self-catering a breeze. The inclusion of a washing machine adds to the convenience, ensuring a hassle-free stay. The living space is complemented by a flat-screen TV with cable channels, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

Privacy and comfort are paramount at Tomas Apartments Durres 1, as evidenced by the thoughtful addition of a private entrance to the accommodation. The air-conditioned apartment features a cozy bedroom, alongside a bathroom equipped with a bidet, catering to all the essentials needed for a refreshing retreat.

For those looking to venture further, the property is conveniently located 41 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of Albania. Adventure seekers will find the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within a 45 km radius, presenting an opportunity to witness breathtaking views from above. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, located just 1.6 km away, offers a unique natural attraction close by.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy complimentary WiFi throughout their stay. For international travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 35 km away, ensures a smooth and accessible travel experience.

Tomas Apartments Durres 1 stands out not just for its strategic location and comprehensive amenities but also for offering a slice of home in the picturesque setting of Durrës. It's a testament to the blend of convenience, comfort, and exploration, making it a top recommendation for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Albania's coast.
