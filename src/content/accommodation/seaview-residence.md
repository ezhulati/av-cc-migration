The space itself is thoughtfully laid out with a single bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring relaxation is just a click away.

For those keen on exploring the local beaches, Seaview Residence is perfectly positioned near several popular spots including the Beach at Government Villas, Ri Beach, and Liro Beach, each offering a unique slice of Vlorë's scenic coastline. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 155 km away, making Seaview Residence an accessible getaway for both local and foreign visitors seeking a serene escape by the sea.
