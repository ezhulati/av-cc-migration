Each air-conditioned room at Hotel Strora is a haven of comfort, boasting a balcony that offers a breath of fresh air and a flat-screen cable TV for entertainment. The attention to detail extends to the provision of a minibar and hairdryer in every room, ensuring guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

The location of Hotel Strora is nothing short of strategic. Situated at the start of the road leading from the bustling center of Sarandë to the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Butrint and its encompassing national park, the hotel serves as an ideal base for explorers. The heart of Sarandë is merely 1 km away, offering easy access to the city's vibrant life, while the historical marvels of Butrint are just 16 km distant, promising a journey through time.

Moreover, the convenience of a public bus stop directly adjacent to the property ensures that guests have the freedom to explore the surrounding area without the hassle of personal transportation.

In essence, Hotel Strora is not just a place to rest; it's a launchpad for adventures into the heart of Albanian culture and history, wrapped in the comfort of modern amenities and the warmth of traditional hospitality.
