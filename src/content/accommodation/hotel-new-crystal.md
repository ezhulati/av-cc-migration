Accommodations at Hotel New Crystal are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is air-conditioned and boasts a private balcony, offering a personal oasis of relaxation. The presence of standard amenities such as cable TV, a fridge, and a table adds to the convenience, while select units also feature kitchenettes with a stove, catering to those who prefer to prepare their own meals.

Dining at the hotel is an experience in itself, with the beach restaurant situated 700 meters from the main building. Here, guests can start their day with a breakfast while gazing out at the sea from the terrace. For a more casual setting, the property's beach bar serves up refreshing drinks, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

Located approximately 3 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and 50 km from Corfu International Airport, accessible via ferry from the nearby port of Sarandë, Hotel New Crystal offers an ideal base for both relaxation and adventure. This hotel stands out as a compelling choice for those looking to experience the beauty and charm of the area, making it a must-visit destination on any traveler's list.
