Upon entering, visitors are greeted by elegantly appointed rooms, each boasting air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with an array of cable channels. The thoughtful design of the rooms ensures that every guest enjoys either a mesmerizing sea view or a captivating cityscape, right from the comfort of their private quarters. Bathrooms are well-equipped with showers, complemented by the added luxury of slippers and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a stay that marries comfort with convenience.

For those seeking a moment of relaxation, select units feature cozy seating areas, providing a perfect nook to unwind after a day of exploration. The hotel's on-site bar serves as a social hub, inviting guests to enjoy a refreshing drink in a relaxed atmosphere.

Strategically located, Hotel JoAn offers easy access to the enchanting Butrint National Park, just 17.5 km away, and the mesmerizing Blue Eye, reachable within 20 km. A mere 50 metres from the hotel, guests can find a local bus station, facilitating exploration of the surrounding area. Additionally, the Sarandë Port, located 750 metres from the hotel, provides a convenient 30-minute ferry line to Corfu Island, home to the Corfu Ioannis Kapodistria International Airport. For those journeying from further afield, Tirana International Airport is 280 km away, making Hotel JoAn an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers alike.

Hotel JoAn emerges as a distinguished choice for travelers seeking a blend of serenity, convenience, and a touch of local charm, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Sarandë.
