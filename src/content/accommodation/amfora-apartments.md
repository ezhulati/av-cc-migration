The apartment itself is a study in spaciousness and light, featuring a welcoming balcony that invites guests to unwind with a view. Inside, two cozy bedrooms and a generous living room offer ample space for relaxation, while the well-equipped kitchen caters to those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals even while away. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

Adding to the appeal is the apartment's commitment to a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and fresh stay for all guests. The quiet street views further enhance the peaceful ambiance, making Amfora Apartments an ideal retreat for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle.

For travelers flying in, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 51 km away, makes arriving and departing a breeze. Amfora Apartments stands out as a top choice for those in search of a tranquil beachfront escape combined with the comforts of modern living.
