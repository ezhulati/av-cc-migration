Accommodations at Vila Lika are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms equipped with slippers, hair dryers, and bidets for a touch of luxury. Safety is a priority, with each unit boasting a safety deposit box for peace of mind. For those seeking a little extra, select rooms boast terraces for private outdoor relaxation, while others provide captivating city views. Storage needs are met with spacious wardrobes in every room.

Dining at the guest house is an experience in itself, with an on-site restaurant catering to a variety of tastes for dinner, lunch, and brunch. Whether it's a hearty meal to start the day or a sumptuous dinner to cap off an evening, the culinary offerings are designed to satisfy.

Situated 72 km from Kastoria National Airport, Vila Lika offers a blend of convenience and charm, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a tranquil escape with the comforts of home.
