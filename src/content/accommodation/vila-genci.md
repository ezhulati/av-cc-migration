Accommodations at Vila Genci are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is a sanctuary of calm, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, alongside the convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi connectivity. The highlight of each unit is undoubtedly the balcony, where guests can soak in breathtaking sea views, a rare find in the heart of Ksamil. The interiors are equally impressive, with a cable flat-screen TV, a cozy dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a private bathroom complete with a shower, a hairdryer, and complimentary toiletries. Adding to the charm, a patio with an outdoor dining setup allows guests to dine al fresco while admiring the mountainous landscape.

Beyond the comfort of their rooms, guests at Vila Genci are invited to explore a range of activities. Cycling enthusiasts will find the surrounding area ripe for adventure. For those traveling with children, an indoor play area ensures entertainment for the younger guests. The sun terrace, a peaceful spot for relaxation, offers an ideal setting to unwind after a day of exploration.

The aparthotel's proximity to notable attractions adds to its appeal. Lori Beach is easily accessible, located less than 1 kilometer away, offering another spot for sun and sea lovers. For a touch of culture and history, Butrint National Park is a mere 2.6 kilometers away, promising a fascinating excursion. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 92 kilometers from the haven of Vila Genci.

In essence, Vila Genci stands as a testament to the perfect balance of leisure and exploration, offering a memorable stay for those venturing to the enchanting shores of Ksamil.
