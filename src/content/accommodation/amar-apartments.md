The highlight of Amar Apartments is its thoughtfully designed accommodation, featuring a bedroom equipped with air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV with cable channels, ensuring guests are never far from the comforts of home. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, alongside a washing machine, adds a layer of convenience for longer stays. The bathroom doesn't disappoint either, with a walk-in shower that promises a refreshing start or end to any day.

One cannot overlook the charm of dining al fresco on the apartment's terrace, where meals are enjoyed against a backdrop of stunning mountain views. The added benefits of soundproofing and a private entrance underscore the apartment's commitment to privacy and peace for all guests.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, the complex invites guests to embrace the outdoors with a dedicated picnic area, perfect for leisurely days under the sun.

For those looking to explore the local area, La Petite Beach and VIP Beach are within easy reach, located 1.7 km and 2.4 km away, respectively. Furthermore, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 95 km away, makes Amar Apartments a convenient option for international travelers.

In summary, Amar Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice in Sarandë, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, privacy, and scenic beauty, ideal for those looking to immerse themselves in the tranquility of the Albanian Riviera.
