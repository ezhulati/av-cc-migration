Each room at Hotel Lili-1 is thoughtfully equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a restful environment regardless of the weather outside. Guests will appreciate the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private bathrooms, making their stay both comfortable and connected. For those requiring a little extra space or wishing to enjoy the vibrant city from a private vantage point, select rooms boast balconies where moments of tranquility can be savored.

The hotel prides itself on its array of facilities, including a bar perfect for evening relaxation and a terrace designed for soaking up the sun. The commitment to guest satisfaction is evident through the provision of a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage services, ensuring a hassle-free experience from arrival to departure.

For explorers eager to discover the local area, La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach are conveniently located just a short distance away, offering serene settings for leisurely days by the sea. Additionally, for those traveling further afield, Hotel Lili-1 is situated 97 km from Ioannina Airport, with a paid shuttle service available to facilitate seamless travel arrangements.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and dedication to guest comfort, Hotel Lili-1 stands out as a prime accommodation choice for visitors to Sarandë, promising an enjoyable and memorable stay.
