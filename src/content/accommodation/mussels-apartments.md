Each apartment within this distinguished complex is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring tiled floors that lead to a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a refrigerator, and a cozy dining area. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV in a living space that opens up to a private balcony, offering an intimate outdoor experience. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, equipped with either a bath or shower, alongside a hairdryer for added convenience. Select units elevate the experience further with captivating lake views, while essentials such as air conditioning and ample wardrobe space are standard across all accommodations.

Beyond the confines of these inviting apartments, guests are treated to an array of facilities designed to enhance their stay. The sun terrace invites leisurely afternoons basking in the warmth, while the children's playground and barbecue area promise delightful moments for families. The provision of a 24-hour front desk and the ubiquity of free WiFi throughout the property underscore Mussels Apartments' commitment to guest satisfaction and connectivity.

Situated merely 1.1 km from the allure of Ksamil Beach 7 and a short drive from the natural wonders of Butrint National Park, 3.1 km away, this apartment complex stands as an ideal base for explorers. Moreover, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, 93 km away, ensures seamless travel arrangements for international guests. Mussels Apartments, with its harmonious blend of convenience, comfort, and natural beauty, secures its place as a top recommendation for those venturing to Ksamil.
