This air-conditioned haven not only promises a serene stay with its garden views but also ensures privacy and ease with private parking on-site and complimentary WiFi. The apartment is a strict non-smoking zone, maintaining a fresh and inviting atmosphere for all guests. Its proximity to Skanderbeg Square, a mere 4.3 km, positions it perfectly for guests eager to explore the city's vibrant heart.

The interior of Rena's Apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It boasts a spacious layout that includes a balcony for those peaceful morning coffees, a cozy bedroom for restful nights, a living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen for culinary adventures. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

For those keen on exploring further, the Bektashi World Centre and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania are within close reach, located 2.7 km and 3.1 km away, respectively. Additionally, the convenience of being just 16 km away from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Rena's Apartment an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in Tirana.
