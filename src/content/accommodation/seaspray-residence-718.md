This modern apartment is a breath of fresh air for guests, offering a serene retreat with its air-conditioned spaces that include a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher and kettle. The convenience of having a bathroom outfitted with a bidet and hair dryer, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxuries of a hotel.

For those who appreciate the ease of access to essentials, the presence of a minimarket on the premises is a thoughtful touch. Seaspray Residence 718 stands out not only for its exceptional location and amenities but also for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, making it an ideal choice for guests who prioritize health and wellness during their stay.
