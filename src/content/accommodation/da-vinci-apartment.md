The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom complete with essential linens and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels for entertainment. The dining space and fully equipped kitchen cater to all culinary needs, whether for a quick breakfast or a full-fledged dinner. Adding to the charm is a balcony that presents an unobstructed view of the sea, perfect for enjoying the tranquil surroundings. It's important to note that Da Vinci Apartment is a non-smoking and soundproof space, ensuring a peaceful and comfortable environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore the area, the apartment is conveniently located less than 1 km from Ri Beach and within easy reach of Vjetër Beach, just 2.3 km away. Historical landmarks such as Independence Square and Kuzum Baba are also within close proximity, making it an ideal base for cultural excursions.

Moreover, Da Vinci Apartment facilitates an effortless exploration of Vlorë and its surroundings by offering both bicycle and car rental services, providing guests with the freedom to discover the local sights and sounds at their own pace.

Situated 152 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment ensures that guests have a smooth and worry-free connection to and from their flights, making it a convenient option for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Da Vinci Apartment emerges as a top-tier accommodation choice for those in search of a comfortable, convenient, and captivating stay in Vlorë, promising an unforgettable experience by the sea.
