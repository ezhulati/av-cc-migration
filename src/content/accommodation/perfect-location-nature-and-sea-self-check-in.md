Guests are treated to a seamless stay with amenities such as free private parking and complimentary WiFi. The establishment prides itself on offering top-notch services including a 24-hour front desk, room service, and currency exchange, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free experience.

Each unit at Perfect Location, Nature and Sea - SELF Check-in is designed with guest comfort in mind. Accessible via a private entrance, accommodations are equipped with air conditioning, soundproofing, and a desk. The private bathrooms feature walk-in showers, adding a touch of luxury to the stay. For those who prefer to dine in, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, stovetop, and kitchenware is available in every apartment, alongside a cozy dining area. Bed linen and towels are thoughtfully provided, enhancing the home-away-from-home feel.

Outdoor enthusiasts will appreciate the barbecue facilities and private patio available for use during the warmer months. The property is located in an area renowned for hiking, offering guests the opportunity to explore the natural surroundings. Additionally, car hire services are available for those wishing to venture further afield.

Families will be delighted with the children's playground and picnic area, ensuring entertainment for all ages. With Qerret Beach and Golem Beach both within close proximity, guests have easy access to some of the area's most beautiful coastlines.

Situated 42 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Perfect Location, Nature and Sea - SELF Check-in also offers a paid airport shuttle service, adding an extra layer of convenience to the travel experience.

In summary, Perfect Location, Nature and Sea - SELF Check-in is a standout accommodation choice for travelers seeking a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty.
