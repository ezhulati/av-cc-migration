This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its spacious layout that includes 2 bedrooms, a bathroom, and a balcony that presents stunning mountain views. The living space is thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV boasting satellite channels, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and the convenience of free private parking, an elevator, and complimentary WiFi makes it an irresistible choice for both short and long stays.

Privacy and comfort are paramount at Big Apartment, as evidenced by the soundproofing and private entrance that ensure a tranquil retreat from the bustling city life. Its proximity to notable landmarks such as Independence Square and Kuzum Baba, both 18 km away, as well as Baro Beach, just 1.7 km away, positions it as an ideal base for exploring the rich culture and natural beauty of the area.

For those flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 167 km away, making Big Apartment a convenient and accessible option for international travelers. Whether it's the allure of the beaches, the comfort of a well-appointed living space, or the convenience of its location, Big Apartment stands out as a top accommodation choice in Orikum.
