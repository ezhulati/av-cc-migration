Designed with accessibility in mind, MEA Centre Apartment ensures a welcoming stay for all guests, including those with disabilities. The space is thoughtfully laid out with an air-conditioned bedroom, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen that boasts an oven and a coffee machine, making it perfect for both short and extended stays. The bathroom is well-appointed with a bidet and slippers, and guests can enjoy the added comforts of towels and bed linen provided in the apartment. For those seeking a sense of seclusion, the apartment features a private entrance and is soundproofed, ensuring a peaceful and uninterrupted stay.

Situated a mere 58 km from Podgorica Airport, MEA Centre Apartment offers an ideal base for exploring the vibrant city of Shkodër and its surroundings. Whether visiting for leisure or business, guests will find this apartment to be a perfect blend of convenience and comfort.
