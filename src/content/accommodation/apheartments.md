The accommodations at ApHEARTments are thoughtfully designed to ensure a relaxing stay. Each unit boasts a balcony that invites guests to enjoy their own private slice of Golem's scenic beauty. The interiors are equipped with a fully functional kitchen, complete with a dishwasher, making self-catering a breeze. The dining and seating areas, featuring a flat-screen TV, provide a cozy space for unwinding after a day of exploration. The convenience of air conditioning, coupled with a wardrobe and a private bathroom that includes a bidet and a hair dryer, adds to the comfort of the stay.

For those incidental needs or quick snacks, the property hosts a minimarket, adding an extra layer of convenience to guests' stay.

Situated 47 km from both the House of Leaves and Rinia Park, and with the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 42 km away, ApHEARTments ensures ease of travel to and from the property. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beaches or the cultural excursions in the vicinity, ApHEARTments serves as a welcoming retreat for travelers looking to experience the best of Golem and its surroundings.
