This apartment boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom, ensuring a stay that combines home comfort with the luxury of a holiday retreat. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complementing the non-smoking environment for a clean, refreshing stay.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests enjoy access to free WiFi throughout the premises, keeping them connected with the world or ready to plan the next day's adventures. For beach enthusiasts, Mali I Robit Beach is conveniently located just 600 meters away, promising sun-soaked days on the sand.

Cultural and historical sites are within easy reach, with the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre 46 km and 15 km away, respectively, inviting guests to immerse themselves in Albania's rich history. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 40 km away, makes Albi Apartment a hassle-free choice for international travelers.

Albi Apartment not only offers a strategic location and comprehensive amenities but also serves as a gateway to experiencing the beauty and culture of Golem and its surroundings.
