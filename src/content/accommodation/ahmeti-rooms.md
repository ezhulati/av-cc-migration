The property boasts a collection of apartments, each designed with the guest's comfort in mind. From private entrances and wardrobes to soundproofing for a peaceful night's sleep, every detail has been considered. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of flat-screen TVs in each unit, while the convenience of private bathrooms adds to the home-away-from-home feel. Select apartments elevate the experience further with terraces or balconies, offering breathtaking sea views and outdoor dining areas, perfect for enjoying the gentle sea breeze. Additionally, the presence of heating facilities ensures a cozy stay regardless of the season.

For families traveling with little ones, Ahmeti Rooms goes the extra mile by providing baby safety gates, showcasing their commitment to guest safety and comfort.

The location of Ahmeti Rooms is ideally situated for exploring the local area, with Ksamil Beach 9 just a kilometer away and the historic Butrint National Park within a short 3 km drive. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is 92 km away, making it a convenient base for both local and international travelers.

With a 24-hour front desk and currency exchange services on offer, guests are assured of round-the-clock assistance, making Ahmeti Rooms a top choice for those seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and tranquility in Ksamil.
