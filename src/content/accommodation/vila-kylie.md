Accommodations at Vila Kylie are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring tiled floors that lead to a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a refrigerator and dining area. Entertainment and relaxation are ensured with a flat-screen TV offering a variety of cable channels, while the privacy of an en-suite bathroom equipped with a bidet and hair dryer adds to the comfort of the stay. For those who appreciate a morning cup of coffee or the tranquility of outdoor spaces, select units boast balconies with garden views, alongside the standard inclusion of a coffee machine, air conditioning, and ample wardrobe space.

Families visiting Vila Kylie will find the addition of a baby safety gate a thoughtful touch, ensuring peace of mind during their stay. The guest house also invites guests to unwind in its lush garden, offering a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Situated a short stroll from Lori Beach and within easy reach of the historical Butrint National Park, Vila Kylie serves as an ideal base for explorers keen to discover the natural and cultural treasures of the area. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making Vila Kylie a convenient and accessible choice for a memorable getaway in Ksamil.
