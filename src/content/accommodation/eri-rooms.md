This 2-star apartment complex doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its air-conditioned units, each featuring a dining and seating area complete with a satellite flat-screen TV for those moments of relaxation. Select apartments elevate the experience further with a patio and breathtaking sea views, ensuring every stay is as picturesque as it is comfortable. The convenience of a private bathroom and wardrobe in all units adds to the seamless stay.

For dining, guests are treated to an outdoor area where meals under the open sky become memorable experiences. The addition of barbecue facilities allows for enjoyable evenings spent grilling under the stars, making the most of the balmy weather Sarandë is known for.

Beyond the comforts of Eri Rooms, adventure awaits with Maestral Beach just 3 km away, offering a sandy escape for beach lovers. Meanwhile, history enthusiasts will find Butrint National Park, located 18 km from the property, a fascinating site to explore. For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 98 km away, making Eri Rooms an accessible haven for both local and international visitors.

In summary, Eri Rooms stands out as a delightful option for those in search of a peaceful beachfront stay, coupled with the convenience of modern amenities and the beauty of Sarandë's natural landscapes.
