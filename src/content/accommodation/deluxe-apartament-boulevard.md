This accommodation doesn't just offer a place to rest but a home away from home, with two elegantly furnished bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, perfect for relaxing evenings after a day of exploration.

Strategically positioned, Deluxe Apartment Boulevard is within walking distance of notable attractions such as the Clock Tower Tirana, Et'hem Bey Mosque, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, making it an ideal spot for those keen to immerse themselves in the local culture. Additionally, the property is located just 2.5 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, offering a glimpse into Albania's past.

For guests interested in cultural experiences, the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves are conveniently nearby. Ease of travel is guaranteed with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 13 km away, making Deluxe Apartment Boulevard a top selection for both leisure and business travelers looking for an exceptional stay in Tirana.
