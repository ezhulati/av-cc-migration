The accommodations at Antonia Holiday Rooms are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each air-conditioned unit boasts a fully equipped kitchen, providing guests with the freedom to craft their own culinary experiences during their stay. The convenience of private bathrooms adds to the comfort, ensuring a home-away-from-home atmosphere. Complimentary WiFi access enhances the experience, keeping guests connected, while select rooms elevate the stay further with the addition of a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to unwind.

For those arriving by air, the nearest gateway is Ioannina Airport, located 98 km away, making Antonia Holiday Rooms an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers. This apartment complex not only promises a stay marked by ease and comfort but also positions itself as a gateway to the enchanting surroundings of Sarandë.
