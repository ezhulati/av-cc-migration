The apartment at Ocean Wisdom 582 is designed with space and comfort in mind, featuring a generous layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, and a terrace that boasts stunning sea views. Modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and air conditioning ensure a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. The kitchen, equipped with a dishwasher and an oven, invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like them, while the inclusion of a bidet in the bathroom adds a touch of convenience.

For those who love to dine al fresco, the property's outdoor dining area provides the perfect setting to enjoy meals with a view. Additionally, the availability of a car rental service directly from the apartment facilitates exploration of the surrounding area, allowing guests to discover local attractions such as the Rock of Kavaje and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha with ease.

Located conveniently 32 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Ocean Wisdom 582 also offers a paid airport shuttle service, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible for guests. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the call of local landmarks, this property stands out as a compelling choice for those looking to experience the best of Durrës.
