Guests will find themselves in a fully equipped haven, with a kitchen that boasts an oven, toaster, fridge, and a mocca coffee maker for those morning delights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV, DVD player, and CD player, complemented by the convenience of free WiFi throughout the premises.

The location is exceptionally strategic, placing visitors within easy walking distance of an array of restaurants, cafes, and shops, perfect for exploring local flavors and crafts. Moreover, the waterfront promenade unfolds right in front of the building, inviting guests for leisurely strolls along the seafront at any time of day.

With the added benefit of free private parking on site, Vlora - Your Home by the Sea presents an ideal blend of accessibility, comfort, and scenic beauty, making it a top recommendation for those looking to experience the best of Vlorë.
