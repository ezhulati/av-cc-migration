The highlight of EniGuesthouse is undoubtedly its spacious apartment. Equipped with a bedroom that promises restful nights, the apartment also features a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen comes complete with a microwave, fridge, washing machine, and all necessary kitchenware, making it perfect for those who prefer to prepare their own meals. Perhaps the most enchanting aspect is the opportunity to dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area, where meals are complemented by breathtaking mountain views, adding a touch of magic to every bite.

For those concerned about health and wellness, it's worth noting that the accommodations are strictly non-smoking, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

Travelers will find the location of EniGuesthouse to be exceptionally convenient, with Ioannina Airport located just 83 km away, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, EniGuesthouse offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and scenic beauty, making it an excellent choice for travelers looking to explore Gjirokastër and its surroundings.
