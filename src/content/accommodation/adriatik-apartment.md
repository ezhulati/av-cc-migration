The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, boasting a balcony that offers breathtaking mountain views. It is perfectly equipped to host a family or group of friends, with its three cozy bedrooms, a living room that invites relaxation with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen that's ready for culinary exploits with a dishwasher and an oven. The convenience of two bathrooms, including a bidet, adds to the comfort of the stay. Moreover, the option to dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area, with views of the serene inner courtyard, elevates the dining experience.

For those eager to explore the local area, Adriatik Apartment is ideally positioned. Guests can immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Durrës, engaging in activities such as diving and fishing. The apartment is also a stone's throw away from other popular attractions, including Golem Beach, Durres Beach, and the Rock of Kavaje, making it a perfect base for those looking to explore the rich offerings of the region.

Convenience is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 38 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

In summary, Adriatik Apartment stands out as a perfect blend of leisure and adventure, offering a comfortable stay with easy access to both the tranquility of the beach and the excitement of outdoor activities, all set against the backdrop of Durrës's stunning natural beauty.
