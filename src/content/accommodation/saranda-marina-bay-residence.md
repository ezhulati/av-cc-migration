The essence of modern living is captured within its air-conditioned spaces, boasting a balcony that invites the outside in. Guests are treated to a realm of amenities including a lift and round-the-clock security, ensuring both accessibility and peace of mind throughout their stay.

Designed to accommodate the needs of the contemporary traveler, the apartment features a generously sized bedroom, a bathroom equipped with essentials, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with streaming services for entertainment at any hour. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen, opens up to a terrace offering serene sea views, while a patio serves as a charming outdoor dining space. The commitment to guest comfort is further underscored by thoughtful touches like soundproofing and a private entrance, ensuring a secluded retreat.

Located conveniently, Saranda Bay Beach is just a short journey away, with Saranda City Beach slightly further, offering guests a variety of coastal experiences. For those looking to explore beyond, the nearest airport in Ioannina is within reach, with the residence providing a paid shuttle service for effortless travel planning.

Saranda Marina Bay Residence stands as a testament to the blend of luxury and practicality, making it an ideal spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Sarandë while enjoying the comforts of home.
