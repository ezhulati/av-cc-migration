The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with essential amenities, and a living area that merges modernity with comfort. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV with streaming services ensures entertainment is always at hand, while the fully equipped kitchen invites guests to craft meals to their liking. Perhaps the most captivating aspect is the balcony, offering unobstructed sea views, perfect for dining al fresco against the backdrop of the garden.

Privacy and security are paramount at Villa Pearl Adriatic Coast 1, with a private entrance for guests and round-the-clock security ensuring a worry-free stay. Additionally, the convenience of an on-site minimarket adds to the appeal, making last-minute needs easily attainable.

Located a brief journey from the cultural heartbeat of Skanderbeg Square and the natural wonder of the Rock of Kavaje, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich surroundings. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport within easy reach, Villa Pearl Adriatic Coast 1 stands out as a distinguished choice for those in pursuit of relaxation, convenience, and a touch of luxury on the Adriatic Coast.
