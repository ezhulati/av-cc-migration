The property boasts a range of amenities designed to enhance the stay of every guest. Among these, the air-conditioned apartment features a welcoming balcony, a cozy bedroom, and a living room that invites relaxation. The kitchen comes well-equipped, including a fridge, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have access to their favorite shows and movies. Adding to the convenience, complimentary WiFi envelops the property, allowing for seamless connectivity.

For those intrigued by local culture and history, the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks such as the Toptani Shopping Centre, National Gallery of Arts Tirana, and Tanners' Bridge enriches any visit. Skanderbeg Square, a mere 1.3 km away, offers a glimpse into the city's bustling life. Further enhancing its appeal, the apartment is located near Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, Saint Paul Cathedral, and the iconic Pyramid of Tirana, making it an ideal base for exploration.

Travelers will appreciate the non-smoking accommodation, ensuring a pleasant and comfortable environment throughout their stay. For those arriving by air, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being just 17 km away eases travel concerns.

In summary, Cosy, Centrally-Located Flat stands as a testament to the blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural immersion. It promises an inviting retreat for those eager to experience the best of Tirana, making it a highly recommended stay for discerning travelers.
