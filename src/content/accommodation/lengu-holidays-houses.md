The property is distinguished by its family-friendly rooms, each boasting a balcony that offers a refreshing air-conditioned haven. Guests are treated to modern conveniences including a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, ensuring a blend of luxury and comfort. For those preferring to cater to their own culinary needs, every unit is furnished with a fridge.

Adding to its allure, Lengu Holidays Houses is situated a mere 14 km from the lush expanses of Butrint National Park, offering an easy escape to nature lovers. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 96 km away, making the property easily accessible for international and domestic travelers alike. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a tranquil retreat, Lengu Holidays Houses stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those exploring the enchanting city of Sarandë.
