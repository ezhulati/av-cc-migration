Each room at Sunset Suites is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air conditioning, a desk, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with bed linen and towels. The highlight of each room is undoubtedly the terrace, offering breathtaking sea views that promise to enchant guests from dawn till dusk. Additionally, the convenience of a fridge in every room adds a touch of home comfort to the luxurious setting.

Guests can start their day on a high note with a continental breakfast served daily, providing the perfect fuel for exploring Sarandë or simply relaxing by the sea. For those who enjoy outdoor dining, the inn's barbecue facilities offer a delightful option for a casual meal under the stars.

Situated just 1.2 km from Saranda City Beach, with La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach within easy reach, Sunset Suites is ideally located for guests wishing to immerse themselves in the beauty of the Albanian Riviera. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is 98 km away, making Sunset Suites an accessible choice for both domestic and overseas visitors.

In summary, Sunset Suites not only promises a stay filled with relaxation and scenic views but also provides all the necessary amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience in the heart of Sarandë.
