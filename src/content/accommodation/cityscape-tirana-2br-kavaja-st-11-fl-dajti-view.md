The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, featuring two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a balcony that invites guests to unwind with a view. The kitchen comes fully equipped, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals, while a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment needs are met.

For dining enthusiasts, a variety of local eateries can be found in the vicinity, offering a taste of Albania's rich culinary heritage. Additionally, the property's proximity to notable sites such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the historical House of Leaves enriches the cultural experience of your visit.

Outdoor lovers will appreciate the close distance to Rinia Park, a mere 2.6 km away, perfect for leisurely strolls or picnics under the sun. The Clock Tower of Tirana, another must-see, is just 2.8 km from the apartment, adding to the list of accessible attractions.

With the convenience of free WiFi and the option for private parking, Cityscape Tirana 2BR Kavaja St, 11 FL, Dajti View caters to both leisure and business travelers. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 13 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze. This apartment stands out as an ideal base for exploring the heart of Tirana, offering both the comforts of home and the excitement of the city.
