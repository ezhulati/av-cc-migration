The accommodations at Sea La Vie Anesti are designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring air conditioning, private bathrooms, and fully equipped kitchens. Select units boast the added luxury of a private entrance, enhancing the sense of exclusivity and privacy.

Guests will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a garden and terrace, perfect for soaking in the breathtaking surroundings or enjoying a quiet moment. The convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi throughout the property allows for a hassle-free experience, ensuring that guests can stay connected and navigate their travels with ease.

For those needing to pick up essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises adds an extra layer of convenience, making Sea La Vie Anesti a self-contained oasis of comfort and relaxation.

Situated 123 km from Ioannina Airport, the property is accessible yet feels worlds away from the hustle and bustle, promising a tranquil retreat for families and solo travelers alike.
