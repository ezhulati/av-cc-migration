The apartment boasts a generous layout, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a pleasant stay.

For convenience, the property includes a snack bar perfect for quick bites and a minimarket for essential groceries. Those looking to unwind will appreciate the tranquil garden, an ideal spot for leisurely afternoons.

Strategically positioned, TownSquare Central apartment is within walking distance of several of Tirana's attractions, including the National Museum of History Albania, Rinia Park, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 13 km away, adds to the appeal for international travelers.

With its combination of strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a comfortable stay, TownSquare Central apartment stands out as a top accommodation choice for visitors to Tirana, whether they're in town for leisure or business.
