Positioned a mere 3.2 kilometers from the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and within easy reach of the National Museum of History Albania, guests are perfectly placed to explore the rich tapestry of Tirana's past. The Rock of Kavaje, a notable natural attraction, is also accessible, located 41 kilometers away, making it an ideal day trip for nature enthusiasts.

The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that presents captivating city views. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and toaster, allows guests the freedom to prepare their own meals, adding a touch of home. The convenience of a private bathroom with a bidet, alongside a separate bath and dressing room, ensures a comfortable and private stay.

For those arriving by car, the added benefit of complimentary WiFi and private parking on-site is a welcome feature, underscoring the property's commitment to guest satisfaction. The apartment's non-smoking policy and soundproofing add an extra layer of comfort, ensuring a peaceful and enjoyable experience.

Notably, the property is situated just 6.1 kilometers from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering an unforgettable journey to the mountainous landscapes that surround Tirana. Additionally, cultural aficionados will appreciate the proximity to the House of Leaves and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, promising enriching experiences just a stone's throw away.

With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 12 kilometers away, Cosy Apartment Tirana stands out as an accessible and inviting option for both short-term and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
