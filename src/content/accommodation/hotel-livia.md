Each room is thoughtfully furnished with a desk and a private bathroom featuring a shower, catering to the needs of modern travelers. The culinary experience at Hotel Livia is not to be missed; the restaurant serves a selection of both local and international cuisine, complemented by an extensive wine list.

Situated on the lively Qemal Stafa Street, Hotel Livia offers easy access to some of Tirana's most significant historical sites. A leisurely 10-minute walk will take you to landmarks such as the 18th-century Tanners' Bridge, the Palace of Culture, the iconic Tirana Clock Tower, and the Et'hem Bey Mosque, immersing you in the city's rich history.

For those arriving by train, the main train station is conveniently located just 1.2 km away, and for international visitors, Tirana Airport is a mere 15 km from the hotel, making Hotel Livia an ideal choice for both convenience and a taste of local culture.
