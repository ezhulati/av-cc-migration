The apartment boasts a generous layout, comprising two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with essential amenities, bed linen, towels, and a living area that opens up to a balcony presenting captivating city views. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV featuring a variety of cable channels, ensuring guests have plenty to watch after a day of exploration.

For those who prefer to dine in, the fully equipped kitchen provides everything needed to prepare meals just the way one likes. Whether it's a quick breakfast before heading out or a cozy dinner to wind down the day, the convenience of home-cooked meals adds a personal touch to the stay.

Situated 17 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport 97 km away, Michalis positions itself as an ideal base for both local and international travelers. Whether it's soaking up the sun on the nearby beaches or embarking on a historical excursion to Butrint, guests find Michalis to be a perfect starting point for their Sarandë adventure.
