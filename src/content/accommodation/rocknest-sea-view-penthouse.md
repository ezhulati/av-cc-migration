Guests are greeted with the convenience of complimentary private parking and seamless connectivity through free WiFi. The property elevates the guest experience with thoughtful touches like a lift and exclusive private check-in and check-out services, ensuring a smooth and personalized arrival and departure.

The penthouse itself is a spacious retreat, offering a terrace that presents breathtaking mountain views. It houses two elegantly appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and an oven, catering to all culinary needs. The presence of two bathrooms, each with a walk-in shower, adds to the comfort, while the air-conditioned space also features a bidet and a dressing room for added convenience. Notably, the apartment is designed to be allergy-free and maintains a strict non-smoking policy, ensuring a comfortable environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore, Maestral Beach is just 1.2 km away, offering a serene escape, while the historic Butrint National Park lies 16 km from the penthouse, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich cultural heritage of the area. For further convenience, the property is situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport and offers a paid airport shuttle service, making travel arrangements effortless.

Rocknest Sea View Penthouse stands out as a distinguished choice for travelers seeking a blend of modern luxury, comfort, and a strategic location to explore the enchanting surroundings of Sarandë.
