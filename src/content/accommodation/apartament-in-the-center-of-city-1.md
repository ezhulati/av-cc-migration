Guests are greeted with a spacious balcony that leads to a terrace offering serene garden views, setting the stage for a tranquil retreat. The apartment, designed to cater to the modern traveler, features two well-appointed bedrooms, air conditioning for those warm Vlorë days, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-cooked meal. Comfort is further ensured with the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, making for a hassle-free stay.

Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the accommodation proudly maintains a non-smoking policy throughout its premises. For those venturing from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 149 km away, making the apartment a convenient base for exploring the vibrant city of Vlorë and its surroundings.

With complimentary WiFi available across the property, guests can easily stay connected with loved ones or plan their next day's adventure in this captivating city. Apartament in the center of city 1 emerges as a top pick for those who value both the freedom of a self-catering option and the proximity to key attractions.
