The accommodation itself is a testament to modern living, featuring an apartment that boasts a balcony with serene garden views. The space is thoughtfully laid out with a separate bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and an oven, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with either a bath or shower, along with the provision of bed linen, towels, and a convenient laundry service, underscores the property's commitment to comfort and convenience.

For those looking to explore beyond the confines of the city, Podgorica Airport is situated a manageable 58 km away, making Historical Center City House 2 an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Shkodër. This property not only promises a stay that is as restful as it is enriching but also positions itself as a gateway to the rich tapestry of experiences that the region has to offer.
