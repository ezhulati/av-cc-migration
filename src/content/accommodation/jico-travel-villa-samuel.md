Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the lush tranquility of a private garden, setting the tone for a stay that promises relaxation and privacy. The villa itself is a haven of comfort, boasting air-conditioned spaces complemented by the modern convenience of complimentary WiFi and on-site private parking. For those looking to explore the area or arrive with ease, the villa thoughtfully provides airport transfer services and the option to rent bicycles directly from the premises.

The interior of Villa Samuel is meticulously designed to cater to the needs of its guests. It features a cozy patio for leisurely evenings, two inviting bedrooms for restful nights, and a living room that serves as a communal hub for guests to unwind. The kitchen does not disappoint, being well-equipped with an oven and a fridge, ensuring that home-cooked meals are within reach. The villa also prioritizes privacy and tranquility, offering a private entrance and soundproofing to create a personal oasis for every guest.

For those keen on exploring, sightseeing tours can be easily arranged near the property, and a car rental service is available for further adventures. Despite its peaceful location, the villa remains well-connected, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport situated 155 km away, making it an accessible choice for both local and international travelers.

Jico Travel-Villa Samuel is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that offers a blend of home-like comfort and the excitement of travel, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a memorable escape.
