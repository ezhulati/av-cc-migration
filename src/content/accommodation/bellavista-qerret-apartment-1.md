Guests at Bellavista Qerret Apartment 1 are treated to accommodations that boast balconies with sweeping sea views, cozy seating areas, and cable flat-screen TVs for entertainment. The presence of a fully equipped kitchenette, featuring a dishwasher and a fridge, adds a touch of home, while the private bathrooms with walk-in showers and hair dryers ensure a refreshing start to each day. Select units also benefit from the luxury of a private entrance, enhancing the sense of exclusivity.

Dining al fresco is a delightful experience here, with an outdoor dining area inviting guests to enjoy meals surrounded by the property's lush garden and tranquil inner courtyard. For convenience, a minimarket on the premises ensures that essentials are always within reach.

Located a mere 2 km from the picturesque Mali I Robit Beach and within easy reach of notable landmarks such as Skanderbeg Square and the Rock of Kavaje, Bellavista Qerret Apartment 1 serves as an excellent base for exploring the region. The property is also conveniently situated 44 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it accessible for international travelers.

Bellavista Qerret Apartment 1 stands out as a prime choice for those seeking a blend of modern amenities, comfort, and a strategic location for their next coastal getaway.
