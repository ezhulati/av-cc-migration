This air-conditioned haven is not just about its prime location; it offers a sanctuary of space and comfort. Guests are welcomed into a generously sized apartment adorned with a balcony that presents tranquil garden views, setting the stage for a relaxing retreat. The interior is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV, catering to both leisure and entertainment needs. The kitchen does not disappoint, being fully equipped with modern appliances including a dishwasher and an oven, ensuring that home-cooked meals are on the agenda for those who desire. The convenience extends to the bathroom, equipped with a bidet, and the option to have towels and linens provided for a nominal supplement.

For those arriving by car, the worry of parking is alleviated with private parking available on site, complemented by the provision of free WiFi, keeping guests connected and easing the planning of local excursions. Situated 28 km from the historical Durres Amphitheatre and within proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the apartment serves as an ideal base for those keen to delve into Albania's rich history and cultural heritage.

Accessibility is further enhanced by the apartment's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 33 km away, making Vala Mar Apartment-A41 an appealing choice for both international visitors and locals looking for a coastal escape. Whether it's basking in the sun on the private beach, exploring the historical sites of Durrës, or simply enjoying the comfort of a spacious apartment, Vala Mar Apartment-A41 stands out as a premier destination for discerning travelers.
