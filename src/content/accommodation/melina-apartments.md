The accommodations at Melina Apartments are designed with the modern family in mind, featuring spacious family rooms that open up to lush garden views and the tranquil sea beyond. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a private balcony in select units, perfect for soaking in the surroundings in solitude or with loved ones. The rooms are well-appointed, offering air conditioning, a dining area, and a cozy seating area equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. Privacy and convenience are paramount, with each unit boasting a sleek private bathroom and complimentary WiFi. For those requiring a little extra space, some units also include a dressing room, adding an extra layer of comfort to the stay.

Adding to the convenience, Melina Apartments provides a car rental service, making exploration of the surrounding area effortless. Guests can venture out to discover nearby attractions such as Mango Beach, located just 1.4 km away, or delve into the natural beauty of Butrint National Park, a mere 14 km from the doorstep. For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 96 km from the accommodation, ensuring a relatively smooth journey to this serene retreat.

Melina Apartments presents a compelling option for those in search of a tranquil getaway, where the beauty of Sarandë can be experienced in comfort and style.
