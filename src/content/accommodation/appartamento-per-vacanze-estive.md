Each apartment is a haven of relaxation, boasting a terrace that presents breathtaking sea views, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The interiors are thoughtfully designed with air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable stay during the warm summer months. Guests will find the living spaces equipped with flat-screen TVs, cozy seating areas, and a well-appointed kitchen that includes a microwave, toaster, fridge, and a coffee machine, catering to all culinary needs.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of crisp bed linen and plush towels, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience. The inclusion of family rooms makes this property an excellent choice for those traveling with loved ones.

Situated a short drive from the historical site of Kuzum Baba and 150 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Appartamento per vacanze estive stands as an ideal base for exploring the rich culture and natural beauty of Vlorë. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beaches or the comfort of the well-equipped apartments, this property promises a memorable stay for all who visit.
