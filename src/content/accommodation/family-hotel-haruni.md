Each guest room at Family Hotel Haruni is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with an array of cable channels. Safety is prioritized with the inclusion of a deposit box, and the private bathrooms come generously appointed with a bidet, complimentary toiletries, and a hairdryer. Select rooms elevate the experience further, offering tranquil garden views and private balconies for an intimate connection with nature. Convenience is enhanced with the inclusion of a fridge in every room, ensuring refreshments are always at hand.

Mornings at Family Hotel Haruni are greeted with a delightful choice between buffet and continental breakfast options, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. For those looking to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the region, Butrint National Park is merely 2.5 km away, offering a perfect excursion opportunity.

Situated 92 km from Corfu International Airport, Family Hotel Haruni represents an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to some of Ksamil's most enchanting beaches and attractions.
