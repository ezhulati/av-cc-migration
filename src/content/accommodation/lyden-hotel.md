Accommodations at Lyden Hotel are thoughtfully appointed with air conditioning, ensuring a restful environment. Each room is equipped with a desk, a safety deposit box for valuables, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a balcony to enjoy the fresh air. The private bathrooms come with a shower, and for a touch of luxury, a minibar is provided in guest rooms.

Culinary needs are well catered to with a daily breakfast selection that includes continental, vegetarian, and halal options, ensuring a delightful start to the day for all guests. For leisure, the hotel offers table tennis facilities and bike hire, perfect for exploring the local area or enjoying a friendly game.

The hotel's proximity to notable beaches such as Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, just 200 meters away, and Mali I Robit Beach, located 2.6 km from the property, makes it an ideal choice for beach lovers. Additionally, for those traveling from afar, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 38 km from Lyden Hotel, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

Lyden Hotel stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to Golem's beautiful beaches.
