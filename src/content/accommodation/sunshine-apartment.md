This apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest; it provides a spacious haven complete with a balcony that invites guests to unwind in the fresh air, two cozy bedrooms for a restful night's sleep, a welcoming living room for relaxation, and a well-equipped kitchen for those who love to cook. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have all the comforts of home. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the accommodation proudly maintains a non-smoking policy.

For guests concerned about connectivity, SunShine Apartment ensures that staying in touch is effortless with free WiFi available throughout the premises. Additionally, the convenience of Sarande Main Beach being just 500 meters away means that the sea's calming presence is always within easy reach.

Travelers will find the journey to SunShine Apartment facilitated by the proximity of Ioannina Airport, located 97 km away, making it a manageable gateway for international and domestic visitors alike.

In essence, SunShine Apartment offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and connectivity, making it an ideal choice for those looking to explore the beauty of Sarandë and its surroundings.
