The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring a terrace that invites relaxation, a cozy bedroom for restful nights, a welcoming living room, and a well-equipped kitchen for culinary adventures. Entertainment needs are well catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring a comfortable stay in a smoke-free environment.

Situated 47 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and just 1.8 km from the iconic Rock of Kavaje, Lulu Suites - Olive is ideally located for both adventure seekers and those looking to unwind. The nearest gateway, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently 37 km away, making this apartment a compelling choice for discerning travelers aiming to experience the best of Golem.
