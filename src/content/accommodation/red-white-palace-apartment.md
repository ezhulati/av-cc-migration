The apartment itself is a testament to contemporary living, offering a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, and a well-equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a toaster. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with a shower adds to the comfort of the space, which is further enhanced by its soundproofing and non-smoking policy. Guests will be delighted by the patio, which opens up to serene garden views, providing a perfect backdrop for relaxation.

For those keen to explore Tirana's rich history and vibrant culture, Red & White Palace Apartment is conveniently located near Tanners' Bridge, the Clock Tower of Tirana, and the Et'hem Bey Mosque. The Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering breathtaking views of the city from above, is just 3.9 km away, while the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is 2.9 km from the property. Travelers will find the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport to be easily accessible, located only 14 km away, making Red & White Palace Apartment an ideal choice for both short visits and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
