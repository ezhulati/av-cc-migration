The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, boasting an air-conditioned space that includes a bedroom, a living room with a satellite flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a fridge. The inclusion of a balcony allows guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the serene atmosphere of the surroundings. Designed with the discerning traveler in mind, the apartment ensures privacy and tranquility through soundproofing and a private entrance.

For those looking to explore the local area, Alex's Apartment facilitates adventure with its car rental service, making it effortless to visit nearby attractions such as the Rock of Kavaje or the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha. The property's commitment to guest convenience is further highlighted by the availability of full-day security, an ATM, and currency exchange services on-site.

Located just 32 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment stands as an accessible retreat for both international visitors and those traveling domestically. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the cultural exploration of Durrës, Alex's Apartment offers a welcoming base for all kinds of travelers.
