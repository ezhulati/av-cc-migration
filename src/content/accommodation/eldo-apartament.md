Located merely 1.4 km from the iconic Skanderbeg Square, the apartment boasts a generous layout. It features a bedroom that promises restful nights, a living room designed for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those inspired to cook, and a bathroom that caters to the essentials. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV for guests' viewing pleasure.

For those keen to explore, Eldo Apartament is conveniently positioned near several of Tirana's attractions. The National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania offers cultural aficionados a taste of local talent, while the National Museum of History Albania invites history buffs to immerse themselves in the country's rich past. Shopping enthusiasts will find their haven at the Toptani Shopping Centre. For travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 13 km away, makes departures and arrivals a breeze.

Eldo Apartament stands out as a stellar accommodation choice, blending convenience, comfort, and the charm of Tirana into an unforgettable stay.
