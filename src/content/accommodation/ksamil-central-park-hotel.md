Accommodations at Ksamil Central Park Hotel are thoughtfully appointed to ensure a restful stay. Each room boasts modern comforts such as air conditioning, a desk, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with fresh bed linen and towels. The added luxury of a balcony presents guests with captivating city views, while the convenience of an in-room fridge adds a touch of home.

Culinary delights await at the hotel's on-site restaurant, where a diverse menu features American, British, and French cuisine, alongside a commendable vegan selection. Guests can start their day on the right note with a variety of breakfast options, ranging from continental to Full English/Irish and Italian specialties.

Families will appreciate the children's playground, ensuring entertainment for the younger guests, while business travelers can take advantage of essential services like fax and photocopying, as well as an ironing service to keep attire crisp.

Situated near popular attractions such as Coco Beach, Ksamil Beach 7, and Bora Bora Beach, the hotel's location makes it an ideal base for exploring the area's natural beauty. For those venturing further, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Ksamil Central Park Hotel a convenient choice for international travelers.

In summary, Ksamil Central Park Hotel stands out as a premier destination for those seeking a harmonious blend of attentive service, comprehensive amenities, and a prime location in the enchanting town of Ksamil.
