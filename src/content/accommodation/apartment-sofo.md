This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its air-conditioned spaces, ensuring a cool retreat from the Mediterranean warmth. The inclusion of both complimentary WiFi and on-site parking adds layers of value, making stays hassle-free for those arriving by car or in need of constant connectivity.

The layout of Apartment Sofo is thoughtfully designed for comfort and convenience, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for those moments when dining in feels right, and a bathroom that caters to the essentials. The presence of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is always at hand, while garden views from the property offer a serene backdrop to mornings and evenings spent indoors.

For the explorers at heart, the proximity to Butrint National Park, located 17 km away, opens up opportunities for adventure and historical discovery. Additionally, with Ioannina Airport 97 km away, the apartment serves as a feasible base for international travelers.

Apartment Sofo, with its blend of comfort, convenience, and location, positions itself as an attractive option for those looking to experience Sarandë in a more intimate, homely setting.
