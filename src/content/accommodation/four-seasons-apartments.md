Designed with families in mind, Four Seasons Apartments not only boasts spacious family rooms but also adds a touch of home with its communal barbecue facilities. The variety of units available ensures that every guest's preference is met, whether they yearn for mountain vistas or the simple pleasure of a private balcony. Comfort is paramount, with each unit boasting air conditioning and a private bathroom, ensuring a restful and rejuvenating stay.

The property's garden offers a tranquil retreat, inviting guests to unwind and soak in the serene atmosphere. For those looking to explore beyond the immediate comforts, Lori Beach is accessible within a 300-metre stroll, and the rich natural heritage of Butrint National Park awaits just 3.1 km away. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport serves as the nearest gateway, located 92 km from the apartments, making Four Seasons Apartments an ideal base for both local and overseas visitors seeking the ultimate Ksamil experience.
