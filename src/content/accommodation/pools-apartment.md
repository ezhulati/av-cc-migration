This air-conditioned apartment is a haven of peace, boasting a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain views. Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, the apartment features a cozy bedroom, a spacious living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven and a toaster, catering to guests' culinary needs, while the bathroom comes with a bidet, ensuring a comfortable stay. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available, and the property prides itself on being non-smoking throughout.

Guests will also appreciate the apartment's thoughtful amenities, including a lift for easy access. Moreover, the stunning city views from the property add to the overall experience, making it an ideal spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the local atmosphere.

For those interested in exploring the natural beauty of the area, Butrint National Park is within a manageable distance, located 18 km from the apartment. Additionally, for travelers coming from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest, situated 98 km away, making Pools Apartment a convenient option for both domestic and international visitors.

In summary, Pools Apartment stands out as a top selection for guests who value both the vibrancy of Sarandë and the tranquility of a comfortable, well-appointed living space.
