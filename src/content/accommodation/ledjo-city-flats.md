The accommodations at Ledjo City Flats are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each apartment boasts a balcony that presents either captivating sea or city views, ensuring that every guest has a picturesque backdrop to their stay. The interiors are thoughtfully equipped with a full kitchen, including an oven, and a cozy seating area complemented by a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal setting for families or groups. Convenience is further enhanced with the inclusion of a washing machine and a sleek private bathroom featuring a bidet and slippers, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, Ledjo City Flats positions guests perfectly to explore the wider area. The enchanting La Petite Beach is just 1.4 km away, inviting for leisurely days spent by the sea. For those intrigued by history and nature, the Butrint National Park, located 17 km from the property, offers a fascinating day out. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 97 km away, facilitates ease of travel to and from the accommodation.

In summary, Ledjo City Flats stands out as a stellar accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and vibrancy of Saranda. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay, it is a compelling option for any traveler visiting the area.
