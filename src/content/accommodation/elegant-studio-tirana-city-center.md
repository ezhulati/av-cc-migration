Positioned just a stone's throw away from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and boasting a convenient elevator access, guests are treated to a hassle-free stay in a smoke-free environment. The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring a well-equipped kitchen complete with an oven and microwave, a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a contemporary bathroom with a walk-in shower. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort, while garden views provide a serene backdrop to unwind.

For those looking to explore, the apartment's proximity to Rinia Park, the Pyramid of Tirana, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana offers a cultural feast just moments away. Additionally, the convenience of a nearby supermarket caters to guests' everyday needs, ensuring a comfortable and self-sufficient stay.

Located 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Elegant Studio - Tirana City Center stands out as an ideal base for both leisure and business travelers aiming to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Tirana.
