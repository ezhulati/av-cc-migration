The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days and a balcony that presents a picturesque view of the garden, inviting guests to relax in a serene environment. The space is thoughtfully designed with a fully equipped kitchen, catering to the needs of those who prefer the comforts of home-cooked meals while traveling. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have access to leisure even within the apartment.

For those intrigued by local attractions, Meli Apartment is conveniently located a short drive from the historic Rock of Kavaje and the intriguing Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, making it an ideal base for explorers keen to delve into the region's rich history. Furthermore, the property is easily accessible from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 42 km away, making it a hassle-free option for international travelers.

Meli Apartment stands as a testament to the blend of convenience, comfort, and accessibility, making it a highly recommended choice for those planning a visit to Golem.
