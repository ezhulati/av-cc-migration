Guests at Monel Hotel are greeted with thoughtfully designed rooms that boast not only the comfort of air conditioning and ample storage but also the modernity of flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms. Each room is an oasis of tranquility, offering bed linen, towels, and the added charm of a balcony or patio, where guests can soak in either the majestic mountain vistas or the lively city panorama. For those who prefer a dip with a view, select accommodations also feature pool views.

Mornings at Monel Hotel start on a high note with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's proximity to Lori Beach, merely 700 meters away, and the historical Butrint National Park, located 2.8 km from the premises, positions it as an ideal base for guests eager to discover the natural and cultural treasures of the area.

For international travelers, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service to Corfu International Airport, 92 km away, adds to the seamless experience Monel Hotel strives to offer its guests. With a commitment to exceptional service, evidenced by a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage facilities, Monel Hotel stands out as a premier destination for those seeking an unforgettable stay in Ksamil.
