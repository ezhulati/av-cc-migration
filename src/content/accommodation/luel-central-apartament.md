This apartment is strategically located within easy reach of Tirana's most celebrated attractions, including the National Gallery of Arts and the Clock Tower, both less than a kilometer away. For those interested in Albania's history, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is just a 1.6 km stroll from the doorstep.

The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services, a fully stocked kitchen with an oven and microwave, and a bathroom complete with a hair dryer. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable stay.

Beyond the comfort of the apartment, guests will find themselves in a prime location to explore some of Tirana's finest cultural institutions, such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet and the National Museum of History, as well as the Toptani Shopping Centre for those looking to indulge in a bit of retail therapy.

For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, with Luel Central Apartament offering a convenient paid airport shuttle service. This property not only promises a peaceful view of the quiet streets but also positions guests perfectly to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Tirana.
