For those seeking relaxation within the guesthouse, a lounge area provides the perfect escape, with the option of packed lunches for day adventurers. The surrounding area of Peshkopi invites guests to immerse themselves in local culture and nature through walking tours. Moreover, the property features a garden equipped with a barbecue, ideal for social gatherings or a tranquil afternoon. Adventurous guests will appreciate the proximity to fishing and hiking opportunities, ensuring a memorable stay filled with outdoor pursuits.

Situated conveniently, Guesthouse Arber is just 97 km away from Ohrid Airport, making it an accessible destination for travelers. This guesthouse stands out as a prime choice for those in search of a blend of leisure and adventure in the picturesque setting of Peshkopi.
