Accommodations at The Home of Diplomacy are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each apartment is thoughtfully equipped with essential amenities including a washing machine, a flat-screen TV, a cozy sofa, and a work-friendly desk. The convenience of a coffee machine and the privacy of a well-appointed bathroom enhance the living experience. Free WiFi is a standard across all units, with select rooms boasting a balcony for guests to enjoy the verdant garden views. The attention to detail extends to the provision of crisp bed linen and plush towels, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those who relish outdoor cooking, the apartment complex offers barbecue facilities, perfect for a leisurely evening under the stars. Despite its serene location, The Home of Diplomacy remains within reach of the wider world, with Ioannina Airport situated 83 km away, making it a convenient choice for both local and international travelers.

In essence, The Home of Diplomacy merges historical charm with modern comforts, offering a sanctuary for families and solo travelers alike in the heart of Gjirokastër.
