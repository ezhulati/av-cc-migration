The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, alongside complimentary access to high-speed WiFi and the luxury of free private parking. For guests who prefer a smoke-free environment, Apartament Amarildo ensures a clean and fresh stay, positioned a significant distance from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, about 42 km away.

Accommodation includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all dining needs with a dedicated dining area. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a shower and the convenience of an in-house washing machine adds to the apartment's appeal, ensuring all the comforts of home are at guests' fingertips.

For those keen to explore the surroundings, the Rock of Kavaje is merely 7.4 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's natural beauty, while the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located 38 km from the apartment, presenting an intriguing excursion for history enthusiasts. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 32 km away, makes Apartament Amarildo an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers looking for a seamless travel experience in Durrës.
