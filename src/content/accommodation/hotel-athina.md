Each room at Hotel Athina is a haven of relaxation, equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. Select accommodations further enhance the guest experience with a terrace or balcony, offering a serene spot to unwind.

Around-the-clock assistance is guaranteed thanks to the 24-hour front desk, making sure every need is promptly met. For those looking to explore beyond the hotel's comforts, Tirana Airport is conveniently located 110 km away, making Hotel Athina an ideal base for both local and international travelers.
