The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, boasting a balcony that offers unobstructed views of the sea. It is well-appointed with a bedroom, a cozy living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a kitchen equipped with modern appliances including an oven and a microwave. The bathroom comes with a bidet, enhancing the comfort of your stay. An outdoor dining area invites guests to savor their meals against the backdrop of mountain scenery, making every dining experience memorable. The provision of bed linen, towels, and laundry services underscores the apartment's commitment to guest convenience.

For those with a penchant for exploration, Bral Currila facilitates adventures with its offering of packed lunches, perfect for day trips to explore the surrounding landmarks. After a day of discovery, the tranquil garden provides a perfect retreat to unwind and reflect on the day's experiences.

Located less than 1 km from the picturesque Currila Beach and within manageable distance from the historic Skanderbeg Square, the apartment serves as an ideal base for both beach lovers and cultural enthusiasts. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 36 km away, guests find Bral Currila - Peaceful Beachfront Apartment not just a place to stay, but a gateway to the rich experiences that Durrës has to offer.
