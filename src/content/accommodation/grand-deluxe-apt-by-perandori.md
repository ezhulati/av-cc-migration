The property boasts an air-conditioned, allergy-free apartment that is a testament to modern living and convenience. With its free WiFi accessible throughout, guests can stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventure in Vlorë without hassle. The apartment itself is a spacious haven, featuring three well-appointed bedrooms, two modern bathrooms, and a living area that combines elegance and functionality. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a fully equipped kitchen ensures that guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

One of the apartment's highlights is its balcony, offering breathtaking mountain views that provide a perfect backdrop for a morning coffee or an evening wind-down. The added benefits of a private entrance and soundproofing ensure that guests enjoy their privacy and tranquility, making it a perfect retreat after a day of exploring the city's sights.

Located just 3.1 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and a mere 150 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Grand Deluxe APT by Perandori positions itself as a prime location for both local and international travelers. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beaches or the convenience of its amenities, this property promises a stay that combines the best of Vlorë's natural beauty and modern luxury.
