The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring three well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complemented by the convenience of free WiFi throughout the premises. The inclusion of two bathrooms, each equipped with a bidet, ensures a comfortable stay for groups or families, providing ample space and privacy.

One of the standout features of this accommodation is its balcony, offering a private outdoor space to enjoy the serene surroundings. Moreover, guests benefit from complimentary private parking, adding an extra layer of convenience to their stay.

For those traveling to or from the area, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 150 km away, making Large Apartment an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors. This property does not just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home in the heart of Vlorë, promising a memorable experience for all who visit.
