The interior boasts a modern aesthetic, highlighted by a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, a dining area poised for memorable meals, and a terrace that opens up to breathtaking mountain views. Comfort is paramount, with air conditioning throughout and a bathroom featuring a bidet and a dressing room, adding a touch of elegance to the stay. The outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy meals under the sky, further enhancing the experience.

Families will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of an indoor play area, ensuring entertainment for children. For those looking to explore the surroundings, Mia Guest House facilitates both bicycle and car rentals, making excursions to nearby attractions like the Rock of Kavaje effortlessly accessible. The property is also ideally located for guests keen on outdoor activities, with cycling and fishing opportunities nearby.

Situated 44 km from the Rock of Kavaje and 79 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Mia Guest House offers a blend of convenience, comfort, and adventure, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a peaceful yet engaging stay in Divjakë.
