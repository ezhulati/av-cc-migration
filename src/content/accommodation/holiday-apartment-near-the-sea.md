The apartment boasts a generously sized living space, complete with a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, catering to a wide range of viewing preferences. For those who prefer to dine in, the kitchen does not disappoint. It is fully stocked with modern appliances including a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, making meal preparation not just convenient but a pleasure.

For everyday essentials, guests need not venture far as a minimarket is conveniently situated within the apartment complex. Those looking to unwind will find the garden an ideal spot for relaxation, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle.

Explorers eager to venture beyond the comforts of the apartment will find Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car within accessible distances, promising adventures and breathtaking views. Furthermore, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 35 km away, adds to the seamless travel experience, making Holiday Apartment Near the Sea a compelling choice for both short and extended stays in Durrës.
