The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring a bedroom with a cozy ambiance, a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment at any hour, and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen is ready for any culinary adventure, complete with an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those keen to discover Tirana's cultural heartbeat, the apartment is conveniently positioned near several of the city's most notable attractions. The Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Museum of History Albania are all within easy reach, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local heritage and arts scene.

Travelers will appreciate the ease of access to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 14 km away, making Rooftop Condo @Skanderbeg Square an ideal base for both short visits and extended stays in Albania's bustling capital.
