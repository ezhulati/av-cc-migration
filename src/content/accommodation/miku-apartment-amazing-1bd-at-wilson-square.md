This property doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a fully immersive experience. The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Tirana days and a balcony to enjoy the city's ambiance. The living space is thoughtfully designed, boasting a fully equipped kitchen complete with a microwave and coffee machine, ensuring all the comforts of home are at guests' fingertips. The inclusion of a bidet and bathrobes in the bathroom adds a touch of luxury, while the provision of towels and bed linen underscores the apartment's commitment to convenience.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate area, Miku Apartment makes it easy with an available car rental service. This added convenience opens up a world of exploration, allowing guests to discover more of Tirana's attractions such as the iconic Pyramid of Tirana, the interactive Reja - The Cloud, and the serene Rinia Park.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 16 km away, making Miku Apartment-Amazing 1BD At Wilson Square an ideal base for both short visits and longer stays. The combination of its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the added perks of a lift and a minimarket on the premises, all accessible via free WiFi, ensures a stay that's as effortless as it is enjoyable.
