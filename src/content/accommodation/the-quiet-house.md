Boasting a thoughtful layout, the air-conditioned residence features a cozy bedroom, a living room designed for relaxation, and a dining area perfect for intimate meals. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, invites guests to prepare their own culinary delights. Perhaps the most enchanting feature is the outdoor dining area, where guests can dine al fresco while soaking in the lush garden views.

Connectivity is seamless, thanks to complimentary WiFi, and the convenience of free private parking adds to the hassle-free experience. For those eager to explore, the former Residence of Enver Hoxha lies 39 km away, while the ancient Durres Amphitheatre is just 6.8 km from the doorstep. Adventure seekers can venture further to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 42 km away, or explore the natural beauty of the Rock of Kavaje, a mere 4.9 km journey.

Situated 32 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, The Quiet House is not only accessible but stands as a perfect base for both relaxation and exploration in the heart of Durrës.
