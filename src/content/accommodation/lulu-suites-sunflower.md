The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, equipped with a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV boasting streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Culinary enthusiasts will be delighted with the fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven, toaster, washing machine, stovetop, and kitchenware, making it a breeze to prepare meals. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort to the experience. Moreover, an outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy their meals amidst the fresh air.

For beach lovers, the proximity to Golem Beach, merely 1.7 km away, and Mali I Robit Beach, at a distance of 2.8 km, offers ample opportunity for sun-soaked leisure. Additionally, the convenience of being 37 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport facilitates stress-free travel arrangements.

Lulu Suites - Sunflower stands out as an exemplary choice for those in pursuit of a tranquil retreat, backed by modern amenities and strategic location, promising a memorable stay in Golem.
