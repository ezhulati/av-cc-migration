Each apartment at Villa Guximi is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring air-conditioned rooms that serve as cool havens on warm days. Guests benefit from the convenience of complimentary private parking and high-speed WiFi, ensuring a stay that is as seamless as it is relaxing.

The living spaces are designed with an eye for detail, showcasing terraces that provide serene garden views, flat-screen TVs with cable channels for entertainment, and dining areas for intimate meals. The kitchens are well-equipped, catering to the needs of those who prefer to prepare their own meals, while the private bathrooms come with showers, complemented by the provision of bed linen and towels, adding a touch of home.

Strategically located, Villa Guximi is a short 700-meter stroll from both Ksamil Beach 9 and Lori Beach, making it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is situated 92 km away, connecting travelers to wider adventures.

Villa Guximi stands out as a prime choice for those in pursuit of a peaceful beachside getaway, where the beauty of the surroundings is matched only by the quality of the accommodations and the warmth of the hospitality.
