The property boasts air-conditioned units, each equipped with a fully functional kitchen that includes a dining area, toaster, kettle, and fridge. Certain units also feature a private bathroom complete with a bidet, slippers, a hairdryer, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. The addition of kitchenware in each unit allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like.

For those who enjoy outdoor dining, a picnic area and a terrace are available, offering splendid views of the garden and the city beyond. The presence of an on-site bar adds to the convenience, providing guests with a cozy spot to unwind after a day of exploration.

Located a short drive from Mali I Robit Beach and within reasonable proximity to Skanderbeg Square, Best Summer Apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the local area. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 38 km away, the property also caters to the needs of international travelers, making it a compelling choice for both short and long stays in Golem.
