Designed with space and modern living in mind, the apartment boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and the convenience of a washing machine. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet underscores the property's attention to detail in ensuring a comfortable stay. Importantly, the entire space is dedicated to being a smoke-free environment, catering to the health and well-being of all guests.

For those traveling to or from the area, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 44 km away, makes Sol Apartment an ideal base. Whether it's the thoughtful amenities, the strategic location, or the promise of a hassle-free stay, this property stands out as a top recommendation for visitors to Lezhë.
