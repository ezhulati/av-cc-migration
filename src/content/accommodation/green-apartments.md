Each apartment at GREEN Apartments is thoughtfully equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable stay regardless of the season. Guests will appreciate the convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi access throughout their stay. For those who prefer to dine in, select units feature a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, and a private bathroom that includes a walk-in shower and hair dryer, catering to all the comforts of home.

Morning risers are greeted with a choice of continental, vegetarian, or vegan breakfast options, providing a perfect start to the day. For added convenience, a minimarket on the premises allows guests to easily pick up essentials without having to leave the comfort of the apartment complex.

Adventure seekers and leisure travelers alike will find plenty to do, with bike and car rental services readily available for exploring the picturesque surroundings. The area is renowned for its excellent fishing spots, promising a rewarding experience for enthusiasts. Families will delight in the children's playground, ensuring entertainment for the little ones, while adults can unwind in the tranquil garden setting.

Located just beyond the immediate tranquility of GREEN Apartments, Coco Beach invites with its inviting shores, merely 1.1 km away, and the historic Butrint National Park is within easy reach, located just 3.4 km from the property. For those arriving from afar, the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service from Corfu International Airport, 93 km away, ensures a hassle-free arrival and departure.

In summary, GREEN Apartments offers an idyllic escape, blending the allure of natural surroundings with the comforts and conveniences of modern living, making it an ideal choice for travelers of all tastes and preferences.
