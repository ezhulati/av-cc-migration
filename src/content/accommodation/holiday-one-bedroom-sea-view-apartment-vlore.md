The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, featuring a well-equipped kitchen with an oven and microwave, ensuring guests can enjoy home-cooked meals with ease. The living space is complemented by a comfortable seating area, a television for entertainment, and a modern bathroom outfitted with a walk-in shower. Attention to detail is evident in the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, enhancing the overall stay experience.

For those venturing further, Vlore Beach awaits at a mere 2.6 km distance, promising relaxing days by the sea. Additionally, the convenience of free WiFi and private parking adds to the seamless experience of staying at this property. Despite its tranquil setting, the apartment remains well-connected, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 149 km away, making it an accessible haven for both local and international visitors.

Holiday One Bedroom Sea View Apartment Vlore emerges as a highly recommended accommodation choice, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of home, making it an ideal base for exploring the vibrant city of Vlorë and its surroundings.
