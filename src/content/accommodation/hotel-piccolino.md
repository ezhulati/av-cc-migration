Dining at Hotel Piccolino is an experience in itself, with a restaurant on-site that serves a variety of continental or buffet breakfast options every morning, ensuring a delightful start to the day. The property also features a terrace, providing an ideal setting for guests to relax and soak in the picturesque surroundings.

The hotel's commitment to service is evident through its multilingual staff, proficient in Greek, English, and Spanish, ready to assist guests at the reception with any queries or needs they might have during their stay.

For those looking to explore, the hotel's prime location offers easy access to Sarandë Port, merely 40 meters away, making it a convenient base for day trips or further travel. Additionally, the UNESCO-protected Butrint National Park, a site of significant historical and natural beauty, is within a reachable distance of 31 km, offering guests an opportunity to experience the rich heritage of the area.

Hotel Piccolino emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a comfortable and convenient stay in Sarandë, with its prime location, excellent amenities, and attentive service creating an unforgettable experience by the sea.
