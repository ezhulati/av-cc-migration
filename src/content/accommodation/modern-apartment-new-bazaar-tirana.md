This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious sanctuary equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable retreat from the bustling city life. The inclusion of a balcony adds a touch of elegance, offering guests a private space to unwind. Housing two bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a well-equipped kitchen complete with a fridge, the apartment caters to all the essentials of modern living. The provision of towels and bed linen underscores the attention to detail and commitment to ensuring a hassle-free stay.

For those concerned about the environment and personal health, it's noteworthy that the accommodation maintains a strict non-smoking policy, promoting a clean and fresh atmosphere.

The apartment's location is unbeatable for those eager to explore Tirana's cultural landmarks. A short walk away lies the historic Tanners' Bridge, the bustling Toptani Shopping Centre, and the prestigious National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, offering a diverse mix of activities for all interests. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 15 km away, makes Modern Apartment, New Bazaar-Tirana an ideal choice for both international visitors and locals looking for a city escape.

With free WiFi available throughout the property and the convenience of a lift, guests are assured of a seamless and connected stay, making Modern Apartment, New Bazaar-Tirana a top recommendation for anyone planning a visit to Tirana.
