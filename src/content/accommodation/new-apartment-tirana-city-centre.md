Located strategically within the city, the apartment is a stone's throw away from significant landmarks such as the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and the Rock of Kavaje, making it an ideal base for explorers keen on discovering what Tirana has to offer. Moreover, cultural hotspots like Skanderbeg Square, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the National Museum of History Albania are conveniently nearby, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of Albania's history and culture.

The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and functionality. It boasts a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services, and a fully equipped kitchen. The inclusion of modern appliances such as an oven, fridge, washing machine, and minibar, along with essential amenities like towels and bed linen, underscores the property's commitment to guest comfort. The soundproof, non-smoking environment ensures a peaceful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle.

Adding to the convenience is the availability of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, catering to the needs of modern travelers. The presence of an on-site bar offers a cozy spot for guests to unwind after a day of adventures.

For those flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is merely 14 km away, making New Apartment Tirana City Centre a highly accessible option for international and domestic visitors alike.

In summary, New Apartment Tirana City Centre stands out as a sophisticated haven that promises not just a stay, but an experience tailored for those who wish to explore, relax, and rejuvenate in the heart of Tirana.
