Guests at ALBERTI are treated to air-conditioned rooms that promise a cool retreat from the warm Mediterranean sun. Each room is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including free WiFi, a washing machine, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home. Select units boast breathtaking sea views, inviting guests to unwind on their private balconies while soaking in the picturesque surroundings.

The convenience extends beyond the rooms, with complimentary private parking making it easy for guests to explore the region at their leisure. The property also features a terrace, offering a perfect spot for a morning coffee or an evening of stargazing.

Located just 49 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, ALBERTI is not only accessible but stands as a welcoming haven for those looking to immerse themselves in the natural and cultural riches of Albania. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the call of historical sites, ALBERTI provides a comfortable and convenient base for all explorations.
