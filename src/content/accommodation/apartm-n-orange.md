Apartmán ORANGE is a breath of fresh air for those who prefer a smoke-free environment. Its location is ideal, positioned just a stone's throw away from Durres Beach and a brief drive from the serene Golem Beach, offering a perfect base to explore the coastal beauty of the area. For those looking to venture further, the historic Skanderbeg Square is within reach, located 42 km away, making for an easy day trip.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air-conditioned spaces designed with guests' relaxation in mind. It houses a bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances including a microwave and fridge, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, and the convenience of a private bathroom, complete with a shower, towels, and bed linen, ensures a stay that feels like home.

For travelers flying in, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 36 km away, makes Apartmán ORANGE an accessible retreat for both short and extended stays. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the comfort of a well-appointed apartment, this property promises a memorable stay in Durrës.
