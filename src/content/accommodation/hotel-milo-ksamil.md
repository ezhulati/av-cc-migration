This 3-star haven boasts a serene garden setting where guests can unwind, complemented by the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking, a rarity in such a sought-after location. The accommodations at Hotel Milo Ksamil are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, offering mountain views that promise a picturesque backdrop to every stay. The inclusion of a patio or balcony with each room, overlooking the lush garden or the bustling city, adds a touch of serenity to the overall experience.

The attention to detail extends to the room amenities, with each space equipped with air conditioning, a desk, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom, ensuring a stay that is as comfortable as it is enjoyable. For those who cherish views, select rooms offer a glimpse of the sea, enhancing the stay with the tranquil sights and sounds of the water. Practical conveniences such as a fridge in every room underscore the hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction.

Culinary needs are well catered to with a choice of buffet or American breakfast, starting each day on a delicious note. The multilingual staff, proficient in Greek, English, and Albanian, are always available to assist, ensuring that every guest's needs are promptly met.

Located just 600 meters from Ksamil Beach 7 and a short drive from the enchanting Butrint National Park, Hotel Milo Ksamil serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and historical richness of the area. With Corfu International Airport 93 km away, the hotel remains conveniently accessible for international travelers.

Hotel Milo Ksamil, with its blend of comfort, convenience, and attentive service, stands as a testament to the delights of Ksamil, promising a memorable stay for all who visit.
