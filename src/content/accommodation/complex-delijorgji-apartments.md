It is well-appointed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. Additionally, the apartment includes a bathroom with a bath, enhancing the comfort of the stay.

For convenience, a minimarket on-site ensures that guests have easy access to essentials without having to leave the comfort of the complex.

For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, facilitating easy arrivals and departures.

In summary, Complex Delijorgji Apartments offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and accessibility, making it a compelling choice for visitors to Tirana looking for a memorable stay.
