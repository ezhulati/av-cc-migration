Each apartment at Beach Duplex is a testament to modern comfort, equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with an array of cable channels. The inclusion of a kitchenette and dining area allows guests the freedom to prepare and enjoy meals at their leisure, while the private bathroom comes complete with slippers, a walk-in shower, and a hair dryer for a touch of luxury. Select units further enhance the experience with a balcony or patio, offering serene views of the quiet streets below. For added convenience, all units are supplied with fresh bed linen and towels.

For those looking to explore the area, Beach Duplex offers a car rental service, making local attractions easily accessible. The historic Skanderbeg Square lies just 48 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the region, while the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 6.4 km from the property, presenting an opportunity for an unforgettable outdoor adventure. Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 42 km away, ensuring smooth travel arrangements for all guests.

Beach Duplex stands as a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and accessibility, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a peaceful retreat by the sea.
