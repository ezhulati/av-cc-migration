Each unit within this apartment complex is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of families and groups, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, a cozy seating area perfect for relaxation, and a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment at any time. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, microwave, fridge, and even a coffee machine, ensures that guests have everything they need to feel at home. Moreover, the convenience of having a private bathroom outfitted with a hair dryer, bidet, and shower, alongside the provision of fresh bed linen and towels, adds to the comfort of the stay.

For those who enjoy a good drink, the on-site bar serves as a perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploration. And speaking of exploration, Gaia Apartments is ideally situated for guests keen on discovering the local area by bike, with bicycle rental services readily available to kick-start an adventure.

Situated a mere 3.5 km from the historic Independence Square and 4 km from the iconic Kuzum Baba, the apartments offer an excellent base for guests looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and history. For international travelers, the convenience of being 151 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Gaia Apartments an accessible haven of comfort and relaxation in the heart of Vlorë.
