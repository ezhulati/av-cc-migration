For those looking to immerse themselves in the rich history and vibrant city life, Skanderbeg Square is accessible at 43 km away, with the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offering breathtaking views at 47 km from the doorstep. Additionally, the property is a stone's throw away, at just 90 meters, from Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, making it an ideal spot for guests wishing to experience the diverse coastal landscape of Albania.

The apartment itself promises a comfortable and hassle-free stay, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It includes one separate bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, ensuring a cozy night in after a day of exploration. Importantly, the accommodation maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

Cultural landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre are within reach, located 44 km and 13 km away, respectively, inviting guests to delve into Albania's rich history. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, situated 38 km from Relax Home Durrës, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

Relax Home Durrës stands out as a beacon for travelers seeking a blend of beachside bliss and cultural exploration in the heart of Durrës.
