Guests are welcomed into a spacious, air-conditioned haven that boasts not only a balcony for soaking in the sea views but also the option for al fresco dining. The apartment is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, ensuring ample space for families or groups. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and an oven, allows for the convenience of home-cooked meals, further enhancing the homely atmosphere.

Entertainment and comfort are not overlooked, with a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area available for guests' enjoyment. The two bathrooms, featuring walk-in showers, are stocked with fresh towels and bed linen, emphasizing the attention to detail and guest comfort. Additionally, the apartment's commitment to privacy and tranquility is evident through its private entrance and soundproofing.

For those arriving by air, the property's location 96 km from Ioannina Airport is made more convenient with the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless travel experience from arrival to departure. The Amazing Sea View Apartment emerges as a top-tier accommodation choice, promising an unforgettable stay in Sarandë.
