Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the convenience of a private entrance, setting the tone for a stay marked by comfort and privacy. Vila Xhafa prides itself on its accommodations, which are thoughtfully designed with air conditioning, ensuring a cool respite from the warm Mediterranean climate. The provision of complimentary private parking and WiFi adds to the seamless experience envisioned for every guest.

The living spaces at Vila Xhafa are a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring terraces and fully equipped kitchens for those who enjoy preparing their meals. Each unit boasts a dining and seating area, complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The charm of the accommodations is further enhanced by private bathrooms with showers and balconies that offer an outdoor dining area against a backdrop of the garden's tranquility. Practicality is not overlooked, as each unit also includes a wardrobe for personal storage.

For those who revel in the joy of dining al fresco, the guest house's picnic area provides the perfect setting for an idyllic day spent in the embrace of nature.

Vila Xhafa's location is ideally suited for explorers and beach lovers alike, with Coco Beach and the historical Butrint National Park within easy reach. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is located 93 km away, making Vila Xhafa an accessible haven for those seeking a blend of relaxation and adventure in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
