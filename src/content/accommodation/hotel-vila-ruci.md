Accommodations at Hotel Vila Ruci are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each unit is equipped with essential amenities such as a wardrobe, a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and hair dryer, a flat-screen TV, and air conditioning. For those desiring a bit more, select rooms also boast terraces, providing an ideal spot for a morning coffee or evening relaxation. Ensuring a restful night's sleep, all units are furnished with quality bed linen and towels.

The aparthotel doesn't just stop at comfortable rooms. Mornings here start with a delightful breakfast selection, featuring fresh pastries, fruits, and juice. Guests have the luxury of enjoying their first meal of the day in their rooms or at the on-site coffee shop and bar, which serves a variety of beverages throughout the day.

For leisure and relaxation, Hotel Vila Ruci offers an outdoor swimming pool complemented by a pool bar, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Families will appreciate the dedicated kids' pool, ensuring fun for all ages. Additionally, the aparthotel facilitates exploration and adventure with both bicycle and car rental services available, inviting guests to discover the local area at their own pace.

Situated near Coco Beach, Bora Bora Beach, and the notable Ksamil Beach 7, the hotel is an excellent base for beach lovers and those keen to explore the local attractions. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is located 93 km away, making Hotel Vila Ruci a convenient and accessible choice for a memorable stay.
