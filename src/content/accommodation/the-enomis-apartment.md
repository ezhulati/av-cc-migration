The Enomis Apartment distinguishes itself with its generously sized 2-bedroom layout, designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. The living room, equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services, serves as a cozy retreat for relaxation after a day of exploration. A fully equipped kitchen awaits those who prefer to prepare their meals, adding a touch of home-away-from-home comfort. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with complimentary towels and bed linen, underscores the apartment's commitment to guest convenience.

Adding to the appeal is the apartment's thoughtful provision of a dressing room, allowing guests to comfortably change and refresh themselves in privacy. The allure of The Enomis Apartment is further enhanced by its commitment to a smoke-free environment, ensuring a pleasant stay for all guests.

Situated a mere 1.6 km from the historic Independence Square and 2.1 km from the iconic Kuzum Baba, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë. For those venturing further, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making The Enomis Apartment an ideal base for both local and international travelers.

With the added conveniences of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, guests at The Enomis Apartment are assured of a seamless and connected stay, making it a top recommendation for those seeking to experience the best of Vlorë in comfort and style.
