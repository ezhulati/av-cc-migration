Each air-conditioned unit at Boho Central Apartment is meticulously designed for comfort and convenience, equipped with a desk, coffee machine, oven, fridge, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom featuring a walk-in shower. Emphasizing health and comfort, the accommodations are both allergy-free and soundproof, ensuring a restful stay. Moreover, the inclusion of bed linen and towels in all units adds a touch of home.

For those who prefer the coziness of homemade meals, the apartment boasts a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests to prepare their own food and enjoy it in the privacy of their dining area. Additionally, the property features a coffee shop, perfect for those mornings when guests wish to savor professionally brewed coffee without leaving the comfort of their temporary home.

Boho Central Apartment is ideally situated near several of Tirana's most sought-after attractions, including the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Museum of History of Albania, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania. For travelers arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 14 km away, making Boho Central Apartment an ideal choice for both convenience and tranquility in the vibrant city of Tirana.
