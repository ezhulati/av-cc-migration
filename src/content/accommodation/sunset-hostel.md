Guests at Sunset Hostel are treated to modern amenities, including complimentary WiFi and the option for a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a hassle-free travel experience. The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring air conditioning for those warm Shkodër days, a separate bedroom for privacy, and a cozy living room for relaxation. The inclusion of bathrobes, slippers, towels, and bed linen adds a touch of luxury to the stay, making guests feel pampered and well-cared for.

One of the standout features of this accommodation is its private entrance, offering guests an added layer of privacy and convenience. Moreover, the apartment complex boasts a charming picnic area, perfect for those looking to enjoy a day outdoors without straying far from their home away from home.

Located just 59 km from Podgorica Airport, Sunset Hostel presents an ideal base for exploring Shkodër and its surroundings. Whether in town for business or leisure, guests will find this apartment complex to be a comfortable, convenient, and memorable place to stay.
