Within its walls, The Cozy Flat offers a well-appointed living space featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment and connectivity are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels and complimentary WiFi. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and an oven, invites guests to prepare their meals with ease, while the inclusion of a washing machine adds to the convenience of longer stays. The bathroom, equipped with a shower, underscores the apartment's commitment to comfort.

For those in need of additional amenities, towels and linens are available upon request. Emphasizing a clean and healthy living environment, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

The Cozy Flat is not just about the comforts of home; its location serves as a gateway to Tirana's rich cultural scene. A stone's throw away, guests can discover historical sites such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, engage in retail therapy at the Toptani Shopping Centre, or enjoy an evening of culture at the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania. For travelers arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 14 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

In summary, The Cozy Flat stands out as an ideal lodging option for those looking to immerse themselves in the sights and sounds of Tirana, all while enjoying the comforts of a thoughtfully furnished apartment.
