The hotel prides itself on its well-appointed, air-conditioned rooms, each boasting a private bathroom and complemented by complimentary WiFi access. Guests are treated to the comfort of premium bed linen and towels, ensuring a stay that marries comfort with luxury. A standout feature of Joelle Premium Hotel is the rooms' balconies, offering breathtaking sea views that promise to elevate the guest experience.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, Joelle Premium Hotel ensures guest needs are met with impeccable service, including room service and a 24-hour front desk, ready to assist at any hour.

Strategically positioned, the hotel is a stone's throw away from Santa Quaranta Beach and less than a kilometer from Flamingo Beach, making it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is located 96 km away, making Joelle Premium Hotel an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Joelle Premium Hotel stands out as a premier choice for those seeking a serene retreat with the promise of luxury, convenience, and unparalleled service in the heart of Sarandë.
