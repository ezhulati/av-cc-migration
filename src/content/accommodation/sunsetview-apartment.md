Boasting comprehensive air conditioning and uninterrupted free WiFi access across the premises, SunsetView ensures a comfortable stay for all its guests. The apartment features a tastefully designed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a well-appointed bathroom, catering to the needs of modern travelers. Importantly, the accommodation maintains a strict non-smoking policy, ensuring a fresh and clean environment for guests.

For those flying in, Ioannina Airport is the nearest hub, situated 97 km away, making SunsetView Apartment an accessible choice for international and domestic visitors alike. Positioned just a few steps from the lively Sarande Main Beach, this apartment promises an unforgettable stay filled with breathtaking sunsets and the soothing sounds of the sea.
