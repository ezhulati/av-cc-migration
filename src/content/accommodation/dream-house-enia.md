The apartment itself is a masterpiece of modern living, boasting a spacious bedroom, a pristine bathroom, and an array of amenities including bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a dining area. The fully equipped kitchen and inviting balcony with garden views offer guests the perfect setting for a private outdoor dining experience. Adding to the exclusivity, a private entrance welcomes guests into a non-smoking and soundproof sanctuary where they can unwind with a glass of wine or champagne, making it an ideal retreat for those seeking peace and privacy.

For those who enjoy a social atmosphere, the property hosts an on-site bar, providing a cozy spot to mingle and relax. The garden invites guests to step outside and embrace the calmness of the surrounding nature, offering a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Located conveniently 59 km from Podgorica Airport, Dream House Enia serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich culture and history of Shkodër and its surroundings. Whether it's for a short stay or a longer retreat, this apartment promises an unforgettable experience for all who visit.
