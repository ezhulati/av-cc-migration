Apartament 15 is a breath of fresh air for those seeking a smoke-free environment. The apartment is a stone's throw away, merely 200 meters, from the tranquil Mali I Robit Beach, offering guests an exclusive retreat with direct access to the sea. The accommodation itself is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days. It houses two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a fully equipped kitchenette complete with a microwave, catering to all your culinary needs. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet and complimentary toiletries adds a touch of luxury to your stay. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV boasting a variety of cable channels, ensuring that guests have access to their favorite shows even while away from home.

For those intrigued by the local history, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 6.5 km journey from the apartment, offering a glimpse into the geological and cultural significance of the region. Additionally, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 48 km away, provides a unique insight into Albania's past. Travelers will find comfort in the apartment's accessibility, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 42 km away, making Apartament 15 an excellent choice for both international visitors and those exploring Albania.

In summary, Apartament 15 emerges as a top contender for guests seeking a harmonious blend of beachfront bliss, cultural exploration, and homely comfort. Its strategic location, coupled with the array of amenities it offers, makes it a perfect getaway for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and history of Golem.
