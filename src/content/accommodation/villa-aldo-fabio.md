The living room, featuring a flat-screen TV, provides a comfortable space for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

One of the standout features of this property is its commitment to guest privacy, highlighted by the inclusion of a private entrance for each accommodation. The addition of a balcony allows visitors to enjoy their own slice of Ksamil's natural beauty, whether it's sipping morning coffee in the fresh air or watching the sunset.

For those arriving by air, the convenience of Corfu International Airport being just 93 km away makes Villa Aldo-Fabio an accessible escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. With the added perks of complimentary private parking and free WiFi, guests are assured a hassle-free stay.

Villa Aldo-Fabio is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the enchanting landscapes and rich history of Ksamil, offering comfort, privacy, and a prime location for an unforgettable holiday experience.
