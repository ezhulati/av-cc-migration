The accommodations at Dolce Italia Residence are designed with tranquility in mind, featuring soundproofing and private entrances to ensure a restful stay. Each unit is well-appointed with modern amenities including air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom equipped with a shower and hair dryer. For added comfort, select rooms offer cozy seating areas, and all are equipped with heating facilities to cater to guests' needs year-round.

Morning at Dolce Italia Residence begins with a delightful culinary journey, offering both Continental and Italian breakfast options. Guests can savor warm dishes, local specialties, and fresh pastries, setting the perfect tone for the day. The on-site restaurant welcomes diners for lunch, dinner, brunch, and cocktails, promising a family-friendly atmosphere with a menu to match every taste.

For those looking to explore the area, Dolce Italia Residence provides an ideal base. The locale is famed for its cycling routes, and the aparthotel facilitates adventure by offering car hire services. Further afield, the historic sites of Kuzum Baba and Independence Square are both 16 km away, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local culture.

Situated 165 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Dolce Italia Residence also offers a convenient paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless travel experience. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a peaceful retreat, Dolce Italia Residence is a prime choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in Radhimë.
