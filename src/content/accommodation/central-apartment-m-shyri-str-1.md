The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting air-conditioned interiors that open up to a balcony offering sweeping city views. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a private patio, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Despite its central location, the option for private parking is a thoughtful addition, albeit at an extra charge.

Accommodation at Central Apartment M. Shyri str.1 is designed with the discerning traveler in mind. The one-bedroom layout is complemented by a fully equipped kitchen, including a fridge and stovetop, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips. The living area, complete with a flat-screen TV, provides a cozy setting for relaxation, while the bathroom is well-appointed with modern fixtures, including a bidet. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided in the apartment, which maintains a strict non-smoking policy.

For those who prefer to dine in or need quick essentials, a minimarket on the premises caters to guests' immediate needs.

The apartment's location is unbeatable for those keen to immerse themselves in Tirana's cultural scene. Within close proximity are notable landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Rinia Park, and the iconic Clock Tower of Tirana. For guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

Central Apartment M. Shyri str.1 stands out as an exemplary choice for travelers who prioritize location, comfort, and the freedom to explore Tirana at their own pace.
