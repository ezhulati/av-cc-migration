This accommodation boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a pleasant stay. Emphasizing a clean and healthy environment, the apartment maintains a strict non-smoking policy.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, Saranda City Beach is within 1.8 km, offering another spot for relaxation and sunbathing. Adventure seekers will find Butrint National Park, located 18 km away, an excellent destination for day trips to immerse in history and nature. Additionally, the convenience of Ioannina Airport being 98 km away facilitates easy access for international travelers.

Thousand Kisses Apartment stands out as an ideal lodging option for visitors to Sarandë, promising a blend of comfort, convenience, and the promise of memorable experiences.
