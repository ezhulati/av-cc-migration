Each apartment within Sweet Holiday House is designed with comfort and ease in mind. Guests can expect to find a cozy terrace offering tranquil garden views, a welcoming seating area for relaxation, and a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. The heart of each apartment, the fully equipped kitchen, comes complete with an oven and a fridge, allowing guests the freedom to prepare meals as they wish. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which are stocked with complimentary toiletries, and each unit's provision of bed linen and towels, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

The property boasts family rooms, making it an ideal choice for those traveling with loved ones. The added convenience of all units having their own private entrance adds an extra layer of privacy and ease for guests during their stay.

For those looking to explore beyond the comfort of Sweet Holiday House, Ksamil Beach 7 and Ksamil Beach are within easy walking distance, located just 300 and 500 meters away, respectively. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km from the property, making Sweet Holiday House a convenient and desirable choice for both local and overseas visitors seeking a tranquil beachside escape.
