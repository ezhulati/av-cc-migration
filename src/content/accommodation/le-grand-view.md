LE GRAND VIEW is not just about its prime location; the holiday home itself is a haven of comfort. Guests are welcomed into a space that boasts air-conditioned accommodation complete with a balcony to enjoy the fresh air and free WiFi to stay connected. The property is equipped with modern amenities including a lift and dedicated bicycle parking, ensuring a hassle-free stay for all guests.

The interior of LE GRAND VIEW is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. The holiday home features a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and an equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and an oven, perfect for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The bathroom comes with a bidet, adding a touch of luxury to the daily routine. Moreover, the patio serves as a charming outdoor dining area, allowing guests to savor their meals amidst the beauty of the garden views. The added convenience of a private entrance enhances the sense of privacy and exclusivity.

For those who love to stay active, LE GRAND VIEW offers the opportunity to play tennis on-site or explore the local area through fishing. The property's proximity to Liro Beach and the historical Kuzum Baba ensures that guests have plenty of options for leisure and exploration.

Situated 155 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, LE GRAND VIEW represents an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty of Vlorë.
