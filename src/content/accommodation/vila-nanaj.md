The apartment itself is a testament to modern comfort and leisure, featuring air-conditioned spaces that extend onto a private balcony. It is equipped with a PlayStation 4, catering to entertainment needs, alongside a fully furnished kitchen that includes an oven, fridge, and stovetop. The living area is thoughtfully designed with a cozy seating and dining area, leading to two well-appointed bedrooms and a bathroom equipped with both a bidet and a bath. The terrace, adorned with outdoor furniture, offers a splendid mountain view, enhancing the stay with an option for wine or champagne to toast to the scenic beauty.

For those who relish outdoor dining, the apartment boasts barbecue facilities and a private patio, perfect for enjoying meals under the sky. The presence of a garden adds a touch of tranquility, inviting guests to unwind in the embrace of nature.

Beyond the comforts of Vila Nanaj, adventurers will find Butrint National Park, a historical gem, just 18 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's rich heritage. For guests traveling by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest, located 98 km from the property, making Vila Nanaj an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors.

In essence, Vila Nanaj stands out as a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it an ideal choice for those looking to explore the charms of Sarandë while enjoying the comforts of a home away from home.
