The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air-conditioned spaces designed for ultimate comfort. It boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a bathroom stocked with fresh linens and towels, and a living area complete with a flat-screen TV offering streaming services. The fully equipped kitchen and dining area open up to a balcony that presents tranquil garden views, allowing guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the city's ambiance. The unique addition of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy.

For those looking to unwind, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot to relax, with the option of packed lunches for day trips. The outdoor dining area and fireplace offer a perfect setting for evening relaxation, especially after engaging in activities like fishing.

Situated 38 km from Skanderbeg Square and 42 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Bral Apartment 3 is conveniently located for exploring the local attractions. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 33 km away, adds to the appeal for both international and domestic travelers.

Bral Apartment 3 stands out as an ideal accommodation choice for those seeking a memorable stay in Durrës, combining the comforts of home with the luxuries of a beachfront location.
