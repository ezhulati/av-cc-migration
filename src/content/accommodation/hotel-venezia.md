Accommodations at Hotel Venezia are thoughtfully designed, featuring televisions, furnished balconies, and either serene garden or captivating sea views. Each room ensures privacy and comfort with an en-suite bathroom equipped with a bidet and shower, alongside complimentary toiletries for an added touch of hospitality.

Beyond the comfort of the rooms, guests have access to convenient amenities such as luggage storage and water sports facilities, enhancing the stay with both leisure and practicality. For those looking to explore the local cuisine, the hotel's restaurant offers a delightful dining experience with a variety of dishes.

Situated a mere 25 km from Tirana Airport, Hotel Venezia presents an accessible retreat for both international travelers and those looking to explore the rich history and culture of Durrës. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest satisfaction, Hotel Venezia emerges as a distinguished option for visitors to this historic city.
