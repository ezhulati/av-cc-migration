Accommodations at Vila Vasiliu Ksamil are thoughtfully appointed to ensure maximum comfort and convenience. Each room serves as a private haven, equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for a touch of modernity. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with a shower and hairdryer, adds to the rooms' appeal, while select accommodations also feature a balcony, offering guests a unique opportunity to soak in the city and mountain views.

The hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction is further evidenced by its comprehensive services, including room service, a 24-hour front desk, and complimentary WiFi access throughout the property. Families will appreciate the children's playground and barbecue facilities, promising fun-filled moments for all ages.

Mornings at Vila Vasiliu Ksamil begin with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's proximity to notable attractions such as Ksamil Beach 9, Lori Beach, and Bora Bora Beach ensures that guests have easy access to some of the area's most beautiful landscapes. For those venturing further, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 92 km away, making Vila Vasiliu Ksamil an ideal choice for both local and international travelers seeking a tranquil yet accessible getaway.
