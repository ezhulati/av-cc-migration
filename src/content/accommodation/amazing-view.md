The apartment itself is a haven of tranquility and modernity. Guests can enjoy the expansive layout, which includes a bedroom, a living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony that serves as the perfect spot for morning coffees or evening relaxation, all while gazing out at the sea. The kitchen is fully equipped with a dishwasher and fridge, catering to all culinary needs, and the bathroom comes complete with a bidet. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided.

Not only is the apartment designed to be non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful retreat, but it also boasts a 24-hour front desk and an elevator, making accessibility and assistance readily available at any hour.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate comforts of AMAZING VIEW, La Petite Beach and Maestral Beach are both within easy reach, located just 2 km and 2.2 km away, respectively. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 97 km from the property.

In summary, AMAZING VIEW offers an unparalleled stay in Sarandë, where modern amenities meet stunning natural beauty, all designed to provide an unforgettable experience for its guests.
