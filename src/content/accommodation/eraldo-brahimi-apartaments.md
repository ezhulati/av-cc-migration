Guests at Eraldo Brahimi Apartments are treated to a serene garden and a welcoming terrace, enhancing the sense of escape. The promise of exceptional service is fulfilled with room service and meticulous housekeeping, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and carefree. Each apartment is a haven of relaxation, featuring air conditioning, complimentary private parking, and the essential free WiFi, catering to the needs of the modern traveler.

The living spaces are thoughtfully designed, boasting balconies that offer picturesque garden views, cozy seating areas for unwinding, satellite flat-screen TVs for entertainment, and fully equipped kitchens for those who enjoy the art of cooking. The inclusion of an oven and fridge adds a touch of home, while private bathrooms with bidets ensure privacy and convenience. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of bed linen and towels, making for a hassle-free stay.

For guests who prefer the intimacy of a home-cooked meal, the option to grill on the barbecue is a delightful addition, offering a perfect way to end the day under the stars.

Explorers will find Sunset Beach within easy reach, just 1 km away, offering another spot for relaxation and sunbathing. The allure of nature is nearby at Butrint National Park, located just 3.4 km from the apartments, presenting an opportunity for adventure and discovery. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the gateway, situated 93 km from the serene retreat of Eraldo Brahimi Apartments.

In summary, Eraldo Brahimi Apartments stands as a testament to the beauty of Ksamil, offering guests a comfortable, convenient, and memorable stay.
