The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a well-appointed bedroom, a living space equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a fridge. The convenience of a washing machine and the luxury of a bathroom featuring a bidet elevate the comfort levels, with towels and bed linen adding a touch of home. Notably, the accommodation prioritizes guest privacy and security, featuring a private entrance and round-the-clock security.

For those everyday essentials, the on-site minimarket is a convenient addition, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips. The location of Apartment Soni is ideally situated for exploring Vlorë's attractions, with Vjetër Beach and Independence Square within easy reach. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is accessible, located 151 km from the property.

With its combination of strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and a focus on privacy and security, Apartment Soni presents itself as an excellent choice for visitors to Vlorë looking for a comfortable and hassle-free stay by the sea.
