This generously sized apartment is well-appointed with a bedroom that promises restful nights, alongside a living space equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is a highlight, boasting full equipment including an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided.

Guests can enjoy al fresco dining in the apartment's outdoor area, perfect for those balmy evenings. Notably, the apartment is within proximity to significant landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre, located 44 km and 13 km away respectively. For those arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a manageable 38 km from the Four Seasons Beach Apartment, making it an accessible option for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Four Seasons Beach Apartment stands out for its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay, whether one is looking to explore the local beaches or delve into the rich history of the surrounding areas.
