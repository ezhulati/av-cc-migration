This holiday home, situated on the ground floor for easy access, boasts two well-appointed bedrooms and a living space enhanced by a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, invites guests to prepare their meals with ease, adding a touch of home comfort to the vacation experience. The air-conditioned unit also includes a bidet and a dressing room, emphasizing comfort and convenience.

Privacy and security are paramount at Villa Olive, with a private entrance for guests and full-day security to ensure a peaceful and worry-free stay.

For those traveling to the area, Ioannina Airport is located 168 km away, making Villa Olive an accessible destination for both local and international visitors. With its combination of natural beauty, modern amenities, and a commitment to guest privacy and security, Villa Olive in Green Coast Resort, Palase, is an exceptional choice for those seeking a tranquil and comfortable beachside escape.
