The hotel prides itself on providing a comprehensive array of amenities designed to enhance the stay of every guest. From the moment of arrival, visitors are greeted with the convenience of complimentary private parking, ensuring a hassle-free start to their stay. The property boasts a welcoming terrace, an inviting restaurant, and a lively bar, setting the stage for relaxing evenings after a day of exploration.

Accommodations at Piazza Hotel JAKOVA are thoughtfully appointed to ensure maximum comfort and convenience. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, and a private balcony that offers captivating views of the cityscape. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, fresh bed linen, and towels, along with a handy fridge, underscores the hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction.

The hotel's dedication to service is evident through its round-the-clock front desk, room service, and the availability of a currency exchange for international travelers. The added convenience of free WiFi throughout the property allows guests to stay connected and share their experiences with ease.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local area, Piazza Hotel JAKOVA serves as an ideal base for engaging in a variety of activities. Whether it's hiking through scenic trails, fishing in serene waters, or snorkeling to discover underwater marvels, the hotel's proximity to natural attractions like Bora Bora Beach and Ksamil Beach enriches the stay of adventure seekers.

Situated a mere 92 km from Corfu International Airport, the hotel also offers a seamless connection for international travelers, making it a compelling choice for those venturing to Ksamil from afar.

In summary, Piazza Hotel JAKOVA distinguishes itself as a beacon of hospitality, where comfort meets convenience, and every stay is crafted to be memorable.
