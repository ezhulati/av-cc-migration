Security is paramount at Haris Apartment Tirana no.3, with round-the-clock security and a 24-hour front desk ensuring a safe and worry-free stay. The convenience of an elevator and the availability of currency exchange services on-site add to the seamless experience for guests.

The apartment itself is a haven of modern living, featuring air conditioning for those warm Tirana days. It boasts a separate bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it easy to unwind after a day of exploration. Importantly, the accommodation maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those keen to discover local attractions, Haris Apartment Tirana no.3 is conveniently located near several points of interest. The Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, Pyramid of Tirana, and Reja - The Cloud are all within easy reach, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the city's unique landmarks. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 17 km away, makes this apartment an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in Tirana.
