The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort, boasting a terrace that offers breathtaking sea views. It is well-appointed with a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a fridge. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the attention to detail in ensuring a comfortable stay. Additionally, the outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy meals with a view, further enhancing the experience.

For those looking to explore the area, Lila Bella Apartment serves as an ideal base. Sarandë is ripe for discovery, with activities such as fishing readily available for enthusiasts. The apartment is also a gateway to the region's natural and historical treasures, being just 13 km from the lush landscapes of Butrint National Park and 48 km from the ancient ruins of Fanoti. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest, located 97 km away, making the apartment accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Lila Bella Apartment offers an unparalleled blend of location, comfort, and amenities, making it a top recommendation for those planning a visit to Sarandë. Whether it's basking in the sun on a private beach, exploring the local culture and natural beauty, or simply enjoying the tranquility of a well-appointed apartment, guests are sure to find an unforgettable experience awaiting them at Lila Bella Apartment.
