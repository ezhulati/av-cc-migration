Upon arrival, visitors are greeted with the convenience of complimentary private parking, a rarity in such a sought-after location. The hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction is further evidenced by its round-the-clock front desk service and the comprehensive coverage of free WiFi throughout the premises, allowing guests to stay connected and share their experiences with ease.

Accommodations at Hotel Bega are thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a range of amenities including a desk for those needing to stay productive, a kettle and minibar for in-room refreshments, a safety deposit box for valuables, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a bidet, alongside a terrace or balcony in every room, adds an extra layer of privacy and comfort. Select rooms also boast city views, offering a unique perspective on the local surroundings.

Beyond the rooms, Hotel Bega encourages guests to immerse themselves in the local area's natural beauty and activities. Whether it's hiking through the scenic landscapes, fishing in the serene waters, or snorkeling to explore the underwater world, there's no shortage of outdoor adventures to be had.

For those looking to explore further afield, the historic Butrint National Park is just 3.3 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich cultural heritage. Additionally, the hotel's proximity to Corfu International Airport, 93 km away, is complemented by the availability of a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring smooth transitions to and from the property.

In summary, Hotel Bega presents an ideal blend of location, amenities, and services, making it a top recommendation for travelers to Ksamil seeking a comfortable and convenient base to explore the wonders of the region.
