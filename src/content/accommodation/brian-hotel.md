Accommodations at Brian Hotel are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and private balconies for a touch of luxury. The en-suite bathrooms are well-appointed with showers, complimentary toiletries, and slippers, ensuring a relaxing stay. For added convenience, each room and apartment also includes a seating area and access to satellite channels, catering to the needs of modern travelers.

Guests at Brian Hotel are greeted by a welcoming 24-hour front desk, ready to assist with any needs or inquiries. The hotel's terrace offers a tranquil spot for relaxation, while those looking to explore the area can take advantage of the guided tours organized upon request. Additionally, a partnership with a nearby restaurant allows guests to enjoy delicious meals at a discounted price, adding value to their stay.

Located 100 kilometers from Tirana Airport, Brian Hotel presents a compelling choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty of Velipojë and its surroundings.
