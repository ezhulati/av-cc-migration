Accommodations at Vila Ebel are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a private bathroom stocked with fresh bed linen and towels. Air conditioning and a wardrobe are standard in all rooms, ensuring a restful and organized stay.

For explorers eager to delve into the region's rich history, the Monastery Saint Naum is also located 43 km from the hotel, offering a glimpse into the area's spiritual heritage. Conveniently, Kastoria National Airport is the nearest airport, situated 70 km from Vila Ebel, making it a strategic choice for travelers seeking both tranquility and accessibility.
