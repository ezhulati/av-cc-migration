Guests are treated to air-conditioned rooms that not only offer breathtaking sea views but also feature thoughtful amenities such as desks and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with connectivity. Each room extends onto a balcony, presenting a splendid mountain vista, and is outfitted with a private bathroom complete with a bidet, free toiletries, and a hairdryer for added convenience. The presence of a flat-screen TV in every unit further enhances the guest experience, allowing for leisurely downtime.

The staff at Vila Olserd Shehu, proficient in both English and Italian, stand ready to assist guests around the clock, ensuring a welcoming and supportive environment throughout their stay.

For those looking to explore the local area, the hotel is conveniently located near popular attractions such as Coco Beach, Bora Bora Beach, and Sunset Beach, offering ample opportunities for sun-soaked adventures. Additionally, for international travelers, Corfu International Airport is within a 93 km reach, making Vila Olserd Shehu an accessible retreat for both domestic and overseas visitors seeking a tranquil yet engaging getaway in Ksamil.
