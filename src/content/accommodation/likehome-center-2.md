The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring a well-equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a bidet adds a touch of luxury to the overall stay. For those who value convenience, the presence of a minimarket within the apartment complex is a thoughtful addition.

Guests interested in exploring the local area will find themselves at an advantage, with Rinia Park, the Pyramid of Tirana, and the intriguing Reja - The Cloud installation all within easy reach. Furthermore, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 15 km away, ensures seamless travel arrangements for international guests.

In essence, Likehome Center 2 stands out as an exemplary accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant culture and history of Tirana, all while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed apartment.
