The hotel prides itself on its commitment to guest satisfaction, offering services such as room service, a 24-hour front desk, and complimentary WiFi throughout the premises. For those in need of assistance, a tour desk, luggage storage, and currency exchange services are readily available.

Each guest room is a haven of comfort, equipped with a wardrobe, air conditioning, and a TV for entertainment. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a refreshing start to each day. Select rooms also feature a balcony, providing a perfect spot to unwind and soak in the surroundings.

Mornings at Three Islands Hotel Of Ksamil are greeted with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's proximity to popular attractions such as Coco Beach, Bora Bora Beach, and Sunset Beach makes it an ideal base for discovering the beauty of Ksamil.

For travelers arriving from afar, Corfu International Airport is located 93 km away, making Three Islands Hotel Of Ksamil a convenient and accessible choice for an unforgettable stay by the sea.
