Guests at Ajla Apartment are treated to a seamless stay experience, courtesy of facilities such as an elevator, private check-in and check-out, and even a currency exchange service on-site. The apartment itself is a spacious haven, equipped with two bedrooms, two bathrooms, and essential comforts including bed linen and towels. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV featuring satellite channels, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook. The balcony, offering serene sea views, serves as the perfect backdrop for morning coffees or evening reflections.

For those interested in exploring the local area, the Rock of Kavaje is just 7.5 km away, offering a glimpse into the natural beauty surrounding Durrës. Additionally, the historical significance of the area is highlighted by the proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 37 km from the apartment. Convenience is further underscored by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 31 km away, making Ajla Apartment an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Durrës. With its non-smoking policy, the accommodation ensures a comfortable and healthy environment for all guests.
