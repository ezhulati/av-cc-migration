The apartment itself is a testament to spacious and well-appointed living spaces, featuring two generously sized bedrooms and a bathroom complete with essential linens and towels. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to all culinary needs, whether for a quick breakfast or a full dinner.
