This apartment stands out for its strategic location, a mere 500 meters from the iconic Tanners' Bridge and equally close to the Clock Tower of Tirana, allowing guests to explore the city's architectural marvels with ease. Skanderbeg Square, a pivotal landmark, is also just a short walk away, ensuring that guests are in the pulse of Tirana's vibrant life.

Retro Seven is designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs, featuring a single bedroom with air conditioning for utmost comfort. The apartment boasts a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals, and a bathroom that combines functionality with style. Entertainment is guaranteed with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, complemented by free WiFi available throughout the property, ensuring that guests stay connected and entertained during their stay.

For those interested in shopping and cultural experiences, Retro Seven is conveniently located near the Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, making it easy for guests to dive into the local culture and retail scene.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, making Retro Seven an ideal choice for both international and domestic travelers looking for a seamless experience in Tirana. This non-smoking accommodation promises a comfortable and memorable stay for anyone looking to explore the best of what Tirana has to offer.
