The accommodations at DUKA Hotel are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with essential amenities such as a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, and a flat-screen TV. The addition of a private balcony and bathroom, along with a seating area in every room, ensures a stay that combines relaxation with the comforts of home.

The hotel prides itself on its attentive staff, proficient in both English and Italian, ready to assist guests with their needs and enhance their stay. Whether it's providing local recommendations or ensuring a smooth check-in process, the service at DUKA Hotel is tailored to make every guest's visit memorable.

For those looking to explore, the hotel's proximity to notable landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, located 3.7 km and 3.9 km away respectively, makes it an ideal base for discovering the rich culture and history of Vlorë. Furthermore, for international travelers, the convenience of being 153 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the hotel's appeal.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and dedicated service, DUKA Hotel emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of leisure and exploration in Vlorë.
