This non-smoking accommodation boasts a meticulously designed apartment that features a bedroom, a bathroom, and is equipped with a flat-screen TV for guests' entertainment. Beyond the comfort of the interiors, guests are treated to captivating garden views, adding a serene backdrop to their stay.

For those keen on exploring the spiritual and historical essence of the area, the Monastery Saint Naum is a short drive away, located 8.8 km from the property, while the Saint Nicholas Church is 19 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's rich cultural heritage. Additionally, the convenience of being 47 km from Ohrid Airport makes Syzo Rooftop 360° an accessible retreat for international and domestic travelers alike.

In essence, Syzo Rooftop 360° presents an inviting option for guests who appreciate the blend of modern comfort, stunning natural scenery, and easy access to historical marvels.
