The villa's offerings are comprehensive, including airport transfer services and the added flexibility of a bicycle rental service, catering to the needs of explorers and leisure seekers alike. Each apartment at Villa Jico is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, boasting balconies with sweeping sea views, cozy seating areas, and flat-screen TVs for unwinding. The fully equipped kitchens, complete with ovens and fridges, alongside private bathrooms featuring walk-in showers and hair dryers, underscore the villa's commitment to guest comfort. Complimentary WiFi access and the provision of bed linen and towels in every unit further enhance the stay.

For those eager to delve into the local culture and landscapes, sightseeing tours are readily accessible near the property, with car rental services available for those who wish to venture further afield. The proximity to Vlore Beach, just 1.6 km away, and the Beach at Government Villas, 2.4 km from the villa, positions guests perfectly to experience the area's coastal beauty. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 154 km away, Jico Travel - Villa Jico stands out as a gateway to exploring the enchanting region, promising an unforgettable stay marked by convenience, comfort, and the allure of discovery.
