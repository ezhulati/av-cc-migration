Guests at Hotel Cakalli are treated to rooms that boast not only air conditioning and a private bathroom but also come equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a fridge. The inclusion of a balcony in each room, offering breathtaking sea views or captivating cityscapes, adds a touch of luxury to the stay.

The hotel doesn't fall short on dining options either, with a daily breakfast menu that caters to various preferences, including continental, American, and vegetarian choices. The on-site garden, restaurant, and bar further ensure that guests have everything they need right at their fingertips.

For those who value assistance and local insights, the hotel's multilingual staff are available around the clock at the front desk, ready to offer support in Greek, English, and Albanian.

Hotel Cakalli's location is also a key highlight, with proximity to popular beaches such as Sarande Main Beach, La Petite Beach, and Maestral Beach. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest, located 97 km away, making the hotel accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Hotel Cakalli emerges as a top-tier accommodation choice for those visiting Sarandë, promising a stay that combines the serenity of beachfront living with the convenience of modern amenities and the charm of Albanian hospitality.
