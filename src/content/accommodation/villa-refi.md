The property is distinguished by its lush garden surroundings, creating a serene atmosphere for guests. It is also conveniently located less than 1 km away from the scenic Ri Beach, making it an ideal spot for beach enthusiasts. For families or groups, Villa Refi offers spacious family rooms designed with comfort in mind.

Each air-conditioned unit at Villa Refi is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The addition of a terrace in every unit invites guests to relax and soak in the tranquil environment. Select accommodations further enhance the stay with a dining area and/or a balcony, perfect for enjoying meals or morning coffee with a view. Practicality is also considered, with ample wardrobe space provided in the units.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate vicinity, Vjetër Beach is located just 2.5 km away, offering another beautiful beach option. The historic site of Kuzum Baba is within a 3.2 km distance, presenting an opportunity for cultural exploration. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being 152 km away makes Villa Refi an accessible retreat.

Villa Refi emerges as a top recommendation for those visiting Vlorë, promising a blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty.
