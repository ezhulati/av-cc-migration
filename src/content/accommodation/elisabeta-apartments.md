Each apartment within this complex is thoughtfully equipped with essential amenities designed to cater to the modern traveler. Guests will find themselves enveloped in comfort with features such as a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, private bathroom facilities, and the added convenience of bed linen and towels. The attention to detail in ensuring a homely atmosphere is evident throughout.

For those who take pleasure in crafting their own meals, the inclusion of a kitchenette in each apartment provides the perfect opportunity to explore culinary creativity with the freshest local ingredients.

Beyond the confines of the city, the allure of nature calls from Butrint National Park, located just 17 kilometers away, offering a splendid escape for those inclined towards historical sites and natural beauty. Furthermore, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 97 kilometers away, underscores Elisabeta Apartments as a strategic choice for both domestic and international travelers.

Elisabeta Apartments not only promises a stay that is as relaxing as it is enriching but also positions itself as a gateway to the enchanting landscapes and rich cultural tapestry of Saranda.
