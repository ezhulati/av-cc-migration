Guests are treated to a suite of amenities designed to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay. The apartment boasts a spacious bedroom, a modern bathroom equipped with fresh bed linen and towels, and a living area that opens up to breathtaking sea views from the balcony. Entertainment and connectivity are guaranteed with a flat-screen TV featuring satellite channels and complimentary high-speed WiFi. The fully equipped kitchen and dining area provide the freedom for guests to prepare and enjoy their meals with a view. Additionally, the convenience of on-site parking and laundry services adds to the hassle-free experience.

For those traveling with little ones, the availability of a children's playground on the premises is a delightful bonus, ensuring fun times are in store for all family members. Moreover, the proximity to local attractions such as Independence Square and Kuzum Baba enriches the stay with cultural exploration opportunities.

Located 151 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, COASTAL RETREAT - Beach Apartment Vlore is not just a place to stay but a gateway to experiencing the enchanting beauty and vibrant life of Vlorë. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the charm of the city, this apartment serves as the perfect home base for an unforgettable Albanian adventure.
