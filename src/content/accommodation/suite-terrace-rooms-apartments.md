Guests are greeted with the promise of comfort and convenience, thanks to the 24-hour front desk, an elevator for easy access, and the added benefit of currency exchange services on-site. The accommodations at Suite Terrace Rooms & Apartments are thoughtfully designed with the guest's needs in mind. Each unit boasts a desk, a private bathroom with a shower and slippers, a flat-screen TV, and air conditioning to ensure a comfortable stay. For those who appreciate the outdoors, select rooms also feature a terrace, providing a perfect setting to unwind and soak in the city or garden views. Additionally, allergy-free options are available, catering to the needs of every guest.

The convenience extends beyond the rooms, with a minimarket located within the guest house, ensuring that essentials are always within reach. For those eager to explore, sightseeing tours can be arranged nearby, offering a glimpse into the rich history and natural beauty of the area. Moreover, a car rental service is available on-site, providing the freedom to discover the surroundings at one's own pace.

Located 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, Suite Terrace Rooms & Apartments stands as an ideal choice for travelers in search of a comfortable, convenient, and culturally rich lodging experience in Korçë.
