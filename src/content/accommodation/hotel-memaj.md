Upon arrival, visitors are greeted with a plethora of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The hotel boasts an inviting outdoor swimming pool, complemented by the luxury of a hot tub, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. For those arriving by car, the convenience of free private parking lifts the burden of finding a secure spot. The serene garden and welcoming bar area provide ideal settings for relaxation and socializing.

Accommodations at Hotel Memaj are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a wardrobe, kettle, fridge, minibar, and safety deposit box, ensuring all needs are met. Entertainment and connectivity are catered for with free WiFi and a flat-screen TV, while the private bathroom with a bidet adds an extra touch of privacy and convenience. Select rooms elevate the experience further, offering breathtaking sea views from private balconies, alongside a cozy seating area for moments of reflection or intimate conversations.

The culinary experience at Hotel Memaj is equally impressive. Guests have the choice of starting their day with a buffet, continental, or Full English/Irish breakfast, providing the perfect fuel for a day of adventure.

For those concerned with logistics, the hotel's 24-hour front desk, airport transfer services, and room service ensure a seamless and hassle-free experience from arrival to departure. Situated 93 km from Corfu International Airport, the journey to Hotel Memaj is a straightforward affair, making it an accessible escape for international travelers.

In summary, Hotel Memaj is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the beauty and charm of Ksamil, offering an unparalleled blend of convenience, comfort, and luxury.
