The property boasts a sprawling garden, complete with a fire pit, where guests can unwind and enjoy the tranquil surroundings. The addition of a balcony enhances the outdoor living experience, allowing for picturesque dining or relaxation moments. With the convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, guests are assured of a hassle-free stay.

Accommodations at this holiday home are thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. The interior features two cozy bedrooms, a spacious living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and an equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and toaster for home-cooked meals. The bathroom is well-appointed with a shower, ensuring a comfortable stay. The charm of the property is further amplified by the outdoor dining area and the warmth of the fireplace, perfect for cooler evenings. Guests are also treated to additional amenities such as fresh fruits, adding a personal touch to their stay.

Situated a mere 3.7 km from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and 45 km from the Rock of Kavaje, the holiday home serves as a convenient gateway to explore the local attractions. For those traveling by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 16 km away, making Spacious House with a Big Garden and Fire Pit an accessible choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a memorable stay in Tirana.
