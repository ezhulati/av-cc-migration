This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a serene retreat with its spacious layout that includes two bedrooms, a living room, and a balcony that presents quiet street views. The inclusion of modern comforts such as air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and an equipped kitchen with an oven and toaster, ensures guests feel at home. The bathroom, equipped with a hot tub, adds a touch of luxury to the stay, making it perfect for relaxation after a day of exploration.

One of the standout features of Gjoni's Apartment is the convenience it offers. Guests will appreciate the complimentary WiFi and on-site parking, making it an ideal choice for those traveling by car. The added benefit of a private entrance enhances the feeling of exclusivity and privacy.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, Gjoni's Apartment facilitates adventure with its car rental service, allowing guests to easily visit nearby attractions such as Maestral Beach and the historic Butrint National Park.

Situated 17 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport 97 km away, the apartment serves as a perfect base for both local and international travelers. Whether it's the allure of the Albanian Riviera or the historical exploration in the region, Gjoni's Apartment positions itself as a comfortable and convenient hub for all kinds of travelers.
