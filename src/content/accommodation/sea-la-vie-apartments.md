Each unit within this esteemed apartment complex is equipped with air conditioning and a private bathroom, ensuring comfort is never compromised. For those who value connectivity, complimentary WiFi is available throughout, with select rooms elevating the experience further with the addition of a terrace, perfect for soaking in the serene surroundings.

Located a brief 3.2 km journey from the enchanting Butrint National Park, Sea La Vie Apartments presents an ideal base for explorers keen to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of local history and natural beauty. For international guests, the journey is made effortless with Corfu International Airport situated 93 km away, making Sea La Vie Apartments a compelling choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and natural beauty.
