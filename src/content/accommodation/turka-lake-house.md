The accommodations at Turka Lake House are thoughtfully designed with family-friendly rooms that blend comfort with elegance. Each unit is equipped with a terrace offering breathtaking mountain vistas, a cozy seating area for relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring a fridge and stovetop for those who prefer to prepare their own meals. The private bathrooms come with a bath and a hairdryer, adding to the convenience. Moreover, guests can enjoy the luxury of a patio with an outdoor dining area, perfect for savoring meals while soaking in the stunning lake views. Practical amenities such as a desk and a kettle in each unit underscore the guest house's attention to detail.

For those who appreciate the outdoors, the property includes a picnic area, ideal for family gatherings or a quiet afternoon amidst nature. Additionally, a minimarket on-site ensures that guests have easy access to essentials during their stay.

Situated a mere 30 km from Ohrid Airport, Turka Lake House serves as an ideal base for exploring the region's natural beauty and historical sites, including the Bay of Bones and the Early Christian Basilica, both located 38 km away. This guest house stands out as a perfect blend of natural beauty, comfort, and convenience, making it a top choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay in Pogradec.
