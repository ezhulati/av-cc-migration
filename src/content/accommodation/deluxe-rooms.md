Guests at Deluxe Rooms are treated to the luxury of private bathrooms and the modern convenience of complimentary WiFi in every unit. The promise of relaxation and ease is further fulfilled by the inclusion of air conditioning and flat-screen TVs, ensuring a stay that combines the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.

For those arriving by air, the Ioannina Airport is located 96 km away, making Deluxe Rooms an accessible retreat for both domestic and international visitors. While private parking is available for an additional fee, the true value of this accommodation lies in its ability to provide a serene and comfortable base from which guests can explore the enchanting surroundings of Sarandë.
