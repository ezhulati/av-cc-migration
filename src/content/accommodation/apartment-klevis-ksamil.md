This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a serene retreat with its breathtaking balcony sea views that promise a tranquil start or end to any day. Inside, guests are treated to modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen boasting an oven, toaster, and fridge, ensuring that home comforts are never far away. The inclusion of a bidet and slippers in the bathroom adds a touch of luxury to the overall experience, while the private entrance ensures utmost privacy and ease of access.

Convenience is further enhanced with both WiFi and parking available at no extra cost, allowing guests to stay connected and explore the surrounding area with ease. The non-smoking policy throughout the property ensures a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

For those needing last-minute essentials or snacks, the on-site minimarket is a welcome feature, adding to the self-sufficient appeal of Apartment Klevis Ksamil. Meanwhile, the proximity to Butrint National Park, just 2.8 km away, offers a perfect opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and historical richness of the area.

Located 92 km from Corfu International Airport, the apartment also caters to international travelers, making it an accessible haven for those coming from afar. Apartment Klevis Ksamil stands out as a top recommendation for those seeking a memorable stay in Ksamil, combining the comforts of home with the beauty and excitement of the Albanian Riviera.
