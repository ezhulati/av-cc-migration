This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a serene haven with its spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a bathroom, and a balcony offering tranquil street views. The attention to detail is evident in the provision of modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and soundproofing to ensure a peaceful stay. Moreover, the apartment is designed to cater to the needs of every guest, being allergy-free and strictly non-smoking.

For those looking to explore Tirana on wheels, TAM Serviced Apartment City Centre No1 facilitates both bicycle and car rental services, adding a layer of convenience to your travel plans. The proximity to significant landmarks like the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Museum of History Albania, and the House of Leaves enriches the stay experience by offering a glimpse into Albania's storied past.

With the added benefits of a 24-hour front desk, full-day security, and complimentary WiFi access throughout the property, guests are assured of both comfort and peace of mind during their stay. Situated just 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, this apartment ensures that your Albanian adventure begins and ends with ease.
