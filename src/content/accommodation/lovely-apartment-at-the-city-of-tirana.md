This air-conditioned one-bedroom haven doesn't just promise a restful night's sleep; it delivers a home-away-from-home experience. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a minibar, ensuring all the comforts of home are at your fingertips. Plus, with complimentary WiFi, staying connected with loved ones or planning the next day's adventures is a breeze.

For those keen on exploring local culture and entertainment, the Lovely Apartment is conveniently positioned near several of Tirana's most sought-after attractions. The Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana are all within easy reach, promising a rich tapestry of experiences. History enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, just 1.5 km away, offering a glimpse into Albania's past.

Situated 43 km from the serene Rock of Kavaje and with the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, only 14 km away, guests at the Lovely Apartment enjoy both the tranquility of a residential setting and the convenience of easy travel connections.

Embrace the opportunity to experience Tirana in unparalleled comfort and style at the Lovely Apartment, where every detail is designed with your satisfaction in mind.
