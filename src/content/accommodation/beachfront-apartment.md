This property stands out for its generous offering of amenities, including complimentary WiFi and private parking, ensuring a hassle-free stay. The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort, featuring a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, bathroom, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. Guests will appreciate the convenience of a fully equipped kitchen, a dining area for intimate meals, and the modern comforts of air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and a walk-in shower adds to the seamless experience, while soundproofing and a strict non-smoking policy ensure peace and tranquility throughout the stay.

For those traveling with children, the availability of outdoor play equipment is a welcome addition, promising fun-filled moments under the sun. The apartment's proximity to Radhimë Beach and Nettuno Beach, along with its easy access to cultural landmarks like Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, positions it as an ideal base for exploring the rich offerings of the region.

Located 165 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Beachfront Apartment is not just a place to stay but a gateway to the myriad of experiences that Orikum has to offer. Whether it's the allure of the beach or the call of the mountains, this property ensures a memorable stay for all who visit.
