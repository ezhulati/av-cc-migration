Guests at Potami Apartment are treated to breathtaking mountain views, enhancing the serene atmosphere of their stay. Each unit is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a washing machine, a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven, and a private bathroom complete with a hair dryer, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The addition of a wardrobe in each unit adds a touch of convenience for guests.

For those traveling to or from the area, Ioannina Airport is located 142 km away, making Potami Apartment an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors. This apartment emerges as a top recommendation for those in pursuit of a peaceful beachside getaway, offering both the beauty of nature and the comforts of modern living.
