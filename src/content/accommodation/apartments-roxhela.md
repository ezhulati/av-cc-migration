Accommodations at Apartments Roxhela are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each apartment boasts a terrace that opens up to breathtaking sea views, providing a tranquil backdrop for your stay. The living spaces are thoughtfully appointed with comfortable seating areas and flat-screen TVs, ensuring a relaxing environment. Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and an oven, making it easy to prepare meals just the way you like them. The inclusion of private bathrooms with either a bath or shower, along with a hair dryer, adds to the comfort of your stay.

For those with specific needs, Apartments Roxhela offers allergy-free units, ensuring a comfortable environment for every guest. The convenience of air conditioning and a desk in all units caters to both leisure and business travelers, ensuring a pleasant and productive stay.

Adding to the appeal, Apartments Roxhela provides a car rental service, making it effortless for guests to explore the surrounding area at their own pace. The proximity to Butrint National Park, just 15 km away, offers an excellent opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and historical significance of the region. Furthermore, the accommodation is conveniently located 96 km from Ioannina Airport, facilitating easy access for international and domestic travelers.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and dedication to guest comfort, Apartments Roxhela presents itself as an outstanding accommodation choice for those looking to experience the best of Sarandë.
