Rooms Miri offers a cozy retreat in its well-appointed apartment, which includes a bedroom that promises restful nights. The space is thoughtfully designed with a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. The inclusion of a modern flat-screen TV ensures that entertainment is always at hand, providing a homely comfort after a day of exploration.

Adding to the appeal, guests are treated to the convenience of complimentary private parking and the luxury of a balcony, perfect for enjoying the serene mornings or tranquil evenings in Tirana. For those looking to venture further, the Rock of Kavaje and the Bektashi World Centre are within accessible distances, offering unique experiences and insights into the local culture.

Situated 20 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Rooms Miri represents not just a place to stay, but a gateway to the myriad of experiences that Tirana has to offer. Whether it's the allure of the city's rich history, the excitement of modern attractions, or simply the comfort of a well-furnished apartment, Rooms Miri caters to the needs of every traveler.
