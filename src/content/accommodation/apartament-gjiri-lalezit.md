The inclusion of a flat-screen TV adds a touch of home, ensuring entertainment options are readily available. One of the apartment's highlights is its garden views, offering a tranquil backdrop for a morning coffee or evening relaxation.

For those keen to explore the rich history and culture of Durrës, the Durres Amphitheatre is located just 2.7 km away, with the House of Leaves museum also within reach for a deep dive into the country's past. For ease of travel, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 31 km from the property, making Apartament Gjiri Lalezit an ideal base for both short getaways and extended stays in this captivating region.
