The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, featuring a bedroom complemented by two well-appointed bathrooms, ensuring ample space and convenience for guests. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels speaks to the property's commitment to guest comfort. Moreover, the dining area and fully equipped kitchen invite guests to feel at home, whether they're enjoying a meal with a view or a quiet night in.

One of the standout features of Villa Giulia Apartment 2 is its terrace, offering unobstructed views of the sea, perfect for those moments of reflection or simply soaking in the beauty of Sarandë's coastline. The apartment's design, with a private entrance and soundproofing, guarantees a secluded and peaceful experience, allowing guests to unwind in their own slice of paradise.

For those with a penchant for the outdoors, the apartment's location offers easy access to fishing and hiking opportunities, ensuring that adventure is never far away. Additionally, the well-maintained garden provides a tranquil setting for relaxation or leisurely strolls.

Located 102 km from Ioannina Airport, Villa Giulia Apartment 2 is conveniently positioned for both local exploration and ease of travel, making it an ideal spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and tranquility of Sarandë.
