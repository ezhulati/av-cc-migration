Each apartment within Golden Villa-Ksamil is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning to beat the summer heat, alongside complimentary private parking and WiFi, ensuring a seamless stay. The living spaces are thoughtfully designed, featuring a terrace that presents breathtaking mountain views, a cozy seating area for relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a fridge for culinary adventures. The private bathrooms add a touch of luxury with their elegant baths, while the balcony extends an invitation to dine al fresco amidst garden views. For added convenience, each unit is furnished with a wardrobe and a coffee machine, catering to all the comforts of home.

The property doesn't shy away from outdoor pleasures, offering barbecue facilities perfect for enjoying the warm weather. Families will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a baby safety gate, ensuring peace of mind for those traveling with little ones. Adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts will find delight in the nearby cycling opportunities, or simply unwinding in the lush garden.

Located less than 1 km from the vibrant Bora Bora Beach and a mere 11-minute walk from the scenic Ksamil Beach 9, Golden Villa-Ksamil promises an idyllic base for exploring the natural beauty of the area. For those arriving from afar, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making it a convenient choice for international travelers seeking a tranquil escape in Ksamil.
