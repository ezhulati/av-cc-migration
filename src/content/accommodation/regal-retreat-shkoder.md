Designed with space and elegance in mind, the apartment boasts a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, ensuring guests are never far from their favorite shows or news. The kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with modern appliances including a dishwasher, an oven, a washing machine, a fridge, and all necessary kitchenware, making it perfect for those who prefer a home-cooked meal even while traveling.

Attention to detail is evident in the inclusion of a bidet and a dressing room, enhancing the comfort and convenience of the stay. For those concerned about health and cleanliness, it's noteworthy that the accommodation maintains a strict non-smoking policy.

Located strategically, Regal Retreat Shkoder is just 58 km away from Podgorica Airport, making it an accessible option for international travelers. This apartment stands out as an exemplary choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury during their visit to Shkodër.
