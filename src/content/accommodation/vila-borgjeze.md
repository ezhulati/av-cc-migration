Each room at Vila Borgjeze is thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a balcony that allows for moments of quiet reflection or a breath of fresh air. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a refreshing start or end to the day. For those who value connectivity and entertainment, the hotel offers free WiFi and flat-screen TVs in every room, with select accommodations also providing captivating city views. The added convenience of air conditioning in each room guarantees a comfortable and relaxing environment regardless of the season.

Situated conveniently close to the Monastery Saint Naum, and with Kastoria National Airport just 71 km away, Vila Borgjeze serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural and natural heritage of the region. Whether visiting for leisure or seeking a peaceful escape, guests will find Vila Borgjeze to be a perfect choice for an unforgettable stay.
