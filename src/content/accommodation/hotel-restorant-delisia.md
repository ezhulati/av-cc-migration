For those with specific dietary preferences, vegetarian, dairy-free, and halal options are readily available, ensuring every guest finds something to satisfy their palate.

Service at Hotel Restorant Delisia is unparalleled, with a multilingual staff ready to assist guests around the clock at the 24-hour front desk. Whether it's providing local insights or facilitating a smooth stay, the team's dedication to guest satisfaction is evident.

Located conveniently, the hotel is a stone's throw away from the historic Monastery Saint Naum, and just 72 km from Kastoria National Airport, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers seeking a blend of convenience and comfort. Hotel Restorant Delisia is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to exploring the rich tapestry of Korçë and beyond.
