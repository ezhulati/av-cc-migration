Beyond the doors of Tree Hotel, visitors find themselves in air-conditioned sanctuaries equipped with free WiFi, ensuring that both leisure and business travelers stay connected. Each room is thoughtfully appointed with a seating area, a flat-screen TV boasting satellite channels, and a safety deposit box for personal valuables. A desk in every unit adds a touch of convenience for those who need to catch up on work.

The hotel doesn't just stop at comfortable accommodations; it extends its hospitality with room service and a 24-hour front desk, ensuring guests' needs are attended to at any hour. The on-site bar offers a cozy spot for evening relaxation or a casual meeting.

For those looking to explore the spiritual side of the region, the Monastery Saint Naum is also 43 km away, making it an easy day trip. And when it's time to say goodbye, Kastoria National Airport is just 72 km away, providing smooth transitions to and from this serene retreat.

In essence, Tree Hotel stands out as a premier destination for those who appreciate the finer details that make a hotel stay memorable.
