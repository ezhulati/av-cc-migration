Accommodations at Modern Beachfront Apartments are designed with guest comfort in mind. Each apartment boasts a balcony that invites guests to enjoy the serene surroundings, a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven for those who prefer to prepare their meals, and a cozy seating area for relaxation. Entertainment and convenience are ensured with a flat-screen TV and a washing machine in every unit, alongside a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and a hairdryer. To further enhance the stay, free WiFi is accessible throughout the property, and select rooms feature a terrace, adding an extra layer of luxury to the experience.

Guests will find themselves enveloped in the tranquility of the area, with Saranda Bay Beach just 1.7 km away, offering an opportunity for memorable beach outings. For those intrigued by history and nature, Butrint National Park is within a 18 km reach, presenting a perfect day trip option. Moreover, the convenience of Ioannina Airport being 98 km away facilitates easy access for international and domestic travelers.

Modern Beachfront Apartments not only promises a stay filled with serene beachfront living but also ensures a comfortable and convenient experience, making it an exceptional choice for those visiting Sarandë.
