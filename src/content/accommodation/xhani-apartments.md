Guests at Xhani Apartments are treated to modern amenities including complimentary WiFi, ensuring connectivity throughout their stay. The apartment features a cozy bedroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine for convenience. The kitchen is thoughtfully equipped with an oven and fridge, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals.

For those keen on exploring the local attractions, the Rock of Kavaje is just 7.9 km away, offering a unique glimpse into the area's natural beauty. Additionally, the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is located 37 km from the property, providing a fascinating excursion for history enthusiasts. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 31 km away, Xhani Apartments presents an accessible haven for both international and domestic travelers seeking the perfect blend of comfort and adventure in Durrës.
