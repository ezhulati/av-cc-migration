The accommodations at Bohemian Boutique Suites are designed with the guest's peace and privacy in mind. Each unit boasts a private entrance and soundproofing, allowing for an undisturbed stay. The rooms are well-appointed with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a private bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower and plush bathrobes. Select rooms further enhance the stay with the addition of a terrace. Practical conveniences such as a desk and a kettle are also provided in every unit.

For those planning to explore the surrounding area, the suites offer the convenience of packed lunches, perfect for day trips to nearby attractions. Additionally, guests have access to a car rental service directly from Bohemian Boutique Suites, making it easy to venture out and discover more of what Vlorë has to offer.

Located 148 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the property also arranges airport transfers, adding to the seamless travel experience. Bohemian Boutique Suites emerges as a top selection for travelers who value comfort, convenience, and a touch of tranquility during their visit to Vlorë.
