The Skerdi Room, a non-smoking haven, offers an air-conditioned sanctuary featuring a bedroom that marries comfort with style, a flat-screen TV for entertainment needs, and a fully equipped kitchenette for those moments when guests prefer a home-cooked meal over dining out.

For guests interested in delving into Albania's rich history and culture, the apartment is conveniently located near the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves, ensuring that the best of the city's attractions are easily accessible. Furthermore, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

Skerdi Room stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Tirana, offering both the serenity of a private apartment and the excitement of the city right at their doorstep.
