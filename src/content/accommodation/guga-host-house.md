Accommodations at GUGA Host House are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each unit is equipped with essential amenities such as a wardrobe, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a stay that combines both comfort and convenience. The private bathrooms come well-appointed with a bidet and complimentary toiletries, adding an extra layer of luxury to the guest experience. Moreover, select rooms elevate the stay further with the addition of a patio, offering a private outdoor space to unwind.

Connectivity is seamless throughout the property, with free WiFi available in all units, allowing guests to stay connected with the world or plan their next adventure in Sarandë. The proximity to notable attractions such as Saranda Bay Beach, just 2.6 km away, and the historic Butrint National Park, 18 km from the property, positions GUGA Host House as an ideal base for explorers keen on discovering the natural and cultural riches of the region.

For travelers arriving from afar, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is located 98 km away, making GUGA Host House an accessible choice for both domestic and international visitors seeking a memorable stay in Sarandë.
