Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking, ensuring a seamless start to their vacation. The apartment prides itself on its 24-hour front desk service and the added ease provided by an elevator, catering to all needs around the clock.

Designed with sophistication in mind, the apartment boasts two elegantly appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, complete with bed linen, towels, and a bidet for an added touch of luxury. The living space is enhanced by soundproofing, ensuring a tranquil retreat from the bustling city life. For entertainment, a flat-screen TV with streaming services is available, perfect for relaxing evenings in. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen offer the freedom to enjoy home-cooked meals, while the balcony presents mesmerizing sea views, inviting guests to unwind in the beauty of their surroundings. Notably, this apartment is an allergy-free and non-smoking environment, catering to the comfort and well-being of all guests.

Situated 4.1 km from the historical Kuzum Baba and 151 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Eka Luxury Two-Bedroom Seaview Apartment is not just a place to stay but a destination to experience the best of Vlorë in luxury and comfort.
