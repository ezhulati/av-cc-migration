Following a comprehensive renovation, Villa Agos presents itself as a ground-floor haven, equipped with modern amenities and designed for relaxation. The home accommodates guests in two tastefully decorated bedrooms and ensures entertainment with a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen, comes complete with an oven, fridge, washing machine, stovetop, and all necessary kitchenware, making it perfect for those who enjoy preparing their meals. Adding to the charm, the holiday home offers a terrace that serves as an enchanting outdoor dining area, allowing guests to dine under the stars.

For those concerned with privacy and security, Villa Agos addresses these needs with a private entrance and round-the-clock security, ensuring a worry-free stay. Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy complimentary WiFi access throughout the property. After a day of exploring, the hot tub awaits to provide a soothing end to the day's adventures.

Located a mere 58 km from Podgorica Airport, Villa Agos stands out as an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and a touch of luxury in their accommodation.
