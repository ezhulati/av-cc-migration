The apartments at Feels Like Home Studio Apartments - FLH Albania are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each unit boasts air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable retreat from the city's hustle and bustle. Guests can enjoy a variety of in-room entertainment options with flat-screen TVs equipped with streaming services. The convenience of a microwave, coffee machine, and kettle in each apartment allows for easy meal preparation, while select units also feature a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a toaster for those who prefer to cook their meals.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of slippers and a desk in every apartment, catering to both leisure and business travelers. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with a shower and hair dryer, ensuring a refreshing start to each day. For added comfort, all units are supplied with fresh bed linen and towels.

The location of Feels Like Home Studio Apartments - FLH Albania places guests in the vicinity of several of Tirana's must-visit attractions, including the National Museum of History Albania, the House of Leaves, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania. For those arriving by air, the convenience of being just 12 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport adds to the appeal of this accommodation.

In summary, Feels Like Home Studio Apartments - FLH Albania offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and thoughtful amenities, making it a standout option for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant culture and history of Tirana.
