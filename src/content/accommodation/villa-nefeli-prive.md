Spanning a generous layout, Villa Nefeli Prive features five elegantly appointed bedrooms and four modern bathrooms, complete with all necessary linens, towels, and thoughtful amenities like slippers for a touch of home-like comfort. The living space is designed for entertainment and relaxation, boasting a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, a cozy dining area, and a fully stocked kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their meals. The air-conditioned interiors extend to a serene terrace, offering breathtaking sea views, and the villa benefits from added features such as soundproofing and a private entrance to ensure utmost privacy.

Morning risers are greeted with a choice of Italian or American breakfast options, including warm dishes, pancakes, and fresh fruits, setting a delightful tone for the day. For outdoor cooking enthusiasts, the villa's barbecue facilities provide a perfect excuse to dine in the comfort of this exquisite setting.

Families visiting Villa Nefeli Prive will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of a baby safety gate, ensuring the little ones' safety and parents' peace of mind.

Situated within proximity to popular attractions like Ksamil Beach 9, Paradise Beach, and Lori Beach, guests have the luxury of exploring the best of Ksamil's coastal beauty. For those venturing further, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making Villa Nefeli Prive an accessible haven for international and domestic travelers alike.
