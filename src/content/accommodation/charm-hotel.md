The hotel's thoughtful provision of a shared kitchen and room service caters to the culinary needs of its guests, while the availability of a currency exchange service adds an extra layer of convenience to the international traveler's experience. The rooms, designed with the guest's comfort in mind, feature essential amenities such as a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. Select accommodations also boast a balcony, offering a private space to unwind and soak in the surroundings.

Morning at Charm Hotel begins on a high note with a choice between an à la carte or continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The multilingual staff, proficient in Greek, English, Spanish, and Italian, stands ready at the reception to assist guests, ensuring a stay that is as seamless as it is enjoyable.

Located a brief drive from the serene Vjetër Beach and the historic Kuzum Baba, the hotel serves as an ideal base for guests eager to explore the local sights. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, with the hotel offering a convenient paid airport shuttle service. Charm Hotel, with its strategic location and comprehensive amenities, positions itself as a top-tier choice for travelers to Vlorë.
