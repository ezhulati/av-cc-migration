Guests at Villa Nicolaos Ksamil are treated to accommodations that boast not only free WiFi and ample private parking but also a delightful children's playground and a lush garden, making it an ideal choice for families and solo travelers alike. The property's strategic location affords stunning city views and places visitors a mere 600 meters from the allure of Ksamil Beach 7.

Each unit within this villa complex is designed with the discerning traveler in mind, featuring private entrances that lead to spaces where elegance meets functionality. The accommodations are equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, a satellite flat-screen TV, and a fully outfitted kitchen, ensuring a stay that is as comfortable as it is luxurious. The presence of a desk and a private bathroom in every unit adds a touch of convenience for both leisure and business travelers.

The option to savor a meal outdoors is made possible by the villa's barbecue facilities, offering a perfect setting for an intimate dinner under the stars or a joyful family gathering.

For those keen on exploring the natural wonders of the area, Butrint National Park is just 2.8 km away, presenting an opportunity to immerse oneself in the rich history and biodiversity of the region. Moreover, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 93 km from the villa, ensures that Villa Nicolaos Ksamil is easily accessible for international travelers seeking a tranquil retreat amidst the beauty of Ksamil.

In summary, Villa Nicolaos Ksamil stands out as a premier destination for those in pursuit of a peaceful getaway, where the beauty of nature, the comfort of luxury accommodations, and the warmth of genuine hospitality converge to create an unforgettable experience.
