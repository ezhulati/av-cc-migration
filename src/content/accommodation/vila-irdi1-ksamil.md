Guest accommodations at Vila Irdi1 Ksamil are a testament to thoughtful design and comfort. Each room is adorned with elegant parquet floors and comes equipped with a kitchenette, including a fridge and coffee machine, ensuring that guests feel right at home. The presence of a dining area, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and complimentary slippers adds layers of convenience and luxury. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony that accompanies every unit, providing an outdoor dining area where guests can enjoy city views and breathe in the coastal ambiance.

For those who prefer to stay connected, the bed and breakfast does not disappoint, offering complimentary WiFi and free private parking, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers. The on-site bar presents an inviting atmosphere for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

For adventurers looking to explore the wider area, Vila Irdi1 Ksamil facilitates this with its car rental service, allowing guests to easily venture out to nearby attractions. Coco Beach is just a kilometer away, offering a perfect spot for sun-seekers, while the historic Butrint National Park, located 3.4 km from the property, promises a fascinating excursion for history buffs. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Vila Irdi1 Ksamil an accessible retreat for those coming from afar.

In essence, Vila Irdi1 Ksamil stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of adventure in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
