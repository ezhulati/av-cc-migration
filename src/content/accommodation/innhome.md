Strategically positioned, Innhome is a mere stone's throw away from cultural landmarks such as the House of Leaves and Rinia Park, and only a brief journey from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha. The serene Rock of Kavaje is also accessible, making it an ideal base for explorers keen on soaking up the local heritage and natural beauty.

Guests are treated to a spacious living area, complete with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The apartment boasts a fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and a fridge, alongside a convenient washing machine, making it perfect for both short and long stays. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of elegance and convenience.

For those in need of additional comforts, Innhome offers the option of towels and linens for a nominal fee, ensuring a hassle-free stay. Located just 12 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment ensures ease of travel for both arriving and departing guests. With its proximity to notable attractions such as the Clock Tower Tirana and Et'hem Bey Mosque, Innhome serves as an ideal spot for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant culture and history of Tirana.
