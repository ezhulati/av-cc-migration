The accommodations at Studio Vlora are thoughtfully designed with comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned family rooms that cater to both individual and group travelers. Each apartment is well-equipped with modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a private bathroom stocked with a shower and hair dryer for convenience. The kitchen, complete with a fridge, stovetop, and kitchenware, invites guests to feel at home by preparing their meals just the way they like.

Strategically located, Studio Vlora is a short distance from other local attractions. Ri Beach is accessible within a 1.6 km journey, offering another spot for sun-seekers and water enthusiasts to explore. For those looking to delve into the area's natural beauty, Vjetër Beach is just 1.8 km away, while the historical site of Kuzum Baba can be reached with a 2.7 km drive, promising a blend of cultural exploration and relaxation.

For travelers flying in, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being 151 km away ensures a relatively straightforward journey to this coastal haven. Studio Vlora emerges as a compelling choice for those in pursuit of a peaceful beachfront getaway, combined with the ease of access to Vlorë's charming sights.
