Each apartment is thoughtfully equipped with cable TV and a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dining area, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals. The inclusion of modern bathrooms equipped with showers and complimentary toiletries adds a touch of luxury to the stay. For those warmer days, selected units boast air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable environment regardless of the weather.

The property is enveloped by a lush garden, providing a tranquil oasis for guests to unwind. For those keen on exploring, the historic Butrint National Park is a mere 3 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. Additionally, the bustling town of Sarandë is easily accessible, located just 13 km from the apartments. Convenience is further enhanced by the provision of free private parking on site, making it an ideal base for those traveling by car.

Ksamil Apartments not only offers a strategic location and essential amenities but also serves as a gateway to the natural and historical treasures of the region, making it a compelling choice for discerning travelers.
