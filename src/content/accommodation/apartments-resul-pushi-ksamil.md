Guests are treated to accommodations that blend comfort with convenience, each unit presenting a private balcony that frames the majestic mountain scenery. The interiors are thoughtfully designed, featuring air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs, dining areas, and well-appointed kitchens. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, where guests can enjoy the luxury of bidets, slippers, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a stay that is as comfortable as it is memorable.

The allure of Apartments Resul Pushi Ksamil is not just in its cozy accommodations but also in its array of amenities. Visitors benefit from the convenience of free private parking and the connectivity of complimentary WiFi, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers. The added perk of all units having their own private entrance adds a layer of privacy and exclusivity to the guest experience.

For those looking to explore the natural beauty of the area, Butrint National Park is a mere 2.9 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich history and biodiversity of the region. Moreover, the property's proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 93 km away, makes it a convenient gateway for international travelers seeking the enchanting beauty of Ksamil.

In summary, Apartments Resul Pushi Ksamil stands out as a delightful haven for those seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and natural beauty, making it a top choice for visitors to this enchanting part of the world.
