The villa itself is a testament to modern luxury, featuring three elegantly appointed bedrooms, a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a gourmet meal, and four well-designed bathrooms, enhancing the comfort of every stay. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have access to leisure at their fingertips. Importantly, the villa maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those seeking moments of tranquility, the villa's garden offers a perfect escape into nature without leaving the comfort of the property.

Situated a mere 89 km from Kastoria National Airport, Luxury Villa Saint Sofia presents an ideal choice for travelers looking for a blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience in the picturesque setting of Voskopojë.
