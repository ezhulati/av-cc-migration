The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting air-conditioned spaces throughout, including a separate bedroom, a bathroom equipped with all essentials, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a dining area perfect for intimate meals. The living room, anchored by a flat-screen TV, provides a cozy retreat for evenings in.

Located a mere 3 km from the picturesque Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, the apartment is also within reasonable distance from some of Albania's most notable attractions. Skanderbeg Square is 39 km away, offering a deep dive into the country's rich history and vibrant culture, while the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 43 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for adventure seekers to explore Albania's stunning natural landscapes from above. For those traveling by air, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 34 km away, makes Lovely Apartment in the Beach, Durres, an accessible haven for both domestic and international travelers.

This property stands out not only for its prime location and comprehensive amenities but also for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a healthy and enjoyable stay for all guests.
