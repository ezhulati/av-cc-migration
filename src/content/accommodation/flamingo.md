The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting a terrace that offers breathtaking sea views. It is well-appointed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, and a bathroom featuring a walk-in shower and bidet. The balcony, adorned with outdoor furniture, presents a picturesque view of the mountains, offering a perfect backdrop for a serene retreat. Additionally, a dressing room is available, providing extra convenience for guests.

For those looking to unwind, the property hosts a bar and lounge area, ideal for relaxing after a day of exploration. Flamingo's location is also a gateway to the area's attractions, with Vjetër Beach just 2.3 km away and the historic site of Kuzum Baba within close proximity at 3.4 km. For international travelers, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest, located 152 km from the accommodation, making Flamingo an accessible choice for both local and international guests seeking a tranquil beachfront escape in Vlorë.
