The apartment itself is a spacious haven, boasting two separate bedrooms, a bathroom, and a living area that comes complete with a flat-screen TV, ensuring entertainment and relaxation for all guests. The heart of the home, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area and minibar, invites families and friends to create and share meals together, making it an ideal setting for those who cherish communal dining experiences.

For those eager to explore, Saranda City Beach is within easy reach, located just 800 meters away, offering another spot for sun-seekers and water enthusiasts to enjoy. Further afield, the historical allure of Butrint National Park, situated 15 km from the apartment, provides a perfect day trip for history buffs and nature lovers alike. Additionally, the convenience of Ioannina Airport, 96 km away, ensures smooth travel arrangements for international and domestic visitors.

Saranda Sea View Family Apartment not only promises a stay filled with serene beachfront moments but also positions itself as a gateway to the wider attractions of Sarandë, making it an excellent choice for those looking to experience the best of this coastal gem.
