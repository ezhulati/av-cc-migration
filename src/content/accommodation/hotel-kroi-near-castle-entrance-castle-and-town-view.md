Guests at Hotel Kroi are treated to a variety of breakfast options each morning, with selections ranging from a hearty buffet to more refined continental and Italian choices, ensuring a delightful start to the day.

The hotel's proximity to significant landmarks, such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 32 km away, and the Rock of Kavaje, 49 km distant, makes it an ideal base for exploring the rich history and natural beauty of the area. Moreover, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 18 km away, Hotel Kroi offers ease of travel for both international and domestic visitors.

Beyond its prime location, Hotel Kroi boasts a range of amenities designed to enhance the stay of every guest. Complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel ensures that guests remain connected, while the on-site restaurant and bar provide delightful dining and relaxation options. Additionally, the availability of family rooms underscores the hotel's commitment to accommodating groups of all sizes.

In summary, Hotel Kroi emerges as a top recommendation for travelers to Krujë, offering a harmonious blend of historical intrigue, modern comforts, and unparalleled convenience.
