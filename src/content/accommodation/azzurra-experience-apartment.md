The apartment itself is a marvel of comfort and convenience, featuring air-conditioned spaces that are meticulously soundproofed, guaranteeing a peaceful stay just 300 meters from the charming La Petite Beach. The interior is thoughtfully designed with a spacious layout that includes one bedroom, one bathroom, and a living area that extends to a patio. Every detail, from bed linen to towels, is carefully selected to enhance the guest experience. The modern amenities don’t end there; a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a terrace offering breathtaking sea views are all part of the package. The balcony, adorned with outdoor furniture, presents a splendid opportunity to admire the mountain scenery in a comfortable setting. Notably, this apartment is an allergy-free and non-smoking environment, catering to the well-being of all guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the apartment, Saranda City Beach is a short 700-meter stroll away, while the historic Butrint National Park lies 15 km from the doorstep, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. For ease of travel, the nearest airport is in Ioannina, 96 km away, with the apartment providing an option for a paid airport shuttle service.

Azzurra Experience Apartment emerges as a top-tier accommodation choice, blending the allure of beachfront living with the comforts of a fully equipped, modern apartment. It's an ideal sanctuary for those seeking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Sarandë while enjoying the comforts of home.
