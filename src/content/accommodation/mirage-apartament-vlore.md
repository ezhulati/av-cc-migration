This apartment doesn't just boast a strategic location; it also provides guests with the luxury of space and privacy. Featuring an air-conditioned layout that includes a separate bedroom, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen, and a modern bathroom, it ensures a stay that combines home comfort with the excitement of travel. The addition of a balcony adds a touch of elegance, offering a private outdoor space to enjoy the balmy Vlorë evenings.

For those arriving by car, the convenience of free private parking is a significant plus, removing the usual hassles associated with finding secure parking spots. Complimentary WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests remain connected, whether it's for leisure browsing or catching up on work.

Situated 6.6 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and 6.9 km from Independence Square, guests are within easy reach of exploring Vlorë's rich cultural heritage. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 156 km away, making Mirage Apartament Vlore a feasible option for both short and extended stays.

In summary, Mirage Apartament Vlore stands out for its excellent location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay in Vlorë, whether one is looking to relax by the beach or explore the local culture.
