Upon arrival, guests are welcomed into air-conditioned rooms that boast not only comfort but functionality. Each room is equipped with a desk, a safety deposit box for valuables, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a private balcony offers a personal oasis where guests can unwind in solitude. Ensuring a seamless stay, all rooms feature a private bathroom equipped with a shower, complemented by fresh bed linen and towels.

The Merar Hotel prides itself on its attentive services, including a 24-hour front desk ready to assist at any hour, airport transfers for hassle-free travel arrangements, room service for in-room dining experiences, and the convenience of free WiFi throughout the property. For those carrying extra luggage, the hotel offers a luggage storage space, adding to the ease of the stay.

Situated just 1.9 km from the serene Mali I Robit Beach and a scenic drive from Skanderbeg Square, the hotel's location is ideal for explorers eager to discover the area's attractions. Moreover, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located only 44 km away, the Merar Hotel serves as a perfect gateway for international travelers looking to experience the beauty of Golem.
