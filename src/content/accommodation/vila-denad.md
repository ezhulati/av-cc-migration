The apartment within VILA Denad is generously proportioned, boasting a terrace that invites relaxation, a cozy bedroom for restful nights, a welcoming living room for leisurely downtime, and a well-appointed kitchen for those who enjoy the art of cooking. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal retreat for both short and extended stays.

For those with a penchant for exploration, the allure of Butrint National Park is within a manageable distance, located 18 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity to immerse oneself in the natural and historical richness of the area. Additionally, the convenience of Ioannina Airport being 95 km away facilitates ease of travel to and from the property.

VILA Denad stands out as a preferred choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of home in the picturesque setting of Sarandë.
