The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort. It features a spacious bedroom, ensuring a restful night's sleep. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and toaster, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, adding a touch of personalization to their stay. Convenience is further amplified by the presence of a washing machine, making longer stays as comfortable as possible. The luxury of a hot tub in the bathroom elevates the experience, offering a private oasis of relaxation. To ensure a seamless stay, towels and bed linen are provided, and the apartment prides itself on being non-smoking and soundproof, guaranteeing a peaceful environment.

For those looking to explore beyond the immediate vicinity, Kallmi Beach awaits at a mere 2.8 km distance, while the historic Skanderbeg Square is 38 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of the area. The apartment's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 33 km away, ensures ease of travel for international guests.

Wonderful City Center 1 stands out as an ideal accommodation choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury during their visit to Durrës.
