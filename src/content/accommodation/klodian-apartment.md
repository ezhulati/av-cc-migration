This apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest; it provides a spacious haven equipped with modern amenities to ensure a memorable stay. The inclusion of a balcony allows guests to enjoy the fresh air and scenic surroundings from the comfort of their accommodation. With three bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a well-appointed kitchen, groups and families can find the space and facilities they need to feel at home. Entertainment is readily available with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have access to leisure even when indoors.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is accessible throughout the property, allowing guests to stay connected with the world or plan their next adventure in Sarandë. For those looking to explore the natural beauty of the area, Butrint National Park is a mere 15 km away, offering a perfect day trip opportunity to immerse in the rich history and biodiversity of the region.

Located 96 km from Ioannina Airport, Klodian Apartment also caters to the needs of travelers looking for seamless connections to and from their destination. This apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the enchanting experiences that Sarandë has to offer.
