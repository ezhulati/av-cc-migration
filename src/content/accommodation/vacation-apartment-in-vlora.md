This inviting apartment, situated just 2.7 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and 3 km from the bustling Independence Square, offers a tranquil retreat with the benefit of free WiFi accessible throughout the premises. Guests will find themselves in a haven of relaxation, with the apartment boasting a tastefully furnished bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those home-cooked meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are also met with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring that guests have all the comforts of home at their fingertips.

For those concerned about the environment and personal health, it's noteworthy that the accommodation maintains a strict non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh atmosphere for the duration of your stay.

Travelers planning to explore beyond the local area will appreciate the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 152 km away, making the Vacation Apartment in Vlora an excellent base for both local and regional adventures.

Ideal for those who value privacy and independence, this apartment offers a seamless blend of convenience, comfort, and accessibility, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of Vlorë.
