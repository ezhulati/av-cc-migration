Accommodations at Santa Quaranta are a testament to modern comfort and elegance. Each room, air-conditioned for guest comfort, features a flat-screen satellite TV and a minibar, ensuring a stay that blends relaxation with luxury. The inclusion of a balcony in all units allows guests to bask in the breathtaking surroundings, with select rooms offering a seating area for an added touch of convenience. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, equipped with showers, slippers, complimentary toiletries, and hairdryers, catering to every need for a pampered experience.

The resort's sun terrace, adorned with sun loungers and parasols, invites guests to unwind under the Mediterranean sun, while the availability of water sports facilities caters to the adventurous spirit. Culinary delights await at the on-site bar and restaurant, promising an array of flavors to tantalize the palate.

Located a mere 3.6 km from the Saranda Ferry Terminal, with easy connections to Corfu Island, and 26 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park, Santa Quaranta Premium Resort serves as the perfect gateway to explore the rich cultural and natural heritage of the region.
