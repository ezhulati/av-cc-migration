The hotel boasts a selection of air-conditioned units, each designed with comfort in mind. Guests are treated to a cozy seating area and a private bathroom equipped with plush bathrobes, slippers, and a hairdryer, ensuring a stay that blends relaxation with the comforts of home. For those looking to unwind, the on-site spa and gym provide the perfect escape, offering a sanctuary for rejuvenation and fitness.

Adding to the convenience, Andon Lapa Hotel & Spa features a 24-hour front desk, ensuring guests' needs are promptly met at any hour. For explorers eager to venture beyond the hotel's serene confines, car hire services are readily available, offering an easy gateway to the surrounding beauty and attractions.

The hotel's prime location places it just 1.3 km from the vibrant center of Sarandë, with the ferry port offering links to Corfu Island merely 900 meters away. This proximity to local highlights and seamless connectivity to nearby islands makes it an ideal choice for both leisure and adventure.

Guests can also delight in the exclusive private beach area and the inviting on-site bar, perfect for those moments of leisure and relaxation under the sun or a refreshing cocktail as the day winds down.

Andon Lapa Hotel & Spa stands out as a distinguished haven in Sarandë, offering guests a harmonious blend of luxury, comfort, and accessibility, making it a top choice for discerning travelers.
