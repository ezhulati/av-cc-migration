This air-conditioned apartment boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a modern bathroom stocked with fresh bed linen and towels, and a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain views, setting the stage for a memorable stay. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a dining area, complemented by a fully equipped kitchen that allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals. For a sweet touch, chocolates or cookies await guests, adding a personal touch to the welcoming atmosphere.

Convenience is at the forefront, with private parking on-site and complimentary WiFi ensuring a hassle-free experience. The non-smoking property is also a mere 7.3 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, inviting guests to explore the natural beauty surrounding Tirana.

For those looking to explore local cuisine, a variety of dining options are within easy reach, and a minimarket on the premises ensures that essentials are always at hand. Notable attractions such as the House of Leaves, Rinia Park, and the National Museum of History Albania are nearby, making Gled Studioflat an ideal base for cultural exploration. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 13 km away, this apartment stands out as a convenient and comfortable choice for both short and long-term stays in Albania's bustling capital.
