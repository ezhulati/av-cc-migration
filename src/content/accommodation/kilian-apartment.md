Guests can enjoy the convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout their stay in this two-bedroom apartment, which is equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment and a fully furnished kitchen, complete with an oven and microwave, for those who love to cook. The inclusion of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free experience.

Adding to the appeal is the apartment's thoughtful design, featuring a private entrance that guarantees an exclusive and undisturbed stay. Located 60 km from Podgorica Airport, Kilian Apartment offers an ideal base for travelers seeking both tranquility and accessibility in Shkodër.
