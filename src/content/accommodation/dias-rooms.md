This apartment doesn't just boast a desirable location; it comes fully equipped to meet all the needs of modern travelers. Featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights, a kitchen outfitted with an oven and fridge for those who love to cook, and the added convenience of a washing machine, it ensures a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of a getaway. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV, complimentary WiFi, and free private parking further enhances the guest experience, making it a hassle-free retreat.

For those who take their comfort seriously, the apartment provides air conditioning, non-smoking rooms, and even slippers and a hairdryer in the bathroom, ensuring a stay that is as comfortable as it is enjoyable. An ironing service is available, adding a touch of convenience for guests looking to look their best.

Not just a place to stay, D, I,A's Rooms is a gateway to the vibrant life and activities Vlorë has to offer. From cycling along the scenic coast to exploring the local markets conveniently located at the apartment, there's never a dull moment. The property's proximity to notable landmarks like Independence Square and Kuzum Baba invites guests to immerse themselves in the rich culture and history of the area.

For those venturing from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km away, making D, I,A's Rooms an accessible haven for international and domestic travelers alike. Whether it's the allure of the beach, the comfort of a well-appointed apartment, or the charm of Vlorë itself, this accommodation is a standout choice for those looking to make the most of their visit to this beautiful coastal city.
