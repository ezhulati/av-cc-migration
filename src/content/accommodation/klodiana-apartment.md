Boasting an impressive view of the city and the tranquility of the sea, the apartment is well-appointed with two bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony designed to capture the essence of Sarandë's scenic beauty. Guests can enjoy modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and microwave, and the convenience of a bathroom equipped with a bidet. The inclusion of towels and bed linen ensures a comfortable stay, making it feel like a home away from home.

Located 18 km from the lush landscapes of Butrint National Park, Klodiana Apartment offers an ideal base for explorers eager to immerse themselves in the area's rich history and natural beauty. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is accessible, located 98 km from the accommodation, making it a convenient option for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Klodiana Apartment presents a compelling choice for those looking to experience the best of Sarandë, combining the comforts of modern living with the charm of coastal life.
