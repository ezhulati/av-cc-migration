The property boasts a collection of well-appointed units, each designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. Guests are treated to air-conditioned spaces that feature a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen and dining area for those preferring home-cooked meals. The private bathrooms come with a hair dryer, a bidet, and a shower, ensuring a comfortable stay. Adding to the charm, each unit opens up to a balcony that presents an outdoor dining area against the backdrop of serene garden views, inviting guests to unwind in nature's lap.

Beyond the confines of these inviting units, Seas Villa doesn't shy away from offering amenities that enhance the stay experience. A sun terrace beckons for moments of relaxation under the sky, while the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking addresses the practical needs of every guest. For those who enjoy the art of grilling, the on-site barbecue facilities promise delightful culinary adventures.

Situated a mere 2.9 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and 92 km from Corfu International Airport, Seas Villa stands as a gateway to exploring the natural and historical treasures of the region. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beaches or the call of ancient ruins, this apartment complex serves as a perfect base for adventurers and leisure seekers alike.
