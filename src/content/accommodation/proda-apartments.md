Guests at Proda Apartments are treated to a host of amenities designed to ensure a stay that is both relaxing and convenient. Each apartment is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for those moments when unwinding indoors is preferred. Safety is also a priority, with each unit equipped with a safety deposit box. The private bathrooms come with a shower, complimentary slippers, and a hair dryer, adding a touch of home-like comfort.

For those who enjoy the option of preparing their own meals, select units come with a well-equipped kitchen, complete with a toaster, fridge, and stovetop, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips.

Proda Apartments is not just about the immediate comforts. Its location serves as a gateway to the wider attractions of the area. The enchanting Butrint National Park is a mere 16 km away, offering an easy day trip option for nature and history enthusiasts alike. Moreover, the property is conveniently situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, making it an accessible choice for both domestic and international travelers.

With its combination of strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay, Proda Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for visitors to Sarandë.
