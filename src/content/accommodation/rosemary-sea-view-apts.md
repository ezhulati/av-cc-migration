Each apartment at Rosemary Sea View is a testament to thoughtful design, offering a balcony that frames the picturesque mountain landscape, alongside a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. Entertainment is readily available through the satellite flat-screen TV, while the fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven, fridge, stovetop, and essential kitchenware, invites guests to feel at home. The convenience of a kettle further enhances the living experience, catering to the needs of tea and coffee enthusiasts alike. Private bathrooms are modern and equipped with walk-in showers and hair dryers, ensuring a refreshing start to each day.

For those who prioritize comfort, the apartment complex does not disappoint, providing plush bed linen and towels in every unit. The proximity to VIP Beach, merely 800 meters away, and the historic Butrint National Park, located 14 km from the premises, positions Rosemary Sea View Apartments as an ideal base for explorers keen to discover the natural and cultural treasures of the region.

Accessibility is also a key feature, with Ioannina Airport situated 96 km away, making the apartments a convenient option for international travelers. Whether it's the allure of the Albanian coast or the promise of a serene getaway, Rosemary Sea View Apartments stands ready to welcome guests to an unforgettable stay in Sarandë.
