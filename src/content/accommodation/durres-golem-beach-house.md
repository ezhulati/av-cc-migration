The Golem Beach House boasts a spacious apartment that is a haven of relaxation and convenience. Guests are treated to three elegantly furnished bedrooms, ensuring a restful night's sleep for all. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring a wide array of cable channels, perfect for those moments of leisure. The heart of the apartment, a fully equipped kitchen, invites guests to prepare their meals just the way they like, with amenities including a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop. Adding to the apartment's charm is a cozy fireplace, setting the scene for memorable evenings.

For those who appreciate the beauty of nature, the apartment opens up to a balcony that offers a tranquil garden view, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the peaceful surroundings. The convenience of free private parking adds to the hassle-free experience, ensuring that guests can explore the area with ease.

Situated 45 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 48 km from the adventurous Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, the Golem Beach House serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural and natural attractions of the area. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 39 km away, makes this property a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Durres, Golem Beach House, offers a unique blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it a must-visit destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse themselves in the serene beauty of Golem.
