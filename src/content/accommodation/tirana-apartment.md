This elegantly appointed apartment is distinguished by its spacious layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that presents captivating city views. Guests will appreciate the modern comforts provided, including a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven, and a contemporary bathroom with a walk-in shower. The addition of air conditioning, a bidet, and a dressing room enhances the overall experience, ensuring a stay that is as comfortable as it is luxurious.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of quality bed linen, towels, and an ironing service, underscoring the apartment's commitment to guest satisfaction. Moreover, the convenience of a minimarket on the premises adds an extra layer of ease to the stay.

For those keen to explore, the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks such as the Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, and Saint Paul Cathedral makes it an ideal base for discovering the city's rich cultural tapestry. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, Tirana Apartment stands out as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, location, and thoughtful amenities.
