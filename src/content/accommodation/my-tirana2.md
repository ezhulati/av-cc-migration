The apartments themselves are a haven of comfort, featuring patios or terraces that invite guests to relax and soak in the mountain views. Situated close to Rinia Park, the Pyramid of Tirana, and the iconic Reja - The Cloud, My Tirana2 is not just a place to stay but a gateway to experiencing the cultural heartbeat of the city.
