Guests at Dajana Apartments are treated to stunning mountain views, complemented by the ease of complimentary WiFi and the assurance of free private parking. The establishment is distinguished by its family-friendly rooms and the added delight of a barbecue area, perfect for those leisurely evenings after a day of exploration.

Accommodations are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels for entertainment. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen and dining area in each unit allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, making it an ideal choice for longer stays. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with a hair dryer, a bidet, and a shower, ensuring a comfortable and convenient stay. Select units boast the luxury of a terrace or balcony, offering serene views of the garden or the cityscape, while essentials like a wardrobe and a kettle are standard across all rooms.

For those keen on exploring the local area, Dajana Apartments facilitates bicycle rental services, making it effortless to venture out and enjoy activities such as fishing in the nearby surroundings. The proximity to Coco Beach and the historical Butrint National Park, just a few kilometers away, presents guests with ample opportunities for adventure and cultural enrichment.

Situated 93 km from Corfu International Airport, Dajana Apartments not only promises a memorable stay with its array of features and prime location but also ensures accessibility for international travelers, making it a compelling choice for those visiting Ksamil.
