Following a recent renovation, the apartment boasts a contemporary design with a focus on guest comfort. The air-conditioned unit includes a spacious bedroom, a well-appointed living room with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony that offers captivating city views. The kitchen is equipped with modern appliances, including an oven and a microwave, catering to guests' culinary needs. Additionally, the apartment features a bathroom with a bath and a separate shower, complemented by a dressing room for added convenience. Emphasizing health and wellness, the apartment is designated as allergy-free and strictly non-smoking.

Security is a top priority, with full-day security services and luggage storage options available, ensuring a worry-free stay for guests. For those needing essentials or a quick snack, a minimarket on the premises adds to the convenience.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the apartment's proximity to notable attractions such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, offering a blend of cultural experiences and retail therapy. For travelers arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 14 km away, making Adore Apartment City Center a compelling choice for both short and extended stays in Tirana.
