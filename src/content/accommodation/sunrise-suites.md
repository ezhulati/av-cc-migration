Each apartment at SunRise Suites is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, a fully equipped kitchen for a home-cooked meal away from home, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment evenings. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with a bidet and hair dryer, adds a touch of convenience to the stay.

Families traveling with little ones will appreciate the thoughtful addition of a baby safety gate, ensuring a worry-free stay for parents. The provision of complimentary private parking and WiFi further enhances the guest experience, allowing for ease of travel and connectivity throughout the stay.

Located just a short drive from the vibrant Coco Bongo Beach and the historic Kuzum Baba, SunRise Suites offers an ideal base for exploring the rich offerings of Vlorë. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 155 km away, making it a manageable distance for international visitors.

SunRise Suites presents a compelling option for those in search of a comfortable, well-appointed accommodation in Vlorë, promising a memorable stay with its blend of convenience, comfort, and accessibility.
