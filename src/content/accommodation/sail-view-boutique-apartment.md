The apartment itself is a study in spaciousness and modern amenities. Guests are treated to a well-appointed living space that includes a bedroom, a living room complete with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher and an oven. The convenience of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Notably, the apartment prioritizes guest privacy and tranquility with its private entrance and soundproofing.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and activities, Sail View Boutique Apartment serves as an ideal base. The surrounding area of Sarandë is ripe for exploration, offering opportunities for fishing among other leisure pursuits. Additionally, the proximity to other popular beaches like Flamingo Beach and Mango Beach, both less than a kilometer away, means guests have ample choice for seaside relaxation.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Ioannina Airport located 96 km away. The apartment facilitates travel arrangements by offering a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a hassle-free connection to and from the accommodation.

In summary, Sail View Boutique Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those visiting Sarandë, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views, making it a must-visit for discerning travelers.
