The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen comes complete with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, ensuring that guests have everything they need to feel at home. The highlight, however, is the balcony that features an outdoor dining area, perfect for meals with a view.

For those looking to explore, the property's location serves as an ideal base. It is situated just 14 km from the lush expanses of Butrint National Park, offering a blend of natural beauty and historical intrigue. Additionally, the ancient site of Fanoti is within reach, located 48 km away, promising a fascinating day trip for history enthusiasts.

Dining options abound in the vicinity, catering to a variety of tastes and preferences, ensuring that guests have a multitude of culinary experiences to choose from.

Accessibility is also a key feature, with the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, located 96 km away, making Golden Seaview Apartment a convenient option for international travelers.

In essence, Golden Seaview Apartment is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the best of Sarandë, offering comfort, convenience, and captivating views, making it an excellent choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and culture of the region.
