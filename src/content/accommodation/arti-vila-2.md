Positioned just 300 meters from the idyllic Paradise Beach, the villa promises a tranquil retreat. Guests are welcomed into a space where comfort meets elegance, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen for those home-cooked meals, and a living area adorned with a flat-screen TV for entertainment.

For those arriving from afar, the Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making Arti Vila 2 an accessible haven for both local and international travelers seeking a peaceful escape by the sea.
