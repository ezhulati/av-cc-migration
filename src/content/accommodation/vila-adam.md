The apartments at Vila Adam are a testament to modern comfort, equipped with air conditioning, free WiFi, and essential amenities including a washing machine and a flat-screen TV. Select units elevate the experience further, boasting terraces that offer breathtaking mountain views, fully equipped kitchens for those who love to cook, and private bathrooms featuring bidets, ensuring a stay that combines comfort with a touch of luxury. Guests are also provided with bed linen and towels, adding to the thoughtful touches that make a stay here truly comfortable.

Located less than a kilometer from the enchanting Bora Bora Beach and a short drive from the historical Butrint National Park, Vila Adam offers an ideal base for explorers keen to uncover the natural and historical treasures of the region. For international travelers, the Corfu International Airport is situated 93 km away, making Vila Adam accessible for a global clientele seeking a tranquil escape in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
