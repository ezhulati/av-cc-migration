Accommodations at Southcoast Apartments are designed with families in mind, featuring spacious family rooms that cater to the needs of both adults and children. Select units elevate the guest experience further, equipped with a cable flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen complete with a dishwasher, and the luxury of a private bathroom outfitted with a bidet and hair dryer, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

For added convenience, a minimarket on the premises allows guests to easily procure essentials without having to leave the comfort of the apartment complex. 

Located strategically, the property is not only a stone's throw away from the beach but also a short distance from notable landmarks such as Vjetër Beach, Kuzum Baba, and Independence Square, making it an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë.

For those traveling to or from the city, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making Southcoast Apartments a convenient choice for both domestic and international travelers. This blend of strategic location, thoughtful amenities, and the allure of the Albanian coast makes Southcoast Apartments a standout option for those visiting Vlorë.
