This generously sized apartment is thoughtfully furnished with two bedrooms, ensuring ample space for guests. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The kitchen leaves nothing to be desired, boasting a full suite of appliances including an oven, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, making self-catering a breeze. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort to the stay.

Designed with the discerning traveler in mind, the apartment features a private entrance and soundproofing, ensuring a serene and uninterrupted retreat. For those who enjoy al fresco dining, the option to dine outdoors adds an extra layer of enjoyment to the experience.

Situated 97 km from Ioannina Airport, Marina Apartment offers a perfect blend of accessibility and tranquility, making it an ideal spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Sarandë while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed accommodation.
