This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a living experience. With two generously sized bedrooms, a modern bathroom, and a balcony offering sweeping views of the city, guests are treated to a home away from home. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen and a cozy dining area allows for intimate meals, whether it's breakfast in the sunlight or dinner under the stars.

Entertainment is never far away, with a flat-screen TV and streaming services available for those evenings in. The entire apartment benefits from air conditioning and complimentary WiFi, ensuring comfort and connectivity throughout the stay.

For those looking to explore beyond the apartment, a car rental service is conveniently available on-site. This opens up a world of exploration, with the Pyramid of Tirana, Reja - The Cloud, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana all within close proximity. Moreover, the property's strategic location just 500 meters from the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha adds a historical dimension to the visit.

Practicality is also a hallmark of Miku Apartment, with facilities such as a lift and a minimarket enhancing the ease of the stay. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further ensures a pleasant and healthy atmosphere for all guests.

Situated 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment ensures that arrivals and departures are as smooth as possible, marking the beginning and end of what promises to be an unforgettable stay in Tirana.
