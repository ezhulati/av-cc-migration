Each accommodation at Hotel Omer The Ottoman House boasts a private entrance, ensuring a sense of exclusivity and privacy. The rooms are equipped with modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms complete with showers and slippers, enhancing the overall guest experience. Additionally, the presence of a dressing room and a desk in every unit adds a layer of convenience for visitors.

Guests can start their day on a high note with a diverse breakfast selection that includes continental and Italian options, alongside local specialties, pancakes, and fruits, all served daily. The convenience is further amplified by the availability of a minimarket within the premises, making it easy for guests to grab essentials without having to leave the comfort of the bed and breakfast.

Situated 117 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Hotel Omer The Ottoman House also offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a hassle-free travel experience for guests. With its strategic location and thoughtful amenities, this bed and breakfast is a compelling choice for those looking to explore Berat in comfort and style.
