The property boasts a well-appointed air-conditioned holiday home featuring a separate bedroom, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it a perfect retreat for relaxation after a day of exploration.

For outdoor enthusiasts or those simply looking to unwind, Marina Appartamenti offers a charming garden and barbecue facilities, available for guest use when the weather is inviting. The addition of a terrace provides an ideal spot for morning coffees or evening relaxation, overlooking the serene surroundings.

Accessibility is a highlight, with Vjetër Beach located just 2.4 km away, and the historic Kuzum Baba viewpoint a mere 2.9 km from the doorstep. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making Marina Appartamenti a convenient base for both local and international visitors.

The property also prioritizes guest convenience with a 24-hour front desk and bicycle parking, ensuring a comfortable and worry-free stay. Marina Appartamenti stands out as a top recommendation for those seeking a memorable stay in Vlorë, combining the comforts of home with the accessibility and beauty of the Albanian coast.
