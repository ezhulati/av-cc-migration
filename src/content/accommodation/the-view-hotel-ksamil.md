Guests are treated to air-conditioned accommodations that promise privacy and comfort, each equipped with a private bathroom and complimentary WiFi to keep visitors connected. The addition of balconies in every room offers a personal outdoor space for guests to enjoy the serene surroundings.

Morning starts on a high note at The View Hotel Ksamil, with breakfast options that cater to all preferences, from a hearty buffet to à la carte and continental selections. This ensures that every guest begins their day with a meal that suits their taste.

Located just a kilometer away from the lively Ksamil Beach 9 and a mere 1.1 km from Lori Beach, the hotel offers easy access to some of the area's most beautiful coastlines. For those intrigued by history and nature, Butrint National Park is a mere 3 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. Moreover, for international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 92 km away, making The View Hotel Ksamil a convenient gateway to the wonders of Ksamil and its surroundings.

In essence, The View Hotel Ksamil stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to explore the beauty of Ksamil, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and proximity to natural and historical sites.
