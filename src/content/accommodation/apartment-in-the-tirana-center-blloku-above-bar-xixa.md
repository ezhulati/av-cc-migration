The apartment itself is a haven of space and comfort, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a TV for those relaxing nights in, a fully equipped kitchen for culinary adventures, and two modern bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.

For those keen to explore beyond the immediate surroundings, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offers breathtaking views and is just a short drive away, while the Rock of Kavaje, perfect for a day trip, is accessible within an hour's drive.

Not to be missed are the nearby attractions such as the iconic Pyramid of Tirana, the whimsical Reja - The Cloud, and the serene Saint Paul Cathedral, all adding to the rich tapestry of experiences awaiting guests in this dynamic area.

Conveniently located, the apartment is also just 16 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible for international and domestic travelers alike.

Apartment in the Tirana Center "Blloku" - above Bar Xixa stands out as an exceptional choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the heart of Tirana, offering both the comforts of home and the excitement of the city right on its doorstep.
