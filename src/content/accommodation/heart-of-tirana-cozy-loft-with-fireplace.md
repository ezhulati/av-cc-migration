This inviting loft boasts a spacious, air-conditioned living space, complete with a balcony that allows guests to soak in the city's ambiance. The inclusion of free WiFi ensures that guests remain connected, whether for leisure or work. The apartment's layout is thoughtfully designed, featuring a comfortable bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer to prepare their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, making it a cozy retreat after a day of exploration.

For those intrigued by Albania's history, the apartment's location offers easy access to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Tanners' Bridge, adding a layer of historical intrigue to the visit. Shopping enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the Toptani Shopping Centre, a modern shopping haven within a short walking distance.

Situated 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the Heart of Tirana Cozy Loft with Fireplace ensures a hassle-free journey to and from the city, making it an ideal choice for both short and extended stays. This loft not only promises a comfortable and convenient stay but also serves as a window to Tirana's vibrant culture and history, making it a compelling option for travelers eager to experience the city's dynamic essence.
