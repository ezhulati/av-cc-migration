The hotel prides itself on its round-the-clock front desk, offering services ranging from airport transfers to dry cleaning, ensuring a hassle-free stay for all guests. Connectivity is never an issue, as complimentary WiFi is available throughout the premises.

Each room at Villa Memaj is a haven of comfort, equipped with a desk, air conditioning, a safety deposit box, and a flat-screen TV, catering to the needs of the modern traveler. Guests have the option to start their day with a choice of buffet, continental, or Italian breakfast, promising a delightful culinary experience.

For those seeking relaxation, the hot tub awaits, offering a perfect escape after a day of exploration. The hotel's proximity to Paradise Beach and Lori Beach, located 500 meters and 600 meters away respectively, presents guests with ample opportunities to immerse themselves in the beauty of the Albanian Riviera. Furthermore, the convenience of being 92 km from Corfu International Airport makes Villa Memaj an accessible haven for both international and domestic travelers seeking a tranquil beachside getaway.
