The hotel prides itself on its comprehensive services, including complimentary private parking, a welcoming terrace, a restaurant serving a variety of culinary delights, and a bar perfect for evening relaxation. The commitment to guest satisfaction is further evidenced by the provision of a concierge service, luggage storage, a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service, and free WiFi throughout the premises.

Accommodations at Hotel Theatro- City Center are thoughtfully designed to ensure a comfortable and restful experience. Each room is furnished with a desk, a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and comes with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and hairdryer. For added luxury, select rooms offer balconies, allowing guests to enjoy their own private outdoor space. Attention to detail is apparent with the inclusion of quality bed linen and towels in every room.

Located a convenient 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Hotel Theatro- City Center stands out as a premier choice for both business and leisure travelers. Its blend of excellent location, superior facilities, and dedicated service makes it a compelling option for those looking to experience the best of Tirana.
