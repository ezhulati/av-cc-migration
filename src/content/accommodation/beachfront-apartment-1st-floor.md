The Beachfront Apartment 1st Floor is designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy the comforts of home-cooked meals. The inclusion of a balcony adds a touch of luxury, providing a serene spot to enjoy the sea breeze and stunning views.

Guests will appreciate the convenience of free private parking, making explorations of the surrounding area hassle-free. For those seeking adventure beyond the beach, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car awaits at a distance of 47 km, offering breathtaking views of the region from above. Additionally, the Rock of Kavaje, located just 1.6 km from the apartment, presents a unique natural attraction.

Situated in a non-smoking property, this apartment ensures a clean and healthy environment for all guests. With its strategic location, the Beachfront Apartment 1st Floor is not just a place to stay but a gateway to experiencing the best of Durrës and its surroundings. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is conveniently located 38 km away, facilitating easy arrivals and departures for international travelers.
