The accommodation boasts a thoughtfully designed apartment that caters to the needs of modern travelers. Guests will find themselves in a tranquil setting, with a balcony offering views of a quiet street, perfect for those moments of reflection or a morning cup of coffee. The apartment features a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and microwave, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their own meals. The convenience of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with complimentary towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Notably, the apartment is designed to be allergy-free and maintains a strict non-smoking policy, ensuring a pleasant environment for all guests.

For those who might need to pick up essentials or snacks, a minimarket on the premises provides the utmost convenience.

Further afield, the adventurous can explore Mali I Robit Beach, located just 1.4 km away, or take a day trip to Skanderbeg Square, 45 km from the apartment. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 39 km away, with the property offering a paid shuttle service for a hassle-free transfer.

12 EUR for MONTHLY STAY - Home By the Sea - HBS stands out as a compelling choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the beauty of the Albanian coastline.
