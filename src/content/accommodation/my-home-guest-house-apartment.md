The accommodation doesn't just stop at providing a picturesque view; it goes above and beyond to ensure guests' needs are met with complimentary WiFi and on-site private parking, removing any worries about connectivity and vehicle security. The interior of the apartment is thoughtfully designed with a cozy seating area and a fully equipped kitchen, catering to those who prefer a home-cooked meal even while on vacation. The inclusion of a private bathroom equipped with slippers and complimentary toiletries adds a touch of personalized hospitality that is hard to find.

For those looking to unwind, My Home Guest House-Apartment boasts a spa center, perfect for relaxing after a day of exploration. Additionally, the barbecue facilities offer a unique opportunity for guests to enjoy grilled delicacies in the comfort of their temporary home.

Conveniently located, the property is a short drive from Tirana Airport, approximately 23 km away, making it an accessible haven for both local and international travelers. My Home Guest House-Apartment stands out as a compelling choice for those in search of a comfortable, well-appointed stay in Durrës.
