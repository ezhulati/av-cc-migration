Each one-bedroom apartment at Atlantis Apartments is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air-conditioned spaces, complemented by the convenience of a fully equipped kitchen, including an oven and fridge, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The living room, adorned with a flat-screen TV offering cable channels, provides a cozy retreat for relaxation after a day of exploration.

Privacy and comfort are paramount at Atlantis Apartments, with each unit boasting a private entrance and a bathroom equipped with a bidet. The addition of free WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests remain connected and entertained throughout their stay.

For those traveling to or from the area, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 168 km away, makes Atlantis Apartments an ideal choice for both short and extended stays. This property stands out as a beacon of comfort and convenience for travelers venturing to Orikum.
