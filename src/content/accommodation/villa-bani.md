The property boasts a selection of well-appointed apartments, each featuring essential modern amenities to ensure a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of a hotel. Guests can enjoy the convenience of air-conditioned spaces, a cozy seating area, and entertainment through flat-screen TVs equipped with cable channels. The inclusion of a fully-equipped kitchen and dining area in each unit adds a touch of home, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals.

A highlight of Villa Bani is the thoughtful integration of outdoor spaces. Each apartment offers a balcony or patio, presenting an outdoor dining area set against the backdrop of the property's lush garden, providing a serene spot to unwind and soak in the tranquil views.

For those venturing out, the property's proximity to notable attractions such as Ksamil Beach 7 and Bora Bora Beach, located just over a kilometer away, positions Villa Bani as an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty of the area. Additionally, the convenience of being 93 km from Corfu International Airport adds to the appeal for international travelers.

With the promise of free private parking and complimentary WiFi, along with the provision of bed linen and towels in all units, Villa Bani ensures a hassle-free and comfortable experience for families and groups alike, making it a standout option for those looking to savor the delights of Ksamil in a setting that feels both welcoming and exclusive.
