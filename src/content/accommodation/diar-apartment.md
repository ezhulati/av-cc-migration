Guests are welcomed into a thoughtfully appointed apartment that boasts a balcony offering tranquil street views-a perfect setting for morning coffees or evening relaxation. The space is well-equipped with a modern living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a kitchen outfitted with an oven and fridge, catering to guests' culinary needs. The inclusion of a comfortable bedroom and a bathroom with a shower ensures a restful and refreshing stay. To enhance the experience, guests are greeted with complimentary fruits, adding a personal touch to the warm hospitality.

For those intrigued by local history and culture, the Rock of Kavaje is located just 7.7 km away, while the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is within a 37 km reach, offering insightful excursions nearby. Moreover, the convenience of being 31 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Diar Apartment a compelling choice for both international visitors and those traveling within Albania.
