Each room at Hotel Vera is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning, free WiFi, a desk, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. For added convenience, bed linen, towels, and a fridge are also provided, ensuring that every need is met with the utmost attention to detail.

Mornings at Hotel Vera begin with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The hotel's prime location allows easy access to the nearby Golem Beach, just 500 meters away, and Durres Beach, located 2.4 km from the property, offering guests a variety of coastal experiences.

For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is situated 37 km from the hotel, with a paid airport shuttle service available to ensure seamless travel arrangements. Beyond its comfortable accommodations, Hotel Vera excels in providing thoughtful services such as luggage storage and currency exchange, catering to the diverse needs of its guests.

In summary, Hotel Vera emerges as a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and attentive service, all set against the backdrop of Durrës's stunning coastal landscape.
