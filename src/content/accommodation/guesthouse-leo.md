The establishment prides itself on its array of air-conditioned units, each thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box, and a private balcony. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, featuring walk-in showers, ensuring a comfortable stay. Privacy is paramount, with each unit boasting its own entrance, alongside the provision of fresh bed linen and towels, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience.

Connectivity is seamless throughout Guesthouse Leo, with free WiFi enabling guests to stay connected with the world. The sun terrace offers a perfect vantage point for mountain views, inviting guests to unwind and soak in the natural beauty. The 24-hour front desk ensures assistance is always at hand, making every stay as effortless as possible.

For those seeking to explore the local area, the guesthouse facilitates bike and car rentals, opening up a world of adventure. The surrounding region is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, with fishing and hiking among the popular pursuits. Families will appreciate the indoor play area, while the garden provides a peaceful haven for relaxation.

A convenient minimarket on-site caters to everyday needs, ensuring guests have everything they require at their fingertips. Guesthouse Leo is ideally located, with Ksamil Beach 9 and Ksamil Beach 7 within easy reach, promising a blend of relaxation and exploration. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Guesthouse Leo an accessible choice for both domestic and overseas visitors seeking a tranquil escape in Ksamil.
