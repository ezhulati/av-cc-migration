The guest house takes pride in its family-friendly rooms, complemented by a picturesque picnic area, perfect for those moments of leisure under the sun. For those who prefer the coziness of a home-cooked meal, selected rooms boast a fully equipped kitchenette with a stovetop, alongside the standard provision of bed linen and towels, adding a touch of convenience to your stay.

Outdoor enthusiasts will find Villa Adrian to be an ideal launchpad for exploring the natural beauty of Ksamil, with opportunities for cycling and fishing just a stone's throw away. The property's garden, complete with a barbecue, invites guests to enjoy serene afternoons or lively evenings grilling under the sky.

Strategically located, the guest house is within walking distance of Ksamil Beach 7 and Ksamil Beach, making it a perfect base for beach lovers. For those arriving from afar, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Villa Adrian accessible for international travelers seeking a tranquil retreat by the sea.
