Set amidst lush gardens and featuring a welcoming terrace, the property presents a refreshing retreat for those seeking tranquility and a touch of nature. The apartment itself is a testament to modern comfort, offering air-conditioned spaces inclusive of two cozy bedrooms, a living area adorned with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen equipped with everything needed for a home-cooked meal.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, the Melinda Beach Apartment serves as an ideal base, located just 45 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and a scenic drive from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car. The Rock of Kavaje, another notable attraction, is merely a short drive away, ensuring guests have plenty of options for day trips.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport situated just 39 km from the property, making arrivals and departures as smooth as the nearby sandy shores. Melinda Beach Apartment stands out as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the allure of the Albanian coast.
