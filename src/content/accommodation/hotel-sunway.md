Guests at Hotel Sunway are treated to a host of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The hotel boasts a welcoming terrace, complimentary private parking, and a lively bar, ensuring that relaxation and social opportunities are always within reach. The commitment to guest satisfaction is further evidenced by the 24-hour front desk, convenient airport transfers, attentive room service, and the availability of free WiFi throughout the premises.

Accommodations at Hotel Sunway are thoughtfully appointed to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a refrigerator, and a kettle, alongside essentials such as a shower, complimentary toiletries, and a wardrobe. Select rooms also offer captivating city views and come with the added luxury of a balcony, providing the perfect spot to unwind after a day of adventure. The inclusion of a private bathroom, hairdryer, and bed linen in every room underscores the hotel's attention to detail and commitment to guest comfort.

For travelers arriving by air, the convenience of Corfu International Airport being just 93 km away makes Hotel Sunway an accessible retreat for international visitors. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and dedication to guest experience, Hotel Sunway emerges as a distinguished choice for those looking to discover the beauty and charm of Ksamil.
