The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, including two bedrooms, a living room, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. It is equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave, and two well-appointed bathrooms with showers. For added comfort, towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, enhancing the home-away-from-home experience.

One of the highlights of Apartment Tefiti is the sun terrace, inviting guests to unwind and soak up the sun in a tranquil setting. The property also offers captivating garden views, adding to the overall ambiance of relaxation and leisure.

For those looking to explore the local area, La Petite Beach is conveniently located less than 1 km away, while Maestral Beach can be reached with a leisurely 11-minute walk. For guests traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest airport, situated 95 km from the accommodation, making Apartment Tefiti an accessible and desirable destination for both local and international travelers.
