Each apartment at Seaview is designed with comfort and convenience in mind, boasting air-conditioned rooms complemented by the luxury of free private parking and seamless WiFi connectivity. The highlight of each unit is undoubtedly the terrace, where guests can soak in breathtaking sea views that promise to enchant. Inside, a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, and a private bathroom with a shower and hairdryer ensure a stay that lacks for nothing. Soundproofing across all units adds an extra layer of privacy, ensuring a tranquil retreat from the world outside.

For those eager to explore, Lori Beach awaits just 1 km away, offering another slice of coastal paradise. Meanwhile, the historic allure of Butrint National Park is a mere 2.5 km from the doorstep, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of the region's past. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, located 92 km from the apartments, making Seaview Apartments an accessible haven of relaxation and convenience.
