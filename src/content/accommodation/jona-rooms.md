Security is paramount at Jona Rooms, with full-day monitoring ensuring a safe and worry-free stay for all guests. Each apartment within the complex is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days, complimentary private parking to ease the worries of travelers with vehicles, and free WiFi to keep guests connected with the world or to share their memorable experiences online.

The attention to detail in the accommodations is evident. Every unit is thoughtfully equipped with a desk for those who need to attend to work or write, a flat-screen TV for entertainment after a day of exploration, and a private bathroom that adds an extra layer of privacy and convenience.

For travelers flying in, the nearest gateway is Ioannina Airport, located 144 km away, making Jona Rooms accessible for both local and international visitors seeking a tranquil getaway by the Albanian Riviera.
