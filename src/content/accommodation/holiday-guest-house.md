The guest house prides itself on impeccable cleanliness and guest comfort, offering thorough housekeeping and dry cleaning services upon request. Inside, visitors will find a cozy holiday home equipped with two bedrooms, a TV for entertainment, and air conditioning to ensure a comfortable environment regardless of the weather outside. Emphasizing a health-conscious atmosphere, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

Located 90 km from Ioannina Airport, Holiday Guest-House presents an accessible escape for those looking to explore the beauty of Përmet or simply seeking a tranquil haven away from the hustle and bustle of daily life.
