The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, an equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a bathroom featuring a bidet and hair dryer for added convenience. The inclusion of a private entrance ensures guests enjoy a sense of seclusion and privacy, making it an ideal retreat for both short and extended stays.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and the Durres Amphitheatre is just a short drive away, promising a glimpse into the area's rich history. For ease of travel, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 38 km from the apartment, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures.

Like Home Apartment stands out as a non-smoking establishment, emphasizing a clean and healthy environment for its guests. Whether visiting for leisure or seeking a peaceful escape near the coast, this apartment offers a blend of comfort, location, and privacy that is hard to match.
