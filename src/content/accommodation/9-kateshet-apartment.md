This apartment distinguishes itself with modern amenities, including air conditioning, a lift for easy access, and the indispensable convenience of an ATM. Guests can enjoy the luxury of seamless connectivity with complimentary WiFi available throughout the premises, making it an ideal spot for both leisure and business travelers.

The accommodation boasts a well-appointed bedroom, ensuring a restful night's sleep. Entertainment is readily available via a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels. A fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and a fridge, invites guests to prepare their meals at their leisure. Additional conveniences include a washing machine, ensuring a hassle-free stay, and a bathroom equipped with a shower. For added comfort, bed linen, towels, and an ironing service are provided.

For those peckish moments, the on-site snack bar serves a variety of refreshments and light bites.

9 Katëshet Apartment is ideally located for guests eager to immerse themselves in Tirana's cultural scene, with the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the Toptani Shopping Centre all within easy reach. For travelers arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 14 km away, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, 9 Katëshet Apartment stands out as a stellar accommodation choice, offering a harmonious blend of home comforts and strategic location for an unforgettable stay in Tirana.
