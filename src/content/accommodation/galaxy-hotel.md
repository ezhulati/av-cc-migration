Guests at Galaxy Hotel are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their stay. Each air-conditioned unit is a haven of comfort, equipped with a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with a bidet, ensuring privacy and relaxation. The soundproof rooms promise a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life, with seating areas in all units for added convenience.

The hotel doesn't just stop at providing luxurious accommodations; it also boasts family rooms, making it a perfect getaway for guests traveling with loved ones. The property's terrace offers a serene spot to unwind and soak in the city's vibrant atmosphere or the tranquil garden views.

For those looking to explore beyond the sandy shores, Coco Beach is merely 1.3 km away, while the historic Butrint National Park is a mere 3.5 km from the hotel, offering a glimpse into the area's rich cultural heritage. Furthermore, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, located 93 km away, adds to the hotel's appeal for international travelers.

With free WiFi, complimentary private parking, and a commitment to guest satisfaction, Galaxy Hotel stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for visitors to Ksamil, promising an unforgettable stay filled with comfort, convenience, and memorable experiences.
