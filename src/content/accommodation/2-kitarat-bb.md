The accommodations at 2 Kitarat BB are designed with comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned family rooms equipped with a cozy seating area. Convenience is key, as each unit is furnished with a fridge, kitchenware, and a coffee machine, complemented by the inclusion of bed linen and towels to ensure a hassle-free stay.

Mornings at 2 Kitarat BB are greeted with an Italian breakfast, a delightful spread of fresh pastries and juice, promising a delicious start to the day. For guests' convenience, a minimarket on the premises caters to any last-minute needs or cravings.

Situated 1.7 km from the historic Independence Square and a mere 1.8 km from the iconic Kuzum Baba, the bed and breakfast serves as an ideal base for exploring Vlorë's rich cultural landscape. For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 150 km away, making 2 Kitarat BB a compelling choice for both local and international visitors seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and exploration in Vlorë.
