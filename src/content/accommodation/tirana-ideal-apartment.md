This air-conditioned apartment boasts a thoughtful layout, including two well-appointed bedrooms that promise restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventures. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and an oven, allows guests the freedom to craft their meals. Additionally, the convenience of a washing machine and the luxury of a bathroom featuring a bidet elevate the living experience. For added comfort, towels and bed linen are meticulously provided, ensuring a stay that combines the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.

Strategically located, Tirana Ideal Apartment is within proximity to notable landmarks such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History Albania, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, making it an ideal base for cultural enthusiasts and shopping aficionados alike. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 14 km away, adds to the appeal for international travelers.

In essence, Tirana Ideal Apartment stands out as a stellar accommodation choice, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and access to Tirana's rich tapestry of attractions.
