Guests will find themselves in close proximity to notable attractions such as the Et'hem Bey Mosque, the intriguing Reja - The Cloud, and the historically significant House of Leaves, making it an ideal base for those eager to dive into the local heritage. The convenience of free WiFi throughout the property and the option for private parking (available at an additional cost) adds to the appeal for both leisure and business travelers.

The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring a balcony that invites guests to unwind, two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a well-appointed kitchen equipped with a fridge. The provision of towels and bed linen ensures a comfortable stay, while the strict non-smoking policy maintains a fresh and inviting atmosphere.

For those interested in exploring further, the apartment is conveniently located near the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Rinia Park, and the Clock Tower of Tirana, offering a glimpse into the city's past and present. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, The House of Colors stands out as a compelling choice for travelers looking for an accessible and enriching experience in Albania's vibrant capital.
