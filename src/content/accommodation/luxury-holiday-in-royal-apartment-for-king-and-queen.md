The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living and modern amenities. Guests are treated to two elegantly appointed bedrooms, a bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower, and a dressing room for utmost privacy. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with streaming services, and a fully equipped kitchen underscores the apartment's commitment to guest comfort. Moreover, the air-conditioned unit opens up to a terrace offering serene sea views, enhancing the overall experience of relaxation and tranquility.

Beyond the confines of the luxurious apartment, the property boasts a range of facilities designed to cater to every guest's needs. A beauty service, a fitness room, and a family-friendly restaurant with complimentary WiFi access ensure that guests have everything at their fingertips. For those looking to maintain their wellness routines, yoga and fitness classes are readily available. Additionally, the convenience of a coffee shop and a minimarket on-site adds to the ease of the stay.

Adventurous guests will appreciate the car rental service and the proximity to hiking trails, allowing for exploration of the surrounding beauty. Noteworthy attractions such as Saranda Bay Beach, La Petite Beach, and Maestral Beach are easily accessible, promising days filled with discovery and enjoyment.

For those traveling from afar, the property's paid airport shuttle service to and from Ioannina Airport, located 97 km away, ensures a seamless arrival and departure. Luxury Holiday in Royal Apartment for King and Queen stands out as a distinguished destination for those desiring a blend of scenic beauty, luxury, and convenience in Sarandë.
