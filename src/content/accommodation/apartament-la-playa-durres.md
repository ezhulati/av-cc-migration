Apartament La Playa Durres boasts a generously sized apartment complete with a bedroom, bathroom, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. The living space is thoughtfully designed to enhance guest comfort, featuring a flat-screen TV with streaming services for entertainment, a dining area for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and a private entrance underscores the property's commitment to privacy and convenience.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy complimentary WiFi and parking, making stays hassle-free. For those little necessities or quick snacks, a minimarket on the premises adds an extra layer of ease to your stay.

The location of Apartament La Playa Durres is not just about the beach; it's a gateway to exploring the vibrant area of Durrës. Whether it's casting a line for some fishing or taking to the trails for hiking, adventure is always within reach. Notable landmarks such as the Rock of Kavaje and the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha are within easy driving distance, offering a glimpse into the region's rich culture and history.

For travelers flying in, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport being just 31 km away makes Apartament La Playa Durres an accessible retreat for both local and international visitors seeking a serene beachfront escape with the comforts of home.
