The accommodations at Blue Sea Apartments are designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air-conditioned guest rooms that boast balconies with scenic views. Each apartment is a self-contained retreat, equipped with a fully functional kitchen, a cozy dining area, and a comfortable seating area centered around a flat-screen TV. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which are stocked with complimentary toiletries, ensuring a stay that is as relaxing as it is convenient. Guests will also appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of bed linen and towels in every unit, adding a touch of home to this beachside haven.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Blue Sea Apartments offers a car rental service, making it easy to venture out to local attractions such as the historic Butrint National Park, located just 13 km away, or the ancient ruins of Fanoti, a further journey at 48 km. The nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is 97 km from the property, making Blue Sea Apartments an accessible destination for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Blue Sea Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those wishing to immerse themselves in the beauty of Sarandë, offering both the serenity of a beachfront location and the convenience of modern amenities.
