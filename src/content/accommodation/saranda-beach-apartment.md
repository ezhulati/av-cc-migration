This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its air-conditioned, spacious layout that includes two bedrooms, a living room equipped with a cable flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen, and a bathroom featuring both a bath and a shower. The highlight, however, is the terrace that offers not only mountain views but also an outdoor dining area where guests can savor their meals against the backdrop of captivating city views, all in a smoke-free environment.

Connectivity is seamless, with free WiFi available throughout the property, ensuring guests stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventure in Sarandë. For those venturing from afar, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km from the apartment, making it accessible for international travelers.

Saranda Beach Apartment stands out as a testament to the blend of natural beauty, comfort, and convenience, promising an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
