The property doesn't just boast an enviable location a mere stone's throw from the water; it also offers a suite of amenities designed to make every stay memorable. Guests can enjoy the convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout their stay, ensuring they remain connected, whether for leisure or work. For those arriving from afar, the accommodation provides hassle-free airport transfers, and a car rental service is available on-site for guests keen to explore the surrounding area.

Each apartment at TreA Accommodations is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort. Guests can wake up to breathtaking sea views from the terrace, an ideal spot for enjoying a morning coffee or an evening cocktail. The interiors are equally impressive, featuring air-conditioned spaces equipped with a well-appointed kitchen, including a fridge, stovetop, and essential kitchenware. The convenience extends to a modern bathroom complete with a bidet and slippers, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience.

For those who prefer dining al fresco, the property boasts an outdoor dining area, perfect for savoring meals with a view. Additionally, a minimarket on the premises means essentials are always within easy reach.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, guests can enjoy a picnic area, offering a delightful opportunity for outdoor relaxation and socializing.

Situated just 2.5 km from Vlore Beach, with Independence Square and Kuzum Baba within easy reach, TreA Accommodations - Roof Top On Beach serves as an ideal base for exploring the best of Vlorë. For international travelers, the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 151 km away, making it a convenient option for both domestic and international visitors.

In summary, TreA Accommodations - Roof Top On Beach is more than just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises an unforgettable beachfront escape in Vlorë, blending modern comforts with the natural beauty of the Albanian coast.
