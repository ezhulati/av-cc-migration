This holiday home boasts a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom designed with modern amenities. The inclusion of air conditioning ensures a comfortable stay regardless of the season. Guests can unwind on the private balcony, taking in the tranquil garden views, a perfect setting for morning coffees or evening relaxation.

For those arriving by car, the convenience of complimentary private parking is a notable perk, alongside the availability of free WiFi, keeping guests connected during their stay. It's important to note that Apartment Rio Palace is committed to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy atmosphere for all guests.

Cultural enthusiasts and adventure seekers will find the location of Apartment Rio Palace particularly appealing. The historic Skanderbeg Square is within a 44 km drive, offering a glimpse into the rich history and vibrant culture of the area. For a unique experience, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is 48 km away, presenting an opportunity for breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 38 km away, adds to the convenience for international travelers.

Apartment Rio Palace stands out as an ideal accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Golem while enjoying the comforts of a home away from home.
