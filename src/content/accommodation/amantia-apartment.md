This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it offers a sanctuary of comfort. Guests are welcomed into a space thoughtfully designed with a fully equipped kitchen, a cozy bedroom, and a modern bathroom, complemented by air conditioning for those warm Tirana days. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring relaxation is always at hand.

For those intrigued by local culture and history, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania is conveniently located just 1.9 km away, promising enchanting evenings of performance art. Meanwhile, the Clock Tower Tirana invites guests to delve into the city's rich past, located just 2.9 km from the apartment.

Shopping enthusiasts and culinary adventurers will find the Toptani Shopping Centre within easy reach, offering a diverse array of local and international brands and eateries. Additionally, the spiritual Bektashi World Centre is situated a brief journey away, offering a unique insight into Albania's religious heritage.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 15 km away, making Amantia Apartment a hassle-free option for international travelers. Whether it's the allure of Tirana's cultural landmarks, the convenience of nearby shopping and dining, or simply the comfort of a well-appointed apartment, Amantia Apartment stands out as a top-tier choice for discerning visitors to Albania's vibrant capital.
