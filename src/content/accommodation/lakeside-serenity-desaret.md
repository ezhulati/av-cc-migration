The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and convenience, boasting a well-equipped kitchen complete with an oven and toaster, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a bathroom fitted with a bath adds a touch of luxury, ensuring guests can unwind in style. While the apartment is a non-smoking zone, it doesn't skimp on the comforts, offering the option to have towels and linens provided for a small supplement. A delightful bonus comes in the form of complimentary fruits, adding a personal touch to the welcoming atmosphere.

For those arriving by car, the convenience of complimentary on-site parking coupled with free WiFi access throughout the property ensures that both digital and physical connectivity is seamless. Situated 38 km from the Early Christian Basilica, Port Ohrid, and the Church of St. John at Kaneo, Lakeside Serenity - DESARET serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich heritage and natural beauty of the region. The nearest airport, Ohrid Airport, is located 44 km away, making the property readily accessible for international and domestic travelers alike.

Lakeside Serenity - DESARET offers a unique blend of serenity, convenience, and a touch of luxury, making it a highly recommended stay for those seeking to explore the beauty of Pogradec and its surroundings.
