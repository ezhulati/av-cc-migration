The property boasts a range of facilities designed to enhance the guest experience, including an elevator for easy access, ample luggage storage space, and the convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout. For those arriving from afar or planning to explore the surrounding area, Jico Travel - Erjon Apartments simplifies travel logistics with airport transfer services and a bicycle rental option, adding a layer of ease to the exploration of Vlorë's scenic landscapes.

Each apartment is a testament to thoughtful living spaces, featuring air-conditioned units adorned with a balcony for private outdoor enjoyment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, fridge, stovetop, and essential kitchenware, invites guests to feel at home, while the inclusion of a kettle caters to morning rituals and cozy evenings alike. The living area, equipped with a flat-screen TV and comfortable seating, provides a perfect setting for relaxation after a day of adventure. Convenience is further emphasized with the provision of a washing machine, private bathroom complete with a bidet and hair dryer, and the thoughtful touch of providing bed linen and towels.

For those keen to explore, the apartment complex is conveniently located near attractions such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, making it an ideal base for sightseeing tours. Additionally, the availability of a car rental service directly from the apartment complex opens up endless possibilities for discovering the hidden gems of Vlorë and beyond.

Jico Travel - Erjon Apartments not only offers a comfortable stay but also serves as a gateway to the rich cultural and natural beauty of Vlorë, making it a compelling choice for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the local experience.
