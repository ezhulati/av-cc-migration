Upon arrival, guests are greeted with the convenience of a private entrance, setting the stage for an experience that prioritizes comfort and privacy. The guest house is renowned for its family-friendly accommodations, complemented by an outdoor fireplace that adds a touch of warmth to the communal spaces.

Accommodations at Old Bazaar Rooms are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each unit is furnished with a cozy seating area, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The private bathrooms are equipped with walk-in showers and slippers, ensuring a blend of luxury and practicality. For those who value connectivity, free WiFi is available throughout the property, with select rooms offering the added luxury of a terrace. The convenience of air conditioning and the modernity of a flat-screen TV are standard across all units, ensuring a stay that marries the comforts of home with the novelty of travel.

For moments of relaxation, the shared lounge area provides a welcoming space for guests to mingle or simply enjoy a moment of quiet reflection.

Situated 43 km from the Monastery Saint Naum and 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, Old Bazaar Rooms offers a perfect base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Korçë and its surroundings. Whether it's the allure of historic sites or the natural beauty of the region, this guest house serves as an ideal gateway to the treasures of Albania.
