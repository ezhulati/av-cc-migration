This one-bedroom apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a slice of home with its air-conditioned spaces, complemented by a fully equipped kitchen, a washing machine, and the convenience of private parking available for a nominal fee. The inclusion of free WiFi, a cable flat-screen TV, along with towels and bed linen, ensures a stay that blends comfort with connectivity.

For those keen on local cuisine, a variety of dining options await just steps from the apartment, promising a taste of Albania's rich culinary heritage. Moreover, the proximity to historical sites such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Tanners' Bridge, and the contemporary Toptani Shopping Centre offers a seamless blend of past and present experiences.

Located 15 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 21° Bs Pazari City Center Comfort stands as a gateway for travelers eager to immerse themselves in the city's dynamic rhythm while enjoying the comforts of a thoughtfully appointed accommodation.
