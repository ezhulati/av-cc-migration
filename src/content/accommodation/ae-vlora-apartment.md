The apartment itself is a study in spacious living, featuring a bedroom designed for relaxation, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV offering a wide range of cable channels, and a balcony that presents captivating city views. The kitchen doesn't skimp on conveniences, providing a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster to cater to guests' culinary needs. Adding to the ease of stay, the building includes a lift, ensuring easy access to this non-smoking property.

For those looking to venture beyond the local beaches and historical sites, Independence Square is located just 2.4 km away, offering a glimpse into the city's vibrant culture and history. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km from the apartment, making AE Vlora Apartment a strategic base for both exploring the local area and planning further travels in Albania.
