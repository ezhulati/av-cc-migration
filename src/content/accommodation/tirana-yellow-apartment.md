This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious haven with a balcony that boasts stunning city views. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a well-appointed living room, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and an equipped kitchenette featuring an oven and a microwave for those who love to cook. The convenience of having a bathroom equipped with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Emphasizing a clean and healthy environment, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

For those looking to explore the city at their own pace, a car rental service is readily available, making it easier to visit nearby attractions. Rinia Park, the Pyramid of Tirana, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana are among the popular points of interest that guests can easily access from the apartment. Additionally, for those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located just 15 km away.

With free WiFi available throughout the property and the added convenience of a lift, Tirana Yellow Apartment ensures a seamless and comfortable experience for all guests.
