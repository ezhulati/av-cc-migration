Guests at La Grand - New Bazaar are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The property boasts around-the-clock security and an ATM for added convenience, ensuring a worry-free experience. Complimentary WiFi accessible throughout the premises allows visitors to stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventures in Tirana.

The apartments at La Grand - New Bazaar are meticulously designed to provide a homely yet luxurious atmosphere. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV complete with streaming services for those moments of relaxation. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, and private bathroom with essential amenities like a hair dryer and shower adds to the comfort of guests. Moreover, the provision of an oven, fridge, and coffee machine caters to the culinary needs of visitors, allowing them to prepare meals at their leisure. To ensure a restful night's sleep, each apartment is furnished with soft bed linen and towels.

For those keen on exploring, La Grand - New Bazaar is conveniently located near several of Tirana's attractions, including the Toptani Shopping Centre, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 15 km away, further enhances its appeal to both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, La Grand - New Bazaar offers a compelling mix of location, comfort, and amenities, making it a top consideration for anyone planning a visit to Tirana.
