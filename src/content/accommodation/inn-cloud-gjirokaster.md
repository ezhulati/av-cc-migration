Guests are treated to a spacious accommodation that includes two bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that opens up to breathtaking mountain views. The villa is equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom featuring a bidet. The addition of air conditioning, soundproofing, and a fireplace ensures a comfortable and peaceful stay, regardless of the season.

The outdoor dining area offers an ideal setting for guests to enjoy a variety of breakfast options, ranging from continental to Full English/Irish and Italian, promising a delightful start to the day.

For those looking to explore beyond the villa, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is conveniently located 84 km away, making Inn Cloud Gjirokaster an excellent base for travelers seeking to experience the beauty and heritage of Gjirokastër and its surroundings.
