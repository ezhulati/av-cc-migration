The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, two cozy bedrooms for restful nights, and a living room designed for relaxation. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, and meal preparation is a breeze in the fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven. The dining area provides the perfect setting for memorable meals with loved ones.

For those interested in exploring the local area, the Rock of Kavaje is accessible at 40 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's natural beauty, while the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is also 43 km from the property, adding a touch of cultural exploration to your stay. Convenience is key, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 28 km away, ensuring smooth travel arrangements to and from the apartment.

Pine Breeze Apartment Gjiri i Lalezit stands out not only for its stunning location and comprehensive amenities but also for its commitment to providing a smoke-free environment, ensuring a clean and healthy stay for all guests. With the addition of free private parking, visitors can enjoy the peace of mind that comes with secure and accessible vehicle storage. This property is a top choice for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle, offering a tranquil setting where relaxation and adventure meet.
