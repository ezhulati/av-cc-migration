The apartment prides itself on modern amenities, including complimentary WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine, ensuring a stay that combines relaxation with the conveniences of modern living. The kitchen, equipped with an oven and microwave, invites guests to prepare their meals, making it perfect for those who prefer a homely touch to their dining experiences. The provision of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of comfort, making it easy for guests to settle in.

Privacy is paramount at Central 2 Apartment, with a private entrance that guarantees a secluded and uninterrupted stay. While the tranquility of the apartment's garden offers a serene retreat, the adventurous at heart will find the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 7.2 km away, an exciting escape to nature's wonders. Moreover, the apartment's proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, a mere 2.6 km away, provides a unique glimpse into Albania's historical narrative.

For those traveling to or from Tirana, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 13 km away, makes Central 2 Apartment a strategic choice for both short and extended stays. This apartment not only promises a comfortable and convenient stay but also an immersive experience into the cultural and historical offerings of Tirana.
