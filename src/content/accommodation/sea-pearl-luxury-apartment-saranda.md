The apartment itself is a haven of space and luxury, boasting a balcony that offers breathtaking sea views. Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, it features a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven for those who prefer to prepare their meals. The inclusion of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Notably, the apartment is both non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful and healthy environment for guests.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises caters to the immediate needs of the guests. Those looking to explore Sarandë and its surroundings will find Sea Pearl Luxury Apartment Saranda an ideal base. The property is not only close to Saranda City Beach, which is 700 meters away, but also offers easy access to the Butrint National Park, a significant cultural and natural landmark 15 km from the apartment. Adventure seekers can indulge in a variety of activities such as diving, fishing, and hiking, making the most of the vibrant local landscape.

Situated 96 km from Ioannina Airport, the apartment ensures that guests have a smooth transition from travel to tranquility. Sea Pearl Luxury Apartment Saranda stands out as a sophisticated retreat that promises an unforgettable stay in one of Albania's most enchanting coastal towns.
