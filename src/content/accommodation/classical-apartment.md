Designed with space and modernity in mind, the apartment features a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for those moments of relaxation. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, being fully equipped with a microwave, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and minibar, catering to all culinary needs. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, ensuring a hassle-free stay in this non-smoking accommodation.

Guests have the unique opportunity to enjoy the outdoors in the apartment complex's designated picnic area, ideal for those wishing to dine al fresco or simply soak in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

For those concerned about travel logistics, Ioannina Airport is located 82 km away, making the apartment both an accessible and enticing option for visitors to the area.
