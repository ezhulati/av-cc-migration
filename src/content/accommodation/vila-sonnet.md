The hotel prides itself on offering impeccable services, including room service and a 24-hour front desk, ensuring guests' needs are met around the clock. The convenience of free WiFi throughout the premises allows for seamless connectivity, catering to both leisure and business travelers.

Accommodations at Vila Sonnet are thoughtfully appointed, ensuring a restful night's sleep. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom, featuring a shower and hairdryer, ensuring guests can refresh and rejuvenate with ease. The modern comforts of a flat-screen TV and air conditioning are standard, while select rooms boast the added luxury of a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to unwind. Practical amenities such as a wardrobe and kettle further enhance the guest experience, making Vila Sonnet a compelling choice for those visiting Korçë.

For those looking to explore beyond the hotel, the Monastery Saint Naum is also located 43 km away, presenting an opportunity for cultural exploration. With Kastoria National Airport 71 km away, Vila Sonnet serves as a convenient gateway for international and domestic travelers alike.
