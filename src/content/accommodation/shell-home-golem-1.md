The interiors of Shell Home Golem 1 are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring flat-screen TVs for entertainment and providing towels and bed linen for a hassle-free stay. The property prides itself on being a non-smoking establishment, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

One of the highlights of this guest house is the serene garden, inviting guests to unwind and relax amidst nature. For those looking to explore the local area, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 4.4 km away, offering a glimpse into the region's natural beauty. Additionally, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, located 46 km from the guest house, presents an intriguing historical excursion.

Conveniently positioned 40 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Shell Home Golem 1 serves as an ideal base for both leisure and exploration in the Golem area, promising a memorable stay with its blend of comfort, convenience, and thoughtful amenities.
