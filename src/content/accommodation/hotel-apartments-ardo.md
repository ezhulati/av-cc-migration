Each apartment at HOTEL Apartments ARDO is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning to ensure a pleasant environment regardless of the weather outside. Guests can expect to find a television for their entertainment needs, alongside a bathroom stocked with complimentary toiletries and outfitted with either a bath or shower, catering to different preferences.

For those who enjoy the flexibility of preparing their own meals, the apartments come fully furnished with a kitchen, including a fridge, stove, and all necessary kitchenware. Adding to the convenience is an on-site restaurant, offering delicious options for those times when guests prefer to dine out.

Accessibility is a breeze with a lift servicing all floors, making it easy for everyone to access their apartments. Moreover, the proximity to key landmarks such as Durres Port and Durres Amphitheatre, located 10 km and 11 km away respectively, makes HOTEL Apartments ARDO an ideal base for exploring the historical and cultural riches of the area.

With the added benefit of complimentary on-site parking, guests arriving by car can enjoy peace of mind knowing their vehicle is securely accommodated. Whether it's the allure of the nearby beaches or the comfort and convenience of the apartments themselves, HOTEL Apartments ARDO promises a stay that combines the best of both worlds.
