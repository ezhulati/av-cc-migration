For those arriving by car, the convenience of free private parking removes any travel hassles. Moreover, the adventurous spirit is awakened with the availability of bike hire, encouraging guests to discover the beauty of the surrounding area at their own pace.

Morning delights are guaranteed with a diverse breakfast selection, ranging from a hearty buffet to à la carte and continental options, catering to all tastes and preferences.

Families will find DIMAL CASTLE particularly appealing, thanks to its dedicated children's playground, ensuring that younger guests are entertained and engaged throughout their stay.

Located 103 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the hotel is a testament to the perfect balance of accessibility and seclusion, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers seeking a memorable escape.
