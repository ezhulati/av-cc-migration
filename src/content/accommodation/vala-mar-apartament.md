Vala Mar Apartament provides a haven of comfort with its air-conditioned spaces, ensuring a cool respite from the Mediterranean warmth. The convenience of private parking on-site and complimentary WiFi adds to the seamless experience of staying here. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the property proudly maintains a non-smoking policy throughout its premises.

The apartment itself is a masterpiece of modern living, featuring a balcony that offers picturesque views of the garden. It is thoughtfully furnished with a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a kitchen that comes complete with a toaster and fridge - perfect for those who enjoy preparing their meals. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the attention to detail in ensuring guest comfort.

Situated 46 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, and with the House of Leaves and the National Museum of History Albania both 42 km away, Vala Mar Apartament serves as an ideal base for cultural excursions. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 34 km away, makes it a convenient choice for international travelers seeking a blend of relaxation and exploration in Albania.
