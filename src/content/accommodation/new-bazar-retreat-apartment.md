This apartment stands out for its spacious, air-conditioned interiors, featuring a welcoming balcony that invites guests to unwind in privacy. Complimentary WiFi ensures that connectivity is never an issue, allowing visitors to plan their adventures or share their experiences with ease. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further enhances the appeal for those in search of a clean, fresh stay.

Accommodation at New Bazar Retreat Apartment includes a bedroom designed for relaxation, alongside a fully equipped kitchen that caters to the culinary needs of its guests. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV adds a touch of modernity, ensuring entertainment options are readily available.

Strategically located, the apartment is within easy reach of notable attractions such as the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, the historic Clock Tower, and the enchanting Et'hem Bey Mosque. Its proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha adds a layer of historical intrigue to the stay. For those inclined towards shopping or cultural performances, the Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania are conveniently nearby.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport situated just 15 kilometers away, making New Bazar Retreat Apartment an ideal base for both short visits and extended stays in Albania's captivating capital.
