Upon entering The City Gate Hotel, guests are greeted with the modern luxury of free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms, ensuring a stay that is both connected and comfortable. Each room is thoughtfully designed with a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, catering to the entertainment needs of every guest.

The accommodations at The City Gate are not just about rest but also about functionality. Rooms are furnished with a seating area, providing a cozy nook for relaxation or catching up on work. The private bathrooms add a touch of privacy and convenience, while select rooms offer captivating city views, allowing guests to soak in the urban landscape from the comfort of their room. Additionally, the inclusion of a fridge in every room ensures that refreshments are always within reach.

Mornings at The City Gate Hotel are greeted with culinary delight, offering guests the choice between a continental or buffet breakfast. It's the perfect start to a day of adventure or relaxation.

For those looking to explore beyond the hotel, Corfu Town is a mere 29 km away, presenting an easy opportunity for a day trip. Meanwhile, the Port of Sarandë, located just 2.6 km from the hotel, serves as a gateway to further exploration.

The City Gate Hotel not only promises a stay marked by convenience and comfort but also positions itself as a gateway to the enchanting city of Sarandë and beyond.
