Boasting a generously sized layout, the apartment features a balcony that presents captivating city views, inviting guests to unwind and soak in the ambiance. The interior is thoughtfully designed with a separate bedroom, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven and a fridge, making it ideal for those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, the convenience of a bathroom equipped with a bidet adds to the comfort of the stay.

Located 18 km from Independence Square and with the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 167 km away, Family Joy Apartment stands out as a strategic base for both short-term leisure stays and longer explorations of the region.
