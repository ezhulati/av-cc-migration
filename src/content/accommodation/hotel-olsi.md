Beyond its enviable proximity to the sand and sea, Hotel Olsi distinguishes itself with a host of amenities designed to enhance every stay. Guests can enjoy the convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout the property, ensuring that staying connected is effortless. The inclusion of free private parking is a boon for those arriving by car, removing the usual hassles associated with finding secure parking in a bustling city.

For those looking to explore beyond the local beaches, La Petite Beach awaits at a short distance of 1.8 km, offering a more secluded spot for sunbathing and swimming. Meanwhile, the natural wonders of Butrint National Park are just 17 km away, promising a day trip filled with historical exploration and natural beauty.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Ioannina Airport located 97 km from the hotel, making Hotel Olsi a convenient choice for international and domestic travelers alike.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest satisfaction, Hotel Olsi emerges as a top choice for visitors to Sarandë, promising a stay that is as comfortable as it is memorable.
