For those seeking a slice of history, BeHOME's proximity to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, just over a kilometer away, makes it an ideal base for cultural exploration. The apartment itself is a haven of comfort and style, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living area with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony perfect for enjoying the views. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and fridge, ensures that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the apartment's location near key attractions such as the House of Leaves, Clock Tower Tirana, and Et'hem Bey Mosque, making it easy to immerse oneself in the local heritage. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 13 km away, BeHOME offers both convenience and charm, making it a top choice for travelers seeking an authentic and comfortable experience in Tirana.
