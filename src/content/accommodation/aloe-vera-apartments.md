Guests at Aloe Vera Apartments are treated to a serene retreat with units that boast air conditioning, ensuring a cool respite from the warm Mediterranean climate. The beachfront allure is complemented by the availability of a balcony in each apartment, offering guests a private outdoor space to enjoy the fresh sea breeze. Complimentary WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests remain connected and entertained throughout their stay.

The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with families in mind, featuring spacious family rooms that cater to the needs of guests traveling with loved ones. The interior of each apartment reveals a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes an oven, toaster, fridge, stovetop, and kitchenware, along with a coffee machine and kettle for those morning or afternoon refreshments. The provision of a private bathroom equipped with a shower and luxurious bathrobes adds a touch of elegance to the living experience.

Moreover, the property doesn't shy away from offering communal spaces where guests can unwind, highlighted by a terrace and a garden that present inner courtyard views for a tranquil ambiance. The attention to detail extends to the inclusion of bed linen and towels in every unit, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

For those looking to explore the wider area, Saranda Bay Beach is located just 1.8 km away, while the picturesque Maestral Beach is a mere 2.2 km from the apartments. Travelers will find Ioannina Airport to be the nearest airport, situated 97 km away, making Aloe Vera Apartments an accessible haven for both local and international visitors.

In summary, Aloe Vera Apartments offers a harmonious blend of convenience, comfort, and connectivity, making it an ideal choice for families and travelers looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Sarandë.
