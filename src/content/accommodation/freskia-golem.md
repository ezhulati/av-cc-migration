The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful accommodation, boasting a balcony that overlooks the lush garden, a cozy bedroom, and a living space equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen comes fully equipped with modern appliances including a dishwasher and an oven, catering to all culinary needs, while the bathroom features a walk-in shower. Attention to detail is evident in the provision of towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable stay. Moreover, the apartment offers the added benefits of a private entrance and soundproofing, promising a secluded and uninterrupted retreat.

Dining at FRESKIA GOLEM is an experience in itself, with the on-site restaurant welcoming guests for dinner, lunch, brunch, and cocktails in a family-friendly atmosphere. The menu and ambiance cater to a wide array of tastes and preferences, making every meal a memorable part of the stay.

For those inclined towards outdoor activities, the surrounding area of Golem offers ample opportunities for fishing and exploration, with Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach located just 1.7 km away. The property's proximity to significant landmarks, including Skanderbeg Square at 46 km, and the convenience of being 41 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, positions FRESKIA GOLEM as an ideal base for both leisure and exploration.

With its comprehensive amenities, strategic location, and dedication to guest satisfaction, FRESKIA GOLEM stands as a distinguished option for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and tranquility of Golem.
