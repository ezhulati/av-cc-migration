The property distinguishes itself with its array of family rooms, each presenting a terrace that frames the breathtaking sea views. Inside, guests are treated to the modern comforts of a satellite flat-screen TV, a kitchen equipped with an oven, fridge, and essential kitchenware, and a private bathroom featuring a walk-in shower and hair dryer. The convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi further enhances the guest experience, ensuring a stay that is as carefree as it is comfortable.

Dining at Nea's Apartment is an experience in itself, with an on-site restaurant that welcomes families and caters to a variety of dining preferences, including dinner, lunch, brunch, and high tea. The option to savor a meal in the outdoor dining area adds a delightful dimension to dining, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the scenic beauty of the surroundings.

Situated just 1.9 km from Durres Beach and a more distant 43 km from Skanderbeg Square, the apartment's location offers both the tranquility of a beachfront retreat and the convenience of nearby attractions. For those arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 37 km away, making Nea's Apartment an accessible choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a serene and picturesque getaway on the Albanian coast.
