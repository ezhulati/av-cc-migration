Spanning a generous layout, the apartment boasts a balcony that invites natural light and fresh air, two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a well-equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs. Entertainment is readily available with the provision of a flat-screen TV, making it an ideal retreat for both relaxation and leisure. Importantly, the accommodation maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

Located strategically, Shkodra Apartment is a mere 48 km away from the Port of Bar, making it an excellent base for explorers keen on maritime adventures. For those traveling by air, Podgorica Airport is conveniently situated just 59 km away, facilitating easy access and departures.

In summary, Shkodra Apartment presents a compelling option for guests looking for a blend of comfort, convenience, and connectivity in the heart of Shkodër.
