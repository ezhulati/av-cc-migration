Each apartment at Ernest Apartments is a testament to thoughtful design, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. Accessible by lift, these accommodations boast a cozy seating area, a dining space perfect for enjoying homemade meals, a cable TV for entertainment, and a bathroom equipped with a shower and hairdryer. The inclusion of a well-furnished kitchen, complete with a refrigerator, an oven, and a stovetop, adds to the appeal, allowing guests the freedom to prepare their meals as they wish. Select rooms also offer the added bonus of sea views, providing a serene backdrop to one's stay.

The convenience of Ernest Apartments extends beyond its rooms. Guests will find a variety of restaurants and shops within walking distance, making it easy to explore the local cuisine and culture. Additionally, the property ensures peace of mind with complimentary private parking on site. For those looking to explore further, Sarande's Harbour is a mere 450 meters away, offering easy access to the wider region.

Ernest Apartments presents a compelling option for those in search of a comfortable, convenient base to explore the enchanting city of Sarandë and its surroundings.
