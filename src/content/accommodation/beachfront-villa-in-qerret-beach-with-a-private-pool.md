The villa itself is a testament to sophisticated living, boasting a spacious terrace that invites guests to bask in the stunning sea vistas. Inside, the accommodation doesn't disappoint, featuring three elegantly appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and an oven for those who love to cook. The presence of two modern bathrooms, each with a shower, ensures convenience for all guests. Adding to the comfort are the thoughtful touches of provided towels and bed linen.

For those moments of relaxation, the villa offers a charming seating area and a fireplace, creating an ambiance of warmth and welcome. The option for outdoor dining elevates the experience, allowing guests to enjoy their meals amidst the beauty of their surroundings.

The property's garden is a serene oasis, ideal for unwinding and soaking in the peaceful atmosphere. Its location is truly enviable, with Qerret Beach merely a few steps away, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the beauty of the Albanian coastline. For those looking to explore further, Mali I Robit Beach is just 1.9 km away, offering another picturesque spot for beach lovers.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 44 km from the villa, ensuring ease of travel for international guests.

Beachfront Villa in Qerret Beach is more than just a place to stay; it's a destination that promises a memorable escape, combining the comforts of home with the beauty of the Albanian shores.
