This air-conditioned, one-bedroom apartment is a haven of comfort, featuring a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for those relaxing nights in. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, boasting full equipment including an oven, ready for guests to whip up their meals. Attention to detail is evident with the inclusion of towels and bed linen, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those keen to explore further, the apartment is conveniently positioned near the National Museum of History Albania, the intriguing House of Leaves, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, making it easy for guests to immerse themselves in the cultural tapestry of the city. Moreover, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, making the Tirana City Center Apartment an ideal base for both short visits and longer stays in this dynamic city.
