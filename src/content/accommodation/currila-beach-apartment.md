The apartment itself is a haven of space and luxury, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms, two modern bathrooms, and a living area that combines elegance with functionality. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The fully equipped kitchen and dining area invite guests to prepare and enjoy meals with a view, thanks to the balcony that overlooks the sea. Additional comforts include bed linen, towels, a bidet, and a dressing room, all within a smoke-free environment.

For those looking to explore beyond the apartment, the property provides helpful services such as airport transfers and car rental options, making travel arrangements seamless. A minimarket on-site adds to the convenience, ensuring essentials are never far away.

While the allure of the beach is undeniable, guests also find themselves within reach of Durrës's other attractions. Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are within a manageable distance, offering opportunities for day trips and adventures.

Located just 34 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Currila Beach Apartment presents an ideal base for both relaxation and exploration in the heart of Durrës.
