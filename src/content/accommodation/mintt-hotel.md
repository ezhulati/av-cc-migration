Rooms at Mintt Hotel are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and private bathrooms stocked with free toiletries. Each room boasts a balcony, presenting a splendid view of the city, ensuring guests start their day with an inspiring panorama. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV in every room ensures entertainment needs are well catered to.

Culinary needs are not overlooked, with a daily breakfast menu that caters to various preferences, including continental, Italian, and vegetarian options. This ensures a delightful start to the day for every guest.

Strategically located, Mintt Hotel is merely 500 meters from the allure of Ksamil Beach 9 and a leisurely walk from the vibrant scenes of Bora Bora Beach. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Corfu International Airport is conveniently situated 92 km away, making Mintt Hotel an ideal base for both local and international travelers.

The hotel's commitment to guest convenience is further highlighted by its 24-hour front desk service and the availability of an ATM on-site, ensuring a hassle-free stay. Whether visiting for leisure or business, Mintt Hotel stands out as a beacon of hospitality in Ksamil, promising an experience that is as relaxing as it is memorable.
