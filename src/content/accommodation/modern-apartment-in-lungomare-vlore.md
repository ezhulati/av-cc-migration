The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes one bedroom, a bathroom, and a living area that merges seamlessly with a fully equipped kitchen. Every detail, from the bed linen to the towels and the flat-screen TV, has been thoughtfully selected to enhance the comfort and convenience of your stay. The inclusion of air conditioning and free WiFi adds to the list of amenities designed to cater to the needs of contemporary travelers.

One of the highlights of this property is the outdoor dining area, where guests can enjoy their meals against the backdrop of the city's panoramic views, adding a touch of magic to every dining experience. The apartment's design, featuring a private entrance, underscores a commitment to privacy and exclusivity.

For those who appreciate the simple pleasures, the on-site coffee shop offers a cozy spot to unwind and savor a cup of coffee. The location is also conveniently close to notable attractions, including the Independence Square, Beach at Government Villas, and the historic Kuzum Baba, making it easy for guests to explore the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë.

Situated 153 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the Modern Apartment in Lungomare serves as an ideal base for both short-term escapes and longer stays, promising an unforgettable experience in one of Vlorë's most sought-after locations.
