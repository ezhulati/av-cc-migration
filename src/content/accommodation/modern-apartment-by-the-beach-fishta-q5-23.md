The apartment is a haven of non-smoking premises, promising a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

For those flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 83 km away, making the Modern Apartment by the Beach Fishta Q5 23 an accessible retreat for international and domestic travelers alike.
