Each room at Hanna Guest House is meticulously outfitted with a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV, catering to the needs of the modern traveler. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with a bidet and plush bathrobes, adds a touch of sophistication to the guest experience. Moreover, select rooms feature a balcony, offering a serene space to unwind. Complimentary WiFi throughout the guest house ensures that guests remain connected, while the thoughtful provision of bed linen and towels underscores the establishment's commitment to comfort.

Situated a mere 90 km from Ioannina Airport, Hanna Guest House stands as a convenient gateway for exploring the enchanting surroundings of Përmet, making it a compelling choice for discerning travelers.
