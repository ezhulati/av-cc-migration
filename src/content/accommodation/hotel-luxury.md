Each air-conditioned room at Hotel Luxury extends an invitation to relaxation with its own balcony, offering a private nook for guests to unwind. The rooms are equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV boasting both cable and satellite channels, a mini-bar, a safe, and a work desk, catering to both leisure and business travelers. The attention to detail is further evident in the private bathrooms, which are well-appointed with showers, hair dryers, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a seamless stay.

Beyond the confines of its luxurious rooms, Hotel Luxury enhances the guest experience with a suite of services. The availability of room service, a parking garage, and a tour desk add layers of convenience, making explorations of Ksamil and beyond a breeze. Culinary delights await at the on-site restaurant, where a menu of Mediterranean and local specialties is served, promising a gastronomic journey just steps from your room.

For those looking to explore further afield, Corfu International Airport lies approximately 50 km away, accessible via a ferry from the Port of Saranda, located 15 km from the hotel. Hotel Luxury emerges as a choice destination for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury on their Albanian coast adventure.
