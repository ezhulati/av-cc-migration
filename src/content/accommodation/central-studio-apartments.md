With its strategic position, only 5.7 km away from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and a mere 44 km from the stunning Rock of Kavaje, guests are perfectly placed to explore the natural beauty surrounding Tirana. Moreover, the property boasts views that capture the essence of the city, combining serene street scenes with the lively urban landscape.

Accommodations at Central Studio Apartments are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV complete with streaming services, and essential kitchenette facilities, including an oven, kettle, toaster, fridge, and stovetop. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which feature a shower, hair dryer, and thoughtful touches like slippers and bidets, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For those who cherish the option to prepare meals in the privacy of their own space, the aparthotel's units with seating areas and comprehensive kitchenette facilities offer the perfect solution. Additionally, the provision of bed linen and towels in each unit adds an extra layer of convenience for guests.

Beyond the comfort of the apartments, Central Studio Apartments facilitates exploration and discovery. Sightseeing tours are readily accessible, inviting guests to delve into Tirana's captivating sights, such as the House of Leaves, Rinia Park, and the iconic Clock Tower.

Connectivity is never an issue, with free WiFi available throughout the property and a paid airport shuttle service ensuring smooth transitions to and from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 16 km away.

Central Studio Apartments stands out as an ideal accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the culture, history, and natural beauty of Tirana, all while enjoying the comforts of a thoughtfully appointed aparthotel.
