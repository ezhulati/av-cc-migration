The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, featuring a bedroom designed for relaxation, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV boasting an array of cable channels, and a kitchen that leaves no culinary desire unmet. With a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop at guests' disposal, the convenience is unparalleled. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home, while the private entrance to the accommodation ensures an undisturbed stay.

Located just a short stroll from the picturesque Currila Beach, and within easy reach of Kallmi Beach, Solen Apartments positions itself as a gateway to the coastal delights of Durrës. For those looking to explore further, Skanderbeg Square offers a cultural excursion 39 km away. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 34 km from the property, underscores the apartment's appeal to both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Solen Apartments stands out for its thoughtful amenities, strategic location, and the promise of a tranquil retreat amidst the vibrant backdrop of Durrës.
