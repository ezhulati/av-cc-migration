Strategically positioned, the property stands a mere 6.4 km from the intriguing Rock of Kavaje and is a short drive from the ancient Durres Amphitheatre, making it an ideal spot for those keen to explore the rich history and culture of the area. Moreover, it's located comfortably away from the hustle and bustle, yet close enough to the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car for those looking to venture further.

The apartment itself is a haven of space and functionality, boasting a bedroom that promises restful nights. The living area, equipped with a TV, invites guests to unwind after a day of exploration. Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the fully equipped kitchenette, complete with modern appliances such as an oven, microwave, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, allowing for the preparation of meals just the way one likes. To add to the convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, ensuring a hassle-free stay.

For those interested in delving into more cultural experiences, the House of Leaves and Rinia Park are both situated 38 km away, offering insightful glimpses into the local heritage. Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located just 33 km from Vacation 951, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, Vacation 951 stands out as a stellar accommodation choice, offering both the tranquility of the beachside and the adventure of urban exploration, all wrapped up in a package of comfort and convenience.
