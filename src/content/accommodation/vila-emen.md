Each apartment at Vila Emen is a haven of comfort, equipped with air conditioning, a fully equipped kitchen, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and hair dryers, catering to the needs of guests. Storage is no issue, as each unit features a spacious wardrobe.

Morning delights await guests with a choice of à la carte or continental breakfast options. The menu tempts with pancakes, fresh fruits, and juice, promising a delightful start to the day.

For those looking to explore, Coco Beach is merely 1.2 km away, offering a perfect spot for relaxation and sunbathing. The historic Butrint National Park, located 3.6 km from the property, offers a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 93 km from Vila Emen, making it a convenient choice for both local and international visitors seeking a tranquil retreat in Ksamil.
