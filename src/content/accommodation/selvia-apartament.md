This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it's a haven of comfort. Guests are treated to a well-appointed living space featuring a single bedroom, complemented by a flat-screen TV for those leisurely evenings in. The kitchen doesn't disappoint, being fully equipped with an oven, microwave, and even a washing machine, catering to all the needs of modern travelers. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the attention to detail in ensuring a hassle-free stay.

Connectivity is seamless, with free WiFi enveloping the property, allowing guests to stay connected with the world or plan their next adventure in Tirana. For those intrigued by culture and history, the apartment is conveniently located near the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania and the National Museum of History Albania, promising enriching experiences just a short walk away. Shopping enthusiasts will find the Toptani Shopping Centre within easy reach, offering a variety of retail delights.

Non-smoking throughout, Selvia Apartment caters to the health and well-being of its guests, ensuring a comfortable and refreshing stay. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, the apartment also scores high on accessibility, making it an ideal choice for both short and extended stays in Albania's vibrant capital.
