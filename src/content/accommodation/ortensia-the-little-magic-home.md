The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. It houses a cozy bedroom, a welcoming dining area, and a kitchen equipped with an oven, ready for guests to whip up their meals. The convenience of having towels and bed linen readily available adds a touch of home to this enchanting space.

For those keen to explore, Independence Square is merely 2.3 km away, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history. Meanwhile, the scenic Kuzum Baba is just a bit further at 2.9 km, promising breathtaking views. For travelers flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km from Ortensia - The Little Magic Home, making it an accessible choice for both domestic and international visitors seeking a magical escape in Vlorë.
