This apartment doesn't just boast a strategic location; it also provides an array of amenities designed to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air conditioning, free WiFi, and a 24-hour front desk accompanied by full-day security, ensuring both comfort and peace of mind. The living space is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a kitchen that comes fully stocked with an oven and microwave - perfect for those who prefer a home-cooked meal. Moreover, the inclusion of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of convenience.

For those concerned with allergens or wishing to stay in a smoke-free environment, TAM Serviced Studio Apartment Myslym Shyri No1 addresses these needs by being an allergy-free and non-smoking property. The views of the garden and the quiet street further enhance the tranquil atmosphere of the apartment, making it an ideal haven for relaxation after a day of exploration.

Adding to the appeal, the apartment offers both bicycle and car rental services, facilitating easy exploration of Tirana's must-visit attractions such as Rinia Park, the Clock Tower, and Et'hem Bey Mosque. For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 15 km away, making the apartment easily accessible.

In summary, TAM Serviced Studio Apartment Myslym Shyri No1 stands out as a top-tier accommodation option for those looking to immerse themselves in the charm and history of Tirana, all while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed, strategically located, and guest-centric apartment.
