This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a spacious sanctuary complete with a balcony that boasts captivating city views. The accommodation includes a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven and a microwave, catering to guests' culinary needs, while the bathroom comes with a bidet, ensuring a comfortable and convenient stay.

For those interested in local culture, Kuzum Baba is located just 2 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich heritage. Furthermore, for guests arriving from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km from the apartment, making it accessible for international travelers.

Odri Apartment distinguishes itself with its combination of location, amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay in Vlorë, making it an attractive option for those looking to experience the city's charm.
