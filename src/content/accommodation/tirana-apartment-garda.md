This one-bedroom apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a living experience. Guests are treated to a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV boasting streaming services, ensuring entertainment is always at hand. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, featuring an oven and a fridge, allows for the convenience of home-cooked meals, adding a personal touch to the travel experience. The comfort extends to a well-appointed bathroom complete with slippers, enhancing the feeling of a home away from home. The air-conditioned unit also boasts a bidet and a dressing room, ensuring guests' needs are met with thoughtful amenities.

Privacy and tranquility are paramount, with the apartment offering a private entrance and soundproofing, creating a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life. The convenience of complimentary WiFi and on-site parking adds to the seamless experience, allowing guests to stay connected and explore the area with ease.

For those needing essentials or a quick snack, the on-site minimarket proves incredibly handy. Tirana Apartment Garda is also perfectly positioned for guests eager to discover local attractions, including the lush Grand Park of Tirana, the historical Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, and the iconic Pyramid of Tirana. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 16 km away, the apartment serves as an accessible gateway for international and domestic travelers alike.

Tirana Apartment Garda is not just an accommodation; it's a gateway to exploring the best of Tirana, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and cultural immersion that makes for an unforgettable stay.
