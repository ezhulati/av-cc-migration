This apartment goes beyond mere accommodation by providing guests with a plethora of amenities designed to ensure a hassle-free and enjoyable stay. Guests benefit from complimentary private parking, round-the-clock front desk assistance, and seamless connectivity with free WiFi throughout their stay. For added convenience, the property boasts full-day security, a handy minimarket, and a currency exchange service.

The interior of JON Apartment KORCE is thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. The apartment features two elegantly furnished bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a balcony that offers captivating city views. It is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a fully outfitted kitchen complete with a dishwasher and an oven, and a bathroom that includes a bidet. Attention to detail is evident in the soundproofed rooms, chic parquet flooring, a warming fireplace, and the commitment to an allergy-free and non-smoking environment, ensuring a comfortable and serene retreat.

For those looking to indulge in local flavors, the on-site snack bar presents a selection of snacks and beverages. Additionally, the apartment facilitates sightseeing tours for guests eager to explore the surrounding area, and a car rental service is available for those who wish to venture further afield.

Situated 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, JON Apartment KORCE stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of tranquility, comfort, and accessibility in Korçë.
