Upon arrival, guests are greeted with a thoughtfully designed space that harmonizes sea views with a cozy atmosphere. Each unit at The Sea House Velipoje is meticulously equipped with a balcony that offers breathtaking views of the sea, alongside a comfortable seating area for relaxation. The presence of a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen featuring a fridge and stovetop, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and hairdryer, ensures a stay that combines the essence of home with the luxury of a holiday retreat. The added convenience of a private entrance, along with the provision of bed linen and towels, underscores the property's commitment to guest comfort.

For those looking to unwind, the property boasts a range of amenities designed to enhance the stay. The children's playground and picnic area offer perfect spots for family enjoyment, while the garden and barbecue facilities present an ideal setting for outdoor dining or simply soaking in the views of the surrounding mountains and gardens.

The Sea House Velipoje doesn't just stop at providing a place to stay; it offers an experience. The availability of packed lunches upon request and a cozy on-site lounge area provide guests with options to relax without having to leave the comfort of the property.

Situated within easy reach of notable attractions such as Rozafa Castle Shkodra and Lake Skadar, and only 85 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, The Sea House Velipoje serves as a convenient base for exploring the rich cultural and natural landscape of the region.

In summary, The Sea House Velipoje presents a compelling proposition for those in search of a tranquil beachfront getaway that doesn't compromise on the comforts of modern living.
