The accommodations at Blue Breeze Attic Apartments are designed with comfort and functionality in mind. Each air-conditioned unit boasts a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a fridge. Select apartments elevate the experience further, offering terraces or balconies that present breathtaking mountain views. To ensure a home-away-from-home feel, all units are supplied with fresh bed linen and towels.

For those looking to unwind with a drink, the on-site bar serves as a cozy spot to relax. Additionally, the apartment complex caters to explorers with a car rental service, making it effortless to venture out into Sarandë or partake in walking tours of the surrounding area.

Situated a mere 1.2 kilometers from Maestral Beach and 15 kilometers from the lush Butrint National Park, Blue Breeze Attic Apartments positions itself as an ideal base for both beach lovers and nature enthusiasts. With Ioannina Airport 95 kilometers away, the journey to this tranquil retreat is a scenic adventure in itself.

In summary, Blue Breeze Attic Apartments stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and calm of Sarandë, offering all the amenities needed for a memorable stay.
