Accommodations at Guesthouse Jasmine are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned units equipped with modern amenities such as a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private balcony. Each room also boasts a private bathroom with a shower, ensuring a private and relaxing stay. For added convenience, all units come with a wardrobe, and guests are welcomed through a private entrance, highlighting the guesthouse's commitment to privacy and ease.

Beyond the comfort of their rooms, guests are invited to unwind in the guesthouse's tranquil garden, a perfect spot for relaxation after a day of sun-soaked adventures.

The location of Guesthouse Jasmine is unbeatable for those eager to explore the area's natural beauty. Lori Beach is accessible within a leisurely walk, offering another spot for beach enthusiasts to revel in. Moreover, the proximity to Butrint National Park, just 2.7 km away, presents an opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the rich history and natural landscapes of the region. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making Guesthouse Jasmine an accessible haven for both local and overseas visitors.

In summary, Guesthouse Jasmine stands out as a top choice for travelers looking for a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of serenity in the picturesque setting of Ksamil.
