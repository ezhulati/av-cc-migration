The property prides itself on a range of facilities designed to enhance the guest experience, including a restaurant that caters to diverse palates, a vibrant kids' club to keep the little ones entertained, and a 24-hour front desk ensuring assistance is always at hand. Connectivity is never an issue, with free WiFi available throughout the premises, and a bar where guests can relax with a drink.

Accommodations at Porta Kavalja are thoughtfully appointed to ensure a comfortable stay. Each room is equipped with a desk, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a private bathroom with a bidet and hairdryer, alongside a minibar, adds to the convenience. Select rooms also feature a seating area, providing extra space for relaxation.

For those looking to indulge in a bit of luxury, the hotel's hot tub offers the perfect escape.

The hotel's location is ideal for beach enthusiasts, with Mali I Robit Beach just a leisurely walk away, and the picturesque Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach within easy reach. For travelers flying in or out, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 41 km away, makes Porta Kavalja an excellent gateway to exploring the beauty of Golem and beyond.
