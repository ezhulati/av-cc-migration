Located a mere 48 km from the Port of Bar, Ideal Home positions itself as a prime base for exploring the area. The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen boasting an oven and a microwave, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. Entertainment needs are met with a flat-screen TV, and the inclusion of a bathroom fitted with a shower adds to the comfort of the stay. Guests will appreciate the thoughtful city views offered, adding a touch of charm to the accommodation.

For travelers concerned about proximity to air travel, Podgorica Airport is conveniently situated just 58 km away, making Ideal Home an excellent choice for both short and extended stays in Shkodër.
