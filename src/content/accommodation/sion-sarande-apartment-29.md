This generously sized apartment is designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a living space equipped with a satellite TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen. The kitchen is a culinary enthusiast's dream, complete with an oven, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and an array of kitchenware. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available, ensuring a comfortable stay.

For guests looking to explore the picturesque surroundings, a car rental service is conveniently available on-site. The apartment's strategic location places guests within easy reach of the tranquil Maestral Beach, less than 1 km away, and the exclusive VIP Beach, just 2.2 km from the doorstep. For those traveling from afar, the nearest airport is in Ioannina, 96 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

Sion Sarande Apartment 29 stands out as an exceptional choice for those desiring a memorable stay in Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with the convenience of a prime location.
