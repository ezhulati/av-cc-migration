The apartment at Sea Breeze is a testament to spacious living and modern comforts. It features a well-appointed bedroom, a bathroom equipped with essential amenities, and the luxury of bed linen and towels. The living space is enhanced by a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining area perfect for intimate meals, and a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook. Perhaps the most captivating feature is the balcony, offering breathtaking sea views that promise to elevate every moment of the stay. For those who prefer dining al fresco, an outdoor dining area is available, offering stunning mountain views in a tranquil setting. Additionally, the apartment benefits from soundproofing, ensuring a peaceful retreat.

For convenience and leisure, Sea Breeze houses an on-site bar and a minimarket, catering to guests' needs without having to leave the comfort of the premises. Dining options abound in the vicinity, providing a taste of local cuisine just a stone's throw away.

Explorers will find the location of Sea Breeze ideal for discovering the beauty of Vlorë. Ri Beach is a mere 2.6 km away, offering a sandy escape, while the historical site of Kuzum Baba is 2.2 km from the apartment, inviting guests to delve into the area's rich past. For those traveling from afar, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making Sea Breeze an accessible choice for both local and international visitors.

In summary, Sea Breeze stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a blend of modern amenities, comfort, and convenience in the heart of Vlorë.
