Accommodations at Vila EKREM are designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air conditioning, flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, and kitchens complete with dining areas. Safety is a priority, as evidenced by the inclusion of a safety deposit box in each room. Private bathrooms come standard with a bidet, complimentary toiletries, and a hairdryer, ensuring a stay that combines both luxury and convenience. For a touch of extravagance, select rooms offer balconies with sweeping city views, while all guests can enjoy the convenience of in-room coffee machines and refrigerators.

Morning risers are greeted with a variety of breakfast options, ranging from buffet and à la carte to continental, ensuring a delightful start to the day. The sun terrace offers a perfect spot for relaxation and soaking in the surroundings.

Situated a mere 43 km from the enchanting Ohrid Lake Springs and the historic Monastery Saint Naum, Vila EKREM serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural treasures of the region. Kastoria National Airport, located 70 km away, provides accessible transportation options for international and domestic travelers alike.

In summary, Vila EKREM stands out as a premier destination for those seeking a harmonious blend of hospitality, comfort, and accessibility in the heart of Korçë.
