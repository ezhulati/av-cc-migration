The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom designed for ultimate relaxation. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and stovetop, ideal for preparing home-cooked meals. Additionally, the inclusion of a washing machine adds a layer of convenience for longer stays. The bathroom, featuring a luxurious hot tub, provides a private spa experience within the comfort of the apartment. Guests will also find peace in knowing that the apartment is both non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a restful night's sleep.

Situated 46 km from the enchanting Lake Skadar and just 51 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Seaside Apartments Rana Hedhun offers an accessible retreat for those looking to explore the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region. With its thoughtful amenities and prime location, this property is an excellent choice for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury by the Albanian coastline.
