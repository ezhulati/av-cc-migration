The apartment itself is a study in spacious and modern living, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure. Culinary enthusiasts will delight in the fully equipped kitchen, complete with a dishwasher and an oven, making meal preparation not just convenient but a joy. Additionally, the inclusion of a washing machine adds a layer of practicality to longer stays. The bathroom, equipped with a bidet, underscores the apartment's commitment to comfort and convenience.

For those arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km away, making Dadaj Apartments an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers seeking the perfect blend of relaxation and exploration in Sarandë.
