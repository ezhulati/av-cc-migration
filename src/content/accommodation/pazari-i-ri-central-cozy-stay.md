This non-smoking, one-bedroom apartment is a haven of modern amenities, featuring complimentary WiFi that keeps guests connected, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a washing machine for added convenience. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, invites guests to prepare their meals, making it a perfect retreat for both short and extended stays. The provision of towels and bed linen ensures a comfortable and hassle-free experience.

Cultural aficionados will appreciate the apartment's proximity to the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, the iconic Clock Tower, and the historic Et'hem Bey Mosque, making it effortless to immerse in the local heritage. For those inclined towards shopping and entertainment, the Tanners' Bridge, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania are nearby, offering a diverse range of activities to enrich any visit.

Accessibility is a breeze, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 15 km away, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures. Whether it's for the strategic location, the array of nearby attractions, or the thoughtful amenities, Pazari i Ri: Central Cozy Stay stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for discerning travelers venturing into Albania's captivating capital.
