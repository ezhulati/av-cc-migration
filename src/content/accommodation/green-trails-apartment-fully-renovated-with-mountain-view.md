Upon entering the apartment, guests are greeted with a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a bathroom complete with walk-in shower, and a dressing room designed for utmost convenience. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services ensures a stay that blends relaxation with entertainment. The heart of the apartment, a fully equipped kitchen alongside a dining area, invites guests to feel at home, while the terrace offers an outdoor dining experience against the backdrop of stunning mountain scenery.

For those seeking to explore the local area, Green Trails Apartment does not disappoint. With bike and car hire readily available, adventurous guests can set off to discover the joys of cycling and fishing in the surrounding region, or perhaps embark on a hiking adventure nearby. Additionally, the convenience of an on-site coffee shop and a minimarket caters to everyday needs, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

Located just 79 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Green Trails Apartment presents an ideal choice for travelers seeking a blend of modern amenities, natural beauty, and outdoor activities.
