Designed with space and modern living in mind, the apartment boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring a restful night's sleep for all guests. The living area is equipped with a flat-screen TV, complete with streaming services for those evenings in. The kitchen doesn't disappoint either, offering a dishwasher among other essential appliances, making it perfect for those who enjoy the option of home-cooked meals while traveling. It's important to note, the accommodation maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and fresh environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the local beaches and historical sites, Independence Square is just 2.6 km away, offering a glimpse into the city's vibrant culture and history. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 151 km from Arjon Apartment, makes this an accessible choice for both short and long stays.

Arjon Apartment stands out as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of local charm in Vlorë.
