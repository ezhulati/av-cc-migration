The apartment itself is a haven of modern living, boasting a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, complemented by free WiFi to keep guests connected and entertained throughout their stay. Additionally, the property prides itself on being non-smoking and soundproof, offering a peaceful retreat from the outside world.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, the Waveside Apartment Getaway is conveniently located. The historic Skanderbeg Square is within a manageable distance, offering a glimpse into the rich culture of the area. Adventure seekers will find the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car a worthwhile visit, providing unparalleled views of the region from above. Moreover, the Rock of Kavaje, a local landmark, is just a short drive away, adding to the list of accessible attractions.

Travelers will appreciate the apartment's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making arrivals and departures a breeze. Waveside Apartment Getaway emerges as a top choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the allure of the Albanian coast.
