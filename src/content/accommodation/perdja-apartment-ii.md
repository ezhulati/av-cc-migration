The interior of Perdja Apartment II is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of its guests. It boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, ensuring ample space for families or groups. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a fridge and kitchenware, invites guests to prepare their meals, while the inclusion of a washing machine adds to the convenience. The bathroom is modern, featuring a walk-in shower, enhancing the overall comfort of the stay.

One of the apartment's highlights is its balcony, which serves as a charming outdoor dining area, offering guests the opportunity to enjoy their meals amidst the fresh air. The sense of privacy and security is paramount, with a private entrance and round-the-clock security ensuring a peaceful stay.

For those who enjoy outdoor activities, the apartment provides a barbecue and a garden, perfect for leisurely afternoons. The location is also ideal for exploring nearby attractions, with Rana e Hedhun Beach just 2.6 km away and the historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra within a 30 km distance. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 83 km from the property, making Perdja Apartment II an accessible and attractive option for both local and international visitors.
