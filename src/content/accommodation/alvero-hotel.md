Accommodations at Alvero Hotel are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room features a flat-screen TV with an array of satellite channels, catering to a variety of entertainment preferences. Select rooms elevate the guest experience further with a cozy seating area, perfect for relaxation after a day of exploration. The allure of nature is brought indoors with rooms offering stunning views of the river, garden, or cityscape. For added convenience, every room includes a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower and bidet, complemented by the thoughtful touches of slippers and a hairdryer.

For those seeking a memento of their stay, the hotel's gift shop presents a collection of unique finds. Adventure enthusiasts will find Alvero Hotel to be an excellent base for engaging in the diverse activities the surrounding area has to offer. Whether it's cycling, fishing, rafting, or canoeing, the hotel's proximity to these outdoor pursuits ensures an unforgettable experience. Notably, the picturesque village of Papigo lies just 43 km away, inviting guests to explore its scenic charm.

Alvero Hotel stands out as a premier choice for travelers seeking a harmonious blend of leisure and adventure in the heart of Përmet.
