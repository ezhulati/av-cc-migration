The apartment itself is a haven of space and comfort, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have everything they need for a home-away-from-home experience.

For those keen to explore the local area, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 5 km away, offering a unique natural attraction, while the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is within a 47 km reach, perfect for day trips. Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach is also nearby, located just 2.1 km from the apartment, offering another beautiful spot for sunbathing and swimming.

Situated 46 km from the vibrant Skanderbeg Square and 41 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the Rented Apartment in Golem, Durres, Albania, serves as an excellent base for both exploring the local sights and for those looking to venture further afield in Albania.
