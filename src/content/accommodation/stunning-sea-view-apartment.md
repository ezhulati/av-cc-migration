This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it is a haven of space and luxury. Guests are treated to a generously sized living space featuring two well-appointed bedrooms and two modern bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for all. The inclusion of bed linen and towels, coupled with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, ensures that guests have all the comforts of home at their fingertips. The dining area and a fully equipped kitchen cater to those who enjoy preparing their meals, offering a homely experience.

Perhaps the jewel in the crown of Stunning Sea View Apartment is its balcony, offering breathtaking views of the sea. This feature, along with an outdoor dining area, allows guests to dine al fresco while soaking in the stunning coastal vistas.

Connectivity is never an issue, thanks to the complimentary WiFi, ensuring that guests can share their memorable experiences with loved ones or keep up with work commitments. For those exploring the wider area, Lake Skadar lies just 44 km away, promising an adventure for nature enthusiasts. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 49 km away, provides convenient travel options for international guests.

Stunning Sea View Apartment stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and scenic beauty in Shëngjin.
