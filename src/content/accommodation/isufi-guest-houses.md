This esteemed establishment prides itself on providing top-notch amenities, including air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs for a truly relaxing stay. Privacy and comfort are guaranteed, with each unit boasting a private bathroom complete with a bath and hair dryer, ensuring guests feel right at home.

For those arriving by car, the convenience of complimentary private parking is a welcome feature, alongside the added benefit of free WiFi throughout the premises. The 24-hour front desk stands ready to assist with any needs, ensuring a hassle-free experience from check-in to departure.

Located 97 km from Ioannina Airport, Isufi Guest Houses offers an accessible retreat for both local and international travelers, making it an ideal base for exploring the enchanting city of Sarandë and its surrounding attractions.
