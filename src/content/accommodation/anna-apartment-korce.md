Security is paramount, with round-the-clock services ensuring peace of mind. Culinary enthusiasts will appreciate the on-site traditional restaurant, while the sun terrace offers a perfect spot for relaxation under the sky. Practical needs are met with efficiency, thanks to the available luggage storage space, a minimarket for essentials, and a currency exchange service, making international travel a breeze.

The apartment itself is a haven of modern living, equipped with free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine. The kitchen is fully stocked with a fridge and kitchenware, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals. For convenience, towels and linens are available upon request. The presence of a fireplace adds a cozy touch to the already inviting ambiance.

Families will find the children's playground an invaluable addition, ensuring entertainment for the younger guests. Located a stone's throw from the Monastery Saint Naum and 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, AnnA Apartment Korce positions itself as an ideal base for both exploration and relaxation.
