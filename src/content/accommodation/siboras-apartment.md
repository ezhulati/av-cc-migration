Further afield, Skanderbeg Square offers a glimpse into the local culture and history, 47 km from the apartment, while the Rock of Kavaje, a notable landmark, is only 5.4 km away. Additionally, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 41 km from the accommodation, makes Sibora's Apartment an ideal choice for travelers seeking both tranquility and convenience during their stay in Golem.
