The apartment itself is a haven of space and modernity, featuring three well-appointed bedrooms and a living area brought to life with a flat-screen TV boasting satellite channels. The kitchen doesn't disappoint, being fully equipped with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and toaster, catering to all culinary needs. Upon entry, a private entrance ushers guests into a welcoming space where complimentary fruits await, adding a personal touch to the experience. Additionally, the provision of bed linen, towels, and laundry service underscores the apartment's commitment to guest comfort.

Situated just 1.5 km from the serene Saranda Bay Beach and a scenic drive from Butrint National Park, Denisi Apartment is ideally positioned for guests eager to explore the natural beauty and historical richness of the area. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is located 98 km away, making the apartment a convenient base for both local and international visitors. With its 24-hour front desk, elevator access, and currency exchange service, Denisi Apartment ensures a hassle-free stay in one of Sarandë's most desirable locales.
