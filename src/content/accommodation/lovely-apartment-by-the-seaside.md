Positioned merely 1.5 km from the bustling Golem Beach and within easy reach of the historic Skanderbeg Square, 48 km away, this apartment serves as an ideal base for explorers keen to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty. The Rock of Kavaje, another notable landmark, is just 6.2 km from the doorstep, adding to the list of nearby attractions.

Guests will find themselves enveloped in spaciousness and modern amenities. The apartment features two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven and toaster, catering to all culinary needs, while the bathroom comes with a shower. For added convenience, towels and linens are available upon request.

Noteworthy sites such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the Durres Amphitheatre lie within a 48 km and 17 km radius, respectively, promising enriching day trips. Furthermore, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 42 km away, ensures seamless travel arrangements for international guests.

Lovely apartment by the seaside stands out as a non-smoking property, emphasizing a clean and healthy environment for its guests. This delightful retreat promises an unforgettable stay, blending the tranquility of seaside living with the comfort of modern amenities.
