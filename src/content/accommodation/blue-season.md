The accommodations at Blue Season are designed with comfort and convenience in mind, featuring air-conditioned units that come equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring guests stay connected and entertained throughout their stay. The inclusion of a fully-equipped kitchen, dining area, and private bathroom with thoughtful amenities like a hair dryer, bidet, and free toiletries, adds a touch of home-away-from-home comfort.

Guests will appreciate the added conveniences of free private parking, making explorations of the surrounding area hassle-free. The availability of a balcony in certain units offers an intimate space for guests to unwind and soak in the serene garden views.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty, Butrint National Park is located just 3 km away, offering a perfect excursion for nature enthusiasts and history buffs alike. Additionally, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, situated 92 km from the property, facilitates easy access for international travelers.

Blue Season not only promises a stay that is both comfortable and convenient but also provides an ideal base for guests to explore the enchanting surroundings of Ksamil.
