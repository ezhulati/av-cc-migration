Accommodations at Villa Luan Ksamil are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each unit boasts a balcony that invites the outside in, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy crafting their meals, and a cozy dining area perfect for intimate dinners. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV in each room, while the private bathrooms are well-appointed with a bath and hair dryer, ensuring a refreshing start or end to any day. The provision of bed linen and towels in all units adds a touch of home-like comfort, making stays here effortlessly pleasant.

For those looking to explore beyond the beach, Coco Beach awaits at just a kilometer away, offering another splendid option for sun, sea, and sand. Meanwhile, the Butrint National Park, located 3.4 km from the guest house, presents an opportunity for adventure and discovery within its rich historical and natural landscape. For international travelers, the convenience of Corfu International Airport being just 93 km away makes Villa Luan Ksamil an accessible haven of relaxation and exploration.

Villa Luan Ksamil not only promises a memorable stay through its family-friendly rooms and exceptional amenities but also positions itself as a gateway to the enchanting beauty of Ksamil and its surroundings.
