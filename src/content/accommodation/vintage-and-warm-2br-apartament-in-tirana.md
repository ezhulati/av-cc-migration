This apartment doesn't just offer a place to rest; it's a slice of home with its spacious layout, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen that includes a dishwasher and microwave. For those longer stays or when you just need to freshen up your wardrobe, a washing machine is at your disposal, ensuring all the comforts of home are at your fingertips. The inclusion of two bathrooms with showers, along with complimentary towels and bed linen, adds an extra layer of convenience to your stay.

One of the apartment's most notable features is its ability to offer tranquility amidst the city's hustle and bustle, thanks to its quiet street views. This makes it an ideal retreat after a day of exploring Tirana's rich tapestry of attractions.

Speaking of exploration, the apartment is a stone's throw away from several of Tirana's must-see sites. Rinia Park, Saint Paul Cathedral, and the House of Leaves are all within a 600-meter radius. For those interested in Albania's history, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is just 500 meters away. Other nearby points of interest include the Pyramid of Tirana, Reja - The Cloud, and the Postbllok - Checkpoint Monument, each offering a unique glimpse into the city's culture and history.

For guests arriving by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 15 km from the apartment, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

In summary, the Vintage and Warm 2BR Apartment in Tirana is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the heart and soul of Albania's capital, all while providing the comforts and conveniences that make for a memorable visit.
