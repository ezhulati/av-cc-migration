Each apartment within this complex is meticulously equipped with modern amenities, including air conditioning for those warm summer days and a cable flat-screen TV for entertainment needs. Privacy and convenience are paramount, as evidenced by the inclusion of an en-suite bathroom complete with a shower and complimentary slippers. To stay connected, guests can enjoy the benefit of complimentary WiFi throughout their stay, alongside the comfort of heating facilities to ward off the evening chill.

Situated a considerable distance from the hustle and bustle, the property is located 119 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it an accessible getaway for both local and international travelers.
