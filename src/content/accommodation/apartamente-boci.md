Each apartment at Apartamente Boci is thoughtfully designed with a bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV in each unit, making it a perfect retreat after a day of exploration.

Situated 42 km from the historic Rozafa Castle Shkodra and 43 km from the scenic Lake Skadar, the location serves as an ideal base for guests looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty. For those traveling by air, the convenience is unparalleled, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located just 49 km away, making Apartamente Boci an excellent choice for both short and extended stays in Shëngjin.
