Set against the backdrop of Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, merely 1.6 km away, the apartment is a haven of tranquility and comfort. Guests are welcomed into a spacious abode that opens up to breathtaking garden views from its terrace. The apartment is thoughtfully designed with two cozy bedrooms, a living room that serves as a perfect relaxation hub, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and microwave for those home-cooked meals. The inclusion of a hot tub in the bathroom adds a touch of luxury, making it an ideal retreat for relaxation and rejuvenation.

The convenience of free private parking and complimentary WiFi enhances the stay, ensuring guests remain connected and stress-free about their vehicle's security. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further underscores the apartment's dedication to providing a healthy and pleasant stay.

For outdoor enthusiasts or those simply looking to unwind, the property's garden offers a peaceful escape, while the outdoor dining area invites guests to enjoy meals amidst nature's beauty.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, the iconic Skanderbeg Square lies 47 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of the area. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, located just 5.4 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for exploration and adventure.

Conveniently situated 41 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, SUNTAN Lovely Apartment stands out as an exceptional choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in the heart of Golem.
