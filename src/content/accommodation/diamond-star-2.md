Each apartment is thoughtfully designed with two bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen featuring an oven and a toaster, and a cozy seating area complemented by a flat-screen TV. The presence of two bathrooms, each with a walk-in shower, underscores the property's commitment to providing a comfortable and convenient stay. The outdoor patio, furnished for relaxation, invites guests to unwind while soaking in the picturesque surroundings.

Privacy and tranquility are paramount at DIAMOND STAR 2, with soundproofing and a private entrance for each apartment ensuring a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle. The property's commitment to a non-smoking environment further enhances the quality of the air and stay for all guests.

Situated just 17 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and with Ioannina Airport 97 km away, DIAMOND STAR 2 serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural and historical wonders of the region. With the convenience of free WiFi and private parking, guests are assured of a hassle-free experience from arrival to departure.
