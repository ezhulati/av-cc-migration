This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its airy, well-appointed spaces. Guests can enjoy the luxury of a spacious balcony that boasts sweeping views of the city, making it an ideal spot for morning coffees or evening relaxation. The interior is equally impressive, featuring two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs with an oven and microwave. The convenience of a modern bathroom with a walk-in shower, complemented by the provision of fresh towels and bed linen, ensures a comfortable stay.

The commitment to guest comfort is evident with the inclusion of air conditioning and complimentary WiFi throughout the property. Additionally, the convenience of private parking on site is a notable perk for those traveling by car. It's worth mentioning that the apartment maintains a non-smoking policy, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for all guests.

Strategically set 2.4 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the apartment is also in close proximity to several of Tirana's cultural gems, including the National Museum of History Albania, the intriguing House of Leaves, and the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania. For guests looking to venture further, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 12 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

The Nice Apartment with Cityview stands out as a stellar accommodation choice for those eager to immerse themselves in the heart of Tirana, offering both the comforts of home and the excitement of the city right at their doorstep.
