This cozy 1-bedroom apartment is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests can relax in the living room, which features a TV with cable channels, or prepare meals in the fully equipped kitchen, boasting an oven and a toaster. The inclusion of slippers, towels, and bed linen adds a touch of home-like comfort. Additionally, the apartment offers an outdoor dining area, perfect for enjoying meals with a view.

For those looking to explore beyond the local beaches, Skanderbeg Square and the Rock of Kavaje are within easy reach, located 47 km and 5.4 km from the apartment, respectively. Convenience is further enhanced by the availability of a paid airport shuttle service to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 41 km away, ensuring a seamless travel experience.

Seashore Serenity Apartment stands out as an ideal choice for travelers who value both the tranquility of a beachside location and the ease of access to cultural landmarks.
