Guests are treated to accommodations that boast not only air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days but also the convenience of complimentary private parking and WiFi connectivity throughout the premises. The well-equipped kitchen and private bathroom, complete with a bidet, hairdryer, and complimentary toiletries, add a touch of home-away-from-home comfort.

Notably, Vila Orion prioritizes health and tranquility, offering allergy-free and soundproof units to ensure a restful stay. The inclusion of bed linen and towels in each unit underscores the complex's commitment to providing a seamless and worry-free experience.

Conveniently, Ioannina Airport is located 97 km away, making Vila Orion an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Vila Orion stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those visiting Sarandë, offering a harmonious blend of location, comfort, and amenities that cater to the needs of modern travelers.
