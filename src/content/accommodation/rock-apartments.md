Each apartment at Rock Apartments is a testament to modern living, boasting air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. The living spaces are thoughtfully designed with a seating area complemented by a cable flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a cozy nook to unwind. The allure of these apartments is further enhanced by the fully equipped kitchen, featuring a dishwasher, an oven, and a microwave, catering to the culinary needs of every guest.

For those with a penchant for adventure, Rock Apartments does not disappoint. The property is equipped with water sports facilities, and for those looking to explore the area at their own pace, car hire services are readily available. The surrounding area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering a variety of activities such as diving and cycling, with the picturesque Paleokastritsa only 33 km away.

Rock Apartments stands as a beacon of comfort and adventure in Sarandë, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to experience the best of what the area has to offer.
