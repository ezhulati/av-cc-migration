The highlight of this accommodation is undoubtedly its apartment, which offers guests a private balcony from which they can soak in breathtaking mountain vistas. The space is thoughtfully arranged with a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a kitchenette complete with a fridge for those who prefer to prepare their meals. The bathroom, featuring a bidet, adds an extra touch of comfort. The outdoor dining area invites guests to relish their meals against the backdrop of stunning lake views, enhancing the dining experience with a scenic city panorama.

For convenience, a minimarket on the premises caters to the everyday needs of the guests, ensuring essentials are always within reach. Those eager to explore Pogradec will find the apartment's location ideal for embarking on walking tours, immersing themselves in the local culture and natural beauty.

Situated a mere 38 km from both the Early Christian Basilica and the vibrant Port Ohrid, as well as the historic Church of St. John at Kaneo, Shtepi pushimi Pogradec serves as a perfect base for travelers looking to delve into the region's rich history and attractions. With Ohrid Airport only 45 km away, the journey to this lakeside retreat is as effortless as it is rewarding.

In essence, Shtepi pushimi Pogradec stands out as a sanctuary for those seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and exploration in the heart of Pogradec, promising an unforgettable stay with every visit.
