Accommodations at MyKsamil Apartments are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring flat-screen satellite TVs for entertainment. Select units elevate the living experience with cozy seating areas adorned with sofas, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The convenience of a fully equipped kitchenette and dining area in each unit allows guests the freedom to craft their meals, adding a touch of home. Privacy and comfort are paramount, with most units offering private bathrooms equipped with showers, ensuring a restful and rejuvenating stay.

The allure of MyKsamil Apartments is further enhanced by its outdoor offerings. Guests are invited to revel in the tranquility of the garden or soak up the sun on terraces available in select units, providing a perfect backdrop for relaxation or intimate gatherings. The promise of a garden view from every room, coupled with complimentary barbecue facilities, ensures that every moment at MyKsamil Apartments is imbued with the natural beauty of its surroundings.

Situated just 13 km from the vibrant city of Sarande, guests have the convenience of exploring the local area while enjoying the peace and privacy afforded by free private parking on site. MyKsamil Apartments stands out as a preferred choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of nature's charm.
