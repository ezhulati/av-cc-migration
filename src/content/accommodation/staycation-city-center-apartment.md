The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services, and a kitchen that leaves nothing to be desired. With an oven, microwave, washing machine, toaster, and fridge at guests' disposal, the culinary possibilities are endless. Additionally, the apartment is designed with soundproofing to ensure a tranquil retreat from the city's hustle and bustle, and it even features a bidet for an extra touch of luxury. For added convenience, guests are provided with bed linen, towels, and access to laundry services.

Situated within walking distance of notable attractions such as Skanderbeg Square, the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and Toptani Shopping Centre, guests are perfectly positioned to explore the best of Tirana. Moreover, with the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, and services like airport transfers and car rental readily available, planning day trips or the journey home is effortlessly manageable.

Staycation, City Center Apartment stands out as an ideal spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrancy of Tirana while enjoying the comforts and conveniences of a thoughtfully appointed apartment.
