The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, boasting a generous layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen does not disappoint, equipped with a dishwasher and an oven, catering to guests' culinary needs. Additionally, the inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet adds a touch of convenience to the stay.

For those keen on delving into Albania's cultural scene, the apartment's proximity to notable landmarks such as the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, the National Museum of History, and the Toptani Shopping Centre, offers a unique opportunity to immerse in the local culture. Moreover, the property's non-smoking policy ensures a pleasant environment for all guests.

Located just 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Golden Center Apartment stands as an ideal choice for travelers looking for a blend of modern amenities and easy access to Tirana's historical and cultural sites. Whether it's the allure of the nearby Rock of Kavaje or the historical significance of the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, guests are in for an enriching stay.
