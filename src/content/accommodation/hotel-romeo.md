The hotel prides itself on its multilingual staff, ready to assist guests around the clock at the front desk, with fluency in Greek, English, Italian, and Albanian, ensuring a smooth and welcoming experience for international visitors.

For those interested in exploring the local heritage, the Monastery Saint Naum is also situated 43 km away, making Hotel Romeo an ideal base for cultural excursions. Additionally, the Kastoria National Airport is conveniently located 71 km away, facilitating easy travel arrangements for guests.
