This beachfront gem boasts a generously sized apartment that not only offers breathtaking sea views from a private terrace but also ensures a comfortable stay with its well-appointed amenities. The apartment features a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. The convenience of a modern bathroom with a walk-in shower adds to the comfort of the stay. Guests have the unique opportunity to enjoy meals in the fresh air, thanks to the outdoor dining area, enhancing the experience of seaside living.

Adding to the appeal, Vjola Apartment is a haven of peace with its non-smoking and soundproof rooms, ensuring a tranquil retreat from the bustling city life. Its location is not only favorable for beach outings but also serves as a great starting point for exploring the local attractions, with Saranda City Beach just 1.7 km away and the historic Butrint National Park within a 18 km reach.

For those traveling from afar, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is located 98 km away, making Vjola Apartment accessible for international and domestic visitors seeking a memorable stay in Sarandë.
