Accommodations at Hotel Meta Ksamil are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms equipped with free WiFi and private bathrooms. Select rooms elevate the experience further, offering balconies that present an urban vista, allowing guests to soak in the city's ambiance from the comfort of their room.

Each room is a haven of relaxation, boasting a cozy seating area and a flat-screen TV with cable channels, ensuring that guests have a space to unwind after a day of exploration. The attention to detail extends to the provision of plush bed linen and towels, enhancing the overall stay.

Mornings at Hotel Meta Ksamil are greeted with an array of breakfast options, catering to diverse palates with buffet, continental, and American choices. This ensures that every day starts on a delightful note, fueling guests for their adventures ahead.

The hotel's prime location places guests within easy reach of popular attractions such as Paradise Beach, Lori Beach, and Bora Bora Beach, making it an ideal base for those eager to explore the area's natural beauty. Additionally, for those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is situated 92 km away, making international travel a breeze.

Hotel Meta Ksamil stands out as a premier destination for those seeking a harmonious blend of luxury, location, and leisure, promising an unforgettable stay in Ksamil.
