The apartment itself is a spacious retreat, featuring a bedroom equipped with a flat-screen TV that includes streaming services for entertainment. The kitchen is fully stocked with modern appliances such as a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, toaster, and fridge, catering to all culinary needs. From the balcony, guests can enjoy breathtaking mountain views, enhanced by the provision of outdoor furniture. The inclusion of bed linen, towels, and laundry services adds a layer of convenience to the stay.

For snacking or on-the-go meals, the snack bar and packed lunch options are perfect. The location is ideal for those looking to engage in activities like windsurfing, cycling, and fishing, with the beachfront offering a serene spot for relaxation. Families will appreciate the children's playground and picnic area, ensuring entertainment for all ages.

Just a stone's throw away, Velipoja Beach is located 80 meters from the property, making it an easy walk to the shore. For those interested in exploring local history, Rozafa Castle Shkodra is a mere 30 km away. For international travelers, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest airport, situated 84 km from Premium Sea View Flat, making it a convenient option for both domestic and international guests. This property promises a memorable stay, blending scenic views, ample amenities, and a strategic location to create the perfect vacation experience.
