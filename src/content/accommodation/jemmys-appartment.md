The apartment itself is a testament to spacious living, boasting a balcony that invites guests to bask in the serene surroundings. It features one bedroom, a cozy living room, and a kitchen equipped with modern amenities including an oven and a fridge, catering to all culinary needs. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, making it a perfect retreat for relaxation after a day of exploration.

Strategically located, Jemmys Apartment is a mere 2.5 km from the historic Kuzum Baba and 2.8 km from the vibrant Independence Square, placing guests in the heart of Vlorë's cultural landmarks. For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making the apartment an accessible haven for both local and international visitors.

In essence, Jemmys Apartment offers a blend of convenience, comfort, and accessibility, making it an ideal choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and history of Vlorë.
