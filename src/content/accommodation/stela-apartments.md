Guests are welcomed into air-conditioned rooms that serve as havens of comfort, complete with complimentary private parking and WiFi connectivity that keeps them in touch with the world while nestled in this picturesque locale. Each apartment is a testament to thoughtful hospitality, featuring a balcony that invites the outdoors in, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their meals, and a cozy dining area. Entertainment and relaxation are guaranteed with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV in the living space. The private bathrooms are meticulously appointed with a bidet and a hair dryer, ensuring a refreshing start and end to each day. Moreover, the provision of bed linen and towels in each unit underscores the guest house's commitment to convenience and comfort.

For international travelers, the Corfu International Airport, located 93 km away, serves as the nearest gateway, making Stela Apartments an accessible haven of relaxation and cultural immersion. This review highlights Stela Apartments as a top-tier choice for those in pursuit of a memorable stay in Ksamil, where comfort, convenience, and natural beauty converge.
