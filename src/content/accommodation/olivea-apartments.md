Each apartment at Olivea is a haven of comfort, boasting balconies that offer breathtaking views of the sea and lush gardens. The interiors are thoughtfully designed with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, satellite flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen, making it a perfect retreat for both short and long stays. The convenience of a private bathroom, complemented by the provision of free WiFi, bed linen, and towels in each unit, underscores the complex's commitment to guest comfort.

Situated 32 km from the tranquil Lake Skadar and just 83 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Olivea Apartments presents an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region. Whether it's a family vacation or a solo adventure, this apartment complex promises a stay that combines the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
