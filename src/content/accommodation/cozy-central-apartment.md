This air-conditioned haven boasts a tastefully furnished bedroom, a cozy living room, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and a coffee machine, ensuring guests feel right at home. The inclusion of a modern bathroom equipped with a bidet and bathrobes, alongside amenities such as a flat-screen TV, bed linen, towels, and a laundry service, adds a layer of comfort to the stay.

For those keen on delving into Albania's cultural heritage, the apartment is conveniently located near the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves, making it effortless for guests to immerse themselves in the country's rich artistic and historical tapestry.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi blankets the entire property, and for guests arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away. The apartment also offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a seamless transition from air to accommodation.

In summary, Cozy Central Apartment stands out as an exemplary choice for travelers who prioritize location, comfort, and the freedom to explore Tirana's treasures at their own pace.
