Upon entering the villa, visitors are greeted by a thoughtfully designed space that boasts two cozy bedrooms and a bathroom, complete with bed linen, towels, and a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services for entertainment. The dining area and fully equipped kitchen cater to every culinary need, while the balcony presents breathtaking mountain views, setting the stage for memorable moments over wine or champagne, complemented by fresh fruits. The villa's private entrance, seating area, and fireplace add a touch of exclusivity and warmth to the stay.

For those seeking leisure within the premises, a snack bar is available, along with the option of packed lunches for day trips. The villa's location is a haven for diving enthusiasts, with car hire services readily available for exploring the surroundings. Guests are also invited to unwind in the villa's lush garden, offering a tranquil escape from the everyday.

Cosy Guest House is conveniently located a short distance from La Petite Beach and the historic Butrint National Park, making it an ideal base for exploring the natural and cultural riches of the area. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 94 kilometers away, ensuring easy access to this exquisite Sarandë haven.
