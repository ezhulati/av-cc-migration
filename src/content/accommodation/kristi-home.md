Guests at Kristi Home are treated to a comfortable stay in a one-bedroom apartment that doesn't skimp on amenities. The apartment is air-conditioned, ensuring a cool retreat from the warm Mediterranean climate. It boasts a flat-screen TV and a washing machine, adding a touch of home comfort to the travel experience. The kitchen is fully equipped, featuring a fridge, which allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like.

One of the highlights of staying at Kristi Home is the enchanting garden views it offers, providing a peaceful backdrop for a morning coffee or an evening unwind. The inclusion of free WiFi ensures that guests remain connected, whether it's for leisure browsing or catching up on work.

For those keen to explore, Kristi Home serves as an excellent base. It is a mere 38 km from the historical Skanderbeg Square and equally close to the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history. Adventure seekers will find the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 42 km away, an exciting day out with stunning views. Meanwhile, the Rock of Kavaje, just 5.8 km from the apartment, presents an opportunity for picturesque hikes.

Convenience is key, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 32 km away, making Kristi Home an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers alike. Whether it's the allure of Durres Beach or the cultural exploration in Durres County, Kristi Home promises a stay that combines comfort with the charm of Albania.
