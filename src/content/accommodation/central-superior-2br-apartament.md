Guests will find themselves in a serene setting, with the apartment boasting quiet street views, yet it is conveniently situated just 1.3 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and a scenic drive away from the Rock of Kavaje. The inclusion of free bikes enhances the appeal for those eager to explore the city's charm at their own pace.

The accommodation itself is a testament to thoughtful design and comfort. It features two well-appointed bedrooms, a bathroom stocked with essential linens and towels, and a living space that includes a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. The balcony offers a splendid view of the city, allowing guests to soak in the urban atmosphere from the comfort of their apartment. Notably, the apartment is non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful stay.

For dining, the on-site restaurant serves a selection of Mediterranean cuisine, complemented by the convenience of free WiFi throughout the premises. Additionally, the coffee shop and the option for packed lunches cater to the needs of guests with various preferences and schedules.

The apartment facilitates exploration and mobility with its car rental service, making it effortless for guests to visit nearby attractions such as the House of Leaves, the National Museum of History Albania, and Rinia Park. For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 13 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for a seamless connection.

Central Superior 2BR Apartment not only provides a comfortable and convenient base for exploring Tirana but also enriches the travel experience with its thoughtful amenities and prime location.
