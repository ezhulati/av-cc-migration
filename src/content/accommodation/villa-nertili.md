Accommodations at Villa Nertili are thoughtfully designed, with each room opening onto its own balcony, offering a breath of fresh air and a slice of the outdoors. For those desiring a touch of home, select rooms are furnished with a kitchenette, complete with a fridge, ensuring refreshments are always at hand. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of TVs equipped with cable channels in every room.

Mornings at Villa Nertili are greeted with the option of a continental breakfast, available daily for a nominal fee, ensuring a pleasant start to the day.

Guests looking to explore the local area or seeking advice on the best activities will find the reception staff knowledgeable and eager to assist. Additionally, the UNESCO-protected Butrint National Park, a site of significant historical and natural beauty, is conveniently located just 5 km away, making Villa Nertili an ideal base for both relaxation and adventure.
