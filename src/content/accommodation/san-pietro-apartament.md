Guests are welcomed into a world of contemporary living, with the apartment boasting air-conditioned spaces, private parking on the premises, and complimentary WiFi. The commitment to a smoke-free environment further enhances the appeal for those in search of a healthier stay. 

The interior of San Pietro Apartment does not disappoint. It features a generous layout that includes two bedrooms, a living room, and a balcony that offers serene garden views. The kitchen is notably well-equipped, catering to the needs of those who prefer home-cooked meals. Entertainment needs are met with the provision of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have access to their favorite shows and movies.

For those keen on exploring the cultural tapestry of Albania, the apartment's location serves as an excellent base. It is a mere 44 km from both the House of Leaves and the National Museum of History Albania, making it convenient for guests to immerse themselves in the country's rich history. Moreover, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, located 48 km away, offers an adventurous escape with its breathtaking aerial views.

Accessibility is another highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 29 km away, ensuring smooth travel arrangements for international guests.

In summary, San Pietro Apartment stands out as a sophisticated haven that marries the ease of modern living with the charm of Durrës' historical landscape, making it an attractive option for both leisure and exploration.
