The hotel's accommodations are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms that come equipped with a desk, a balcony offering captivating city views, and a private bathroom. Each room is a retreat of comfort and style, complete with a flat-screen TV, fresh bed linen, towels, and the convenience of a fridge.

Morning at Envy Hotel begins with a choice between an à la carte or continental breakfast, promising a delightful start to the day. Beyond the comforts of their rooms, guests will find a range of services designed to enhance their stay, including room service, a 24-hour front desk, and currency exchange facilities.

For those looking to explore beyond the hotel's immediate surroundings, Skanderbeg Square is located 39 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of the area, while the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, 43 km from the hotel, presents an adventure for those seeking to experience breathtaking views from above. Conveniently, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is a mere 34 km away, making Envy Hotel an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers seeking a blend of convenience, comfort, and culture.
