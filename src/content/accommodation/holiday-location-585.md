The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and functionality, featuring a well-equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge, a cozy seating area for relaxation, and a modern bathroom with a bidet. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is never far away, while the air-conditioned space, complete with a shower and dressing room, guarantees a comfortable retreat from the heat. Importantly, the accommodation upholds a non-smoking policy, ensuring a fresh and welcoming environment for all guests.

For those looking to explore beyond the apartment, Holiday Location 585 facilitates adventure with both airport transfer services and a car rental option, making local exploration both easy and accessible. Additionally, the presence of a coffee shop and bar on-site promises delightful moments of leisure and refreshment.

Situated 39 km from the historic Skanderbeg Square and 43 km from the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, the apartment serves as an ideal base for guests wishing to delve into the cultural and natural attractions of the area. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport only 33 km away, Holiday Location 585 represents a convenient and charming choice for both domestic and international travelers looking for a memorable Albanian coastline experience.
