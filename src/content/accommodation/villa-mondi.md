Each room at Villa Mondi is thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV, comfortable seating area, refrigerator, and dining table, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. The convenience of a private bathroom with a shower adds to the comfort of guests.

For those drawn to the allure of the sea, Villa Mondi presents a private beach area, inviting guests to bask in the sun or dive into the crystal-clear waters. The hotel's on-site restaurant caters to culinary desires, serving a variety of dishes that promise to satisfy any palate.

Adventure enthusiasts will find the hotel's location ideal, with opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, and cycling readily available. The proximity to Butrint National Park, just 4 km away, offers a chance to explore the rich history and natural beauty of the area.

Adding to the appeal, Villa Mondi ensures convenience with free private parking, making it an effortless choice for those traveling by car. This property stands out as a premier destination for those seeking a blend of leisure, comfort, and exploration in Ksamil.
