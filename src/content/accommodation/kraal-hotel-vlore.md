Accommodations at Kraal Hotel Vlore are thoughtfully equipped to meet the needs of modern travelers. Each air-conditioned room comes furnished with a desk, kettle, minibar, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with a bidet, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and convenient. The inclusion of bed linen and towels in every room adds an extra layer of comfort to the guest experience.

Morning at the hotel begins with a choice of buffet, continental, or Italian breakfast, offering a delightful start to the day. Located just 1.9 km from Vlore Beach and 2.7 km from Independence Square, the hotel's proximity to local attractions makes it an ideal base for exploring the vibrant surroundings. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making Kraal Hotel Vlore a strategic choice for both domestic and international travelers.

With its commitment to providing exceptional service around the clock, including airport transfers and room service, alongside the convenience of free WiFi throughout the property, Kraal Hotel Vlore ensures a stay that is as seamless as it is enjoyable.
