This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it's a haven of comfort and convenience. Guests are treated to a spacious layout that includes two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. Modern amenities are not spared, with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, an equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher and an oven for those home-cooked meals, and two bathrooms with walk-in showers ensuring a comfortable stay. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home.

For those minor necessities or quick snacks, the apartment conveniently houses a minimarket. While the allure of the beach is undeniable, guests also have the opportunity to explore further afield. Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are within driving distance, offering unique experiences and scenic views of the area. Moreover, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 36 km away, makes Sea View Nik Apartment a practical choice for both international and domestic travelers.

In essence, Sea View Nik Apartment stands out as a stellar accommodation option, combining the tranquility of beachfront living with the comforts of a fully equipped, modern apartment. It's an ideal getaway for those looking to enjoy the beauty of Durrës and its surroundings.
