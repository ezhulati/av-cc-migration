Guests at Xhesi Apartment are treated to a spacious sanctuary that boasts a well-appointed balcony, offering a tranquil spot to unwind. The apartment features a generously sized bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a fully equipped kitchen that caters to all culinary needs. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Notably, Xhesi Apartment prides itself on being a non-smoking property, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for all guests. Its proximity to significant landmarks, such as the Rock of Kavaje and the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, adds to its appeal, making it an ideal base for exploring the rich history and culture of the area.

For those traveling to or from the region, the convenience of being located just 38 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes Xhesi Apartment a highly accessible option. With its combination of comfort, location, and thoughtful amenities, Xhesi Apartment stands as a top recommendation for visitors to Durrës looking for a memorable stay by the sea.
