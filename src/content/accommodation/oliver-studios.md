The inn prides itself on its array of amenities designed to enhance the guest experience. A shared kitchen and round-the-clock front desk service ensure needs are met at any hour, while the entire property boasts free WiFi, catering to the modern traveler's connectivity needs. For those seeking a smoke-free environment, Oliver Studios stands out as a breath of fresh air.

Accommodations at Oliver Studios are thoughtfully appointed to maximize comfort and satisfaction. Each room opens to a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to unwind. Inside, air conditioning and flat-screen TVs are standard, with select rooms offering captivating city views. The assurance of a private bathroom with every room underscores the inn's commitment to privacy and convenience.

For dining al fresco, the on-site barbecue facilities provide an ideal setting for casual meals under the sky.

The inn's location is unbeatable for beach lovers and explorers alike, with Coco Beach, Bora Bora Beach, and the lesser-known Ksamil Beach 7 within easy reach. For those venturing further, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Oliver Studios an accessible retreat for international travelers.

In summary, Oliver Studios presents a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty, making it a compelling choice for those visiting Ksamil.
