Each apartment at Villa Bushi is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air conditioning, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, and a private bathroom that includes a shower and hair dryer for convenience. Select units also boast a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, catering to those who prefer the option of home-cooked meals. Additionally, ample storage is provided with the inclusion of a wardrobe in every unit.

For those looking to explore the local cuisine, a variety of dining options are readily available near the apartment, offering a taste of the local flavors. Moreover, Villa Bushi facilitates exploration and adventure by offering a car rental service, allowing guests to venture beyond the immediate area with ease.

Situated just 3 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and 92 km from Corfu International Airport, Villa Bushi presents an ideal base for both relaxation and exploration. Whether it's lounging on the beach or discovering the historical and natural wonders of the region, Villa Bushi caters to a diverse range of interests and preferences, making it a standout accommodation choice in Ksamil.
