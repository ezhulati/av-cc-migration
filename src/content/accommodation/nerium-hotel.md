Guests are greeted with a host of thoughtful amenities designed to enhance their stay. Each room is a sanctuary of comfort, equipped with air conditioning, a wardrobe, and a balcony that offers a picturesque view of the city. The inclusion of a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, bed linen, towels, and a fridge in every unit underscores the hotel's attention to detail and dedication to guest satisfaction.

The start of each day at NERIUM Hotel is marked by a delightful continental breakfast, ensuring guests are well-fueled for their adventures ahead. The hotel's proximity to Coco Beach, less than 1 km away, and Butrint National Park, just 3.4 km away, positions it as a perfect base for exploring the natural beauty and historical richness of the area. For international travelers, the convenience of Corfu International Airport being only 93 km away adds to the hotel's appeal.

With its 24-hour front desk, ATM, and currency exchange services, NERIUM Hotel stands out as a beacon of hospitality, catering to the needs of its guests at any hour. Whether visiting for leisure or business, guests will find NERIUM Hotel to be a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and attentive service, making it a distinguished choice in Ksamil.
