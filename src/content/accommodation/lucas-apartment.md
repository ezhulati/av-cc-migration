Guests of Lucas Apartment are treated to the luxury of both complimentary WiFi and on-site parking, ensuring a hassle-free stay from start to finish. The apartment prides itself on being a non-smoking facility, catering to the comfort and well-being of all guests.

This accommodation boasts a well-appointed 2-bedroom apartment equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, PS3, and air conditioning, alongside a cozy living room. The kitchen is fully equipped, allowing guests the freedom to prepare meals at their leisure. For added convenience, towels and linens are available upon request, ensuring a comfortable and worry-free stay.

One of the highlights of Lucas Apartment is undoubtedly its offering of stunning mountain views, providing a serene backdrop to an already memorable stay. While the Rock of Kavaje is a bit further afield at 44 km, the proximity to Bunk'Art 1 Museum, just 2.8 km away, offers a unique cultural experience close by. For those traveling by air, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 15 km from the apartment, making arrivals and departures as smooth as possible.

Lucas Apartment stands out as an exceptional choice for those looking to explore Tirana, offering a blend of convenience, comfort, and scenic beauty.
