The apartment itself is a haven of modern living, boasting an air-conditioned space that includes a cozy bedroom, a welcoming living room complete with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a bathroom outfitted with a bidet and washing machine. The commitment to a non-smoking environment ensures a pleasant stay for all guests.

Adding to the appeal is the convenience of private parking on site and complimentary WiFi, allowing guests to stay connected and plan their explorations with ease.
