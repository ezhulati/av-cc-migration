The interior of Apartment Perla is thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers. It boasts a spacious layout with a bedroom that promises restful nights. Entertainment is readily available through a flat-screen TV equipped with satellite channels, ensuring guests are never far from global happenings or local culture. The kitchen is a highlight, fully equipped with a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and fridge, making self-catering a delightful option for those who prefer to dine in. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home, ensuring a comfortable stay.

An added feature that enhances the privacy and convenience of this accommodation is its private entrance, allowing guests to come and go as they please without disturbances. The terrace is another pleasant space where guests can unwind and soak in the beauty of Berat's unique landscape.

For those venturing from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 119 km away, making Apartment Perla an accessible choice for international and domestic travelers alike. This apartment not only promises a stay filled with comfort and convenience but also offers a gateway to exploring the rich heritage and natural beauty of Berat.
