Each room at CLASS Hotel is a haven of relaxation, equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV, complemented by high-quality bed linen and towels, promising a stay that marries comfort with functionality. Mornings at CLASS Hotel start on a high note with a choice of buffet or continental breakfast, catering to a variety of tastes and preferences.

The hotel's prime location places guests within easy reach of Ksamil's most beloved beaches, including Ksamil Beach, Sunset Beach, and Coco Beach, making it an ideal base for those eager to explore the natural beauty of the region. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 93 km away, making CLASS Hotel an accessible gateway to the wonders of Vlorë County.
