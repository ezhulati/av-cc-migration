Boasting a modern one-bedroom layout, the apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, a washing machine, and a bathroom complete with a bidet, catering to the needs of contemporary travelers. The inclusion of a balcony adds a touch of elegance, offering guests a private space to unwind after a day of exploration.

For those interested in the city's green spaces and architectural marvels, Rinia Park, the Clock Tower of Tirana, and the Et'hem Bey Mosque are conveniently located near the apartment, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local culture. Furthermore, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, just 14 km away, makes Tirana Urban Gateway 1 an ideal choice for both short visits and extended stays, promising an experience that combines the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
