The accommodations at Hotel Agimi do not disappoint. Each air-conditioned room boasts a furnished balcony, offering guests a private outdoor space to relax and unwind. The rooms are equipped with an LCD satellite TV, a minibar, and a desk, catering to both leisure and business travelers. The en-suite bathrooms, complete with a shower and hairdryer, add to the comfort of the stay. Guests can stay connected with the outside world thanks to the free WiFi available throughout the property.

Dining at Hotel Agimi is an experience in itself. The on-site bar and restaurant serve a delightful mix of Mediterranean and Albanian cuisine. The large terrace, overlooking the sea, provides a stunning backdrop for meals, making dining here a memorable part of any stay.

For those looking to explore, Hotel Agimi's location is ideal. Just 200 meters from the Promenade, guests are within easy reach of Sarande's vibrant center. The Main Bus Station and the Ferry Port, offering connections to the island of Corfu, are also conveniently close. Adventure seekers will appreciate the proximity to Lekursi Castle and the UNESCO-protected Butrint National Park, both offering unique insights into the region's rich history and natural beauty.

Additional conveniences include free public parking, a souvenir shop, and a grocery shop on the premises. For those looking to add a splash of excitement to their stay, boat and jet-ski rentals can be arranged through the hotel staff.

In summary, Hotel Agimi presents a compelling package for travelers to Sarande, combining essential amenities with a prime location, all designed to make guests' stays as enjoyable and hassle-free as possible.
