Guests are treated to the convenience of complimentary private parking and the modern necessity of free WiFi, ensuring that both vehicles and digital needs are well catered for. Each air-conditioned unit at Draci Apartments is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a terrace to enjoy the serene views.

For those willing to venture further, the ancient city of Fanoti is a 50 km drive, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, located 95 km away, makes Draci Apartments a practical choice for international travelers seeking a blend of relaxation and adventure in Sarandë.
