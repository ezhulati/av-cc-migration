The apartment's layout is thoughtfully designed, featuring a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who enjoy preparing their own meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are well catered to with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a pleasant stay.

Strategically located, Cozy Apparemment 1+1 is within easy reach of notable landmarks. It is a short drive from the historical Rock of Kavaje, only 4.3 km away, while the cultural heart of the country, Skanderbeg Square, and the adventurous Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are both within a 43 km radius. Additionally, the apartment is conveniently situated 33 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it an accessible option for international travelers.

For those intrigued by history, the former residence of Enver Hoxha is also 39 km away, and the ancient Durres Amphitheatre is just 7.2 km from the property, offering a glimpse into Albania's rich past.

Cozy Apparemment 1+1 stands out as an ideal lodging for guests who appreciate the blend of tranquility and the opportunity to explore the vibrant surroundings of Durrës.
