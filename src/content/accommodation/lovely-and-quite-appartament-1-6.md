Positioned conveniently 1.9 km from the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and merely 2 km from the National Museum of History Albania, guests are in the perfect spot to immerse themselves in the cultural heartbeat of the city. The Toptani Shopping Centre, a mere 2.3 km away, offers a delightful shopping experience, while the iconic Skanderbeg Square is just 3 km from the doorstep, promising a glimpse into the vibrant local life and history.

The apartment itself is a haven of comfort, featuring air-conditioning for those warm Tirana days, a fully equipped kitchen for culinary adventures, and a cozy bedroom designed to ensure a restful night's sleep. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, complemented by free WiFi throughout the property, ensuring guests stay connected and entertained. Emphasizing a smoke-free environment, the accommodation promises a fresh and inviting atmosphere.

For those intrigued by historical architecture, Tanners' Bridge is within a 2.5 km reach, and the Clock Tower of Tirana, a symbol of the city, is also 3 km away. Convenience is further underscored by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located just 14 km from the apartment, facilitating easy travel arrangements.

Lovely and Quiet Apartment 1-6 stands out as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and a touch of Albanian charm, making it a memorable part of any Tirana adventure.
