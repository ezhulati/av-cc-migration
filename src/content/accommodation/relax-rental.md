Guests are greeted with a variety of amenities designed to enhance their stay, from the tranquility of a private balcony overlooking the garden to the practicality of free private parking. The property prides itself on offering a 24-hour front desk service and an elevator, catering to the needs of every guest at any hour.

Each apartment is a haven of comfort, featuring a terrace that invites guests to bask in the stunning sea vistas, a cozy seating area for unwinding, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen, equipped with an oven, microwave, fridge, stovetop, and coffee machine, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals just the way they like. The convenience of a private bathroom with a bidet adds to the allure, ensuring a private and comfortable experience.

For those looking to enjoy a drink without straying far, the on-site bar serves as a perfect spot to relax and socialize. The strategic location of Relax Rental places guests within easy reach of notable landmarks, with Independence Square just 3.2 km away and the historic Kuzum Baba at a distance of 3.8 km. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km away, making Relax Rental an accessible retreat for both local and foreign visitors seeking a tranquil escape by the sea.
