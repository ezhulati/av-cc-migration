The villa itself is a testament to luxury living, boasting a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room complete with a flat-screen TV for entertainment, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a bathroom featuring a bath. The highlight of the accommodation is undoubtedly the fireplace, adding a touch of warmth and coziness to the already inviting atmosphere.

For those interested in cultural excursions, the Church of St. John at Kaneo is located 35 km away, offering a glimpse into the area's rich historical tapestry. Additionally, the Monastery Saint Naum, just 2.7 km from the villa, provides a peaceful and spiritual experience for visitors. With Ohrid Airport only 41 km away, Drilon Luxury Villa ensures ease of access for international and domestic travelers alike, promising a stay that combines the comforts of home with the allure of exploration.
