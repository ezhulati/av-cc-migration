The interior reveals a generously sized living space, featuring two bedrooms, a living room that invites relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen ready for culinary exploits, and a bathroom designed with comfort in mind. Entertainment is readily available through the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have access to leisure at their fingertips.
