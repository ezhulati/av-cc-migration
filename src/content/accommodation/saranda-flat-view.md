The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring a well-equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a bathroom fitted with a shower, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, underscores the attention to detail and guest comfort. Notably, the apartment is designed to be non-smoking and soundproof, providing a serene retreat from the bustling city life.

For those who relish outdoor dining, the apartment facilitates an experience to dine under the stars, adding a memorable touch to the stay. Its proximity to La Petite Beach and the enchanting Butrint National Park, located 17 km away, presents ample opportunities for exploration and adventure.

Travelers will find the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, 97 km away, making Saranda Flat View a convenient choice for both domestic and international visitors. This apartment not only serves as a gateway to the natural and historical treasures of Sarandë but also offers a tranquil and comfortable haven for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.
