Security is paramount at GardenView, with round-the-clock surveillance complemented by the convenience of an on-site restaurant and a quaint picnic area, catering to all dining needs. The presence of an ATM, along with the exclusivity of private check-in and check-out services, underscores the apartment's commitment to guest comfort and privacy.

The interior of the apartment does not disappoint, boasting a well-appointed kitchen equipped with a fridge and essential kitchenware, making in-stay dining a breeze. The living space is thoughtfully designed, featuring a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, ideal for unwinding after a day of exploration. The privacy is further enhanced by a separate entrance, and the bathroom comes well-equipped, whether with a bath or shower, catering to personal preferences.

For those moments of tranquility, the garden offers a peaceful retreat, inviting guests to relax amidst nature's embrace.

Explorers looking to venture beyond the immediate comforts of the apartment will find Mali I Robit Beach and Durres Beach within easy reach, located 2.6 km and 2.9 km away, respectively. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently situated 38 km from the apartment, making GardenView at the Sea Apartment an accessible and appealing choice for both domestic and international visitors.
