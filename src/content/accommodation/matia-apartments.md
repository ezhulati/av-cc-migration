The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, including two bedrooms, a living room adorned with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony that presents a picturesque view of the garden. The kitchen comes fully equipped with modern amenities such as an oven and a toaster, ensuring that guests have everything they need at their fingertips. The inclusion of a private bathroom, complete with a bath, towels, and bed linen, adds a touch of home-like comfort. Designed with privacy and peace in mind, the apartment features a private entrance and soundproofing, creating a personal oasis for guests.

For those looking to enjoy a leisurely cup of coffee or needing to pick up some essentials, a coffee shop and a minimarket are conveniently located on the premises.

Explorers eager to soak in the local culture will find Saranda City Beach less than 1 km away, with the charming La Petite Beach just a bit further at 1.8 km. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from Matia Apartments.

In summary, Matia Apartments stands out as a stellar accommodation choice, offering spacious and serene living spaces complemented by thoughtful amenities, all set against the backdrop of Sarandë's vibrant coastal landscape.
