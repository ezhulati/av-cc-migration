Guests are treated to a seamless blend of convenience and luxury, with each accommodation unit boasting a balcony or, in some cases, a terrace or patio that presents breathtaking mountain or city panoramas. The interiors are thoughtfully designed, featuring a fully equipped kitchenette, a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, complete with bidets and hair dryers, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. Moreover, the provision of bed linen and towels in each unit underscores the aparthotel's commitment to guest comfort.

Beyond the confines of these well-appointed suites, FAMILY SUITE RESIDENCE caters to the needs of its guests with a range of facilities. A convenient minimarket on-site addresses any last-minute needs or snacks, while the availability of a baby safety gate underscores the property's family-friendly ethos. For those looking to explore the beauty of Vlorë, sightseeing tours can be easily arranged nearby.

Situated at a comfortable distance from notable attractions such as Vjetër Beach and Kuzum Baba, and with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport 153 km away, the aparthotel also offers a paid airport shuttle service, ensuring smooth transitions to and from the property.

FAMILY SUITE RESIDENCE is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the charm and beauty of Vlorë, offering a perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and scenic beauty, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay.
