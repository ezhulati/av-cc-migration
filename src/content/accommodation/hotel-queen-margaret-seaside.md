Guests are welcomed into a world of elegance and comfort, with each air-conditioned room boasting free WiFi, a private bathroom, and thoughtful touches such as a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kettle, and complimentary toiletries. The attention to detail extends to the provision of bed linen and towels in all rooms, ensuring a comfortable stay. Select accommodations offer the added luxury of sea views and a patio, inviting guests to unwind in an idyllic setting.

The hotel takes pride in its culinary offerings, with an à la carte breakfast served each morning to kickstart the day. The shared lounge, terrace, and on-site restaurant provide ample spaces for relaxation and socializing, while the bar serves as a cozy retreat for evening drinks.

For those looking to explore beyond the hotel's comforts, a range of activities awaits. From hiking and fishing to snorkeling, the surrounding area offers abundant opportunities for adventure and leisure, especially along the beachfront. The convenience of airport transfers and a car rental service further enhances the guest experience, making exploration effortless.

Situated within easy reach of notable attractions such as Coco Beach and Ksamil Beach 7, Hotel Queen Margaret Seaside stands as a gateway to the beauty of Ksamil. With Corfu International Airport just 93 km away, the hotel remains accessible for international travelers, promising a stay that combines the allure of Ksamil's beaches with the unmatched hospitality of Hotel Queen Margaret Seaside.
