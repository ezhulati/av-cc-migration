A mere 700 meters from the iconic Skanderbeg Square, guests at Signature Apartment 5 enjoy the luxury of private parking and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a seamless stay from start to finish. The property is equipped with modern amenities including a lift and a shared kitchen, catering to the needs of every guest.

The apartment itself is a testament to thoughtful design, offering a balcony that presents serene garden views. Inside, the space is generously proportioned, featuring two bedrooms, a cozy living room with a satellite flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, and two bathrooms complete with a walk-in shower and bidet. The addition of a private entrance and soundproofing guarantees a tranquil retreat for guests.

Convenience is further enhanced by an on-site minimarket, while families will appreciate the outdoor play equipment designed for children. Signature Apartment 5 is also within easy reach of the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana, placing guests at the center of Tirana's vibrant cultural scene.

For those arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, with the apartment offering a paid shuttle service for effortless transfers. Signature Apartment 5 stands out as a premier choice for travelers who value both the comforts of home and the excitement of exploring Albania's capital.
