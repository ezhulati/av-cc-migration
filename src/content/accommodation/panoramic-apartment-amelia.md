Inside, the apartment unfolds into a spacious retreat featuring two bedrooms, a cozy living room equipped with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen boasting an oven and microwave. The presence of two bathrooms, each with a shower, ensures privacy and comfort for all guests. Adding to the convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, ensuring a seamless stay.

For those who enjoy dining under the open sky, the property boasts an inviting outdoor dining area. Additionally, an on-site bar offers a spot for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.

Located in a region celebrated for its outdoor activities, Panoramic Apartment Amelia is perfectly positioned for guests interested in fishing, hiking, or simply exploring the beauty of Sarandë. Car hire services available at the property further facilitate adventures in the surrounding area.

Notably, the apartment is within easy reach of popular attractions such as Saranda City Beach, La Petite Beach, and Maestral Beach, making it an ideal base for beach lovers. For those traveling further afield, Ioannina Airport is 96 km away, connecting guests to broader destinations.

Panoramic Apartment Amelia stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering a blend of comfort, convenience, and captivating views, making it a must-visit for those planning a Sarandë getaway.
