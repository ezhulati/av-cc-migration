Guests are treated to a selection of air-conditioned accommodations, each boasting a range of amenities designed to ensure a stay that is both relaxing and convenient. From the comfort of a private balcony or patio, one can enjoy breathtaking mountain views or dine al fresco in the outdoor dining area. The units come fully equipped with modern necessities such as a desk, coffee machine, dishwasher, fridge, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a shower. To further enhance the guest experience, each unit is supplied with fresh bed linen and towels.

For those who prefer to cater to their own culinary needs, the apartments feature fully-equipped kitchens, complemented by private dining areas. Additionally, the complex houses a coffee shop for those morning or afternoon caffeine fixes.

VILA SHEHAJ Luxury doesn't just stop at luxurious accommodations; it also offers a range of activities to enrich the stay of its guests. With complimentary access to bicycles and car hire services, exploring the surrounding area, known for its cycling-friendly routes, has never been easier. The property also boasts a tennis court for sports enthusiasts and a picnic area perfect for enjoying the great outdoors.

Located a mere 3 km from Ri Beach and 1.5 km from the historic Kuzum Baba, the complex is ideally positioned for guests wishing to explore the local attractions. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, with the convenience of a paid airport shuttle service offered by the property.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest satisfaction, VILA SHEHAJ Luxury is the go-to choice for discerning travelers visiting Vlorë.
