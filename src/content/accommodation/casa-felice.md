The apartment itself is a spacious haven, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms, a living area equipped with a flat-screen TV and streaming services for entertainment, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with modern appliances such as a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, microwave, and fridge. The outdoor dining area invites guests to savor their meals against the backdrop of the sea, adding a touch of magic to every dining experience. The added convenience of a private entrance ensures that privacy is paramount.

For those who wish to explore the local area, Casa Felice provides an excellent base. The property facilitates a range of activities, from hiking and walking tours to cycling and fishing, with both bicycle and car rental services readily available for guests. The presence of a minimarket on-site further adds to the convenience, ensuring essentials are always within reach.

Located within proximity to notable attractions such as the Beach at Government Villas and Liro Beach, Casa Felice positions itself as an ideal spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and activities of Vlorë. With the nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 154 km away, the journey to this coastal retreat is a worthwhile venture for both local and international travelers.

In summary, Casa Felice stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice, offering a perfect blend of location, amenities, and privacy, making it a highly recommended stay for anyone visiting Vlorë.
