Guests at Lake Drive Rooms&Apartments are treated to an array of amenities designed to enhance their stay. The establishment boasts a shared lounge perfect for relaxation, alongside the practicality of a shared kitchen. Security is prioritized with full-day monitoring, ensuring a worry-free experience. Connectivity is seamless, with complimentary WiFi accessible throughout the premises, and for those arriving by car, private parking is available for a nominal fee.

The highlight of the property is undoubtedly its spacious apartment. Featuring a balcony that offers tranquil lake views, the apartment is well-appointed with three bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped, including a dishwasher, catering to all culinary needs. Two bathrooms, with either a bath or shower, add to the convenience, ensuring a comfortable stay. Notably, the accommodation includes a private entrance, offering guests an added layer of privacy.

Located a mere 2.5 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and 44 km from the natural beauty of Rock of Kavaje, Lake Drive Rooms&Apartments is ideally positioned for guests eager to explore the local sights. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 16 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

Lake Drive Rooms&Apartments presents a compelling option for those in search of a blend of modern amenities, comfort, and strategic location in Tirana.
