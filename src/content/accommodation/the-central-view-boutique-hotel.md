Accommodations at The Central View Boutique Hotel are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with essential amenities such as a desk for those needing to stay connected, a kettle and fridge for in-room refreshments, a safety deposit box for valuables, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The private bathrooms, complete with showers, ensure a refreshing start or end to the day, while the inclusion of bed linen and towels adds a touch of home.

Morning at the hotel begins on a high note with a choice between a buffet or continental breakfast, offering guests the perfect fuel for their adventures ahead.

For those looking to venture beyond the hotel's comforts, Vlore Beach is just a kilometer away, offering a picturesque setting for relaxation or leisure. Further afield, Ri Beach, located 2.5 km from the hotel, provides an alternative spot for sun-seekers. For international travelers, the convenience of Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 150 km away, ensures a hassle-free journey to and from this exquisite retreat.

The Central View Boutique Hotel not only serves as a gateway to the enchanting sights and sounds of Vlorë but also as a sanctuary of comfort and luxury, making it an ideal choice for discerning travelers.
