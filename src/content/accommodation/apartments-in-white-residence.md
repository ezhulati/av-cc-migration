The apartment itself is a testament to modern comfort and elegance, featuring a spacious layout that includes a bedroom, a living room, and a balcony overlooking the sea. It is fully equipped with a flat-screen TV, a kitchen that includes a microwave and toaster, and a bathroom with a walk-in shower. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily available, ensuring a comfortable stay.

One of the unique aspects of Apartments in White Residence is the opportunity for guests to enjoy outdoor dining, allowing them to savor their meals against the backdrop of Sarandë's stunning coastal scenery. Moreover, the location is perfect for those looking to immerse themselves in local activities, with fishing being a popular pastime in the area.

The property is also conveniently situated near several of Sarandë's most sought-after beaches, including Flamingo, Mango, and Maestral Beach, making it an ideal base for beach lovers. For those traveling from afar, Ioannina Airport is the nearest, located 97 km away, ensuring easy access for international guests.

In summary, Apartments in White Residence is a top choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort, convenience, and the beauty of Sarandë's coastal landscape.
