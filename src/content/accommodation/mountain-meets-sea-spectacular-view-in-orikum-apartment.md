The accommodations are thoughtfully designed with families in mind, featuring air-conditioned rooms that promise comfort in every corner. Guests are treated to a plethora of amenities including a terrace that boasts stunning sea views, a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment needs. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, ensures that home-cooked meals are never off the menu, while the private bathroom with a bidet and hairdryer adds an extra layer of convenience. The cherry on top is the balcony, available in all units, offering outdoor furniture set against the backdrop of captivating city views.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of bed linen and towels in each unit, ensuring a hassle-free stay. For those looking to venture beyond, Independence Square awaits at a distance of 19 km, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history.

Situated 168 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Mountain Meets Sea-Spectacular View in Orikum Apartment stands out as an accessible retreat that promises a memorable stay for those looking to enjoy the best of both worlds: the peaceful embrace of the mountains and the refreshing touch of the sea.
