The accommodations at Nino's Rooms are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a cool respite from the warm Mediterranean climate. The presence of a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom, complete with a bidet and hair dryer, adds a touch of home. Select units boast enchanting garden views, and the option of a balcony provides a private outdoor space to enjoy the serene surroundings. Practicality is not overlooked, as every room includes a wardrobe for personal storage.

The guest house is not just about comfortable lodging; it is enveloped in a lush garden, offering a tranquil escape for those seeking peace and relaxation. For adventurers eager to explore the rich history of the area, Butrint National Park is merely 2.5 km away, promising a journey through time with its ancient ruins.

Situated 92 km from Corfu International Airport, Nino's Rooms presents itself as a gateway to the enchanting beauty of Sarandë, making it a coveted choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in the picturesque Albanian Riviera.
