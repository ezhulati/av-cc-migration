Upon stepping into Hotel MIK, guests are greeted with a meticulously maintained garden that sets the tone for a tranquil retreat. The on-site restaurant and bar further elevate the experience, promising delightful culinary adventures with every meal.

The guest rooms at Hotel MIK are thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs. Each room is equipped with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, and air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing stay. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which are furnished with a shower, complimentary toiletries, and slippers, adding a touch of luxury to the daily routine.

For those looking to explore the surrounding area, the hotel's reception is a treasure trove of information, offering invaluable guidance to help guests navigate the local attractions with ease.

Situated 71 km from Kastoria National Airport, Hotel MIK stands as a prime choice for travelers seeking a blend of tranquility, convenience, and attentive service in the heart of Korçë.
