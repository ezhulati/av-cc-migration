Guests are treated to breathtaking city views from each room, complemented by the modern comforts of free WiFi, ensuring connectivity is never an issue. The hotel prides itself on being an allergy-free environment, making it a safe haven for all guests. For those looking to explore, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car is located just 4.7 km away, offering an adventure not to be missed.

The accommodations at StarLight Hotel are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Each room is equipped with a desk, a private bathroom featuring a shower and hairdryer, a flat-screen TV, and air conditioning for those warm Tirana days. Select rooms also boast a balcony, offering a private outdoor space to relax and take in the city's vibrant atmosphere. For added peace of mind, all rooms include a safety deposit box.

The hotel's multilingual staff, proficient in Greek, English, Italian, and Albanian, are available around the clock at the 24-hour front desk, ready to assist with any inquiries or recommendations to enhance your stay.

For those keen to explore Tirana's rich cultural landscape, StarLight Hotel is conveniently located near several key attractions, including the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the National Museum of History Albania. Additionally, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, making arrivals and departures a breeze.

StarLight Hotel stands out as a premier destination for those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Tirana, offering a perfect blend of location, amenities, and service.
