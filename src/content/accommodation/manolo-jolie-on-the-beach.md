Guests are treated to the comfort of air-conditioned rooms, each boasting a private bathroom and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a stay that blends convenience with tranquility. The presence of a terrace adds to the allure, offering a perfect spot for guests to unwind while soaking in the scenic surroundings.

The accommodations at Manolo & Jolie On The Beach are thoughtfully designed, featuring balconies that present breathtaking sea views. Each room is a cozy enclave, equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, catering to the entertainment needs of modern travelers.

Beyond the comfort of their rooms, guests have access to attentive services including room service, a 24-hour front desk, and luggage storage, ensuring a hassle-free experience from arrival to departure.

Strategically located, the hotel is a mere 400 meters from Maracit Beach and less than 1 km from Prinos Beach, making it an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. For those looking to explore beyond the local area, Ioannina Airport is 143 km away, connecting travelers to wider adventures.

Manolo & Jolie On The Beach stands out as a delightful choice for those seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and scenic beauty in Himare.
