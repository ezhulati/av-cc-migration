This air-conditioned apartment is a haven of space and comfort, boasting four well-appointed bedrooms complemented by two modern bathrooms equipped with a bidet and a shower. While the apartment ensures a self-sufficient stay with its comprehensive amenities, it also offers the option of providing towels and bed linen for those looking for an extra layer of convenience.

For guests looking to explore Tirana's picturesque surroundings, SUN TOWER-CENTER of TIRANA is ideally situated, just 6 km from the scenic Dajti Express and a mere 1 km from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha. The property also caters to the needs of sports and art enthusiasts with the National Sport Park and the National Arts Gallery located within a 300 m and 650 m radius, respectively.

Adding to the appeal, SUN TOWER-CENTER of TIRANA boasts a terrace that invites guests to unwind and soak in the city's ambiance. For those eager to venture further, the property facilitates exploration with both bicycle and car rental services, ensuring a seamless experience for discovering Tirana's charms.

Convenience is further underscored by the property's proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated 14.4 km away, with a paid airport shuttle service available to ease travel worries.

In summary, SUN TOWER-CENTER of TIRANA stands as a testament to thoughtful accommodation that prioritizes guest comfort and accessibility, making it an excellent choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the cultural tapestry of Tirana.
