The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience, while the private entrance and round-the-clock security ensure guests' privacy and peace of mind.

For those looking to explore the surroundings or simply enjoy the seaside, Sunset House does not disappoint. The property offers a bicycle rental service and exclusive access to a private beach area, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the local landscape. Situated within proximity to popular attractions like Liro Beach, Beach at Government Villas, and Coco Bongo Beach, the location is ideal for those eager to discover Vlorë's coastal charm.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport located 156 km away, ensuring that guests can easily reach this haven of relaxation and comfort. Whether it's the allure of the beachfront setting, the comprehensive amenities, or the prime location near sought-after points of interest, Sunset House stands as a distinguished destination for discerning travelers.
