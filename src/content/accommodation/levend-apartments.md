The establishment prides itself on its array of accommodations, each thoughtfully designed to cater to the diverse needs of its guests. Select units boast balconies and cozy seating areas, complete with flat-screen TVs, ensuring a comfortable stay whether one is seeking a leisurely retreat or a cozy nook. The presence of air conditioning and heating in every room guarantees comfort in any season, while allergy-free and soundproof options underscore Levend Apartments' commitment to guest satisfaction and well-being.

For those eager to explore the local area, the property is ideally located within walking distance of not only Ksamil Beach but also the vibrant Coco Beach, offering a perfect base from which to enjoy the area's renowned crystal-clear waters. The allure of adventure beckons with Bora Bora Beach and the historic Butrint National Park, both less than 3.1 km away, promising a blend of relaxation and exploration.

Accessibility is seamlessly addressed, with Corfu International Airport situated 93 km from the apartments, making Levend Apartments an attractive choice for both domestic and international travelers. The addition of a 24-hour front desk ensures that guests' needs are promptly met at any hour, adding an extra layer of convenience to an already delightful stay.

Outdoor seating areas invite guests to unwind and soak in the pleasant weather, making Levend Apartments not just a place to stay, but a destination to experience.
