The accommodations at Martin Ferraj Riviera are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each room features a terrace that presents breathtaking mountain views, setting the stage for a tranquil retreat. The interiors are thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV, a cozy seating area, a well-appointed kitchen, and a private bathroom complete with a bidet, hairdryer, and complimentary toiletries. For those seeking a picturesque backdrop, select units offer captivating river views, and the added luxury of a balcony. Moreover, every unit is furnished with a desk, catering to the needs of business travelers or guests who wish to stay connected.

Situated conveniently close to Ri Beach, merely 2.6 km away, and Independence Square at 2 km, guests at Martin Ferraj Riviera are perfectly positioned to explore the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of Vlorë. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 150 km away, making the property an accessible choice for both domestic and international visitors.

Martin Ferraj Riviera emerges as a compelling choice for families and solo travelers alike, offering a harmonious blend of city convenience and serene landscapes. Its thoughtful amenities and strategic location make it a standout option for anyone looking to experience the best of Vlorë.
