This apartment doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home. Guests are treated to a spacious layout that includes two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a balcony that presents captivating city views. The inclusion of modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen with an oven and a fridge, and a bathroom equipped with either a bath or shower, ensures a stay that combines comfort with convenience. To further enhance the experience, towels and bed linen are thoughtfully provided, ensuring guests can travel lighter.

Connectivity is never an issue, as free WiFi is accessible throughout the property, allowing guests to stay connected with the world or plan their next day's adventures. For those arriving from afar, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is located 97 km away, making Apartament Piro a reachable destination for international and domestic travelers alike.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and the promise of a memorable stay, Apartament Piro is more than just an accommodation; it's a gateway to the delights of Sarandë.
