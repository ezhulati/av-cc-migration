The property is distinguished by its family-friendly rooms, each boasting a private entrance for that extra touch of exclusivity. Guests can expect well-appointed accommodations equipped with desks, wardrobes, and in some cases, a cable flat-screen TV. For those who prefer to cater to their culinary needs, select units feature a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven. The convenience of a private bathroom, outfitted with a walk-in shower and hairdryer, adds to the comfort of the stay.

Social and relaxation spaces at Vila Anxhelo are designed to enhance the guest experience. The on-site bar serves as a cozy spot for guests to unwind, while the property's barbecue and garden invite outdoor enjoyment, weather permitting.

Situated a mere 3 km from the historic Independence Square and 3.8 km from the iconic Kuzum Baba, Vila Anxhelo offers an ideal base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry of Vlorë. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 151 km away, making it a manageable distance for both domestic and international travelers.

Vila Anxhelo, with its thoughtful amenities and prime location, promises a memorable stay for those visiting the picturesque city of Vlorë.
