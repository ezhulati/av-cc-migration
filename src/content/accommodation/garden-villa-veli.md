Boasting a lush garden setting, Garden Villa Veli provides a tranquil retreat complete with modern amenities. Each apartment is air-conditioned, offering a private balcony for those moments of quiet reflection or intimate conversations under the stars. Complimentary private parking and WiFi throughout the property add to the seamless experience, ensuring guests stay connected and stress-free.

Accommodations at Garden Villa Veli are thoughtfully designed with guests' comfort in mind. The living spaces feature flat-screen TVs for entertainment, while the private bathrooms come equipped with both bath and shower options, alongside complimentary toiletries. The kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, allows guests the flexibility to prepare meals as they wish, complemented by the provision of fresh bed linen and towels for a home-away-from-home feel.

Morning delights await as a continental breakfast offering a variety of options including pancakes, fruits, and juice is served, promising a delightful start to the day. The on-site coffee shop and minimarket provide convenient options for snacks and essentials, ensuring every need is met without having to venture far.

For those looking to explore, Bora Bora Beach is a mere 800 meters away, with Ksamil Beach 7 also within close proximity. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport is 92 km away, making Garden Villa Veli an accessible haven for both local and overseas visitors.

In essence, Garden Villa Veli stands out as a premier choice for those seeking a peaceful getaway with the comforts of modern living, all within the beautiful setting of Ksamil's coastal landscape.
