This beachfront gem offers guests the luxury of stepping out onto their private balcony to breathe in the fresh sea air, all from the comfort of a non-smoking environment. Inside, the apartment is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air conditioning for those warm Mediterranean days. Guests will find themselves at home with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with an oven and fridge. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

Connectivity is never an issue, as guests can enjoy the benefits of complimentary WiFi, keeping them connected with the world or planning their next day's adventure in Vlorë. For those traveling from afar, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is located 149 km away, making this apartment a reachable retreat for international and domestic travelers alike.

This apartment not only offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience but also serves as a gateway to the enchanting city of Vlorë, promising an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
