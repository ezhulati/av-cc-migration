The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting two well-appointed bedrooms and a living area that opens up to a breezy balcony. Modern amenities are not spared, with a flat-screen TV equipped with cable channels, a fully equipped kitchen that includes a microwave and toaster, and the convenience of a washing machine. The inclusion of air conditioning ensures a comfortable stay regardless of the weather outside. The bathroom, complete with a bidet, adds an extra layer of comfort. Guests are treated to serene garden views, a tranquil backdrop for a relaxing getaway.

For those who enjoy outdoor dining, the property's barbecue facilities are a delightful bonus, perfect for making the most of the warm Mediterranean climate.

While the allure of the beach is undeniable, guests also have the opportunity to explore further afield. The historic Skanderbeg Square lies 47 km away, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of Albania. Closer to home, the Rock of Kavaje, just 6 km from the apartment, presents an interesting excursion. For ease of travel, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 42 km away, making Ap Relax, Golem - Mali Robit a compelling choice for both domestic and international travelers looking for a serene beachfront retreat.
