Connectivity is never an issue at Koko Hotel, thanks to the provision of free WiFi throughout the premises. The hotel prides itself on its attentive service, offering both room service and a 24-hour front desk to cater to guests' needs at any hour. Families will find a warm welcome here, with spacious family rooms available to accommodate groups of all sizes.

Morning at Koko Hotel begins on a high note with a choice of breakfast options, including a hearty American breakfast or a diverse buffet, setting guests up perfectly for a day of exploration or relaxation.

The hotel's enviable location places it within easy reach of the area's most sought-after beaches. Bora Bora Beach is a mere 300 meters away, while Ksamil Beach 7 is just a short 400-meter stroll, offering guests a variety of coastal experiences. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is 93 km away, making Koko Hotel an accessible choice for international travelers seeking a serene getaway in Ksamil.
