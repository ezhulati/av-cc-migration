The property boasts a selection of units, each designed with the guest's comfort in mind. Air conditioning, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels are standard in every room, ensuring a relaxing stay. For added convenience, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, bathrobes, and slippers are provided. Select units elevate the experience further with a well-appointed kitchen, complete with an oven, a minibar, and kitchenware, making them ideal for those who prefer a home-cooked meal even while traveling.

Beyond the confines of their rooms, guests are invited to explore the outdoor amenities that Vila Balani has to offer. An outdoor fireplace and a picnic area provide perfect settings for a leisurely day spent in the company of friends or family. The terrace offers a serene spot to unwind while taking in the breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and gardens.

For those looking to immerse themselves in the local landscape, the villa is conveniently located near popular attractions such as Coco Beach, Bora Bora Beach, and Ksamil Beach 7. Adventure enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to cycling and fishing opportunities, allowing for an active engagement with the natural beauty of the area.

Accessibility is also a highlight, with Corfu International Airport situated 93 km away, making Vila Balani an accessible retreat for international travelers.

In summary, Vila Balani presents a compelling proposition for those in search of a tranquil yet engaging getaway in Ksamil. With its thoughtful amenities, strategic location, and the promise of a memorable stay, it stands as a beacon for discerning travelers.
