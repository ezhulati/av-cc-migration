The space itself is thoughtfully designed, featuring a bedroom that promises restful nights, a fully equipped kitchen for those moments when dining in feels right, and not one, but two bathrooms, adding an extra layer of convenience for guests. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, making downtime enjoyable.

Strategically located, Gerald Ferhati places guests within easy reach of Tirana's most sought-after attractions. The Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, offering breathtaking views from above, is just a short drive away, while the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is within a 2.6 km radius. For those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture, Skanderbeg Square, the House of Leaves, and Rinia Park are nearby, promising enriching experiences.

Accessibility is further enhanced by the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, situated just 13 km away, making Gerald Ferhati an ideal base for both exploring the vibrant city of Tirana and ensuring smooth arrivals and departures.
