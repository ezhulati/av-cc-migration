Accommodations at Guest House Ani are designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each room opens to a serene patio, providing a tranquil space to unwind. The interiors are equipped with air conditioning, ensuring a comfortable environment regardless of the weather outside. Entertainment and connectivity are guaranteed with the presence of a flat-screen TV in every room. The private bathrooms are a highlight, featuring walk-in showers and slippers for that extra touch of home. For convenience, all units are supplied with fresh bed linen and towels.

The on-site coffee shop presents an inviting atmosphere for guests to enjoy a leisurely drink or a quick caffeine fix, adding to the guest house's appeal.

For those looking to explore the surroundings, Zaravina Lake offers a picturesque escape, located just 45 km away. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 83 km from the property, makes Guest House Ani an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers seeking a blend of historic charm and modern amenities.
