Guests are treated to accommodations that open up to tranquil views of the garden and the city beyond, from either a terrace or balcony. Each unit is thoughtfully equipped with air conditioning, a cozy seating area, a satellite flat-screen TV, and a fully functional kitchen. The addition of a patio with an outdoor dining area invites guests to savor their meals amidst nature. For added peace of mind, each unit also includes a safety deposit box.

The presence of a quaint coffee shop on-site, along with a handy minimarket, ensures that guests' needs are met without having to venture far. For those eager to explore the surrounding area, The Garden Guest House facilitates sightseeing tours, and offers both bicycle and car rental services, making it easy for guests to engage in nearby activities such as cycling and fishing.

Situated 78 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the property also provides a convenient paid airport shuttle service, ensuring a hassle-free travel experience for guests. The Garden Guest House stands as a testament to the perfect blend of leisure and adventure, promising an unforgettable stay for all who visit.
