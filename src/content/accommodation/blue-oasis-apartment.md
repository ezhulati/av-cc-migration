The apartment is thoughtfully designed to cater to the modern traveler's needs, featuring a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The inclusion of a bathroom with a bidet, along with complimentary towels and bed linen, adds a touch of home-like comfort. Moreover, the commitment to a clean, allergy-free, and non-smoking environment underscores the property's dedication to guest well-being.

For those requiring a bit more convenience, the apartment offers a lift, ensuring easy access to all its levels, along with private check-in and check-out services, streamlining the arrival and departure process. Guests are also invited to unwind in the shared lounge area, a perfect spot for meeting fellow travelers or simply relaxing after a day of exploration.

Located 148 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Blue Oasis Apartment presents an ideal base for both short-term and extended stays in Vlorë, promising a blend of comfort, convenience, and serene views.
