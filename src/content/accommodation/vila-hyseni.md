Guests at Vila Hyseni are treated to accommodations that boast not only garden and inner courtyard views but also a range of amenities designed for comfort and convenience. Select rooms feature private entrances, ensuring a sense of exclusivity and privacy. Each is thoughtfully equipped with a desk and wardrobe, catering to both leisure and business travelers. For those seeking a touch of luxury, options include terraces and seating areas complemented by flat-screen TVs, along with the essential air conditioning and heating for year-round comfort. Notably, the guest house offers allergy-free and soundproof units, underscoring its commitment to guest satisfaction.

The presence of an on-site bar adds a layer of convenience, allowing guests to unwind with a drink without having to venture far. For those inclined to explore, the Rock of Kavaje is a mere 3.8 km away, while the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is accessible at 40 km, offering a glimpse into the country's intriguing past.

Situated 34 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Vila Hyseni stands out as a compelling choice for travelers seeking a blend of tranquility, convenience, and a touch of local flavor. Its proximity to Shkëmbi i Kavajës Beach, just 2.5 km away, further cements its status as a prime location for those looking to experience the best of Durrës.
