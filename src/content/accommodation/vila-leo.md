Guests at Vila Leo are treated to the comfort of air-conditioned accommodations, featuring a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a fridge, invites guests to feel at home, while the inclusion of a cable flat-screen TV ensures entertainment is always at hand. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of convenience to the stay, emphasizing the aparthotel's commitment to guest comfort. Moreover, the property prides itself on being non-smoking and soundproof, ensuring a peaceful retreat for all guests.

The allure of Golem Beach lies just 1.7 km away, offering a perfect spot for sun-seekers and beach lovers, while the historic Skanderbeg Square is accessible for those looking to immerse themselves in the local culture. For travelers, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, located 42 km from Vila Leo, means ease of access and a smooth transition to and from this haven of tranquility.

Vila Leo stands out as a distinguished choice for those in pursuit of a serene getaway, coupled with the convenience of modern amenities and the charm of local attractions.
