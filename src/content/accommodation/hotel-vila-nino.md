Guests at Hotel Vila Nino are treated to rooms that are not only equipped with air conditioning for those warm Berat days but also feature a desk, a balcony overlooking the serene garden, and a private bathroom. For added comfort, each room is furnished with a flat-screen TV, crisp bed linen, and fluffy towels, with the convenience of a fridge also provided.

Located 119 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Hotel Vila Nino simplifies travel arrangements by offering a paid airport shuttle service, making the journey as smooth as possible for guests. Additionally, the hotel prides itself on exceptional guest services, including room service, luggage storage, and currency exchange, ensuring a hassle-free experience from arrival to departure.

Hotel Vila Nino is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the charming city of Berat, promising an unforgettable experience with its thoughtful amenities and attentive service.
