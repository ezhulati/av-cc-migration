This non-smoking accommodation doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers a home away from home. Guests are greeted with a spacious layout that includes a balcony, two cozy bedrooms, a welcoming living room, and a well-equipped kitchen. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV adds a touch of modern comfort, perfect for those moments of relaxation. Moreover, visitors are treated to serene garden views, providing a tranquil backdrop to their stay.

Deluxe Rent Apartment is conveniently located near several of Tirana's must-visit attractions. The National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, National Museum of History Albania, and Toptani Shopping Centre are all within easy reach, making it an ideal base for cultural enthusiasts and shoppers alike. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is just 14 km away, ensuring smooth travel plans to and from the city.

In summary, Deluxe Rent Apartment offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and accessibility, making it a top recommendation for those looking to explore the best of Tirana.
