The apartments themselves are a haven of tranquility, boasting air-conditioned spaces complete with a balcony that opens up to either a lush garden or a quaint inner courtyard view. Each unit is thoughtfully furnished with a fully equipped kitchen, featuring modern appliances such as an oven and a fridge, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The inclusion of a bathroom equipped with a shower, hair dryer, and washing machine, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds a layer of convenience to the experience.

For those who appreciate the quieter moments, the property's garden offers a peaceful escape, inviting guests to unwind amidst nature. Despite its tranquil setting, Mali i Robit Apartments remains well-connected, with the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, House of Leaves, and Rinia Park all within a 48 km radius. Additionally, the proximity to Podgorica Airport, located 70 km away, ensures ease of travel for international guests.

In essence, Mali i Robit Apartments stands out as an ideal choice for travelers looking for a blend of historical exploration and modern comfort, all wrapped up in a package of serene hospitality.
