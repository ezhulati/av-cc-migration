This accommodation doesn't just offer a place to stay; it provides a home away from home with its array of amenities. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free WiFi, a television for entertainment, and a washing machine, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. The kitchen comes fully equipped with a dishwasher and oven, perfect for those who prefer to prepare their own meals. Moreover, the provision of towels and bed linen adds an extra layer of comfort to the experience.

The apartment's balcony offers tranquil garden views, allowing guests to unwind in a peaceful setting. For those looking to explore the area, the property facilitates adventure with its car rental service and airport transfers, ensuring a seamless travel experience.

Beachfront Sion Sarande Apartment 2 is not just about its impressive interior but also its proximity to local attractions. Flamingo Beach, Mango Beach, and Santa Quaranta Beach are popular spots nearby, inviting guests to explore the natural beauty of Sarandë. For travelers flying in, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km away, making the apartment an accessible choice for international and domestic visitors alike.

In summary, Beachfront Sion Sarande Apartment 2 stands out as a top-tier accommodation for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and culture of Sarandë, offering both comfort and convenience in one of the most picturesque regions of Vlorë County.
