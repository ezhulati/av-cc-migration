Each air-conditioned apartment at Hotel Kaci is thoughtfully designed to provide a comfortable and private retreat. Guests will find a separate bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen for those home-cooked meals, and a modern bathroom. Entertainment needs are met with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring a relaxing stay.

For travelers flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 84 km away, making Hotel Kaci an accessible choice for both domestic and international guests seeking a peaceful escape by the Albanian coast.
