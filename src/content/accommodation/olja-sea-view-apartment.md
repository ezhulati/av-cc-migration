The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting air-conditioned interiors that open up to a balcony offering breathtaking sea views. Designed to accommodate the modern traveler, the apartment features a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The living area, complete with a flat-screen TV and cozy seating, invites guests to relax after a day of exploration. The convenience of a walk-in shower in the bathroom, along with the provision of towels and bed linen, adds to the thoughtful amenities that make Olja Sea View Apartment feel like home.

For those arriving by air, the property is accessible from Ioannina Airport, located 96 km away, with a paid airport shuttle service available to ensure a seamless travel experience. This combination of strategic location, well-appointed accommodations, and attentive services makes Olja Sea View Apartment a standout option for visitors to Sarandë, promising a memorable stay that blends the comforts of home with the excitement of travel.
