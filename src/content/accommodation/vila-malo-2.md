Each apartment at Vila Malo 2 is designed with privacy in mind, featuring a separate entrance to enhance the experience of solitude and peace. The interiors are thoughtfully appointed with modern comforts such as air conditioning, complimentary private parking, and high-speed WiFi, catering to the needs of the contemporary traveler.

Guests are treated to a suite of in-room amenities, including a terrace that offers breathtaking pool vistas, a cozy seating area, and a satellite flat-screen TV for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven and fridge, allows guests the freedom to prepare their meals, while the private bathroom boasts a bidet and hair dryer for added convenience. Each unit also features a balcony, providing an outdoor dining area where guests can enjoy meals against the backdrop of city views. Essential items like bed linen and towels are thoughtfully provided in all units.

Families will appreciate the inclusion of a kids' pool, ensuring that younger guests have their share of fun under the sun.

Situated merely 800 meters from the idyllic Paradise Beach and a stone's throw from Lori Beach, Vila Malo 2 offers an unparalleled location for beach enthusiasts. For those looking to explore beyond, Corfu International Airport is located 92 km away, making it a manageable journey for international travelers.

Vila Malo 2 stands as a testament to the perfect blend of location, amenities, and privacy, promising an unforgettable stay for those who choose to visit Ksamil.
