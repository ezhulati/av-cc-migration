This accommodation doesn't just promise a place to stay; it offers an experience. With garden views that calm the soul, a terrace for those moments of reflection, and a bar to unwind, guests are treated to an array of amenities designed for relaxation and enjoyment. The commitment to a non-smoking environment further ensures a clean and fresh stay for all guests.

The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring two well-appointed bedrooms and a living space equipped with a flat-screen TV that includes streaming services for endless entertainment. The kitchen is fully equipped with a dishwasher and fridge, catering to those who prefer home-cooked meals. Additionally, the convenience of a washing machine, coupled with the luxury of a bidet in the bathroom, elevates the living experience. Air conditioning, a necessity for the warm, sunny days, ensures comfort throughout the stay.

Not just focusing on the essentials, GardenApartments1 goes above and beyond by providing bed linen, towels, and even a dry cleaning service, ensuring that every need is met with the highest standards of hospitality.

For those looking to explore, Vjetër Beach is a short 2.4 km journey away, offering another scenic spot to soak in the sun. The historical Kuzum Baba is also within reach, located just 3.3 km from the apartment, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history. For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 152 km away, making GardenApartments1 an accessible retreat for both local and international guests seeking a memorable stay by the Albanian coastline.
