The apartment itself is a haven of tranquility and style, featuring a well-appointed bedroom that promises restful nights. The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances, including an oven and a toaster, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. The living area, complete with a TV and cozy seating, is the perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploration. The bathroom, fitted with a bidet, adds an extra touch of convenience.

One of the highlights of this property is undoubtedly the balcony. Offering breathtaking sea views, it is furnished with outdoor seating, inviting guests to relax and soak in the picturesque surroundings.

For those looking to explore further afield, the apartment offers a car rental service, making it easy to venture out and discover the beauty of the surrounding area.

Situated 95 km from Ioannina Airport, Breeze Cuddling Your Holidays is not just a place to stay but a gateway to experiencing the best of Sarandë and its enchanting environs.
