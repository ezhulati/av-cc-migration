Each accommodation unit at Vila "Cerca de Mar" is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring air-conditioning and complimentary WiFi access to ensure guests stay connected. The living spaces are thoughtfully designed with a flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, making it a perfect retreat after a day of adventure. The highlight, however, is the balcony in every unit, presenting an unobstructed view of the sea, inviting guests to unwind and soak in the serene surroundings.

For those who prefer to cater to their culinary needs, a kitchenette equipped with a microwave is at their disposal, adding a touch of home-away-from-home comfort. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, where towels are provided, and a shower is available, ensuring a refreshing start or end to the day.

Convenience is key at Vila "Cerca de Mar," with free private parking making it easy for guests to explore the area at their leisure. Its proximity to Saranda City Centre, just a 15-minute drive away, positions it as an ideal base for guests looking to immerse themselves in the local culture and attractions.

Vila "Cerca de Mar" emerges as a distinguished choice for travelers seeking a blend of relaxation, convenience, and a touch of adventure, making it a memorable stay in the heart of Ksamil.
