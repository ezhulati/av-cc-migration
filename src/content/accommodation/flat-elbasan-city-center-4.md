The apartment itself boasts a generous layout, featuring a well-appointed bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to cook, and a modern bathroom. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures guests can enjoy a touch of home entertainment after a day of exploration.

One of the standout amenities is the terrace, offering a serene spot for guests to unwind, complemented by the convenience of free WiFi throughout the premises. For families traveling with little ones, the provision of a baby safety gate underscores the apartment's commitment to guest safety and comfort.

Adding to the ease of exploration, the property's staff are on hand to arrange shuttle services, ensuring seamless travel to and from the area's must-visit sites. Notably, the apartment is a short journey away from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha and the lush expanses of the Grand Park of Tirana, both located 39 km away, inviting guests to delve into the local history and natural beauty.

For those flying in, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently 52 km from Flat Elbasan City Center 4, making it an accessible retreat for international and domestic travelers alike. This apartment not only promises a comfortable stay but also serves as a perfect base for adventurers eager to discover the enchantments of Elbasan and beyond.
