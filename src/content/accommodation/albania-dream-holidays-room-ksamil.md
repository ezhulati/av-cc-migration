The accommodations boast modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV, while the provision of fresh towels and bed linen adds a touch of home. Beyond the comfort of the rooms, guests are treated to picturesque garden views, adding to the property's tranquil ambiance.

Mornings at Albania Dream Holidays Room Ksamil are greeted with a delightful continental breakfast, setting the tone for a day of exploration or relaxation. The garden invites guests to unwind in its peaceful embrace, offering a perfect nook for leisurely afternoons.

Strategically positioned, the bed and breakfast is a short distance from the vibrant Ksamil Beach, merely 1 km away, and Coco Beach, which is 1.3 km from the property. For those drawn to the allure of ancient history and natural beauty, Butrint National Park is within easy reach, located just 3.7 km away. For international travelers, Corfu International Airport serves as the nearest gateway, situated 94 km from this haven of tranquility.

Albania Dream Holidays Room Ksamil stands out as an idyllic destination for those seeking a blend of relaxation, natural beauty, and convenience, promising an unforgettable experience by the Albanian coast.
