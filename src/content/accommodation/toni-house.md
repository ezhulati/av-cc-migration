This apartment, strictly non-smoking, boasts a layout that includes a single bedroom, a bathroom, and a kitchen that comes fully equipped to cater to all culinary needs. Entertainment is readily available with the inclusion of a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a cozy night in if they choose to.

For those who prefer to dine in or need quick essentials, a supermarket is conveniently located within a short distance, making last-minute shopping hassle-free.

Cultural enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to Independence Square and Kuzum Baba, both located 1.7 km from the apartment, offering a glimpse into the local heritage and history of Vlorë.

For international travelers, the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 150 km from Toni House, making it a manageable distance for both short and extended stays.

Toni House stands out as an ideal accommodation for those looking to explore the enchanting city of Vlorë, offering both the comforts of home and the convenience of a prime location.
