Beyond its enviable location, N HOTEL CITY CENTER BY NOSHI prides itself on delivering exceptional service and amenities. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free private parking, an inviting garden to unwind, and a lively bar to relax after a day of exploration. The hotel's commitment to guest satisfaction is evident through its 24-hour front desk, efficient airport transfer services, attentive room service, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a seamless and worry-free stay.

Accommodations at N HOTEL CITY CENTER BY NOSHI are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a wardrobe, kettle, minibar, and safety deposit box, alongside a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The private bathrooms, complete with showers, are stocked with fresh bed linen and towels, adding a touch of home to the hotel experience.

For those keen to delve into the cultural heart of Tirana, the hotel's proximity to notable landmarks such as the Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, Toptani Shopping Centre, and the National Gallery of Arts Tirana offers an unparalleled opportunity to immerse in the city's vibrant history and contemporary life. With the Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, N HOTEL CITY CENTER BY NOSHI serves as an ideal base for both international visitors and those looking to discover the local charms of Tirana.
