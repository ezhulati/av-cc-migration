The elegantly appointed apartment features a spacious bedroom, a cozy living room, and a balcony that presents breathtaking mountain vistas. Modern conveniences such as air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and private parking enhance the guest experience, ensuring a stay that combines comfort with convenience. The kitchen, equipped with an oven and toaster, invites guests to prepare their meals, while the outdoor patio, furnished for relaxation, offers a picturesque setting to enjoy the lake views.

Notably, Vila Roas 2 Ksamil is a smoke-free environment, emphasizing the property's commitment to providing a clean and healthy stay. The apartment's thoughtful design includes a private entrance, offering an added layer of privacy and exclusivity.

Situated a short distance from the allure of Ksamil Beach and the natural beauty of Butrint National Park, and with Corfu International Airport within reach, Vila Roas 2 Ksamil stands as an ideal base for explorers seeking to immerse themselves in the area's rich cultural and natural heritage.
