Guests at Port Side Sarande are treated to accommodations that boast breathtaking sea views from private balconies, ensuring a stay that is as picturesque as it is relaxing. Each apartment is thoughtfully designed with a terrace, a cozy seating area, and a flat-screen TV, catering to the needs of modern travelers. The inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, microwave, toaster, fridge, and stovetop, adds a touch of home, while the private bathroom with a bidet and complimentary slippers underscores the property's attention to guest comfort.

The convenience of air conditioning and ample wardrobe space in all units further enhances the guest experience, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. For those looking to explore local dining options, a variety of eateries are conveniently located near the property, offering a taste of the local cuisine.

Beyond its immediate comforts, Port Side Sarande serves as an ideal base for exploring the wider area. Maestral Beach is just 2 km away, inviting guests to unwind on its pristine sands, while the historic Butrint National Park, 17 km from the property, offers a fascinating glimpse into the region's rich heritage. For travelers arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the accommodation.

In summary, Port Side Sarande stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those looking to immerse themselves in the beauty and culture of Sarandë, offering a perfect blend of location, comfort, and convenience.
