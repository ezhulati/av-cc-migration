This apartment doesn't just boast an enviable location; it offers a haven of space and luxury. Guests can unwind in a generously sized apartment that features a balcony with breathtaking sea views, two well-appointed bedrooms, a cozy living room, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment. The kitchen comes fully equipped with an oven and microwave, catering to all culinary needs, while two bathrooms with a hot tub add an extra layer of relaxation to the stay. Not to forget, the mountain views that provide a stunning backdrop to this coastal escape.

For dining, the on-site family-friendly restaurant presents a delightful menu of French cuisine, available for both lunch and dinner, ensuring that guests have convenient and delicious dining options right at their doorstep.

Situated a short drive from notable landmarks such as Kuzum Baba and Independence Square, Belleview Apartment 3 serves as an ideal base for exploring the rich history and culture of Vlorë. For those traveling from afar, the property is 152 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport and offers a paid shuttle service for a seamless travel experience.

With the added perks of free WiFi, free private parking, and a strict non-smoking policy, Belleview Apartment 3 stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those visiting Vlorë, whether for leisure or adventure.
