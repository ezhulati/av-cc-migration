The apartment itself is a testament to comfort and convenience, featuring a cozy bedroom, a well-appointed bathroom complete with bed linen and towels, and a living area that includes a flat-screen TV with streaming services for entertainment. The dining area and fully equipped kitchenette allow for home-cooked meals, which can be enjoyed on the patio overlooking the garden. For those with sensitivity concerns, it's noteworthy that the accommodation is allergy-free, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.

Adding to the appeal is an on-site minimarket, eliminating the need for distant errands and providing an extra layer of convenience. For the explorers at heart, the apartment's location serves as a perfect base for engaging in local activities such as snorkeling, cycling, and fishing. The provision of a car rental service upon request further facilitates the discovery of the surrounding beauty and attractions.

Notably, the Welcome Beach Apartment is strategically positioned within reach of notable landmarks, including the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car and the Rock of Kavaje, enhancing the appeal for those keen to explore the region's natural and cultural offerings. For guests arriving by air, the proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, coupled with the option of a paid airport shuttle service, ensures a smooth transition from air to accommodation.

In summary, Welcome Beach Apartment stands out as a meticulously designed retreat that caters to the diverse needs and preferences of modern travelers, making it an excellent choice for those looking to experience the best of Hamallaj and its environs.
