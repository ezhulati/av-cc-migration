Accommodations at Vila A Hotel are thoughtfully designed with guest comfort in mind. Each room is a sanctuary of relaxation, featuring a cozy seating area, a state-of-the-art flat-screen TV, and a safety deposit box for personal valuables. The attention to detail extends to the private bathrooms, which are well-appointed with a shower, complimentary toiletries, and a hairdryer, catering to the needs of the modern traveler. Additionally, a desk in every room provides a convenient space for guests to work or plan their next adventure.

For those arriving by air, the proximity to Kastoria National Airport, located 72 km away, makes Vila A Hotel an accessible gateway to the region's attractions. Whether visiting for leisure or business, guests will find Vila A Hotel to be an impeccable base for exploring the rich cultural tapestry and natural beauty of Korçë and its surroundings.
