Accommodations at Hotel Chris are thoughtfully designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring air-conditioned spaces complemented by cable flat-screen TVs for a touch of modernity. The private bathrooms, equipped with baths and bathrobes, add an extra layer of privacy and luxury. Selected rooms elevate the experience further with terraces that invite guests to unwind in their own secluded area. Ensuring a seamless stay, all units are well-appointed with bed linen and towels, and guests can stay connected with complimentary WiFi access throughout their stay.

The guest house takes pride in its family-friendly restaurant, a culinary haven that delights guests with its array of Greek cuisine options for lunch and dinner. The inviting atmosphere and flavorful dishes make dining here a memorable part of the stay.

For moments of relaxation, the property boasts a well-maintained garden, offering a tranquil escape where guests can immerse themselves in the serene surroundings. Additionally, the convenience of free private parking and the assurance of full-day security underscore the guest house's commitment to guest satisfaction and peace of mind.

Beyond its immediate comforts, Hotel Chris serves as an ideal base for exploring the local area. Lori Beach and the lively Bora Bora Beach are both less than a kilometer away, promising days filled with sun, sea, and sand. For those looking to venture further, Corfu International Airport is 92 km away, making Hotel Chris a practical choice for international travelers.

In summary, Hotel Chris presents a compelling option for those in search of a welcoming and well-positioned accommodation in Ksamil, offering a blend of convenience, comfort, and culinary delights.
