The property is well-equipped to cater to families, featuring spacious family rooms complemented by an outdoor fireplace that adds a touch of warmth to the evening atmosphere. Each apartment is designed with comfort in mind, offering air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with a bidet and hair dryer. The highlight of each unit is undoubtedly the terrace or balcony, providing guests with their own outdoor dining area and serene views of the inner courtyard.

For those planning to explore the surrounding area, Kiri S.Apartaments thoughtfully prepares packed lunches, ensuring that guests have the convenience of meals on the go. Additionally, the complex is a haven for families, boasting a children's playground and a picnic area where guests can enjoy leisurely afternoons.

Located conveniently close to Saranda City Beach and Maestral Beach, the property serves as an ideal base for beach enthusiasts. Furthermore, with Ioannina Airport just 98 km away, it offers a feasible option for international travelers.

In summary, Kiri S.Apartaments emerges as a compelling choice for those visiting Sarandë, providing a harmonious mix of natural beauty, family-friendly amenities, and thoughtful conveniences to enhance the stay of every guest.
