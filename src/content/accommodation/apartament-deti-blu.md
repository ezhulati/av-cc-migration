The apartment boasts two well-appointed bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen ready for culinary adventures with an oven and a fridge, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, and the inclusion of a bathroom fitted with a shower, along with towels and bed linen, adds to the comfort of the stay. Designed with a non-smoking and soundproof environment, Apartament Deti Blu prioritizes guest comfort and tranquility.

Situated a mere 2.2 km from the vibrant Coco Bongo Beach and close to the historical Kuzum Baba at 6.7 km, the apartment is perfectly positioned for guests to discover the local attractions. For those traveling further afield, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 156 km away, making Apartament Deti Blu an excellent base for both local and international visitors. Independence Square, a notable landmark, is also within easy reach at 6.9 km, offering a glimpse into the area's rich history and culture.
