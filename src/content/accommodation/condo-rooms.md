Each apartment within the complex is thoughtfully equipped with essential amenities including a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom complete with bed linen and towels. The highlight of each unit is undoubtedly the patio, featuring an outdoor dining area where guests can enjoy meals against the backdrop of breathtaking sea views. Select apartments further boast a dressing room, adding an extra layer of privacy and convenience for guests.

For those looking to explore the area at their own pace, Condo Rooms facilitates this with both bicycle and car rental services available on-site. The property's strategic location also places guests within easy reach of Saranda City Beach, just 1.9 km away, and the picturesque Maestral Beach, located 2.9 km from the premises. For international travelers, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, situated 98 km from the accommodation.

Condo Rooms not only promises a stay marked by comfort and convenience but also offers a gateway to the stunning coastal beauty of Sarandë, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and adventure seekers.
