Guests are greeted with an array of facilities designed to enhance their stay, including a restaurant, elevator access, and round-the-clock security, complemented by complimentary WiFi accessible throughout the premises. For those arriving from afar, the convenience of airport transfer services and the option for car rental services directly from the property ensures a seamless travel experience.

The accommodation itself, a spacious apartment, is thoughtfully appointed with modern comforts and conveniences. It features a bedroom, a living area with a flat-screen TV equipped with streaming services, and a fully equipped kitchen boasting a dishwasher, oven, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop. The air-conditioned space also includes a bidet and a dressing room, with the added benefits of bed linen, towels, and laundry service to make guests feel at home.

For leisure and convenience, the property offers a coffee shop and a minimarket on-site. Active guests will appreciate the bicycle rental service and the ski pass sales point, inviting exploration of the surrounding ski areas.

Cultural enthusiasts and shoppers will find the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, National Museum of History Albania, and Toptani Shopping Centre within easy reach, making Skyline Sunset City Center an ideal base for discovering the best of Tirana. With Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 14 km away, guests enjoy both the vibrancy of city life and the ease of travel to and from this distinguished accommodation.
