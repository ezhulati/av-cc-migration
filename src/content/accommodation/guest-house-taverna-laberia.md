Privacy and tranquility are paramount at Taverna Laberia, with features such as a private entrance and soundproofing contributing to a peaceful retreat. Guests can enjoy the beauty of the surrounding garden, providing a perfect backdrop for relaxation. Additionally, the convenience of complimentary WiFi and private parking on site enhances the overall guest experience.

Situated just 44 km from the picturesque Zaravina Lake and with Ioannina Airport 83 km away, the location of Guest House Taverna Laberia makes it an ideal base for exploring the beauty of the region. Whether visiting for leisure or seeking a peaceful escape, this guest house promises a memorable stay in Gjirokastër.
