Offering a serene retreat, Central Studio is equipped with air conditioning and ensures a secure stay with full-day security services. For guests looking to venture beyond, the property facilitates exploration with airport transfer services and the availability of a car rental service.

The apartment itself is a testament to modern living, featuring free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a washing machine. The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven and fridge, catering to all culinary needs, while towels and bed linen add a touch of home. Emphasizing a healthy living environment, the accommodation is strictly non-smoking.

Strategically positioned, Central Studio is within easy reach of notable attractions such as the National Museum of History Albania, the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Albania, and the House of Leaves. For those arriving by air, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is conveniently located 13 km away, making Central Studio an ideal base for both short and extended stays in Tirana.
