The apartment itself is a haven of modern amenities, featuring air-conditioned spaces that open up to a balcony offering breathtaking sea views. Guests can enjoy the luxury of free WiFi access throughout the premises, ensuring connectivity and convenience are never compromised. The commitment to a smoke-free environment further enhances the appeal, catering to those who prioritize health and wellness during their stay.

Accommodation at Breeze Seaview Apartment includes a well-appointed bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and fridge, and a cozy seating area perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. The inclusion of a flat-screen TV ensures entertainment needs are met, while the bathroom, complete with a bath, adds an extra layer of comfort. The provision of towels and bed linen underscores the attention to detail and guest convenience.

Outdoor enthusiasts will appreciate the property's garden and terrace, which offer an idyllic setting for dining al fresco while admiring the sea vistas. For those looking to explore the local area or venture further afield, the availability of a car rental service directly from the apartment adds a layer of ease to travel plans.

Located just 2.3 km from Golem Beach and within a manageable distance of 41 km from Skanderbeg Square, Breeze Seaview Apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the region's attractions. The proximity to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 36 km away, coupled with the option of a paid airport shuttle service, ensures a seamless travel experience for arriving and departing guests.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest satisfaction, Breeze Seaview Apartment stands out as a top accommodation choice for those visiting Durrës.
