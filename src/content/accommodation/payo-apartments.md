Each apartment at Payo Apartments is designed with the modern traveler in mind, featuring balconies that offer sweeping cityscapes, cozy seating areas for relaxation, and cable flat-screen TVs for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchens, complete with ovens and fridges, allow guests the freedom to prepare their own meals, adding a touch of home. The inclusion of private bathrooms with showers and slippers enhances the comfort of each stay.

For those who prioritize connectivity, complimentary WiFi is available throughout the property, ensuring that guests remain connected with the world. Select units further elevate the experience with expansive terraces, perfect for enjoying the serene surroundings. The convenience of air conditioning and ample wardrobe space in every apartment ensures a comfortable and hassle-free stay.

Situated 17 km from the enchanting Butrint National Park and within proximity to Ioannina Airport, 97 km away, Payo Apartments serves as an ideal base for exploring the natural and historical wonders of the region. Whether traveling as a family or solo, Payo Apartments offers a welcoming retreat in Sarandë, promising a memorable stay marked by comfort and convenience.
