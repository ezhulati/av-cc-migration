The hotel prides itself on offering well-appointed family rooms, each equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV for ultimate comfort and entertainment. The attention to detail is evident in the private bathrooms, which come with a shower, complimentary toiletries, and the added convenience of free WiFi throughout the property. Select rooms also feature balconies, allowing guests to soak in the vibrant atmosphere of Sarandë from the comfort of their accommodation.

For those interested in exploring beyond the beach, Hotel Rai serves as an excellent base. Maestral Beach is just a short distance away, offering another picturesque spot for relaxation and sunbathing. Meanwhile, the historic Butrint National Park, a treasure trove of ancient ruins and natural beauty, is only 17 km away, promising an enriching day trip. For guests arriving by air, Ioannina Airport is the nearest gateway, located 97 km from the hotel.

Hotel Rai emerges as a top contender for visitors to Sarandë, combining the convenience of its location with the quality of its facilities and the warmth of its hospitality. Whether it's lounging on the beach, exploring local attractions, or simply enjoying the view from a balcony, this hotel caters to a wide range of preferences, making it a sought-after destination for discerning travelers.
