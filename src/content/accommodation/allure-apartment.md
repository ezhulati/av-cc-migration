Guests can enjoy the modern comforts of air-conditioned units, each equipped with a seating area perfect for relaxation, a flat-screen TV with streaming services for entertainment, and a fully-equipped kitchen and dining area for those who prefer to prepare their own meals. The safety deposit box adds an extra layer of security, while the private bathroom comes complete with a shower, a hair dryer, and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a comfortable stay.

What sets Allure Apartment apart is the breathtaking river view from the terrace, where guests can also find an outdoor dining area to enjoy meals with a view. The promise of sea views from the balcony in all units adds a touch of serenity to the stay, making it an ideal retreat for those looking to unwind.

For those exploring Sarandë and its surroundings, the convenience of an on-site car rental service cannot be overstated. With Santa Quaranta Beach just 500 meters away and the historic Butrint National Park within a 13 km drive, guests have easy access to some of the area's most sought-after attractions. Additionally, the proximity to Ioannina Airport, 97 km away, offers peace of mind to international travelers.

Allure Apartment not only promises a stay filled with comfort and convenience but also positions itself as a gateway to the enchanting beauty of Sarandë and its beaches, making it a top recommendation for those planning their next getaway.
