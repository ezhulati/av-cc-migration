The accommodations at Appartamenti Vista Mare Saranda are thoughtfully designed with the modern traveler in mind. Each unit boasts a private balcony where guests can soak in breathtaking sea views, a cozy seating area for relaxation, and a cable flat-screen TV for entertainment. The fully equipped kitchen, complete with a fridge, stovetop, and essential kitchenware, adds a touch of home, while the inclusion of a kettle ensures a warm cup is never out of reach. The private bathrooms are well-appointed with showers and hair dryers, ensuring a refreshing start or end to the day. For added convenience, bed linen and towels are provided in all units.

The vicinity of Appartamenti Vista Mare Saranda is dotted with dining options, allowing guests to explore a variety of local flavors just steps from their door. For those looking to immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Sarandë, activities such as snorkeling and fishing offer an exciting glimpse into the local culture and natural beauty.

Further afield, the allure of VIP Beach awaits at a mere 1.6 km distance, while the historic Butrint National Park, located 15 km away, offers a fascinating excursion for history buffs. With Ioannina Airport 96 km away, the aparthotel serves as an accessible retreat for both domestic and international travelers.

In summary, Appartamenti Vista Mare Saranda is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the best of Sarandë, combining the comforts of home with the thrill of exploration.
