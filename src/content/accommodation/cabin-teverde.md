The villa itself is a masterpiece of comfort, featuring air conditioning for those warm summer days, a separate bedroom for privacy, a cozy living room for relaxation, and a bathroom equipped with all essentials. Entertainment is readily available with a flat-screen TV, making each stay both enjoyable and comfortable. The added benefit of a private entrance enhances the sense of exclusivity and privacy for guests.

Culinary delights await at the on-site restaurant, where a diverse à la carte breakfast menu featuring warm dishes, local specialties, and fresh fruits promises a delightful start to the day. The family-friendly dining venue extends its offerings to include dinner, lunch, brunch, and an enticing selection of cocktails, catering to all tastes and preferences.

Families visiting Cabin Teverde will appreciate the thoughtful inclusion of outdoor play equipment, ensuring that younger guests are entertained and engaged. For those looking to explore the surrounding area, the villa conveniently offers a car rental service, making local excursions effortlessly accessible.

Situated a mere 62 km from Podgorica Airport, Cabin Teverde presents an ideal retreat for travelers seeking a blend of serenity, comfort, and convenience in the heart of Shkodër.
