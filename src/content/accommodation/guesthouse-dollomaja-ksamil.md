This 3-star guesthouse ensures a comfortable stay with air-conditioned accommodations that boast fully equipped kitchens, including a fridge and stovetop, alongside a dedicated dining area. Select units elevate the experience with terraces offering breathtaking sea views. Privacy and comfort are paramount, with each room featuring a private entrance, bathroom, and thoughtful amenities such as bed linen and towels.

For those eager to explore, Guesthouse Dollomaja Ksamil doesn't disappoint. The property facilitates memorable sightseeing tours around the neighborhood, and for the more independently spirited, both bicycle and car rental services are readily available. This positions guests perfectly to discover nearby attractions, including the vibrant Bora Bora Beach and the serene Ksamil Beach 7 and 9.

Accessibility is seamless, with Corfu International Airport located 93 km away, making Guesthouse Dollomaja Ksamil an ideal choice for both international travelers and those looking to immerse themselves in the natural beauty and local culture of Ksamil.
