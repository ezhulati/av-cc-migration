This air-conditioned apartment is a haven of tranquility, offering guests a serene retreat with stunning city views. The balcony provides a perfect vantage point for soaking in the sea views, making every moment of the stay memorable. Inside, the apartment is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen complete with an oven and a microwave, and a cozy bathroom with a bath. The provision of towels and bed linen adds a touch of home comfort to the accommodation, which maintains a strict non-smoking policy to ensure a pleasant environment for all guests.

For those interested in exploring the rich history and culture of Durrës, the Durres Amphitheatre is just 3.3 km away, while the House of Leaves lies 36 km from the apartment. Moreover, the convenience of being 31 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport makes SkyLine 574 an ideal base for both short and extended stays in the region.

With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to guest comfort, SkyLine 574 is an excellent choice for travelers looking to experience the best of Durrës.
