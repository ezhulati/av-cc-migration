The apartment itself is a haven of space and light, boasting a generous layout that includes a bedroom, a living area with a flat-screen TV, and a balcony that invites guests to bask in the breathtaking coastal ambiance. The kitchen is thoughtfully equipped with modern appliances such as a microwave, toaster, washing machine, fridge, and stovetop, ensuring that every culinary need is met. For added convenience, towels and bed linen are readily provided, enhancing the home-away-from-home feel.

Guests can also enjoy al fresco dining in the property's outdoor area, perfect for those balmy evenings under the stars. While the allure of the beach is undeniable, Apartmán Kamelie also serves as an excellent base for exploring further afield. The historic Skanderbeg Square and the adventurous Dajti Ekspres Cable Car are within accessible distances, at 43 km and 47 km respectively, promising enriching day trips for those inclined to venture beyond the shore.

Situated 37 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, the apartment ensures ease of travel for international guests. With its strategic location, comprehensive amenities, and commitment to non-smoking, clean living spaces, Apartmán Kamelie stands out as a top-tier accommodation choice for those visiting Durrës, whether for leisure or exploration.
