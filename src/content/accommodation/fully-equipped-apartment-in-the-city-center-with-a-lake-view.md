This beachfront gem boasts modern amenities in a smoke-free environment, ensuring a comfortable and convenient stay. Guests can enjoy the luxury of air conditioning, free WiFi, and a flat-screen TV, alongside practical features such as a washing machine and a fully equipped kitchen complete with a microwave and kitchenware. The highlight, however, is undoubtedly the balcony, which presents a stunning vista of the lake, promising serene mornings and tranquil evenings.

Situated 40 km from both Port Ohrid and the Cave Church Archangel Michael, the apartment serves as an ideal base for exploring the wider region. Moreover, with Ohrid Airport just 46 km away, access to this haven of relaxation and adventure is hassle-free.

This lakeview apartment in Pogradec is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing the beauty and history of the region, all from the comfort of a home-like setting.
