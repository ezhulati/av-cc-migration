The studio apartment is a marvel of modern design and functionality, featuring a soundproof environment that promises undisturbed tranquility just 200 meters from Sarande Main Beach. Guests are treated to a suite of amenities including a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen, all complemented by a balcony that presents breathtaking sea views. The air-conditioned unit also boasts a bidet and a dressing room, catering to the needs of the most discerning guests. Notably, the apartment is designed to be allergy-free and maintains a strict non-smoking policy, ensuring a comfortable stay for all.

Convenience is further enhanced by the inclusion of a lift and a minimarket within the property, alongside complimentary WiFi access throughout, allowing guests to stay connected and supplied with essentials without having to leave the comfort of their retreat.

For those seeking to explore the vibrant life in and around Sarandë, the studio serves as an ideal base. From hiking adventures that beckon the spirited traveler to leisurely strolls along Saranda City Beach, located just 1.4 km away, there is no shortage of activities to enrich your stay. Saranda Bay Beach is also within easy reach, merely 1.6 km from the studio, offering another picturesque spot for relaxation and sunbathing.

An on-site bar provides the perfect setting for evening relaxation or socializing, offering a selection of drinks in a welcoming atmosphere.

Situated a considerable distance from Ioannina Airport, 98 km away, guests are advised to plan their travel arrangements accordingly to enjoy the seamless tranquility and luxury that Eriks Studio with Amazing Sea View has to offer. This property stands out as a jewel in Sarandë, promising an unforgettable stay with its blend of natural beauty, comfort, and convenience.
