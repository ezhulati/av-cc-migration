A mere stone's throw away from the historical Former Residence of Enver Hoxha, and a short drive from the natural wonder of the Rock of Kavaje, the apartment serves as an ideal base for both cultural aficionados and nature lovers. Additionally, the National Museum of History Albania is located just under 2 km away, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of the country's past. For those seeking adventure, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car awaits at a distance of 4 km, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

The apartment itself is a sanctuary of modern living, featuring air conditioning for those warm Albanian days. It boasts a separate bedroom, a cozy living room for relaxation, a fully equipped kitchen for culinary exploits, and a pristine bathroom. Entertainment needs are catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

For shopping enthusiasts, the Toptani Shopping Centre is conveniently located 2 km away, offering a variety of retail options. Meanwhile, the historic Tanners' Bridge, 2.3 km from the apartment, provides a glimpse into the architectural heritage of Tirana. The nearest airport, Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, is 14 km away, making Lovely and Quite Apartment 1-3 a an excellent choice for travelers seeking both convenience and a tranquil retreat in the heart of Tirana.
