The commitment to guest satisfaction is evident through the provision of room service and the assurance of assistance at any hour with a 24-hour front desk. For those who appreciate a view, select accommodations offer balconies that present an enchanting cityscape, promising a serene backdrop to mornings or evenings spent in the privacy of one's room.

Culinary enthusiasts or those preferring the comfort of home-cooked meals will find solace in rooms equipped with a kitchen, including a fridge, catering to the convenience of extended stays or simply those spontaneous moments of culinary inspiration.

Strategically positioned, Hotel Driloni is a mere 600 meters from the vibrant Bora Bora Beach and closely located to Ksamil Beach 7, making it an ideal base for beach lovers and explorers alike. For international travelers, the proximity to Corfu International Airport, 92 km away, underscores the hotel's appeal as a gateway to the enchanting region of Ksamil.

Hotel Driloni stands out as a testament to thoughtful hospitality, where every detail is curated to ensure a memorable stay in one of Albania's most picturesque locales.
