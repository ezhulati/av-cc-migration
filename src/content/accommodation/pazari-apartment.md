This air-conditioned apartment boasts a thoughtful layout, featuring two separate bedrooms, a cozy living room, a fully equipped kitchen for those who love to prepare their meals, and a bathroom designed with modern amenities. Entertainment needs are well catered to with a flat-screen TV, ensuring guests have a comfortable stay. The entire space is a non-smoking area, maintaining a fresh and welcoming environment for all guests.

Adding to the appeal is the apartment's terrace, an ideal spot for guests to relax and enjoy the city's ambiance. Free WiFi throughout the property ensures that guests stay connected, whether for leisure or work. For those looking to venture further, the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car offers breathtaking views and is just a short drive away, while the Rock of Kavaje presents a natural escape for outdoor enthusiasts, albeit a bit farther.

Conveniently located, Pazari Apartment is about 14 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, making it an accessible retreat for international and domestic travelers alike. Whether in town for culture, shopping, or simply a relaxing getaway, Pazari Apartment provides a comfortable and strategically located home base in Tirana.
