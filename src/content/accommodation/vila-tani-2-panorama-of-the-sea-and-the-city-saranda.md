Guests at Vila Tani 2 are treated to accommodations that are not only air-conditioned but also come with the added benefits of complimentary private parking and WiFi access. Each apartment is designed to maximize comfort and aesthetic appeal, featuring balconies that present breathtaking city views. The interiors are thoughtfully equipped with satellite flat-screen TVs, dining areas, well-appointed kitchens, and private bathrooms complete with showers and hair dryers. For those in search of a little extra space, select units also offer seating areas and/or terraces, enhancing the living experience.

Attention to detail is evident in the provision of bed linen and towels in all units, ensuring a stay that combines the comforts of home with the luxuries of a hotel. For guests looking to explore beyond the city, the nearest airport, Ioannina Airport, is conveniently located 95 km away, making Vila Tani 2 an ideal base for both local and regional adventures.

In summary, Vila Tani 2 Panorama Of the Sea and the City Saranda stands out as a compelling choice for visitors to Sarandë, offering a harmonious blend of location, amenities, and comfort.
